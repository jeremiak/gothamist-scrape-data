					<p>On Tuesday, New York City will finally be blessed with 1,760 calorie desserts when The Cheesecake Factory <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161027020608/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/01/cheesecake_factory_nyc.php">opens its first restaurant</a> in the five boroughs. Located in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161027020608/http://www.shopqueenscenter.com/">Queens Center</a>&#x2014;not to be confused with Queens Place, a similar, smaller mall right next door&#x2014;the restaurant looks exactly like other locations elsewhere in the USA. There&apos;s the labyrinthine seating, the army of staff (they&apos;ve employed 285 people for this location alone), the tempting cheesecake case just beyond the host&apos;s station, which you&apos;ll become intimately acquainted with suffering the chain&apos;s notorious waits for a table.</p>

<p>The robust portions&#x2014;seriously, that is <em>too</em> much food!&#x2014;and equally hefty menu are also in full effect, with classics like the Bang Bang Chicken &amp; Shrimp and cauldron-sized bowls of cream-sauced pastas. As a chain restaurant, Cheesecake Factory is obligated to post calorie counts on its menus; try not to read the fine print if you plan on enjoying your dinner (and dessert).</p>

<p>When it opens, the restaurant will seat about 190 for lunch and dinner.</p>

<p><em>The Cheesecake Factory is located inside the Queens Center at 90-15 Queens Boulevard in Elmhurst, (718) 699-1212; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161027020608/http://www.thecheesecakefactory.com/">cheesecakefactory.com</a></em></p>
