<p><strong>[Update: The event is now sold out!] </strong>Gothamist&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.ticketfly.com/purchase/event/343631">first ever trivia night</a> goes down in less than a week on October 2nd and we are PUMPED! We&apos;ll be taking over the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.thebellhouseny.com/event/343631-gothamist-trivia-night-know-brooklyn/">Bell House</a> next Wednesday with our friends at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.trivworks.com/blog/">TrivWorks</a> to dish out some exciting NYC trivia. <strike>We <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.ticketfly.com/purchase/event/343631?utm_medium=bks&amp;wrKey=E0B7A7365E3DE73060F02EF427834DF8">still have a few tickets</a> available</strike><strong> We&apos;re excited to report that the event is sold out!</strong></p>

<p>Need more incentive than bragging rights among fellow Gothamist readers? Expect some <em>very special surprise guests</em> with important ties to the Gothamist community. We&apos;ve also got some sick prizes for our overall trivia winners, as well as runner up prizes for 2nd and 3rd place teams.  </p>

<p>Our friends at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.bbcamericashop.com/">BBC America</a> put together a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://instagram.com/p/esZ4BCLEL7/">dope prize package</a> with DVDs, t-shirts and even a <em>lamp</em> from cult favorite TV show <em>Doctor Who</em>. We&apos;ve also got passes to the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.cbgb.com/conference-film.php">CBGB Festival</a>, bowling nights at the city&apos;s best venues, delicious treats from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.fairwaymarket.com/">Fairway Market</a>, drinks at some fancy restaurants, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://millionairetv.dadt.com/"><em>Who Wants To Be A Millionaire?</em></a> prize packages and much more to be announced at the event!</p>

<p>We&apos;ve also got an incredibly fitting reward for our Grand Prize: everyone on the 1st Place team wins a <strong><em>year long membership</em> to NYC&apos;s fantastic <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://citibikenyc.com/">Citi Bike</a></strong> bike share program, with second and third place teams scoring week-long and day-long passes, respectively. Your blue chariots await you!</p>

<p>Oh, and did we mention you can also win <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://instagram.com/p/eU4lr_LENE/"><strong>CRONUTS</strong></a>? Boom.</p>

<p>Below, some handy FAQs from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.trivworks.com/blog/">TrivWorks</a> about what to expect at the Gothamist Know Your City trivia night: <br>
</p><ul><br>
<li><strong>What time does it start/end?</strong> Doors are at 7:30 p.m., with an 8 p.m. event start. We aim to wrap up between 9:30 - 10 p.m.</li><br>
<li><strong>Am I guaranteed a seat?</strong> Please note, The Bell House is a mixed seating/standing venue. As such, we encourage you to arrive early for best seating options (we will also be piping in the audio into the Front Lounge).</li><br>
<li><strong>Are tickets still available?</strong>No. As of Monday, September 30th the event is sold out. <strong>We may be releasing a few extra tickets in the next day&#x2014;stay tuned!</strong></li><br>
<li><strong>What is the format of the evening?</strong> We will be announcing multiple rounds of trivia questions to the entire room, with each round having a specific NYC theme.</li><br>
<li><strong>Do I need to be part of a team, or can I play alone?</strong> This is intended as a team trivia contest, so please arrive with your team. However, if you are planning to attend on your own, Gothamist staff will gladly help you find a team.</li><br>
<li><strong>How many people can be on a team? Is there a minimum-person team size?</strong>&#xA0;To keep the event as fair as possible, we are limiting team sizes to 8 people. There&apos;s no minimum, however, to help make the event run as smoothly and efficiently as possible, we ask that smaller teams to join forces to make larger teams.</li><br>
<li><strong>Do we need a team name?</strong> Yes! You will be required to submit your team name immediately upon arrival, so please give it some thought in advance (funny/timely team names strongly encouraged).</li><br>
<li><strong>Is cheating allowed?</strong> No. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u613pJSLcfk">Come on!</a></li><br>
<li><strong>How can I prepare?</strong> Easy! Review our handy <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://www.trivworks.com/2013/08/gothamist-trivia-night/">Gothamist trivia night study guide</a>!</li><br>
<li><strong>Will there be food?</strong> Yes! Brooklyn&apos;s own <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150705175117/http://dalypie.myshopify.com/">Daly Pie</a> will be on hand selling their delicious hand pies - the perfect trivia treat, you&apos;ll love them!</li><br>
<li><strong>I have more questions - what do I do?</strong> Easy. Send an email to tips@gothamist.com or tweet to us @Gothamist &amp; @TrivWorks using the #GothamistTrivia hashtag - we&apos;ll get back to you right away!</li><br>
</ul><p></p>
