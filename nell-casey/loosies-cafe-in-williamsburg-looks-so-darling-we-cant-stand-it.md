					<p>Loosie&apos;s Cafe&#x2014;the third installment in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616110804/http://gothamist.com/2015/08/11/loosies_kitchen_south_williamsburg.php#photo-1">Loosie fleet</a> that includes a New Orleans-inspired bar and a restaurant called Loosie&apos;s Kitchen on Williamsburg&apos;s South 6th Street&#x2014;opens Friday adjacent to its sister dining and drinking establishments. The all-day cafe will serve coffee drinks and pastries in the morning, which evolves into day-drinking when happy hour begins at noon. If the words &quot;frozen green goddess matcha margarita&quot; mean something to you, this is your new go-to spot.</p>

<p>Same goes for things like avocado tartines, grain bowls and &quot;Matcha Spirulina Donuts,&quot; all of which are part of the lunch menu. They&apos;re also doing a BEC Biscuit with a bacon gravy, which lightens up the preciousness a bit. Croissant, cookies, doughnuts and Nutella beignets made by pastry chef Kyla Kelly round out the all-day menu.<br>
 <br>
In keeping with Loosie&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616110804/http://gothamist.com/2015/08/11/loosies_kitchen_south_williamsburg.php#photo-1">design aesthetic</a>, there&apos;s plenty of greenery and even some hammocks to relax in. A non-functioning bathtub has been repurposed into a makeshift table, setting the scene for an avalanche of Instagrams.</p>

<p>If you&apos;re lucky, you may even meet Bobby, co-owner Vincent Marino&#x2019;s dog.</p>
