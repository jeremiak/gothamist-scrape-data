<p>Outstanding <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/http://gothamist.com/2014/12/18/best_restaurants_nyc_2014.php">2015 newcomer</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/http://www.tuomenyc.com/">Tuome</a> has begun a <strong>passed dim sum</strong> program to add to their menu on the weekends. Some things you might see being whirled around the tables: <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/https://www.instagram.com/p/BNfaDD6jLXO/">dumplings in duck pho broth</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/https://www.instagram.com/p/BM7YW4wjq-q/">oxtail croquettes</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/https://www.instagram.com/p/BOGW0NeD0cl/">housemade beef pastrami</a>, Uni Shu Mai and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/https://www.instagram.com/p/BO8FrKVDJdF/">Tempura Shrimp</a> with caviar and creme fraiche. </p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/https://veganshopup.wordpress.com/"><strong>Vegan Shop Up</strong></a> is back at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/http://www.pineboxrockshop.com/">Pine Box Rock Shop</a> this Saturday, starting early at 11 a.m. and concluding at 4 p.m. During that time, gorge on vegan empanadas, doughnuts, pecan pie, pizza, macarons and more ready-made treats, plus stock up on vegan beauty products, spreads, cheeses, teas and more. The Rock Shop also has a crazy bloody mary selection, if that&apos;s your thing.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="011316ottos.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_nellcasey/011316ottos.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> (courtesy Den Hospitality and Otto&#x2019;s Tacos)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Saturday day drinking at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/http://www.thegarreteast.com/">The Garret East</a> now includes the option of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/http://ottostacos.com/">Otto&apos;s Tacos</a> without having to leave the bar. Beginning this weekend, the Otto&apos;s team will be setting up a <strong>mobile taco stand</strong> serving up their signature corn tortillas stuffed with meats, veggies and their salsas from 1 p.m. to 5 p.m. The bar&apos;s also going to be mixing up two drink specials: Coconut Coffee Horchata and a Frozen Michelada. </p>

<p>Don&apos;t call it brunch&#x2014;this is <strong>weekend lunch</strong> debuting at two new restaurants. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/http://www.fishcheeksnyc.com/">Fish Cheeks</a> in NoHo is doing a Thai lunch of dishes like Senn Lek Tom Yum with Prince Edward Island mussels, shrimp, squid, thin rice noodles, soft boiled egg, cilantro, galangal, shallot and lemongrass broth and Pla Muk Kai Kem with stir fried squid, salted duck egg, scallion, celery leaves, onion and long red chili over rice. Over in Brooklyn, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170116043904/http://www.21greenpoint.com/">21 Greenpoint</a> will offer Whole Roasted Acadian Redfish with warm potato salad and a Spanish Omelette with Linguica sausage, chives, Pecorino Romano and a side salad. </p>
