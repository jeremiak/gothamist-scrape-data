<p>Yankees pitcher and all-around good dude Mariano Rivera continued his <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130719045445/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/28/mariano_rivera_will_throw_out_first.php">farewell tour</a> of the country&apos;s stadiums with a stop at the Oakland Athletics stadium this week. This time, the closer made a special delivery to the facility&apos;s mail room, where he <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130719045445/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/06/13/pizza-man-mariano-rivera-surprises-as-employee-with-pregame-delivery/">surprised longtime employee Julie Vasconcellos with a pizza</a>.</p>

<p>The Sandman recognized Vasconcellos for her 25 years of service to the organization with the pie, a signed ball and plenty of hugs and kind words. &#x201C;This is my way to say thank you, thank you for what you do,&#x201D; Rivera told Vasconcellos. &#x201C;Twenty-five years, Julie, that&#x2019;s great! You&#x2019;re special, Julie, that&#x2019;s what it is. People here love you.&#x201D; And we love <em>you</em>, Mo!</p>

<p>The Yankees great, who is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130719045445/http://gothamist.com/2013/03/09/exit_sandman_marino_rivera_announce.php">retiring at the end of the season</a>, has been recognized at each of the stadiums the team visits this year. Rivera, in turn, has been taking time to thank the fans and behind-the-scenes staff for their loyalty and dedication over the years. So far on his &quot;Mo-ment of Thanks&quot; tour, Rivera has recognized Cleveland&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130719045445/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/04/11/mariano-keeps-it-classy-in-cleveland-thanks-indians-staffers-for-memories/">infamous drummer</a> and fans from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130719045445/http://www.freep.com/article/20130407/COL38/304070244/Jeff-Seidel-Yankees-closer-Mariano-Rivera-s-rare-talent-worth-celebrating">Detroit</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130719045445/http://mlb.mlb.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20130511&amp;content_id=47269862&amp;vkey=news_mlb&amp;c_id=mlb">Kansas City</a>. </p>
