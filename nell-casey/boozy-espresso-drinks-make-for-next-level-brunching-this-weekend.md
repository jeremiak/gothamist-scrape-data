<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160917155242/http://www.pineboxrockshop.com/">Pine Box Rock Shop</a> hosts the <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160917155242/https://veganshopup.wordpress.com/">Vegan Shop Up</a></strong> on Saturday, offering a playground of cruelty-free eats and dry goods like lipstick and handbags. Highlights of this weekend&apos;s pop-up include newcomer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160917155242/https://www.facebook.com/PhoebesSourdoughBakeryInc/">Phoebe&apos;s Sourdough</a>, offering freshly baked breads; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160917155242/http://kickingmule.com/">Kicking Mule</a> vegan chocolate milk; animal-free chocolates and candy from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160917155242/https://www.hunnybon.com/">Hunnybon</a>; and delicious doughnuts all the way from Texas courtesy of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160917155242/https://www.facebook.com/Cakethieves/photos">Cake Thieves</a>. The bar&apos;s exemplary bloody marys will also be up for grabs.</p>

<p>A walk-around wine tasting event benefitting NYC&apos;s disabled communities comes to City Winery this Saturday afternoon. The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160917155242/http://www.wineonwheels.org/"><strong>Wine On Wheels</strong></a> Grand Tasting features over 200 wines from 30 different wine regions for sipping and sampling from 1 p.m. to 4 p.m. Tickets are $95 in advance and $125 at the door and include all the wine tasting plus access to &quot;fast track seminars,&quot; where professional wine folks discuss their fields and major topics related to wine.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160917155242/http://fairweatherbushwick.com/">Fair Weather Bushwick</a>, the neighborhood&apos;s newly revamped <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160917155242/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/21/fair_weather_bushwick.php">tasting menu restaurant</a>, launches an <strong>a la carte brunch</strong> this weekend in their 20-seat space. Nosh on a Churro Waffle, a Belgian waffle spiced with Mexican seasonings like clove, allspice and cinnamon; a Breakfast Sandwich with either turkey or avocado on baguette with basil pesto, honey chipotle and egg; or next level Avocado Hash Toast: a potato cake topped with smashed avocado, radish and chipotle aioli with the choice to add proteins like eggs or hanger steak. </p>

<p>There&apos;s even a boozy coffee drink, the Shochu Latte, made with espresso, shochu and hazelnut syrup topped with steamed cream.</p>
