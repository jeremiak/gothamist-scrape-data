					<p>A refurbished section of a food court opens Wednesday in Flushing, bringing some delicious-sounding&#x2014;and seasonally appropriate&#x2014;foods to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161210134755/http://www.queenscrossing.com/">Queens Crossing</a>, the giant development on Main Street. There will eventually be nine stalls populating the space, and the first three debut this week: Curry Bo, Young Street Poke Co., and Happy Lemon.</p>

<p>Happy Lemon, a bubble tea operation that&apos;s already got another Flushing location, brings beverage offerings like Red Bean Matcha Lattes and Grapefruit Lemon Slushies. They&apos;re also doing their signature treat Rock Salted Cheese, a whipped up mix of enriched cheese and rock salt combined with a choice of tea, an item found at their many outlets across Taiwan, Hong Kong and Shanghai and beyond. </p>

<p>Curry Bo, meanwhile, offers renditions of the classic Japanese comfort food by Japanese Chef Takanori Akiyama, who&apos;s doing a thicker version of the South Asian classic in both meat and vegetarian preparations with rice and pickles. Finally, Young Street Poke&apos;s doing Hawaii&apos;s prized dish by native Natalie Graham, with pre-designed poke don plus customizable bowls. </p>
