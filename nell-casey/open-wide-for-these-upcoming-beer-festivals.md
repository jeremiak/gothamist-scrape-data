<p>Beer week means every week for most of us, but the official <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.craftbeer.com/news-and-events/american-craft-beer-week">American Craft Beer Week</a><a> celebrates the sudsy stuff beginning today through May 19th. The </a><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.brewersassociation.org/">Brewers Association</a> organized <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.craftbeer.com/news-and-events/calendar?event_type=530">tons of events</a> happening all across the country, including several local tastings and other beer-centric gatherings. There are also upcoming festivals at Eataly and the craft beer and food festival Savor on the horizon as well.</p>

<p>The headliner event in New York City goes down Thursday, May 16th at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.nybeercompany.com/index.html">New York Beer Company</a>, where the first ever <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://brooklynbrewery.com/blog/event/nyc-brewers-guild-one-year-anniversary-party-nationwide-cheers/"><strong>American Craft Beer Week Coast to Coast Toast</strong></a> brings together NYC Brewers Guild members for drinking and toasting. Pints will be poured from local breweries like Bronx, Chelsea Brewering Co, Sixpoint and Shmaltz starting at 7 p.m., with everyone raising their glasses for the big nationwide toast at 8 p.m.</p>

<p>From May 17th through the 27th, brewmaster and Birreria Partner Teo Musso will put the spotlight on Italian craft beers with the <strong>Eatalian Beer Festival</strong>. Musso will offer four beers named for their alcohol content&#x2014;4, 6, 8 and 10  % ABV&#x2014;for $5 with unique dish pairings at most of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.eataly.com/">Eataly</a> restaurants. The restaurant-market is also offering a beer and cheese pairing at their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.eataly.com/la-piazza/">Eccellenze Corner</a> for $10 during the beer festival. </p>

<p>Fast forward to June when <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.savorcraftbeer.com/"><strong>Savor</strong></a> comes to town, bringing together brewers and chefs for two days of drinking and dining. Try out barrel aged fruit beers or explore different types of lager beers at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.savorcraftbeer.com/attendee-guide/salons/salon-schedule/">many events</a> taking place at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.metropolitanevents.com/">Metropolitan Pavilion</a> on June 14th and 15th. You can also check out <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130724063123/http://www.savorcraftbeer.com/attendee-guide/week-events/">events going on around the festival</a>, like a Meet the Brewers from Long Island event at Jimmy&apos;s No. 43 on Saturday, June 15th.</p>
