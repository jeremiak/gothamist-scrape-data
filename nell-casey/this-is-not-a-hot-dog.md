<p>When is a hot dog not a hot dog? When it&apos;s an ice cream sandwich. Yes, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110104951/http://gothamist.com/2014/06/25/the_ny_state_department_of_taxation.php">sandwich</a>.</p>

<p>This weekend, when everyone&apos;s setting their dogs on fire on the grill, Morgenstern&apos;s Finest Ice Cream and Daniel Boulud are cooling things down with the DBGB Cool Dog, an ice cream-based creation invented by Boulud and Nicholas Morgenstern. A log of frozen creme anglaise takes the place of the tubular meat, making this a vegetarian-friendly way to celebrate hot dog season.</p>
