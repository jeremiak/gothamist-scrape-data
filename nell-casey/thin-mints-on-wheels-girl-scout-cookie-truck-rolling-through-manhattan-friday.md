<p>Girl Scout Cookie pop-up shops are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150527013838/http://gothamist.com/2012/02/22/girl_scout_cookie_pop-up_shops_are.php">so last year</a>. This year gals in green are taking it to the streets in their own <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150527013838/http://blog.girlscouts.org/2013/02/hey-new-york-check-out-national-girl.html">mobile cookie dispensing truck</a> in honor of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150527013838/http://blog.girlscouts.org/2013/01/girl-scouts-coast-to-coast-national.html">National Girl Scout Cookie Day</a> this Friday, February 8th. The Scouts are partnering with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150527013838/http://www.sweeterynyc.com/">Sweetery NYC</a>&#x2014;a baked good-centric food truck that has <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150527013838/http://gothamist.com/2012/05/02/free_food_alert_sweetery_giving_out.php">teamed up with other organizations in the past</a>&#x2014;to sell cookies at four Manhattan locations throughout the day.</p>

<p>Almost the entire <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150527013838/http://www.girlscouts.org/program/gs_cookies/meet_the_cookies.asp">GS Cookie collection</a> will be available for purchase, including Trefoils, Samoa&apos;s, Do si Dos, Tagalongs, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150527013838/http://gothamist.com/2012/01/05/new_girl_scout_cookie.php">Savannah Smiles</a> and, <em>of course</em>, beloved Thin Mints. Boxes can be purchased directly from the truck for $4.  </p>

<p>If you can&apos;t make it to one of the Truck stops&#x2014;or don&apos;t have a niece or little cousin to hook you up with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150527013838/http://gothamist.com/2013/01/16/meet_the_new_healthy_girl_scout_coo.php">the latest shiitake-infused cookie creation</a>&#x2014;you can <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150527013838/https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/girl-scout-cookie-finder/id593932097?mt=8">download the Official Girl Scout Cookie Finder App</a> to locate a patch-seeking young lady near you.</p>
