<p>Looking for the kind of lunch that induces an immediate nap? The new lunch menu at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170302024013/http://www.pigbleeckernyc.com/">Pig Bleecker</a> should satisfy. Beginning today and running Monday - Friday from noon to 3 p.m., nosh on sandwiches like a Pit Beef with horseradish cream and caramelized onions, Fried Chicken Thigh with smoked jalapeno pineapple coleslaw, and a Pimento Grilled Cheese on white bread. Also on offer: Smoked Mozzarella Sticks with Sunday Gravy and a Mason Jar Oreo Cheesecake. </p>

<p>If you can duck out from your desk job on the earlier side or need a weekend stopover, April Bloomfield&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170302024013/http://www.salvationburger.com/">Salvation Burger</a> begins happy hour this week daily from 4 p.m. to 6 p.m. Holy Cow Kolsh ($6), two Gotham Project wines ($7 Gruner, $8 Malbec) and a scotch cocktail ($9) are all on special, as is a new menu item: sliders. An order of two ($10) includes two beef patties each, house-made American cheese, pickles &amp; special sauce on sesame seed potato buns. </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="022717met.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170302024013im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_nellcasey/022717met.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> Metropolis (Liz Clayman)</i></div> <p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170302024013/http://www.metropolisnewyork.com/">Metropolis</a>, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170302024013/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/12/metropolis_blue_water_grill.php#photo-1">subterranean oyster bar</a> underneath Union Square&apos;s Blue Water Grill, debuts <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170302024013/http://www.metropolisnewyork.com/live-music/2017-03/">Speakeasy Thursday Nights</a></strong> featuring live jazz music. Beginning at 5:30 p.m., the restaurant hosts a rotating cast of performers in the unique space who&apos;ll soundtrack an evening of oyster slurping and martini sipping. The restaurant specializes in seafood, like flavored oysters and shellfish platters, but also offers dishes like cheesesteaks and roasted chicken.</p>

<p>Explore the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170302024013/http://gothamist.com/2016/11/28/hindu_temple_cafeterias.php#photo-1">spiritual and culinary importance</a> of the Ganesh Temple of the Hindu Temple Society of North America with a tour and dinner put on by the Queens Dinner Club. On Tuesday, the group takes us on <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170302024013/https://www.eventbrite.com/e/queens-dinner-club-presents-a-passage-to-south-india-tickets-32164959259?aff=es2">A Passage To South India</a></strong> with a tour of the temple followed by a completely vegetarian meal in the Temple Canteen. The unique meal includes Keralan and Adrahan specialties and the multi-course menu offers dishes like koottu curry with Keralan plantain and chickpea curry and masala dosa filled with potatoes. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170302024013/https://www.eventbrite.com/e/queens-dinner-club-presents-a-passage-to-south-india-tickets-32164959259?aff=es2">Tickets $35</a>. </p>
