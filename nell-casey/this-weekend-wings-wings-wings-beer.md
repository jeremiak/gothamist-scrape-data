<p>To mark the occasion of its first spin around the sun, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160828/http://coneyislandbeer.com/">Coney Island Brewery</a> will spend Saturday and Sunday <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160828/https://www.facebook.com/events/224982254568515/">throwing itself a birthday bash</a></strong> that&apos;s conveniently timed with <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160828/http://coneyislandbeer.com/2016/08/04/national-hard-root-beer-float-day/">National Root Beer Float Day</a></strong>. They&apos;ll be whipping up ice cream creations using their hard root beers, cream sodas and the like (see above) on Saturday, with tours of the brewery on Sunday. They&apos;ll also be pouring a keg of Bourbon Barrel Aged Hard Root Beer, which has been aging inside a few Bourbon barrels for the past four months.</p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160828/http://www.hesterstreetfair.com/">Hester Street Fair</a> observes <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160828/http://www.hesterstreetfair.com/events/jamaican-independence-day/">Jamaican Independence Day</a></strong> on Saturday with all-day music from Rockers NYC, Deadly Dragon, Queen Majesty, and Damon Degraff and plenty of dominoes to go around. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160828/https://www.facebook.com/SpurTreeNYC/">Spur Tree</a> will be cooking up $10 plates of Jerk Chicken, Salmon or Curry Tofu served with Rice and Peas and Pineapple Salsa and if you <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160828/http://www.hesterstreetfair.com/events/jamaican-independence-day/">buy your plate in advance</a>, they&apos;ll throw in a free beer.</p>

<p>Head to Staten Island&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160828/http://theflagshipbrewery.com/">Flagship Brewery</a> on Sunday for their second annual <strong>Wing Fest</strong>, beginning at noon inside the brewery&apos;s taproom and their adjacent parking lot. Swoop in for $6 pints of beer plus wings from 12 SI and nearby restaurants including Pier 76, Kettle Black, Afternoones and The Hop Shoppe. There&apos;ll be a selection of different types of wing sauces, including some made with Flagship&apos;s beers. </p>
