</div></div>					<p>This past weekend the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429140904/http://bbg.org/">Brooklyn Botanical Garden</a> celebrated Japanese culture (and the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429140904/http://gothamist.com/2012/03/22/brooklyn_botanic_gardens_cherry_blo.php">past peak Cherry Blossom trees</a>) with a myriad of performances and events in their annual Sakura Matsuri festival. Participants were treated to two days of Taiko-style drumming and meet-and-greets with Pokemon voice artist Veronica Taylor as well as workshops on Bonsai and Sohenryu-Style tea ceremonies. The colorful celebration culminated in a Cosplay fashion show curated by the NY Anime Festival.</p>

<p>Just in case you missed it, we collected our favorite eye candy photographs to tide you over until next year&apos;s celebration.</p>
