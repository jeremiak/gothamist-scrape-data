<p>If last week&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150501172221/http://gothamist.com/2012/12/07/free_food_alert_hot_chocolate_and_c.php">promotional airline freebies</a> left you wanting, don&apos;t worry, because there&apos;s another truck making the rounds handing out sweet snacks on Saturday. Christina Tosi, pastry maven of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150501172221/http://gothamist.com/tags/momofukumilkbar">Momofuku Milk Bar</a>, will be trucking around town handing out complimentary cookies beginning at 11 a.m. First stop: Central Park South near Columbus Circle. </p>

<p>&quot;Because the holidays are all about cookies and family,&quot; Christina will be joined by her mom and the two will give out three varieties of cookie: Chocolate-Chocolate Cookie, Cornflake Chocolate Chip Marshmellow Cookie and Compost Cookie. The holiday cookie truck will make stops in both Manhattan in Brooklyn&#x2014;see the full schedule below&#x2014;until 5 p.m. And in case you thought this giveaway was just out of the goodness of David Chang&apos;s heart, the truck is sponsored by American Express.</p>
