<p>Chefs Club in Nolita has become the go-to spot for out-of-town chefs to bring their craze-worthy signature items to NYC. Recently, the space played host to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/01/federal_donuts_in_nyc.php">Philly&apos;s Federal Donuts</a> and now, it&apos;ll host Chicago&apos;s Au Cheval, which cooks up one of the Windy City&apos;s best burgers&#x2014;depending on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://chicagoist.com/2012/07/24/does_au_cheval_have_the_best_burger.php">who you ask</a>, of course.</p>

<p>The four-year-old restaurant will be teaming up with buzzy <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://gothamist.com/tags/emily">Emily</a> (you know, they of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://gothamist.com/2016/08/11/the_best_pizza_nyc_2016.php">pizza fame</a>), <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://www.grubstreet.com/2016/09/au-cheval-burger-will-be-available-in-nyc-for-one-night-only.html?mid=full-rss-grubstreet">GrubStreet tells us</a>, for a <strike>one-night-only</strike> two-nights-only affair on October 9th and 10th <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://www.opentable.com/chefs-club-by-food-and-wine">beginning at 5:30 p.m.</a> People wait <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://chicagoist.com/2015/07/24/who_doesnt_want_to_wait_in_line_for.php">upwards of three hours</a> to eat the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://chicagoist.com/2015/10/05/best_burger_post.php">celebrated but simple </a> burger of griddle patties, American cheese, Dijonaise and pickles. Catch up on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://gothamist.com/2015/07/06/a_pizzeria_serves_one_of_brooklyns.php">the Emily burger here</a>. </p>

<p>Reservations (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://chicagoist.com/2015/07/24/who_doesnt_want_to_wait_in_line_for.php">thank god</a>) <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108014838/http://www.opentable.com/chefs-club-by-food-and-wine">begin at 5:30 p.m.</a> and the burgers will be $18 each.</p>

<p><em>This post has been updated with information about the additional night of Au Cheval&apos;s residency.</em></p>
