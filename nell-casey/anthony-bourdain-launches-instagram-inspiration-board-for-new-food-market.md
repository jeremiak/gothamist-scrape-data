<p>Anthony Bourdain&apos;s upcoming <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161016141523/http://gothamist.com/2015/05/13/anthony_bourdain_market_pier_57.php">international food market at the Super Pier</a> in Chelsea has unveiled a new Instagram account/inspiration board to store thoughts and desires for the design and food vendors. The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161016141523/https://www.instagram.com/bourdainmarket/">Bourdain Market account</a> features photos of various markets around the world, from scouting adventures the team has apparently taken to provide some influence&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161016141523/http://gothamist.com/2015/04/09/anthony_bourdain_runner.php">outside of <em>Blade Runner</em></a>, of course.</p>

The account also features some renderings made by the design team, where you can see the eclectic structure that features bits and pieces from other markets around the globe. There&apos;s going to be a lot under the 155,000-square-foot roof, including the Singapore-style hawker market Bourdain has touted <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161016141523/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/09/bourdain_market.php">since the beginning</a>, plus options from April Bloomfield and Ken Friedman, and the many international chefs and hawkers who&apos;ll be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161016141523/http://gothamist.com/2015/09/28/anthony_bourdain_new_market_details.php">scouted by the team</a>. Check out some delicious food porn <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161016141523/https://www.instagram.com/bourdainmarket/">on Instagram</a>. 
<br><br>
The market now has an <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161016141523/http://www.bourdainmarket.com/">official website</a> and a mobile app intended to keep interested parties informed about the various food partners who&apos;ll be involved when the market opens in 2017.
