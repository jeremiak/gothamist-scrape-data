<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170317193708/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/14/march_blizzard_falls_short.php">blizzard that was not</a> has left city sidewalks with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170317193708/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/14/central_park_snow_day.php#photo-1">thick layers of packed snow</a> topped with just enough ice to make walking in clunky snow boots an adventure. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170317193708/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/15/forecast_storm_departs_but_the_cold.php">Venturing out today takes bravery</a>, friends, or a strong desire to inhale hamburgers, which is what drives the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170317193708/http://burgerconquest.com/2017/03/14/the-5th-ever-polar-brrrger-club-to-happen-at-shake-shack-hq/">Polar Brrrger Club</a>, which is convening for its 5th annual sub-zero Shake Shack run today.</p>

<p>This afternoon at 1 p.m. those in the Madison Square Park area may spot a few folks in Hawaiian shirt and sunglasses ignoring the elements in search of burgers. Led by founder and organizer Rev Ciancio of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170317193708/http://burgerconquest.com/">Burger Conquest</a>, the group will nosh on ShackBurgers and fries in the elements, which today includes below freezing temperatures and wind gusts up to 26 miles per hour. </p>

<p>Anyone&apos;s welcome, Hawaiian shirts or no Hawaiian shirts; just show up and join in the <strike>insanity</strike> revelry. Those of us who would prefer to enjoy lunch <em>without</em> getting hypothermia can watch the festivities <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170317193708/https://www.shakeshack.com/location/madison-square-park/">on Shake Shack&apos;s webcam</a>. </p>
