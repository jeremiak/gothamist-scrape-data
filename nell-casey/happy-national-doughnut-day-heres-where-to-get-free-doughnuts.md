<p>With a soggy Friday dampening spirits, at least we have a fake food holiday to distract us from our weather misery. To celebrate National Doughnut Day, many of our fine doughnut purveyors&#x2014;and chain restaurants&#x2014;are offering gratis grub in the form of fried dough if you stop by their bakeries. While you likely won&apos;t <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://twitter.com/doughbrooklyn/status/342944135243759616">get a free Cronut</a> out of the deal, you can still stuff your face with sugar-soaked confectionaries at these generous bake shops:</p>

<ul>
<li><strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/http://doughnutplant.com/">Doughnut Plant</a></strong>: One complimentary <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=544266878945776&amp;set=a.147745271931274.26273.127805843925217&amp;type=1&amp;theater">Classic Mini Cake Doughnut with all-natural Carnival Sprinkles</a> with every purchase today, while supplies last. <em>(379 Grand Street; 220 West 23rd Street)</em></li>

<p></p><li><strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/http://dunwelldoughnuts.com/">Dun Well Doughnuts</a></strong>: Get one delicious, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://twitter.com/DunWellDoughnut/status/342704629596975106">vegan cake doughnut or doughnut hole free</a> with the purchase of an espresso drink. <em>(222 Montrose Avenue, Bushwick)</em></li><p></p>

<p></p><li><strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://twitter.com/VeganLunchTruck">Cinnamon Snail</a></strong>: Another <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/http://gothamist.com/2012/10/09/cinnamon_snail.php">awesome vegan spot</a> giving away free grub, swing by the truck in midtown today for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10151613279784367&amp;l=30a51ff296">one free donut per person</a>. <em>(Park Avenue between 46th and 47th)</em></li><p></p>

<p></p><li><strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://twitter.com/doughbrooklyn">Dough</a></strong>: We expected better from this Bed-Stuy bakery, who are only offering a <em>chance</em> to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://twitter.com/doughbrooklyn/status/342944135243759616">win four doughnuts a week</a> for a year. Not even a free doughnut hole? Sigh. <em>(448 Lafayette Avenue, Bedford-Stuyvesant)</em></li><p></p>

<p></p><li><strong>Dunkin Donuts</strong>: In addition to releasing their new <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/03/behold_the_dunkin_donuts_glazed_don.php">Glazed Donut Breakfast Monstrosity</a> today, the chain is also offering a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://www.dunkindonuts.com/dunkindonuts/en.html">free donut with the purchase of any beverage</a>. <em>(<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/http://www.dunkindonuts.com/content/dunkindonuts/en/stores.html">Multiple Locations</a>)</em></li><p></p>

<p></p><li><strong>Krispy Kreme</strong>: You don&apos;t even have to buy a coffee to get a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://www.facebook.com/events/565447133478485/">free doughnut of any variety</a> from the glaze-heavy shop. There&apos;s only <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/http://locations.krispykreme.com/location/new-york">one outpost in Penn Station</a>, but rumor has it they&apos;re giving out Hot Fresh glazed doughnuts outside the Fox Building on 48th and 6th. You might also catch <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://twitter.com/ClaytonMorris/status/342981228389097475">an Eddie Money performance</a>. <em>(2 Penn Plaza-Amtrak Level; truck on 48th and 6th Avenue)</em></li><p></p>

<p></p><li><strong>Tim Hortons</strong>: If you like &quot;Canadian&quot; doughnuts, swing by one of the chain&apos;s locations for a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=574926655885058&amp;set=a.278058075571919.72924.235359349841792&amp;type=1&amp;theater">free donut with any purchase</a>. <em>(<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103323/http://www.timhortons.com/us/en/about/nyc.html">Multiple Locations</a>)</em></li><br>
</ul><p></p>
