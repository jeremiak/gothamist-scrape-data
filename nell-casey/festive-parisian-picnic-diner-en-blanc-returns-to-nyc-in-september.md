					<p>The white-out Parisian pop-up picnic <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021133203/http://newyork.dinerenblanc.info/">Diner en Blanc</a> returns to NYC next month&#x2014;but where it ends up, nobody knows (until moments before it&apos;s scheduled to start). The annual fete unfolds on September 15th at an as-yet-undisclosed public space somewhere in the five boroughs. Will this be the year they <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021133203/http://gothamist.com/2014/08/18/diner_en_blanc_may_one_day_go_beyon.php">finally head to Brooklyn</a>? </p>

<p>Last year, a record 5,000 picnickers and francophiles <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021133203/http://gothamist.com/2015/07/29/diner_en_blanc_2016.php#photo-1">congregated in Tribeca&apos;s Hudson River Park</a>, bringing their finest <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021133203/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/19/photos_thousands_of_white-clad_dine.php#photo-36">Eiffel Tower hats</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021133203/http://gothamist.com/2015/07/29/diner_en_blanc_2016.php#photo-21">elaborate centerpieces</a>, one of which contained <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021133203/http://gothamist.com/2015/07/29/diner_en_blanc_2016.php#photo-19">two live (white!) birds</a>. Anything goes&#x2014;even alcohol, so long as it&apos;s either wine or champagne&#x2014;and as long as it&apos;s white. See the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021133203/http://newyork.dinerenblanc.info/about">attendance guidelines here</a>.</p>

<p>Celebrity chef Todd English is once again on board curating special dinners of some kind, while wines are sponsored by Apothic and Mo&#xEB;t &amp; Chandon. If you&apos;ve attended a previous iteration, you&apos;ll be invited to join the registration process today, August 22nd, at noon. If you&apos;ve been referred by a past attendee, registration opens at 9 a.m. on August 25th. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021133203/http://newyork.dinerenblanc.info/register">Phase 3 registration</a>, for the plebs who are totally new, <strike>has not yet been announced</strike> opens Tuesday, August 30 at 9 a.m. Break out the bleach while you&apos;re waiting.</p>
