<p>The restaurant at &quot;gastrohall&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://www.unionfare.com/">Union Fare</a> launches brunch this weekend, following in the spirit of their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/https://www.instagram.com/p/BJaV8OhD1Ug/?taken-by=unionfare">insane croissants</a>. The star of the new menu: The Ultimate Breakfast Sandwich, a gargantuan mess of apple sausage, egg and cheese on a sliced cinnamon roll with a bacon glaze. If you&apos;re not ready to start taking a statin just yet, there is also a Grain Bowl with fried eggs...and a Breakfast mac n Cheese with bacon and sausage. Balance!</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="090916sangriapop.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_nellcasey/090916sangriapop.jpg" width="640" height="480"> <br> <i> (Fruta POP)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>The newish <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/https://www.facebook.com/events/384346708402228/">Bulletin Market</a> on Driggs Avenue in Williamsburg has been a festive addition to the neighborhood&apos;s seasonal outdoor markets, with a full booze and food lineup plus indie designers and sometimes a slip and slide. This weekend, swing by for one of the last sweltering weekends for boozy ice pops by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://www.frutapop.com/">Fruta POP</a>, a local company making all-natural flavor ice. They&apos;ll be offering a few flavors, including fruit-based Altaneve Prosecco Pomegranate Punch and Mimosa ($6) and pick-me-up flavors Cold Brew Coffee and Latte ($4). </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="90916tastetalks.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_nellcasey/90916tastetalks.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> (Taste Talks)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>All weekend long, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://www.brooklyn.tastetalks.com/">Taste Talks</a> series offers chef-driven meals, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://www.brooklyn.tastetalks.com/eventstt/2016/4/3/conference">panels</a> on all things food-related, and a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://www.brooklyn.tastetalks.com/eventstt/2016/7/27/all-star-bbq-schedule">great big bbq</a> on the Williamsburg waterfront. Local restaurants are joining forces for collaborative bbq dishes, like Emily X Hometown BBQ making a grilled pizza with pulled pork, and Greenpoint Fish &amp; Lobster pairing with Blue Point Brewing for grilled oysters with seaweed butter and Prop Stopper Seaweed IPA. Tickets for the bbq are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://www.brooklyn.tastetalks.com/tickets">$49</a>. </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="090916waffle.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_nellcasey/090916waffle.jpg" width="640" height="600"> <br> <i> (CoolMess)</i></div> <p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://www.coolmess.com/">CoolMess</a>, a brand-new ice cream parlor where patrons make their own ice cream, has collaborated with Instagram stars the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/https://www.instagram.com/brunchboys/">Brunch Boys</a> to create a brunch-time ice cream sundae that would have given your 12-year-old incarnation a serious sugar high. The creation features Belgian waffle pieces, vanilla ice cream, hot fudge and a handful of Cap&apos;n Crunch cereal and chocolate-covered espresso beans. </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="072215_organic.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_rebeccaf/072215_organic.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> Organic Grill via Yelp</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Brooklyn&apos;s &quot;First Veggie Burger Competition&quot; rolls into <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://ourwickedlady.com/">Our Wicked Lady</a> on Sunday, tasking participants with creating the best meat-free veggie sliders in the borough. Stop by to sample the contenders yourself or chill on the bar&apos;s roof deck. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://www.brownpapertickets.com/event/2591940">Tickets are $15</a> and the event runs from 2 - 4 p.m. Will any of the burgers <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170311020243/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/26/david_chang_bloody_veg_burger.php">bleed</a>? </p>
