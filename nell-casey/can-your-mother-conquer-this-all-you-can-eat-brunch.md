<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813002218/http://www.citymeals.org/">Citymeals on Wheels</a>, which delivers meals to home-bound, elderly New Yorkers, has launched a <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813002218/http://www.citymeals.org/events-and-news/%252Fbrooklyn-brunches">Brooklyn Brunches</a></strong> program to raise funds for their endeavors. From now through the end of the month, dine at one of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813002218/http://www.citymeals.org/events-and-news/%252Fbrooklyn-brunches">participating Brooklyn restaurants</a>, each of whom offer either a monetary donation on a specific dish or the option to leave a customized donation. Jack The Horse, Peaches, Speedy Romeo, Colonie and Llama Inn are a few of the restaurants involved. </p>

<p>For Mother&apos;s Day, Williamsburg&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813002218/http://humboldtandjackson.com/">Humboldt &amp; Jackson</a> is doing an <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813002218/http://humboldtandjackson.com/hj-event/mothers-day-brunch-by-chef-j-jackson/">all-you-can-eat buffet brunch</a></strong> prepared by guest <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813002218/http://www.chefjjackson.com/#!about/c42f">Chef J. Jackson</a>. On the buffet line: build-your-own parfait, soft scrambled eggs with scallions, milk bread toast with honey butter and the red velvet cinnamon rolls pictured above, among other items. The brunch is $15.99 per person. The space is also hosting a <strong>Kentucky Derby viewing party</strong> on Saturday with free local cheeses and charcuterie to guests who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813002218/http://humboldtandjackson.com/hj-event/kentucky-derby-viewing-party-2/">RSVP in advance</a>.</p>

<p>If <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813002218/http://juelanclub.com/menus/dim-sum-brunch/">bottomless brunch</a></strong> is more your style, check out <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813002218/http://juelanclub.com/">Jue Lan Club&apos;s</a> inaugural offering. The deal includes three courses for $34.95, beginning with an appetizer, entree (Roasted Pork Egg Foo Young or Crunchy Vegetable Rice, for example) and dessert. Also included, unlimited bloody marys, lychee bellinis, mimosas or screwdrivers. </p>
