										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="040913FREECONEDAY.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430232007im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_nellcasey/040913FREECONEDAY.jpg" width="250" height="262" class="image-left"> </span>It&apos;s that <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430232007/http://gothamist.com/2012/04/02/free_food_alert_ben_jerrys_free_con.php">time of year again</a> when frozen milk lovers are rewarded with a gratis scoop of Ben &amp; Jerry&apos;s ice cream. Today is the chain&apos;s annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430232007/http://www.benjerry.com/">Free Cone Day</a>, where <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430232007/http://www.benjerry.com/scoop-shops/scoop-shop-locator">participating locations</a> will be dishing out free scoops of ice cream, frozen yogurt and sorbet from noon until 8 p.m. Yes, the lines can be outrageous, but given today&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430232007/http://gothamist.com/2013/04/08/spring_today_and_a_hint_of_summer_t.php">glorious forecast</a> there are definitely worse ways to kill your lunch break. </p>

<p>All of the wacky chain&apos;s longtime favorite flavors will be on offer, including Cherry Garcia and Chunky Monkey. But maybe now is the time to stock up on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430232007/http://gothamist.com/2011/03/03/jimmy_fallon_immortalized_in_ben_je.php#photo-3">Late Night Snack</a> now that <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430232007/http://gothamist.com/2013/04/03/nbc_will_announce_jimmy_fallon_as_t.php">Jimmy Fallon is moving on</a>. </p>
