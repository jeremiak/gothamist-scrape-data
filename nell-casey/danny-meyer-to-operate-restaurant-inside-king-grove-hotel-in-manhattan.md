<p>Does Danny Meyer sleep? Between opening a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504172412/http://gothamist.com/2013/10/01/shake_shack_opens_in_grand_central.php">new Shake Shack</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504172412/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/14/shake_shack_coming_to_dumbo_next_ye.php">every other week</a> while simultaneously <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504172412/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/09/four_words_shake_shack_cronut_concr.php">collaborating with the Cronut king</a>, it&apos;s a wonder the man can stay upright let alone <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504172412/http://www.ushgnyc.com/news/archives/press-releases/ushg-announcement-4/">open yet another restaurant</a>, which is what his Union Square Hospitality Group announced this morning. The <strike>possible vampire</strike> busy restaurateur is slated to operate the new restaurant inside <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504172412/http://www.kingandgrove.com/nyc-hotels/hotel-new-york/gallery">King &amp; Grove New York</a>, the Manhattan version of the trendy Williamsburg hotel that houses <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504172412/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/18/paul_liebrandt_creates_artistic_fre.php#photo-1">Paul Liebrandt&apos;s new restaurant The Elm</a>. </p>

<p>Meyer tapped <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504172412/http://gothamist.com/2009/11/10/maialino_danny_meyers_italian_eater.php#photo-1">Maialino</a> Executive Chef Nick Anderer to helm the new food and beverage program, which includes a ground floor restaurant&#x2014;serving Italian cuisine from breakfast through late night&#x2014;as well as the hotel bar and other food options. Terry Coughlin, partner and Director of Operations at Maialino, joins Anderer as front of house manager; the duo will also continue to operate Maialino inside the Gramercy Park Hotel. </p>
