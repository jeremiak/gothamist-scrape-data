<p>And <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://gothamist.com/2015/09/18/urbanspace_vanderbilt_food_hall.php#photo-1">YOU</a> get a food hall, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://gothamist.com/2016/01/11/pennsy_penn_station_msg_eats.php#photo-1">YOU</a> get a food hall, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/11/gansevoort_market_20.php#photo-1">YOU</a> get a food hall. And now Chinatown gets a food hall. It&apos;s the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://gothamist.com/2015/11/10/guide_to_nyc_food_halls.php">food hall&apos;s city</a> and we&apos;re just eating in it. </p>

<p>In November, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://www.canalstreet.market/">Canal Street Market</a> arrives at 265 Canal Street, with 12,000-square-feet of both food, beverage and retail components. The full lineup of over 11 vendors hasn&apos;t been announced, but an early release names <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://www.daveysicecream.com/">Davey&apos;s Ice Cream</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://www.bobaguys.com/">Boba Guy&apos;s</a> (a bubble tea operation), and Yori Nori, a new food offering from market and mobile vets <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://mokbar.com/">Mokbar</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://korillabbq.com/">Korilla</a>. A hybrid newsstand and coffee shop will also be &quot;curated&quot; by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://officemagazine.net/">Office Magazine</a>.</p>

<p>The new &quot;lifestyle experience&quot;&#x2014;founded by real estate developer Philip Chong, who&apos;s developed <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015230423/http://therealdeal.com/issues_articles/a-different-delancey/">other downtown properties</a>&#x2014;promises 27 &quot;artist and brand booths,&quot; a common seating area and an indoor garden. </p>
