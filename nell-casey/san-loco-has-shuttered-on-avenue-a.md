<p>Reliable late-night Mexican munchies mecca <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150419233818/http://www.sanloco.com/">San Loco</a> has served its last Burrito Grande on Avenue A. The &quot;Gringo-Mex&quot; joint <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150419233818/http://evgrieve.com/2014/02/san-loco-on-avenue-has-closed-after-15.html">closed its doors</a> after 15 years, reports EV Grieve; the location on 2nd Avenue, as well as the LES and Williamsburg outposts, are still in operation. </p>
