</div></div>					<p>Flushing&apos;s gigantic 700,000 square foot shopping mall <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140424125040/http://www.shopskyviewcenter.com/">SkyView Center</a> has skewed towards large American brands like Target, Applebee&apos;s and Bed, Bath &amp; Beyond and now they&apos;ve welcomed Mongolian restaurant <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140424125040/http://littlelambusa.com/">Little Lamb</a>, an international restaurant that operates nearly 600 locations throughout the world. It&apos;s the second location to open in the States&#x2014;the first <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140424125040/http://boston.eater.com/tags/xiao-wei-yang-company">opened in Boston</a> last year&#x2014;and will be the flagship location in this country.</p>

<p>The restaurant specializes in hot-pot dishes, where vegetables and meats are simmered at the table in steaming pots of savory liquids like Strengthen Tomato Broth, Mongolian Spicy Herbal and Special Hot &amp; Spicy. They&apos;ll offer proteins like sliced rib eye, sliced lamb and sliced beef tongue and dozens of vegetables including baby bok choy, spinach and Chinese broccoli. Besides hot-pot, the restaurant also offers Mongolian barbecue dishes like lamb chops, ribs and kebabs, plus different types of noodles, dumplings and pastries</p>

<p><em>The Shops at SkyView Center, 40-24 College Point Boulevard, Level B, (718) 359-1668</em></p>
