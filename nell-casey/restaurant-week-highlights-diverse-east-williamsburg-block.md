<p>While the city&apos;s enormous <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420043654/http://www.nycgo.com/restaurantweek/">Restaurant Week</a> celebration rages, a more humble version unfolds along a stretch of East Williamsburg. The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420043654/http://grandstbklyn.com/event/grand-street-restaurant-week-february-17-23/">Grand Street Restaurant Week</a> begins today, offering up dining deals on the street&apos;s diverse collection of mom-and-pop restaurants. Spanning Grand between Bushwick and Union Avenues, the mini-festival highlights a variety of ethnic cuisines on the portion of the street east of the BQE.</p>

<p>The 14 participating restaurants are all offering $25, three-course menus during dinnertime, with about half also offering $10 two-course lunches. On the docket are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420043654/http://grandstbklyn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Curry-Dinner-Menu.pdf">Indian delights</a> from Curry Heaven, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420043654/http://grandstbklyn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Restaurant-Week-Menu-2014-1.pdf">El Salvadorian fare</a> from Bahia, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420043654/http://grandstbklyn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Mahzen-Grill-Dinner-Menu.pdf">Turkish specialties</a> from Mahzen Grill and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420043654/http://grandstbklyn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Lily-Thai-Dinner-Menu.pdf">Thai treats</a> from Lily Thai. Killer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420043654/http://gothamist.com/2014/02/12/best_all_night_restaurants.php">24-hour eatery</a> Tacos Morelos also makes the list, offering up choices like Molajete and Chile Relleno entrees plus two beers included with every meal during dinner. </p>

<p>Take a peek at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420043654/http://grandstbklyn.com/event/grand-street-restaurant-week-february-17-23/">all the menus</a> at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420043654/http://grandstbklyn.com/">Grand Street BID&apos;s</a> website. This is the first ever restaurant week for the street; the deals start today and run through Sunday, February 23rd.</p>
