					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616014408/http://turn-style.com/">TurnStyle</a>, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616014408/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/19/turnstyle_boutique_columbus.php#photo-1">shopping and dining venue</a> that opened last spring in a subway station passageway at Columbus Circle, has changed up its vendor lineup to include some incendiary new tenants.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616014408/http://championpizzanyc.com/">Champion Pizza</a>, which slings pies in Nolita, has brought its signature square &quot;Grandma&quot; pies to Midtown. Champion&apos;s packed pies come in flavors like Chicken BBQ, Mac n Cheese and Chicken Bacon Ranch, but the showmanship belongs to owner Hakki Akdeniz, who handles pies that are <em>literally</em> on fire. </p>

<p>Venezuelan restaurant <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616014408/http://www.arepafactory.com/">Arepa Factory</a> has also <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616014408/http://gothamist.com/2015/11/02/arepa_factory_east_village.php">expanded from downtown</a> to set up shop at TurnStyle. They&apos;ll have a DIY arepa option with various meat and vegetarian fillings, while also serving their signature arepas, including Pabellon with shredded beef, black beans, sweet plantains and guyanese cheese; and the Verde Fresca vegan option made with a chia arepa stuffed with avocado, tomato, arugula and roasted peppers.</p>

<p>Two new purveyors of sweet treats will also be debuting in the coming weeks. Panda Bubble Tea will offer milk- and juice-based teas with a choice of fillings like traditional tapioca balls. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616014408/http://16handles.com/">16 Handles</a> will also be opening in the space offering their signature mix-and-match, self-serve froyo and toppings model.</p>

<p>Though they&apos;re not brand new to the space, vegan spot <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616014408/http://blossomdujour.com/">Blossom du Jour</a> and crepe eatery <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170616014408/http://by-suzette.com/">By Suzette</a> have joined forces for a collaboration item that&apos;s available now at the By Suzette stall. They&apos;ll be doing a vegan mac n cheese crepe topped with a creamy vegan cheese sauce. So far as we know, no pyrotechnics were involved in the process. </p>
