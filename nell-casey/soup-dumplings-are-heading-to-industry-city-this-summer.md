<p>The food scene at Industry City: <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/http://gothamist.com/2015/09/27/industry_city_food_hall.php#photo-1">so hot right now</a>. Earlier this week, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/http://gothamist.com/2017/04/03/avocaderia_avocado_restaurant_brook.php">&quot;world&apos;s first&quot; avocado bar</a> opened in the food hub&#x2014;promptly <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/http://www.extracrispy.com/food/2547/brooklyns-avocado-bar-ran-out-of-avocados-in-three-hours">selling out</a> of their wares&#x2014;and now Gothamist has learned that popular Downtown Brooklyn dumpling spot <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/http://yasotangbao.com/">Yaso Tangbao</a> will be opening an outpost there this summer. </p>

<p>The Shanghainese eatery will occupy a 4,500-square-foot lot that will accommodate both a commercial kitchen for dumpling-making, as well as a seating area for eating on-site. They&apos;ll be serving the same <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/http://yasotangbao.com/menu.html">menu</a> they currently offer in Downtown Brooklyn, which includes several different types of soup dumplings, soups and noodles. </p>

<p>Yaso Tangbao <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/http://gothamist.com/2015/08/25/soup_dumplings_dobro.php">first opened in Downtown Brooklyn</a> in 2015 and recently announced plans to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/https://commercialobserver.com/2017/04/yaso-tangbao-lease-220-east-42nd-street-sl-green-realty-corp/">expand to Midtown East</a>. Prepare your body by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/31/best_soup_dumplings_nyc.php">revisiting the city&apos;s best soup dumplings</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/31/how_to_eat_a_xiao_long_bao.php">prepping your skillz</a> so you don&apos;t <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170420110210/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/30/soup_dumpling_fail.php">make a fool of yourself</a>.</p>
