<p>Urban sprite and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091241/http://gothamist.com/2015/12/10/bill_murray_life_cult.php">spiritual cult leader</a> Bill Murray <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091241/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/05/city_continues_to_be_enraptured_by.php#photo-1">regularly</a> appears in unexpected places. You could be singing some karaoke and all of a sudden <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091241/http://gothamist.com/2011/01/05/why_doesnt_bill_murray_ever_just_dr.php">BOOM Bill Murray</a>. Or eating sushi in Williamsburg when he <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091241/http://gothamist.com/2013/12/19/bill_murray_spotted_through_perfect.php">manifests outside the window</a>. This weekend, he&apos;ll be tending bar at a forthcoming Greenpoint bar&#x2014;but sorry, this scheduled appearance is invite-only.</p>

<p>Murray&apos;s son Homer is opening up a new restaurant and bar, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091241/http://www.21greenpoint.com/">21 Greenpoint</a>, in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091241/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/25/best_nachos_nyc_2016.php">former River Styx space</a> and tapped his dad to pour drinks at a pre-opening celebration Friday and Saturday nights. It seems the signals got crossed, however, when Homer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091241/http://ny.eater.com/2016/9/14/12921600/homer-murray-21-greenpoint-bill-murray">told Eater</a> that everyone was welcome.</p>

<p>&quot;People can walk in; just be nice,&quot; he told the website. &quot;What my dad lacks in experience, he makes up for in tequila.&apos;&apos;</p>

<p>Hold up: turns out, that&apos;s not true. Reps for the restaurant tells us this is a &quot;closed, by-invite only event with Bill Murray, and it is not open to the public. <strong>The public will not be able to get in on Friday and Saturday.</strong>&quot; </p>

<p>Sorry folks, looks like you&apos;ll have to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091241/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/24/have_you_run_into_bill_murray_yet.php">try your luck in the wild</a> or eat every possible meal at the new restaurant&#x2014;which opens to the public September 21&#x2014;until he finally appears. It shouldn&apos;t <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091241/http://gothamist.com/2014/05/27/bill_murray_crashes_bachelor.php">take too long</a>.</p>
