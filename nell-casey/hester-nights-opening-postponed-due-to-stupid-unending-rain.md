title: Hester Nights Opening Postponed Due To Stupid, Unending Rain
---

<p>Sadly, we must report that tonight&apos;s debut of Hester Nights&#x2014;the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120528150934/http://gothamist.com/2012/05/15/the_hester_street_fair_brings_twili.php">twilight food festival</a> brought to you by the Hester Street fair folks&#x2014;has been postponed due to the unending rain that&apos;s been falling off and on since <em>forever</em>. Tonight&apos;s debut would have brought gourmet food from vendors like Luke&apos;s Lobster and Pies &apos;n&apos; Thighs to the Hotel Eventi Courtyard on Sixth Avenue, but now we&apos;ll have to wait until next Thursday, May 31th to nibble under the stars when the festival does their grand opening, take two. </p>
