<p>A retrofitted VW Bus will be rolling through Bushwick tonight on a three bar cocktail crawl complete with dancing, costumes and twee photos. The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161025133616/https://www.facebook.com/nyphotobus/">NYPhotoBus</a>, a company that transforms cars into mobile photo booths, has organized the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161025133616/https://www.eventbrite.com/e/nyphotobus-presents-the-best-of-bushwick-cocktail-tour-tickets-27053401452?aff=efbeventtix">Best of Bushwick Cocktail Tour</a>, a dance party slash scrapbook filler slash fundraiser.</p>

<p>Even if you aren&apos;t formally participating, the crew&apos;s gonna be hard to miss. The bus&apos;ll be blasting some house music between each bar stop, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161025133616/http://bushwickdaily.com/bushwick/categories/arts-and-culture/4102-vw-bus-converted-into-a-photobooth-cocktail-tour-of-bushwick">according to Bushwick Daily</a>, and participants are encouraged to wear costumes and dance alongside the bus as it makes its way between stops. </p>

<p>&quot;It&apos;s about encouraging and sharing the different ways people express themselves,&quot; NYPhotoBus owner Madison Reyes explained. When it&apos;s not rolling through the streets to Brooklyn, Melo&#x2014;the 1964 VW Bus that <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161025133616/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B6TcvFpGtlA">took eight years to restore</a>&#x2014;pops up at weddings and special events.</p>

Should you want to join along in tonight&apos;s Bar Crawl Of Self Expression, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161025133616/https://www.eventbrite.com/e/nyphotobus-presents-the-best-of-bushwick-cocktail-tour-tickets-27053401452?aff=efbeventtix">grab a $40 ticket</a> and head to House of Yes, where the tour kicks off at 7 p.m. From there, the bus rumbles over to Central Station, before reaching its conclusion at Forrest Point. A cocktail sample from each bar is included in the ticket price, as are photos from inside the bus.
<br><br>
The fundraising angle to the evening benefits <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161025133616/http://www.rippleeffectartists.com/">Ripple Effect Artists</a>, an organization that uses theatre and film to bring about awareness and social change on issues like human trafficking, the group&apos;s main focus for this year.
