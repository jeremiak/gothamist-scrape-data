The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161207143536/http://gothamist.com/tags/cronut">Pastry That Launched A Thousand Knockoffs</a> apparently doesn&apos;t pack enough sugar power to make it the country&apos;s most Instagrammed eating spot. That distinction belongs to Sugar Factory, a Meatpacking District &quot;restaurant&quot; known for its goblets of candy-topped sugar water. Sometimes, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161207143536/https://www.instagram.com/p/4DVHLSI5eJ/?taken-at=214748265&amp;hl=en">fire</a> is involved, too.
<br><br>
According to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161207143536/http://www.cnn.com/2016/12/01/travel/instagram-year-in-review-2016/index.html">data published by CNN</a>, NYC boasts six of the 10 top Instagrammed dining establishments, including TAO Downtown in the #3 slot, Katz&apos;s at #4 and the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161207143536/http://gothamist.com/2016/01/27/black_tap_milkshake_insanity.php">milkshake pushers</a> at Black Tap rounding out the top five, proving that people doing the most prolific Instagramming are probably tourists.

Expected tags like <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161207143536/https://www.instagram.com/explore/tags/pizza/">#pizza</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161207143536/https://www.instagram.com/explore/tags/sushi/">#sushi</a> complete the top three.
