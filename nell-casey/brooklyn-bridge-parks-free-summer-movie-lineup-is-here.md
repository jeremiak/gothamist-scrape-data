<p>Summer may be a few months away but it&apos;s never too soon to start penciling in all the free events you plan on taking advantage of this season. To that end, the Brooklyn Bridge Park Conservancy just announced the lineup for their annual free film series <em>Syfy Movies With A View</em> this morning, which kicks off July 11 with <em>Ferris Bueller&apos;s Day Off</em>. The 14th season, which is one again sponsored by the Syfy channel, will run the free films every Thursday at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506170405/http://www.brooklynbridgepark.org/the-park/pier-1">Pier 1</a> starting at dusk, with DJs spinning beforehand beginning at 6 p.m.</p>

<p>This year&apos;s theme is &quot;With Love From...&quot; other cities of the world that<em> aren&apos;t</em> New York. (Heh, what do THEY know about love?) Here&apos;s the full schedule, which includes&#x2014;as is tradition&#x2014;a fan vote for the final film:</p>
