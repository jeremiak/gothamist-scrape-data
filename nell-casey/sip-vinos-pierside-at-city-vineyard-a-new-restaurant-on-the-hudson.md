					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160006/https://www.citywinery.com/">City Winery</a> has expanded its Tribeca footprint with a new sister restaurant on the water at Pier 26. The intimate venue has opened <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160006/http://www.cityvineyardnyc.com/">City Vineyard</a>, bringing its wines and a small menu of accompanying snacks out to the Hudson River.</p>

<p>The breezy spot boasts some great views across the water into Jersey and some interior views that prominently feature 1 WTC. The bi-level space has plenty of seating outside, but the all-season restaurant also has an interior dining room and bar when temps cool off. Pergolas draped with grape vines support the wine-making motif. </p>

<p>Tap wines produced by their in-house winery are served alongside a curated list of the venue&apos;s favorite global wineries. They&apos;re also serving beer and spirits.</p>

<p>Unlike the full dinner menu served in the venue, food at the Vineyard is meant for snacking while drinking. There are cured meat boards and cheese plates, a few salads, and some homemade dips including a romesco version served with raw veggies and pita bread. </p>

<p><em>233 West Street at Pier 26, (646) 677-8350; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017160006/http://www.cityvineyardnyc.com/">website</a></em></p>
