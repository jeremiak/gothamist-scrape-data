					<p>Bi-annual outdoor market <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://urbanspacenyc.com/mad-sq-eats/">Mad. Sq. Eats</a> returns for its second encampment of General Worth Square inside Madison Square Park this week. With 90 degree days <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/12/one_last_gasp_of_summer_heat_on_tap.php">likely behind us</a>, it&apos;s time to actually enjoy being outdoors again until we&apos;re <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/02/a_mound_that_an_old_man_knows_good.php#photo-1">entombed in ice shelters</a> for several months. </p>

<p>The impressive roster of vendors includes some new faces like <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://www.mr-bing.com/">Mr Bing Beijing Street Food</a>, which <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/23/mr_bing_pop-up.php">popped up on the Bowery</a> serving savory Chinese crepes and has been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/28/vendy_awards_2016.php">nominated for a Vendy Award</a> in the Rookie of the Year category. They&apos;re joined by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://www.amalimounyc.com/">Amali Mou</a>, a Greek eatery that originated at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://gothamist.com/2015/09/18/urbanspace_vanderbilt_food_hall.php#photo-1">Urbanspace Vanderbilt</a> last year.</p>

<p>Popular stands from previews incarnations have included the frozen confections from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://iceandvice.com/">Ice &amp; Vice</a>, who are doing Instagram-friendly Strawberry &amp; Cream Ice Cream Floats along with scoops of their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://gothamist.com/2015/06/08/ice_vice_lower_east_side.php#photo-1">fun flavors</a>. With poke <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://gothamist.com/2016/01/28/poke_explainer_guide_nyc.php">surging in popularity</a>, expect a queue at Gotham Poke, too. </p>

<p>The Urbanspace <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021125146/http://urbanspacenyc.com/mad-sq-eats/">Mad. Sq. Eats</a> is located at the plaza between 24th and 25th Streets, where Fifth Avenue and Broadway intersect, and is open daily from 11:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. through October 7.</p>
