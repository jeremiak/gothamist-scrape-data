<p>It&apos;s usually Smorgasburg doing the heavy lifting in the food department, but sister operation <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://brooklynflea.com/">Brooklyn Flea</a> offers some destination-worthy dining of its own with events like their annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/17/what_to_eat_at_todays_ice_cream_day.php#photo-1">Ice Cream Bonanza</a>. Now they&apos;re gearing up for a BBQ Weekend dedicated to all things meaty&#x2014;and a few non-meats, too. This Saturday at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://brooklynflea.com/markets/fort-greene/">Fort Greene Flea</a> and Sunday at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://brooklynflea.com/markets/dumbo-2/">DUMBO Flea</a>, eight barbecue purveyors will be slinging &apos;cue amidst the vintage collectibles, offering an <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/27/regional_bbq_styles_in_nyc.php#photo-4">around-the-country</a> array of delectable smoked foods.</p>

<p>Names like <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://www.mightyquinnsbbq.com/">Mighty Quinn&apos;s</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://www.lonestarempirenyc.com/">Lonestar Empire</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://www.kimchismoke.com/">Kimchi Smoke</a> are familiar to Smorg regulars, as is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://www.monksmeats.com/">Monk&apos;s Meats</a>, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://gothamist.com/2016/08/09/vegan_bbq_nyc.php">rising vegan barbecue</a> outlet, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://www.chickpeaandolive.com/">Chickpea &amp; Olive</a>, the vegan sandwich makers who are rocking the jackfruit in place of animal protein. </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="081616chickpea.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_nellcasey/081616chickpea.jpg" width="640" height="480"> <br> <i> Chickpea &amp; Olive</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Brisket specialists <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://www.delaneybbq.com/">Delaney Barbecue</a> will also be making an appearance at the event and they&apos;ll even be offering a hickory smoked seitan, in keeping up with the vegan trend. Crown Heights newcomer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://www.urbansmokenyc.com/">Urban Smoke</a> will also be on hand, serving smoked chicken thighs and pimento cheese, as will Bed-Stuy&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161014093247/http://beastofbourbonbk.com/">Beast of Bourbon</a>.</p>

<p>BBQ Weekend at the Brooklyn Flea runs from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Saturday at the Fort Greene Flea (176 Lafayette Ave.) and 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Sunday at the DUMBO Flea (Manhattan Bridge Archway Plaza). Check out the full vendor menu below. </p>
