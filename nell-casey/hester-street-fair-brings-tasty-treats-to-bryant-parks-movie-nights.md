<p>There&apos;s more than just cool movies to look forward to at Bryant Park&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820200425/http://www.bryantpark.org/plan-your-visit/filmfestival.html">HBO Summer Film Series</a> this year. <a href="www.hesterstreetfair.com">Hester Street Fair</a> has lined up a rotating group of vendors to set up shop at the popular outdoor film screenings, selling snacks and confections to nosh on during the show. Though we&apos;re fond of packing our own picnic&#x2014;better to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820200425/http://gothamist.com/2012/08/09/behold_the_brand_loophole_that_stym.php">hide adult beverages</a>&#x2014;this is a great option for a last-minute decision to catch a screening under the stars.</p>

<p>Though the exact list will change week-to-week, expect vendors like Oaxaca Taqueria, Minnie&apos;s Bake Shop, Arancini Bros and Ludlow Pizza. Kobayashi&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820200425/http://www.rastellidirect.com/kobidog/">Kobi Dog</a> and wichcraft burgers serve up traditional picnic fare while Two Teaspoons brings along eats for the veggie set. Artistic Taste, a catering company by Todd English Food Hall Chef Andr&#xE9;, brings a new menu of elegant dishes each week, including macaroni and cheese with black summer truffles, crab cakes and Kobe beef empanadas.</p>

<p>Vendors begin selling at 4 p.m. so you can arrive early to dine and then repair to the lawn when it opens at 5 p.m. This coming Monday the park screens the 1964 thriller <em>Hush...Hush, Sweet Charlotte</em>, starring screen legends Bette Davis and Olivia de Havilland. Upcoming films include The African Queen, Norma Rae and E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial. BYO Reese&apos;s Pieces. </p>
