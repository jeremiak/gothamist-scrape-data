<p>Science has <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170322182744/http://gothamist.com/tags/alcohol">tried</a>, but there is still no cure for Mondays. The symptoms can be treated, however, and today Dairy Queen attempts to alleviate feelings of fatigue and despair with some free soft serve.</p>

<p>The chain <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170322182744/http://gothamist.com/2015/03/16/free_dq_woo_hoo.php">annually</a> celebrates the first day of spring with free small vanilla soft serve cones through this evening. </p>
