</div></div>					<p>The list of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425193656/http://gothamist.com/tags/rooftopbars">skyline drinking</a> establishments continues to grow with the opening of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425193656/http://theroofny.com/">The Roof</a> tomorrow inside the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425193656/http://www.viceroyhotelsandresorts.com/en/newyork">Viceroy Hotel</a> just south of Central Park. And that&apos;s just what you&apos;ll be looking at when you&apos;re up there, in addition to neighboring buildings and points north. It may not be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425193656/http://gothamist.com/2014/03/03/bar_54.php#photo-1">the highest</a>, but the 1,200 square foot outdoor terrace&#x2014;which opens daily at 3 p.m. and stays open until 2 a.m. Thursdays through Saturdays&#x2014;affords lovely views all the way to the top of the park even when the sun sets. </p>

<p>The equally large interior bar area should &quot;evoke the aesthetic of a luxury yacht,&quot; according to the press release. What does that mean? &quot;Ipe floors, brass details, custom walnut-and-leather sofas and thoughtfully curated images of sky, sea and clouds by famed photographer Dalton Portella.&quot; It&apos;s like a millionaire&apos;s Man Cave with billion dollar views.</p>

<p>Name-checking yachts implies pricey booze but the cocktails ($13 - $16) are standard for the genre. Drinks like a GG Manhattan, Sider F1 and Spicy Palmoa are mixed using small batch spirits and fresh ingredients; beers are local, wines international. Marc Murphey&apos;s Kingside restaurant, which <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425193656/http://gothamist.com/2013/11/01/marc_murphy_opens_kingside_inside_t.php#photo-1">opened inside the hotel</a> last fall, provides bar bites like a Burger with house-made American cheese ($20), a Grilled 3 Cheese sandwich ($14) and Chickpea Falafel with pomegranate yogurt ($16). </p>

<p><em>124 West 57th Street, 212-707-8008; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425193656/http://theroofny.com/">theroofny.com</a>. The lounge operates daily from 3 p.m. to 4 a.m.; the terrace opens daily at 3 p.m. and closes at 1 a.m. Sunday through Wednesday and 2 a.m. Thursday through Saturday</em></p>
