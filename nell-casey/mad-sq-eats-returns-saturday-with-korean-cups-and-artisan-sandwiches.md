<p>Bi-annual outdoor market <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170615192052/http://urbanspacenyc.com/mad-sq-eats/">Mad. Sq. Eats</a> reopens Saturday, May 13th, operating daily from 11 a.m. until 9 p.m. through June 9th. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170615192052/http://urbanspacenyc.com/">Urbanspace</a>, the company behind lots of these outdoor and indoor food experiences, curated 25 vendors for the Worth Square encampment, which is a little pedestrian plaza next to Madison Square Park.</p>

<p>There are a few newcomers for this round. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170615192052/http://makesandwich.com/">Make Sandwich</a> will offer artisan sandwiches including a spinach and artichoke with cheesy cream sauce on a Pain D&apos;Avignon baguette and sides like pickled cucumbers and cookies. Popular Korean food purveyors <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170615192052/http://korillabbq.com/">Korilla</a> have a new &quot;cup&quot; concept called kBBQ that they&apos;re bringing to the market. Finally, hot dogs by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170615192052/https://www.facebook.com/Coney-Shack-589987747726730/">Coney Shack</a>.</p>

<p>There are many familiar faces rounding out the vendor list, from stalwarts like <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170615192052/http://www.robertaspizza.com/">Roberta&apos;s</a> to nearby businesses including <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170615192052/http://hillcountry.com/nyc/">Hill Country Barbecue Market</a>. Read more about the full lineup <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170615192052/http://urbanspacenyc.com/mad-sq-eats/">here</a>. </p>

<p><em>The Urbanspace Mad. Sq. Eats is located at the plaza between 24th and 25th Streets, where Fifth Avenue and Broadway intersect, and is open daily from 11:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. through June 9th.</em></p>
