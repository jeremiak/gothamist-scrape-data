	<p>Two new Italian eateries are opening in a space inside Macy&apos;s Flagship Herald Square store next Monday, serving pizza and pastas in one and gelatti and sorbeti in the other. Though accessible from inside the store on the sixth floor, a dedicated elevator on 35th Street brings guests directly the restaurants, which will both stay open past Macy&apos;s store hours. In addition to tasty Italian fare, views that were previously unavailable to the public show diners the Empire State Building and Herald Square below. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605044229/http://www.patinagroup.com/restaurant.php?restaurants_id=139">Stella 34 Trattoria</a> will serve lunch and dinner seven days a week, including Neapolitan-style pizzas made with imported Caputo flour and baked in wood burning ovens. Al forno entrees like Tortino di Melanzane ($17), an eggplant dish, and a b&#xE9;chamel Lasagna ($20) will also be baked in the ovens; a variety of pasta, fish and meat entrees are also available. Traditional Italian appetizers&#x2014;Piccoli Piatti&#x2014;like Arancini ($6), marinated olives ($5) and baby octopus ($9) are designed for sharing between the table. Come lunchtime, oven-baked sandwiches ($15) stuffed with veal meatballs, chicken Milanese and other meats are added to the menu.</p>

<p>Located just inside Stella 34 is Vivoli Il Gelato, a third-generation Florentine gelato shop that specializes in frozen treats without additives or flavorings. The gelateria, opening for the first time outside of Italy, will offer a rotating selection of nine gelati and three sorbetti, with flavors like Stracciatella (chocolate chip), Crema all&apos;Arancia (creme with bitter orange zest) and Pera (baked Bosc pear) sorbeti. Cups of the creamy stuff&#x2014;no cones!&#x2014;can be enjoyed inside the store or taken home in pints and quarts. </p>

<p><em>Stella 34 Trattoria, located inside Macy&apos;s at 151 West 34th Street on the 6th Floor,  serves lunch Monday - Sunday from 11:30 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. and dinner Monday - Sunday from 4:00 p.m. - 10:00 p.m.; for reservations call (212) 967-9251</em></p>

<p><em>Vivoli Il Gelato, located located inside Macy&apos;s at 151 West 34th Street on the 6th Floor, is open Monday - Friday from 9 a.m. to 8 p.m. and Saturday and Sunday from 10 a.m. to 8 p.m.</em></p>
