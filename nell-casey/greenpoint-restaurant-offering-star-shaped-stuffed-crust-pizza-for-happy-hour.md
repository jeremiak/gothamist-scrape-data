<p>Happy hour deals that include food are the <em>best</em> kind of happy hours. At <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126001553/http://www.21greenpoint.com/">21 Greenpoint</a>, chef Sean Telo&apos;s baking up special pies for the restaurant&apos;s happy hour on <strong>Tuesday - Friday</strong> from 6 - 8 p.m. Stop by for special star-shaped pizzas with a cheese stuffed-crust topped with meat and hot peppers. The pie plus a glass of house beer or wine will set you back $15.</p>

<p>On <strong>Wednesday</strong>, celebrate the birth of Scottish poet Robert Burns at two different NYC bars. At <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126001553/http://www.analoguenyc.com/">Analogue</a> they&apos;ll be pouring a special Scotch flight ($40) featuring a Highland single-malt Dalmore 12 Year, Balvenie 12 Year Doublewood from Speyside, and the heavily-peated Ardbeg 10 Year from Islay. In Bushwick at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126001553/http://www.therookerybar.com/">The Rookery</a>, it&apos;s all about slam poetry and Scotland&apos;s greatest exports: scotch and haggis. Show up in a kilt and score a free schooner of Belhaven. The festivities begin at 8 p.m.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="012317burke.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126001553im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_nellcasey/012317burke.jpg" width="640" height="404"> <br> <i> Lamb (Burke &amp; Wills)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Observe Australia Day on <strong>Thursday</strong> with a five-course prix fixe dinner at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126001553/http://www.burkeandwillsny.com/">Burke &amp; Wills Australian Bistro</a> on the Upper West Side. Available beginning at 5:30 p.m., the feast includes a choice of dishes including Truffle Burrata, Australian Lamb Rack, Cavatelli with a broccoli rabe pesto, and Pavlova. The dinner costs $65 for just the food or $105 with an all Australian wine pairing. </p>

<p>There are two events supporting <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126001553/https://www.plannedparenthood.org/">Planned Parenthood</a> in the city this week. On <strong>Monday</strong>, head to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126001553/http://gothamist.com/2016/12/15/best_new_bars_2016.php">cute new Italian cocktail den</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126001553/https://www.yelp.com/biz/august-laura-brooklyn">August Laura</a> for an open bar from 6 - 7 p.m. (suggested donation $20) and Fernet Branca, Branca Menta &amp; Antica Formula and St. Germain cocktails thereafter til 10 p.m. Then on <strong>Wednesday</strong>, women-led <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126001553/http://www.taproom307.com/">Taproom 307</a> will be pouring Revolution Brewing beers&#x2014;they&apos;re promising lots of styles and some &quot;rarities&quot;&#x2014;from 6 p.m. - 10 p.m. If you haven&apos;t yet made a donation online (or want to make another), a rep from PP will be on hand to receive them, plus answer questions.</p>
