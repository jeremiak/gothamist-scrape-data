<p>Apple the Cat dreads the morning alarm clock, the exhausting search for something to wear and the misery of trying to cook a decent breakfast without opposable thumbs. It&apos;s a tough life out there for a hungry kitty, who just wants to eat some god damn pancakes before a day spent slogging away at the local mouse population. Watch below as Apple navigates the kitchen in an attempt to make a fuel to power her through the hunt:</p>

<p>The video is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429062721/http://wedid.it/campaigns/615">part of a fundraiser</a> for the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429062721/http://www.nyccah.org/">New York Coalition Against Hunger</a>, which represents over one thousand not-for-profit soup kitchens and food pantries and also seeks innovative ways to make sure all Americans have access to food. They&apos;re trying to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429062721/http://wedid.it/campaigns/615">raise $5,000 in 30 days</a> and have harnessed the power of the Internet cat the make it happen. </p>
