					<p>On Monday night, dinner service begins at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016035614/http://www.massoninyc.com/">Massoni</a>, chef and <em>Top Chef</em> competitor Dale Talde&apos;s first Manhattan restaurant in the Arlo NoMad hotel, where he&apos;s promising to deliver some &quot;inauthentic Italian&quot; food. The fusion motif&apos;s a popular one for Talde, who&apos;s previously <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016035614/http://gothamist.com/2012/01/10/talde_will_serve_hawaiian_pork_buns.php">mined pan-Asian flavors and dishes</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016035614/http://gothamist.com/2013/11/06/gorge_on_gourmet_pub_grub_at_pork_s.php">elevated pub grub</a> and once <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016035614/http://gothamist.com/2014/11/17/thanksgiving_ramen.php">made Thanksgiving ramen</a>. </p>

<p>Massoni&apos;s firmly rooted in Italian dishes, but Talde&apos;ll flip the switch a bit, like the Biriyani Rice Balls with cool ranch raita (a yogurt-based condiment) and spicy tomato dipping sauces, a Caesar salad with nori, and Vietnamese Beef Shank Agnolotti bathing in a charred onion beef broth with bean sprouts and thai basil.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="111716massoni2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016035614im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_nellcasey/111716massoni2.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> Pepperoni pie (<a href="claywilliams.photoshelter.com">Clay Williams</a>/Gothamist)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>The core of the restaurant&apos;s offerings come from the carb category, including pastas&#x2014;the Rigatoni a la Singapore with clams, shrimp, Italian sausage, Chinese broccoli and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016035614/https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/XO_sauce">XO sauce</a> sounds particularly enticing&#x2014;along with pizzas that look to share some DNA with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016035614/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/20/emmy_squared_detroit_pizza_heaven.php#photo-1">a popular Brooklyn pizzeria</a>. The pizzas&#x2014;which are available at the restaurant and by the slice at the downstairs takeout counter&#x2014;look hefty and come in combinations like clam, potato and parsley and sweet fennel sausage, tomato and mozzarella with a side ranch.</p>

<p>Talde and team will provide all the food and beverage in the hotel, so in addition to Massoni and the takeout counter, they&apos;ll also be shaking up cocktails at the second floor bar, BARlo, where guests can eat the full restaurant dinner menu alongside. Then in the spring, they&apos;ll open The Heights, a rooftop watering hole serving cocktails inspired by NYC locales with &quot;Heights&quot; in the name, such as Crown, Washington and Brooklyn.</p>

<p><em>11 East 31st Street, (212) 951-1141; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016035614/http://www.massoninyc.com/">massoninyc.com</a></em></p>
