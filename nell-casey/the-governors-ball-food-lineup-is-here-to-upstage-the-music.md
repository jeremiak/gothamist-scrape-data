title: The Governor's Ball Food Lineup Is Here To Upstage The Music

										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="042717mozz.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_arts_john/042717mozz.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> Expect many, many shots like this (Scott Lynch/Gothamist)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>To hell with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2017/01/04/governors_ball_2017_lineup.php">the music</a>, we&apos;re going to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/https://www.governorsballmusicfestival.com/">Governor&apos;s Ball</a> to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/23/panorama_food_photos.php#photo-1">get fed</a>. The festival unleashed its <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/https://www.governorsballmusicfestival.com/food/">lineup</a> of food vendors this morning and it&apos;s chock full of all the places the Instagrammers have been waiting on line for as of late. You&apos;ve got your <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2017/01/19/raw_cookie_dough_shop_nyc.php">raw cookie dough</a>, your <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/28/wowfulls_lower_east_side.php">waffle cups</a>, your <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/29/mister_dips_now_slinging_burgers_an.php#photo-9">Airstream burgers</a>, your <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/18/get_soft_serve_in_fish-shaped_panca.php#photo-1">fish-shaped ice cream</a>. What no <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2017/04/03/avocaderia_avocado_restaurant_brook.php">avocado bar</a>? File your complaints with curators <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/https://www.theinfatuation.com/new-york">The Infatuation</a>. </p>

<p>When you&apos;re <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2017/01/04/governors_ball_2017_lineup.php">taking a Tool break</a>, meander to one of the food tents to find <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/16/nyc_best_fried_foods_2017.php">Big Mozz Sticks</a>, Luke&apos;s Lobster, Roberta&apos;s, Dough, Arancini Bros and other regulars in the market and event food lineup. Harry &amp; Ida&#x2019;s Meat and Supply Co. are bringing something (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2015/09/08/pastrami_perfection_at_harry_and_id.php#photo-1">hopefully pastrami</a>!) to the fest, too, and Taqueria Diana will also be on hand (hopefully <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/25/best_nachos_nyc_2016.php">with nachos</a>!) for the three-day event.</p>

<p>Organizers promise &quot;multiple free water stations throughout the grounds&quot;&#x2014;fledgling event producers <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170427220930/http://gothamist.com/2016/08/15/nyc_african_food_fest_fubar.php">take note</a>!&#x2014;and there&apos;ll be a Beer Garden offering &quot;the complete Miller family of beers,&quot; plus other beer stations pouring pints of Lagunitas, liquor gardens shaking up cocktails, and &quot;wine and rose bars.&quot; </p>
