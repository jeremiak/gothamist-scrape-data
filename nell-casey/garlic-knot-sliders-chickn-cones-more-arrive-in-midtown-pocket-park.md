<p>It&apos;s officially <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/18/outdoor_food_markets_guide.php">outdoor market</a> season, when food vendors creep back into public spaces to tempt you with pizza and doughnuts when you promised yourself a healthful kale salad would be your lunch of choice. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://urbanspacenyc.com/broadway-bites/">Broadway Bites</a>, one of the many outdoor and indoor markets run by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://urbanspacenyc.com/">Urbanspace</a>, returns to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/https://www.nycgovparks.org/parks/greeley-square-park">Greeley Square</a> on Broadway between 32nd and 33rd Streets Thursday, bringing a bounty of mom-and-pop eats with it.</p>

<p>All told there are 30 vendors participating for the early summer session, which runs through Friday, July 14th and operates daily from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m. All manner of cravings can be met, from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://meltkraft.com/">grilled cheese</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://iceandvice.com/">ice cream</a> to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://www.lemonrenegade.com/">flavored lemonades</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/https://www.holdmyknots.com/">garlic knot sliders</a>, which I didn&apos;t know was a thing one could crave until writing this sentence. </p>

<p>Newcomers for this season include West Village doughnut pushers <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://www.thedoughnutproject.com/">Doughnut Project</a>; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/https://www.chickncone.com/">Chick&apos;n&apos;Cone</a>, which also <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://gothamist.com/2017/05/31/riis_park_beach_food_2017.php#photo-1">made a splash at Riis Park</a>; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://www.daadumplings.com/">Daa! Dumplings</a>, which serves its Russian dumplings in a cone; Mi Burger from the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/https://www.boilboilramen.com/">Boil Boil Ramen</a> crew; and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171101092506/http://www.hanksjuicybeef.com/">Hanks Juicy Beef</a>, where you can prep your body for a midday nap with giant Chicago-style beef sandwiches. Other newcomers to Broadway Bites are noted with an asterisk below.  </p>
