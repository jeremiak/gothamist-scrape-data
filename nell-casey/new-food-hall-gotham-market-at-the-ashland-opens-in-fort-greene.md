					<p>Gotham Organization, which runs the Gotham West Market <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170204005847/http://gothamist.com/2013/11/21/gotham_west_market_brings_great_eat.php#photo-1">in Hell&apos;s Kitchen</a>, debuted their Brooklyn spinoff Gotham Market at The Ashland inside the luxury development of the same name. </p>

<p>John Stage of Dinosaur Bar-B-Que has a big stake in the new food hall, including an Italian restaurant and pizzera, Apizza Regionale; a rotisserie restaurant called Flip Bird, which serves an excellent spiced fried chicken (at a preview event, anyway) along with rotisserie birds; Bar Granger, serving cocktails and wine; and the forthcoming Egg at the Bird, serving breakfast items like sandwiches, house-made breads and coffee</p>

<p>Mason Jar, a Midtown Southern bar and BBQ joint, will also operate a stall, along with a temporary pop-up from Crabby Shack, the Crown Heights seafood spot serving very tasty crab rolls and the like. In February, tapas spot Boqueria will also open up a sit-down restaurant in the space. </p>
