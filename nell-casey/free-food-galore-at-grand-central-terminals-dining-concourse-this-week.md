<p>Some of Grand Central Terminal&apos;s most popular <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170301150304/http://gothamist.com/2014/06/16/where_to_eat_grand_central.php#photo-19">eateries</a> will be giving away free food all week as part of the Terminal&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170301150304/http://www.grandcentralterminal.com/event/taste-of-the-dining-concourse/2145484671?nomobile=1">&quot;Taste of the Dining Concourse&quot;</a> event, starting today in the lower level of the building. </p>

<p>Running all week until Friday from 11 a.m. to noon and again from 4 p.m. to 5 p.m., the Taste event features free samples from 12 concourse eateries. Magnolia Bakery will be offering up bites of banana pudding, the new Prova Pizzabar will hand you meatballs and pizza margarita, Cafe Spice treats everyone to papdi chaat, and Shake Shack tempts with Brownie Batter Hot Chocolate.
