<p>Dine around the charming Queens enclave of Sunnyside at the neighborhood&apos;s now-annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113152816/http://www.sunnysideshines.org/sunnyside-restaurant-week">Restaurant Week</a>, an adaptation of the large city-wide event that&apos;s been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113152816/http://gothamist.com/2016/08/03/grand_street_restaurant_week.php">replicated by communities</a> to celebrate their varied dining options. </p>

<p>Holding court in the city&apos;s most diverse borough, Sunnyside has its own wide-reaching collection of eateries, 30 of which are offering special menus of $25, three-course dinners, with some even offering a $25 meal <em>for two</em>. </p>

<p>On the docket: Jamaican, Irish, Tibetan, Peruvian, Japanese and many other represented countries and cuisines. Tire company-turned-haute-restaurant-guide has taken note of the neighborhood, too, naming two restaurants as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113152816/http://gothamist.com/2015/09/24/michelin_bib_gourmand_nyc_2015.php">Michelin Bib Gourmand recipients</a>, both of whom (Salt and Fat and Venturo Osteria &amp; Wine Bar) are participating.</p>

<p>Sunnyside Restaurant Week kicks off on Monday, September 19th and runs, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113152816/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/12/restaurant_week_2016.php">true to its name</a>, until Sunday, September 25th. Check out the full list and map of restaurants below and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113152816/http://www.sunnysideshines.org/sunnyside-restaurant-week">peep the menus</a> as they become available.</p>
