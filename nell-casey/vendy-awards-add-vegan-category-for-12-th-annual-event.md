<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110090209/http://www.vendyawards.streetvendor.org/">The Vendy Awards</a> are rolling over to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110090209/https://govisland.com/">Governors Island</a> in September, celebrating the best of NYC&apos;s mobile and market food vendors. There&apos;s always plenty to indulge in at the event and lots of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110090209/http://gothamist.com/2015/09/14/vendy_awards_2015.php#photo-1">very deserving winners</a> to be selected, including the first ever vegan category this year at the fest, now in its 12th year. </p>

<p>There have been vegan vendors at the award event before&#x2014;Cinnamon Snail has been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110090209/http://gothamist.com/2012/09/16/vegans_tacos_take_top_honors_at_str.php#photo-1">a big winner</a> in the past&#x2014;but this is the first time vegan food makers will have their own category. Six animal-free purveyors will be going head-to-head, competing with things like vegan barbecue, veggie burgers and Indian cuisines.</p>

<p>Smorgasburg vets <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110090209/http://www.monksmeats.com/">Monk&apos;s Meats</a> will be offering their vegan barbecue, an oxymoron of type for the traditionally meat-centric food. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110090209/https://twitter.com/jerkshacknyc">Jerk Shack</a>, who&apos;ve been up at Vendy Plaza, will be doing Caribbean cuisine; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110090209/https://www.facebook.com/MysttikMasaala/">Mysttik Masaala&apos;s</a> in for homemade Indian; Vegan Bandwagon&apos;s doing veggie burgers and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110090209/http://www.yeahdawgvegan.com/">Yeah Dawg</a> classic (but vegan) hot dogs. Finally, Bamboo Bites will be doing Southeast Asian street foods and snacks.</p>

<p>The rest of the categories are the same as previous iterations (see complete list below), and one winner will be crowned in each category by a panel of judges with additional accolades for the People&#x2019;s Choice Award. The Vendys will be Saturday, September 17th; tickets are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110090209/https://www.eventbrite.com/e/12th-annual-nyc-vendy-awards-september-17-2016-tickets-24935227938">on sale now</a>.</p>

<p><strong>Best Market Vendor:</strong> Big Mozz, Arepalicious, NJS, MATZAHBREI, and Tuson Sate</p>

<p><strong>Best Dessert:</strong> Delmy&#x2019;s Obleas, A Lil&#x2019; Bit of Fun, The Good Batch, Sweetface Snoballs, and Raindrop Cake</p>

<p><strong>Rookie of the Year:</strong> Puran Dhaka, Los Viajeros, Harlem Seafood Soul, Sisig City, The Basket, and Mr. Bing</p>
