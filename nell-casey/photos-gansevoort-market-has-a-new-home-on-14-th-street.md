					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161215103807/http://www.gansmarket.com/">Gansevoort Market</a> has packed its bags and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161215103807/http://gothamist.com/2014/10/25/gansevoort_market_1.php">moved from its namesake street</a> over to 353 West 14th Street at Ninth Avenue. The new market doesn&apos;t have that adorable VW bus or the pretty clinging vines like the OG outpost, but it does boast some brand new vendors to get excited about should you find yourself in need of a pick-me-up post-Highline.</p>

<p>Rookie fried dough pushers Doughnut Project debuted their second location inside the market, joining the next location of Big Gay Ice Cream, which will debut in the coming weeks. There are a ton of sweets now, actually, including gelato shop Mo, macaron from Dana&apos;s Bakery and sweet pancakes from Crepe Sucre.</p>

<p>Pasta food truck Ponti Rossi makes a stop at the new market, along with another outpost of Luke&apos;s Lobster and empanadas from La Sonrisa. Oppa KBBQ, Gotham Poke, Sabi Sushi, Tease Tea and Cocoa Grinder round out the new market vendors.</p>
