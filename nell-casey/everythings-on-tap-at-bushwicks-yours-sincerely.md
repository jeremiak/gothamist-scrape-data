					<p>A 29-tap cocktail &quot;laboratory&quot; debuts this New Year&apos;s Eve in Bushwick, where the owners of Dear Bushwick are expanding their footprint with Yours Sincerely. The all-draft program includes 20 on tap cocktails, which the owners think will streamline the &quot;pretentious&quot; mixology process, as it cuts down on the time between order and inebriation. Six beers, two wines and an on-tap whiskey are also on draught.</p>

<p>The somewhat <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160605050057/https://www.instagram.com/p/_xNolPxeOH/?taken-by=yourssincerelybk">experimental new concept</a> features co-owner Darren Grenia&apos;s takes on classic cocktails, including an organic date infused bourbon Old Fashioned given the cringe-worthy name Swipe Right ($8). A gin &amp; tonic (&quot;Emperical Formula,&quot; $8) made with Dorothy Parker gin employs their house tonic water; they&apos;re also offering three house-made sodas in ginger, black lemonade and grapefruit flavors, which make up the bulk of the bar&apos;s &quot;Almost Non-Alcoholic Cocktails&quot; ($5). Drinks are served in beakers, of course.</p>

<p>Smaller stuff means Cocktail Shots ($4) like one with chicory-infused bourbon, Jamaican jerk-infused Owney&apos;s Rum and molasses (&quot;Chaos Theory&quot;) and some nibbles like Crackling Pork.</p>

<p><em>41 Wilson Avenue in Bushwick; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160605050057/http://yourssincerely.co/">website</a></em></p>
