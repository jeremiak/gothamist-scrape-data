					<p>Are picklebacks making a comeback? Did they <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170126203653/http://gothamist.com/2015/08/20/mayor_of_picklebacktown.php">ever leave</a>? These are questions to ponder while throwing back shot glasses of Tromba Plata tequila paired with a tomatillo, cilantro, mint and jalapeno juice. Sad jarred pickle juice this is not.</p>

<p>Rittenhouse rye with Manischewitz garam masala curry and raisin and Sochu with fermented pineapple, lemongrass, habanero are two of the other shot-based options. Regular sipping cocktails employ pop culture-based nods like You Say He&#x2019;s Just a Friend ($12) with Blackwell&#x2019;s Jamaican Rum, mint white peppercorn, Salers Gentian, Yellow Chartreuse, lime, and Peche de Vigne, and cocktails named for individuals including Rob Zombie, Senor Hemingway and Donkey Kong.</p>

<p>The 20-seat bar&apos;s currently in soft open mode on Myrtle Avenue with plans for a grand opening Wednesday, January 25th. For now, a limited food menu includes a take on the classic cafeteria sandwich the Sloppy Joe ($14), a Hot Italian Sausage &quot;Corndog&quot; ($12) and Escarole Caesar Salad ($10). It&apos;s a tight space, especially if you need a seat for dining, but come the spring, the back patio opens with room for about 30 more.</p>

<p><em>507 Myrtle Avenue</em></p>
