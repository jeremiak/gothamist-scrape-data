					<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015110101/http://i.huffpost.com/gen/2947210/thumbs/o-KRISPY-KREME-570.jpg?1">Hot Now</a> sign will always be activated at Wylie Dufresne&apos;s forthcoming donut shop <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015110101/http://www.dusdonuts.com/">Du&apos;s Donuts and Coffee</a>, which is opening as part of the William Vale Hotel&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015110101/http://gothamist.com/2016/12/16/leuca_william_vale_williamsburg.php#photo-1">dining complex</a> on Wednesday, April 26th. The innovative chef&#x2014;who&apos;s been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015110101/http://gothamist.com/2016/05/31/curry_burger_dufresne.php">tooling around</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015110101/http://gothamist.com/2016/01/14/ansel_dufresne_breakfast_sandwich.php">town</a> since closing his <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015110101/http://gothamist.com/2015/05/05/alder_tasting_menu_wylie_dufresne.php#photo-1">East Village restaurant Alder</a> in 2015&#x2014;will be making sugared cake donuts to order in Strawberries and Cream and Cinnamon Apple, which will be served ~hot out of the fryer~. </p>

<p>Not all of the donuts will be hot, but all of them will be cake-based. Flavors include Mexican Hot Chocolate, Creamsicle, Grapefruit Chamomile, Pistachio Pink Lemonade, Banana Graham, Malted Coffee, Pomegranate Tahini, and Peanut Butter Yuzu. We&apos;ll wait patiently for an <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015110101/http://www.eater.com/2015/1/14/7533485/wd50-eggs-benedict-wylie-dufresne-eater-elements">eggs benedict flavor</a>, chef. </p>

<p>The coffee element of the operation will be supplied by Brooklyn Roasting Company, who&apos;ve brewed their way across the land after <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015110101/http://gothamist.com/2011/08/08/dumbo_buzzing_from_newly_opened_bro.php#photo-1">opening in DUMBO in 2011</a>. </p>

<p><em>107 North 12th Street; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015110101/http://www.dusdonuts.com/">dusdonuts.com</a></em></p>
