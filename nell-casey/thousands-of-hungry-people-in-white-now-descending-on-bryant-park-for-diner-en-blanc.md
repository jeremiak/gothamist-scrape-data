<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128062847/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/05/break_out_the_bleach_diner_en_blanc.php">Diner en Blanc</a> has begun! The annual dress-all-in-white picnic is underway and thousands of folks with chairs, tables and picnics strapped to their backs are now making their way to this year&apos;s secret location: Bryant Park. Attendees have only <em>30 minutes</em> to drop what they&apos;re doing, give they bosses an excuse to leave early and hightail it to 42nd Street.</p>

<p>Organizers traded in last year&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128062847/http://gothamist.com/2012/08/21/photos_diner_en_blanc_1.php#photo-1">Lincoln Center setup</a> for one in the popular midtown park. If you had any independent plans to picnic there this evening, we suggest making alternative arrangements. </p>
