<p>It&apos;s been 25 years and 75, 512 pounds of pasta since <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://www.tonylovestina.com/shows_tony_tina_wedding_1.cfm?eid=31">Tony n&apos; Tina&apos;s Wedding</a> defined the genre of dinner theater for a generation of tourists. Visitors to NYC are still &quot;toasting the happy couple&quot; off-Broadway, but if you&apos;re looking for a subtler rendition of the style, there are several upcoming performances that will satisfying your cravings for both fine culture and fine cuisine.</p>

<p>The <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://www.eatmyheartoutsupperclub.com/">Eat My Heart Out Supper Club</a></strong>, a combination storytelling session and feast, began a year ago this month with a five-course vegetarian dinner and storytelling sets from novelists, comedians and musicians. On April 1st they return for their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://www.kitchit.com/local_events/heart/NYC_April2013">third Brooklyn incarnation</a> at DUMBO&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://winkelstudio.com/directions.php">Winkel Studio</a> with a new lineup of performers, including comedian and filmmaker <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://www.seatonsmith.com/">Seaton Smith</a>, writer and photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://www.bazima.com/">Blaise Kearsley</a>, comedian and author <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://davehillonline.com/">Dave Hill</a> and a few other orators. Chef Luke Davin is creating the accompanying, fish-heavy menu that&apos;s themed around Poisson d&apos;Avril, the French version of April Fool&apos;s Day. For a taste of what&apos;s in store, check out <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://vimeo.com/60762778">this video</a> from the last dinner. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://www.kitchit.com/local_events/heart/NYC_April2013">Tickets run $75 - $90</a> for the evening&apos;s feasting and festivities. </p>

<p>Also on April 1st, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://isa.gg/">ISA</a> in Williamsburg presents an encore presentation of <strong><em><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://vimeo.com/53892161">The Three Stages of Murgatroyd</a></em></strong>, a three-act musical chronicling the &quot;tale of a strange, sweet 18th-century boy whom the world turns into a dirty old perv.&quot; Along with the campy tale, a three-course dinner of rustic Mediterranean cuisine is served, which includes a Wild Arugula, Fennel and Castelvetrano Olive Salad; House-made Cavatelli with Herb Roasted Mushrooms or Heritage Pork Stew; and finishes with a Chocolate Budino. A complimentary glass of bubbly is included in the $65 ticket price, but nobody should pass up on the &quot;Endless Wine&quot; supplement for just $23 more. Only 30 seats are available, so make a reservation at rsvp@isa.gg ASAP. </p>

<p><a href="www.brasseriebeaumarchais.com/">Beaumarchais&apos;</a> ongoing burlesque series <strong>Nuit Blanche</strong> celebrated their 1st anniversary last Wednesday and next Wednesday, March 27th they&apos;ll be welcoming the new season with their &quot;Spring Serenade.&quot; Expect the same decadent and exotic burlesque performances from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604150952/http://dancesofvice.com/">Dances of Vice</a> but even more floral and colorful. Three sets of cabaret performances unfold over the evening, during which patrons can order a la carte dishes from the restaurant&apos;s updated spring menu. The French-centric spring offerings include C&#xF4;telettes d&apos;Ageneau Grill&#xE9;e ($33), lamb chops with eggplant Cannelloni filled with tomato confit and goat cheese with chipea panisse and Homard Poche&#xE9; aux Palourdes ($38), Lobster and Manila clams steamed in bear. Afterwards, stick around for a bacchanalian all night dance party. The performances are free of charge&#x2014;though you pay for any dinner and drinks, obviously&#x2014;and reservations are recommended by emailing reservations@beaumarchaisny.com or calling 212-675-2400.</p>
