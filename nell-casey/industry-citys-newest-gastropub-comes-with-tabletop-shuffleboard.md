					<p>Vincent Chirico, who runs the excellent <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017145352/http://vairestaurant.com/restaurant/">Vai Restaurant</a> on the Upper West Side, officially opened his third NYC restaurant today inside Industry City. There, he&apos;s operating Filament at The Landing, which is being branded as a &quot;gastropub&quot; of sorts, and is serving some of Chirico&apos;s greatest hits from Vai and his other restaurant, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017145352/https://www.coarsenyc.com/">Coarse</a>, in the West Village.</p>

<p>Among those dishes are the Hamachi Duo ($14), a raw preparation with avocado, cucumber and preserved ginger. Also making the move to Industry City is the chef&apos;s Charred Octopus ($15), resting on a bed of jalapeno pesto and fingerling potatoes, and a Chicken Under A Brick ($13) with vegetables. Check out the rest of the menu&#x2014;including breakfast and lunch options for locals&#x2014;below.</p>

<p>The new space boasts more than just the food, however. A combined indoor and outdoor area includes a courtyard bar&#x2014;with craft beer and cocktails&#x2014;plus large format games like pool tables and tabletop shuffleboards. </p>

<p><em>Industry City, 220 36th Street in Sunset Park</em></p><em>
