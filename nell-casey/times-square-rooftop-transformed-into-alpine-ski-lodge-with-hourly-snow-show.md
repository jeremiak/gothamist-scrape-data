</div></div>					<p>Times Square&apos;s Haven Rooftop at the Sanctuary Hotel has <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425193710/http://gothamist.com/2012/06/20/get_drunk_and_high_above_times_squa.php#photo-1">ditched the bikini angels</a> for snow bunnies, transforming the airy green oasis into a snow-flecked winter wonderland. Opening next Thursday, the space reopens as an alpine ski lodge, covered by a transparent tent for viewing the winter sky and heated by personal fireplaces. They&apos;ve also built custom chalets where you and your ski team crew can huddle beneath faux-fur throws and imagine you&apos;re in exotic Aspen or St. Moritz or <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425193710/http://www.herogohome.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/Superman3SkiChalet.jpg">Ross Webster&apos;s roof in <em>Superman III</em></a></p>

<p>Instead of the apres ski burgers and fries most of us are accustomed to, the kitchen turns out dishes like Bouillabaise ($28), Steak Au Poivre ($28) and Mushroom Risotto ($14), should you need to fortify after <strike>hitting the slopes</strike> braving Times Square. If drinking&apos;s what&apos;s on your mind, they&apos;re doing hot cocktails ($14) like spiked cider, peppermint hot chocolate and caramel Irish coffee. And don&apos;t forget the Shot Ski: four shots of Russian Standard Platinum vodka, taken simultaneously atop a ski. Just like they do in the Alps.</p>

<p>For dessert they&apos;ve got fondue and s&apos;mores kits, served tableside so guests can roast their own marshmallows. With the open flames and hourly snow show blanketing the tent in fresh flakes, it&apos;ll almost feel like you really are high up in the Rockies; at least until the bros the next chalet over down one too many Shot Skis. </p>

<p><em>132 W. 47th Street, 212-466-9000; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425193710/http://www.havenrooftop.com/">havenrooftop.com</a>. Opens December 12th, 4 p.m. daily.</em></p>
