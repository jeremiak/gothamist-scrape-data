					<p>The city&apos;s most innuendo-filled fried chicken purveyor, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170128175929/https://www.stickys.com/">Sticky&apos;s Finger Joint</a>, opens their fourth Manhattan restaurant today in the Financial District on Maiden Lane. And with it: really cheap baskets of food. From 4 p.m. to 7 p.m. the shop will be selling $1 chicken finger baskets, which includes french fries. </p>

<p>Jon Sherman debuts some new menu items and dipping sauce flavors with the new location. Among them, a &quot;Bacon Mac Sandwich&quot; with jalapeno cheddar sauce, chopped bacon, caramelized onions and pickles and a &quot;Hot Chicken Sandwich&quot; with Nashville Numb hot sauce, Sticky&#x2019;s Sauce, pickles and cumin. </p>

<p>There&apos;s also a new Vindaloo BBQ sauce &quot;inspired by Jon&apos;s mom&apos;s childhood brisket sauce and his hometown of Edison, NJ.&quot; <br>
 <br>
<em>21 Maiden Lane</em></p>
