<p>It&apos;s Friday, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601001932/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/20/the_ultimate_pizza_debate_grima.php">the Day of Pizza</a>. And while we could sit here arguing about the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601001932/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/24/patsy_grimaldi_doesnt_approve_of_yo.php">wrong way to eat a slice</a>, let&apos;s focus on the positive. Where on a slice can the <em>best</em> bite be found? Our office was divided on this topic (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601001932/http://gothamist.com/tags/tastetest">surprise, surprise</a>), but most staffers agreed that the &quot;Crossover Bite&quot;&#x2014;the hybrid area where the toppings meet the crust&#x2014;is the most savored bite. Where do you fall on the slice spectrum?</p>
