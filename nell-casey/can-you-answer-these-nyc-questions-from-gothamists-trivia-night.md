</div></div>					<p>Gothamist hosted our <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/06/announcing_gothamists_first-ever_tr_1.php">first ever trivia night</a> last night at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.thebellhouseny.com/">The Bell House</a> with a sold out crowd, Citi Bike and some truly epic air guitar. Over 60 teams competed in four rounds of trivia&#x2014;created with our friends at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.trivworks.com/">TrivWorks</a>&#x2014;and the competition was <em>intense</em>. Still, winners were crowned, many beers were consumed and there was even time to yell at Gothamist about <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/18/the_best_pizza_in_nyc.php">our taste in pizza</a>. </p>

<p>First place winners Team Roebling took home year-long memberships to NYC&apos;s bike share program <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://citibikenyc.com/">Citi Bike</a>; 2nd place winners Ladder 157 scored tickets to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://anightwithjanisjoplin.com/">A Night With Janis Joplin</a> on Broadway; and the third place team, repping Levy&apos;s Unique New York, won a night of bowling at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.bowlmor.com/">Bowlmor</a> in the Village. Even the last place team was rewarded with tickets to see the badass ladies of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.gothamgirlsrollerderby.com/">Gotham Girls Roller Derby</a>.</p>

<p>Surprise visits from NY1&apos;s Pat Kiernan and Roger Clark were a highlight of the evening, with the duo recreating the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/02/videos_air_guitar_god_roger_clark_h.php">viral air guitar video</a> broadcast earlier this summer. </p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150618231649if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/cvSqlwFi490" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Kiernan&#x2014;who regularly <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.trivworks.com/2013/09/pat-kiernan-holiday-trivia/">teams up with TrivWorks</a>&#x2014;then hosted one of the night&apos;s mini-games, which invited attendees on stage for quick-fire trivia rounds to win additional prizes from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.bowlluckystrike.com/">Lucky Strike</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.parlorsteakhouse.com/">Parlor Steakhouse</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.cbgb.com/">CBGB Festival</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.bbcamericashop.com/">BBC America</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://www.fairwaymarket.com/">Fairway Market</a>. Two lucky folks even scored <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://dominiqueansel.com/">Cronuts</a> from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150618231649/http://dominiqueansel.com/">Dominique Ansel Bakery</a> to share with their teammates as part of one of many raffles throughout the evening.</p>
