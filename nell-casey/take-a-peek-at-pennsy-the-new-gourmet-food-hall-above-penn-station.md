					<p>To describe the dining landscape in and around Penn Station as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170623214850/http://gothamist.com/2015/01/17/penn_station_ousted_slew_of_fast_fo.php">bleak</a> would be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170623214850/http://gothamist.com/2013/11/12/doh_closes_penn_station_fast_food_p.php">an understatement</a>. But a new food hall&#x2014;yes, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170623214850/http://gothamist.com/2015/11/10/guide_to_nyc_food_halls.php">another one</a>!&#x2014;seeks to take some of the pain out of a pre-train/pre-MSG meal with a lineup of top chefs (and one literal Top Chef), some of whom are debuting new fast casual concepts. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170623214850/http://www.thepennsy.nyc/">The Pennsy</a>&#x2014;the nickname of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170623214850/https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pennsylvania_Railroad">Pennsylvania Railroad</a>&#x2014;opens for service today at 2 Penn Plaza inside the old Borders Books space. Remember <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170623214850/http://gothamist.com/2012/03/10/mommy_porn_book_lets_ladies_love_er.php">books</a>? </p>

<p>Most importantly, the food hall will be home to Cinnamon Snail&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170623214850/http://gothamist.com/2015/09/10/cinnamon_snail_nyc.php">first brick-and-mortar</a> outpost after <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170623214850/http://gothamist.com/2015/02/14/rip_cinnamon_snail_truck.php">taking their food truck</a> out of daily circulation last year. Their delicious vegan delights&#x2014;their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170623214850/http://gothamist.com/2014/07/24/the_best_vegan_dishes_in_nyc_2.php">Korean BBQ Seitan</a> is the stuff of dreams&#x2014;will be sharing space with Mario by Mary, an Italian fast casual spot by Mario Batali and Mary Giuliani; Lobster Press, an on-the-go crustacean stall by Marc Forgione; Pat LaFrieda Meat Purveyors, serving up carnivore-friendly sandwiches; and the second location of The Little Beet, a vegetable eatery by <em>Top Chef Masters</em> star Franklin Becker. There&apos;s also a bar.</p>

<p>The Pennsy will serve during lunch and dinner hours beginning at 11 a.m. daily with special post-game or concert hours &apos;til 11 p.m. Monday through Saturday and an 8 p.m. curfew on Sundays. </p>
