<p>You&apos;ll have plenty of food options if you&apos;re <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://gothamist.com/2013/04/03/cherry_blossom_watch_nycs_botanical.php">checking out the cherry blossoms</a> at the Brooklyn Botanic Garden this weekend: the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://www.nycfoodtrucks.org/2013/03/first-food-truck-rally-of-the-2013-season">first Food Truck Rally</a> of the season begins Sunday. The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://gothamist.com/2012/09/21/food_trucks_in_grand_army_plaza_thi.php">annual gathering</a> of mobile food vendors has parked itself at Grand Army Plaza on the first Sunday of each month for the past few years and this year they&apos;ll also populate the plaza the third Sunday of each month. </p>

<p>From 11 a.m. to 5 p.m. you can buy treats from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://www.nycfoodtrucks.org/2013/03/first-food-truck-rally-of-the-2013-season">fifteen trucks</a>, including, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://www.philssteaks.com/">Phil&apos;s Steaks</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://bigdsgrub.com/">Big D&apos;s Grub Truck</a>,  and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://www.vanleeuwenicecream.com/?page_id=15">Van Leeuwen Ice Cream</a>. Two of our favorites&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://www.kimchitacotruck.com/">Kimchi Taco Truck</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://www.morrisgrilledcheese.com/">Morris Grilled Cheese</a>&#x2014;are also attending, but our <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145406/http://gothamist.com/2012/10/09/cinnamon_snail.php#photo-1">food truck of dreams</a> continues to elude us. <br>
</p>
