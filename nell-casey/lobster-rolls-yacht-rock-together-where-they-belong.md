<p>In observance of National Fried Chicken Day&#x2014;because of <em>course</em> that&apos;s a &quot;thing&quot;&#x2014;mini chicken chain <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091140/http://sweetchicknyc.com/">Sweet Chick</a> will be offering an expanded menu of the <strong>Chicken &amp; Waffles dishes</strong> at both their Williamsburg and LES locations. Swing by July 6th through July 10th for combos ($18) including Korean Glazed Fried Chicken with Milk Jam Waffle, Nacho Cheese Smothered Fried Chicken with Jalapeno &#xAD;Black Bean Waffle,  Badass Curry with Coconut Waffle, and Chicken Parm with Basil Parmesan Waffle; among many others.</p>

<p>Newly-minted dine-in cinema <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091140/http://syndicatedbk.com/">Syndicated</a> in Bushwick begins a new <strong>Wednesday TV Night</strong> series this week, with a three-course special TV Night dinner menu. The series begins with <em>Firefly</em>, the short-lived but cult sci-fi show with Nathan Fillion, among others. They&apos;ll show the entire 14-episodes starting with the longer series premiere &quot;Serenity,&quot; followed by the next two episodes each Wednesday the following weeks. The special $33 menu includes Grilled Calamari with pancetta, green garlic and spinach; Fish &amp; Chips with beer-battered cod; and Blueberry Cobbler with vanilla ice cream; among other possible selections. </p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091140/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jMTI8vg7A5U">smooth</a> sounds of yacht rock are sailing into <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091140/http://thehallbrooklyn.com/">The Hall at MP</a> this Thursday and they&apos;re bringing a <strong><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091140/http://www.ticketweb.com/t3/sale/SaleEventDetail?eventId=6760325&amp;pl=cegpresents&amp;dispatch=loadSelectionData&amp;REFID=mp">Brooklyn Lobster Fest</a></strong> with them. Lubricate your ear drums with the sounds of The Yucht Brothers while feasting on a seafood platter consisting of a lobster roll, corn on the cob, bread and butter pickles and french fries. Tickets (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091140/http://www.ticketweb.com/t3/sale/SaleEventDetail?eventId=6760325&amp;pl=cegpresents&amp;dispatch=loadSelectionData&amp;REFID=mp">$35</a>) include the tickets plus the platter and GA tickets (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091140/http://www.ticketweb.com/t3/sale/SaleEventDetail?eventId=6760325&amp;pl=cegpresents&amp;dispatch=loadSelectionData&amp;REFID=mp">$15</a>) just entry into the show. </p>
