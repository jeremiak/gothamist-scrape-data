	<p><br>
As any good Jewish boy knows, if you ride a motorcycle, you&apos;re probably going to die&#x2014;even if you don&apos;t get into an accident, the amount of guilt and agita you&apos;ll receive from your mother is enough to send you to an early grave. Thankfully, there are some brave Jewish people out there who don&apos;t let an aversion to pig products stand in the way of getting on a hog. The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130609202454/http://chairiders.org/">Chai Riders</a> are just that group&#x2014;we ran into them today at Columbus Avenue and West 79th Street as they prepared to ride in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130609202454/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9124327">Celebrate Israel Day Parade.</a> Check out some photos above, and a video of them riding in the parade a few years ago below.</p>
