The weekend is almost over, but you still have some time to check out lots of the cool stuff going on in Brooklyn at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150717082308/http://gothamist.com/tags/dumboartsfestival">DUMBO Arts Festival.</a> There are TONS of projects, booths, projections and art projects that will be up until 6 p.m.; and tonight, you can see installations such as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150717082308/http://www.dumboartsfestival.com/event/2012/08/27/skylightsdumbo-3">Skylights: DUMBO</a>, the interactive <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150717082308/http://www.dumboartsfestival.com/event/2012/08/27/superhero-3">Superhero</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150717082308/http://www.dumboartsfestival.com/event/2012/08/29/soundmoves-3">Soundmoves</a>, the gorgeous <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150717082308/http://www.dumboartsfestival.com/event/2012/08/01/codex-dynamic">Codex Dynamic</a> and a performance from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150717082308/http://www.dumboartsfestival.com/event/2012/09/10/will-scott-2">Will Scott</a>. Check out <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150717082308/http://www.dumboartsfestival.com/find-art/">the event schedule here</a>. Click through to see some photos from the weekend, and watch some videos of those psychedelic-tinged projections below.</p>
