</div></div><p>When they&apos;re not posing philosophical questions <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120124131451/http://gothamist.com/2011/03/04/dear_mayor_bloomberg_how_do_magnets.php">about magnets</a> or being closely monitored <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120124131451/http://gawker.com/5854041/the-fbi-is-afraid-of-the-juggalo-army">by the FBI</a>, the Insane Clown Posse like to go on massive tours across the country, bringing their unique brand of theatrical face-painted rap-rock to the Juggalo masses. The group came through NYC earlier this week and played Hammerstein Ballroom. Photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120124131451/http://www.flickr.com/photos/jimkiernan/sets/72157627987624642/with/6285330226/">Jim Kiernan</a> once noted the group really goes all out <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120124131451/http://chicagoist.com/2011/08/16/in_pictures_the_gathering_of_the_ju.php#photo-3">with their fireworks</a>&#x2014;and it seems they do the same with their &quot;Faygo Cola,&quot; tons of bottles of which took up part of the stage. Click through to see photos from the show, and check out two videos from the show below, as well as the promo for the American Psycho Tour.</p>
