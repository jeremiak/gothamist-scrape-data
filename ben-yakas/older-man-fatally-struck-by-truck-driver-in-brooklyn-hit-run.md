<p>A pedestrian was fatally struck by a truck driver in a hit and run in Brooklyn yesterday. According to police, the incident happened around 3:20 p.m. at Atlantic Avenue and Williams Place. The man, who is believed to be in his 70s, was pushing a shopping cart westbound on Atlantic Avenue as he walked by the parked truck; as the man passed the driver&apos;s side door, the truck left the space and struck the victim.</p>

<p>The driver left the scene; the victim was taken to Brookdale Hospital where he was pronounced dead. No arrests have been made yet, and the investigation is ongoing. The truck is described as a 3 axle, dark colored cab, straight flatbed truck with a boom.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
