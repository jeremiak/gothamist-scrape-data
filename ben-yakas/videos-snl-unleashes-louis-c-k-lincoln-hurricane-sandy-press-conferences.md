For the first time in years, we were actively anticipating this week&apos;s new episode of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508175016/http://gothamist.com/tags/snl"><em>Saturday Night Live</em></a>&#x2014;and while it wasn&apos;t perfect, it included some of the best things we&apos;ve seen on SNL ever. And a lot of that was thanks to host <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508175016/http://gothamist.com/tags/louisck">Louis C.K.</a>, who absolutely killed it with his monologue (he actually told jokes!), and especially, the brilliant sketch Lincoln, a spoof on his show <em>Louie</em>: &quot;I just kinda think that owning a person is not cool, you stupid dick.&quot;</p>

<p>The cold open, as expected, was a spoof on Mayor Bloomberg&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508175016/http://gothamist.com/tags/hurricanesandy">Hurricane Sandy</a> press conferences (which included Lydia Callis, as well as Chris Christie and his own Jersey translator). Click through for those sketches, plus Mitt Romney&apos;s desperate Weekend Update plea for votes (&quot;I&apos;d there&apos;s anything people should know about Mitt Romney at this point, it&apos;s that nothing I&apos;ve said in the past should be any indication of my positions in the future&quot;), Donald Trump on &quot;Fox and Friends,&quot; a very gross make-out session, and music from the band fun.</p>
