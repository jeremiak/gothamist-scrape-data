<p>In case you&apos;ve been too busy forcibly holding your sheets over your head to have noticed, it&apos;s dreary out there today&#x2014;except for some remarkably <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130329003156/http://gothamist.com/2013/02/23/did_the_pope_resign_because_of_a_cr.php">scandalous Pope reports</a>, most of the city seems in a pre-Oscar coma. Contemplating brunch at 4 p.m. or another marathon of <em>House of Cards</em> does not make for a particularly inspiring day. Normally we&apos;d take a moment to encourage you to go out and embrace the world or something, but then someone might <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130329003156/http://gothamist.com/2013/02/23/dont_threaten_to_kill_l_train_rider.php">threaten you on the subway,</a> and we certainly don&apos;t want to be responsible for that. Instead, we offer you this supercut of babies laughing at dogs&#x2014;a viral opiate for the masses, a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130329003156/http://gothamist.com/tags/kateupton">Kate Upton dancing the Cat Daddy</a> for the SFW types. If this doesn&apos;t keep you in bed for 5 more minutes, then there&apos;s no hope for you.</p>
