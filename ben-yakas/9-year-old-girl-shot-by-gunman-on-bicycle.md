<p>A nine-year-old girl was shot by a gunman on a bicycle in the Bronx last night. Police say the incident happened around 9:30 p.m. at Bailey Avenue and West 234th Street in Kingsbridge. The girl was walking across the street towards her building when she was shot; police do not believe she was the intended target.</p>

<p>The girl <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130618051850/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/bronx/gunman-bike-shoots-9-year-old-girl-bronx-article-1.1373503">was taken</a> to Jacobi Medical Center with non life-threatening injuries, where she is listed as stable condition. The suspect is believed to be between 15-20; no arrests have been made yet.</p>
