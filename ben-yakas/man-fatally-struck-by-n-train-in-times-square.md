<p>A man was fatally struck by a train in midtown Manhattan this morning. According to FDNY, the incident happened around 8:15 a.m. at the Times Square subway station. The man, in his 40s, was struck by a northbound N train, and died at the scene. Police add that no criminality is suspected. </p>

<p>This is at least the third <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130319081420/http://gothamist.com/tags/subway">subway incident</a> this week: on Thursday, a man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130319081420/http://gothamist.com/2013/03/14/person_fatally_struck_by_train_at_b.php">was fatally struck</a> by a train at the Bryant Park station; and on Friday, a man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130319081420/http://gothamist.com/2013/03/15/man_struck_by_1_train_at_canal_stre.php">was struck</a> by a 1 train at Canal Street.</p>
