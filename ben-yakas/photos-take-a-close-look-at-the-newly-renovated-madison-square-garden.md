After three years of renovations and tense communiques, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173708/http://gothamist.com/tags/madisonsquaregarden">Madison Square Garden</a> unveiled its new look this week&#x2014;and from the looks of it, it was $1 billion well spent (for, er, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173708/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/25/msg_evicted.php">10 years or so worth of usage...</a>). Click through above to take a tour of the new MSG.</p>

<p>The most prominent new additions are the two <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173708/http://gothamist.com/2013/10/15/check_out_madison_square_gardens_ne.php#photo-1">&quot;Chase Bridges,&quot;</a> sky bridges that hang from the Garden&apos;s ceiling (there&apos;s also a food and beverage station near those seats). In the third phase of renovations, the Garden ceiling was fully restored, a new GardenVision center-hung multimedia display was installed, and a new suites area with &#x201C;center stage&#x201D; views for concerts was added. </p>
