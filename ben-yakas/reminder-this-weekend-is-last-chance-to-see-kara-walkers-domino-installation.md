If you&apos;re looking for something fun to do this weekend now that all the fireworks have been snuffed out, this is your last chance to check out <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502143717/http://gothamist.com/tags/KaraWalker">Kara Walker&apos;s</a> impressive installation at the former Domino Sugar Refinery in Williamsburg. The centerpiece <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502143717/http://gothamist.com/2014/05/06/sneak_peek_at_kara_walkers_domino_i.php#photo-1">of the installation</a>, titled <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502143717/http://creativetime.org/projects/karawalker/">&quot;A Subtlety, or the Marvelous Sugar Baby,&quot;</a> is a sugar-coated sphinx-like figure that is 75.5-feet long, 35.5 feet high, and 26 feet wide. We can safely say that it needs to be seen in person (and the lines ain&apos;t that bad).</p>

<center><iframe src="//web.archive.org/web/20150502143717if_/http://instagram.com/p/qE5xYJrevK/embed/" width="612" height="710" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowtransparency="true"></iframe></center>

<p>You can see a video of Walker and her team <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502143717/http://gothamist.com/2014/05/23/video_watch_kara_walker_created_a_s.php">constructing it below.</a> It&apos;s open today and tomorrow from 12 to 6 p.m.</p>
