</div></div>					<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429152240/http://cityroom.blogs.nytimes.com/2013/06/12/spinning-on-citi-bikes/">NY Times raised a few eyebrows</a> this morning when it published a short Metropolitan Diary about stationary biking at a Citi Bike dock: <strong>&quot;The &#x201C;biker&#x201D; responded, &apos;Laugh if you want, but I never have to pay for a spin class again.&apos;&quot;</strong> It seems that the NY Times inadvertently stumbled upon a hot new trend that&apos;s been going on as long as Citi Bike has been available&#x2014;the New Yorker <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429152240/https://twitter.com/Dreymann/status/339467372710723584">even predicted it on a cover</a> earlier this month. Check out some photos and videos of some of these emboldened spinners.</p>
