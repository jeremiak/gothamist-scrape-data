<p>Yesterday marked the return of our favorite holiday-related <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141108064505/http://gothamist.com/2011/10/24/photos_more_dogs_in_costumes.php#photo-1">animal costume parade</a> of the year: the 22nd annual Tompkins Square Park Halloween Dog Parade. While <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141108064505/http://gothamist.com/2011/10/23/photos_adorable_return_of_tompkins.php#photo-1">last year&apos;s event</a> was full of OWS-related protest pooches, meat-dress clad Lady Gagas and even a dog dressed as Dave Mustane of Megadeth, we had quite a different array of costumes this year, including Mutt Romney, Evita, an ET homage, and...a dog with a face on its butt.</p>
