	<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left"> <img alt="2013_06_drunkdriving.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130708094421im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_06_drunkdriving.jpg" width="250" height="309"> <br> <i style=" width:250px; ;display:block"> Photograph by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130708094421/http://www.shutterstock.com/gallery-783130p1.html">Boris Sosnovyy</a>/Shutterstock</i></div> Three off-duty city employees were arrested yesterday for drunk driving-related charges. NYPD officer Elias Blunt, 30, was arrested for drunk driving after cops stopped him in Brooklyn around 6 p.m., and he refused to take a breath test. He has been charged with driving while intoxicated. <p></p>

<p>Then MTA Bus Driver Bernard Alexander, 47, was arrested and charged for refusing to take a breath test and driving while his ability was impaired around 11 p.m. in the Bronx. And since there&apos;s always room for one more, NYC Department of Corrections Captain Alcidnea Knupp, 48, was arrested after hitting two parked cars along Cross Bay Blvd. in South Ozone Park around 11:15 p.m. She was charged with resisting arrest and driving while ability impaired.</p>
