<p>A Manhattan man has been arrested for allegedly sexually abusing a toddler in Riverside Park. Police say that Jason Carroll, 30, was arrested Tuesday around 5:15 p.m. in the vicinity of Riverside Park (in the West 70&apos;s). He has been charged with a criminal sex act, sex abuse, and child endangerment for the incident. </p>

<p>According <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928051858/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/man-sexually-abused-toddler-uws-park-article-1.1467097">to the News</a>, a good Samaritan spotted him abusing the 2-year-old girl, and alerted a Parks Department employee, who then called the cops. Carroll, who lives near Columbus Circle, allegedly knows the toddler&#x2019;s mother. They add that cops located the toddler&apos;s stroller near the men&#x2019;s bathroom across from W. 75th Street.</p>
