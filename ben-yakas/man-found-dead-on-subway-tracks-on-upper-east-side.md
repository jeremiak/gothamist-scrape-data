<p>A man was found dead on the subway tracks on the Upper East Side early this morning. According to police, the 48-year-old man was found unconscious and unresponsive lying on the tracks of the 86th Street station around 3:30 a.m. Sunday. He was pronounced dead at the scene.</p>

<p>Police said the name of the victim would be released after the victim&apos;s family was notified. They couldn&apos;t say if the man was struck by a train, or if any criminality was suspected; the investigation is ongoing.</p>
