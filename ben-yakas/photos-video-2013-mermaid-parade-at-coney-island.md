                            <p><br>
For many months, it appeared that Hurricane Sandy may have ruined the annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928035259/http://gothamist.com/tags/mermaidparade">Mermaid Parade at Coney Island</a>, one of the most fun and colorful NYC traditions. But thanks to the efforts of Amanda Palmer and Coney Island USA, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928035259/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/29/its_official_the_mermaid_parade_is.php">the event was saved</a>, and New Yorkers were able to enjoy a beautiful, warm day parading around the boardwalk on Saturday in their weirdest facepaint and costumes. Check out a lot of photos above (some NSFW!) taken by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928035259/http://www.todseelie.com/">Tod Seelie</a>, and a video document of the event below.</p>
