</div></div>					<p>It was a perfect day <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170325190525/http://gothamist.com/2011/06/18/reminder_the_mermaid_parade_is_happ.php">yesterday</a> to be a fish out of water&#x2014;the annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170325190525/http://gothamist.com/tags/mermaidparade">Coney Island Mermaid Parade</a> rolled down the Boardwalk (err, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170325190525/http://gothamist.com/tags/coneyislandboardwalk">concretewalk</a>) during the beautiful afternoon. It was a colorful gathering of mermaid costumes, face painters, ambiguous sea creatures and revelers of every ilk. &quot;It&apos;s become a better and better show every year,&quot; Scott McBride, who was wearing purple jeans, black bat wings and a ruffled turquoise shirt, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170325190525/http://www.nydailynews.com/ny_local/2011/06/19/2011-06-19_fin_fans_flock_to_sea_coney_isle_mermaids.html?r=ny_local&amp;utm_s">told the News</a>.
