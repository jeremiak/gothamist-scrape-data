</div></div>					<p><br>
After last week&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427175112/http://gothamist.com/2013/02/10/videos_justin_bieber_and_his_belieb.php#photo-1">mostly terrible (and very GLICE) Justin Bieber episode</a>, we were feeling pretty depressed about the current state of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427175112/http://gothamist.com/tags/snl"><em>Saturday Night Live</em></a>&#x2014;three bad episodes in a row can really do that to you. Thankfully, things were much improved this week, and a lot of that is thanks to the effervescent skills of host Christoph Waltz, who brought some much needed energy and excitement.</p>

<p>Waltz was particularly great in an excellent Tarantino parody, <em>Djesus Uncrossed</em>, which included nods to all of his films (Taran Killam as Brad Pitt from <em>Inglorious Basterds</em> hunting &#x201C;Ro-Mans&#x201D; and Kenan Thompson as a perfect <em>Pulp Fiction</em>-era Ving Rhames); as the adopted third Jamarcus Brothers who definitely knows a few things about where the sex is supposed happen; and as the game show host of the existential &quot;What Have YOU Become?&quot;</p>

<p>Click through for those highlights, plus the cold open on the disaster poop cruise, desperately thirsty Marco Rubio on an overall great Weekend Update, a Papal Securities ad, &quot;Casual Hitler,&quot; the return of &quot;Fox and Friends,&quot; Waltz as a well-meaning but creepy guard with an office crush, and performances by the Alabama Shakes.<br>
</p>
