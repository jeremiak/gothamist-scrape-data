<p>Hundreds of people in their knickers, skivvies, and pantaloons bum-rushed the subway today as part of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115144452/http://improveverywhere.com/missions/the-no-pants-subway-ride/">Improv Everywhere&apos;s</a> annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115144452/http://gothamist.com/tags/nopants">No Pants Subway Ride</a>. One reader who participated in the event said, &quot;They asked who the oldest and youngest people were in the group. The oldest was 76, the youngest was 3.&quot; We&apos;ll have more pictures tomorrow, and if you have your own shots from today&apos;s event, send them to us at photos[at]gothamist[dot]com, or upload them to Flickr and tag them &quot;gothamist.&quot;</p>
