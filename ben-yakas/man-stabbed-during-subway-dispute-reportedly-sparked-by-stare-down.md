<p>A man was stabbed during a &quot;dispute&quot; on the subway this morning in Manhattan. According to police, a 27-year-old man sustained stab wounds after a &quot;dispute on the train&quot; around 9:45 a.m. The victim was stabbed in the left arm and left rib on the southbound D train somewhere between the Bronx and Columbus Circle. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130607143836/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9104987">According to ABC</a>, he reportedly had gotten into a &quot;stare down&quot; with another person on the train as they rode from the Bronx. The victim got off the train at 59th Street/Columbus Circle and walked to nearby Roosevelt Hospital where he was treated. Cops added that no one has been arrested in the incident. </p>
