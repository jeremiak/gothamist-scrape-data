										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left" style=" width:280px; "> <img alt="12311fakebeard.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429162924im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/byakas/12311fakebeard.jpg" width="280" height="210"> <br> <i>Surveillance shot of the suspect, sans disguise (via CBS)</i></div> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429162924/http://cityroom.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/12/03/man-in-fake-beard-fires-at-officers-in-queens-police-say/?partner=rss&amp;emc=rss">Police are searching for</a> an oddly-dressed man who shot at cops in Queens yesterday afternoon. Two uniformed rookie officers were patrolling in the vicinity of 76th Street and Roosevelt Avenue in Jackson Heights around 4 p.m. when they came in contact with the suspect, who they say was acting suspiciously and wearing a black trench coat with a fake beard, a hat and dark glasses. They approached him and asked for identification: &#x201C;They kept asking him to take his hands out of his pocket, and after a struggle, he pulled [a revolver] out and just opened fire point-blank,&#x201D; a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429162924/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/queens/fake_beard_gunman_fires_on_qns_cops_pHN2gicXsj2GAtpimaWLaP?CMP=OTC-rss&amp;FEEDNAME=">source told the Post.</a><p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429162924/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2011/12/02/man-dressed-in-fake-beard-hat-and-dark-glasses-shoots-at-nypd-officers-in-queens/">Police say</a> two shots were fired, and neither hit the officers&#x2014;one bullet did go into a nearby dental office packed with kids though. The officers pursued the man, but he got away; his fake beard, hat and glasses were recovered. The suspect is described as a male in his 30s standing about 5-foot-9. According to the Times, a police report about the incident described the man as &#x201C;well dressed.&#x201D;</p>
