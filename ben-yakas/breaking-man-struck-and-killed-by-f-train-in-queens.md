<p>A man was fatally struck by an F train in Queens this morning. According to the FDNY, the incident happened at 11:13 a.m. at the Roosevelt Avenue / 74th Street station in Jackson Heights. Officials say the unidentified individual was pronounced DOA at the scene. </p>

<p>&quot;Person just hit by F train at Roosevelt Ave stop. Victim still under train (?). Cops clearing platform,&quot; said the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130326204846/https://twitter.com/ShantRS/status/304264787708223488">user who tweeted</a> the above photo. </p>

<p>FDNY couldn&apos;t comment on how the person ended up on the tracks, but we&apos;ll update once we learn more. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130326204846/http://www.mta.info/status/serviceStatus.html">MTA adds</a> that there are delays on the E, F, M, &amp; R train service in both directions at this time. </p>
