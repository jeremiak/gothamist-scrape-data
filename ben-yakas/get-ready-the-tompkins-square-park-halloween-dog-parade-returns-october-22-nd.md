					<p>October is right around the corner, which means Halloween is right around the corner, which means DOG HALLOWEEN is even <em>more</em> right around the corner. The only thing better than morning-after costumed walks of shame are puppies dressed up as bumblebees, F trains, and Bernie Sanders (but not sexy nurses, you don&apos;t want your pup to be promoting <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108030743/https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/business/wp/2016/09/29/is-your-dogs-halloween-costume-sexist/?postshare=5791475170267307&amp;tid=ss_tw">sexist cliches</a>). To that end, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108030743/http://gothamist.com/tags/tompkinssquarehalloweendogparade">26th Annual Tompkins Square Dog Halloween Parade</a>, &quot;the largest dog costume parade in the world,&quot; will return in less than a month.</p>

<p>TV personality Giuliana Rancic will be the guest emcee for the event, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108030743/http://www.tompkinssquaredogrun.com/halloween/">which takes place</a> from noon to 3 p.m. at Tompkins Square Park on Saturday, October 22nd. No one has to pre-register and anyone (well, any dog in a costume) can participate in the runway portion of the event: &quot;Suggested Donations is $5 at the door. A parade of all contestants and spectators will follow after Best In Show is announced.&quot;</p>
