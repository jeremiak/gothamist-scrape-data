<p>Yeah, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160111145747/http://gothamist.com/2011/07/17/video_gas_mask_dancing_on_the_subwa.php">we&apos;ve seen</a> some <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160111145747/http://gothamist.com/2008/12/31/video_underground_dancer.php">pretty good</a> dancing on the subways before. But The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160111145747/http://thedailywh.at/2011/09/13/omg-adorbz-of-the-day-43/">Daily What</a> pointed us in the direction of some dance-moves-to-rule-them-all in the adorable <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160111145747/http://thecounterculture.tumblr.com/post/10178613670/thedailywhat-omg-adorbz-of-the-day-keaton">video below</a>: &#x201C;This little boy was rocking the F out for the entire ride from Chambers St. to Myrtle-Broadway, dancing for nearly a half an hour, without a care in the world. Every time he put his hand on his chest like Usher I cracked up.&#x201D; The kid sings, sways, fans and finger points as unselfconsciously as is humanely possible&#x2014;<em>and</em> his mother ties his shoe laces. Can a subway ride get any better?</p>
