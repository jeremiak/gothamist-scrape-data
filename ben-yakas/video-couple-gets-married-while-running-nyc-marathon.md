<p>Besides <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160228172453/http://gothamist.com/2011/11/06/video_its_sunday_is_your_dog_in_a_b.php">Baby Bjorn dogs</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160228172453/http://gothamist.com/2011/11/06/occupy_wall_street_gets_free_health.php">doggy healthcare</a>, NYC was all about the 42nd annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160228172453/http://gothamist.com/2011/11/06/2011_ing_nyc_marathon_takes_over_bi_1.php">NYC Marathon</a> today. Geoffrey Mutai of Kenya <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160228172453/http://gothamist.com/2011/11/06/firehiwot_dado_geoffrey_mutai_win_2.php#photo-1">won the men&apos;s race</a>, breaking the previous record by over a minute-and-a-half, and Firehiwot Dado of Ethiopia won the women&apos;s race. One person had <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160228172453/http://gothamist.com/2011/11/06/marathon_runner_has_apparent_heart.php">an apparent heart attack</a>, and one couple even got married while running! Watch video of those crazy, middle-aged lovebirds below:</p>
