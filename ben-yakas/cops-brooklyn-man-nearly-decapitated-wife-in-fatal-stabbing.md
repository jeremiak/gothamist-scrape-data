<p>A Brooklyn man nearly decapitated his wife during a fatal stabbing this morning. Cops told us the incident happened around 11:45 a.m. at E. 102nd Street in Canarsie. They found 51-year-old Hazel Robinson unconscious and unresponsive with &quot;severe injuries to her neck.&quot; She was pronounced DOA at the scene. </p>

<p>A 58-year-old man, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130326202640/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/brooklyn-man-decapitated-wife-cops-article-1.1266458">whom the Daily News identifies</a> as her husband, has been taken into custody. No charges have been filed yet. The identity of the victim hasn&apos;t been released, and police say that the investigation is ongoing.<br>
