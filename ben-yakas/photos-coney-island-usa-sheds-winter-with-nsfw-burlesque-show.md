                            <p>On Saturday night, Webster Hall opened its gates to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928035003/http://www.coneyisland.com/">Coney Island USA</a> for its <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928035003/http://www.coneyisland.com/gala.shtml">annual fundraiser event</a>, the Coney Island USA Spring Gala. It was an evening filled with burlesque performances, fire-eating, and a theatrical performance based on founder Dick Zigun&apos;s <em>The Burlesque Manifesto</em>, all to raise money to repair damages from Hurricane Sandy. </p>
