<p>As if you weren&apos;t already psyched <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140205195100/http://gothamist.com/2013/12/26/score_free_tickets_to_de_blasios_ro.php">about scoring front row tickets</a> to Mayor-elect <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140205195100/http://gothamist.com/tags/billdeblasio">Bill de Blasio&apos;s</a> artisanal inauguration, the special guests for the show have just been announced: former President Bill Clinton and former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton will make cameos, with Bill swearing BDB in as the 109th Mayor of NYC. </p>

<p>&quot;I was honored to serve in President Clinton&apos;s Administration and on Secretary Clinton&apos;s campaign for U.S. Senate, and I am honored again that they will both join our celebration for all of New York City,&quot; BDB said in a press release. &quot;Wednesday&apos;s ceremony will be an event for every New Yorker from all five boroughs, and Chirlane and I couldn&apos;t be more excited to have President Clinton and Secretary Clinton stand with us.&quot; President Clinton will swear in BDB using a bible once owned by President Franklin Delano Roosevelt, which could only be topped by Bubba busting out <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140205195100/http://www.nj.com/warrenreporter/index.ssf/2013/12/centenary_stage_hosts_internat.html">Igor Butman&apos;s</a> sax and sealing the deal with a little <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140205195100/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=izGwDsrQ1eQ">&quot;Careless Whisper.&quot;</a> </p>

<p>In other BDB news, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140205195100/http://nypost.com/2013/12/28/de-blasios-going-slow-on-filling-team/">Post notes that</a> de Blasio is on track to make the fewest appointments entering City Hall of any mayor in decades. With that taking some time, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140205195100/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/politics/mayor-elect-bloomberg-officials-stay-article-1.1560108">BDB has asked</a> some of Mayor Bloomberg&#x2019;s commissioners to stay past Inauguration Day because their replacements have not been selected. This is probably the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140205195100/http://www.citybiz.com/newyorkcity/2013/12/23/why-it-matters-that-bill-de-blasio-is-always-late/">least surprising BDB news</a> to ever exist&#x2014;so who is taking on bets on what time he shows up to the noon inauguration? </p>
