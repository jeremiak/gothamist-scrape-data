<p>In case you were confused by Google putting up their commemorative <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502151754/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TYHCeUfoAnw&amp;feature=youtu.be">Lennon doodle</a> a day early, today would have been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502151754/http://gothamist.com/tags/johnlennon">John Lennon&apos;s</a> 70th birthday. And there are tributes to the former Beatle going up all over the world: Yoko Ono, Ringo Starr, Brian Wilson, Run DMC and many, many others put up video tributes to Lennon <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502151754/http://www.youtube.com/johnlennon#p/u">on YouTube</a>. There are official birthday events happening in diverse locations such as Cleveland, Liverpool, and Reykjavik. In NYC, you can see the Lennon documentary, &#x201C;John Lennon: A Journey in the Life,&#x201D; at the Paley Center for Media at w 52nd st, or gather at the Strawberry Fields memorial in Central Park near w 72nd st. Tonight, you can head to Central Park to attend <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502151754/http://gothamist.com/2010/09/25/new_lennon_in_nyc_documentary_to_pr.php">a free screening</a> of the new Lennon-in-NYC documentary, <em>LENNONYC</em>.</p>

<p>Below, you can see a vintage clip of Lennon performing a fiery, lively rendition of the rock standard &quot;Stand By Me.&quot; </p>
