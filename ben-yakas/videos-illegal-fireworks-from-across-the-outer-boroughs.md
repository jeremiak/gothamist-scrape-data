<p>Mayor Bloomberg and his special <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813085307/http://gothamist.com/tags/fireworks">Fireworks Crackdown Division</a> have been doing their darndest to track down every sparkler peddler in the five boroughs, even if it meant driving down <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813085307/http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2013/07/fdny_firework-buying_road_trip.html">to the sixth borough.</a> They <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813085307/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/04/photo_nypd_bust_queens_man_with_mas.php">made 48 seizures</a> leading up to the 4th, but that wasn&apos;t enough to stop many NYers from setting off fireworks in their neighborhoods. Check out some videos of illegal fireworks below, and everyone be grateful they <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813085307/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D5jI92Ht99Y">still have their fingertips.</a></p>
