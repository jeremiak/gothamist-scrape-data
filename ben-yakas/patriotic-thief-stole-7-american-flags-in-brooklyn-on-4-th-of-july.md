<p>While most New Yorkers were content to celebrate America&apos;s birthday with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140707134106/http://gothamist.com/tags/fireworks">fireworks displays</a> both legal and illegal, at least one overt patriot needed a little more stimulation to get in the spirit of the holiday. Police say bicycle-riding thief stole American flags from seven different Brooklyn homes on Independence Day morning. You can see a video of the suspect below.</p>

<p><iframe src="//web.archive.org/web/20140707134106if_/http://player.vimeo.com/video/99982181" width="640" height="432" frameborder="0" webkitallowfullscreen="" mozallowfullscreen="" allowfullscreen></iframe> </p><p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140707134106/http://vimeo.com/99982181">1359-14 72 pct</a> from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140707134106/http://vimeo.com/gothamist">Gothamist</a> on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140707134106/https://vimeo.com/">Vimeo</a>.</p><p></p>

<p>Cops say that the suspect rode up on a bicycle and removed American flags from residential homes in the vicinity of Sherman Street and 10th Avenue around 5:30 a.m. on Friday. He is described as a white, male, wearing blue denim shorts and a bandana, and carrying two backpacks.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto <a href="WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM">the Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
