	<p><br>

It was beautiful and warm outside, but the ground was still a bit of a mess. However, with a strong lineup featuring Nas, Guns N&apos; Roses, the re-scheduled Kings Of Leon, Animal Collective, Kendrick Lamar, Cut Copy, Dirty Projectors and more, it was a much more successful outing. Check out some videos of those performers below, as well as 28 photos of the festivities above.</p>
