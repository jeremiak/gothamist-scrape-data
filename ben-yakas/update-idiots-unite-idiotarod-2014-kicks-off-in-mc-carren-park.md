The 10th annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150103063022/http://gothamist.com/tags/idiotarod">Idiotarod</a>&#x2014;or as it&apos;s now known thanks to some humorless Alaskans, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150103063022/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/24/idiotarod_forced_to_change_name_sti.php">the Idiotarodorama NYC</a>&#x2014;kicked off today at noon in McCarren Park. The annual gathering of costumed shopping cart enthusiasts will wind its way through Brooklyn throughout the day. We like these creative &apos;idiots&apos; a lot more <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150103063022/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/25/video_daring_idiots_record_themselv.php">than certain other idiots</a>, so check out some photos from the start line above. </p>

<p>You can follow the route and events all day via their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150103063022/https://twitter.com/IdiotarodNYC">Twitter account</a>, including all the different bar stops&#x2014;the first one is at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150103063022/https://twitter.com/IdiotarodNYC/status/427131411255291904">Red Star on Greenpoint Avenue at 1 p.m.</a>. We&apos;ll update with more locations, and have a full roundup of photos from the event tomorrow. </p>
