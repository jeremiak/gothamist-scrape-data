</div></div>					<p>Yesterday, we were indoors writing about <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150201094550/http://gothamist.com/2011/01/27/form_and_function_the_bagel_scarf_b.php">bagel scarves</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150201094550/http://gothamist.com/2011/01/27/ny_post_now_simply_publishing_anti-.php">anti-bike lane</a> love letters, and were understandably grumpy that we missed out on all the lazy snow day fun. But we did find some vicarious joy in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150201094550/http://gothamist.com/2011/01/27/photos_venus_de_snowmilo_and_friend.php">all of the pictures</a> of &quot;snowfigures&quot; made by readers, including one particularly neat Venus de SnowMilo. Even better, some readers and snow enthusiasts took part in the first ever &quot;snow sculpt-off&quot; at the High Line during the snowcation. So take a look at some of the cool designs, including the snow dragon, octopus, rats, Empire State Building, and more!</p>
