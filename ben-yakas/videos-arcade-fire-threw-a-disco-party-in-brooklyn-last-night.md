<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429153546/http://gothamist.com/tags/arcadefire">Arcade Fire</a> performed the first of two <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429153546/http://gothamist.com/2013/10/17/tickets_for_arcade_fires_intimate_b.php">intimate live performances</a> as The Reflektors last night in Brooklyn. James Murphy introduced the band before a packed warehouse filled with be-costumed revelers at 299 Meserole. They played an enthusiastic but short 9 song set, including 7 new tunes from forthcoming record <em>Reflektor</em> and oldies &quot;Sprawl II&quot; and &quot;Neighborhood #3 (Power Out).&quot; In case you weren&apos;t lucky enough to snag a ticket to the exclusive event, you can check out lots of videos and photos from the disco-tinged event below. </p>
