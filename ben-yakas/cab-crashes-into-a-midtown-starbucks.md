<p>A tipster wrote us that a cab rammed into the Starbucks at the corner of 56th Street and 6th Avenue in midtown Manhattan a little before 10 a.m. this morning.  Check out some photographs of the damaged store and cab above&#x2014;one person <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150530045625/http://twitter.com/#!/A_Morescalchi/status/69767870158086144">Tweeted</a>, <strong>&quot;Yellow cab crashed into Starbucks window: espresso urge gone sour!!!&quot;</strong></p>

<p>Three years ago, a taxi and another car <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150530045625/http://gothamist.com/2008/07/11/car_swerves_into_starbucks_near_lin.php">crashed into</a> each other at Broadway and West 63rd Street, sending one vehicle spinning into a Starbucks and hitting a pedestrian.</p>
