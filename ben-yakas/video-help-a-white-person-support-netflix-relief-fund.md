<p>With every good thing, there comes a grave price: just as Netflix has started <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425173003/http://gothamist.com/2011/04/06/netflix_will_be_your_mad_men_fix_th.php">showing <em>Mad Men</em></a>, so too have they drastically and unjustly <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425173003/http://gothamist.com/2011/07/26/netflix_suprised_more_people_werent.php">raised their prices</a>. Tears are falling on Macbook Pros and Pier One pillows as we type&#x2014;now, how will white people get to watch every movie where Jason Statham plays a hitman? Thankfully, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425173003/http://www.funnyordie.com/videos/15be7bfd8f/netflix-relief-fund-with-jason-alexander">Netflix Relief Fund</a> is here to help. Watch below:</p>

<p>Obviously, the Netflix Relief Fund has already started helping white people, as it seems they&apos;ve somehow been able to grow Jason Alexander some new hair! However, this is still only the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425173003/http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&amp;v=X_z3RTwIWOw">second-best fake charity</a> which Alexander has been tied to.</p>
