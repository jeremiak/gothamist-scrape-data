<p>With the NBA currently <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150222074109/http://nba-point-forward.si.com/2011/07/27/nba-owners-players-likely-to-meet-next-week/">in a lockout</a> that is threatening to derail the entire 2011-2012 season, many players have begun discussing the possibility of playing overseas in the interim. Amare Stoudemire said he had <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150222074109/http://www.allheadlinenews.com/articles/90055361?Knicks%20star%20Amare%20Stoudemire%20says%20foreign%20clubs%20have%20come%20calling">already been approached</a> by teams from Israel, China, Turkey, and Spain. But it seems another Knick has beaten Stat to China, and he loves hanging out <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150222074109/http://blogs.thescore.com/tbj/2011/07/28/carmelo-anthony-meets-a-panda/?utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+TheBasketballJones+%28The+Basketball+Jones%29">with pandas</a>.</p>

<p>Knicks forward Carmelo Anthony is on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150222074109/http://www.businessinsider.com/heres-a-photo-of-carmelo-anthony-chillin-with-a-panda-to-kick-off-your-day-2011-7">a promotional tour of China</a> with fellow NBA star (and future Knick?) Chris Paul, and took some time out to go hang out with some panda bears. As one Gothamist writer enthused, &quot;he looks like he&apos;s trying to be smug to cover up <strong>the childlike glee he&apos;s feeling</strong>.&quot; And who could blame him? Everyone knows <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150222074109/http://gothamist.com/tags/panda">pandas</a> are the best.</p>

<p>But here&apos;s the question: whose panda shot is better? Anthony&apos;s, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150222074109/http://www.nbahorn.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Kevin-Durant-Panda-Bear-e1298332261853.jpg">Kevin Durant&apos;s</a>, or <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150222074109/http://www.buzzfeed.com/mjs538/shaq-holding-a-panda">Shaq&apos;s?</a></p>
