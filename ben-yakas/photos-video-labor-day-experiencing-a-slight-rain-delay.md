<p>Well, this is really going to put a crimp in our <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131007065907/http://gothamist.com/tags/daggering">daggering plans</a>: the National Weather Service issued a flash flood warning for Manhattan, Queens, The Bronx and Brooklyn this afternoon. It&apos;s already flooding in parts of Queens, as you can see in videos and photos below, delaying US Open matches. The warning lasts at least through 3 p.m., so try to stay off the roads if you can and plan ahead accordingly&#x2014;at the same time, this is <em>just</em> some rain. Don&apos;t let yourself be freaked out and bullied by your cell phone like <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131007065907/https://twitter.com/adamlevine/status/374578533555007488">Adam Levine.</a></p>
