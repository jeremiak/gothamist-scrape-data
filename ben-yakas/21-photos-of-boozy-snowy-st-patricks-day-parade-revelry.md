	<p><br>
The weather was certainly less-than-perfect, but a little snow, rain and bitter cold couldn&apos;t stop over a million people from taking to Fifth Avenue on Saturday to watch the 252nd <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604142227/http://gothamist.com/tags/stpatricksday">St. Patrick&apos;s Day Parade</a>. There were bag pipes, Bloomberg, kilts, green make-up and spandex, and even <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604142227/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/manhattan/erin_go_brrr_KcCJecmd2bD0fdW6x7xS3K?utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_content=Manhattan">an NYPD proposal</a>. And afterwards, there were plenty of people who kept the party going at various bars around Manhattan. Since you probably <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604142227/http://gothamist.com/2013/03/15/map_how_to_avoid_the_st_paddys_day.php">took our advice</a> and did your best to avoid those crowds, take a look at the photos above from the East Village to see what you missed out on. </p>
