<p>A Long Island mother <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130507235714/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/05/05/cops-li-mother-left-4-kids-unattended-in-car-as-she-drank-at-a-bar/">has been accused</a> of leaving her four kids in the car while she went out drinking at a bar last night. Police say that Maria Cepparo, 40, left her four young kids in an unlocked car while she drank at Momo&#x2019;s Sports Bar and Grill in Holbrook. </p>

<p>The four kids&#x2014;including her 13-year-old son who is autistic, 8-year-old son and twin 6-year-old daughters&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130507235714/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Long-Island-Woman-Arrested-Leaving-Children-Alone-in-Car-While-Drinking-Holbrook-Bar-Suffolk-County-206193031.html">were allegedly found</a> by police just after midnight Sunday outside in a 2012 Ford Focus with no heat and the doors unlocked. The car was also parked illegally in a fire zone. The kids are now with their grandmother. Cepparo has been charged with endangering the welfare of a child.</p>
