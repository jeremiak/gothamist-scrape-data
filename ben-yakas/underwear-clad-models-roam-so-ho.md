</div></div><p>A group of models hit the streets of SoHo <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130506105455/http://www.nydailynews.com/ny_local/2010/08/27/2010-08-27_sexy_models_prance_around_soho_wearing_nothing_but_their_undies.html">in their underwear yesterday</a>, turning heads and cameras alike. They ran around the streets near Crosby St. and Broadway, jumping over and around boxes and crates, while being videotaped by filmmaker Jason Stein for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130506105455/http://www.desigual.com/">Desigual</a>. The company&apos;s rep <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130506105455/http://www.nickydigital.com/index.php">said</a> they were not shooting an underwear commercial though. </p>

<p>According to the Daily News, gawkers, mawkers, pedestrians and hanger-ons were encouraged to take pictures. Nick Gannascoli, 40, enjoyed it: &quot;It is like being at the beach but in Manhattan. A little beauty never hurt anybody.&quot; Not that you can please everyone; Gina Curatolo, a 46-year-old Canadian tourist, said, &quot;This is a disgrace. Everything has to be taken to the 10th degree.&quot; We respectfully disagree, ma&apos;am. FYI&#x2014;the gallery is all SFW, since everyone&apos;s in their skivvies.</p>
