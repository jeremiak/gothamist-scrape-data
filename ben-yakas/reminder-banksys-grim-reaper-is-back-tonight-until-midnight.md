<p>In case you didn&apos;t have a chance to swing by Houston and Elizabeth Streets last night to see <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131203044842/http://gothamist.com/tags/banksy">Banksy&apos;s</a> bumper car Grim Reaper bring some holiday cheer to lower Manhattan, fret not! The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131203044842/http://gothamist.com/2013/10/25/new_banksy_is_at_elizabeth_and_hous.php#photo-1">skeletal Blue Oyster Cult aficionado</a> will be at the spot from dusk until midnight tonight and tomorrow night. Check out a fantastic video below of last night&apos;s event, via <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131203044842/https://vimeo.com/user323322">Matt Adams</a>:</p>

<p>And not to get all fatalistic or anything, but life is fleeting and we only have five more days of surprise Banksy projects left. It&apos;s been a hell of a fun month, and we probably won&apos;t get another citywide art scavenger hunt like this anytime soon, so let&apos;s all soak in our last days with ol&apos; Bankster.</p>
