<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140226200822/http://gothamist.com/tags/snl"><em>Saturday Night Live</em></a> can&apos;t stop, won&apos;t stop adding new faces this season: first there were the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140226200822/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/16/snl_adds_new_cast_members_anoints_c.php">six new castmembers</a>, then they added <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140226200822/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/06/snl_casts_black_female_cast_member.php">Sasheer Zamata</a> and two <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140226200822/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/08/saturday_night_live_also_adds_two_f.php">new female black writers</a>, and now they&apos;ve announced the newest Weekend Update co-anchor. Current head writer Colin Jost <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140226200822/http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/snl-names-new-weekend-update-673533?utm_term=hollywoodreporter_breakingnews&amp;utm_content=bufferc7397&amp;utm_medium=social&amp;utm_source=twitter.com&amp;utm_campaign=buffer">will take Seth Meyer&apos;s spot</a> and join Cecily Strong behind the desk after Meyer&apos;s final appearance on Feb. 1st.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20140226200822if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/1N9v6Cj2Wjw" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Jost, a 2004 graduate from Harvard University who was also president of the Harvard Lampoon, has been a writer on the show since 2005, and head writer since 2012. He wrote and stars in the film <em>Staten Island Summer</em>, coming out later this year, which apparently features <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140226200822/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iRqyp2jZWiA">Ashley Greene in a bikini</a>. And he also has been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140226200822/http://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/news/rashida-jones-steps-out-with-boyfriend-colin-jost-fellow-harvard-alum-picture-2013910">dating Rashida Jones</a>, as if you needed another reason to hate him.</p>
