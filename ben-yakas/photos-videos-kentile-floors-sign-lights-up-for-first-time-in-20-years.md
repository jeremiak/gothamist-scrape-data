The iconic Kentile Floor sign in Gowanus was lit up for one night only on Saturday, the first time since 1992. If you didn&apos;t get a chance to get over there and enjoy the 3D projection show&#x2014;which was put on by filmmaker Tim Bartlett with George Del Barrio of the Vanderbilt Republic and Karl Mehrer of K2imaging&#x2014;plenty of other people did share their Instagrammable moments. Check out some solid videos and photos of the show below, including a very clear video from photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502121819/http://barryyanowitz.com/photostream/2014/5/4/kentilelet-there-be-light">Barry Yanowitz.</a>.</p>
