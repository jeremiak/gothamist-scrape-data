                            <p>Two workers became trapped in broken scaffolding atop a building in midtown Manhattan this afternoon. The FDNY is currently attempting to rescue the two window washers, who were working on the 46-story Hearst Tower, located at 57th Street and Eighth Avenue, when the scaffolding broke. You can watch live video of the rescue in progress below, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928032852/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/hearst-tower-rescue-midtown-fire-deparment-211249151.html?_osource=SocialFlowTwt_NYBrand">via NBC</a>.</p>
