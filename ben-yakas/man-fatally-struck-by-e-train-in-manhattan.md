<p>A man was fatally struck by an E train in Manhattan this morning, making it the seventh subway-related incident in the past five days. According to the MTA, the incident happened around 9:30 a.m. at the W. 23rd Street A/C/E station; the man was struck by a southbound E train. NYPD added that they believe the person was &quot;a jumper.&quot;</p>

<p>FDNY said they were still at the scene. The MTA adds that C trains are running express from 59th Street to Canal Street, and southboung E trains are running express from 50th Street to Canal Street. </p>

<p>On Wednesday, a man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130330084654/http://gothamist.com/2013/02/20/breaking_person_reportedly_struck_a.php">was fatally struck</a> by an F train at the Roosevelt Avenue / 74th Street station in Jackson Heights; another man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130330084654/http://gothamist.com/2013/02/20/breaking_another_person_struck_by_a.php">was injured</a> after falling onto the tracks at Columbus Circle that day. On Thursday, another person <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130330084654/http://gothamist.com/2013/02/21/person_fatally_struck_by_train_in_q.php">was fatally struck</a> by an F train in Queens. On Friday morning, a man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130330084654/http://gothamist.com/2013/02/22/reports_woman_struck_by_1_train_on.php">was struck</a> by a 1 train on the Upper West Side; that evening, a man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130330084654/http://gothamist.com/2013/02/23/man_fatally_struck_by_subway_at_pen.php">was fatally struck</a> by a 2 train at Penn Station. And last night, a man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130330084654/http://gothamist.com/2013/02/24/mans_legs_severed_by_subway_after_f.php">had his legs severed</a> after falling onto the tracks and being struck by a train at Yankee Stadium.</p>
