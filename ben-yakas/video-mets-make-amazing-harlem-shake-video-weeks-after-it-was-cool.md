It is wholly appropriate that the Mets would make a &quot;Harlem Shake&quot; video&#x2014;a damn good one, at that&#x2014;weeks after everyone stopped making (and caring about) &quot;Harlem Shake&quot; videos. It is equally appropriate that the whole thing is led by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429062647/http://online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424127887323605404578380592367577484.html">butt dialer extraordinaire</a> Jay Horwitz, the team&apos;s beloved longtime director of media relations. Is this video a cry for attention? Is it a sleight of hand to distract you from the fact you can&apos;t name a single Mets outfielder? Or did Horwitz just learn about the &quot;Harlem Shake&quot; this week? Watch and decide.</p>
