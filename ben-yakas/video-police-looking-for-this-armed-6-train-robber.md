<p>Police are looking a man who robbed a straphanger at gunpoint on a 6 train this week. The incident happened Wednesday morning around 10:30 a.m. on a northbound 6 train at the 103rd Street subway station. The suspect approached the 35-year-old victim, pointed a firearm at him and demanded his personal property. Below, you can see surveillance video of the suspect at a convenience store:</p>

<p><iframe src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150424005343if_/http://player.vimeo.com/video/48656156?title=0&amp;byline=0&amp;portrait=0" width="640" height="480" frameborder="0" webkitallowfullscreen="" mozallowfullscreen="" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The robber took the victim&apos;s wallet and cell phone, then fled the subway at the 110th Street and Lexington Avenue stop. Police describe the suspect as 20-25 years old, 5&apos;8&quot;-5&apos;9&quot;, 160 lbs. Anyone with information in regards to this gunpoint robbery is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). </p>
