A series of manhole explosions occurred in SoHo this afternoon, setting several cars on fire. The explosions happened just after 2 p.m. at 94 Prince Street, near Mercer Street. FDNY said that the fire is under control as of 2:43 p.m., there were no injuries, and &quot;there&apos;s going to be an investigation&quot; into what started the explosions. There were minor evacuations due to raised CO levels, but they&apos;re back to normal now; they also speculated that the cars may have been positionied over the manholes. You can check out some Tweets from the scene below. </p>
