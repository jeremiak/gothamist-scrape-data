</div></div>					<p>Steven Soderbergh is really handling retirement well: despite <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150630015234/http://www.vulture.com/2013/01/steven-soderbergh-in-conversation.html">giving up filmmaking to work on painting</a>, he&apos;s been hard at work transforming the Lower East Side into NYC of 1910 today for his latest project, <em>The Knick</em>. The Cinemax miniseries is set to star Clive Owen, and will tell the story of the Knickerbocker Hospital during the early 1900s. And Soderbergh <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150630015234/http://www.vulture.com/2013/05/soderbergh-to-produce-direct-show-for-cinemax.html">will reportedly direct</a> all the episodes of the miniseries!</p>
