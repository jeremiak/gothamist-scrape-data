title: Video: Woman Turns Herself In After Liquor Store Tantrum
---

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2011_02_liquorsto.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20110305155107im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2011_02_liquorsto.jpg" width="365" height="258" class="image-left"> </span>The Nyack woman who had a temper tantrum in a Rockland County liquor store, nonchalantly smashing more than $1,600 worth of alcohol, turned herself in, according <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110305155107/http://online.wsj.com/article/AP266435b0dd174ac79333525b6a776a4a.html">to police</a>. Mahogany Morrow, 31, of Spring Valley, became agitated because she felt a clerk was taking too long to wait on her at the Rite-Buy Wines &amp; Liquors in Nyack. So in retaliation for the loss of her precious time, she swept her arm through a shelf of high-priced booze. And the whole incident was caught on surveillance video, which you can watch below.</p>

<p>Chris Giacopelli, the son of the store&apos;s owner, lamented the loss of four Johnnie Walker Blue Label Scotches (worth $180 each) <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110305155107/http://www.lohud.com/article/20110304/NEWS03/103040349/Angry-rampage-Rite-Buy-Liquor-astounds-owner-destroys-1-600-booze?odyssey=tab|topnews|text|Frontpage">to LoHud</a>: &quot;Twenty-five years in the business and I&apos;ve never seen anything like it. Unbelievable, just unbelievable.&quot; </p>

<p>Altogether, he estimates that Morrow destroyed 51 bottles of liquor and wine, from Firefly Iced Tea-Flavored Vodka to Bacardi Dragon Berry-Flavored Rum.
