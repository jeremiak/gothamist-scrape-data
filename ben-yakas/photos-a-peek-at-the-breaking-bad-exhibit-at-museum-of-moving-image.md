</div></div>					<p>Earlier this summer, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429102149/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/11/breaking_bad_exhibit_coming_to_muse.php">it was announced</a> that the Museum of Moving Image in Queens would be hosting an exhibit to commemorate the end of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429102149/http://gothamist.com/tags/breakingbad"><em>Breaking Bad</em></a>: &quot;From Mr. Chips to Scarface: Walter White&apos;s Transformation in Breaking Bad.&quot; You still have until October 27th to head over to Astoria and check it out in person. But in the meantime, you can see some of the items on display&#x2014;including the pink teddy bear, Gus&apos;s box cutter, the Heisenberg sketch, and those iconic tighty whities&#x2014;in the photos above.</p>

<p>In other <em>Breaking Bad</em> news, the Saul Goodman prequel <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429102149/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/11/breaking_bad_spinoff_better_call_sa.php">has gotten the go-ahead</a>, Jimmy Fallon parodied the show <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429102149/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/12/jimmy_fallons_breaking_bad_parody_i.php">with &quot;Joking Bad,&quot;</a> New Mexico Goodwill will be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429102149/http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/wireStory/nm-goodwill-hold-donated-breaking-bad-auction-20249831">auctioning off memorabilia from the show</a>, actor Dean Norris <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429102149/http://www.rollingstone.com/movies/news/dean-norris-explains-hanks-moral-code-on-breaking-bad-20130912">thinks Hank hasn&apos;t broken bad</a>, New Yorkers are lining up for their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429102149/http://blogs.wsj.com/metropolis/2013/09/13/hat-in-hand-breaking-bad-fans-crowd-manhattan-haberdashery/">own Heisenberg-esque pork pie hats</a>, and this Sunday&apos;s episode is probably going to be insane and amazing.</p>
