Jeremy Renner was the host on last night&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150325052658/http://gothamist.com/tags/saturdaynightlive"><em>Saturday Night Live</em></a>, but the two stars of the show were the ongoing Petraeus scandal and NJ Gov. Chris Christie. SNL devoted no less than three segments to Petraeus: the cold open was Paula Broadwell reading excerpts from her <em>50 Shades</em>-esque book, Wolf Blitzer kept playing the same clip of Jill Kelley over and over, and Seth Meyers broke down the winners and losers: &#x201C;Sex scandals are like Jewish New Yorkers. Eventually they all make their way to Florida.&#x201D; </p>

<p>Christie dropped by Weekend Update to give a Sandy relief update, chide Meyers for being such a Seth Meyers, and quote NJ saint Bruce Springsteen. Click through to check out all those sketches, as well as an <em>Avengers</em> spoof, &quot;The Californians&quot; Thanksgiving celebration, a very long stand off digital short, Maroon 5 and more.</p>
