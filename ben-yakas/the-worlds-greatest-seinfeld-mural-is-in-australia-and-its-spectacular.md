<p>Australians seem to like <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161119143844/http://gothamist.com/tags/seinfeld"><em>Seinfeld</em></a> almost as much as New Yorkers do. Earlier this year, Dave Barrett <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161119143844/http://gothamist.com/2016/01/02/george_costanza-themed_bar_now_open.php">opened up &quot;George&apos;s Bar,&quot;</a> a Melbourne bar themed after everybody&apos;s favorite fictional neurotic Queens native. And now, a local artist has created a giant mural in honor of the show, with several standout moments, characters and catchphrases all over it.</p>

<p>Perth mural artist and illustrator named <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161119143844/https://twitter.com/eldeejo">Paul Deej</a> has created the giant canvas in Mount Lawley (more specifically, for anyone traveling to Perth anytime soon, &quot;The wall is behind the IGA on Beaufort St.&quot;). Here&apos;s a (teeny-tiny) banner of the entire thing all in a row:</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="111516seinfeld1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161119143844im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/byakas/111516seinfeld1.jpg" width="640" height="65"> <br> <i>(<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161119143844/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_arts_john/111516Seinfeld_Stitch.jpg">CLICK TO ENLARGE</a>) Paul Deej</i></div> <p></p>

<p>There is Jerry Seinfeld in the puffy shirt (&quot;But I don&apos;t wanna be a pirate&quot;), Tom&apos;s Restaurant, Elaine saying &quot;Yadda Yadda Yadda,&quot; George&apos;s philosophy on lying, Kramer&apos;s pimp mugshot, Yankee Stadium, flags for Vandelay Industries and Kramerica, Estelle with Frank Costanza shouting &quot;serenity now&quot; on the phone, George with a baseball bat, a be-caped Larry David as Frank&apos;s lawyer, Crazy Joe Davola in clown makeup, Newman with his famous mail quote, Elaine&apos;s little kicks, Puddy&apos;s 8 ball jacket, Jackie Chiles, the Soup Nazi, a giant Pez dispenser, Junior Mints, Suzanne licking the envelopes, a traditional Festivus pole, Bubble Boy with the &quot;Moops&quot; card, the back of George Steinbrenner&apos;s head, and Jerry&apos;s standup stage.</p>
