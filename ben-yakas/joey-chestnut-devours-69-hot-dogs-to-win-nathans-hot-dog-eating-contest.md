</div></div>					<p>America&apos;s hero Joey Chestnut SEVENpeated as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504174026/http://gothamist.com/tags/nathanshotdogeatingcontest">Nathan&apos;s Hot Dog Eating Champion</a> today in Coney Island. Chestnut, who was carried into the arena on a Nathan&apos;s-emblazoned throne, broke his own world record by <strike>eating</strike> demolishing 69 hot dogs and buns during the 10 minute competition. Or as the ESPN announcer put it after the final dog, <strong>&quot;with that swallow, history was made.&quot;</strong></p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="7413chest.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504174026im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/byakas/7413chest.jpg" width="640" height="360"> <br> <i> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504174026/https://twitter.com/bubbaprog/status/352829576315011073">via Timothy Burke</a></i></div> <p></p>

<p>Chestnut, 29, ate one more hot dog than he did last year to break his own record, and unseat Kobayashi as the all-time winningest Nathan&apos;s hot dog eater. Chestnut <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504174026/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/brooklyn/joey-chestnut-wins-hot-dog-contest-article-1.1390200">told the News that</a> he does not intend to retire: &#x201C;Seven is not the end. Ten is looking more and more likely.&#x201D; Sonya Thomas, known as &#x201C;The Black Widow,&#x201D; won the women&#x2019;s contest, eating 36 and three-quarters hot dogs and buns to Juliet Lee&#x2019;s 36 hot dogs. </p>
