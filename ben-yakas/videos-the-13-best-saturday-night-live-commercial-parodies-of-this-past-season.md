                            <p><br>
It was a mixed season (at best) for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130920000445/http://gothamist.com/tags/snl"><em>Saturday Night Live</em></a>, with more than a couple complete dud episodes (Vince Vaughn, Daniel Craig, Jeremy Renner, Justin Bieber) along with a lot of highly disappointing ones (Kristen Wiig, Jennifer Lawrence, Christina Applegate). But even when the writing on the sketches seemed to drag through one-note premises and regurgitated <em>Californians</em>, SNL never lost a step with their commercial parodies. </p>

<p>Often times, these sketches were highlights of the evening (the Mother&apos;s Day one from Wiig&apos;s episode, Z-Shirts from Kevin Hart&apos;s episode); sometimes they even dominated the show (there were three high quality ones in Joseph Gordon-Levitt&apos;s episode).
