<p>As we mentioned <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120119111619/http://gothamist.com/2011/06/24/stephen_nation_army_colbert_and_jac.php">earlier today</a>, Stephen Colbert and Jack White were at The High Line this morning selling copies of Colbert&apos;s brand new single  &quot;Charlene II (I&apos;m Over You).&quot; After a brief introduction by White (he described working with Colbert as &quot;like adding onto a sculpture in a museum&quot;), Colbert came out, gave a shout-out to Chelsea, and harmonized with White on The Star-Spangled Banner: <strong>&quot;Hands over your hearts, hats off motherfuckers.</strong>&quot; He then performed the single with the Black Belles. </p>
