After weeks of furry-ously <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110412172613/http://gothamist.com/2010/11/23/video_cookie_monster_wants_to_host.php">campaigning</a>, the Cookie Monster succeeded in his quest to <strike>host</strike> make an appearance on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110412172613/http://gothamist.com/tags/snl">Saturday Night Live</a> last night. He sang &quot;Silver Bells&quot; with host Jeff Bridges, who brought his affable presence (though not enough Lebowski charm) to the final show of 2010. The episode brought the usual mix of SNL sweet spots: a laugh-free political cold-open, an effervescent Miley Cyrus Show (with added salvia references), Bill Hader&apos;s fantastic Julian Assange (by-way-of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110412172613/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Count_von_Count">Count Von Count</a>) parody, and a team-up by Weekend Update all-stars Gov. Paterson, Stefon, and Snookie to sing &#x201C;O Christmas Tree.&#x201D; Click through to see those videos, plus a &quot;Crunkmas&quot; commercial, and the new Lonely Island digital short, &quot;I Just Had Sex,&quot; featuring cameos by Jessica Alba, Blake Lively, John McEnroe, and Akon (and serious, if we just had sex with Joanna Newsom, we&apos;d be shouting about it too). </p>
