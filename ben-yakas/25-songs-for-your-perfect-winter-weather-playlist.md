<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429215025/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/03/snow_gallery.php">winter storm</a> blew through town last night, leaving in its wake <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429215025/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/03/photos_cats_dogs_enjoy.php">happy pets</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429215025/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/03/breaking_dante_de_blasio_shoveled_t.php">happy children</a> and an impressively well-coordinated clean-up process. Oh, and freezing temperatures that will only get worse as the evening progresses. Whether you decide to heed <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429215025/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/03/de_blasio_winter_storm_update_serio.php">Mayor Striptease&apos;s advice</a> and not venture outside, or take a long stroll under the street lamps, or maybe just <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429215025/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/03/best_blizzard_photo_area_man_relaxe.php">lay back in your hot tub</a>, below you&apos;ll find 25 wonderful chilly weather songs to get the mood just right. </p>

<p>Note: We also think you can&apos;t go wrong with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429215025/http://www.youtube.com/channel/UCEBgE26BBZk7jnXKzcmVs0A">Phillip Glass</a>, or Kate Bush&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429215025/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8pocXNt9D64"><em>50 Words For Snow</em></a>, or Animal Collective&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429215025/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nagI_slIviA"><em>Merriweather Post Pavilion</em>.</a> But clearly there are too many wonderful songs in the universe to include all of them, so make sure to mention more favorites below. Except that stupid Red Hot Chili Peppers song. Nobody bring that one up.</p>
