</div></div>					<p><br>
Bruno Mars pulled double duty as host and musical guest on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150409201654/http://gothamist.com/tags/snl"><em>Saturday Night Live</em></a> last night. Sure, he was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150409201654/http://gothamist.com/2012/10/21/louis_ck_hosting_saturday_night_liv.php">no Louis C.K.</a>, but he definitely exceeded low expectations and did a fine job, even if he relied on musical-related sketches a bit too much. It ended up being one of the better episodes this season, with the biggest highlights the cold open about the second presidential debate, two Tom Hanks cameos, a series of creepy Brad Pitt commercials, and the hilarious return of Stefon.</p>

<p>Click through to check out those highlights, as well as a somewhat surreal Sad Rat sketch, Seth Meyer&apos;s do&apos;s and don&apos;ts for the presidential candidates, the Merryville robots, and some performances by Bruno. Unfortunately, a pretty good sketch (&#x201C;Pandora Internet Radio Systems Headquarters&#x201D;) in which Bruno had to do lots of musical impressions isn&apos;t online. <br>
