</div></div>					<p>Oh sure, once the dogs are on the green turf in the center of Madison Square Garden in front of all those cameras at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502175124/http://gothamist.com/tags/westminsterdogshow">Westminster Kennel Club Dog Show</a>, everyone looks like they could be the next cover star of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502175124/http://www.moderndogmagazine.com/">&quot;Modern Dog.&quot;</a> But for many of the pups, it takes a whole lot of primping and prepping to turn them into Rin Tin Tin. From the curlers that keep the dogs from peeing on themselves to the blow dryers and brushes that make their hair stand straight up, take a peek at how groomers and owners make their dogs look as good as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502175124/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=beOM4cJHfTw">Air Bud</a>.</p>
