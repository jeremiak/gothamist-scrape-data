<p>A man was struck by a train in Manhattan this afternoon. According to FDNY, the man was hit by a southbound 4/5 train at the Bowling Green subway station just after 1:50 p.m. He reportedly sustained facial injuries, but they are considered non life-threatening. </p>

<p>According <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717095608/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/06/06/person-struck-by-train-at-bowling-green-station/">to CBS</a>, the man apparently walked into the side of a departing 5 train at the station. FDNY added that the man was being transported to Beth Israel Hospital.</p>
