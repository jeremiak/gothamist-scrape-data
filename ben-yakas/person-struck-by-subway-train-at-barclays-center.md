<p>A person was struck by a train at the Atlantic Avenue-Barclays Center station today. According to FDNY, they are currently on scene at the station where a person was struck by a B train; one person has been injured, but it&apos;s not clear yet what the extent of those injuries are. We&apos;ll update when we learn more information. </p>

<p>As of 12:50 p.m., there are no B trains between Brighton Beach and 145 Street in both directions. Northbound Q trains are running on the N line from Coney Island-Stillwell Avenue to DeKalb Avenue. Southbound B and Q trains are bypassing Atlantic Avenue-Barclays Center. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131013020449/http://new.mta.info/">Check here</a> for updated MTA info.<br>
