<p>Brooklyn and Queens residents had to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150819122443/http://gothamist.com/2012/08/15/photos_brooklyn_queens_is_flooding.php#photo-1">get out their paddles</a> when flash floods swept through the boroughs midweek. But on Friday, surfers took out real paddles to circumnavigate Manhattan for the annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150819122443/http://www.seapaddlenyc.org/seapaddle-around-nyc">Sea Paddle</a> charity race. The 26.5 miles event raises money for autism charities and the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150819122443/http://www.seasurfer.org/">Surfers&apos; Environmental Alliance</a>&#x2014;click through to see lots of photos of men and women standing upright in the East River. </p>
