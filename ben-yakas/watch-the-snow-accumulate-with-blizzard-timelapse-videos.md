<p>While you were huddled inside last night <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140206154053/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/02/bad_weather_tipping.php">ordering moo shu pork</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140206154053/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/03/new_netflix_instant_movies.php">watching <em>Good Burger</em>,</a> snow was falling. And it was blowing around. And it was accumulating. We can confirm all this is true thanks to people like <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140206154053/http://controlgeek.net/blog/2014/1/3/brooklyn-snow-storm-time-lapse">John Huntington of ControlGeek</a>, who documented the storm with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140206154053/http://gothamist.com/tags/timelapse">timelapse videos</a>. Below, check out several such videos and see what you missed out on while you were sleeping.</p>
