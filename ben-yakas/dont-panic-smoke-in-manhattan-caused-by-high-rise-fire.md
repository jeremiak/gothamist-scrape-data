<p>We&apos;ve been getting reports of heavy smoke rising up over Manhattan, and chatter on Twitter has swiftly turned to mild panic: &quot;Enjoying an afternoon on the waterfront and notice smoke beginning to billow up from the Manhattan the skyline. Scary... #911&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509165228/http://twitter.com/#!/kelseymeuse/status/112604782576926721">tweeted @kelseymeuse</a>. But don&apos;t worry: there is a high rise fire at E 25th Street and Madison Avenue. The fire was caused by a transformer on the roof of the 32-floor building. </p>

<p>We know everyone&apos;s nerves are frayed this weekend, with the tenth anniversary of 9/11 and the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509165228/http://gothamist.com/2011/09/10/terror_threat.php">uncorroborated threat of another attack</a> hanging over our heads precariously. But we haven&apos;t gotten attacked by Al Qaeda, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509165228/http://twitter.com/#!/UNCLEPAULIENYC/status/112605441556627457">Uncle Paulie</a>. So let&apos;s all try to remember that trucks with Arabic writing on the side are probably not being driven by terrorists. And let&apos;s all agree to follow in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509165228/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phrases_from_The_Hitchhiker&apos;s_Guide_to_the_Galaxy#Don.27t_Panic">Douglas Adams&apos;s footsteps</a> for the rest of the weekend...<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509165228/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1horffGyO0">or Coldplay&apos;s, if that&apos;s more your thing. </a></p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509165228/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1horffGyO0">
