<p>Police are looking for two men who harassed a couple on a subway platform last week, then proceeded to beat and choke them.</p>

<p>The incident happened on Monday September 5th, around 5:50 p.m. at the 149th St.-Grand Concourse station in The Bronx. Cops say the men were harassing the 28-year-old female victim when her 21-year-old boyfriend intervened. One of the assailants then put him in a headlock, while the other started punching him in the face and body. When the woman tried to stop them, one of the men started punching her in the face and knocked her to the ground.</p>

<p>Police released video of the attack, taken by a witness, which you can see below.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021110207if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/Qn6dBKyIZFY" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The male victim suffered fractures to his face; both victims were treated for injuries at Lincoln Hospital. </p>

<p>The suspects are described as both 18-to-25 years old, 5&apos;8&quot;-5&apos;10&quot;; one was last seen wearing a black and gray t-shirt with the &quot;ninety&quot; and the number 3&quot; on it and white jeans, while the other suspect was last seen wearing a black t-shirt and dark colored pants.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). All calls are strictly confidential.</p>
