Tattoo enthusiasts, motorcyclists, and body art aficionados came together in Industrial City in Brooklyn this weekend for the annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509151847/http://www.nycurbantattooconvention.com/">NYC Urban Tattoo Convention</a>. It was a cornucopia of pit bulls, spiked clothing, and tattoos of every possible variety. </p>

<p>Photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509151847/http://colormekatie.blogspot.com/">Katie Sokoler</a>, who took the photos above, said it was an incredibly friendly atmosphere, if somewhat NSFW: &quot;There were lots of girls laying down getting tattooed with their tops off or in their underwear, and it was kind of funny since there were a bunch of young kids there. The little boys were just staring. Kind of shocked and confused.&quot;</p>

<p>Check out the photos above&#x2014;and if you feel inspired, the Convention will still be happening today until 10 p.m. at 233 37th Street in Brooklyn.</p>
