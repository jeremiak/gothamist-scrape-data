<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108043533/http://gothamist.com/tags/billyjoel">Billy Joel</a> is a busy man, between his never-ending MSG residency, his <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108043533/http://bigstory.ap.org/article/35da6c56ca4e4114b61050c460e301b8/billy-joel-join-cuomo-ny-breast-cancer-motorcycle-ride">motorcycle rides</a> with governors, and being the patron saint of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108043533/https://twitter.com/WGladstone/status/744012962298531840">screaming conversations on the LIRR.</a> He would be totally justified in kicking back and spending his Fridays enjoying a bottle of red at a cozy Italian restaurant&#x2014;but instead, Joel spent his Friday evening this week playing a surprise set with a Billy Joel cover band on Long Island.</p>

<p>Joel turned up at the Paramount Theatre in Huntington, NY to perform three songs alongside tribute band Big Shot. &quot;My man Mike!&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108043533/http://www.newsday.com/entertainment/music/billy-joel/billy-joel-surprises-fans-at-the-paramount-in-huntington-1.11965338">Joel said</a> to guitarist/singer Mike DelGuidice during the surprise set. &quot;I only live down the road from here.&quot; Joel performed The Beatles&apos; &quot;With a Little Help From My Friends,&quot; The Rolling Stones&apos; &quot;Honky Tonk Women&quot; and &quot;You May Be Right&quot; (with some bits of Led Zeppelin&apos;s &quot;Rock and Roll&quot; thrown in for good measure). You can watch the full set below.</p>

<p>Of course, as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108043533/http://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/watch-billy-joel-perform-surprise-set-with-cover-band-on-long-island-20160625">Rolling Stone points out</a>, several members of Big Shot are members of Joel&apos;s touring unit. Big Shot next perform July 1st at Pittsburgh&apos;s PNC Park, which may be a bit too far for Joel to roll out of bed and join them for, but ya never know!<br>
