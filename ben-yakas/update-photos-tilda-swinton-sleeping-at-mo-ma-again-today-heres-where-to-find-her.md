It&apos;s been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502152359/http://gothamist.com/2013/03/25/tilda_swinton_is_sleeping_at_moma_a.php#photo-1">about three weeks</a> since the last <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502152359/http://gothamist.com/tags/tildaswinton">sleeping Tilda Swinton</a> spotting at MoMA&#x2014;but any fans of nap-inspired art are in luck today, because Swinton is back! MoMA confirmed to us that Swinton is at the museum today performing her piece &quot;The Maybe&quot; on the third floor.</p>

<p>She&apos;s close to the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502152359/http://www.moma.org/visit/calendar/exhibitions/1343">Bill Brandt: Shadow and Light exhibit</a>&#x2014;she&apos;s also right across from the the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502152359/http://www.moma.org/collection/object.php?object_id=80049">Bauhaus Stairway</a></p>

<p>Maybe we should have all seen this coming&#x2014;Daniel Lametti certainly did. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502152359/https://twitter.com/danlametti/status/324214708817252352">He tweeted</a> last night: &quot;<strong>Tilda Swinton was on my LHR to JFK flight, which means she&apos;ll be in a glass box at the MOMA tomorrow.</strong>&quot; That was a bold assumption&#x2014;what if she was just in town to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502152359/http://gothamist.com/2013/04/16/citibike_sells_5000_annual_membersh.php">purchase her CitiBike membership</a> in person?</p>
