Earlier today, Brooklyn and Queens were hit <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428150413/http://gothamist.com/2012/09/08/weather_alert_tornado_may_hit_parts.php#photo-1">with a completely unlikely tornado</a>&#x2014;and now, there&apos;s a possibility that ANOTHER tornado could touch the city sometime this evening. A severe thunderstorm warning has been issued for the five boroughs&#x2014;heavy rains and wind are expected, as well as a &quot;possible tornado.&quot; </p>

<p>In addition to the city, Westchester, Rockland, Putnam, and East Orange counties&#x2014;as well as Passaic, Union, Bergen, Essex and Hudson counties in New Jersey&#x2014;have all also been issued the thunderstorm warning until 6:30 p.m. We&apos;ll keep updating this post as we learn more&#x2014;the weather has already provided some fittingly ominous clouds over Manhattan.</p>
