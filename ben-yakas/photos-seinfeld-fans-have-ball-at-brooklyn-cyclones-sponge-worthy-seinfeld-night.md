<p>The Brooklyn Cyclones hosted their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428180613/http://gothamist.com/2014/04/16/brooklyn_cyclones_go_full-seinfeld.php">much-anticipated &quot;Seinfeld Night&quot;</a> yesterday in Coney Island, complete with an Elaine dance contest, the real life Kramer and Soup Nazi, a rebranded Vandelay Industries park, Keith Hernandez bobbleheads, and lots more <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428180613/http://gothamist.com/tags/seinfeld"><em>Seinfeld</em></a> ephemera. The Cyclones of course were blown out 18-2, but what else do you expect from a Mets affiliate? Most people were there for the <em>Seinfeld</em> references, so check out our rundown of the Seinmoments below.</p>
