<p>It was only a few months ago that <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131203062001/http://gothamist.com/tags/paulmccartney">Paul McCartney</a> serenaded Brooklyn with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131203062001/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/09/videos_paul_mccartney.php">Beatles and solo songs galore at Barclays Center</a>, but he&apos;s already back in NYC this week to promote his forthcoming record, <em>New</em>. And it seems that he will be performing at the Frank Sinatra School of the Arts this afternoon, judging by the breathless tweets of teens. Hey, at least no one has asked &quot;Who is Paul McCartney?&quot; yet. </p>
