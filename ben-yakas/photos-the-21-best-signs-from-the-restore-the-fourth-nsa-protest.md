Yesterday, while you were debating the merits of cheap beer and marveling at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131021091957/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/04/hot_dog_eating_contest_winner.php">Joey Chestnut&apos;s hot dog achievements</a>, hundreds of people took to Union Square for the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131021091957/http://www.restorethefourth.net/">&quot;Restore The Fourth&quot;</a> rally, a nationwide protest of NSA spying and government intrusion. </p>

Once there, Reverend Billy Talen orated on the widespread surveillance of citizens by its government, and the Fourth Amendment was read aloud.
