In case you were wondering why <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430033720/http://www.robertaspizza.com/">Roberta&apos;s Pizza</a> was mysteriously closed for most of yesterday, here&apos;s your answer: Bill, Hillary and Chelsea Clinton were eating pizza in Bushwick last night! Certainly this pizza date was not as history-shaking as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430033720/http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-srv/politics/special/clinton/stories/pizza121998.htm">some of Bill&apos;s previous pizza dates.</a></p>

<p>We&apos;ve contacted <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430033720/http://gothamist.com/tags/robertas">Roberta&apos;s</a> for more information; one employee told us that everything was being kept hush-hush about the private event, but we&apos;ve at least gotten confirmation that <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430033720/http://www.ecorazzi.com/2012/09/06/bill-clintons-new-nickname-the-vegan-fox/">now-vegan</a> Bill Clinton and family were there. You can check out some tweets about it below. We&apos;ll update once we get more details.</p>
