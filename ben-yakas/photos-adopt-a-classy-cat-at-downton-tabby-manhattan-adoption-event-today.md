The season finale (okay, the Christmas special that already aired across the pond) of the third season of America&apos;s favorite aristocratic British import, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150922225030/http://gothamist.com/tags/downtonabbey"><em>Downton Abbey</em></a>, will air on PBS tonight. So what better way to celebrate the end of another season of people making Edith&apos;s life miserable than to open your heart to an Edith-like kitty! <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150922225030/https://www.facebook.com/pages/Anjellicle-Cats-Rescue-Fan-Page/200369229987929">Anjellicle Cats Rescue</a> is hosting a <em>Downton Tabby</em> cat adoption event today at Petco on the Upper West Side (Columbus Ave. and 100th Street) from 1-6 p.m. </p>

<p>Click through to better get acquainted with some of the kitties, including stand-ins for Mary Crawley, Matthew Crawley, Bates, and Carson. Of course, there is no cat based on Edith above, because the world must keep shitting on her&#x2014;but there <em>is</em> a Dowager Catniss, so all is forgiven. Then after you adopt one of these animals, you can cuddle with them tonight and enjoy watching [INCREDIBLY UNIMPORTANT SPOILER ALERT] Ms. Patmore&apos;s newly-hatched romantic life flourish together.</p>
