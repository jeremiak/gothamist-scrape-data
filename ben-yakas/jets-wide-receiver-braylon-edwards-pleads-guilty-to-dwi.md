										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left" style=" width:300px; "> <img alt="72211braylon.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160528073508im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/byakas/72211braylon.jpg" width="300" height="221"> <br> <i>Jets wide receiver Braylon Edwards outside Manhattan criminal court this past spring (AP)</i></div> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160528073508/http://gothamist.com/tags/jets">Jets</a> star wide receiver Braylon Edwards <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160528073508/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2011/07/22/2011-07-22_new_york_jets_wide_receiver_braylon_edwards_pleads_guilty_to_drunkdriving_charge.html?r=news&amp;utm_source=feedburner&amp;ut">pled guilty to</a> driving drunk in a plea deal today, successfully avoiding jail time. Edwards admitted to driving while intoxicated <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160528073508/http://gothamist.com/2010/09/21/jets_wide_receiver_braylon_edwards.php">last fall</a>, hours after the Jets had defeated the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160528073508/http://gothamist.com/2010/11/30/new_york_jets_to_battle_ugg_boots_s.php">New England Ugg Boots Spokesmen</a>&#x2014;he must pay a $500 fine, and continue to participate in an NFL substance-abuse program, which he had already enrolled in. &quot;Now it&apos;s just really time to focus on football again. I feel like it came to a fair conclusion,&quot; he excitedly told reporters after leaving court today.<p></p>

<p>With the NFL lockout <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160528073508/http://espn.go.com/espnw/news-opinion/6792518/about-last-night-nfl-lockout-final-countdown">on the verge of ending</a>, Edwards has his mind on free agency, just like fellow Jet Antonio Cromartie. Except unlike Cromartie, who mad it clear that he&apos;d play for the highest bidder, Edwards said he &quot;most definitely&quot; wants to re-sign with the Jets. As long as they put a backflip-clause into his contract, we&apos;d be happy with that:</p>
