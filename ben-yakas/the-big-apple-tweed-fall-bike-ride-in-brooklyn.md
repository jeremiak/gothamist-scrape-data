<p>Did you see a flash of tweed while walking around Brooklyn yesterday? Well don&apos;t worry, it wasn&apos;t deja vu from last night at The Trendy Anachronistic Speakeasy: it was the second annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151005135106/http://bigappletweed.wordpress.com/2010/11/13/fall-tweed-ride-fashion-inspiration/">Big Apple Tweed Fall Ride</a>, a day to appreciate biking and fashion, in a 19th century sort of way. The ride began at the Grand Army Plaza entrance of Prospect Park, made a stop in Boerum Hill and ended in Park Slope. Riders donned different elegant tweed designs, a la old English cycling attire, along with bowler hats, pipes and other accessories. Take a look at some of the posh clothes of yesterday&apos;s participants.<br>
