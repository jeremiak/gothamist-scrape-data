<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130424110717/http://gothamist.com/2013/04/12/lots_of_track_changes_happening_on.php">Remember last weekend</a>? Remember how the MTA performed track work on almost every subway line and it gave you stress headaches just thinking about it? Remember how you comforted yourself by thinking, &quot;well, let&apos;s get this over with. At least it&apos;ll be better next week&quot;? We have some bad news for you: the MTA will be performing maintenance that will disrupt 14 different lines this weekend. Check out all the pertinent details below, but first take a deep breath (or maybe <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130424110717/http://gothamist.com/2013/04/20/celebrate_420_with_these_sticky_ick_1.php">a really deep breath</a>), because there&apos;s a lot to go through. </p>

<p><strong>1 Train</strong>: Downtown trains will skip 238th Street due to staircase repair at 238th Street from 9:45 a.m. to 2 p.m. Saturday.</p>

<p><strong>4 Train</strong>: Uptown trains will run express from Union Square to Grand Central Terminal from 11:45 p.m. Saturday to 6:30 a.m. Sunday and then 11:45 p.m. Sunday to 5 a.m. Monday. Downtown trains will run express between 125th Street and Grand Central during the same times. </p>

<p><strong>6 Train</strong>: Uptown trains will run express from Union Square to Grand Central from 11:45 p.m. Friday to 5 a.m. Monday. Downtown trains will run express from 125th Street to Grand Central from 11:45 p.m. Friday to 5 a.m. Monday. Downtown trains will also run express from Parkchester to Hunts Point Avenue from 7:45 a.m. to 4 p.m. both Saturday and Sunday.</p>

<p><strong>A Train</strong>: There will be no A trains between 168th and 207th Streets from 11:45 p.m. Friday to 5 a.m. Monday. Free shuttle buses will make stops at 175th, 181st, 190th and Dyckman Streets. All weekend, Manhattan-bound A trains in Brooklyn will run express from Euclid Avenue to Broadway Junction.</p>

<p><strong>C Train</strong>: There will be no C trains between 145th and 168th Streets from 6:30 a.m. to 11 p.m. Saturday and Sunday (A trains will run local between those stations.) Uptown C trains will run express from Canal Street to 59th Street, and run express in Brooklyn from Euclid Avenue to Broadway Junction during the same times. </p>

<p><strong>D Train</strong>: There will be no service between Coney Island-Stillwell Avenue and Bay Parkway from 5:30 a.m. to 8 p.m. Saturday and Sunday due to Weekend Fastrack.</p>

<p><strong>N Train</strong>: There will also be no service between Coney Island-Stillwell Avenue and Bay Parkway from 5:30 a.m. to 8 p.m. Saturday and Sunday due to Weekend Fastrack.<br>
 <br>
<strong>E Train</strong>: Jamaica Center-bound E trains run local from Roosevelt Avenue to Forest Hills-71st Avenue from 12:30 a.m. Saturday to 5 a.m. Monday.<br>
 <br>
<strong>F Train</strong>: Jamaica-bound F trains will run over the A line from Jay Street-Metro Tech to West 4th Street from 11:45 p.m. Friday to 5 a.m. Monday. Jamaica-bound F trains also run local from Roosevelt Avenue to Forest Hills-71st Avenue from 12:30 a.m. Saturday to 5 a.m. Monday. Coney Island-Bound F trains skip 4th Avenue-9th Street, 15th Street-Prospect Park and Fort Hamilton Parkway from 11:45 p.m. Friday to 5 a.m. Monday.</p>

<p><strong>G Train</strong>: Church Avenue-bound G trains skip 4th Avenue-9th Street, 15th Street-Prospect Park and Fort Hamilton Parkway from 11:45 p.m. Friday to 5 a.m. Monday. Many Queens-bound G trains will have their last stop at Bedford-Nostrand from 5 a.m. to 11:59 p.m Saturday and Sunday&#x2014;trains will run every 20 minutes during that time.</p>

<p><strong>J Train</strong>: There is no J train service between Crescent Street and Jamaica Center from 5:30 a.m. Saturday to 10 p.m. Sunday. J trains will run between Crescent Street and Chambers Street. Free shuttle buses operate between Crescent Street and 121st Street and connect with the E at Jamaica-Van Wyck.</p>

<p><strong>M Train</strong>: M train service will be suspended between Fresh Pond Road and Central Avenue from 11:45 p.m. Friday to 5 a.m. Monday. Free shuttle buses will run between Metropolitan and Myrtle Avenues making all stops.</p>

<p><strong>Q Train</strong>: Manhattan-bound Q trains will run express from Kings Highway to Prospect Park from 10:45 p.m. Friday to 5 a.m. Monday.</p>

<p><strong>S Train</strong>: Franklin Avenue Shuttle service will be suspended from 11:45 p.m. Friday to 5 a.m. Monday.</p>

<p>We all got through this week. So we think we can all agree that a few inconveniences to our travel schedules isn&apos;t the end of the world.</p>
