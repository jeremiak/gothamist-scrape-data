According to the MTA&apos;s Kevin Ortiz, subway service resumes at 6 a.m. Fares are back in effect, but the &quot;bus bridges&quot; between Manhattan and Brooklyn are free. He recommends adding 30-60 minutes to the commute, which seems low.  </p>
