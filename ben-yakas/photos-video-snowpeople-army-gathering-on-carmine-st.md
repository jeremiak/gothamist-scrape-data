<p>Yesterday, reader RozzoR <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130224081352/http://gothamist.com/2011/01/28/photos_everyone_loves_snowpeople.php">commented</a> on Thursday&apos;s city-wide snow day, &quot;I really don&apos;t think I&apos;ve ever seen a day with so many happy New Yorkers since the blackout of 2004.&quot; And what crystallizes that particular feeling better than snowmen? Maybe the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130224081352/http://www.flickr.com/photos/dogseat/5395886907/">video</a> below of a snow army gathering on Carmine Street. But as long as we keep receiving your snowmen, we&apos;ll keep posting them, so send them to pics@gothamist.com, or tag your photos as &quot;Gothamist&quot; on Flickr.</p>
