<p>A legally blind woman <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605040110/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Bronx-Blind-Woman-Thrown-Roof-Melrose-Police-Stab-Death-200401281.html">was found stabbed</a> behind a Bronx building yesterday, and police are investigating whether she was thrown off the roof of her building. According to cops, the 49-year-old woman was found around 2:40 p.m. Wednesday on Courtlandt Avenue near E 154th Street in Melrose. Sources <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605040110/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/bronx/bronx_woman_dies_after_being_stabbed_9NcWRPlgemuO9ZCUIEwM9M">told the Post that</a> she had been stabbed &quot;several times in her neck and side during a struggle with her boyfriend before she plunged to the ground.&quot;</p>

<p>Police added that the victim had been stabbed multiple times in the stomach and was pronounced dead at the scene. Cops are waiting for the medical examiner&apos;s report to determine whether the woman died of the stab wounds or from the fall. No arrests have been made, and the investigation is ongoing. </p>

<p><strong>Update</strong>: Police have now arrested a 35-year-old man in connection with the death of the 49-year-old blind woman. No other information is known about the suspect right now, and police are still investigating the fall. But <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605040110/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Bronx-Blind-Woman-Thrown-Roof-Melrose-Police-Stab-Death-200401281.html?_osource=SocialFlowTwt_NYBrand">NBC adds</a> that authorities found blood in the woman&apos;s bathroom and believe she may have been killed there and moved elsewhere. The woman was found lying naked in the street.</p>
