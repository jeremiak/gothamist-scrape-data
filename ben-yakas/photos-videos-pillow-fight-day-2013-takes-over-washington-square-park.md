Pillow advocates and pillow hate groups collided on Saturday in Washington Square Park for the eighth annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405202553/http://gothamist.com/tags/pillowfight">International Pillow Fight Day</a>. Tensions ran high as the public displays of pillowing left mass casualties of feathers strewn across the park. Our own <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405202553/https://twitter.com/aaronjmarks">Aaron Marks</a> said the place was packed (200-300 people in the northern part of the park), and he was lucky to survive in one piece: &quot;It was pretty wild, somehow I escaped with no feathers in my hair and only hit by 2 or 3 pillows&#x2014;so I&apos;d say a rousing success!&quot;</p>
