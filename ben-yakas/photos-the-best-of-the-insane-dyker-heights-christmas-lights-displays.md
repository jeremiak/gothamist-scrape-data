					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161209164813/http://gothamist.com/2015/12/10/dyker_heights_lights_photos.php#photo-1">Every year</a>, the Dyker Heights community bands together to transform their neighborhood into the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161209164813/http://gothamist.com/tags/dykerheights">craziest holiday lights display</a> in the entire city. The annual display includes everything from blinged-out nativity scenes to blown-up Marvel superheroes, from glittery candy cane soldiers to sparkly candy cane front lawns. Behold the most complex and eye-catching displays up above, courtesy of photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161209164813/http://saimokhtari.com/">Sai Mokhtari.</a></p>

A compromise <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161209164813/http://www.brooklyneagle.com/articles/2016/12/5/cops-forge-compromise-dyker-christmas-parking-fight">was arranged</a> after locals became upset. </p>
