<p>A man was fatally struck by a train in midtown Manhattan this afternoon. According to FDNY, the incident happened just after 4 p.m. at the Bryant Park station at 42nd Street and 6th Avenue. The man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510154943/https://twitter.com/NYScanner/status/312299015318413312">is reportedly</a> dead at the scene, but police could not provide any more details about the incident. We&apos;ll update as we learn more information.</p>
