<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130909233525/http://gothamist.com/tags/jonstewart">Jon Stewart</a> was off finding himself in the Middle East this summer when <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130909233525/http://gothamist.com/tags/anthonyweiner">Anthony Weiner&apos;s</a> mayoral comeback attempt hit the Carlos Danger zone, which left John Oliver <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130909233525/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/25/videos_colbert_report_daily_show_ta.php#photo-1">to wade through the murk</a> and try to figure out when Weiner &quot;fell off the dick wagon.&quot; Last night, Stewart finally addressed <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130909233525/http://gothamist.com/2011/06/14/video_weiners_waxed_bod_bothers_jon.php">his old friend&apos;s</a> disastrous campaign, covering his anger issues, his <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130909233525/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/03/video_anthony_weiner_asks_west_indi.php">fake Caribbean accent</a>, and his &quot;charm that borders on the edge of dickishness.&quot; </p>

<p>For a moment, Stewart contemplated defending Weiner, only to conclude: <strong>&quot;He&apos;s just a guy with self control issues who never should have run for mayor.&quot;</strong> Check out all that, plus a look at a weird Speedy Gonzalez dick pic, in the <em>Daily Show</em> clips below.</p>
