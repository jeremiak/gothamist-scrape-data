<p>The Bluth Frozen Banana Stand has been traveling around NYC all week promoting <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103819/http://gothamist.com/tags/arresteddevelopment"><em>Arrested Development</em></a> and giving out free chocolate-covered bananas to the people. Today is its last day in the city, and fans who swallowed their pride and trekked to Times Square were in for a treat: stars Jason Bateman and Will Arnett are there right now literally tossing bananas out out to the crowd. Check out some Vines and Tweets below, and hurry over there if you want to catch them!</p>
