<p>Last month, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151108084427/http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20130801/williamsburg/dunkin-donuts-opening-right-outside-bedford-avenue-subway-station">it was announced</a> that Northside Pharmacy on Bedford Avenue and N 7th Street in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151108084427/http://gothamist.com/tags/williamsburg">Williamsburg</a> would be converted into a Dunkin&apos; Donuts. The store has now entered the hiring phase of development, as you can see from the photo above; it&apos;s (literally) a sign for many people that heralds the absolute death knell for the neighborhood, one that no amount of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151108084427/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/17/record_store_opening_in_brooklyn.php">new record stores</a> can fix. Check out some of the memorial tweets below&#x2014;but always remember that Williamsburg died when the first kid said, &quot;Williamsburg&apos;s not dead, Williamsburg&apos;s not dead.&quot;</p>
