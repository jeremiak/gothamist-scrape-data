					<p>Despite its <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017041549/http://gothamist.com/2016/10/02/kanye_kim_meadows_fest_paris_nyc.php">abrupt ending</a>, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017041549/http://gothamist.com/tags/meadowsfestival">Meadows Festival</a>, the newest music festival kid in town, enjoyed a generally successful debut weekend outside Citi Field in Flushing, Queens. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017041549/http://gothamist.com/2016/10/02/kanye_kim_meadows_fest_paris_nyc.php">The most talked-about moment</a> came near the end of the festival on Sunday night, when headliner Kanye West cut his set short, walking off stage in the middle of performing his song &quot;Heartless,&quot; letting the audience know there was a &quot;family emergency.&quot; It later emerged that his wife, Kim Kardashian West, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017041549/http://gothamist.com/2016/10/03/kim_kardashian_west_paris_robbery.php">had been held up at gunpoint</a> inside her Paris hotel room by masked men. </p>

<p>Prior to the abrupt ending to the fest, everything else ran remarkably smoothly and on time the entire weekend, with performances by Chance The Rapper, Mac Miller, The 1975, J Cole and more. Despite the drama <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017041549/http://gothamist.com/2016/08/01/festival_truthing.php">over the location of the event</a> (it wasn&apos;t actually in Flushing Meadows Corona Park, as was first announced), it seems the Citi Field parking lot could actually contain thousands of dancing people without piles of trash building up.</p>

<p>In addition to the music and the spectacular views of the outer shell of Citi Field, there was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171017041549/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/28/meadows_festival_queens_food.php">a &quot;Feastival of Queens&quot; area</a> with local food deals; a voter registration booth <em>and</em> a TSA pre-check booth; a &quot;Sober Meadows&quot; booth that held meetings and acted as a safe/drug free place for people in recovery; and copious amounts of Kanye West merch, to the point that the merch line went from being labelled &quot;KANYE MERCH plus all Meadows event merch and other artist merch&quot; on Saturday to just &quot;KANYE MERCH&quot; on Sunday.</p>
