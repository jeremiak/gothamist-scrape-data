<p>An unidentified man was fatally struck by an F train in Queens this morning. According to the MTA, the incident happened just before 9 a.m. on the northbound F tracks at the 169th Street station. FDNY and NYPD confirmed that the man was deceased at the scene. </p>

<p>Because of the incident, northbound F train are running express from Kew Gardens-Union Turnpike to 179th Street, northbound E trains are running local from Forest Hills-71 Avenue to Briarwood-Van Wyck Boulevard, and southbound F trains are bypassing the 169 Street Station for the time being. Check for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130921023241/http://new.mta.info/status/subway/BDFM/22991876">more MTA updates here.</a></p>

<p><strong>Update</strong>: According <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130921023241/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/queens/decapitated-man-discovered-subway-tracks-queens-article-1.1459762#ixzz2fGLy1qmW">to the News</a>, a straphanger found the decapitated victim at the northern end of the local platform this morning. Officials are trying to determine when exactly he had been struck.</p>
