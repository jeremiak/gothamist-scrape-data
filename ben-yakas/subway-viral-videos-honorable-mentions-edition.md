<p>This week, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/2011/12/30/top_10_viral_subway_videos_of_2011.php#photo-1">we compiled a list of our top ten favorite subway viral videos of 2011</a>, which included people licking their shoes, senseless beatdowns on the L train, spaghetti scuffles, and rats climbing all over unsuspecting targets. But there were <em>a lot</em> of videos to choose from&#x2014;so per your suggestions, we&apos;ve compiled a list of ten honorable mentions, including at least one which rightly deserved to be among the top ten: &quot;Bloody Loco.&quot; Check them all out below.</p>

<p><strong>Blood Loco</strong>: In March, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/2011/03/22/video_10.php">a man calling himself &quot;Bloody Loco&quot;</a> did his very best to intimidate another passenger on the subway, to no avail. The fearless victim <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/2011/03/23/bloody_loco.php">eventually spoke about</a> the confrontation; we also <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/2011/03/24/bloody_loco_the_drinking_game.php">played a drinking game</a> based on of the incident.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/-aRJDI3v4AM" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p><strong>Armed Man Hijacks</strong>: In a very scary incident, an emotionally disturbed person wielding a screwdriver climbed through the window of a 5 train motorman&apos;s cab in the Bronx, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/2011/07/26/video_armed_man_hijacks_subway_trai.php">in an apparent attempted to hijack it.</a> Thankfully, no one was hurt, and the man was captured.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/Or1tNUdw-oY" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p><strong>U.S.A. Chant Fail</strong>: For a few days, most of America was united in nationalistic bloodlust at news of the death of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/tags/osamabinladen">Osama bin Laden</a>. But that still doesn&apos;t mean New Yorkers are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/2011/05/03/video_usa_chant_falls_flat_on_l_tra.php">going to join in on your dumb chant.</a></p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/Jox5vMFASLA" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p><strong>Subway Grooming Is Gross</strong>: While we may all be locked into a never-ending argument about whether <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/2011/03/18/video_subway_spaghetti_spat_sparks.php">eating on the subway</a> is acceptable or not, one thing is for certain: <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/2011/04/01/video_clipping_your_nails_on_the_su.php">nail clipping on the subway is not cool, dude.</a></p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/cf7Qj-N33zg" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p><strong>Candy Peddling Makes For Good Business</strong>: Bronx resident Alex &quot;Tracks&quot; McFarland has been selling candy in NYC&apos;s subway system since he was 11 years old...and he claims he makes $150 a day doing it.</p>

No, it&apos;s still not socially acceptable to just take off all your clothes and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212820/http://gothamist.com/2011/07/22/video_young_lady_tries_to_beat_the.php">rollick around the subway naked.</a></p>
