<p>Police are looking for an &quot;extremely short&quot; suspect who is wanted in connection with a sexual assault on a train in Brooklyn last month. The incident happened on a J train at Broadway Junction in Brownsville around 2:45 p.m. on Monday, January 6th. The suspect, who police describe as &quot;extremely short in stature,&quot; allegedly grabbed the butt of a 36-year-old woman, then fled.</p>

<p>The suspect, who you can see in the photo insert above right, was wearing white pants, an &quot;I &#x2665; New York&quot; black hooded sweatshirt, and a white baseball cap. The Post, ever classy, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140307045305/http://nypost.com/2014/02/13/cops-looking-for-dwarf-in-groping-on-brooklyn-straphanger/">simply describes</a> the suspect as a &quot;dwarf.&quot;<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS.  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
