<p>A man was stabbed while riding a subway train this afternoon. According to FDNY, the incident happened just after 3 p.m. on the 5 train in The Bronx. The man, whose name hasn&apos;t been released, was stabbed near Esplanade and Paulding Avenues while aboard the train. He has been taken to Jacobi Hospital in serious condition. </p>

<p>The NYPD press office had no information about the incident. We&apos;ll update when we learn more information.</p>
