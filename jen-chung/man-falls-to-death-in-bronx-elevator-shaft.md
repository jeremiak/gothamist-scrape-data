										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2010_03_mtmat.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506164610im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2010_03_mtmat.jpg" width="100" height="82" class="image-right"> </span>A man moving into an apartment in the Bronx fell into an empty elevator shaft and died.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506164610/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=7305832">WABC 7 reports</a> that  Joseph Ryan and his wife were carrying a mattress to the elevator: &quot;Eyewitness News is told the elevator doors opened, but the elevator was not there. Ryan, unaware of this, backed into the elevator without looking and he fell.&quot;  He was pronounced dead at St. Barnabas Hospital around 1 a.m.; police are investigating.</p>
