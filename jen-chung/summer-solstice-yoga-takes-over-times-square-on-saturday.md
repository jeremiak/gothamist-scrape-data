<p>Tomorrow, Broadway between 42nd and 44th Streets will be closed so thousands of yogis can participate in an all-day yoga-thon in the heart of Times Square.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140622235533/http://www.timessquarenyc.org/events/solstice-in-times-square/instructor-bios/index.aspx#.U6R-FtzoaCg">Registration is closed</a>, but don&apos;t worry&#x2014;now you don&apos;t have to schlep to Times Square at 5:30 a.m. for the Sunrise Vinyasa or at 8 p.m. for the Sunset Flow because the whole day will be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140622235533/http://www.timessquarenyc.org/">webcast on TimesSquareNYC.org</a>.</p>
