<p>Bloomberg and Gaga also danced, in the mayor&apos;s apparent bid to appeal to Little Monsters.  Other Dick Clark&apos;s New Year&apos;s Rocking Eve performers include Pitbull and Justin Bieber and Santana, while Drake and the Roots were on NBC&apos;s Carson Daly New Year&apos;s show.  </p>

<p>Police Commissioner Ray Kelly was guessing that <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160501214811/http://gothamist.com/2011/12/31/cops_begin_new_years_eve_closures_i.php">last night&apos;s turnout</a> in Times Square may <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160501214811/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/mayor-bloomberg-kisses-lady-gaga-bring-2012-year-article-1.999425">have been the biggest ever</a>.
