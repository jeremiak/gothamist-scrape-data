title: Off-Duty Cop Kills Man Trying To Mug Him In Bushwick
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right" style=" width:325px; "> <img alt="2012_01_copshooting.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160202015210im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_01_copshooting.jpg" width="325" height="215"> <br> <i>Via WABC 7</i></div> Police say that an off-duty police officer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160202015210/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=8523864">fatally shot a man who was trying to mug him</a> at Broadway and Grove Street in the Bushwick section of Brooklyn. According to WABC 7, two men approached the cop: &quot;One of the men simulated holding a gun while the other knocked the victim down to the ground with a cane. The detective opened fire on the muggers, hitting the cane-wielding suspect once in the chest.&quot;<p></p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160202015210/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/brooklyn/man_shot_by_off_duty_nypd_detective_uAwkdg7sU0vTVr1jSRaH8L">Post reports</a> that the officer had been &quot;walking on Grove Street in Bushwick toward a subway station at 11:50 p.m., when two men &apos;told him to run his pockets&apos;&quot; and fired once.  The suspect was pronounced dead at Woodhull Hospital.  The other suspect ran away&#x2014;the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160202015210/http://www.wpix.com/news/wpix-nypd-off-duty-shooting-013012,0,366861.story">police are seeking him</a>.</p>
