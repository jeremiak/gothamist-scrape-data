The victim took photographs of the suspect, which investigators have shared with the public.<p></p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on Saturday, March 19th, around 3:10 p.m.: &quot;While aboard a Manhattan bound R train that was entering the Elmhurst Avenue subway station, the suspect put his hand inside his pants and masturbated as he stared at the 33 year-old female victim. The suspect exited the train at the 59th Street station in Manhattan.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect, who is wanted for public lewdness, is described as being between 25 and 30 years old, 6&apos;0&quot; and 175 pounds and was last seen wearing a black jacket, an orange shirt, gray sweatpants and gray and green sneakers.</p>
