title: Punch to the Face Kills Queens Man
---

	
										<p><img alt="2008_09_licman.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170919035024im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2008_09_licman.jpg" width="125" height="117" class="right"><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170919035024/http://www.wnbc.com/news/17387598/detail.html?dl=mainclick">WNBC reports</a> that 66-year-old Nick Nowillo died after a confrontation with an attacker on Crescent Avenue in Long Island City.  The 43-year-old attacker, who was arrested but not identified, allegedly hit Nowillo several times, including punching him so hard that he died at Astoria General Hospital later.  Nowillo, who had been escorting a woman home because she felt the suspect &quot;was...acting strangely&quot; in the street, was a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170919035024/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=6369509">beloved figure in the neighborhood</a>, nicknamed the &quot;Mayor of Long Island City.&quot;  </p>
