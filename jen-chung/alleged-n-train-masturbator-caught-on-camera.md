										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021110649/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_06_ntrainma.jpg"> <img alt="2016_06_ntrainma.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021110649im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2016/06/2016_06_ntrainma-thumb-450x829-954011.jpg" width="250" height="460" class="image-right"> </a> </span>A subway rider took photographs of a man who allegedly started masturbating while staring at her on the N train last weekend. Now investigators are hoping the public can help identify him.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident happened on a southbound N train in Brooklyn at 11:15 p.m. on Sunday, June 26th. Police say the man sat down across from the 24-year-old victim, &quot;exposed his penis and masturbated while he stared at her.&quot;</p>

<p>The woman left the train at the Stillwell Avenue station and told police, who canvassed the station for the man but could not find him.</p>

<p>The victim share photos of the suspect with the police, who say he is 25-30 years old and was last seen wearing a red baseball cap, a white t-shirt, white shorts and black and white sneakers.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021110649/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577. All calls are strictly confidential.</p>

<p>The NYPD is working to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021110649/http://gothamist.com/2016/06/22/cuomo_pandering.php">crack down on subway perverts</a> and strongly encourage victims and witnesses to report incidents. If you see or experience sexual misconduct in the subway (this includes seeing a masturbator, being groped, being grinded on, etc.), <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021110649/http://mta-nyc.custhelp.com/app/ask_sh">you can report it to the MTA and police on this website</a>. There&apos;s also a place for you to upload photos and/or video. Don&apos;t let the perverts win.<br>
