title: Cops: Duo Robbed Brooklyn Apartment After Handcuffing Tenant
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2014_03_suspect175.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140402035714im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_03_suspect175.jpg" width="300" height="230"> <br> <i style=" width:300px; ;display:block"> Suspect 1 </i></div> The NYPD released video of one of two people suspected of robbing a Brooklyn apartment on Thursday.<p></p>

<p>According to police, the 49-year-old male victim had a friend, known only as &quot;Eric,&quot; over to his apartment in East New York. A little after 1 a.m. on March 27th, while the victim was in the bathroom, &quot;Eric&quot; let a second suspect into the apartment. </p>

<p>When the victim exited the bathroom, &quot;Eric&quot; told him to lay on the floor face down, and then handcuffed him. The other suspect removed items, including money, credit cards and the victim&apos;s phone, from the apartment. Then the two suspects fled.</p>

<p>The police released video of &quot;Eric&quot; using the victim&apos;s ATM card. Anyone with information in regards to this robbery is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140402035714/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
