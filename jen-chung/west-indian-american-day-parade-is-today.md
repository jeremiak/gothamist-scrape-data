<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905084901/http://www.wiadcacarnival.org/">West Indian American Day Parade</a> starts today at 11 a.m. It&apos;s a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905084901/http://gothamist.com/2012/09/03/photos_the_2012_west_indian_day_par.php#photo-1">pretty colorful and energetic time</a>, what with the great music and food&#x2014;and the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905084901/http://gothamist.com/2012/09/04/the_best_daggering_videos_from_the.php">daggering</a> in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905084901/http://gothamist.com/2012/09/05/the_quadruple_decker_daggering_vide.php">all forms</a> (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905084901/http://gothamist.com/2012/09/05/the_nypd_daggering_photos_youve_bee.php#photo-1">sometimes in uniform</a>). </p>

<p>The parade is the climax of a weekend of festivities, with J&apos;Ouvert, the pre-parade parade, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905084901/http://instagram.com/p/dwr6hujFJj/">ending this morning</a>. </p>
