<p>Earlier yesterday, 125 people from dozens of countries <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140939/http://www.dnainfo.com/20111028/downtown/125-immigrants-take-citizenship-oath-on-lady-libertys-birthday">took their citizenship oaths</a>. Statue of Liberty National Monument superintendent David Luchsinger told them, &quot;She&apos;s the symbol, but you&apos;re the reality. This country is built on its citizens. Her torch is as bright as what you will make it to be.&quot;</p>

<p>The Statue of Liberty is now closed for a year-long renovation&#x2014;but go! And here&apos;s a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140939/http://gothamist.com/2011/10/28/the_statue_of_liberty.php#photo-1">look back at when she was wee</a>.</p>
