<p>The police are asking for the public&apos;s help in finding a suspect in an attempted rape that happened yesterday in Brooklyn.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 12:30 a.m. on August 29th, a 20-year-old woman was walking along Saint Mark&apos;s Avenue approaching Underhill Avenue. A male &quot;approached her from behind, grabbed her by the waist and covered her mouth with his hand,&quot; police say. Then he allegedly threw her to the ground and tried to remove her clothing. The victim managed to resist and he eventually fled.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as approximately 19 years-old, 5&apos;6&quot;, black hair and weighing approximately 160 pounds</p>
