<p>A 59-year-old man&apos;s thumb was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170501155848/http://abc7ny.com/news/queens-residents-rattled-after-terrifying-meat-cleaver-attack/1938593/">reportedly &quot;severed&quot;</a> during a vicious attack in a Queens home last month. </p>

<p>The incident, which police are calling a robbery, took place at 1:20 p.m. on April 23rd, in the 113th Precinct. Apparently the 59-year-old man and a 62-year-old man were confronted by &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170501155848/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2017/05/01/queens-meat-cleaver-attack/">three men... [who were] ransacking [the] house looking for something to steal</a>.&quot; According to the NYPD, the 59-year-old was attacked with a meat cleaver while he was trying to flee; the 62-year-old managed to escaped, and then the suspects fled as well. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170501155848/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/queens/thieves-cut-man-thumb-meat-cleaver-queens-robbery-article-1.3121334">The Daily News reports</a> that the 59-year-old man had arrived at his apartment &quot;when a man approached him, pulled out a pair of gloves and demanded &apos;money and dope,&apos; police sources said. A second robber with a gun forced him on the ground and started tying him up, but he broke free and managed to take his attacker&#x2019;s gun, police sources said.&quot;<br>
 <br>
A third suspect allegedly used the cleaver to slash him all over his head and hands. He needed 100 stitches and thumb-attachment surgery, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170501155848/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Man-Comes-Face-to-Face-With-Armed-Intruders-Assaulted-With-Meat-Cleaver-Police-420862963.html">according to NBC New York</a>.</p>

<p>Police released surveillance video of one of the suspects, who is described as 30-40 years old, 5&apos;8&quot;-5&apos;9&quot; and 150-160 pounds and allegedly spoke with a West Indian accent. He was last seen wearing black sunglasses and a black leather baseball hat.</p>

<p>The suspect who was accused of using the meat cleaver was described as 30 - 40 years old and 150 - 160 pounds, last seen wearing jeans, while the suspect who allegedly had the gun is described as being 5&apos;10&quot; with salt and pepper hair, wearing a white or gray t-shirt. </p>
