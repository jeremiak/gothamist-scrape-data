<p>It&apos;s official: Fairway Market <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130527061903/http://www.ny1.com/content/top_stories/180875/fairway-signs-lease-for-new-chelsea-store">will open a 20,000-square foot store</a> on Sixth Avenue, between 25th and 26th Street, in Chelsea. This will be the chain&apos;s fifth Manhattan location.</p>

<p>In February, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130527061903/http://www.crainsnewyork.com/article/20130226/REAL_ESTATE/130229912">Crain&apos;s had reported</a> that Fairway was eyeing the space, &quot;About 17,000 square feet is on the ground floor, with the remainder in the basement. The store will be at the base of a 33-story apartment building called the Chelsea Landmark.&quot; It&apos;s likely the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130527061903/http://gothamist.com/2012/10/17/fairway_introduce_2nd_round_of_anti.php#photo-1">olives will have covers</a>.</p>

<p>Fairway, which <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130527061903/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9053796">had an IPO last week</a>, will join the Trader Joe&apos;s on Sixth Avenue between 21st and 22nd Streets and the Whole Foods on Seventh Avenue and 24th Street. Also, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130527061903/http://jobs.info.com/jobs?q=Fairway+Market&amp;l=Chelsea,+NY&amp;pp=infocom">they are hiring</a>!</p>
