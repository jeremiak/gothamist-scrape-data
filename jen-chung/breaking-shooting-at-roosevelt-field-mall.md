<p>A shooting at the Roosevelt Field Mall on Long Island sent holiday shoppers running in a panic this afternoon.</p>

<p>Nassau County police say that it was a &quot;possible robbery with shots fired,&quot; and the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160426115813/https://twitter.com/nicolefuller/status/679366314436263936">police commissioner said</a>, &quot;We have one individual shot. We have one subject in custody.&quot; Witnesses <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160426115813/http://pix11.com/2015/12/22/shots-fired-at-scene-of-robbery-at-roosevelt-field-mall/">told PIX11</a> that dozens of shoppers were seen running out of the mall.</p>
