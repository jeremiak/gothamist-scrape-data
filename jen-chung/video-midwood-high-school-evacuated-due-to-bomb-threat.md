title: Video: Midwood High School Evacuated Due To Bomb Threat
---

	
										<p>A Brooklyn high school has been evacuated following a phoned-in bomb threat.</p>

<p>The NYPD says that <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307043223/http://www.midwoodhighschool.org/">Midwood High School</a>, at  2839 Bedford Avenue, received the threat at 11:12 a.m. No details of the call were made available.</p>

<p>Students were evacuated, and video shows them outside the building and in the streets.</p>

<p>Police are currently investigating at the scene. An unidentified official <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307043223/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/bomb-threat-prompts-evacuation-brooklyn-high-school-article-1.2984527">told the Daily News</a> that the NYPD bomb squad has thus far found no explosives at the school, which <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307043223/https://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20170228/midwood/midwood-high-school-evacuated-bomb-threat">has 3,800 students</a>. </p>
