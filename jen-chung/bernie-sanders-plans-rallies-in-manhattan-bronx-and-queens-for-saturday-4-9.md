<p>After his appearance <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002184353/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/08/pope_francis_bernie_bro.php">on Morning Joe</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002184353/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/08/bernie_sanders_subway_school.php">visit to The View</a>, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002184353/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/08/bernie_sanders_brooklyn_today.php">Brooklyn rallies</a>, somehow Bernie Sanders managed to plan <em>three</em> free rallies for Saturday, April 9. They will be in Washington Heights in Manhattan, University Heights in the Bronx and Long Island City in Queens. Here are the details:</p>

<p>The first rally is Washington Heights at the United Palace, 4140 Broadway, New York. Doors open at 8 a.m. and the program begins at 10 a.m. You can <br>
<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002184353/https://go.berniesanders.com/page/event/detail/rally/44jbd">RSVP here</a>.</p>

<p>Bronx Community College&apos;s Gould Memorial Library Auditorium (2155 University Avenue) will host the second rally. Doors open at 11 a.m. and the program begins at 1:30 p.m.; the public <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002184353/http://sendy.berniesanders.com/l/kogaJU763Y260ESEG7r5Ug763A/hdsp3KSgwt1juYFgIaG763wg/Bdqx8ImV5BCf763CQiiK38dw">can RSVP here</a>.</p>

<p>Sanders&apos; Democratic opponent, Hillary Clinton, is holding an organizing event in Industry City in Brooklyn on Saturday; she&apos;ll at the Landing Caf&#xE9; in Building 2 from 5:30 to 6:30 p.m. (doors open at 3:30 p.m.); <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002184353/https://www.hillaryclinton.com/events/view/7TREPQR2U4BIEC33/">public RSVP here</a>.</p>
