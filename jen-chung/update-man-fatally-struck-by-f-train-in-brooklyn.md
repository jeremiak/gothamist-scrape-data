<p>A person was struck by a train at a Brooklyn train station this morning.</p>

<p>The FDNY says they received a call at 9:18 a.m. about someone who apparently fell into the tracks at the 4th Avenue-9th Street station. The FDNY is still at the scene.</p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013232/http://www.mta.info/status/subway/BDFM/23533320">MTA announced these service changes</a>: &quot;There is no F train service between Church Av and Jay St-MetroTech in both directions. There is no G train service between Church Av and Bedford-Nostrand Avs in both directions. As an alternative customers are advised to use the following buses making nearby stations stops: B57, B61, B67 and B69. Allow additional travel time.&quot;</p>

<p><b>Update</b>: The NYPD says that at 9:20 a.m., police found a 28-year-old man, unconscious, in the tracks between the 4th Avenue and 7th Avenue stations. The man was pronounced dead and it was later determined that he was struck by a southbound F train. The investigation is still ongoing.</p>
