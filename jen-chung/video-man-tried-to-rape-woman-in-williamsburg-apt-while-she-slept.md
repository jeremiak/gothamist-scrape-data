<p>The police are looking for a man suspected of breaking into a Williamsburg apartment and trying to rape a woman sleeping inside.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred around 12:25 a.m. on Sunday, September 8, in the area of Metropolitan Avenue. The 21-year-old woman was sleeping when a man climbed on top of her and tried to rape her. The victim struggled and the suspect fled out a window. The victim had minor injuries.</p>

<p>The police also released video of the suspect, who is seen wheeling a bicycle.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20130912232934if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/HLlob-HJ6s0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspect is described as being 5&apos;4&quot;-5&apos;7&quot;, wearing a white t-shirt with a design on the left shoulder. </p>

<p>Police urge anyone with information in regards to this incident to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130912232934/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM </a>or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
