										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left" style=" width:230px; "> <img alt="2011_08_carcrash.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153257im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2011_08_carcrash.jpg" width="230" height="183"> <br> <i>Via WABC 7</i></div> Last night around 8:30 p.m., three cars were involved in an ultimately incendiary collision on the Upper West Side.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153257/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=8342004">WABC 7 reports</a> that the cars crashed at West 104th Street and Amsterdam Avenue: &quot;One of the cars burst into flames.  Another rammed into a bus shelter and ended up on the sidewalk.&quot;<p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153257/http://bwog.com/2011/09/04/car-crash-on-amsterdam-and-105th/">Bwog posted last night</a>, &quot;The fire is out now after a quick response from the FDNY and everybody has been removed from the wreck, but there is still a heavy amount of smoke around that you may have smelled.&quot;  Apparently six people were taken to the hospital with non-life-threatening injuries.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153257/http://www.dnainfo.com/20110905/upper-west-side/car-bursts-into-flames-after-multiple-vehicle-crash-on-uws">DNAinfo has more pictures</a> (including one of a car under another car) and  passerby took video of the car fire&#x2014;scary stuff:</p>
