title: Police Search for Robbers Who Pose As Cops
---

<p><img alt="2008_09_bklynsusp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20111117124255im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2008_09_bklynsusp.jpg" width="130" height="101" class="right">The police <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111117124255/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=6383910">released photographs of three men</a> suspected of impersonating police officers during robberies in Brooklyn.  According to WABC 7, &quot;Detectives say the men walked into a building on McDonald Avenue, pulled out guns, and displayed police badges.&quot;  They also handcuffed their victims before robbing them.  Their images were captured on a surveillance camera and the suspects are described as &quot;white males in their 30s.&quot;  Police are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111117124255/http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2008/09/police_seek_help_in_identifyin.html">asking for people with any information</a> to call Crimestoppers (800-577-TIPS) or to text 274637 and enter &quot;TIP577.&quot;</p>
