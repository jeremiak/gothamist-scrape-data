<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2010_06_prpade.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130618085626im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2010_06_prpade.jpg" width="120" height="112" class="image-right"> </span>Today is the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130618085626/http://www.nationalpuertoricandayparade.org/">Puerto Rican Day Parade</a> down Fifth Avenue.  The festivities, which are expected to have around 80,000 marchers and 3 million spectators, begin at 11 a.m., starting at 44th Street and ending at 86th Street.  The parade be airing on Fox 5 and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130618085626/http://myfoxny.com/">MyFoxNY.com</a> will have streaming video of the parade.  This year&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130618085626/http://gothamist.com/2010/06/10/marc_anthony_will_be_puerto_rican_d.php">grand marshal or &quot;godfather&quot; is Marc Anthony</a>, a last minute replacement after the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130618085626/http://gothamist.com/2010/06/08/soap_star_quits_parade_over_controv.php">original godfather stepped down</a> to his past <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130618085626/http://gothamist.com/2010/05/24/puerto_rican_day_parade_leader_caus.php">domestic abuse conviction</a>.
