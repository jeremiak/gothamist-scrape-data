<p>Police are investigating a fatal crash that occurred in Midtown Manhattan early this morning. <b>Update [more details below]</b>: The driver has been arrested.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred around 3:41 a.m. at Sixth Avenue and West 54th Street. A Nissan sedan was traveling north on Sixth Avenue when it mounted a curb and then hit a fire hydrant. The 31-year-old male driver was taken to Bellevue in critical condition, while the 33-year-old male passenger was taken to St. Luke&apos;s, where he was pronounced dead.</p>

<p>The car was badly damaged, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161028104602/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-Car-Slams-Into-Hydrant-Midtown-1-Dead-1-Injured-Detectives-Speed-381357211.html">NBC New York reports</a> the driver and passenger were &quot;trapped inside the crippled car and had to be removed by rescuers.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161028104602/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2016/05/31/deadly-midtown-crash/">A witness said</a>, &quot;I see the car coming, he was speeding, the light was red. It looked like he tried to stop, but when you&#x2019;re going that fast, the car goes haywire and he came and hit the fire hydrant. That&#x2019;s what stopped him.&#x201D;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161028104602/http://nypost.com/2016/05/31/passenger-killed-after-out-of-control-driver-crashes-into-nyc-fire-hydrant/">A police source told the Post</a>, &#x201C;Seeing that car the way it was, there was no way that it was doing the speed limit.&quot;</p>

<p>The police are investigating the incident.</p>

<p><b>Update</b>:  Police identified the deceased passenger as Juan Avila, 33, of Jackson Heights, Queens. The driver, Shiva Sharma, 32, of Wyandanch, Long Island, was arrested and charged with vehicular manslaughter. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161028104602/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/manhattan/manhattan-driver-crashes-fire-hydrant-killing-passenger-article-1.2655405">The Daily News reports</a> that police sources say Sharma was drunk.</p>
