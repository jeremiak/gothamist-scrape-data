title: NYPD: This Guy Stole $6,239 From The Paris Theatre
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_05_parist1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140529032853im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_05_parist1.jpg" width="320" height="204" class="image-right"> </span>The police are looking for a man suspected of stealing thousands from a Manhattan movie theater.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, a man entered the Paris Theatre at 4 West 58th Street (the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140529032853/http://gothamist.com/2013/03/06/the_best_movie_theaters_in_nyc.php">best &quot;jewel-box cinema&quot;</a> in the city) just after midnight last Saturday, May 17th. He claimed to be an office cleaner and managed to take $6,239 from the safe before fleeing.</p>

<p>He&apos;s described as being in his 20s, about 5&apos;9&quot; and 125 pounds. He was last seen carrying a skateboard and wearing a large backpck. Anyone with information in regards to this burglary is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140529032853/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">the Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
