<p>A van driver ran over a teenage girl in Queens shortly after midnight this morning and left her to die in the street. </p>

<p>Police say the 16-year-old girl, who has not yet been publicly identified pending family notification, was crossing Sunrise Highway in the southeast crosswalk at the intersection of Francis Lewis Boulevard shortly after 12:15 a.m. Investigators believe she was killed by the driver of a dark colored van traveling eastbound on Sunrise Highway.</p>

<p>Police and EMS responded to the scene, where they found the 16-year-old unconscious and unresponsive in the road. She was taken to Jamaica Hospital where she was pronounced dead.</p>

<p>The van driver did not stop and kept going east. Police say they are still investigating.</p>
