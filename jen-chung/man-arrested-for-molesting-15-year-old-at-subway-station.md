<p>Mario Escudero-Flores, 29, of Brooklyn, was taken into custody after a<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150501173513/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/suspect-mohawk-accused-groping-15-year-old-girl-subway-platform-nabbed-cops-article-1.1096169?localLinksEnabled=false"> tipster recognized him</a>. <p>Escudero-Flores was charged with sexual misconduct, forcible touching, and endangering the welfare of a child.  </p>
