</div></div>					<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503144315/http://sfist.com/2012/03/06/its_not_the_ipad_3_its_the_ipad_hd.php">new iPad&#x2014;the third generation</a>&#x2014;is officially on sale today and, like clockwork, there are lines outside stores.  This is the first product release for the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503144315/http://gothamist.com/2011/12/08/more_photos_of_the_grand_central_ap.php">new Apple Store at Grand Central</a>, and it seems like the terminal&apos;s cavernous halls are perfect for waiting during this miserable day, some of the lines are inside long hallways.</p>

<p>One person at Grand Central&#x2014;CNN tech writer Julianne Pepitone&#x2014; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503144315/https://twitter.com/#!/julpepitone/status/180626250719703040">Tweeted</a>, &quot;People are literally running up the stairs at Grand Central Apple Store to claim a new #iPad, as store staffers cheer.&quot; She also <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503144315/https://twitter.com/#!/julpepitone/status/180632503772651521">shared this exchange</a>, &quot;Me: &quot;Hi, I&apos;m from CNN. Can I interview you about the #iPad?&quot; Middle-aged woman: &quot;F--- you.&quot; Yep, it&apos;s time to head back to the office.&quot;</p>
