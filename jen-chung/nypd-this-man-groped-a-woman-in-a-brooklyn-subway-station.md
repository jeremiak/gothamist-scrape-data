<p>Police are searching for a man who groped a woman at a subway station at the Broadway Junction subway station in Brooklyn.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred during the late afternoon commute, at about 4:45 p.m. on February 8th. Police say the man &quot;approached the 33 year-old female victim in the mezzanine inside the Broadway East New York &apos;A&apos; train subway station&quot; and then &quot;proceeded to grab the victim&apos;s buttocks and midsection over her clothes before fleeing to parts unknown.&quot;</p>
