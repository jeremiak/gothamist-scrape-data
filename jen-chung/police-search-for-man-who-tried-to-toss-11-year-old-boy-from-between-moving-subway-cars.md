title: Police Search For Man Who Tried To Toss 11-Year-Old Boy From Between Moving Subway Cars
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_10_movingsubway.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016014746im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_10_movingsubway.jpg" width="640" height="425"> <br> <i> istockphoto</i></div> <p></p>

<p>A 11-year-old boy was nearly thrown off a moving 4 train by a stranger, and police are looking for the suspect, who is now wanted for assault.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016014746/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_10_usassault.jpg"> <img alt="2016_10_usassault.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016014746im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2016/10/2016_10_usassault-thumb-300x411-969516.jpg" width="250" height="342" class="image-right"> </a> </span>The incident occurred last Wednesday, October 5th, around 3:20 p.m. on a downtown 4 train. The boy <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016014746/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Stranger-Grabs-Picks-Up-Shakes-Boy-Moving-Subway-Train-Manhattan-396741951.html">was with friends</a> when, police say, the suspect &quot;pulled the 11 year-old male victim into the area in between the train cars, picked him up and shook him as he was going to throw him off the train.&quot;</p>

<p>A 26-year-old female passenger stopped the man and managed bring the boy, who had bumped his head on the side of the train, back inside, according to the NYPD. Then the boy and the woman, whom police described as a good Samaritan, left the train at Union Square/14th Street. The perpetrator remained on the train. </p>

<p>Police released a sketch of the suspect (right) and describe him as being in his 30s and around 6&apos;. He was last seen wearing a black sweater and a multi-colored baseball cap.<br>
