<p>A 13-year-old boy on a C train in Brooklyn was stabbed last night after an apparent argument with another passenger who police have identified as a teenage girl.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred around 6 p.m.: The victim was on a Manhattan-bound C train that was heading towards the Ralph Avenue station &quot;when he became engaged in a verbal dispute with the individual being sought. As the train car doors opened at the Ralph Avenue subway station, the individual displayed a knife from her bag and stabbed the victim&quot; once in the abdomen.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314142519/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_04_suspect81pct.jpg"> <img alt="2016_04_suspect81pct.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314142519im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2016/04/2016_04_suspect81pct-thumb-400x385-942136.jpg" width="300" height="288" class="image-right"> </a> </span><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314142519/http://nypost.com/2016/04/07/teen-girl-accused-of-stabbing-boy-in-stomach-on-the-subway/">The Post reports</a>, &quot;The boy staggered off the train and told an MTA employee manning the station booth, &apos;A girl stabbed me!&apos;&quot; The MTA worker called 911 and the victim was taken to an area hospital, where he is in stable condition. </p>

<p>The police released images of the suspect, describing her as being 14-16 years old, about 5&apos;5&quot; and 130 pounds with brown eyes, last seen wearing a black leather jacket, and light blue jeans.</p>
