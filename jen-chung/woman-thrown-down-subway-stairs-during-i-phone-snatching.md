title: Woman Thrown Down Subway Stairs During iPhone Snatching
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_10_bxburg1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012305im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_10_bxburg1.jpg" width="220" height="221" class="image-right"> </span>A woman was violently attacked during a cellphone snatching on Monday.</p>

<p>According to the police, at around 9:30 a.m., a 25-year-old woman was on a southbound 6 train that stopped at St. Lawrence Avenue in the Bronx. When the train doors open, the NYPD says that &quot;an unknown male snatched the victim&apos;s cellphone (white iPhone 5) from her hand as she exited the train.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim struggled with the thief, and she was ultimately &quot;thrown down the stairs leading to the street causing bruising and lacerations to her right hand,&quot; according to police. The suspect fled and the victim was treated by EMS at the scene. Police released video of the suspect, who is described as being about 5&apos; and weighing 140 pounds: </p>
