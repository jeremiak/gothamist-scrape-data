</div></div>					<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012239/http://www.esbnyc.com/">Empire State Building</a> brought back its Halloween Light Show last night. The building&apos;s LEDs can display <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012239/http://www.esbnyc.com/explore/tower-lights">&quot;16 million colors, which can change instantaneously.&quot;</a> So you weren&apos;t having a stroke when you saw the ESB last night&#x2014;it was a show&#x2014;and if you missed it, here&apos;s an aerial view:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510012239if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/JzRwGHrE_cA?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The show was simulcast to a program on iHeartMedia, Z100 and 103.5 KTU and songs included &quot;This Is Halloween,&quot; &quot;Ghostbusters,&quot; &quot;Monster Mash,&quot; &quot;Halloween&quot; and &quot;Somebody&apos;s Watching Me.&quot;</p>
