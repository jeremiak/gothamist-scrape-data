<p>For a peek at what New Yorkers were curious about in 2014, Google has compiled its top searches for New York. </p>

<p>Naturally, the World Cup, the weeks-long international event, was #1 for &quot;Top trending searches&quot; and &quot;Top trending news/events&quot; but <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011116/http://gothamist.com/tags/avonteoquendo">Avonte Oquendo</a>, the autistic boy who went missing from his public school in 2013 and was later found dead, was New York&apos;s &quot;Top trending person.&quot; </p>

<p>New Yorkers were also very interested in Flappy Bird, Unemployment Extension, Ferguson Missouri and Captain American 2. They can&apos;t let go of Frozen either.</p>
