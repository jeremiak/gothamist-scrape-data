					<p>It happened: <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170913055140/http://gothamist.com/2015/10/09/mets_playoffs_sports_meaning.php">The 2015 New York Mets</a> are the champions of National League and are headed to the World Series! </p>

<p>The Mets brought the broom to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170913055140/http://chicagoist.com/2015/10/21/_its_do_or_die.php">Wrigley Field</a> last night, defeating the Chicago Cubs 8-3 in Game 4, and leaving Cubbies fans without a World Series appearance, yet again. The Mets&apos; sterling pitching was aided by Lucas Duda, who hit a home run in the first inning and had five RBIs by the end of the second inning. Travis D&apos;Arnaud also hit a first inning home run.</p>

<p><iframe src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170913055140if_/http://m.mets.mlb.com/shared/video/embed/embed.html?content_id=525138083&amp;topic_id=94788780&amp;width=640&amp;height=360&amp;property=mlb" width="640" height="360" frameborder="0">Your browser does not support iframes.</iframe></p>
