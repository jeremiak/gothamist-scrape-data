<p>Police are hoping the public can help them find a man accused of groping a girl at a Brooklyn train station earlier this month.<br>
 <br>
<span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_06_ftsuspect.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108032336im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_06_ftsuspect.jpg" width="250" height="274" class="image-left"> </span>The NYPD says the forcible touching incident occurred just before 5 p.m. on Thursday, June 9, when the suspect &quot;approached the 12 year-old female victim inside the mezzanine area of the Stillwell Avenue subway station and grabbed her buttocks.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as being in his 30s, and police released a surveillance image of him. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108032336/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
