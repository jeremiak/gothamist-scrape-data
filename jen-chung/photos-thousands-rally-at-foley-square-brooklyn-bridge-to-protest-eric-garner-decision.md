</div></div>					<p>A day after a Staten Island grand jury r<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115093813/http://gothamist.com/2014/12/03/eric_garner_grand_jury.php">efused to indict a police officer </a>who put an unarmed black man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115093813/http://gothamist.com/2014/07/18/asthmatic_staten_island_man_dies_wh.php">in a fatal chokehold</a>, protesters are back on the streets. Thousands&#x2014;one report says over 7,500&#x2014;have gathered at Foley Square in lower Manhattan.</p>

<p>The protest is breaking into a number of groups and spreading out&#x2014;a couple thousand at the bridge, a few thousand heading to the West Side highway and around 4,000 at Chambers and Broadway. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115093813/https://www.facebook.com/events/450074491800495/">Another protest is planned for Sara D. Roosevelt Park</a> on the Lower East Side; organized by the Trayvon Martin Organizing Committee NYC.</p>
