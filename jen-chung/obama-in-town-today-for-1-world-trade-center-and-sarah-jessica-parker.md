<p>President Obama is in town to raise money for his re-election campaign and rub shoulders <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506145133/http://gothamist.com/2012/06/10/obama_visits_nyc_this_week_to_shake.php">with Sarah Jessica Parker and Anna Wintour</a>. And, oh, yeah, he and the First Lady will tour 1 World Trade Center with Mayor Bloomberg, NY Governor Cuomo and NJ Governor Christie.</p>

<p>The 1 WTC visit, which is a preview to the topping-off ceremony, is scheduled for 4:30 p.m. Which means the gridlock alerts begin well before then. For instance, the PATH between Exchange Place and the WTC is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506145133/https://twitter.com/NotifyNYC/status/213044076247920641">being suspended between 3 p.m. and 6 p.m.</a>  Here&apos;s the scoop on traffic on city streets&#x2014;watch out, West Village and Midtown!&#x2014; from Gridlock Sam:</p>
