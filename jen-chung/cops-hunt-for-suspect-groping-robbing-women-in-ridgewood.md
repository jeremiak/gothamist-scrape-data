										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_03_ridgewoodsusp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160904051926im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_03_ridgewoodsusp.jpg" width="290" height="360" class="image-right"> </span>Last Saturday, two women in Queens were shoved and groped by a man trying to rob them&#x2014;and now the police have released a video and sketch of the suspect.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the first incident occurred at 1:42 a.m. on March 19: A 20-year-old woman was walking home when an unknown male &quot;approached her from behind and pushed her to the ground. The individual then grabbed the victim&apos;s genitals over her clothing and fled the location on foot without having removed any property.&quot;<br>
 <br>
Police say the second incident occurred six minutes later, when a 25-year-old woman walking near Harman Street &quot;when an unknown individual approached her from behind and put his hand around her neck. The individual then tried to put his hands inside the victim&apos;s pants, and removed the victim&apos;s purse and its contents, before fleeing the location on foot.&quot;</p>
