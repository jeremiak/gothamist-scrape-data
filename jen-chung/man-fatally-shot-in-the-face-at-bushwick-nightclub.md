<p>A 22-year-old died after being shot in the face at a Brooklyn club earlier this morning.</p>

<p>Police say that officers responded to a 911 call about a shooting at 1269 Flushing Avenue in Bushwick around 5:21 a.m. Inside, officers found a 22-year-old man with a gunshot wound to his face, according to the NYPD, which adds that EMS took him to Woodhull Hospital where he was pronounced dead.</p>

<p>The location is known as Brooklyn Asylum, where <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170821065431/https://allevents.in/brooklyn/shamrock-suffle/387792984924345">dance events</a> and music performances are held. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170821065431/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/22-year-old-fatally-shot-brooklyn-club-article-1.3002579">The Daily News reports</a>, &quot;The assailant shot a fellow reveler once in the face.&quot;</p>

<p>The police are still investigating the homicide.</p>
