<p>Investigators are looking for a suspect wanted for grand larceny&#x2014;for stealing hundreds of thousands of dollars in cash from an armored truck earlier in the summer.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the Rapid Armored Corporation Armored Truck was parked near East Fordham Road and East Kingsbridge in the Bronx on July 30 just before 6 p.m. No one was in the truck&#x2014;which was left running&#x2014;when a suspect entered the vehicle, moved it to 2540 Valentine Avenue, and grabbed <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130915063743/http://www.ny1.com/content/news/188637/police-seek-man-who-allegedly-stole-more-than--400-000-from-armored-truck-in-bronx">about $400,000 in cash</a> before leaving in a waiting silver car.</p>
