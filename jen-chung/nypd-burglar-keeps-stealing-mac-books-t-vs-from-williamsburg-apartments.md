<p>The NYPD wants the public&apos;s help to find a suspect wanted for 15 burglaries, plus an attempted burglary, in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150514060707/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/precincts/precinct_090.shtml">90th Precinct</a> since September.</p>

<p>The police detailed all the incidents, and it seems that the suspect is targeting Apple products (MacBooks, iPads), TVs, gaming systems, cash, credit cards and jewelry:</p>

Incident#4: On Sunday, October 12,2014 at 1230 hours, in the vicinity of Meserole Avenue and Banker Street, suspect removed (4) Apple MacBook&#x2019;s and a ring.<br>
Incident#5: On Tuesday, October 14,2014 at 0845 hours, in the vicinity of Bushwick Avenue and Humboldt Street, suspect removed a TV, PlayStation and an X-Box.<br> 
Incident#6: On Saturday, October 18,2014 at 1400 hours, in the vicinity of Ainslie Street and Woodpoint Road, suspect entered the location through a rear window but was unable to remove any property from the location.<br>

<p>Police describe the suspect as being a male who was last seen wearing a winter jacket, pants, sneakers and a winter hate (the photo is from the October 31 incident). <br>
