<p>A 52-year-old woman was raped at a karaoke club in Flushing last month, and now the police are releasing photographs of the suspects in hopes the public can help find him.</p>

<p>The incident occurred at Flower Karaoke, located at 162-05 Crocheron Avenue in Flushing, at around 2:39 a.m. on July 14. The victim &quot;socialized with the unknown individual for approximately two hours, after which time he raped her inside of a rented room at the location,&quot; according to the NYPD.<br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as being 40-50 years old and last seen wearing an orange shirt and black pants. He also spoke Korean. </p>
