title: Off-Duty Corrections Cop Shot in the Bronx
---

<p><img alt="2008_06_bxcopshot.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20110611051449im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2008_06_bxcopshot.jpg" width="260" height="194" class="left">An off-duty corrections officer was reportedly shot four times in what <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110611051449/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=6203115">WABC 7 calls</a> &quot;an apparent gunfight with several men.&quot;  The officer had been walking home around 1:45 a.m. and the shooting occurred at 1791 Walton, at East 175th Street.  </p>

<p>The officer was found by police, who had <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110611051449/http://www.wnbc.com/news/16595524/detail.html?dl=mainclick">responded to a 911 call about gunfire</a>--witnesses told WNBC &quot;they heard as many as a dozen shots ring out.&quot;  The victim, who may have also fired, had four gunshot wounds, two in the arms and two in the buttocks, and was taken to the hospital in stable condition.</p>

<p>The officer is assigned to Rikers Island.  Police are looking for suspects, as well as a motive for the shooting.</p>
