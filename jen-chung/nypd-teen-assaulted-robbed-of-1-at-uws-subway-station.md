title: NYPD: Teen Assaulted, Robbed Of $1 At UWS Subway Station
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420070601/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_04_cpwrobbery.jpg"> <img alt="2014_04_cpwrobbery.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140420070601im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2014/04/2014_04_cpwrobbery-thumb-450x470-839045.jpg" width="280" height="292" class="image-right"> </a> </span>The police are looking for a man who allegedly attacked and then mugged a girl at the West 96th Street and Central Park West B/C subway station last month.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident happened at 10 a.m. on Saturday, March 1. The 15-year-old victim was entering the subway stairwell when she was approached by a man. The man allegedly said, &quot;Give me everything you have,&quot; assaulted her and then stole $1 from her jacket before fleeing. </p>

<p>The police released images and videos of the suspect. Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). </p>

<p>The public can also submit their tips to <a href="www.nypdcrimestoppers.com">Crime Stoppers&apos; website</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.</p>
