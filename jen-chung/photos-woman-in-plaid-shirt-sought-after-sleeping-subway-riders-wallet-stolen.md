										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_04_plaidthief.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150501173438im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_04_plaidthief.jpg" width="280" height="220" class="image-right"> </span>Pro-tip: Don&apos;t fall asleep on a subway train unless you want your wallet stolen and a Jewel-lookalike to use your ATM card!</p>

<p>The NYPD wants your help in locating a woman wanted in connection to a grand larceny that occurred on January 6 of this year: Apparently a guy was sleeping on an N train at 9th Street subway station in Brooklyn around 10:45 a.m. When he awoke, he realized his wallet, watch and cellphone were gone! </p>

<p>The police released these images of a suspect, described as a white woman, using the victim&apos;s ATM card. </p>

<p>Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto Crime Stoppers&apos; website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150501173438/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.</p>
