title: Woman Fights Off Rapist In Central Park Near West 64th Street
---


                            
                            
                            
                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_07_cprape2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807013107im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_07_cprape2.jpg" width="200" height="267" class="image-right"> </span>Early yesterday morning, a woman was attacked and almost raped in Central Park. The police released a surveillance photograph of the suspect.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, a 30-year-old woman was near West 64th Street and West Drive at 2:40 a.m. when a man tried to rape her. He eventually fled and the victim was not injured. </p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807013107/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/broadway-actress-attacked-raped-central-park-article-1.1388897?localLinksEnabled=false">Daily News reports that the victim is a Broadway actress</a>, &quot;The victim, 30, had gone out drinking at a bar following the play&apos;s Monday night production and later struck up a conversation with the suspect, sources said. The woman, who was intoxicated, managed to fight the suspect off by bashing him with her purse, a cop source said. The creep then snatched the victim&apos;s pocketbook and bolted.&quot;</p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807013107/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/actress_fends_off_rapist_hkhY12kM360gcGw7wsJazM?utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_content=Local">Post&apos;s sources say</a> the victim was &quot;intoxicated as she entered the park...and the man began to help her. He then tried to rape her, but ran off with her purse after she fought back, cops said.&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807013107/http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20130702/times-square-theater-district/broadway-actress-sexually-assaulted-robbed-central-park">DNAinfo says</a> the suspect is described as a man about 5&apos;8&quot; with black hair and black eyes.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807013107/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM&quot;&gt;the Crime Stoppers website</a> at or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>

<p>Last week, a man was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807013107/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/30/man_arrested_for_attempted_rape_of_1.php">arrested for attempted rape</a> of a jogger in Central Park.</p>
