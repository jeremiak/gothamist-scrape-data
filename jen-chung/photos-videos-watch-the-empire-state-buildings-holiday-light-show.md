</div></div>					<p>Have you noticed the Empire State Building&apos;s lights twinkling around 7 p.m. the past couple nights? That&apos;s because the iconic skyscraper is in the middle of its Holiday music-and-light show&#x2014;and there are three more nights to bask in its glory!</p>

<p>On Saturday, &#x201C;Jingle Bell Rock&quot; from Bobby Helms and and Keith Richards&apos; version of &#x201C;Run Rudolph Run&#x201D; were set to the building&apos;s LED lights, with the music on 106.7 Lite fm and IHeartRadio&apos;s app. Last night, it was Brenda Lee&apos;s &quot;Rockin&#x2019; Around the Christmas Tree&quot; and Karmin&apos;s &quot;Sleigh Ride.&quot;</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510010936if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/-tDijrYWQP0?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510010936if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/Mto70o7UkKc?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Tonight, the songs are Dean Martin&apos;s &quot;Let It Snow&quot; and Darlene Love&apos;s &quot;All Alone on Christmas&quot; and Tuesday has Burl Ives&apos; &quot;Holly Jolly Christmas&quot; and The Waitresses&apos; &quot;Christmas Wrapping.&quot; Here&apos;s where you can see the shows.</p>
