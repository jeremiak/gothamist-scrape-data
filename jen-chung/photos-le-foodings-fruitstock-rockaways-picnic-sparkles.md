</div></div>					<p>Le Grand Fooding kicked off its Rockaways picnic lunches yesterday with bright flavors that perfectly complemented the gorgeous summer weather. </p>

<p>The picnic area at Beach 97th Street has been transformed into <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504173940/http://evenements.lefooding.com/2014/le-grand-fooding/new-york/">Le Fooding &amp; San Pellegrino Fruitstock at The Rockaways</a>, with a rotating group of chefs offering their twists on beach eats. Yesterday, Ivan Orkin of Ivan Ramen created a light, yet surprisingly filling, summer chilled lemon shio dashi ramen. And today, Los Angeles chef Ludo Lefebvre is making grilled veal breast, crispy artichoke, parmesan cream, dried bonito; on Sunday, Cosme&apos;s Enrique Olvera is serving red chilaquiles and burrata. </p>

<p>In addition to the rotating meat dishes, all weekend long, Rockaway Taco&apos;s Andrew Field will hit the savory spot with cornmeal crostini sopes. Momofuku Milk Bar dessert guru Christina Tosi, who is serving up popsicles this weekend, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504173940/http://gothamist.com/2014/07/09/le_fooding_rockaway.php">told us earlier this week</a>, &quot;I love to make refreshing desserts when it&apos;s hot out! Something light with citrus notes or effervescence is always a great start. Popsicles are an easy go to if I have the foresight&quot;&#x2014;diners can pick between grapefruit popsicles or cereal milk pudding popsicles. Mixologist Lynnette Marrero&apos;s Italian &quot;mocktails,&quot; made with San Pellegrino&apos;s Clementine and Pomegranate-and-Orange sodas also round out the menu. </p>

<p>Plus, there&apos;s music from the New York Brass Bands and a rotating group of DJs&#x2014;the food-loving duo <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504173940/http://cibomattocibomatto.blogspot.com/">Cibo Matto</a> (remember <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504173940/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EN9auBn6Jys">&quot;Sugar Water&quot;</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504173940/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=COMWwwv_MTk">&quot;Know Your Chicken&quot;</a>?) were there yesterday:</p>

<center><iframe src="//web.archive.org/web/20150504173940if_/http://instagram.com/p/qUxXrRAzOh/embed/" width="612" height="710" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowtransparency="true"></iframe></center>

<p>Tickets, which are $40 per person, are sold out but <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504173940/http://www.nydailynews.com/life-style/eats/fruitstock-rockaways-article-1.1863756">some tickets will be available</a> during each of the two sessions&#x2014;between 12 p.m. and 4 p.m.&#x2014; today and tomorrow. Ten percent of proceeds are going to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504173940/http://www.graybeards.com/">Graybeards</a>, a Rockaways community non-profit.</p>
