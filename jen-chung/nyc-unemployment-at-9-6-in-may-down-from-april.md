title: NYC Unemployment At 9.6% In May, Down From April
---

<p>The NY State Department of Labor released the latest job numbers (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811112348/http://www.labor.ny.gov/stats/pressreleases/pruistat.pdf">PDF</a>), and NY State&apos;s unemployment rate for May was 8.3%, down from April&apos;s 8.4% (the<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811112348/http://online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424052748703650604575313183415518878.html?mod=WSJ_NY_LEFTTopStories"> lowest since April 2009</a>).  And for NYC, the rate was 9.6% in May, vs. 9.8% in April.  According to the Wall Street Journal, &quot;New York City added 14,700 private-sector jobs last month, which is in line with the historical average for May, [a Labor Department economist] said. It was city&apos;s fifth consecutive month of growth.&quot;</p>
