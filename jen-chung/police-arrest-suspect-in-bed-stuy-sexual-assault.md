<p>Police say they have arrested a man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425175312/http://gothamist.com/2014/05/18/video_cops_seek_suspect_in_bed-stuy.php">who allegedly grabbed a woman</a> walking on a Brooklyn street and then sexually assaulted her earlier this month. </p>

<p>The NYPD had said a 22-year-old woman, who was walking on the sidewalk in the 79th Precinct around 1 a.m. on Saturday, May 17th, was followed by a man who eventually pulled her between two cars, threatened her with a gun, and sexually abused her. The suspect fled and the victim was treated for her injuries.</p>

<p>Police, who shared <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425175312/http://gothamist.com/2014/05/21/police_release_new_video_showing_be.php">two videos of the suspect</a>, now say that Elijah Lofton, 19, was arrested on Wednesday and charged with arrested with felony criminal sex act.</p>
