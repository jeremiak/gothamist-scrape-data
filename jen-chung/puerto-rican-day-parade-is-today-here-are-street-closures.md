<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140611121526/http://www.nprdpinc.org/">57th Annual Puerto Rican Day Parade</a> is today. Two million people are expected to gather in Manhattan to cheer and show their Boricua pride.</p>

<p>With a theme &quot;Un Pueblo, Muchas Voces&quot;/&quot;One Nation, Many Voices,&quot; this year&apos;s parade features Grand Marshals Congresswoman Nydia Velazquez and City Council Speaker Melissa Mark-Viverito. Others honorees include actress Lauren Velez; DJ Polito Vega; actress and Puerto Rican Traveling Theatre founder Mirian Colon.</p>
