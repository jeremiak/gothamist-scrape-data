<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820064041/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/08/nyc_hits_record_electricity_use_to.php">It&apos;s been a</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820064041/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/08/slightly_cooler_drier_but_still_uns.php">gross steam bath</a> in New York City over the past week. And while we&apos;re all praying for a thunderstorm to take the edge off of things, let&apos;s be happy we&apos;re not dealing with the insane <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820064041/http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/ottawa/story/2013/07/08/toronto-weather-heavy-rain.html">rain dumped on Toronto today</a>&#x2014;Canadian weather services estimated nearly 100 mm/four inches would have fallen by the evening!</p>
