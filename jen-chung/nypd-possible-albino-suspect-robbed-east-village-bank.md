<p>The police are looking for the public&apos;s help in identifying the suspect in a bank robbery that took place yesterday in downtown Manhattan.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the suspect entered the Chase Bank at 130 2nd Avenue (you know, the former 2nd Avenue Deli location) yesterday at 2:34 p.m. &quot;where he handed a bank teller a note claiming to have a gun and demanding money. The teller complied and the suspect fled the location with $1,080.&quot;</p>

<p>Here&apos;s the police description of the suspect: &quot;white male (possibly albino), 5&apos;10&quot;, mid to late 20s, light colored hair with a medium build.  He was last seen wearing a black and grey jacket, dark rimmed glasses, black sneakers with a red bandana around his neck.&quot; <br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013048/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
