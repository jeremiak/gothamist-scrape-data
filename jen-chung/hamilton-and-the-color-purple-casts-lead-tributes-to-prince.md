										<p>Spike Lee&apos;s exuberant <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160904032235/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/22/spike_lee_prince_block_party.php">Prince block party</a> wasn&apos;t the only NYC tribute to the late, great musician: The Broadway casts of <em>Hamilton</em> and <em>The Color Purple</em> took time after their performances to have a moment celebrating <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160904032235/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/21/prince.php">Prince&apos;s incomparable legacy</a>.</p>
