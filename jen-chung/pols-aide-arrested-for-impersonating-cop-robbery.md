Earlier this year, Arroyo&apos;s grandson pleaded guilty to stealing over $115,000 from her non-profit&#x2014;and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130617141217/http://gothamist.com/2010/04/28/state_payroll_still_includes_convic.php">still remained on her payroll</a> as her chief of staff!</p>
