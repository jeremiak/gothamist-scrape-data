<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2009_08_outcvan.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20110707162441im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2009_08_outcvan.jpg" width="145" height="97" class="image-right"> </span>Last night, a woman was injured when an out-of-control van <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110707162441/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/2009/08/30/2009-08-30_queens_van_crash_hurts_virginia_woman.html">came &quot;skidding&quot; off the Flushing Meadows-Corona Park exit</a> in Queens.  According to the Daily News, &quot;the woman, who had come from Virginia to attend a high school reunion, was talking on a cell phone when the van smashed into the parked Toyota she was standing next to, police sources said.&quot; A witness <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110707162441/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=6989864">told WABC 7</a>, &quot;The white van was on the expressway changing lane to lane and going very fast. <strong>For some reason it then turned towards the park, came on the grass, crashed into a blue car then hit one of the girls. She went flying up into the air and then the van just kept going and spinning around, eventually around the tree</strong>.&quot; The victim, Paula Jimenez, is reportedly in critical condition.  The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110707162441/http://nypost.com/seven/08302009/news/regionalnews/runaway_van_strikes_woman_in_qns__187220.htm">Post reports</a> the driver, Jian Seng He, 49, was &quot;charged with assault, reckless endangerment and leaving the scene of an accident&quot;&#x2014;he also may have been fleeing from another accident at the time.  </p>
