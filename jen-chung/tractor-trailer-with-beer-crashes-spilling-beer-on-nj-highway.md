<p>NJ roads have not been kind to libations lately. Last month, a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130930090424/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/05/tanker_full_of_6000_gallons_of_scot.php">tanker full of Scotch overturned</a> in Woodbridge and caught fire. Today, a tractor-trailer carrying Coors and Coors Light crashed on I-78 in Clinton.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130930090424/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_jersey&amp;id=9170267">According to WABC 7</a>, the tractor-trailer carrying the beer sideswiped another truck (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130930090424/http://www.nj.com/hunterdon/index.ssf/2013/07/beer_truck_crash_on_i-78_in_clinton_kills_driver_blocks_traffic.html#incart_river_default">a flatbed carrying steel pipes</a>), &quot;which was parked on the side of the road.&quot; Two people were taken to the hospital.</p>

<p>Cases of beer are strewn on the highway and the investigation is ongoing. </p>
