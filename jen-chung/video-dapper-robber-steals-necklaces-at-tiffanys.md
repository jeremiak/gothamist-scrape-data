<p>The police are looking for a man wearing a suit and tie who stole two necklaces from the Fifth Avenue Tiffany &amp; Co. And there&apos;s video!</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, last Friday at about 3 p.m., the man entered Tiffany&apos;s and &quot;employee in conversation.&quot; Then he took two necklaces from the counter and fled. </p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20131027091917if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/2ifJrfRrU_Y" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The police describe this as a grand larceny, which means the necklaces were <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131027091917/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Larceny#Grand_larceny">worth more than $1000</a>&#x2014;the Post says <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131027091917/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/jewel_thief_hits_tiffany_9F3akwnqCtWEyRTYokIKHN?utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_content=Local">they were worth $90,000</a>!</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131027091917/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
