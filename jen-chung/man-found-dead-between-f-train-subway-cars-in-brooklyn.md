<p>Police are investigating the death of a 24-year-old man whose body was found between subway cars in a downtown Brooklyn subway station early this morning.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, officers responded to a 911 call at 2:31 a.m. about an unconscious male on a southbound F train at the Jay Street-MetroTech stop. Cops at the scene found the man, unconscious and unresponsive, between two train cars. He showed signs of head trauma.</p>

<p>The man was pronounced dead at nearby Brooklyn Hospital Center.</p>
