										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160507203123/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_12_tmusa20.jpg"> <img alt="2015_12_tmusa20.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160507203123im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2015/12/2015_12_tmusa20-thumb-500x614-927407.jpg" width="250" height="307" class="image-left"> </a> </span>Police are hoping the public can help them find a man accused of public lewdness&#x2014;a search which may be somewhat facilitated by the distinctive writing on his face.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on Sunday, December 27th on the northbound 2 train. Investigators say the man &quot;had his hands down his pants and appeared to be manipulating his penis for more than 30 minutes while looking at a 29-year-old female victim&quot; as the train headed north from the President Street train station around 4 p.m.</p>

<p>The NYPD released a photograph of the suspect taken by the victim; the picture (click for larger image) shows the words &quot;TEAM USA&quot; on his right forehead and another design on his right cheek.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS.The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160507203123/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>

<p>You can also report any inappropriate subway behavior to the MTA: Here is their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160507203123/http://mta-nyc.custhelp.com/app/ask_sh">website where you can detail the incident</a>, as well upload a photo.</p>
