<p>Charming former <em>Degrassi</em> actor and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015139/http://gothamist.com/2016/05/15/snl_videos_drake_tackles_his_meme_m.php#photo-1">SNL host</a>&#x2014;and rap superstar&#x2014;Drake is coming back to New York for his and Future&apos;s Summer Sixteen tour which starts a four-night stand at Madison Square Garden tomorrow. And of course he has the latest performer accessory: A pop-up shop.</p>

<p>Drizzy announced on social media that his pop-up shop would be at 208 Bowery from Thursday, August 4th through Sunday, August 7th (days after <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015139/http://www.complex.com/style/2016/08/future-nyc-pop-up-preview">Future&apos;s pop-up</a> opened at 76 Wooster Street):</p>

<p>This is actually Drake&apos;s second pop-up this year&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015139/https://twitter.com/pitchfork/status/722178921941360641">he had one for his album <em>Views</em></a> in April, and this time around (according to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015139/http://www.hotnewhiphop.com/drake-announces-his-summer-sixteen-pop-up-shop-is-opening-in-nyc-this-week-news.23245.html">Hot New Hip Hop</a>) &quot;some of the items that will be in stock include a sweatshirt that reads &quot;REVENGE&quot; across the chest, t-shirts with Drake&apos;s face screen printed on the front, some of his most notable catch phrases, the praying hands emoji and of course, the typical Summer Sixteen tour merch.&quot; Hope the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015139/http://gothamist.com/2010/06/15/near_riot_nypd_shuts_down_drakes_se.php#photo-1">riot police are ready</a>. </p>

<p>Pop-up shops for musicians are now de rigueur, with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015139/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/19/photos_video_kanye_west_pop-up.php#photo-1">Kanye West</a> and Justin Bieber&apos;s successful forays into fashion (Barneys even carried Bieber&apos;s Purpose line). <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015139/http://www.nytimes.com/2016/07/14/fashion/justin-bieber-kanye-west-concert-merchandise.html?_r=0">In a trend piece</a>, the NY Times spoke to fashion blogger Chiara Ferragni who bought a Bieber t-shirt, &#x201C;I saw it on one of my friends and I was like, &apos;I have to get that.&apos; Something that back in the day we would have found so stupid and never be caught dead wearing is now the hit piece of the season.&quot;</p>
