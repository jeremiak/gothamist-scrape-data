										<p>Last year, Jimmy Fallon got Mets ace Matt Harvey to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506143015/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/16/video_mets_ace_matt_harvey_asks_bas.php">ask Mets fans about Matt Harvey</a> in a hilarious bit. Now, with the Yankees ready <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506143015/http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/baseball/yankees/robbie-jogging-back-returns-bronx-m-article-1.1771212">to face the Seattle Mariners</a> and former Yankee Robinson Cano, Fallon enlisted Cano for some more fun.  </p>

<p>Yankees fans said they&apos;d totally boo Cano&#x2014;who left for a $240 million contract&#x2014;on his return to Yankee Stadium, so the Tonight Show staffer at Bryant Park told them to yell at a huge cardboard box with Cano&apos;s face on it. Take a guess who emerged from the box:</p>

<p>The fans, some of who said things like &quot;You don&apos;t belong here!&quot; and &quot;You suck!&quot;, were agog when they saw Cano. They eagerly shook his hand and said, &quot;Welcome back!&quot; and &quot;Listen... about that...&quot;</p>
