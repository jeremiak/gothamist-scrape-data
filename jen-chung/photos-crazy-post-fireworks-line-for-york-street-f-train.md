</div></div>					<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150523014201/http://gothamist.com/2014/07/05/photos_videos_epic_july_4th_firewor.php">triumphant return of the fireworks to the East River</a> meant many people were able to see the dazzling show from Brooklyn. However, in the five years since the last East River display, Brooklyn has become the &quot;hip&quot; borough&#x2014;which means the wait for the F train at the York Street station was insane. The perfect antidote to your pyrotechnic, patriotic buzz!</p>
