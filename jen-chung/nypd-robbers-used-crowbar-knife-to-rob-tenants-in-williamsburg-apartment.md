<p>Police are asking for the public&apos;s help in identifying three robbery suspects who struck in Brooklyn early yesterday morning.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the robbery took place at 1:45 a.m. on Wednesday, October 21: Three males &quot;armed with a crowbar and a knife, entered an apartment on Hewes Street, through an open door, and demanded property.&quot; The victims, two 30-year-old men and a 38-year-old man, agreed and police say the suspects &quot;fled with an undetermined amount of money.&quot;</p>

<p>No one was injured. The police released surveillance video of the suspects:</p>
