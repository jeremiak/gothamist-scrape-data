										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2016_04_slashginqueens.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160818120626im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_04_slashginqueens.jpg" width="400" height="273"> <br> <i style=" width:400px; ;display:block"> (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160818120626/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-Livery-Cabbie-Slashed-in-Attempted-Robbery-in-Queens-NYPD--375936411.html">NBC New York</a>)</i></div> A cab driver was slashed in the neck and back by a passenger in Queens earlier this morning, according to police who say the passenger was trying to rob the driver.<p></p>

<p>According to the NYPD, police officers responded to the attack around 12:36 a.m. A 43-year-old livery cab driver had dropped off his passenger on Van Cleef Street in Corona and asked the passenger for the fare. </p>

<p>The passenger then allegedly demanded the driver&apos;s money, but the driver refused and was slashed multiple times during a struggle, according to police.</p>

<p>The suspect fled and the driver was taken to Elmhurst Hospital. </p>

<p>Police are still investigating. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160818120626/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2016/04/16/cab-driver-slashed-queens/">WCBS 2 reports</a>  &quot;the attacker left a cell phone and wallet behind.&quot;</p>
