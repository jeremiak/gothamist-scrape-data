<p>Investigators are searching for a suspect wanted for robbing a man at the Times Square subway station just after midnight today. The NYPD released this video:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604142303if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/lob6nlx9VPQ" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>According to the police, at 12:02 a.m. today, a 35-year-old man was in the mezzanine level of the Times Square subway station at West 42nd Street and 7th Avenue when a man &quot;approached him, briefly spoke to him, and then punched him in the face, knocking him to the ground.&quot; Then the suspect took the victim&apos;s cellphone and ran from the scene toward the uptown 1 train platform.</p>

<p>The NYPD describes the suspect as &quot;a black male, about 30 years old, 6&apos;3&quot;, with dark skin and afro-style hair. He was wearing a gray hooded-sweatshirt, and black or dark gray pants.&quot;<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information regarding this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604142303/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.  All calls are strictly confidential.</p>
