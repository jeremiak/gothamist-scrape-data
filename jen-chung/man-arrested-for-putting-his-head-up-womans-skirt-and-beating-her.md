										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013729/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_09_84fts1.jpg"> <img alt="2014_09_84fts1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013729im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2014/09/2014_09_84fts1-thumb-300x649-859118.jpg" width="150" height="324" class="image-right"> </a> </span>Less than a day after the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013729/http://gothamist.com/2014/09/12/nypd_this_pervert_put_his_head_unde.php">police released his photograph</a>, the man accused of putting his head up a woman&apos;s skirt&#x2014;then beating her&#x2014;at a Brooklyn subway station has been arrested.</p>

<p>The authorities had said that on August 8th, at 10 p.m. a 32-year-old woman was walking up the stairs at the Dekalb Avenue D and M subway station &quot;when the suspect put his head under her skirt.&quot; </p>

<p>The victim tried to push him away, but police say that he &quot;threw her down the stairs and began punching and kicking her&quot; before fleeing. The victim was not hospitalized, but she did suffer bruises. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013729/http://www.nydailynews.com/blogs/theshack/skirt-peeker-upper-penned-straphanger-61-charged-sexually-abusing-assaulting-train-commuter-blog-entry-1.1938075">The Daily News reports</a>, &quot;Two days later, she saw [the suspect] on another train, snapped his picture and gave it to police, who released the image Thursday.&quot;</p>

<p>Police charged James Barnes, 61, with assault and sexual abuse.</p>
