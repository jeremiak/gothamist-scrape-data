<p>The police are looking for the public&apos;s help in identifying a man suspected of attempted rape in Queens&apos; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130818062842/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/precincts/precinct_102.shtml">102nd Precinct,</a> which covers Kew Gardens, Richmond Hill East, Richmond Hill, Woodhaven, and the Northern part of Ozone Park.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 3:15 a.m. on Friday, July 5. A 21-year-old woman walking near 115th Street and 85 Avenue was approached by a man who &quot;grabbed her from behind, threw her onto the ground and attempted to rape her.&quot; A car passed by, scaring the suspect away. Some grainy surveillance video was released of the suspect:</p>

<p>The suspect is described as a man, 5&apos;9&quot;, 180 pounds with a medium complexion. Anyone with information in regard to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8447). The public can also submit their tips at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130818062842/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
