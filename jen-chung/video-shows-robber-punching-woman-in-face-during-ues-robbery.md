<p>A 54-year-old woman was viciously beaten in her apartment building&apos;s lobby by a mugger who also stole her purse.</p>

<p>The incident occurred around 11 p.m. on Friday, December 2nd, at the victim&apos;s apartment building on East 67th Street. The NYPD says the suspect followed her into the entrance area, &quot;demanded her bag and punched her in the face and body. The victim complied and the individual fled the location on foot in an unknown direction.&quot;</p>

<p>Apparently the victim and suspect were engaged in a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161207161632/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/man-beat-robbed-woman-upper-east-side-apartment-building-article-1.2898350">&quot;long struggle.&quot;</a> Video shows the suspect backing the victim against a wall:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161207161632if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/JwfTFwMr0x0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The victim was taken to New York Presbyterian Hospital for a laceration near her left eye and facial bruising. The suspect, who is described as being around 40-45 years old and 5&apos;4&quot;, was last seen wearing a black hat, black coat and black pants.<br>
 <br>

All calls are strictly confidential.</p>
