<p>Police released images of a suspect accused of forcibly touching a woman in Prospect Park yesterday afternoon.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at around 2:40 p.m. on May 25, a 24-year-old woman was &quot;was approached by a an unknown male&quot; who then &quot;grabbed the victims&apos; hand and lead her into Prospect Park, where he [grabbed] the victim buttocks and breasts over her clothing. The individual then pushed the victim against a tree and exposed his penis.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim fled and reported no injuries.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as being around 20 years old, 5&apos;8&quot; and 160 pounds, with black eyes and short black hair; he was last seen wearing gray jeans, white sneakers and a blue top.<br>
