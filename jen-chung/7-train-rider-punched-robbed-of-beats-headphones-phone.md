										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_11_34throbbery.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160310000818im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_11_34throbbery.jpg" width="686" height="431" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are looking for a pair of suspects accused of attacking and then robbing a subway rider earlier this week.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, a 24-year-old man was on a northbound 7 train in Manhattan around 5:30 a.m. &quot;when the suspects grabbed him by the arms and began to punch him on the head.  The suspects then forcibly removed his Beats headphones and his iPhone before fleeing the train as it entered the 34 Street and 11 Avenue station.&quot;<br>
 <br>
