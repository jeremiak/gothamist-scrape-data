<p>Four women who were enjoying the sights of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161206113345/http://gothamist.com/2016/12/01/rockefeller_christmas_tree.php">Rockefeller Center Christmas tree</a> were allegedly sexually abused by a former NYPD employee on Thursday night. </p>

<p>Atta Saad was at Rockefeller Plaza when, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161206113345/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/ex-nypd-employee-rubs-crotch-4-women-promptly-arrested-article-1.2896294?utm_content=buffer7e3d7&amp;utm_medium=social&amp;utm_source=facebook.com&amp;utm_campaign=buffer">the News reports</a>, police officers say they saw him &quot;approach&quot; victims &quot;from behind at about 7:15 p.m.&quot; Saad allegedly rubbed his privates against 4 different women in the crowd over a 20 minute period. According to court records, one woman Saad rubbed up against scared him off by turning and glaring at him.</p>

<p>A police officer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161206113345/http://abc7ny.com/news/ex-nypd-traffic-cop-charged-with-sex-abuse-at-rockefeller-tree/1636734/">wrote in the criminal complaint</a>: </p><blockquote>I followed the defendant and observed the defendant push his groin up against a woman&apos;s buttocks and repeatedly rub against her. I observed that there was enough room behind the defendant such that he did not need to be pressed up against the woman. I observed no conversation between the woman and the defendant.</blockquote>The News says that Saad was an  NYPD traffic enforcement agent who resigned in 2015. He was charged with multiple counts of forcible touching and sexual abuse; and was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161206113345/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Man-Arrested-Sex-Abuse-Rockefeller-Center-Christmas-Tree-404376166.html">released on $750 bail</a>. <p></p>
