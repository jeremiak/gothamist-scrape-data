<p>A woman was slashed this morning on a Manhattan sidewalk, shortly before 6:30 a.m.</p>

<p>An NYPD spokesman says the victim was slashed at 24th and 7th Avenue by an unknown assailant. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161222161217/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2016/01/06/chelsea-slashing/">Local TV news stations</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161222161217/http://abc7ny.com/news/woman-slashed-in-chelsea/1148071/">show police activity</a> on West 23rd Street, between 7th and 8th Avenues, in front of a stretch of buildings that includes the Malibu Diner. </p>

<p>The victim is in stable condition at Bellevue Hospital. The investigation is ongoing. We&apos;ll update as we find out more.</p>
