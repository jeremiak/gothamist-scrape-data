<p>The police are hoping the public can help them identify the suspect who robbed at Bronx Carvel store on Thursday.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, a man walked into the Carvel at 1250 Castle Hill Avenue at 10:25 p.m. He ordered an ice cream cone, paid for it and then, allegedly, took out a gun and demanded the clerk give him money. Police say the clerk complied and the &quot;suspect fled with an undetermined amount of money.&quot; The clerk was not injured.</p>

<p>The police describe the suspect as a male around 25 years old, 5&apos;9&quot; tall and weighing 150 pounds, adding that he may have a tattoo on his right arm.  Anyone with information in regards to this robbery is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS.<br>
The public can also submit their tips to the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015355/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or text their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577. All calls are confidential. </p>
