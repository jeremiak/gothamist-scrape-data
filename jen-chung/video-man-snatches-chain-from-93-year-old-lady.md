<p>Police are looking for two people who have been targeting numerous women in the Bronx for their necklaces. In one instance, one of the suspects snatched a chain from the neck of a 93-year-old woman standing in her driveway.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incidents have occurred this month in the 49th Precinct, which includes neighborhoods like Allerton, Morris Park, Van Nest, Pelham Parkway, Eastchester Gardens and Pelham Gardens. Here are the incidents:</p><blockquote>Incident# 1  On April 10, 2013 at approximately 4:35 p.m., the suspect approached a 55-year-old woman, who was walking her dog, and attempted to remove a chain from her neck.  The suspect was unsuccessful and fled without any property.<br>
 <br>
Incident# 2  On April 16, 2013 at approximately 1:50 p.m., two suspects approached a 71-year-old woman and snatched a gold chain from her neck.  The suspects fled the location with the gold chain.<br>
 <br>
Incident#3  On April 16, 2013 at approximately 2:30 p.m., while standing in her driveway, the victim, a 93-year-old woman, was approached by the suspect who snatched two gold chains from the her  neck.  The suspects fled the location with the gold chain.<br>
 <br>
Incident# 4  On April 17, 2013 at approximately 2:40 p.m., the suspect approached a 62-year-old woman, who was walking her dog, engaged her in a conversation while suspect# 2 removed a chain from her neck.  The suspects fled the location with the victim&apos;s chain.<br>
 <br>
Incident# 5  On April 29, 2013 at 6:55 p.m., the victim, a 61-year-old woman, was approached by the suspect who asked for direction and snatched the victim&apos;s gold chain from her neck.  The suspect fled the location, possibly in an older model gray sedan.</blockquote>Here&apos;s video of the incident with the 93-year-old victim:<p></p>
