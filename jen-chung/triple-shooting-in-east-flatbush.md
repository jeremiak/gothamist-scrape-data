title: Triple Shooting in East Flatbush
---

<p>Three people <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811140244/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=6226304">were shot last night</a> around <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811140244/http://maps.google.com/maps?q=east+94th+Street+and+Church+brooklyn+ny&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;oe=utf-8&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;ll=40.65489,-73.917046&amp;spn=0.009767,0.020921&amp;z=16&amp;iwloc=addr">East 94th Street and Church</a> in Brooklyn.  A car is also full of bullets.  WABC 7 reports that while the &quot;conditions of the victims are unknown,&quot; one victim may have been shot in the head, another was shot in the arm, and a third victim was able to drive to the hospital.  Police are investigating.  </p>
