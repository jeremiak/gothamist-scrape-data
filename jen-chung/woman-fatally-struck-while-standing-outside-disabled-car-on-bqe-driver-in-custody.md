title: Woman Fatally Struck While Standing Outside Disabled Car On BQE, Driver In Custody

										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_08_bqedeath.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091503im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_08_bqedeath.jpg" width="640" height="444"> <br> <i> (WABC 7)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>A 49-year-old woman was killed while standing outside her disabled car on the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway in Greenpoint this morning. And the driver who struck her is now in custody.</p>

<p>The crash occurred around 2:50 a.m., near Meeker and Morgan Avenues. The woman&apos;s car had stalled in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091503/http://pix11.com/2016/08/29/woman-standing-outside-car-fatally-struck-on-bqe-in-brooklyn/">left westbound lane</a>, and a passing car struck her. </p>

<p>She was thrown from the BQE onto the street below, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091503/http://abc7ny.com/traffic/woman-standing-outside-car-fatally-struck-by-another-car-on-bqe/1488958/">according to WABC 7</a>.</p>

<p>Police say the woman was taken to Elmhurst Hospital, where she was pronounced dead. The 43-year-old male driver of the passing vehicle was taken to Woodhull Hospital in stable condition. </p>

<p>He is in custody, with charges pending (possibly DWI related). </p>
