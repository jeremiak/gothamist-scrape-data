<p>A Mini Cooper <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013748/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Bronx-River-Parkway-Mini-Cooper-Crash-NYPD-DOA-Fire-Traffic-274866881.html">traveling south on the Bronx River Parkway</a>, south of the Cross Bronx Expressway, crashed, killing two people and critically injuring another. Speed may have been a factor.</p>

<p>The crash occurred around 4:15 a.m. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013748/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/09/12/2-killed-1-seriously-injured-in-bronx-river-parkway-crash/">WCBS 2 reports</a>, &quot;A car carrying three people hit a barrier on the shoulder and went airborne, police said. The car then slammed into a tree, split in two and caught fire.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013748/http://7online.com/traffic/2-dead-1-critical-after-fiery-crash-on-bronx-river-parkway/305628/">According to WABC 7</a>, &quot;The rear passenger was pronounced dead at the scene. The front two passengers were ejected and taken to Jacobi Hospital with severe injuries. One later died.&quot;</p>

<p>Police are investigating the incident. The crash caused miles of delays for the morning commute.</p>
