<p>Police say that a woman was sexually assaulted early yesterday in Bed-Stuy. Surveillance footage and photos of the suspect were released in hopes the public can help identify him.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the male suspected followed a 22-year-old victim who was walking on the sidewalk around 1 a.m. on Saturday. He allegedly pulled her between two cars, threatened her with a gun, and sexually abused her. He then fled the scene. The victim was treated at an area hospital.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as being in his early 30s, 5&apos;10&quot; tall and 160 pounds; he was wearing an Adidas t-shirt. Anyone with information in regards to this sexual abuse is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425175316/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">the Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
