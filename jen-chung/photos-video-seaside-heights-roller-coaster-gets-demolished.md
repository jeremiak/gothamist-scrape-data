title: Photos, Video: Seaside Heights Roller Coaster Gets Demolished
---


	
	
	
	<p>Yesterday, crews removed the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103612/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/13/tomorrow_demolition_set_for_nj_roll.php">Jetstar Roller Coaster</a> from the Atlantic Ocean. The roller coaster had been a Seaside Heights, NJ attraction, but it slid into the water during Hurricane Sandy. </p>

<p>A new roller coaster be constructed&#x2014;and it&apos;s going to be called Super Storm. Seaside Heights Mayor Bill Akers <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717103612/http://blogs.wsj.com/metropolis/2013/05/14/new-thrill-ride-to-be-called-superstorm/?mod=WSJBlog">said</a>, &quot;We hope that it&#x2019;s perceived in a positive way, that we&#x2019;re back, we know the force of Mother Nature, and that we respect that, and this is our way of celebrating,&quot; but one boardwalker business owner told the Wall Street Journal, &quot;I don&#x2019;t want a reminder of superstorm Sandy, that&#x2019;s how I feel about it.  would have thought of something different.&quot;</p>
