										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_02_foodtruckslashing12.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630070813im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_02_foodtruckslashing12.jpg" width="640" height="444" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are looking for the suspect in a violent Bronx encounter last week that resulted in a man being slashed in the head.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at around 2 a.m. on February 14th, a 24-year-old man was waiting on line for a food truck near Jerome Avenue and East 174th Street in the Bronx when &quot;an unidentified male cut the line and an argument began.The male displayed an unidentified object and slashed the male victim in the head and fled.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was taken to Bronx Lebanon Hospital for treatment. <br>
 <br>
Police released video of the suspect, who is described as being 25-30 years old and 5&apos;10&quot;- 6&apos;0&quot; tall with a slim build.</p>
