<p>Someone stocked up on marbles and threw them at dozens of businesses in the Bronx last night. Luckily, no one was injured in the strange marble-spraying spree.</p>

<p>The marblings occurred between 7:15 p.m. and 8:40 p.m. last night, and police are now looking for a white, four-door sedan with dark tinted windows. According to the NYPD, an unknown number of suspects &quot;used marbles as projectiles to break glass windows at commercial establishments and various other locations.&quot; Here&apos;s a list of the businesses targeted:</p>
