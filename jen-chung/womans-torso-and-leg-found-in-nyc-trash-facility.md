<p>All work has stopped at a Bronx waste transfer station where a female torso and leg were found this morning. </p>

<p>Police say that around 4:45 a.m. officers responded to a 911 call about human remains found at the Metropolitan Transfer Station. (The facility, at 287 Halleck Street, handles trash sorting.) The cops &quot;discovered a torso and leg of an unidentified adult female,&quot; according to the NYPD.</p>

<p>A worker at the trash station <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015415/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Body-Found-Trash-Station-Bronx-Hunts-Point-Woman-Torso-Leg-NYPD-Police-Investigation-410915625.html">told NBC New York</a> &quot;all work was stopped... to assist in the police investigation.&quot;</p>

<p>There are no arrests yet. The Medical Examiner will determine cause of death.</p>
