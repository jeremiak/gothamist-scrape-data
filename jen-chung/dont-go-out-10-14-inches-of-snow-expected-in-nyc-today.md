					<p>Sure, NYC school children <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170404021559/http://gothamist.com/2017/02/08/snow_day_nyc_schools_closed_thursda.php">get a snow day</a> for today&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170404021559/http://gothamist.com/2017/02/08/summers_almost_over.php">big winter storm</a>, but many people still had to head to their jobs, fighting the intense wind and snow along the way. The National Weather Service is predicting that 8-12&quot; inches of snow fall, and the NYC Emergency Management suggests it may go up to 14&quot;! </p>
