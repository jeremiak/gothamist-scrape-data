title: Man Stabbed In Front Of Midtown 99 Cent Pizza Joint
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_07_midassault.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150426151151im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_07_midassault.jpg" width="440" height="221" class="image-right"> </span>The police are looking for a man who allegedly stabbed someone outside a pizzeria in Midtown East on Thursday.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 12:15 a.m. on Thursday, July 10, a 30-year-old man was standing outside the 99 Cents Fresh Pizza at 151 East 43rd Street when he was suddenly attacked. The suspect, police say, stabbed him in the neck with an unknown objected.</p>

<p>The victim was taken to Bellevue for treatment while the suspect fled. The NYPD released images of the suspect.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150426151151/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
