	<p><img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608134351im_/http://gothamist.com/upload/2013/06/2013_05_fedex1.jpg" width="640" height="480" alt="2013_05_fedex1.jpg"></p>

<p>On Monday night, a 10-year-old boy <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608134351/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/04/10-yr-old_brooklyn_boy_fires_gun_to.php">managed to disarm a home invasion robber</a> who was trying to get into his Brooklyn bedroom&#x2014;the boy slammed his door into the robber&apos;s arm, prompting the man to drop the weapon. Then the boy took the robber&apos;s gun and fired it, scaring off the perp and his crony. Now the police say that the boy actually traded gunfire! </p>

<p><img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608134351im_/http://gothamist.com/upload/2013/06/2013_05_fedex2a.jpg" width="640" height="474" alt="2013_05_fedex2a.jpg"></p>

<p>According to the police, at about 5:15 p.m., two men entered the East 80th Street home in the Canarsie section. One was wearing a FedEx uniform and holding a box. The second man followed, and both demanded money from nine people in the home. The police say the suspect wearing the FedEx uniform tried to enter the boy&apos;s bedroom, but dropped his gun after the boy slammed the door. Then the boy fired the gun, which then resulting in the second man firing his gun. </p>

<p><img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608134351im_/http://gothamist.com/upload/2013/06/2013_05_fedex2.jpg" width="640" height="466" alt="2013_05_fedex2.jpg"></p>

<p>No one was injured, but the police are looking for the suspects, who are described as men in their 20s. Here&apos;s video of the suspect entering the home and going up the stairs</p>
