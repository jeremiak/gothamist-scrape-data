	<p>For the very last time, New York City&apos;s political reporters mocked Mayor Bloomberg in their annual Inner Circle Show. The event raises money for charity and is capped off with a performance by the mayor himself, who has previously &quot;pretended&quot; to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104852/http://gothamist.com/2012/04/01/photos_video_mayor_bloomberg_sings.php#photo-1">read &quot;Fifty Shades of Grey&quot;</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104852/http://gothamist.com/2008/03/16/mayor_bloomberg_40.php">worn a headband</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104852/http://gothamist.com/2009/03/29/mayor_mike_lets_out_his_colorful_se.php#photo-1">danced with crustaceans</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104852/http://gothamist.com/2010/03/28/trippy_man_hippie_bloomberg_meets_s.php#photo-1">&quot;smoked pot&quot;</a> and hung out with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104852/http://gothamist.com/2010/03/28/trippy_man_hippie_bloomberg_meets_s.php#photo-5">Snooki and The Situation</a>. This year, he did a mash-up of various Broadway shows, from <em>Annie</em> to <em>The Phantom of the Opera</em>, and ending with <em>Rock of Ages</em>. Here&apos;s a video&#x2014;though you might need a Bloody Mary before watching it:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104852if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/aKgKYjanwS8?list=UUIxapChazpLOEZQa2X353Aw" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The Daily News <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104852/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/bloomberg-prods-christie-pope-annie-cast-article-1.1297722">has some details of the show</a>: &quot;At another point, the Franklin D. Roosevelt character reassures Annie, &apos;The only thing we have to fear is fear itself. Or if (mayoral hopeful) John Liu wins, that would be pretty scary.&apos;&quot; And when Annie&apos;s dog Sandy was mentioned, Bloomberg said, &quot;For me, when I hear that name I expect Chris Christie to hug me.&quot;</p>
