										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_01_dieyuppies.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160507203239im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_01_dieyuppies.jpg" width="640" height="429" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are searching for a man who entered a Brooklyn construction site to write &quot;Die Yuppies&quot; and draw a swastika in wet cement.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on January 29th at 1:45 a.m., when the man entered the construction site on Myrtle Avenue and wrote &quot;Die Yuppies&quot; along with a swastika into wet cement. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as being a male about 5&apos;11&quot; and 150 pounds with long hair and last seen wearing a dark waist length jacket. Surveillance video was released.<br>

<p>The suspect is wanted for criminal mischief.<br>
