<p>You may be fist-pumping in excitement over the upcoming return of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160812235454/http://gothamist.com/tags/jerseyshore"><em>Jersey Shore</em></a>, but will you be saying da or nyet to <em>Russian Dolls</em>? <em>Russian Dolls</em> is Lifetime&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160812235454/http://gothamist.com/2010/10/07/brighton_beach_reality_show_picked.php">new reality show</a> about Russian women (and men) living in Brooklyn.  Sheepshead Bites <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160812235454/http://www.sheepsheadbites.com/2011/07/first-look-russian-dolls-brighton-beach-reality-show-video-preview/?utm_source=twitterfeed&amp;utm_medium=twitter&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+SheepsheadBites+%28Sheepshead+Bites%29">got a hold of the trailer</a>, and it does seem to live up to the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160812235454/http://www.sheepsheadbites.com/2011/07/first-look-russian-dolls-brighton-beach-reality-show-video-preview/?utm_source=twitterfeed&amp;utm_medium=twitter&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+SheepsheadBites+%28Sheepshead+Bites%29">producer&apos;s promise</a>, &quot;There will be plenty of vodka, techno music and guys wearing Adidas pants, leather jackets and gold chains, and driving souped-up cars. There will also be a lot of hot, decked-out Russian girls.&quot;  Added bonus: Cocktail weiners in puff pastry!</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="390" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160812235454if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/s79NpamUO2M" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160812235454/http://www.mylifetime.com/shows/russian-dolls">Lifetime calls</a> the show a &quot;rare and entertaining look at Brighton Beach&#x2019;s colorful multi-generational families whose dramas and dreams contend with their Russian heritage while living in this famous, alluring, vibrant and highly protective community.&quot; Which sort of fits the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160812235454/http://gothamist.com/2010/02/23/is_brighton_beach_the_next_jersey_s.php">initial casting call for the show</a>: &quot;Are you the Russian Snooki or The Situation? Are you a super outgoing and fun-loving Russian-American that sometimes sneaks kalbaska, pel&apos;meni and vodka from the fridge? Can people hear the Euro/Techno/Russian music blasting from your car before they see you pull up? ... The cameras will roll as you do what you do best&#x2014;eat, drink and PARTY.&quot;</p>
