<p>If you happen onto Google&apos;s homepage today, you&apos;ll see a Takashi Murakami doodle celebrating the Summer Solstice!  As the Japanese pop artist <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160712234927/http://twitter.com/#!/takashipom/status/83128849038184448">Tweeted</a>, &quot;Amazing ! I &amp; kaikaikiki team are so happy this Happening ! Thanks a Lot team Google !&quot;  </p>

<p>When you click on the Doodle, you&apos;ll get <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160712234927/http://www.google.com/search?q=Summer+Solstice&amp;ct=murakami_summer-hp&amp;oi=ddle">details about the summer solstice</a>. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160712234927/http://twitter.com/#!/google/status/83040770055086080/photo/1">According to Google&apos;s Twitter</a>, Murakami has also created a Google Doodle for the Winter Solstice (December 22). </p>
