title: Suspicious Package Suspends Metro-North's Hudson Line
---

	
										<p>Move over, suffering LIRR riders: The Metro-North&apos;s Hudson Line is suspended now! The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150407153426/http://www.lohud.com/article/20100827/NEWS02/100827005?utm_source=twitterfeed&amp;utm_medium=twitter">Journal News reports</a>, &quot;A suspicious package left on the train tracks in Irvington this morning has suspended all Hudson Line service temporarily and prompted evacuation of some businesses in downtown Irvington. Metro-North spokesman Dan Brucker said Irvington police asked for a halt in service while they checked out the item.&quot;</p>
