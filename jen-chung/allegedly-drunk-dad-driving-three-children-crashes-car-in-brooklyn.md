title: Allegedly Drunk Dad, Driving Three Children, Crashes Car In Brooklyn
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_03_dd3.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153610im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_03_dd3.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> Photograph by RLJR NEWS</i></div> <p></p>

<p>A man driving with three children in the car crashed into a pole on Eastern Parkway and Dean Street this morning, critically injuring one of the kids. Police say that Anthony Pollydore was drunk when the crash occurred.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153610/http://nypost.com/2014/03/23/dad-charged-with-dwi-in-crash-that-critically-injured-kid/">According to the Post</a>, &quot;Pollydore &#x2014; who has previously been busted for vehicular assault &#x2014; stopped abruptly at a red light near Dean Street and skidded into the pole, cops said.&quot; In his car were his five-year-old son, and 11-year-old and 12-year-old stepsons. The 11-year-old suffered &quot;potentially life-threatening injuries&quot; while the other two children were not injured.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_03_dd1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153610im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_03_dd1.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> Photograph by RLJR NEWS</i></div> <p></p>

<p>A neighbor <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153610/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/brooklyn/girl-11-badly-hurt-brooklyn-car-crash-article-1.1731160">told the Daily News</a>, &quot;I woke up to a loud boom. I didn&#x2019;t hear any squealing at all. By the time I looked out the window, there were already ambulances outside.&quot;</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_03_dd2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153610im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_03_dd2.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> Photograph by RLJR NEWS</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Pollydore, 65, reportedly failed a Breathalyzer test.  He has been charged with aggravated vehicular assault, driving while intoxicated, and vehicular assault. His past DWIs were in 2004 and 2009.</p>
