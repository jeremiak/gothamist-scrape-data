title: Cops: Man Groped 11-Year-Old At East Village Dunkin Donuts
---

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120316175838/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_02_suspev1.jpg"> <img alt="2012_02_suspev1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20120316175838im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2012/03/2012_02_suspev1-thumb-300x361-700520.jpg" width="250" height="300" class="image-right"> </a> </span>The NYPD released a police sketch and surveillance video of a man suspected of forcibly touching a young girl in the East Village.</p>

<p>According to the police, on Wednesday, March 7 at around 11:30 a.m., an 11-year-old girl was at the Dunkin Donuts at 361 East 1st Street when a man &quot;approached her, touched her inappropriately, and then fled the scene.&quot;  </p>

<p>The suspect is described as a white man who is heavy set with short grey hair.  The police encourage anyone with information about the incident to to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or submit tips via the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120316175838/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a>.</p>
