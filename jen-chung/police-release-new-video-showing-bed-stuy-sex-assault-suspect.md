										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_05_bedstuysusp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425175315im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_05_bedstuysusp.jpg" width="640" height="434" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>A few days ago, the police released <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425175315/http://gothamist.com/2014/05/18/video_cops_seek_suspect_in_bed-stuy.php">surveillance images and footage of a man</a> who allegedly attacked a woman and sexually assaulted her in Bedford-Stuyvesant on Saturday. Now, the NYPD has new, clearer video.</p>

<p>According to the police, a 22-year-old woman was walking on the street around 1 a.m. when the suspect grabbed her, pulled her between two cars, threatened her with a gun, and sexually abused her. He then fled the scene. The victim was treated at an area hospital.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150425175315if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/nMqH-YI74Gc" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspect is described as being in his early 30s, 5&apos;10&quot; tall and 160 pounds; he was wearing an Adidas t-shirt. Anyone with information in regards to this sexual abuse is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425175315/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
