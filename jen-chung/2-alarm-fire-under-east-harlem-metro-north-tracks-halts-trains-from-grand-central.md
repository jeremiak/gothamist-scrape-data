<p>The MTA has halted Metro-North&apos;s Harlem and Hudson lines to and from Grand Central Terminal because of a two-alarm fire under the tracks in East Harlem. The FDNY is currently on the scene at East 118th Street and Park Avenue.</p>

<p>According to the Fire Department, they received the call about the fire at 6:42 p.m. and the fire appears to be from construction debris and construction shanties.
