<p><strong>UPDATE 5:20 p.m.:</strong> The suspect has been located along with his 3 year-old daughter in New Jersey, according to the NYPD. The child is unharmed and the suspect has been taken into custody. </p>

<p>The police are looking for a 51-year-old man accused of attacking his ex in the Bronx and abducting their young daughter. An Amber Alert was issued in an attempt to find Diomedes Valenzuela, who is driving a grey 2004 Dodge Caravan with NJ license plate number K24-ECS.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, Valenzuela stabbed his 31-year-old ex multiple times at 1:44 p.m. this afternoon at 2974 Perry Avenue during a domestic dispute. He then fled with their three-year-old daughter Rebecca Valenzuela.The victim was taken to St. Barnabas Hospital in critical condition.</p>

<p>Valenzuela is described as bald with brown eyes, about 6&apos;1&quot; and 270 pounds. The daughter, Rebecca is described as about 2&apos;6&quot; and 30 pounds with black hair and brown eyes. <br>
 <br>
