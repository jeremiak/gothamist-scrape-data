<p>Grand Central Terminal will be more of a circus than usual today&#x2014;Ringling Bros. and Barnum &amp; Bailey Circus is giving visitors and commuters <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120301035937/http://www.dnainfo.com/20120221/murray-hill-gramercy/grand-central-become-big-top-with-ringling-bros-circus">a sneak peak</a> of its new show, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120301035937/http://www.ringling.com/SectionLandingPage.aspx?id=45450">Dragons</a>. Plus, Ringling Bros. is also <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120301035937/http://www.ringling.com/clownauditions/">conducting clown auditions</a>!</p>

<p>Performances&#x2014;which include ones from the Shaolin Warriors&#x2014;and auditions will be held today between 11 a.m. and 3 p.m. at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120301035937/http://www.grandcentralterminal.com/info/vanderbilthall.cfm">Vanderbilt Hall</a>.  And Dragons won&apos;t be at Madison Square Garden, but it will be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120301035937/http://www.ringling.com/SectionLandingPage.aspx?id=45450">playing in the tri-state area</a>.</p>
