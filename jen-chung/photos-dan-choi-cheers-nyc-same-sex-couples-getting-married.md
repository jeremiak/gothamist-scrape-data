</div></div>					<p>The first day of NY State allowing gay marriages meant hundreds of happy couples tied the knot yesterday.  At the City Clerk&apos;s office in Manhattan, it was a joyous scene as couples waited for their numbers&#x2014;yes, each couple received a number&#x2014;to be called. Dan Choi, the former National Guard officer who was discharged after coming out, was on hand and ended up serving as a witness for the wedding of Lisa Winters and Eileen Scroggins, a couple who has been together for 13 years.  Choi even got Judge Doris Ling-Cohan&#x2014;the judge <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150408143835/http://gothamist.com/2005/02/04/gay_marriage_may_be_coming_to_ny.php">who ruled in 2005</a> that gay and lesbian couples in NY must have equal access to marriage&#x2014;to perform the ceremony!</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150408143835/http://colormekatie.blogspot.com/">Katie Sokoler</a> was on the scene for us and she took some stunning photographs.  </p>
