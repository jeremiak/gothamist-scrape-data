<p>The police are asking for the public&apos;s help is locating a man suspected of five different incidents where he has forcibly touched women in the Bronx, all in the 46th Precinct.</p>

<p>The first incident was on March 5th, around 3:10 p.m. A 36-year-old woman was walking in the vicinity of Popham Avenue when the suspect approached her from behind, groped her and then fled. </p>

<p>On March 9th, at 12:30 a.m., a woman in her University Heights-area building lobby was approached by the suspect and groped. Here&apos;s video from that incident:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150502175649if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/vKrHNEljPcw" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Later that day, at 3:15 p.m., a 24-year-old woman walking into her building, again in the University Heights section, was approached from behind and groped. Then the suspect fled.</p>

<p>On March 15th, at 5:40 p.m., a 20-year-old woman was walking into her Bedford Park building when she was approached from behind and forcibly touched. The suspect fled as well.</p>

<p>Then, this past Tuesday, on March 18th, at 8 p.m., a 27-year-old entering her University Heights building was approached from behind and groped. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as a dark-skinned male, 22 years old, 5&apos;10&quot; and weighing 170 pounds. Anyone with information in regards to any of these incidents is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
