title: Thief Allegedly Stole Woman's Purse At Bar, Then Burglarized Her Apartment
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160712155946/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_03_burg16.jpg"> <img alt="2016_03_burg16.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160712155946im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2016/03/2016_03_burg16-thumb-200x267-937200.jpg" width="200" height="267" class="image-right"> </a> </span>A Manhattan woman&apos;s stolen purse apparently led the thief right to her apartment, and now the police are looking for the burglar.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on February 27th in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160712155946/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/precincts/precinct_017.shtml">17th Precinct</a>, which covers &quot;Sutton Area, Beekman Place, Kipps Bay, Turtle Bay, Murray Hill, Manhattan East and the Rose Hill Community.&quot; At around 1:30 a.m., police say that a 25-year-old woman&apos;s purse was stolen at a bar and then the suspect &quot;used her keys to gain access to her apartment and removed the property of a 35-year-old male while he was asleep inside the location.&quot;<br>
 <br>
Police released an image of the suspect, who is described as being 40-50 years old, about 5&apos;7&quot; with a light complexion and salt and pepper hair. <br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or for Spanish 1-888-57-PISTA (74782)<br>
The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160712155946/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then entering TIP577.</p>
