<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128061557/http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20130322/tribeca/cabbie-busted-for-swiping-two-ipads-after-fare-fight-cops-say">DNAinfo reports</a>, &quot;A 24-year-old woman had her $4,800 black Chanel purse stolen after she left it on a couch at the swanky new SoHo lesbian bar The Dalloway, at 525 Broome St., on March 7.&quot; Police sources say the purse was swiped while she got up to use the bathroom. Welcome to New York City, international jet-setters!</p>
