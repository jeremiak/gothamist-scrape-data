---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_12_52pct.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160503164251im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_12_52pct.jpg" width="350" height="186" class="image-right"> </span>The police are hoping the public can help them identify a person accused of groping a subway rider&apos;s breasts on Sunday.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 5 a.m. on November 29th, a 22-year-old woman fell asleep on a northbound D train. When she woke up at the Bedford Park Boulevard Station, she &quot;discovered an unknown male suspect touching her breasts.&quot;</p>

<p>Polices say she managed to shove the assailant off and flee the train, escaping on a southbound D train. </p>

<p>Investigators released surveillance footage and an artist&apos;s sketch of the suspect. <br>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160503164251/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577. All calls are kept strictly confidential.</p>

<p>If you see or experience gross subway behavior, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160503164251/http://gothamist.com/2015/07/10/subway_masturbation_advice.php">you can do something about it</a> (but stay safe!).
