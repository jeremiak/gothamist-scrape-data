</div></div>					<p>Finally, someone has created a Tumblr that documents Very Important Moments in Law &amp; Order.  No, not the screen cards that show the various fake addresses (600 West 78th Street would be in the Hudson River!)&#x2014;the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150310071138/http://law-order-food.tumblr.com/">Law &amp; Order &amp; Food Tumblr</a> has screengrabs of Law &amp; Order characters eating food. Time to feast your eyes on Detective Lennie Briscoe eating some street meat!</p>

<p>So far, it looks like there are images of Briscoe, Mike Logan, Anita Van Buren, Ben Stone, Jack McCoy, Adam Schiff, and Claire Kincaid&#x2014;no Max Greevey, Phil Cerreta, Donald Cragen or Paul Robinette yet.  We think the Tumblr needs a little Profaci, if possible (he seems like someone who would have eaten in the squad room).  Anyway, this make us nostalgic&#x2014;what would Lennie think of all these <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150310071138/http://gothamist.com/2012/05/16/five_food_trucks_wed_love_to_see_th.php">fancy food trucks</a>?</p>
