<p>Police are looking for a suspect who robbed a woman walking in the East Village yesterday morning.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, a 35-year-old woman was walking past 405 East 6th Street around 5 a.m. when someone approached her from behind. The male suspect displayed a knife and demanded her property, the police say, leading her to drop her purse containing $300 and an iPhone. The victim managed to flee. </p>

<p>Police released a video of the suspect right after the robbery&#x2014;it looks like he&apos;s holding a purse and drops a phone.</p>
