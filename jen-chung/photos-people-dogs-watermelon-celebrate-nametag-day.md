</div></div>					<p>Here&apos;s the answer to the question <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150609024238/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/08/what_if_everyone_in_new_york_city_w.php">&quot;What if everyone wore a nametag?&quot;</a>! Yesterday, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150609024238/http://nametagday.com/">Nametag Day</a> organizers handed out thousands of nametags to make the city a little less anonymous. </p>

<p>Photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150609024238/http://colormekatie.blogspot.com/">Katie Sokoler</a> was in Wiliamsburg and captured a few of the nametagged folks, two- and four-legged. She says, &quot;It was a lot of fun! People were really into it and it was nice to walk past a stranger and say &apos;Hi Helen!&apos;&quot; </p>
