<p>It&apos;s the day you&apos;ve been waiting for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011417/http://gothamist.com/2014/06/04/doughnut_news.php">since June 4th</a>: <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011417/http://doughnutplant.com/">Doughnut Plant</a> announced it will open its Prospect Heights location next week!</p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011417/http://gothamist.com/2013/11/06/the_best_doughnuts_in_nyc.php">beloved doughnut purveyor</a> will open up at 245 Flatbush Avenue at Bergen Street on Wednesday, December 3rd, to bring their incredible array of doughnuts (yeast, cake and doughseed) outside of Manhattan. </p>

<p>A few months ago, they said <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011417/http://gothamist.com/2014/09/25/doughnut_plant_lic.php">they were moving to a new production plant</a> in Long Island City, with owner Mark Israel promising, &quot;more flavors, more doughnuts, more deliciousness.&quot;</p>

<p>For the holidays, Doughnut Plant has marzipan, panettone, pomegranate, and gingerbread flavors. But don&apos;t forget about the classics, from the blackout to the carrot cake, from peanut butter &amp; blackberry jam to the decadent tres leches.</p>
