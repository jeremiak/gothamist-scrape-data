<p>The young gay couple <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130717095352/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/03/adorable_gay_couple_named_cutest_co.php">who was voted the &quot;Cutest Couple&quot;</a> for their upstate NY high school yearbook are talking about the attention they&apos;ve received so far. Bradley Taylor, 17, told the Journal News, &quot;It&#x2019;s been insane. We are very, very proud to show that Carmel High School is the way it is.&quot;</p>

<p>According to the Journal News, &quot;Taylor and [Dylan] Meehan have been together since June 14, after they were introduced by a friend and then went on a tour of Brown University together. The two came out to their family and friends a week later.&quot; And this fall, they&apos;ll be attending NYU&apos;s Gallatin School of Individualized Study (they have 4.0 GPAs and got scholarships!).</p>

<p>Taylor and Meehan said they were fortunate that everyone has been supportive. As for anyone saying something mean to them at school, Taylor pointed out, &quot;We&#x2019;re both six-foot something. What are they going to say?&quot; Yesterday, the couple attended their senior prom:</p>
