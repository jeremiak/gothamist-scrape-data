										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_08_shooting1har.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014339im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_08_shooting1har.jpg" width="640" height="432" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are looking for a single suspect in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014339/http://gothamist.com/2014/08/17/two_dead_in_harlem_drive-by_shootin.php">fatal shooting of two men outside a Harlem bodega</a> early Sunday morning. The NYPD is sharing surveillance video of the suspect, shown brandishing a gun, as well as his car.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at around 2:53 a.m. Sunday, officers responded to a 911 call about two men who were shot outside the Fedel Gourmet Deli on Broadway at 151st Street. When police got there, they found a 21-year-old man, unconscious, with a gunshot wound to his neck and a 29-year-old man, also unconscious, with a gunshot wound to his back.</p>

<p><img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014339im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_08_hshooting3.jpg"><br>
<span class="photo_caption">Photograph by David Torres</span></p>

<p>The 21-year-old, Kenny Jimenez, who lived on the same block, was pronounced dead at the scene while the 29-year-old, Francisco Mercedez of the Bronx, died at Harlem Hospital. </p>

<p>Police say that the suspect &quot;displayed and discharged a firearm&quot; in front of the deli, and then &quot;fled in a white Nissan Altima with dark tinted windows to parts unknown.&quot; The suspect is described as wearing a short sleeve gray hooded sweatshirt, black t-shirt, camouflage shorts, and white sneakers.</p>

<p>The victims were aspiring rappers, who just came back from an event in Connecticut. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014339/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/2-Dead-in-Harlem-Street-Shooting-Broadway-West-151st-Street-Kenny-Jiminez-Francisco-Mercedez-271579451.html">Jimenez&apos;s sister said</a>, &quot;He didn&apos;t deserve this. He was only 21.&quot;<br>
