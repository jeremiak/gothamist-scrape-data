title: Intense Video Shows Murder Suspect Shooting At Victim
---

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2011_04_shootvideo.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20110419215925im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2011_04_shootvideo.jpg" width="365" height="282" class="image-left"> </span>The police have released video of a suspect in a fatal Brooklyn shooting.  And the video (below) is pretty explicit: Surveillance footage shows a man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110419215925/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2011/04/18/2011-04-18_video_of_man_fatally_shooting_teen.html?r=ny_local&amp;utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+nydnrss%2Fny_local+%28NY+Local%29&amp;utm_content=Google+Reader">following 18-year-old Andre Pitts out of an elevator</a> on Sunday evening and then shooting at him.  Video from another camera outside clearly shows his face. </p>

<p>The shooting occurred at 230 Lott Avenue at 7:30 p.m. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110419215925/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2011/04/18/suspect-sought-in-fatal-brooklyn-shooting-of-18-year-old-andre-pitts/">According to WCBS 2</a>, &quot;Police described the shooting suspect as a male in his 20s and 5-foot-8. He was last seen wearing a black jacket with a green hood, which had green, white and red stripes on the sleeves. He was also wearing a black doo-rag and red sneakers.&quot;</p>

<p>Anyone with information is asked to contact NYPD Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS or the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110419215925/http://a056-crimestoppers.nyc.gov/crimestoppers/public/index.cfm">NYPD Crime Stoppers&apos; website</a>. </p>
