<p><img alt="2008_12_alto.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20110424103755im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2008_12_alto.jpg" width="142" height="114" class="right">Police <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110424103755/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=6560991">found three suspects</a> involved in the shooting of the operator of Alex&apos;s Tobacco shop on Broadway at West 71st Street. WABC 7 reports the three were found at the Port Authority Bus Terminal. The store&apos;s manager is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110424103755/http://gothamist.com/2008/12/17/store_owner_shot_on_upper_west_side.php">stable condition</a> after the 4:30 p.m. incident yesterday; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110424103755/http://www.nypost.com/seven/12182008/news/regionalnews/manhattan/no_cigar_for_these_gunmen_144767.htm">according to the Post</a>, &quot;One of the men fired a shot into the air to announce they were robbing the store...[Then] <strong>one of the robbers then grabbed a box as the other fired a shot that hit the charging owner in the hip</strong>.&quot; (The shop is a popular lottery ticket destination, as it&apos;s right near the West 72nd 1/2/3 station.) Witnesses had seen <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110424103755/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2008/12/17/2008-12-17_west_side_smoke_shop_owner_shot_in_holdu.html">two masked men leave</a> in an idling car, which was later found abandoned on West End Avenue.  And a resident told the Daily News, &quot;<strong>I can&apos;t believe this happened in this neighborhood. It must be the economy</strong>.&quot;  </p>
