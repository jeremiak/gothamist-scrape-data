										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_12_bedstuycreep.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160408035917im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_12_bedstuycreep.jpg" width="640" height="564" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police released a surveillance picture of a man who was allegedly lurking in a Brooklyn woman&apos;s apartment as she slept.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on November 23rd, 2015 at 8:19 a.m.: &quot;The suspect entered the 38 year-old female victim&apos;s apartment on Herkimer Street while she was inside sleeping. The suspect fled out of the apartment and to parts unknown when the victim began to scream.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect, who is wanted for burglary, is described as being about 27 years old, 5&apos;8&quot; and 140 pounds. Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160408035917/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
