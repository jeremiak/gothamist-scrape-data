<p>Christie and Foxx were raising money for the Apollo Theater;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014329/http://pagesix.com/2014/08/17/chris-christie-busts-out-wild-dance-moves-at-hamptons-party/"> from Page Six</a>:</p><blockquote>After performances by Gladys Knight, Jon Bon Jovi, Sting and Pharrell Williams at Ronald Perelman&#x2019;s East Hampton estate, Oscar winner Foxx called out to Christie in the audience, &#x201C;I know you&#x2019;ve got a dance in you, Chris Christie.&#x201D;<p></p>

<p>Christie, there as a guest of Bon Jovi, raced to the stage for some rambunctious gyrating. Not to be upstaged, Sen. John McCain then joined Foxx and performed the robot dance before McCartney and Spike Lee showed off more muted moves.</p>

<p>Foxx told us after the show, which raised $4 million for programs at the Harlem venue, that he felt he had to step up on last year&#x2019;s performance, which saw <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014329/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/26/videos_pharell_sings_as_colin_powel.php">Colin Powell dance on stage</a>. Of his dancing partners, he added, &#x201C;Its always the ones you don&#x2019;t expect. Republicans love to dance &#x2014; in the Hamptons.&#x201D;</p></blockquote>That&apos;s right&#x2014;McCain did the robot:<p></p>
