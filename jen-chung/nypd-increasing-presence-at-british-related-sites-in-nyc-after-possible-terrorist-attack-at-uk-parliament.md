title: NYPD Increasing Presence At British-Related Sites In NYC After Possible Terrorist Attack At UK Parliament
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2017_03_londparl.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170324171448im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_03_londparl.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> Outside Parliament in London after the attack (Getty Images)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>A man was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170324171448/https://www.nytimes.com/2017/03/22/world/europe/uk-westminster-parliament-shooting.html?_r=0">shot in London today after allegedly stabbing a police officer</a> and trying to storm Parliament, and pedestrians were run over by a driver in a 4x4 car <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170324171448/http://www.nbcnews.com/news/world/shooting-reported-near-britain-s-house-commons-london-n736991">on the nearby Westminster Bridge</a>. The Metropolitan Police says it&apos;s &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170324171448/https://twitter.com/metpoliceuk/status/844572599342518273">treating this as a terrorist incident until we know otherwise</a>.&quot; </p>

<p>One woman who was on Westminster Bridge <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170324171448/http://www.bbc.com/news/uk-39355940">has died</a>. BBC News reports, &quot;A doctor at St Thomas&apos; Hospital said a number of other people were hurt - some with &apos;catastrophic&apos; injuries.&quot; The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170324171448/https://www.nytimes.com/2017/03/22/world/europe/uk-westminster-parliament-shooting.html?_r=0">NY Times says</a>, &quot;It was not immediately clear if the motorist was also the knife assailant, but the knife attack and the ramming on the bridge quickly stoked fears of terrorism.&quot;</p>

<p>The NYPD is increasing its presence at locations with British connections in NYC. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170324171448/http://abc7ny.com/news/nypd-securing-certain-british-sites-after-violence-at-parliament/1812870/">According to ABC 7</a>, those locations include British Consulate and United Nations Mission.</p>
