                            <p>Unless you&apos;re 12 years old and under, <strong>bicycling on NYC sidewalks is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807012514/http://www.nyc.gov/html/dot/html/bicyclists/biketips.shtml">against the law</a></strong>. It&apos;s also <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807012514/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/14/nyc_bike_etiquette_in_five_simple_t.php">insanely rude</a> and gives anti-bike people ammo to bitch and moan about how cyclists are inconsiderate and reckless. Yet there&apos;s an even more elitist group of cyclists out there:<em> Celebrity</em> cyclists, like Jake Gyllenhaal who ride (to their spinning classes, probably) on the sidewalk, RUINING THINGS FOR EVERYONE.</p>

<p>Other sidewalk-biking stars include Joe Jonas&#x2014;in Soho on Wednesday&#x2014;and Leonardo DiCaprio. DiCaprio is a particularly egregious offender: Check out him <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807012514/http://www.popsugar.com/Leonardo-DiCaprio-Blake-Lively-Bike-Riding-Pictures-18556994">salmoning with Blake Lively</a>!</p>

<p>Perhaps they heard how Robin Williams <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807012514/http://gothamist.com/2011/04/20/robin_williams_avoids_ticket_for_si.php">got his sidewalk cycling ticket thrown out</a> (the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807012514/http://gothamist.com/2010/01/25/cops_crack_down_on_sidewalk_bike_ri.php">fine is $100</a>). But celebs are not just like us, because <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807012514/http://gothamist.com/2009/06/05/husband_wasnt_missing_just_jailed_f.php">us plebes would probably be in jail</a>. At any rate, we salute Keira Knightley for properly dismounting a bicycle in a street before placing it in a rack!</p>
