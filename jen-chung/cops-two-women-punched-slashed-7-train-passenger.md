title: Cops: Two Women Punched, Slashed 7 Train Passenger
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427150451/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_05_7trainassault.jpg"> <img alt="2014_05_7trainassault.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427150451im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2014/06/2014_05_7trainassault-thumb-468x303-844867.jpg" width="320" height="207" class="image-right"> </a> </span>Police are hoping the public can help them find two suspects accused of assaulting a man in Queens. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident happened on May 24, at 9:46 a.m., at the 74th Street and Roosevelt Avenue 7 train station. A 22-year-old man got into a verbal altercation with two women on the Manhattan-bound platform. The two women started to punch the man in the head and body, and then one of the suspects slashed his arm and shoulder with an unknown object. </p>

<p>The victim was treated and released from Elmhurst General Hospital.</p>

<p>The police made a photograph of one of the suspects public; she is described as a 35-year-old woman with black hair in a ponytail. Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427150451/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
