<p>The police released video of a suspect in a robbery that took place yesterday evening at a Brooklyn grocery.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 7:38 p.m., a man walked into the Myrtle Grocery at 297A Myrtle Avenue. After displaying a gun and demanding money, the police say he &quot;discharged one round from the firearm, striking the wall, and removed $900 cash from the register, before fleeing west on Myrtle Avenue.&quot; </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_09_myrtleavnue.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013020im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_09_myrtleavnue.jpg" width="640" height="324"> <br> <i> Via Google Maps</i></div> <p></p>

<p>The video shows the suspect climbing over the counter for the money. Luckily, no one was injured. </p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510013020if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/_Ye0WTtOB_o?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe><br>

Police say the suspect is about 5&apos;9&quot; tall and 160 pounds, and was wearing a black mask, a gray jacket, black jeans and black sneakers. The gun was a black .40-caliber semi automatic.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013020/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
