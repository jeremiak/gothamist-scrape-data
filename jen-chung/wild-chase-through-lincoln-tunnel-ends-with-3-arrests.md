<p>Apparently New Jersey State Police had tried to pull over a Mercury Villager minivan in Newark on the New Jersey Turnpike at 7:28 p.m.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307192935/http://www.northjersey.com/story/news/crime/2017/03/03/arrests-made-after-chase-inside-lincoln-tunnel/98717766/">According to the Record</a>, &quot;The driver instead didn&apos;t stop and proceeded to lead police on a chase that ended in the Lincoln Tunnel. As the driver entered the tunnel, state troopers alerted Port Authority Police.&quot;</p>

<p>When the minivan got to the Manhattan side, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307192935/http://www.northjersey.com/story/news/crime/2017/03/03/arrests-made-after-chase-inside-lincoln-tunnel/98717766/">driver and two occupants allegedly </a>&quot;jumped out the vehicle after exiting the tunnel and ran&quot; before the Port Authority Police <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307192935/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Lincoln-Tunnel-Delays-Police-Activity-Chase-Manhattan-New-Jersey-415372063.html">arrested them</a>.</p>
