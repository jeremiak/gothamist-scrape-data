<p>A grocery deliveryman was seriously hurt after falling into an empty elevator shaft yesterday in the Bronx.</p>

<p>He had just brought groceries to a resident on the second floor of 1160 Cromwell Avenue. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011053/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/12/17/man-falls-several-stories-down-elevator-shaft-in-the-bronx/">According to WCBS 2</a>, &quot;The woman who ordered the groceries said the elevator door opened, but the car was not there.&quot; </p>

<p>The Fire Department says the man, who had a shopping cart with him, fell three stories and suffered head and chest trauma, as well as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011053/http://7online.com/news/deliveryman-rescued-after-falling-down-elevator-shaft-with-shopping-cart-in-bronx/441220/">lacerations to his legs</a>. He was taken to Lincoln Hospital in serious condition.</p>

<p>The Department of Buildings website says that the FDNY has <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011053/http://a810-bisweb.nyc.gov/bisweb/OverviewForComplaintServlet?requestid=2&amp;vlcompdetlkey=0001815169">ordered an investigation</a>.</p>
