					<p>We gave you a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427035352/http://gothamist.com/2015/11/16/holiday_train_show_video.php">sneak peek video yesterday</a> of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427035352/http://www.nybg.org/hts15/">New York Botanical Garden&apos;s annual Holiday Train Show</a>&#x2014;now here are some photos of the dazzling display.</p>

<p>There&apos;s nearly half a mile of tracks for the trains, which wind their way through reproductions of the NYC landmarks like the Brooklyn Bridge, City Hall, Rockefeller Center and the Guggenheim Museum. </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2015_11_train4.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427035352im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_11_train4.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427035352/http://saimokhtari.com/">Sai Mokhtari</a> / Gothamist</i></div> <p></p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2015_11_train3.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427035352im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_11_train3.jpg" width="640" height="427"> <br> <i> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427035352/http://saimokhtari.com/">Sai Mokhtari</a> / Gothamist</i></div> <p></p>

<p>The show opens on Saturday, and the NYBG recommends advance reservations. And if you want to experience the show without the kids, check out <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427035352/http://www.nybg.org/hts15/evenings.html">Bar Car Nights</a>, where you can sip a cocktail and admire the craftsmanship and beauty. (We did this last year and it&apos;s a really good time; if you&apos;ve never been up there you should note that it&apos;s just a quick MetroNorth train ride away!)</p>

<p>Plus, there are new &quot;360&#xB0; viewing opportunities of some of the most intricate structures&quot;!</p>
