<p>The police are asking for the public&apos;s help in identifying a suspect who assaulted someone in Manhattan last month.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on May 10, at 9:40 p.m. when an 18-year-old man was at the corner of Third Avenue and  East 23rd Street. Cops say the vicim &quot;was talking to the suspect... about a dance competition when the suspect began to punch him in the face.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect fled and the victim was treated at Bellevue for a broken jaw, fractured eye socket and abrasions to the head.</p>

<p>Police describe the suspect as an Asian male in his early twenties. Here&apos;s surveillance video:</p>
