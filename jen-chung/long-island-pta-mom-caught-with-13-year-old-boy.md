The VP of the Baldwin Middle School PTA and boy were in the &quot;parked, running vehicle&quot; behind the school, according to Newsday.  The police said officers approached the car and &quot;tapped on a rear window.&quot;  In spite of a foggy window, Det. Lt. Kevin Smith said the officers also &quot;<strong>noticed both of the individuals were not clothed from the waist down</strong>.&quot;  The boy first claimed he was 18, but then said he was 15; it was at the hospital where cops found out he was 13.  Tuckruskye was charged with endangering the welfare of a child and may face more serious charges &quot;if police determine a sexual act took place.&quot; The Baldwin school system issued a statement, &quot;The district is working to maintain a normal and calm school environment for all students while police investigate,&quot; but parents <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141223182608/http://www.wcbs880.com/PTA-Official--Teen-Found-Half-Naked-in-Car/3501952">were shocked</a>.</p>
