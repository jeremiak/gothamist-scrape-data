<p>A man and a woman stole jewelry from a Madison Avenue store last week, and the authorities hope the public can help identify them.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the grand larceny occurred at 3:10 p.m. on December 14th, at the Links of London store at 535 Madison Avenue: &quot;Suspect #1 removed eight (8) pieces of jewelry from a display case while Suspect #2 blocked the employee&apos;s view of the theft.  The suspects then fled to parts unknown.&quot;</p>

<p>Suspect #1 is described as a woman in her late 40&apos;s with blonde hair and glasses, while Suspect #2 is a man in his early 50&apos;s, approximately 5&apos;10&quot;, wearing black framed glasses. Police released these images of them:<br>
