					<p>After missing a shot at the crown, er, dish <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170217160039/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/17/winning_dog_annoying_name.php#photo-1">last year</a>, a five-year-old German Shepherd named Rumor Has It won Best In Show at the 141st Westminster Dog Show. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170217160039/https://www.nytimes.com/2017/02/14/sports/winner-westminster-dog-show-best-in-show.html?_r=0">The best-in-show judge said</a>, &quot;The German shepherd standard talks about quality and nobility. When you recognize it, it hits you at home, and that&apos;s what it really is. She is just magnificent.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170217160039/http://www.lockenhausgermanshepherds.com/ch.-rumor.html">Rumor</a>, who is named for the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170217160039/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-_6BBAVfzqM">Adele song</a>, actually came out of retirement to compete this year (a year where there <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170217160039/http://gothamist.com/2017/02/13/cats_at_the_dog_show_whoa.php#photo-1">were actually cats!</a>); <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170217160039/http://www.espn.com/espn/story/_/id/18688828/rumor-german-shepherd-wins-best-show-westminster">according to ESPN</a>, &quot;Rumor pulled off something of a shocker. She had been at home in Wisconsin for months, a house pet headed toward having puppies, when she suddenly jumped back into the show ring in January... The 5-year-old Rumor beat out a Norwegian elkhound, a Pekingese, a miniature poodle, an Irish setter, a boxer and a Norwich terrier in the final ring. The Irish setter called Adrian finished second.&quot;</p>

<p>The only other German Shepherd to win best in show was one named Manhattan in 1987.</p>

<p>Rumor&apos;s handler Kent Boyles <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170217160039/https://www.nytimes.com/2017/02/14/sports/winner-westminster-dog-show-best-in-show.html?_r=0">said</a>, &quot;She had a pretty good night.&quot; And now she&apos;s having a busy morning&#x2014;she&apos;s on the press circuit!</p>
