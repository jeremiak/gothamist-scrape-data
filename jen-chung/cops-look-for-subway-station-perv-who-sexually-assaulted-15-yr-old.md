<p>The police released photographs of a man suspected of sexually assaulting a teenaged girl in a Brooklyn subway station two weeks ago. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at around 5 p.m. on Thursday, May 31, a 15-year-old girl was in the Fort Hamilton N train station when the suspect approached her from behind and sexually assaulted her.  The suspect then fled.<br>
 <br>
The police describe him as a Hispanic male, between 20 and 25 years old, with a mohawk style haircut.  He was last seen wearing a black t-shirt with white and grey lettering, white and dark colored striped shorts, black sneakers. <br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150501173515/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
