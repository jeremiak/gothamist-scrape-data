According to WCBS 2, the SUV was carrying six people (including four children) and it &quot;went through a cement barrier and plunged about 15 to 20 feet onto I-280.&quot; Here&apos;s a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130522113231/http://photos.nj.com/star-ledger/2013/04/truck_pushes_suv_over_fence_on_14.html">picture of the drop</a>.<p></p>

<p>The trailer trailer was on Freeway Drive when it hit the SUV, sending the SUV careening over the 8 foot fence, according to police. A resident nearby <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130522113231/http://www.nj.com/essex/index.ssf/2013/04/tractor-trailer_crash_in_east.html#incart_river_default">told the Star-Ledger,</a> &quot;I heard it. I&apos;ve seen over 100 accidents here, this is the worst.&quot;</p>

<p>All of the SUV&apos;s passengers were taken to the hospital; the father, who had to be extricated from the car, has serious injuries. The Star-Ledger also <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130522113231/http://photos.nj.com/4504/gallery/truck_pushes_suv_over_fence_onto_interstate_280/index.html">has many more photos of the crash</a>.<br>
