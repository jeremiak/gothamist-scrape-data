<p>We&apos;ve been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821204100/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/16/submit_your_questions_to_democratic.php">reminding you</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821204100/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/19/what_are_your_burning_questions_for.php">reminding you</a> but now it&apos;s actually go time: Tonight is the Official Campaign Finance Board Democratic Mayoral Debate&#x2014;and you can watch the name calling begin at 7 p.m.!</p>

<p>Gothamist and our partners, NY1 News, NY1 Noticias, WNYC, Citizens Committee for New York City, Citizens Union, Hispanic Federation, and Transportation Alternatives, are sponsoring the debate. And given the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821204100/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/21/de_blasio_and_quinn_at_each_others.php">aggressive attacks and spinning</a>, be sure to watch out for these things unfold at Town Hall:</p>

<p>- Bill de Blasio and Christine Quinn, the two frontrunners, going after each other<br>
- Anthony Weiner reminding everyone he didn&apos;t vote to extend term limits<br>
- John Liu being extremely liberal<br>
- Bill Thompson positioning himself as a strong contender<br>
- Sal Albanese keeping everyone else on their toes<br>
- Erick Salgado introducing himself to a bigger audience</p>

<p>WNYC has <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821204100/https://twitter.com/WNYC/status/370148408591273985/photo/1">created a Debate Bingo card</a> and the Campaign Finance Board has a scorecard (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821204100/http://www.nyccfb.info/PDF/scorecard.pdf">PDF</a>) for you to gauge the candidates&apos; performances. The primary is on September 10.</p>

<p>You will be able to watch/listen to the 90-minute debate in different ways: Via TV on NY1/NY1 Noticias; via radio on WNYC; and via online on Gothamist. Or head to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821204100/http://www.nyccfb.info/debates/schedule-2013.html">one of the CFB&apos;s watch parties</a> (see the column at far right). We&apos;ll also be live-chatting&#x2014;stayed tuned.</p>
