                            <p>A five-story grow house in the Bronx was raided by police.  Narcotics detectives, who were tipped off from someone in the community about the 610 Morris Park Avenue home, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813235336/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=8526160">took 800 plants</a>, some of them were 6 feet tall.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813235336/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Bronx-Marijuana-Farm-Morris-Park-Avenue-138431314.html">NBC New York reports</a>, &quot;Sources tell NBC New York that each floor of the building was used for a different stage of growth for the plants. It had been outfitted with an intricate ventilation and hydration system.&quot; Three people were arrested.</p>
