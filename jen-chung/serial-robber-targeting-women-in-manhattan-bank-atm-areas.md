										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2015_11_banka.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160404150325im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_11_banka.jpg" width="640" height="451"> <br> <i> The Bank of America at 835 8th Avenue (Google Maps)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>The police are looking for an individual accused of robbing six women from four different banks in Midtown West and the Upper West Side this month.</p>

<p>The police say that the suspect, described as being about 5&apos;7&quot; and 180-190 pounds, has struck six different times. Here are the NYPD details:</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_11_brpa.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160404150325im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_11_brpa.jpg" width="200" height="252" class="image-right"> </span></p><blockquote>1: On Sunday, November 15, 2015 at approximately 1740 hours, the suspect approached the 23 year-old female victim while she was using an ATM inside of Bank of America, located at 835 8 Avenue, and displayed a knife and demanded cash.  The suspect removed $300 in cash and fled.<br>
 <br>
2: On Friday, November 20, 2015 at approximately 2125 hours, the suspect approached the 27 year-old female victim while she was using an ATM inside of Bank of America, located at 835 8 Avenue. The suspect demanded money, and grabbed the victim by the neck and held an unknown object to her side.  The suspect removed $260 in cash from the victim and fled.<br>
 <br>
3: On Saturday, November 21, 2015 at approximately 2000 hours, the suspect approached the 25 year-old female victim inside 2260 Broadway after she used the ATM therein. The suspect proceeded to place a sharp object to her chin and demand money.  The suspect removed $121 in cash and fled.<br>
 <br>
4: On Sunday, November 22, 2015 at approximately 1553 hours, the suspect approached the 53 year-old female victim as she was using an ATM inside the Citibank, located at 170 West 72 Street and attempted to put her into a chokehold.  The victim was able to maneuver out of the hold and the suspect fled with no proceeds.<br>
 <br>
5: On Sunday, November 26, 2015 at approximately 1605 hours, the suspect approached the 29 year-old female victim while she was using an ATM inside Bank of America, located at 1886 Broadway. The suspect removed $60 and punched the victim in her face as he attempted to flee.<br>
 <br>
6: On Sunday, November 22, 2015 at approximately 1810 hours, the suspect approached the 31 year-old female victim from behind while she was using an ATM inside of Bank of America, located at 835 8 Avenue.  The suspect proceeded to put a knife to the victim&apos;s neck and demanded she withdraw $500 in cash.  The suspect proceeded to remove the victim&apos;s $500 and fled.</blockquote><p></p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to these incidents is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160404150325/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers website</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then entering TIP577.</p>
