<p>A woman managed to fight off an attempted rapist in Manhattan yesterday evening, according to the NYPD.</p>

<p>The authorities released surveillance video of the suspect and say that the attack occurred around 7:15 p.m. on July 11th, near East 102nd Street and Park Avenue. A 31-year-old woman was followed into her building by the suspect, who then tried to rape as she headed up the stairs, police say. </p>

<p>The victim managed to fight off the male suspect, who then fled. He is described as being 18 years old, 5&apos;5&quot; and 130 pounds.</p>
