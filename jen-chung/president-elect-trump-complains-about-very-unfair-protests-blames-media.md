<p>Sure, President-elect Donald Trump <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170918205608/http://gothamist.com/2016/11/09/trump_president.php#photo-1">struck a conciliatory tone</a> during his victory speech, promising, &quot;I pledge to every citizen of our land that I will be president for all Americans.&quot; And he was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170918205608/http://dcist.com/2016/11/trump_i_have_great_respect_for_obam.php">respectful and positive after meeting</a> with President Obama at the White House. But at 9:19 p.m., after Trump returned to his <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170918205608/http://gothamist.com/2016/11/10/here_have_more_nightmares.php">fortified NYC penthouse</a>, and seeing a second night of protests, he Tweeted, &quot;Just had a very open and successful presidential election. Now professional protesters, incited by the media, are protesting. Very unfair!&quot;</p>
