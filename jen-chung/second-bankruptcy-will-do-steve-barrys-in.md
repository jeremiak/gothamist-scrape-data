title: Second Bankruptcy Will Do Steve & Barry's In
---

	
										<p><img alt="2008_11_sbarry.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160401005321im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2008_11_sbarry.jpg" width="130" height="118" class="right">Steve &amp; Barry&apos;s, the cheap clothing retailer that offered inexpensive fashions from celebrities like Sarah Jessica Parker, Amanda Bynes and Stephon Marbury, <a ref="http://www.newsday.com/business/ny-bzstev215936010nov21,0,1714159.story">filed for bankruptcy</a> for the second time in three months. Steve &amp; Barry&apos;s, whose bankruptcy filing said it had no &quot;prospects for continued financing of their business,&quot; will start liquidating and closing its 177 stores. Newsday reports that a group of employees who were laid off on Monday are suing because the company did not give them the required 60-day notice. Back in July, the Long Island-based company <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160401005321/http://gothamist.com/2008/07/09/did_898_dresses_do_steve_barrys_in.php">came under scrutiny</a> during its financial troubles; it did<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160401005321/http://gothamist.com/2008/08/05/life_line_for_steve_barrys.php"> receive a $163 million lifeline</a> to restructure but the current economic situation was unforgiving.</p>
