title: Cops Seek Driver Who Fatally Struck Pedestrian In Brooklyn Hit-And-Run
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_11_fleeingcar.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012034im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_11_fleeingcar.jpg" width="640" height="452" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The police are hoping the public can help them identify the driver <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012034/http://gothamist.com/2014/11/01/pedestrian_fatally_struck_by_hit-an_1.php">who killed a pedestrian in a hit-and-run last week</a>. </p>

<p>The incident occurred at 2:20 a.m. on Saturday, November 1, when Alex Davis, 59, was trying to cross Rockaway Parkway between Clarkson Avenue and Winthrop Street in Brownsville. Davis was apparently crossing mid-block when he was struck, and he was pronounced dead at Brookdale Hospital. </p>

<p>Police released video of the suspected vehicle, noting that it was &quot;a grey 2013-2014 Ford Mustang with a V6 engine that sustained front end damage from the collision causing a piece of the grille to fall off.&quot;</p>
