<p>A woman was terrorized by a man with a bag of feces in Manhattan on Monday, and now police are hoping video will help the public identify the revolting perpetrator.</p>

<p>The NYPD says that on June 27th, at 6 p.m., a 27-year-old woman was walking down East 74th Street, between First and York Avenues, when a man approached her from behind, grabbed her waist, shoved &quot;a bag of feces down her pants and grabbed her buttocks.&quot; After shoving her, the suspect fled west down East 74th. </p>

<p>Video shows the disturbing incident, which features the suspect appearing to take off a pair of gloves and throwing them to the side:<br>

<p>The victim ran into a nearby parking garage; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171025132243/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/man-shoves-bag-feces-woman-shorts-upper-east-side-article-1.2693508">an attendant told the Daily News</a>, &quot;She couldn&apos;t speak. She was shaking. She couldn&apos;t even call 911. She tried twice.&quot; Her boyfriend and mother brought clean clothes for her; the attendant added that the suspect &quot;is insane. He was dirty ... There was a guy across the street who screamed, &#x2018;Hey, you! What&#x2019;s happening?&#x2019; And he ran off.&quot;</p>

<p>The male suspect is described as 40 years old, 6&apos; tall and 200 pounds. It&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171025132243/http://nypost.com/2016/06/30/sicko-caught-on-camera-shoving-his-poop-into-womans-shorts/">unclear if the feces were human or animal</a>.<br>
