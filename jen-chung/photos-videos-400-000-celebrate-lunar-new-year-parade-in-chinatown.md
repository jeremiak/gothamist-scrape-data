title: Photos, Videos: 400,000 Celebrate Lunar New Year Parade In Chinatown
---

	
										<p>Hundreds of thousands packed Chinatown&apos;s streets yesterday to celebrate the Lunar New Year&apos;s Year of the Water Dragon.  A Better Chinatown Society spokesperson <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140504201216/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/year-dragon-thunders-chinatown-article-1.1013706?localLinksEnabled=false">estimated that nearly 400,000</a> watched the colorful pageantry.  </p>

<p> Better Chinatown Society President <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140504201216/http://manhattan.ny1.com/content/top_stories/155058/thousands-march-through-chinatown-to-ring-in-year-of-the-water-dragon">Peter Sang explained</a>, &quot;Water represents money, so the dragon will bring fortune and money to our community. That&apos;s what we need now, right? Jobs and money.&quot;  </p>
