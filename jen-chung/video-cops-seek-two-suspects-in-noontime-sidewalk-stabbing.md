<p>The police have released a video of an attack that occurred last week in hopes the public can help identify the suspects. </p>

<p>On Saturday, May 25, at around noon, a 17-year-old man at the corner of 37th Avenue and 111th Street was approached by two men who engaged him in an argument. Then the two men stabbed him in the stomach and in the lower back.</p>
