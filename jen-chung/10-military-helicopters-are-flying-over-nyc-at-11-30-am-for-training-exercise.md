<p>Since a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170317101109/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/13/blizzard_nyc_snow.php">huge blizzard is going to bury</a> the Eastern seaboard tomorrow, there&apos;s been a change of plans for the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170317101109/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/11/heads_up_military_helicopter_to_con.php">previously-announced military flyover</a>.</p>

<p>The NYPD has just announced that due to tomorrow&apos;s blizzard forecast, &quot;the military exercise previously scheduled for 3/14 will occur today, 3/13. A flight of ten military helicopters will conduct a training exercise over New York City today, 3/13, at 11:30 AM. The aircraft will fly over the Verrazano Bridge, the Hudson River, the Harlem River, the East River and the Statue of Liberty at an altitude of 500-1000 feet.&quot;</p>
