title: Lawsuit Accuses DEP Of Sexism
---

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2009_04_depnyc.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20110623151850im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2009_04_depnyc.jpg" width="130" height="92" class="image-right"> </span>Two female employees with the city&apos;s Department of Environmental Protection are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110623151850/http://www.nyc.gov/html/dep/html/home/home.shtml">suing the department</a> on federal discrimination charges.  The Daily News reports, &quot;Lillian Padilla and Magda Rodriguez say they get the hardest and dirtiest jobs, are routinely threatened and insulted, and are denied showers or changing rooms after working in sewage.&quot;  The pair say they&apos;ve had pornography put in their trucks, been called &quot;bitches&quot; and &quot;dykes&quot; (they are lesbians) and been threatened when they&apos;ve complained.  They also claim that other female employees have left due to the atmosphere&#x2014;out of the 426 DEP laborers, five are women&#x2014;with Padilla adding, &quot;I once had a supervisor telling me that he would never have a woman telling a man what to do.&quot;  The DEP said it is investigating the complaint, which has been filed with the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110623151850/http://www.eeoc.gov/">EEOC</a>.  Rodriguez said, &quot;I just want the policies changed so that women have equal rights. I have no shower. She has no shower. We work in sewage. That&apos;s what we do all day.&quot;  </p>
