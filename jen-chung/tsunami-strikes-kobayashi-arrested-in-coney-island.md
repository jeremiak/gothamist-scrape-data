<p>After watching <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131700/http://gothamist.com/2010/07/04/nathans_hot_dog_eating_contest_is_o.php">Joey Chestnut hold onto the Mustard Belt</a> from the sidelines, thanks to a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131700/http://gothamist.com/2010/07/01/kobayashi_contest_organizers_are_tr.php">contract dispute</a>, beloved competitive eater Takeru &quot;Tsunami&quot; Kobayashi was arrested after Nathan&apos;s Famous Hot Dog Eating Contest.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131700/http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2010/07/04/AR2010070403091.html">According to the AP</a>, &quot;Kobayashi went on stage after the competition. Police officers grabbed him, and he tried to hold onto police barricades as they took him into custody.&quot; </p>

<p>A <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131700/http://twitter.com/johnheywood/status/17733703781">Twitterer says</a> he was wearing a &quot;Free Kobi&quot; shirt while <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131700/http://twitter.com/jmjones/statuses/17734772745">another decides</a>, &quot;Kobayashi getting hauled off by the NYPD at Nathan&apos;s is the cherry on the top of a delicious celebration. That&apos;s for Pearl Harbor, bitch!&quot;</p>
