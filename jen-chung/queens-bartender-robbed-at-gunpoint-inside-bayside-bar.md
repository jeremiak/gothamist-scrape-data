title: Queens Bartender Robbed At Gunpoint Inside Bayside Bar
---

	
										<p>A bartender at a Bayside watering hole was robbed by an armed man last week, police say, and surveillance video of the suspect holding up the employee has been released.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170308204506if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/j6061sq_UIc" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The incident occurred at 3:11 a.m. on Monday, February 27th, at J.D.&apos;s Saloon, at 208-11 35 Avenue in Queens. According to the NYPD, the suspect &quot;displayed a handgun and demanded money from the 35 year-old male employee. The employee complied and gave the individual approximately $700 in cash.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect, who fled, was described as being 30-40 years old and was last seen wearing a black leather jacket, a red hooded sweater, dark colored pants and a black ski cap. The authorities also released an image of the suspect.</p>
