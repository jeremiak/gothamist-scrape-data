<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right" style=" width:366px; "> <img alt="2012_02_qnshoa.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20120322054303im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_02_qnshoa.jpg" width="366" height="243"> <br> <i>Via WABC 7</i></div> Last night, at least a dozen people were trapped when a floor collapsed in an Arverne home of Queens.  Apparently there were 100 people at a St. Patrick&apos;s Day house party when the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120322054303/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=8585427">third floor collapsed onto the second floor</a> before 11 p.m. at 451 Beach 72nd Street.  <p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120322054303/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/queens/residents_rescued_in_qns_house_collapse_hCNvKdWi77UFMiW72JdO3O">According to the Post</a>, &quot;Eight people were rescued using a bucket ladder and the other four were able to leave on their own.&quot;  Two people&#x2014;a 19-year-old woman and 16-year-old girl&#x2014;were taken to a hospital for injuries.  </p>

<p>Apparently the teen partiers were in the vacant third floor space: 16-year-old Chelsea Poyser <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120322054303/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/queens/floor-collapse-sends-teens-scrambling-life-queens-house-party-article-1.1041717?localLinksEnabled=false">told the Daily News</a>, &quot;My whole life just flashed before my eyes.&quot;  A neighbor told the Post, &quot;We couldn&#x2019;t help but hear the noise, music, loud talking and yelling, then a lot of banging. Then the noise stopped.&#x201D;</p>

<p>The building has Department of Buildings complaints about <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120322054303/http://a810-bisweb.nyc.gov/bisweb/OverviewForComplaintServlet?requestid=2&amp;vlcompdetlkey=0000613415">illegal renovations from 2005</a>. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120322054303/http://usnews.msnbc.msn.com/_news/2012/03/18/10739125-third-floor-of-nyc-building-collapses-during-party?ocid=twitter">MSNBC noted</a>, &quot;Earlier tweets had said that an object appeared to have fallen from an aircraft and hit the building.&quot;</p>
