title: Stock Futures Up WIth Citi Bailout News
---

<p>News that the federal government will <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111030164817/http://gothamist.com/2008/11/24/government_in_talks_to_bail_out_cit.php">help bail out Citigroup</a>seems to be reassuring investors as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111030164817/http://www.cnbc.com/id/27886816">futures are up</a> before today&apos;s trading.  CNBC reports that Citi&apos;s shares &quot;jumped over 30% in pre-market trading&quot; (last week it lost 60% of its value) while other bank stocks, like JPMorgan Chase and Goldman Sachs are also up.  Also a source of some positive sentiment: News about President-elect Obama&apos;s economic team--Timothy Geithner for Treasury Secretary and Lawrence Summers for the National Economic Council-- and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111030164817/http://gothamist.com/2008/11/22/presidentelect_obama_offers_his_sti.php">economic stimulus plan</a>.</p>
