										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_06_suspectsorb11.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140628170915im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_06_suspectsorb11.jpg" width="640" height="577" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are asking for the public&apos;s help in location two suspects who have been stealing items from cars in Ozone Park Queens. Apparently homeowners in the neighborhood are comfortable with leaving their checkbooks and various iGadgets in their unlocked vehicles.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the grand larcenies occurred on June 12 through June 17&#x2014;and in each of the cases, the &quot;suspects enter open vehicles in driveways.&quot; </p>

<p>Here are the incidents:</p><blockquote>1)  Thursday, June 12, 2014, 2200 hours. The suspects removed Apple iPads, check books and a debit card.<br>
 <br>
2) Saturday, June 14, 2014, 0453 hours. The suspects removed a GPS.<br>
 <br>
3) Tuesday, June 17, 2014, 0001 hours. The suspects removed clothing, an Apple iPad, an iPod and sunglasses.<br>
 <br>
4) Tuesday, June 17, 2014, 0131 hours. The suspects removed a Macbook Laptop from the vehicle along with some clothing and an airline ticket.</blockquote>The cops released the above surveillance image of the suspects. Anyone with information in regards to any of these incidents is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS.<br>
The public can also submit their tips at the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140628170915/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577. All calls are confidential. <p></p>
