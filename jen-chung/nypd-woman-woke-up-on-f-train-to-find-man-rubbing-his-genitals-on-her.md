<p>The police are hoping the public can help them identify a suspect accused of committing a criminal sex act on an early morning F train this week. </p>

The victim pushed the male away who then exited the train at the Neptune Avenue subway station to parts unknown.&quot;</p>

<p>The police released a sketch of the suspect (right), describing him as about 20 years old, 5&apos;11&quot; and 160 pounds with brown eyes and brown hair, last seen wearing a dark hoodie, dark blue jeans and a green jacket.<br>
