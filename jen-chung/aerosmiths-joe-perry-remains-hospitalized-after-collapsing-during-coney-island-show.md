<p>Legendary Aerosmith guitarist Joe Perry collapsed while performing in Brooklyn last night. He appeared to be ill from the first song, and sat in front of the drum kit and played for a while until he left the stage. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170925174024/https://www.instagram.com/p/BHs_BDyjhN2/?taken-by=leeniepics">A concertgoer said</a> then he &quot;went behind a small wall and passed out. FDNY and NYPD carried him off quickly.&quot;</p>
