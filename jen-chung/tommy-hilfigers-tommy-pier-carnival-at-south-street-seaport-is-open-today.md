					<p>Last night, fashion designer Tommy Hilfiger turned Pier 16 at the South Street Seaport into his own personal <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015514/http://usa.tommy.com/shop/en/thb2cus/search/women-tommynow">&quot;Tommy Pier&quot;</a> for his fall Fashion Week show, complete with amusement park rides, a record shop, temporary tattoos doughnuts, lobster rolls, burgers and more. And today the pier is opening to the public.</p>

<p>In a change from showing spring clothes in the fall, Hilfiger is joining some other designers in a &quot;see now, buy now&quot; approach, showing clothes that are immediately available for purchase. So naturally there are clothes&#x2014;specifically from his collaboration with model Gigi Hadid&#x2014;at the pier for visitors to buy. This also meant that models at last night&apos;s show were wearing heavy sweaters and coats:</p>
