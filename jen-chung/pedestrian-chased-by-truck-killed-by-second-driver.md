Martinez was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130630112800/http://www.newsday.com/community/news/northshoresuffolk/huntington/ny-lichase0817,0,7470339.story">arrested and charged with driving under the influence of drugs</a>, but police say he had nothing to do with the chase, &quot;He was just a motorist on the road.&quot;</p>
