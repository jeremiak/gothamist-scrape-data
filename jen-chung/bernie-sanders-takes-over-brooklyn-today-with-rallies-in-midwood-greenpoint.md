<p>As a warm-up to the big <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160926135202/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/05/battle_of_brooklyn_clinton_sanders.php">Navy Yard debate</a> and New York primary, Senator and presidential hopeful Bernie Sanders will be holding two rallies in Brooklyn today. The first is in Midwood at 3 p.m. and the second is in Greenpoint at 5 p.m. Here are details:</p>

<p>The Midwood rally will be held on the block of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160926135202/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/11/bernie_goes_to_brooklyn.php">Sanders&apos;s childhood home</a>; the program begins at 3 p.m. but doors open at 1 p.m. While the event is free, Bernie&apos;s campaign <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160926135202/https://go.berniesanders.com/page/event/detail/rally/44kwz">recommends the public RSVP here</a>, as &quot;Admission is first come, first served.&quot; Oscar-nominated actor and activist Mark Ruffalo will be a special guest. The crowd entrance will be at Avenue P and East 26th Street.</p>

<p>The campaign&apos;s Greenpoint event is at WNYC Transmitter Park, with doors opening at 3 p.m. and the program starting at 5 p.m. This is also free with admission &quot;first come, first served&quot; with the crowd entrance via Greenpoint Avenue; here&apos;s the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160926135202/https://go.berniesanders.com/page/event/detail/rally/44k4g">RSVP for the Greenpoint event</a>. Susan Sarandon will be appearing. </p>

<p>His Bronx rally <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160926135202/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/01/bernie_sanders_bronx_rally.php">had about 18,000 attendees</a>. </p>

<p>It&apos;s unclear if he&apos;ll be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160926135202/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/02/feel_the_bullseye_bern.php">taking the subway</a> to any of these events. Anyway, in true NYC fashion, this Bernie mural got bombed:</p>
