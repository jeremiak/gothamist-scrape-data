</div></div><p>After <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120529102017/http://gothamist.com/2012/05/01/mitt_romney_had_private_meeting_wit.php">meeting with Mayor Bloomberg for breakfast</a>, Republican presidential contender Mitt Romney delivered lunch&#x2014;from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120529102017/http://www.joespizzanyc.com/">Joe&apos;s Pizza</a>&#x2014;to some lower Manhattan firefighters.  And he showed up with Mr. 9/11 himself, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120529102017/http://gothamist.com/tags/rudygiuliani">Rudy Giuliani</a>.  </p>

<p>Reuters&apos; Sam Youngman <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120529102017/https://twitter.com/#!/samyoungman/status/197375031939047424">Tweeted</a>, &quot;As Romney leaves Joe&apos;s Pizza carrying 3 pizzas, someone on the sidewalk shouts &apos;Just don&apos;t put &apos;em on the roof of the car.&apos;&quot;  And, when at Engine 24/Ladder 5,<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120529102017/http://thehill.com/blogs/blog-briefing-room/news/224821-romney-heckled-at-nyc-firehouse-appearance-with-giuliani?utm_campaign=briefingroom&amp;utm_source=twitterfeed&amp;utm_medium=twitterfeed"> someone yelled</a>,  &quot;Mitt Romney is a racist!&quot;  Oh, and Romney and Giuliani apparently <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120529102017/http://nymag.com/daily/intel/2012/05/romney-giuliani-pizza-firehouse-photo.html">dumped the pizzas on an aide</a> once they got to the firehouse and then, during the actual lunch, Romney opted for cheese pizza.</p>

<p>Of course, going to a fire house where many firefighters died on September 11, 2001 with the Mayor of 9/11-town might seem political to some, but it&apos;s obviously less political than <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120529102017/http://gothamist.com/2012/04/29/gop_unironically_angry_that_obamas.php">mentioning the killing of Osama bin Laden</a>.  </p>
