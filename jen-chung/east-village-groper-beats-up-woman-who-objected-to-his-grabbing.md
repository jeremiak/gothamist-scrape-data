<p>A woman who was groped in the East Village last weekend was beaten after she confronted her attacker, and now police are asking for the public&apos;s help finding him.</p>

<p>The assault took place at 1:45 a.m. on Saturday, July 23rd, in front of 208 First Avenue between 12th and 13th Streets. Police say the man grabbed the 37-year-old victim&apos;s buttocks, and when she confronted him about the incident, &quot;the suspect punched her in the face numerous times.&quot; </p>

<p>The suspect, who fled north on First Avenue, is described as being 20-25 years old, 5&apos;8&quot; and 140 pounds. He was last seen wearing a dark colored shirt and blue jeans.<br>
