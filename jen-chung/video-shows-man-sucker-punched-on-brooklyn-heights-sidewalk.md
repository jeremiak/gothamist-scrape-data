<p>A 65-year-old man was punched in the face without warning while walking down a Brooklyn street near his home two weeks ago. Now police are releasing surveillance video of the suspect and his companions in hopes of making an arrest.</p>

<p>The incident occurred at 11:15 p.m. in Brooklyn Heights. Police say that officers responded to a call about a man who was attacked in front of 99 Joralemon Street: There they &quot;discovered a 65-year-old male victim with a laceration to his left eye.&quot; The victim told them &quot;he was approached by three males and one female. One of the males punched him in the face with a closed fist breaking his glasses and causing him to fall to the ground before the group fled in an unknown direction.&quot;</p>

<p>Video shows the victim, who was treated at a hospital, falling into the garden bed outside 99 Joralemon after being hit:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020912if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/Ei90U12xaoE" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspect is described as being about 5&apos;10&quot; and 160 pounds, last seen wearing black sneakers and a white t-shirt. Three other people&#x2014;two males and one female&#x2014;were also with him. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782).
