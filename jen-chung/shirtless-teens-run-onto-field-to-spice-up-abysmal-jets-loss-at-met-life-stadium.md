<p>Diehard Jets fans/masochists are hoping that Gang Green can lose the rest of the season&apos;s games for a higher draft position. Even so, it&apos;s unlikely that many fans were hoping for the &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020358/http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/football/jets/safe-jets-unacceptably-bad-41-10-loss-colts-article-1.2899805">unacceptably bad</a>,&quot; &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020358/http://nypost.com/2016/12/05/lifeless-jets-make-case-to-fire-everyone-in-all-time-disaster/">all-time disaster</a>&quot; 41-10 loss to the Colts at MetLife Stadium&#x2014;and that the highlight of the game would be two shirtless kids running into the field during the third quarter.</p>

<p>The embarrassing game was broadcast on <em>Monday Night Football</em>, but the streaking didn&apos;t make it to TV. Thank goodness for social media:</p>
