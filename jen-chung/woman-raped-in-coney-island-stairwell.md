title: Woman Raped In Coney Island Stairwell

										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_12_coneysusp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160825152453im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_12_coneysusp.jpg" width="250" height="278" class="image-right"> </span>Police are looking for a suspect accused of raping one woman in Brooklyn and then robbing another on Sunday night.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the victim was raped at 9 p.m. on December 6, in a Coney Island apartment building: &quot;A 20-year-old female was followed into the elevator by a male, and when she exited at her floor, he grabbed her from behind and displayed a firearm. The male suspect then pulled the victim into a stairwell where he raped her and removed her cash and keys.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160825152453/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/police-hunting-accused-coney-island-rapist-robber-article-1.2458382">The Daily News reports</a> that the suspect &quot;pressed a button for a floor higher than the woman&#x2019;s, but when she tried to exit on her floor, the man grabbed her from behind in a bear hug and threatened her with a firearm that had a laser shining from it.&quot; He allegedly told her, &quot;I&#x2019;m not going to hurt you. I just want your money,&quot; before raping her, and also said, &quot;Now you&#x2019;ve seen my face, I have to kill you.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect fled and the victim was treated at Coney Island Hospital. </p>

<p>Then, police say, at 9:55 p.m., a 46-year-old woman was robbed after she exited the 16th floor of 2885 West 12th Street: &quot;The suspect followed the victim out of the elevator, displayed a firearm and removed $30 from her pocket book.&quot; The robbery victim was not injured.</p>

<p>The authorities released surveillance footage of the suspect, noting he is about 20 years old with with black hair and brown eyes. He was last seen wearing a black &amp; white skull cap, a black leather jacket, gray sweatpants and white sneakers.and was holding a black bag. <br>
