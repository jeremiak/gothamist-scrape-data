The woman followed the suspect onto a 6 train and took this photograph.<p></p>

<p>The incident occurred on October 23, around 9:30 a.m.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150916053559/http://www.dnainfo.com/20111031/murray-hill-gramercy/19yearold-woman-groped-at-union-square-subway-station">According to DNAinfo</a>, &quot;The suspect allegedly grabbed the 19-year-old victim by the shoulder, lifted her dress, and grabbed her buttocks and genitals.&quot; </p>

<p>The cops say the suspect is Hispanic, and around 5&apos;10, 200lbs.  They urge people with information to contact <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150916053559/http://a056-crimestoppers.nyc.gov/crimestoppers/public/index.cfm">NYPDCrimeStoppers.com</a>.  </p>
