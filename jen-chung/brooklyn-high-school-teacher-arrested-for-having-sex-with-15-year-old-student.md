Evelyn Rogers was charged with rape in the third-degree.<p></p>

<p>Special Commissioner of Investigations Richard Condon <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131006175222/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/brooklyn/pervy_teach_admits_having_with_year_I2UXiJq4ufMRwwJwEnBJkM?utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_content=Brooklyn">said they acted on a tip</a>, &quot;We went out and we interviewed the teacher and we interviewed the student. <strong>Both told us that they had been having sexual intercourse going back to August of last year.</strong> We conferred with the Brooklyn District Attorney&#x2019;s office and they told us to arrest her.&quot; The pair allegedly met many times; the student is now 16.</p>

<p>Rogers, 33, has been working with the Department of Education since 2011 and was teaching special education at the High School for Innovation in Advertising and Media in Canarsie.  Earlier this month, a teacher is Queens was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131006175222/http://gothamist.com/tags/danielreilly">charged with raping one of his middle school students</a>.</p>
