</div></div>					<p>July 31st, 2013 will now forever be known as the day that Maxwell&apos;s, the beloved and iconic NJ music venue, closed down its beloved Hoboken home. Yesterday the club went out with a bang, thanks to a raucous block party as well as performances from &quot;a&quot;, the Bongos, The Individuals and Delicate Steve.  </p>

<p>Maxwell&apos;s helped foster NJ musical acts as well as nurtured <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429191748/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/04/video_the_best_bands_played_maxwell.php">emerging bands from other cities that later became huge</a>. The news about Maxwell&apos;s demise was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429191748/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/03/noooooo_legendary_nj_institute_maxw.php">announced last month</a>, but more recently there&apos;s been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429191748/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/11/maxwells_to_throw_farewell_block_pa.php">word of a possible Jersey City venue</a> from co-owner Todd Abramson.</p>
