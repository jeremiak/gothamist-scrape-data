<p>A woman was raped by a home intruder who barged into her apartment located somewhere in the 33rd Precinct (which covers Washington Heights) last night. Police say the 20-year-old woman answered her door at approximately 11:30 p.m., at which point the unidentified man pushed his way into the apartment. The suspect then raped the woman before fleeing on foot.</p>

<p>The victim was taken to Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center in stable condition.</p>

<p>The police released an image of the suspect, who is described as 20-25 years old, approximately 6&apos;9&quot; and wearing black pants and a white shirt with dark horizontal stripes.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regard to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015651/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting tips to 274637(CRIMES), then enter TIP577.</p>
