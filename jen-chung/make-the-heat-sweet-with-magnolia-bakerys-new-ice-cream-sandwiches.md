                            <p>Last week, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821091932/http://magnoliabakery.com/">Magnolia Bakery</a> introduced a new line of ice cream sandwiches, just in time for the heat wave that&apos;s hammering us.</p>

<p>Made with ice cream from Ronnybrook Farms, the bakery chain is offering a Red Velvet Whoopie Pie with vanilla ice cream and a Lemon Bar with lemon ice cream (it&apos;s dotted with lemon curd), plus vanilla and chocolate ice cream sandwiches with brownies or Blondies. And then there&apos;s the Magic Bar: Basically its the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821091932/http://store.magnoliabakery.com/magic-cookie-bars-p6.aspx">Magic Bar you know</a>&#x2014;graham cracker, shredded coconuts, sweetened condensed milk and nuts&#x2014;but mixed with ice cream and covered in chocolate. </p>

<p>The summer sandwiches are available at Magnolia Bakery locations in Rockefeller Center, Bloomingdale&apos;s, the Upper West Side, and the West Village and will also be sold at Magnolia&apos;s newest ice cream cart location at South Street Seaport, which is estimated to open up next week.</p>

<p>The sandwiches range in price from $6 to $6.50 and they are very big, so consider sharing. Also, like pretty much all of Magnolia&apos;s offerings, they are definitely for those with sweet tooths, even the Lemon one! You can find them at Magnolia&apos;s Upper West Side, Rockefeller Center and South Street Seaport locations.</p>
