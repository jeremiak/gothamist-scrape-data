title: Video, Photos: Adorable, Adoptable Kittens "Graduate" From ASPCA Kitten Nursery
---

	
										<p><iframe frameborder="0" width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20161127152201if_/http://www.dailymotion.com/embed/video/k1RLsFE0VrtJg3dMu9W?autoPlay=1" allowfullscreen></iframe><br>
<span class="photo_caption">Video by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161127152201/http://twitter.com/j_leibowitz/">Jessica Leibowitz</a></span></p>

<p>When you see these kittens, the first words that come to mind are &quot;cute,&quot; &quot;aww&quot; and &quot;GAHH! I must have them all!&quot; But the word &quot;survivor&quot; also applies to these fluffy balls of joy, most of whom have been raised by the ASPCA at their Kitten Nursery on the Upper East Side. </p>

<p>Yesterday, another group of healthy and happy eight-week-old kittens &quot;graduated,&quot; meaning they will be available for adoption at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161127152201/https://www.aspca.org/nyc/aspca-adoption-center">ASPCA Adoption Center</a> (424 East 92nd Street). </p>
