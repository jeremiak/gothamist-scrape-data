title: NYPD: This Man Sexually Assaulted Woman At Flushing Beauty Salon
---

	
										<p>Police are hoping the public can help them find a man accused of attacking a woman and then sexually assaulting her in a Queen business last week. Authorities have released surveillance footage of the suspect:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314190227if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/ZFarsAdznSg" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The incident occurred on March 9, around 4 a.m. The suspect, police say, &quot;entered a beauty salon located in the vicinity of 164 Street and engaged a 29-year-old female victim in a dispute and punched the victim about the face and body. The unidentified male then sexually assaulted the victim and fled the location in an unknown direction.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was taken to a nearby hospital in stable condition. </p>

<p>The suspect, who is wanted for attempted rape, is described as being 30-35 years old, 6&apos; and 200 pounds, with a tattoo on his right arm. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or for Spanish 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314190227/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
