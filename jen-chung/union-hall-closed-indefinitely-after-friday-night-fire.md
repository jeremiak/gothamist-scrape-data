<p>The FDNY arrived at 702 Union Street, between 5th and 6th Avenues, at 11:50 p.m. and firefighters had the all-hands fire under control by 12:36 a.m. The fire apparently started in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170327221151/http://www.fireengineering.com/articles/print/volume-158/issue-8/departments/the-ol-professor/cockloft-vs-attic.html">cockloft</a> of the four-story building.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170327221151/http://www.brooklynvegan.com/fire-at-union-hall-on-friday-night-no-one-hurt-but-closed-till-further-notice/">Brooklyn Vegan reports</a>, &quot;SAVAK were headlining Friday night, along with Lake Ruth, and alarms went off as they were finishing their last song.&quot;  Lake Ruth member Hewson Chen said of the upstairs, &quot;Place is wrecked.&quot;</p>
