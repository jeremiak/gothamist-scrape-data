</div></div>					<p>Casey Affleck, Ben Affleck&apos;s seemingly Oscars-bound younger brother and star of <em>Manchester by the Sea</em> hosted <em>Saturday Night Live</em> last night. He played a breakdancing Jesus, a naughty elf, a Boston Dunkin&apos; Donuts fan and a comic nativity scene play actor in the show&apos;s Christmas episode.</p>

<p>After the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021215142/http://gothamist.com/2016/12/18/trump_putin_snl_cold_open.php">Trumpy cold open</a>, Affleck acknowledged his movie is &quot;an incredibly depressing picture... it&apos;s really a downer.&quot; He then got an assist from Alec Baldwin and John Goodman, who reassured him he didn&apos;t need to sing to host SNL, and then Black Santa (Kenan Thompson) appeared to show him a little holiday magic. (And, no, the show <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021215142/http://nymag.com/thecut/2016/12/casey-affleck-avoids-past-sex-harassment-allegations-on-snl.html">didn&apos;t reference</a> the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021215142/https://mic.com/articles/157108/casey-affleck-was-accused-of-sexual-harassment-why-isn-t-he-treated-like-nate-parker-was#.EoEfWu6rQ">sexual harassment allegations</a>Affleck <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021215142/https://www.theguardian.com/film/2010/sep/15/casey-affleck-settles-harassment-lawsuits">later settled out of court</a>.)</p>

<p>The best sketches of the night were two taped segments: The first was a look at the real customers of Dunkin&apos; Donuts, which featured the usual camera-ready types and then Affleck&apos;s scruffy Boston native. The other was Kate McKinnon reprising her Hillary Clinton character, with a plea to an elector, in a send-up of <em>Love Actually</em>&apos;s creepy stalker subplot. </p>

<p>Chance the Rapper returned to the show, performing  &quot;Same Drugs&quot; and &quot;All Night.&quot; He also appeared in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021215142/http://gothamist.com/2016/12/18/jingle_barack_the_run_dmc-inspired.php">fun &quot;Jingle Barack&quot; rap</a> and a skit about a &quot;hilarious&quot; nativity play. Former cast member Fred Armisen also appeared, helping out in a &quot;Microsoft Robot&quot; sketch and then heading to &quot;Weekend Update&quot; to talk Putin. </p>

<p>Recurring sketches included the alien abduction encounters, with Kate McKinnon&apos;s hapless Colleen Rafferty and the naughty elves bit, with Kenan Thompson and Vanessa Bayer.</p>

<p><em>Saturday Night Live</em> returns on January 14, with Felicity Jones as host and musical guest Sturgill Simpson.</p>
