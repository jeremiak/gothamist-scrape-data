</div></div>					<p>Amid the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160520004419/http://gothamist.com/2010/02/09/snowpocalypse_predicted_nyc_bears_d.php">snow storm barreling</a> into the NYC region today, firefighters battled a two alarm fire at Village Paper (which one <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160520004419/http://www.yelp.com/biz/village-paper-new-york#hrid:B-zQOAFjxenxaAADsYA2Rg">Yelp user described as</a>, &quot;Like [Halloween], Village Paper is colorful, overstimulating, a little creepy, and wonderfully foolish&quot;) in Greenwich Village that began after 7 a.m.  Readers sent us these photographs of the scene&#x2014;one said, &quot;There was a huge bang at around 7:10am this morning that sounded like a tire exploding&quot; and that the FDNY was on the scene within minutes.  Currently, the fire is under control.  </p>
