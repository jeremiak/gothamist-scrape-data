										<p>Last night Chris Rock worked out some material for his upcoming &quot;Total Blackout&quot; tour&#x2014;his <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170925094328/http://laist.com/2016/12/06/chris_rock_blackout_tour.php">first tour in nine years</a>&#x2014;which kicks off on Valentine&apos;s Day. Rock took the stage at the Comedy Cellar on Macdougal Street, where he followed Jerry Seinfeld, and was then joined on stage by Amy Schumer, Aziz Ansari, and Dave Chappelle.</p>
