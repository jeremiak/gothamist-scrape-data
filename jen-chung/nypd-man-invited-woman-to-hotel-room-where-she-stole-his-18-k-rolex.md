<p>Stranger danger: A man woke up in his Manhattan hotel room to find the woman he invited up gone... as well as his $18,000 watch. Now police have released surveillance images of the woman, who is now wanted for grand larceny.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_11_pcsusp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161201162445im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_11_pcsusp.jpg" width="412" height="301" class="image-right"> </span>The incident occurred on November 3, when according to the NYPD, the victim, a 33-year-old man, met the female suspect at Faces &amp; Names around 1:20 a.m. He invited her to his room at the Park Central Hotel, a few blocks away from the bar, and police say, &quot;The victim and unidentified male proceeded to the hotel room a short time later.&quot;</p>

<p>When the man woke up at 10 a.m., he &quot;discovered that the unidentified female was gone and that his Rolex watch valued at $18,000 had been removed from his wrist.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as having medium-length blonde hair and was last seen wearing black pants, a black top and carrying a black purse. The photos are from the hotel, while the video is from Faces &amp; Names:</p>

<p>Stealing expensive watches from sleeping men <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161201162445/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/17/fancy_watch_thief.php">is nothing new</a>.</p>
