</div></div><p>Sure, rain may have forced naked bike riders to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110623131559/http://gothamist.com/2011/06/12/naked_bike_riders_scared_off_by_a_l.php">postpone their event till next month</a>, but barbecue fans braved Mother Nature yesterday to enjoy the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110623131559/http://www.bigapplebbq.org/">9th Annual Big Apple Barbecue Block Party</a> at Madison Square Park. With <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110623131559/http://gothamist.com/2011/06/08/big_apple_barbecue_block_party_smok.php">16 pitmasters on hand</a>, including beloved North Carolina <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110623131559/http://gothamist.com/2011/05/30/pitmaster_ed_mitchell_talks_big_app.php">&apos;cue expert Ed Mitchell</a>, as well as drinks and music, people were definitely going the whole hog.</p>

<p>The event is going on today until 6 p.m.</p>
