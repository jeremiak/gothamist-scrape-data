Well, now the cops have released a very brief second video showing the paunchy pervert.</p>

<p>According to the police, on Wednesday, March 7 at around 11:30 a.m., an 11-year-old girl was at the Dunkin Donuts at 361 East 21st Street when a man &quot;approached her, touched her inappropriately, and then fled the scene.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect is described as a white man who is heavy set with short grey hair. The police encourage anyone with information about the incident to to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or submit tips via the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427151206/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a>.</p>
