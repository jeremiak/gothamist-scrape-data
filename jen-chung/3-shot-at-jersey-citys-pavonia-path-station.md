title: 3 Shot At Jersey City's Pavonia PATH Station
---


                            
                            
                            
                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left"> <img alt="2013_08_pathst.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130828052522im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_08_pathst.jpg" width="300" height="226"> <br> <i style=" width:300px; ;display:block"> Via WCBS 2</i></div> PATH service was suspended when three people were <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130828052522/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/08/25/shooting-on-path-train-triggers-partial-service-suspension/">shot at the Pavonia station</a> in Jersey City. Apparently an argument broke out on a train at 5:30 a.m. and then spilled onto the platform, prompting the violence.<p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130828052522/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_jersey&amp;id=9218225">According to WABC 7</a>, witnesses say &quot;a man bumped a woman on the train and was confronted by the woman&apos;s brother. The man then pulled a gun and shot the woman in the ankle and the brother in the leg. The brother then pulled out his own weapon and fired back.&quot; </p>

<p>The victims&apos; injuries are not considered to be life-threatening. </p>

<p>Service, which had been suspended between Journal Square and Hoboken, is mostly <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130828052522/http://www.nj.com/hudson/index.ssf/2013/08/most_path_service_back_after_shooting_at_pavonianewport_station.html#incart_river_default">back to normal</a>. </p>
