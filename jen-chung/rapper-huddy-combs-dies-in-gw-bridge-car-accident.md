title: Rapper Huddy Combs Dies In GW Bridge Car Accident
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2010_10_huddycomsb.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153310im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2010_10_huddycomsb.jpg" width="230" height="187" class="image-left"> </span>A Harlem hip-hop artist and rap promoter was killed in an accident yesterday morning on the George Washington Bridge.  Huddy Combs, also known as Andre Hudson and Huddy 6, 33, was in a car on the NJ-bound side of the bridge  when <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153310/http://cityroom.blogs.nytimes.com/2010/10/13/huddy-rap-promoter-dies-in-car-crash/">it collided with a tractor-trailer</a> around 5:15 a.m. he was pronounced dead at the scene.</p>

<p>It&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153310/http://www.northjersey.com/news/Man_killed_in_GWB_crash_was_Harlem_hip-hop_star.html">unclear what caused the accident</a>, which caused delays and closed down the upper level of the westbound GW Bridge until 9:15 a.m. CityRoom reported, &quot;Mr. Hudson worked as a party promoter mostly, but also had worked behind the scenes with local D.J.&#x2019;s and rap groups. He was at the foundation of the Harlem World movement, anchored by the rapper Mase and a slew Harlem rappers who appeared on Mase&#x2019;s multi-platinum CD, Harlem World, released in 1997.&quot;</p>

<p>There were also many <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153310/http://www.nydailynews.com/ny_local/2010/10/13/2010-10-13_harlem_hiphop_artist_huddy_combs_dies_in_car_accident_on_george_washington_bridg.html?r=ny_local&amp;utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+nydnrss%2Fny_local+%28NY+Local%29&amp;utm_content=Google+Reader">Tweets from rappers mourning the loss</a>; Juelz Santana <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427153310/http://twitter.com/thejuelzsantana/status/27262368454">said</a>, &quot;A! R.I.P. HUDDY 6 @hud6 SMFH if u don&apos;t or didn&apos;t know who he was jus know this, he was HARLEM!!!!&quot;</p>
