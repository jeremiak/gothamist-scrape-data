<p>Police are looking for two individuals who allegedly attacked a sleeping subway passenger in Brooklyn.</p>

<p>The incident occurred last week, on Monday, October 10th, at around 3:20 a.m., on a southbound F train at 4th Avenue and 9th Street. &quot;[The two individuals punched the 35 year-old male victim in the face repeatedly as he slept,&quot; according to the NYPD. &quot;The individuals fled off the train and up to the street.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim, who suffered swelling and bruising to his face, was treated at the scene by EMS.<br>
 <br>
The authorities released images of the suspects, who are described as being in their 20s; one was last seen wearing a dark colored hooded sweater and dark colored pants while the other was last seen wearing a dark colored hooded sweater.</p>
