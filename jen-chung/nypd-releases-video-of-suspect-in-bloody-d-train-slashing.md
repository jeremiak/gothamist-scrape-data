<p>The man who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016011635/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/22/d_train_subway_slashing.php">was slashed by a fellow D train passenger</a> apparently tried to follow his attacker out of the train, onto the platform and out of station yesterday morning. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016011635/http://abc7ny.com/news/video-of-suspect-in-42nd-street-bryant-park-subway-station-slashing/1522140/">A witness said of the victim</a>, &quot;[He] looked like he was OK, just dripping from a lot of blood.&quot;</p>

<p>According to police, the victim, a 38-year-old man, and the male suspect got into a physical dispute on a northbound D train at 10:18 a.m.: &quot;During the altercation the suspect cut the victim with a sharp object. The victim suffered lacerations to his face, chest, stomach and hands.&quot; The suspect then fled the train at Bryant Park-42nd Street station.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016011635/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Man-Stabbed-Leaving-Subway-Bryant-Park-Manhattan-NYPD-FDNY-394432191.html">NBC New York reports,</a> &quot;NYPD Assistant Transit Chief Vincent Coogan said witnesses told police they couldn&apos;t tell what they were arguing about. Police say they don&apos;t believe the two knew each other but may have both boarded the train at West 4th Street.&quot; The news station also obtained video showing the victim walking around the station:</p>
