<p>Champs Diner, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170223190841/http://gothamist.com/2014/07/24/the_best_vegan_dishes_in_nyc_2.php">a popular vegan restaurant</a> in Brooklyn, was burglarized early yesterday morning by a thief who took thousands in cash as well as a number of tech gadgets. The suspect was caught on surveillance video, and now the cops hope someone can help identify him.</p>

<p>The burglary happened around 1:30 a.m. on February 20th. The NYPD says that suspect entered the restaurant, located at 197 Meserole Street in Williamsburg, &quot;through a side door leading into the restaurant, and removed four iPads, a Galaxy tablet, an Amazon Fire Kindle, and two safes which contained approximately $10,000.&quot;</p>

<p>In the surveillance video, the suspect <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170223190841/http://nypost.com/2017/02/21/cops-searching-for-man-who-robbed-10k-from-popular-diner/">can be seen &quot;rattling&quot; a bicycle</a>.</p>
