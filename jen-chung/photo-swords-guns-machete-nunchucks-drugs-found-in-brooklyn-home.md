<p>The NYPD says officers found a startling cache of guns, ammunition, swords, Codeine and some marijuana at a Brooklyn home yesterday morning.</p>

<p>According to the police, 62 Precinct Field Intelligence Sergeant Jeremy Berson &quot;developed&quot; enough information for the 62 Precinct Special Operations Unit to obtain a search warrant for 175 Dahill Road in Brooklyn. When Berson and other officers executed the warrant, they found:</p>

<p><em>9 MM Highpoint Rifle-Defaced<br>
380 Cal loaded Bryco semi-automatic handgun<br>
357 Cal Taurus Revolver<br>
38 Cal INA Brazilian Revolver<br>
50 rounds of 45 cal ammunition<br>
50 rounds of 9MM ammunition<br>
67 rounds of 347 cal ammunition<br>
50 rounds of 38 cal ammunition<br>
17 rounds of 380 cal ammunition<br>
A 45 cal magazine<br>
8 swords<br>
1 machete<br>
8 Codeine tablets<br>
Assorted knives and air pistols</em></p>

<p>The photo also shows a pair of nunchuks, because you can never be too careful these days?<br>
 <br>
Bashkim Sylmetaj, 36; Michael Sylmetaj, 42; Adem Sylmetaj, 38; and Zenaida Vaysberg, 32 were arrested and charged with criminal possession of a weapons, criminal possession of a controlled substance and unlawful possession of marijuana. </p>
