</div></div>					<p>The New York Botanical Garden&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160310000807/http://gothamist.com/2015/05/15/photos_frida_kahlo_blooms_at_the_ny.php#photo-1">brilliant exhibit about Frida Kahlo</a> and her relationship with nature, <em><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160310000807/http://www.nybg.org/frida/">Frida: Art Garden Life</a></em> closes after next weekend, so you should make plans to see it soon!</p>

<p>The show includes an interpretation of Kahlo&apos;s home that she shared with Diego Rivera, Casa Azul, as well as her garden. There are also over a dozen of Kahlo&apos;s paintings and drawings on display, showcasing Kahlo&apos;s strong connection to flora and fauna. </p>
