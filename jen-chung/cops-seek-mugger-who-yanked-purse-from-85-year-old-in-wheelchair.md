<p>Just days after a man was arrested for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021115317/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/09/man_bun_mug_thug.php">stealing cash from the bra</a> of an elderly woman who was in a wheelchair, police have released another video showing another wheelchair-bound senior citizen being robbed.</p>

<p>The incident occurred yesterday, September 11th, 2016, at 2:16 p.m. in the Bronx: The NYPD says the &quot;85-year-old female victim was sitting in a wheelchair in the vicinity of East 179 Street and Marmion Avenue when an unknown individual grabbed her pocketbook from her and ran. The pocketbook contained cash and a bank card.&quot;</p>

<p>Police released video of the female suspect, described as being about 5&apos;6&quot; and 240 pounds: </p>
