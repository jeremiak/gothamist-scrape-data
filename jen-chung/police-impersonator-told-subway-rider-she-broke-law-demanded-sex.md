<p>Police are searching for a man who said he was a cop and would ignore a violation if a young woman would perform a sex act on him.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, this happened in the Bronx last month, on Tuesday, August 30th, at 7:40 p.m.: The victim, an 18-year-old woman, &quot;was approached by a male after she entered the subway system at Westchester Avenue and Grant Circle. The male stated that he was a police officer and that the female had illegally entered the subway system for which he would issue her a summons. He also stated that if the victim performed a sexual act he would not give her the ticket.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim declined and left the scene. </p>

<p>The suspect, who is wanted for criminal impersonation, was caught on surveillance video; he is described as 5&apos;3&quot;, 220 pounds and bald:<br>
