                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_10_shootinguws.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20131019005830im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_10_shootinguws.jpg" width="640" height="420" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>A man was apparently chased into an Upper West Side street where he was shot earlier this morning. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131019005830/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Shooting-NYPD-UES-Chest-227790811.html">According to NBC New York</a>, the police are now looking into it after &quot;a man walked into a Manhattan hospital with a gunshot wound to his chest.&quot;</p>

<p>A deli worker <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131019005830/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9287593">told WABC 7</a> he heard three shots and surveillance footage shows the confrontation, with the victim being followed into Amsterdam Avenue near 88th Street. </p>
