										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_10_83sar.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151223222801im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_10_83sar.jpg" width="250" height="354" class="image-left"> </span>The police are searching for a man accused of sexually assaulting a woman at knifepoint in Brooklyn last week. The suspect also allegedly robbed her.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 12:01 a.m. on October 15th, when a 22-year-old woman was entering her building on Evergreen Avenue in Bushwick: &quot;[S]he was approached from behind by the suspect who was armed with a kitchen knife. The suspect then forced the victim to the fourth floor landing where he removed her pocketbook and forced her to commit a criminal sex act on him.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect fled, and the victim was taken for treatment at a local hospital. </p>

<p>Police released a sketch of the suspect, who is described as being in his mid to late twenties, 5&apos;11&quot; to 6&apos;2&quot; tall, and weighing 180-190 pounds.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151223222801/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">the Crime Stoppers website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then entering TIP577.</p>
