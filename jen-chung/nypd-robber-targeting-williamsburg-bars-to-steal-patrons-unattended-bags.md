<p>When hanging out in bars in Williamsburg (or anywhere in NYC, for that matter), DO NOT LEAVE YOUR BAGS UNATTENDED. Otherwise you be targeted by thieves, like a person who is suspected of four grand larcenies at different bars since September.</p>

<p>The police gave details of the incidents:</p><blockquote>1: On Sunday, September 14, 2014 at approximately 2100 hours, the suspect entered &quot;The Well&quot;, located at 272 Meserole Street and removed the 23 year-old victim&apos;s unattended bag, which contained an iPhone and multiple credit cards.<br>
 <br>
2: On Saturday, October 4, 2014 at approximately 0300 hours, the suspect entered &quot;Tutus Brooklyn&quot;, located at 25 Bogart Street and removed the 26 year-old female victim&apos;s bag while she was having a cigarette outside the location.  The victim&apos;s bag contained $30 in cash and multiple credit cards.<br>
 <br>
3: On Sunday, October 12, 2014 at approximately 0330 hours, the suspect entered the &quot;Trash Bar&quot;, located at 256 Grand Street and removed the 34 year-old female victim&apos;s unattended purse.  The purse contained an iPhone and multiple credit cards.<br>
 <br>
4: On Sunday, October 19, 2014 at approximately 0320 hours, the suspect entered &quot;Privilege&quot;, located at 140 Graham Avenue and removed the 47 year-old female victim&apos;s bag. The victim&apos;s bag contained an unknown amount of cash, multiple credit cards, and an iPhone 4S.</blockquote>And here&apos;s video from the incident at Privilege:<p></p>

Police describe the suspect as a &quot;black Hispanic&quot; male about 30 years old, approximately 5&apos;10&quot;, and 160 pounds with short black hair. He was last seen wearing a white baseball hat and black jacket.</p>
