title: UPDATE: Two Double Decker Tour Buses Collide In Times Square, 11 Injured

<p>Two double decker tour buses crashed into each other&#x2014;right by the popular TKTS booth&#x2014;at 47th Street and 7th Avenue in Times Square. <b>Update below</b>: 14 people were injured.</p>

<p>The FDNY says 11 people were injured&#x2014;eight have minor injuries and three have serious non-life-threatening injuries. A person who posted a photo of the aftermath said, &quot;Bus jumped the sidewalk and hit a traffic light nearly hitting a baby in a stroller!&quot; </p>

<center><iframe src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510015025if_/http://instagram.com/p/rVAkjtkAt2/embed/" width="612" height="710" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowtransparency="true"></iframe></center>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015025/http://7online.com/traffic/double-decker-buses-collide-in-times-square/242563/">WABC 7 reports</a>, &quot;The force of the crash brought down a light pole into the plaza next to the booth.&quot; A woman told the station she grabbed her daughter just in time, &quot;The bus came at a very high rate of speed and landed right near my daughter.&quot;</p>

<p>The buses involved appear to be a Grey Line sightseeing bus and a City Sightseeing bus.</p>

<p><b>Update</b>: 14 people were reportedly injured, with most of the injuries coming from the falling traffic light. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015025/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Double-Decker-Bus-Crash-Midtown-Times-Square-270036951.html">NBC New York reports</a>, &quot;One person on board one of the buses, a tour guide, was also injured when she was thrown forward and her head became lodged in the dashboard, officials said. She&apos;s expected to be OK.&quot;</p>
