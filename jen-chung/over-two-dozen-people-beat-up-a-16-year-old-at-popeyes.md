<p>NYPD investigators are trying to locate 25 people accused of beating a teenager at a Bronx fast food restaurant&#x2014;and they need the public&apos;s help.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on the afternoon of September 22nd, around 3:50 p.m., 25 people burst into the Popeye&apos;s at 557 Grand Concourse and assaulted the 16-year-old victim. The young man sustained a laceration on his head and &quot;is in stable condition.&quot;<br>
 <br>
Police released images of the suspects, who are wanted for gang assault. Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160308152813/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
