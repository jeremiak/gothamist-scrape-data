										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2010_09_babyrob.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160705143449im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2010_09_babyrob.jpg" width="90" height="121" class="image-right"> </span>It&apos;s the old &quot;I have a baby, therefore I&apos;m not dangerous&quot; ploy: <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160705143449/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2010/09/09/nypd-hunts-for-robbers-who-used-baby-as-crime-aid/">WCBS 2 reports</a> that police are searching for a group of robbers who used a baby as part of their cover, &quot;Investigators have released surveillance video of the suspects leaving the Manhattan Avenue apartment building in Morningside Heights. Police said the woman, carrying a small child in her arms, knocked on an apartment door last Wednesday afternoon. When the victim opened the door, two men flashed guns to get into the apartment - where they stole money and an iPhone.&quot; (The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160705143449/http://www.nydailynews.com/ny_local/2010/09/09/2010-09-09_mom_helps_out_pair_of_armed_robbers_in_harlem_home_invasion.html?r=ny_local&amp;utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+nydnrss%2Fny_local+%28NY+Local%29&amp;utm_content=Google+Reader">Daily News says</a> &quot;thousands of dollars&quot; were stolen.) The police say that suspects were spotted getting out of a silver BMW before the robbery; anyone with information should contact the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160705143449/http://a056-crimestoppers.nyc.gov/crimestoppers/public/publicMWGallery.cfm">NYPD&apos;s CrimeStoppers</a>.  </p>
