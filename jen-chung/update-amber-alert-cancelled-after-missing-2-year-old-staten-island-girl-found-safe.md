										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2017_02_woo.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170723043859im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_02_woo.jpg" width="352" height="218"> <br> <i style=" width:352px; ;display:block"> Kim Woo, left; Johnny Woo</i></div> An Amber Alert has been issued to find a two-year-old girl who was taken from her Staten Island home, apparently by her father. The NY State Police say, &quot;Johnny Woo took Kim Woo under circumstances that lead the Police to believe that the child is in imminent danger.&quot; <b>Update</b>: The NYPD found the toddler and her father in the Bronx this afternoon. Kim is safe. [More below]<p></p>

<p>Police sources <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170723043859/http://pix11.com/2017/02/07/missing-2-year-old-girl-from-staten-island-may-have-been-taken-by-her-father-police/">tell PIX 11</a> that Kim Woo&apos;s parents had a dispute this morning, and the father, Johnny Woo, &quot;allegedly took the girl from a home on Ismay Street and left at about 8:45 a.m. Tuesday.&quot;</p>

<p>The NY State police say that Kim Woo is 2 years and 11 months old, about 30&quot; and 30 pounds with brown eyes and brown hair. She was last seen wearing pink pajamas. Johnny Woo, 46, is described as 5&apos;4&quot; with brown eyes and black hair. He was last seen driving a black 2014 Kia Sorrento, with NY Registration GHH8886. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in the case is urged to call 911 or 1-866-NYS-AMBER (1-866-697-2623).</p>

<p><b>UPDATE</b>: Authorities <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170723043859/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Father-Takes-2-Year-Old-Child-Attack-Estrange-Wife-Woman-Staten-Island-Willowbrook-413042193.html">pulled over Woo</a> on the Major Deegan Expressway in Bronx. The toddler was safe and taken into custody.</p>

<p>Woo allegedly took the child after an argument with his estranged wife at her Staten Island home this morning. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170723043859/http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2017/02/amber_alert_update_cops_find_f.html#incart_breaking">The Staten Island Advance reports</a>, &quot;The child&apos;s mother, 35, an off-duty officer, was punched in the torso during the dispute, police said. The mom, who works in Brooklyn, was taken to Richmond University Medical Center, West Brighton, with a stomach injury, said a law enforcement source. The couple is estranged and in the process of a divorce, the source said.&quot;</p>
