										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_05_knickshc.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173726im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_05_knickshc.jpg" width="640" height="358" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The NYPD released more surveillance images of people police officers would like to speak with about the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173726/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/07/knicks_fans_accused_of_beating_up_g.php">May 5 beating of a gay couple</a> near Madison Square Garden. </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_06_khc2ndim.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173726im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_06_khc2ndim.jpg" width="229" height="281" class="image-right"> </span>On that Sunday night, Nick Porto, 28, and his boyfriend Kevin Atkins, 22, had been walking on Eighth Avenue between 34th and 35th Streets when a group of Knicks fans harassed them. Porto had posted on Facebook, &quot;A group of Knicks fans were upset about the game and they just decided to &#x2018;fag bash&#x2019; the two of us.  They broke my nose and his wrist, and broke several hundreds of dollars worth of personal property.&quot;</p>

<p>He <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173726/http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20130506/midtown/gay-couple-assaulted-by-rowdy-knicks-fans-cops-say">also said</a>, &quot;They called us f----ts. They made fun of my jeans &#x2014; I made the jeans myself, for that day,&quot; and, when he asked why the group was bothering them, explained, &quot;Fists started flying. I was on the ground, and the only thing I could do, I reached out and grabbed someone&apos;s hair.&quot;</p>

<p>Police previously <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173726/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/09/video_knicks_fans_wanted_for_questi.php">released video footage of eight people</a> wanted for questioning (see below); the newly released images show three people. </p>

<p>There has been a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173726/http://gothamist.com/tags/hatecrime">spike in anti-gay hate crimes recently</a>. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506173726/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/03/city_to_hold_self-defense_classes_p.php">Free self-defense classes for the LGBT community</a> are being offered by the city. Also, police urge anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at www.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.com or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
