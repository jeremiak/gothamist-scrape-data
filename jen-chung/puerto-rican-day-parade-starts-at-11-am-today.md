<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130729115415/http://www.nationalpuertoricandayparade.org/parade.html">National Puerto Rican Day Parade</a> kicks off at 11 a.m. today, with the marchers starting on Fifth Avenue at 44th Street and going up Fifth to 79th Street. This year&apos;s Grand Marshal is the amazing actress-dancer-singer Chita Rivera.</p>
