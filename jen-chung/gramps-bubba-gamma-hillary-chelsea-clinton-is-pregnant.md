<p>Former (and potentially future) First Daughter Chelsea Clinton has <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502174307/https://twitter.com/AP/status/456893533362716672">announced that she&apos;s pregnant</a>. She <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502174307/http://www.washingtonpost.com/blogs/post-politics/wp/2014/04/17/chelsea-clinton-announces-shes-pregnant/">said at an event with the Lower East Side Girls Club</a>, &quot;I just hope that I will be as good a mom to my child and hopefully children as my mom was to me.&quot;</p>

<p>Clinton, 34, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502174307/http://gothamist.com/2010/08/01/photos_newlyweds_chelsea_clinton_ma.php#photo-1">married Marc Mezvinsky back in 2010</a> and since then, her parents haven&apos;t shut up about the possibility of them spawning. While she was Secretary of State, Hillary Clinton said she&apos;d <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502174307/http://www.dailymail.co.uk/femail/article-1357242/Hillary-Clinton-Chelsea-grandchildren-Ill-unbearable-grandmother.html">probably be an &quot;unbearable&quot; grandmother</a>. Last year, when asked if Hillary would rather be President or a grandmother former President Bill Clinton <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502174307/http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/09/24/bill-clinton-hillary-clinton-grandmother_n_3981223.html">said</a>, &quot;I think she&apos;d say grandmother, but I have found it best not to discuss that issue.&quot;</p>

<p>Chelsea Clinton <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502174307/http://abcnews.go.com/blogs/politics/2013/10/chelsea-hints-at-making-hillary-clinton-a-grandma-in-2014/">told Glamour magazine last year</a>, &quot;We want, God willing, to start a family. So we decided we were going to make 2014 the Year of the Baby. And please call my mother and tell her that. She asks us about it every single day.&quot;</p>
