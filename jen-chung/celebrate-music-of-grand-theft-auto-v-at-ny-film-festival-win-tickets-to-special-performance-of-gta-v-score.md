                            <p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131003202620/http://www.filmlinc.com/nyff2013">New York Film Festival</a> is about <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131003202620/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/26/kent_jones.php">bringing the best of world cinema</a> to the Big Apple for 17 days&#x2014;but there are many other programs, like <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131003202620/http://www.filmlinc.com/nyff2013/series/nyff51-convergence">the Convergence section</a>, which explores storytelling on different platforms. And one of the projects that is being highlighted is the world of the so-popular-that- <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131003202620/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/18/young_men_posed_as_nypd_cops_to_cut.php">people-pose-as-cops-to-cut-lines</a> video game <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131003202620/http://www.rockstargames.com/V/restricted_content/restricted_content_agegated/ref?redirect=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.rockstargames.com%2FV%2F&amp;hash=efbbd75c6ccf8845ad023bf548a66936"><em>Grand Theft Auto V</em></a>. </p>

<p>Tomorrow&#x2014;Sunday, September 29&#x2014;there&apos;s a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131003202620/http://www.filmlinc.com/nyff2013/films/visit-los-santos-and-blaine-county-the-world-of-grand-theft-auto-v">panel with the Rockstar Games creative team</a> to discuss its &quot;highly detailed and satirical re-imagining of modern day Southern California, from the bustling metropolis of Los Santos to the surrounding countryside, rivers, mountains, lakes and beaches of Blaine County.&quot;</p>

<p>Then on Monday night, September 30, there&apos;s a music concert, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131003202620/http://www.filmlinc.com/nyff2013/films/live-from-los-santos-the-music-of-grand-theft-auto-v">Live From Los Santos: The Music of Grand Theft Auto V</a>, featuring the game&apos;s soundtrack composers Tangerine Dream, Woody Jackson, and Alchemist &amp; Oh No. The artists will play the score with 20 other musicians at Church of St. Paul the Apostle in Midtown Manhattan, with art and other game visuals. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131003202620/http://www.filmlinc.com/nyff2013/films/live-from-los-santos-the-music-of-grand-theft-auto-v">Tickets for the 9 p.m. concert are $40</a> (for another $85, you can go to an afterparty at Le Poisson Rouge)&#x2014;but you can win a pair of tickets by entering a contest below! </p>

<p><b>This contest has closed!</b></p>
