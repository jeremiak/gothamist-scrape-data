title: Man Arrested For Last Month's Anti-Gay Attack In Chelsea
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2013_09_quinagat.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509194108im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_09_quinagat.jpg" width="640" height="403"> <br> <i> City Council Speaker with victims Peter Notman and Michael Felenchak (Via NBC New York)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Last month, a gay couple holding hands leaving the Chelsea Cinemas <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509194108/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/14/a_man_was_attacked_in.php">was attacked</a>, with a group of men kicking and punching them <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509194108/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/16/police_search_for_suspects_in_anti-.php">while yelling things like</a>, &quot;What are you looking at f--?&quot; Now police announced they arrested one suspect in connection with the attack.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2013_09_agatta.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509194108im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_09_agatta.jpg" width="325" height="235"> <br> <i style=" width:325px; ;display:block"> The victims&apos; injuries</i></div> Alfonso Lanier, 23, a Manhattan resident, was charged with aggravated harassment as a hate crime and assault as a hate crime for the August 14 incident on 25th Street and 9th Avenue. <p></p>

<p>Victim Peter Notman <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509194108/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/08/14/nypd-two-gay-men-attacked-outside-theater-in-chelsea/">described to WCBS 2</a>, &quot;It was six of them against the two of us, typical of the cowards they are... I was hit with brass knuckles down the side of my face, and I had contusions; had to have an MRI, and Michael received several stitches in his mouth where they punched us.&quot;</p>

<p>The police are still looking for another suspect.</p>
