</div></div>					<p>It&apos;s April 20th, and while the Internet offers <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170421022317/http://www.cnn.com/2017/04/20/health/420-origin-trnd/">various explanations</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170421022317/http://www.cnbc.com/2017/04/20/the-origins-of-420-marijuanas-high-holiday.html">on why 4/20 Day</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170421022317/http://www.cnbc.com/2017/04/20/the-origins-of-420-marijuanas-high-holiday.html">is 4/20 Day</a>, let&apos;s look at how the NYPD is (possibly) marking the day: With a bust of an illegal grow house in the Bronx.</p>

<p>The 49th Precinct Tweeted these images of the bust&#x2014;involving 50 pounds of marijuana&#x2014;yesterday, insensitively bragging about the investigation on 4/20 Eve. They even added a Dad Joke hashtag, #notoregano.</p>
