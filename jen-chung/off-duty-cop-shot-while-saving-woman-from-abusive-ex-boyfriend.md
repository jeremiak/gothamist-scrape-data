										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2013_06_pokoch.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504123007im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_06_pokoch.jpg" width="240" height="280"> <br> <i style=" width:240px; ;display:block"> Police Officer Joseph Koch</i></div> Last night at 10 p.m., an off-duty police officer who was shot in the hand when he struggled with a domestic violence suspect. It turns out that Police Officer Joseph Koch was at a Father&apos;s Day barbecue in Jamaica, Queens, when he heard a woman <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504123007/http://www.nytimes.com/2013/06/17/nyregion/2-are-wounded-including-an-off-duty-officer-in-a-shooting-in-queens.html?partner=rss&amp;emc=rss&amp;_r=1&amp;">screaming &quot;Don&apos;t kill me&quot;</a> and then, as he approached the house, saw a boy run out and say his mother was being attacked by an ex-boyfriend.<p></p>

<p>Koch went inside the house with his firearm drawn. The NYPD says that Joseph Bernazard, 40, attacked him and tried to grab Koch&apos;s gun. During the struggle, three shots were fired&#x2014;Koch was ht in the hand while Bernazard was struck in the stomach. Both were taken to Jamaica Hospital and are in stable condition.</p>

<p>The victim of the assault had an order of protection against Bernazard because he <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504123007/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/queens/off-duty-nypd-shot-checking-father-day-fight-article-1.1374515?localLinksEnabled=false">previously tried to strangle her</a>. </p>

<p>According to the NY Times, &quot;Moments before the shooting, he had been outside the home, banging on the front door and demanding to be let in; he eventually climbed through a window and began beating the woman.&quot; She had facial bruising and a 7-inch laceration on her head that required numerous stitches to close. <br>
