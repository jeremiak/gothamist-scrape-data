<p>The man suspected of carjacking a man at gunpoint in Staten Island was arrested in the borough&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150410012153/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/police-arrest-man-run-cops-article-1.1860149">while trying to carjack another person</a>. </p>

<p>Tyrell Brown, 25, was caught while wearing a wig last night, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150410012153/http://gothamist.com/2014/07/08/12000_reward_for_gunpoint_carjackin.php">ending a two-day manhunt</a>. He allegedly stole the carjacked Hummer on Sunday and ended up in a shootout with MTA police at the Verrazano Narrows Bridge early Monday. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150410012153/http://nypost.com/2014/07/09/carjacker-high-on-molly-nabbed-in-staten-island/">According to the Post&apos;s anonymous source</a>, &quot;His passenger was arrested at the scene and told cops Brown was high on Molly.&quot; Brown later ditched the car and the police offered a $12,000 reward.</p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150410012153/http://www.silive.com/news/index.ssf/2014/07/on_staten_islands_north_shore_1.html">Staten Island Advance says</a> that Brown will be questioned for two murders on Sunday, &quot;both of which took place within minutes of each other.&quot; </p>
