<p>Police are searching for an SUV driver who killed a 62-year-old Bronx woman early Sunday. It was the fourth time a driver killed a pedestrian or cyclist this weekend.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, officers responded to a 911 call about a pedestrian who was hit by a driver at 1:19 a.m. on Sunday, June 12th. At the intersection of East Gun Hill Road and White Plains Road, police officers found Sharon Smith, 62, &quot;lying on the roadway with severe trauma about the body.&quot; </p>

<p>Smith was pronounced deceased at Montefiore Hospital. The NYPD Highway Unit&apos;s Collision Investigation Squad found that Smith had been trying to cross East Gun Hill Road when she was struck by the driver of a dark colored SUV going east on East Gun Hill Road. The driver never stopped.</p>
