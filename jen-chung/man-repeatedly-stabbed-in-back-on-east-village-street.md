<p>Police are looking for two suspects who allegedly assaulted a man on a Manhattan street over the weekend.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 4:40 a.m. on Saturday, December 5th, a 49-year-old man was standing in front of 311 East 9th Street when &quot;the suspects approached him and stabbed him several times in the back.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was treated at Bellevue Hospital and released. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160404152408/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Two-Men-Stab-Man-Standing-East-Village-Street-New-York-City-361339791.html">It&apos;s unclear</a> if he knew the attackers. </p>
