	<p>A truck caught fire at the entrance of the Queensboro Bridge on the Manhattan side, causing traffic delays. According to the FDNY, it was a &quot;standard engine block fire&quot; but the truck was carrying oxygen tanks, and one of them exploded, which startled those nearby (one Reddit user said <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130512025418/http://www.reddit.com/r/nyc/comments/1bvp9b/huge_bang_near_queensboro_bridge/">it sounded like lightning</a> while another said it was a &quot;huge bang&quot;).</p>

<p>There was thick black smoke as well:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130512025418if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/qcyLWMvVYHA" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The FDNY received the call about the fire at 6:57 p.m. and it was under control by 8:05 p.m. There were no injuries.</p>
