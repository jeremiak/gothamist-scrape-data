<p>The older brother of singer Nicki Minaj has been accused of raping a child on Long Island. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427034846/http://www.newsday.com/long-island/jelani-maraj-brother-of-pop-star-nicki-minaj-charged-with-rape-source-says-1.11191338">Newsday reports</a>, &quot;Jelani Maraj, 37, of Baldwin, was arraigned Thursday on first-degree rape and first-degree course of sexual conduct against a child, according to online court and jail records. The child is 12, according to the source.&quot;</p>

<p>He was released on $100,000 bail. Newsday says the Nassau County DA&apos;s office &quot;declined to comment on the case, including the circumstances of the case, the age of the child and how the matter came to authorities&#x2019; attention.&quot;</p>

<p>Maraj recently got married. Minaj <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427034846/http://pagesix.com/2015/08/23/nicki-minaj-happily-paid-for-her-brothers-hamptons-wedding/">paid for the $30,000 August wedding</a> at the Coral House in Baldwin, NY.</p>
