<p>Yesterday, a teenager was killed on Fulton Street in Brooklyn&apos;s Bedford-Stuyvesant neighborhood. Now police have released video of suspects fleeing the scene. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the police were called to the scene at 4:05 a.m., finding Kamau Chandler with two gunshot wounds in his head and one in his chest in front of 1198 Fulton Street. He was pronounced dead at Interfaith Hospital.</p>

<p>Police say that the victim and his friends were walking down the street &quot;when they were approached from behind by the two suspects.&quot; They started arguing, and then one suspect shot Chandler.</p>

<p>Police describe the shooter as being in his 20s, thin with short hair and wearing an orange shirt with a dark stripe on it. The second suspect is described as being about 6&apos;2&quot; and wearing a navy blue cutoff hoodie. Here&apos;s video of the pair:</p>
