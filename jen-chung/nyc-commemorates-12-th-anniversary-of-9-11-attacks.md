<p>Today is the 12th anniversary of the September 11 attacks at the World Trade Center. The city is holding the remembrance ceremony this morning at the National September 11 Memorial plaza at the World Trade Center. There are six moments of silence&#x2014;two for when the planes hit the towers, two for when each tower fell, one for the attack on the Pentagon and one for Flight 93 in Shanksville, PA. The names of all victims from the WTC, Pentagon and Flight 93 attacks will be read, as well the 1993 WTC attack victims, will be read by their relatives. Here&apos;s the city&apos;s program:</p>

<blockquote><strong>8:39 AM</strong>         Program begins<br>
Bagpipers and drummers (from the NYPD, FDNY and PAPD) lead WTC flag through memorial to the stage<br>
Brooklyn Youth Chorus performs the National Anthem<br>
<strong>8:46 AM </strong>        Moment of silence (observance of time AA Flight 11 struck North Tower)
(Houses of worship will toll their bells throughout the City)<br>
Family members will begin reading the names in pairs<br>
&#xB7;         There will be 125 pairs (250 readers) who will continue until all names are read<br>
&#xB7;         Music will provide a backdrop throughout the program<br>
<strong>9:03 AM </strong>        Moment of silence (observance of time UA Flight 175 struck South Tower)<br>
Reading of names continues/music resumes<br>
<strong>9:37 AM</strong>         Moment of silence (observance of time AA Flight 77 struck the Pentagon)<br>
Reading of names continues/music resumes<br>
<strong>9:59 AM   </strong>      Moment of silence (observance of time of fall of the South Tower)<br>
Reading of names continues/music resumes<br>
<strong>10:03 AM</strong>       Moment of silence (observance of time UA Flight 93 crashed near Shanksville, Pennsylvania)
Reading of names continues/music resumes<br>
<strong>10:28 AM    </strong>   Moment of silence (observance of time of fall of the North Tower)<br>
Reading of names continues to conclusion/music resumes<br>
Brooklyn Youth Chorus performs &#x201C;You Raise Me Up&#x201D;<br>
                        Taps performed by three trumpeters (NYPD, FDNY, PAPD)<br>
<strong>12:30 PM</strong>        Program concludes<br>
<strong>8:00 PM</strong>          National September 11 Memorial Closes</blockquote>There are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130914054434/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/pr/pr_2013_09_10_traffic_september_11_memorial_ceremony.shtml">numerous street closures due to the ceremony</a>. Also, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130914054434/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/28/tribute_in_light_coming_back_for_91.php">Tribute in Light will return at sunset today</a> and fade away when the sun rises tomorrow.
