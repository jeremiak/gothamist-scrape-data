<p>The NYPD is asking for the public&apos;s help in identifying a suspect wanted for robbery in the 5th Precinct. </p>

<p>According to investigators, last Sunday, July 21st, at 10:45 a.m., an 85-year-old woman was in her Chinatown apartment building&apos;s elevator with the suspect. He got off the elevator before she did, but when she entered her apartment, he shoved her to the floor and &quot;removed an undetermined amount of money form her as well as her residence and fled the location.&quot; The victim refused medical attention.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as a man in his 20s, 5&apos;7&quot; tall weighing 140 pounds. He was wearing a white t-shirt with lettering on it. The police also released a photo of him.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this robbery is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928040338/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
