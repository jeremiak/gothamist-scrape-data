<p>The police are hoping the public can help them find a man who opened fire in Bedford-Stuyvesant early yesterday morning. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 1:10 a.m. on Sunday, July 6, a 25-year-old man was sitting on the stoop of 184 MacDonough Street when another man opened fire, hitting the victim multiple times. Here&apos;s surveillance footage:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150425175303if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/RSHPT7dWoCM" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The footage appears to show the gunman and victim talking before the gunman removes his gun from his waistband. The victim was taken to a hospital in critical but stable condition.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as a man with a stocky build, wearing a t-shirt with the number 12 on the back. Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS.  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425175303/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/"> WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
