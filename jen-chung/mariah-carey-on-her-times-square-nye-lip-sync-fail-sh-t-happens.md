					<p>Before New Year&apos;s Eve, the producers of the <em>Dick Clark&apos;s Rockin&apos; New Year&apos;s Eve with Ryan Seacrest</em> were <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/http://pagesix.com/2016/12/30/special-team-tasked-with-getting-mariah-to-nye-performance-on-time/">just worried</a> about Mariah Carey getting to the Times Square performance stage before midnight. Little did they know that they&apos;d have to worry about...disastrous lip-syncing.</p>

<div><blockquote class="twitter-video" data-lang="en"><p lang="en" dir="ltr">Mariah Carey&apos;s Times Square meltdown was an appropriate finale to 2016 <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/https://t.co/rjiKuQVmF9">https://t.co/rjiKuQVmF9</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/https://t.co/BiB83fEzhr">pic.twitter.com/BiB83fEzhr</a></p>&#x2014; Deadspin (@Deadspin) <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/https://twitter.com/Deadspin/status/815427057731719168">January 1, 2017</a></blockquote>
<script async src="//web.archive.org/web/20170518010145js_/http://platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" charset="utf-8"></script></div>

<p>Carey had been singing &quot;We Belong Together,&quot; but the problems started before that. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/http://www.nytimes.com/2017/01/01/arts/music/mariah-carey-new-years-eve-times-square.html?_r=1">The NY Times reports</a>, &quot;Mariah Carey suffered through a performance train wreck in Times Square on New Year&#x2019;s Eve as technical malfunctions left her at a loss vocally during her hit song &apos;Emotions,&apos; struggling to reach notes and to sync the lyrics and music.&quot; </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/http://nypost.com/2017/01/01/mariah-carey-closes-2016-with-disastrous-nye-performance/?_ga=1.105926917.729017374.1480337807">She said</a>, &quot;Well, Happy New Year. We can&#x2019;t hear, but I&#x2019;ll just go through the motions,&quot; and &quot;I&#x2019;m trying to be a good sport here.&quot;</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/UkTy_vSPkWc" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The Times called it &quot;a rare meltdown on national television by one of the top-selling recording artists of all time.&quot; Carey was the headliner for the show&#x2014;a Page Six source <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/http://pagesix.com/2016/12/30/special-team-tasked-with-getting-mariah-to-nye-performance-on-time/">had earlier revealed</a>, &quot;when Dick Clark producers called to lock in her arrival time, Mariah&#x2019;s team actually asked for clarification, &#x2018;What time exactly does the ball drop?&apos;&quot;</p>

<p>In 2014, Carey had a disastrous performance at the Rockefeller Center Christmas Tree lighting ceremony, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/http://theconcourse.deadspin.com/isolated-unedited-vocals-of-mariah-careys-awful-holida-1666529981">singing live (and not hitting high notes</a>). Apparently Carey was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/gossip/la-et-mg-mariah-carey-bombs-rockefeller-center-all-i-want-for-christmas-is-you-20141204-story.html">unable to pre-tape her vocals</a>; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170518010145/http://www.latimes.com/entertainment/gossip/la-et-mg-mariah-carey-bombs-rockefeller-center-all-i-want-for-christmas-is-you-20141204-story.html">she apologized</a>, &quot;Last night&apos;s situation was beyond my control. I apologize to all that showed up, you know that I would never want to disappoint you.&quot;</p>
