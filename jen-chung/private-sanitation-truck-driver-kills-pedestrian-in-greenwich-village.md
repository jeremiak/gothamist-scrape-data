<p>A woman crossing a Greenwich Village street was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170517164452/https://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20170517/greenwich-village/woman-dies-garbage-truck-greenwich-avenue-nypd">killed by a private sanitation truck driver</a> late Tuesday night.</p>

<p>Police say officers responded to a 911 call at around 11:40 p.m. regarding a person who was struck at Greenwich Avenue and West 8th Street. There they found a 60-year-old woman unconscious and unresponsive with severe body trauma. EMS took her to Lenox NY Healthplex, where she was pronounced dead.</p>

<p>According to a preliminary investigation, police say the unnamed woman was crossing West 8th Street from north to south when the truck driver, who was traveling east, struck her. </p>
