<p>Police are investigating a fatal stabbing that took place early this morning on a Manhattan sidewalk. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, police officers responded to a 911 call about a 22-year-old man suffering from stab wounds at Metropolitan Hospital. The cops found that he &quot;sustained multiple stab wounds to the chest and determined that the incident occurred at the intersection of Third Avenue and East 94 Street.&quot;</p>

<p>Someone <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160923062817/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NY-Man-Critically-Injured-Got-in-Fight-Stabbed-Upper-East-Side-375358971.html">apparently helped the victim</a> get a cab to take him to the hospital, which is at First Avenue and 97th Street. The victim died at the hospital.</p>
