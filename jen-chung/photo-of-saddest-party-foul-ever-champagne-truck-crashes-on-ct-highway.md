<p>Usually, there are tears of joy at the euphoric sound of a champagne bottle popping open. But yesterday afternoon bubbly-lovers wept in despair when a tractor-trailer carrying many bottles of champagne <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429154958/http://www.wfsb.com/story/23467229/champagne-spills-over-i-395-in-griswold-highway-shutdown?utm_source=twitterfeed&amp;utm_medium=twitter">crashed, overturned and split open</a> on I-395 in Connecticut.</p>

<p>The incident occurred in Griswold at 2:30 p.m., and it seems the truck&apos;s driver  &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429154958/http://www.theday.com/article/20130918/NWS04/130919565/1047#.UjoIqjPoLTt.twitter">failed to move left for the construction pattern</a>&quot; and hit a Department of Transportation truck. Both drivers had <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429154958/http://www.nbcconnecticut.com/news/local/I-395-Closed-in-Griswold-224278781.html?_osource=SocialFlowTwt_CTBrand">minor injuries</a>.</p>

<p>An environmental contractor was called to the scene to clean up the champagne.  The Day also has an <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429154958/http://www.theday.com/article/20130918/NWS04/130919565/1047#.UjoIqjPoLTt.twitter">amazing photograph of the carnage</a>&#x2014;those orange boxes make us think this was Veuve Clicquot! </p>

<p>Pour one out for champagne tractor-trailer, which joins <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429154958/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/05/tanker_full_of_6000_gallons_of_scot.php">scotch whisky tanker</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429154958/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/12/tractor-trailer_with_beer_crashes_s.php">beer tractor-trailer</a> in that boozy wasted party hole in the sky.</p>
