                            <p>The West Indian American Day Parade is a magnet for politicians eager to show their allegiance to New Yorkers celebrating in Brooklyn on Labor Day, and this election year saw almost all the candidates marching the parade route. Of course Anthony Weiner was among them, and he happened to be the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128045644/https://twitter.com/andyjayhawk/status/374578637594705920">only candidate with his own float</a>.</p>

<p>The Daily News City Hall Bureau Chief Jennifer Fermino shot these videos of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128045644/http://www.nydailynews.com/blogs/dailypolitics/2013/09/mc-anthony-weiner-parades-west-indian-style-sorta-anybody-here-from-jamaica">Weiner adopting a Caribbean accent to yell</a>, &quot;Anybody here from Jamaica? Anybody here from Barbados? Anybody here from Guyana?... Anybody here from Staten Island?&quot;</p>
