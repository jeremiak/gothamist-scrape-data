<p>The police are looking for an apparent arsonist who struck on Thanksgiving in Queens.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 1:15 a.m. on Thursday, November 26th, an &quot;unknown suspect&quot; entered a front yard at 89th Avenue and 80th Street. and then ignited &quot;a fire to 40 religious Hindu flags before fleeing southbound on 80th Street.&quot;</p>

<p>No one was injured. Police released footage of the suspect, described as an adult male, wearing a a three quarter length jacket and baseball cap and pulling a medium size travel bag:<br>
