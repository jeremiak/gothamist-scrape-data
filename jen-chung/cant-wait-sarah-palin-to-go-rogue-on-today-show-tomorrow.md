<p>When Good Morning America announced that Katie Couric would &quot;sub&quot; in for GMA anchor Robin Roberts this week, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120405012211/http://mediadecoder.blogs.nytimes.com/2012/04/01/booking-battle-at-the-morning-shows/">Today Show went into overdrive to respond</a>.  The reigning morning show teased that there would be a Today Show legend today (it turned out to be... Meredith Vieira) AND that Sarah Palin would co-host tomorrow. </p>

<p> This morning, Matt Lauer, Ann Curry, Al Roker and the whole Today Show gang chatted with Palin about the gig&#x2014;and Lauer poked fun at Palin&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120405012211/http://www.cbsnews.com/video/watch/?id=4561826n&amp;tag=contentMain;contentBody">infamous interview with Couric</a>, &quot;Are you reading some newspapers?&quot;  Oh, no he didn&apos;t!</p>

<p>Vieira told Palin, &quot;Matt will deface your property, so don&apos;t bring anything of value. Ann hits the limoncello on a pretty regular basis, and if Al asks to let him &#x2018;show you his neck of the woods,&#x2019; run.&quot;  Of course, Palin<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120405012211/http://www.breitbart.com/Big-Journalism/2012/03/31/sarah-to-katie-game-on"> had told Breitbart News</a>, &quot;I see this as a good opportunity to bring an independent, common-sense conservative perspective to NBC.
