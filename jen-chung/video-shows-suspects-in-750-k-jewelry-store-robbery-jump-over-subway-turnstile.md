<p>The suspects in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170708235024/http://gothamist.com/2017/05/26/brooklyn_heights_jewel_heist.php">violent Brooklyn Heights jewelry store robbery </a>were caught on video as they were making their getaway, police say. </p>

<p>Around 5:20 p.m. on May 25, authorities say that three males entered a jewelry store at 60 Court Street and assaulted the 67-year-old owner. They were allegedly armed with a firearm and took $500,000 in jewelry and $250,000 in cash. </p>

<p>The three suspects were allegedly wearing construction outfits, while a fourth male was standing outside the store as a lookout and wearing a Tyvek paint suit and holding sign that read &quot;Danger Asbestos&#x2014;Men Working.&quot;</p>

<p>The owner suffered a laceration to his head and was taken to a hospital.<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170708235024/http://gothamist.com/2017/05/26/brooklyn_heights_jewel_heist.php"> A witness said</a>, &quot;He had his head bashed open. He was like running around screaming: &#x2018;Help me, help me.&#x2019; A lot of people were calling the cops&#x2014;it was really crazy.&quot;</p>
