<p>The Official Campaign Finance Board Mayoral Debate for the Democratic candidates is on Wednesday, August 21&#x2014;and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130827053958/http://www.google.com/moderator/#16/e=208860">you can submit questions</a>!</p>

<p>Gothamist and our partners, NY1 News, NY1 Noticias, WNYC, Citizens Committee for New York City, Citizens Union, Hispanic Federation, and Transportation Alternatives, are the debate, and questions from the public have been used during the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130827053958/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/13/spitzer_and_stringer_continue_attac.php">Democratic Comptroller</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130827053958/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/16/public_advocate_debate.php">Democratic Public Advocate</a> debates. </p>

<p>You&apos;ll have until noon on Wednesday, August 21 to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130827053958/http://www.google.com/moderator/#16/e=208860">submit questions</a> and then we&apos;ll send them onto the moderators. You will be able to watch/listen to the debate in different ways: Via TV on NY1/NY1 Noticias; via radio on WNYC; and via online on Gothamist. Or head to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130827053958/http://www.nyccfb.info/debates/schedule-2013.html">one of the CFB&apos;s watch parties</a> (see the column at far right). We&apos;ll also be liveblogging!</p>
