Charles Rangel to his constituents in Harlem: A &quot;glossy, full-color&quot; flyer with tax season tips, which has Rangel saying, &quot;Hopefully, the information in this guide can help put money back into your pockets,&quot; while the flyer itself adds, &quot;This mailing was prepared, published and mailed at taxpayer expense.&quot; Rangel, of course, has been notoriously <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110629193710/http://gothamist.com/2009/08/29/really_rangel_more_questions_about.php">terrible with filing taxes</a> on his various investments or home sales, even though he chaired the House Ways and Means Committee, which writes the tax code (he recently <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110629193710/http://gothamist.com/2010/02/26/ethics_panel_rangel_broke_the_rules.php">stepped down</a>).  One resident reflected, &quot;<strong>Well, I think if anyone is going to tell you how to pay - or not pay - taxes, he&apos;s your guy.</strong>&quot;</p>
