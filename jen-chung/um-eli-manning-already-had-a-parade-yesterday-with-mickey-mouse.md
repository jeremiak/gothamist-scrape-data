</div></div>					<p>While most of the Giants <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151228102750/http://www.nj.com/giants/index.ssf/2012/02/super_bowl_champion_giants_wel.html">arrived at Newark International Airport</a> yesterday afternoon, there was one notable member of the squad missing: Super Bowl XLVI MVP Eli Manning.  It seems that the quarterback was cheating on his team with... Disney characters.</p>

<p>Manning made good on his post-victory <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151228102750/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FH2oXFXj8BI">promise to go to Disney World</a> and was at the Orlando theme park for a parade done the Magic Kingdom&apos;s Main Street, U.S.A.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151228102750/http://disneyparks.disney.go.com/blog/2012/02/walt-disney-world-resort-parade-celebrates-super-bowl-mvp-eli-manning/">According to Disney&apos;s blog</a>, &quot;Afterward he played around with Mickey and Minnie and some NFL Youth players.&quot; And you can see video of the festivities&#x2014;it&apos;s definitely tamer than <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151228102750/http://gothamist.com/2012/02/06/videos_giants_fans_celebrate_in_bay.php#photo-1">what was going on in Bay Ridge</a> on Sunday night:</p>
