<p>Tonight, the Empire State Building will light up in the colors of the NCAA men&apos;s basketball finals teams&#x2014;red and white for the University of Louisville and maize and blue for the University of Michigan. And when the game is over and a winner has been crowned, the Empire State Building will &quot;celebrate by sparkling the team&#x2019;s colors for 30 minutes followed by lighting up the New York City skyline in their static colors until sunrise.&quot;</p>

<p>The Empire State Building also did this <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503145518/http://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=642140805803094&amp;set=pb.153817204635459.-2207520000.1365459107&amp;type=3&amp;theater">for the Final Four</a>.</p>
