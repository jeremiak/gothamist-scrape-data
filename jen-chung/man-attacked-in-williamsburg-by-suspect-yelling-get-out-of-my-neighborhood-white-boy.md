title: Man Attacked In Williamsburg By Suspect Yelling "Get Out Of My Neighborhood White Boy"
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_11_biasassault.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170327110418im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_11_biasassault.jpg" width="640" height="506" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police say a man was punched on a Williamsburg street corner by a man allegedly yelling &quot;anti-white statements,&quot; which reportedly included, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170327110418/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/cops-hunt-suspect-punched-man-brooklyn-bias-attack-article-1.2455040">&quot;Get out of my neighborhood white boy.&quot;</a></p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 6:20 a.m. on Wednesday, December 2nd: The victim, a 42-year-old white male, &quot;was standing at the corner of Berry Street and Division Avenue when the suspect walked up to him and punched him in the back of the head while yelling anti-white statements. The victim fell to the ground and the suspect fled the location, running into the Roberto Clemente Housing Development across the street from the location.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170327110418/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/cops-hunt-suspect-punched-man-brooklyn-bias-attack-article-1.2455040">The Daily News reports</a> that the attacker said, &quot;Get out of my neighborhood white boy. Get out of my neighborhood, cracker.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim refused medical attention. Police released surveillance footage of the suspect, and the NYPD describes him as &quot;a Hispanic male, 5&apos;11&quot; tall, weighing 190 pounds.&quot;</p>
