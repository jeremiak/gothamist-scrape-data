<p>Last night, an 18-year-old man was killed inside a Domino&apos;s Pizza in East New York. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, cops responded to a 911 call at 2196 Pitkin Avenue at 9:45 p.m. and found Dashawn Cameron stabbed in the torso. Cameron was pronounced dead at Brookdale Hospital. Police say &quot;the victim was inside the location when several male blacks walked into the location and began to assault him with one of the suspects stabbing the victim. The suspects then fled the location.&quot;  </p>

<p>The investigation is ongoing. </p>
