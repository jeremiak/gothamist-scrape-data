<p>Police are looking for the suspect who raped a woman in her Bronx apartment building&apos;s stairwell last month.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred around 9:44 p.m. on January 5th. The victim, whom the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312135513/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/bronx/cops-hunt-teen-raped-woman-56-bronx-article-1.2522741">Daily News says</a> is 56-years-old, entered her building on East 183rd Street in the University Heights neighborhood &quot;when an unidentified male followed her into the elevator. As the elevator door opened the male grabbed her from behind and began to assault her. The unidentified male then removed cash and two cellphones from the victim and then raped her in the stairwell.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect fled and the victim was taken to a local hospital for treatment. </p>

<p>Police described the suspect as being about 18-years-old and 5&apos;8&quot;; he was last seen wearing a grey hooded sweatshirt. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312135513/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers website</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then entering TIP577.</p>
