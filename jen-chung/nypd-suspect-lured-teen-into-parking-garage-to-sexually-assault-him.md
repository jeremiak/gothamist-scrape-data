title: NYPD: Suspect Lured Teen Into Parking Garage To Sexually Assault Him
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_09_suspectatt6pct.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170902141600im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_09_suspectatt6pct.jpg" width="640" height="373" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The police are looking for a person who allegedly tried to sexually assault a 17-year-old last week in Greenwich Village.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 4:20 p.m. on Friday, September 12, when the male suspect &quot;did lure a 17-year-old male to the lower level of the Hilary Gardens Garage located at 741 Broadway. The suspect then attempted to sexually assault the victim.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim, who was not injured, ran away while the suspect fled. The police released video of the suspect fleeing:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20170902141600if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/so9SiQXSK4Q?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspect is described as a male, approximately 20 years of age, 5&apos;6&quot; in height, 150lbs with a medium complexion.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170902141600/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
