</div></div><p>The NYPD release surveillance video and stills of a man suspected of raping a 64-year-old woman in the Rockaways over the weekend.  </p>

<p>According to police, at around 5:30 a.m. on Sunday, June 10, a 64-year-old woman was walking near Rockaway Beach Boulevard and Beach 91 Street when the suspect approached her.  He forced her to the ground and raped her.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as a black male, around 5&apos;10&quot;, who was last seen wearing green pants, a dark colored tee shirt and a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120617121249/http://www.strictlyfitteds.com/blog/2012/01/new-fitteds-hat-club-custom-new-era-x-mlb-marlins-59fifty-fitted-cap/">Miami Marlins baseball hat.</a></p>
