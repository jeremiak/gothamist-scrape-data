<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160720060409/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/04/st_patricks_parade_nyc_2016.php">Last weekend</a> was just the warm-up to St. Patrick&apos;s Day&#x2014;here are more notable parades in the area, including the big one on March 17:<br>
<strong><br>
SUNDAY, MARCH 13:</strong></p>

<p><b><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160720060409/http://tnsaintpatricksdayparade.blogspot.com/">Throggs Neck St. Patrick&apos;s Day Parade</a></b><br>
<em>Bronx</em><br>
12 p.m. Parade begins at East Tremont and Lafayette Avenues (route continues down East Tremont and then takes a right onto Harding Avenue and concludes at Harding and Brinsmade Avenues)</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_02_stpatshat.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160720060409im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_02_stpatshat.jpg" width="640" height="438"> <br> <i> istockphoto</i></div> <p></p>

<p><strong>THURSDAY, MARCH 17 </strong></p>

<p><b><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160720060409/https://www.nycstpatricksparade.org/">New York City St. Patrick&apos;s Day Parade</a></b><br>
<em>Midtown Manhattan</em></p>

<p>Mayor Bill de Blasio <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160720060409/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/03/de_blasio_st_patricks_parade.php">will be joining the parade this year</a>, making him the first Democratic mayor in 20 years to march. The parade attracts 2.5 million spectators, who are advised &quot;Best views to those who show up early.&quot;</p>

<p>11 a.m. Parade start on Fifth Avenue at 44th Street (the parade goes up Fifth Avenue and ends at 79th Street) <br>
<strong><br>
SUNDAY, MARCH 20:</strong></p>

<p><b><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160720060409/http://www.brooklynstpatricksparade.com/parade.html">Brooklyn St. Patrick&apos;s Day Parade</a></b><br>
<em>Park Slope, Brooklyn</em><br>
1 p.m. Parade Step-off at Prospect Park West and 15th Street (the route goes down 15th Street to 7th Avenue; then along 7th Avenue to Garfield; up Garfield to Prospect Park West and along PPW to 15th Street)</p>

<p><b><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160720060409/http://www.bayridgestpatricksday.com/">Bay Ridge St. Patrick&apos;s Day Parade</a></b><br>
<em>Bay Ridge, Brooklyn</em><br>
1 p.m. Parade kick off at Third Avenue and Marine Avenue (the route goes down to 67th Street)</p>
