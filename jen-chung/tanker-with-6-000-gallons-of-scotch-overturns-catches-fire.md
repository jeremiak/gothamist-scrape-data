<p>Of all the horrors: A tanker carrying 6,000 gallons of scotch whisky overturned in New Jersey&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140205194446/http://www.mycentraljersey.com/article/20130604/NJNEWS/306040021/Tanker-truck-full-Scotch-whisky-overturns-Woodbridge">and then the scotch caught fire</a>! </p>

<p>The incident occurred yesterday morning in Woodbridge, NJ. According to the authorities, the truck (owned by B-Line Trucking of Newark) was on King Georges Road. Then it &quot;was making a turn when it clipped a telephone pole, hit a parked Cadillac and ignited.&quot; </p>

<p>The driver suffered minor injuries. It&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140205194446/http://www.nj.com/middlesex/index.ssf/2013/06/tanker_truck_full_of_scotch_wh.html">unclear how much scotch whisky was lost</a> or what brand it was.</p>
