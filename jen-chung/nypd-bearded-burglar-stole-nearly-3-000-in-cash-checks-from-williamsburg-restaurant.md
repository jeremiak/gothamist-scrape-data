<p>Southside Williamsburg restaurant <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170419022612/http://gothamist.com/2015/04/26/pokito_williamsburg.php#photo-1">Pokito</a> was robbed last week and police are currently on the lookout for a bearded suspect.</p>

<p>The burglary took place around 4:21 a.m. on April 3rd, at 155 South 4th Street. According to the NYPD, the burglar got into Pokito &quot;through the front door and removed $980 cash and two checks worth $2000, before fleeing the scene.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect is described as approximately 6&apos;0&quot; and 200 pounds, with curly blonde hair and a long beard. He was last seen wearing a hooded sweatshirt, pants, a baseball cap and glasses.<br>
