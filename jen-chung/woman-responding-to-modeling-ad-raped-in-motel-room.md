<p>Police are searching for a suspect accused of raping a young woman in the Bronx last month.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on January 11th, 2017 around 3 p.m., when a 21-year-old responded to a modeling ad&#x2014;apparently posted on Craigslist&#x2014;at the Paradise Motor Inn at 2990 Boston Road. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307110630/http://nypost.com/2017/03/03/man-wanted-for-allegedly-raping-woman-after-luring-her-to-motel/">The Post reports</a>, &quot;The woman talked briefly with the suspect before he demanded she get on the bed. When the victim attempted to leave, he raised his fist, threatened to hit her and proceeded to rape the woman.&quot;</p>

<p>Police released surveillance footage of the suspect, who is described as being about 5&apos;10&quot;. <br>
