<p>A southbound 1 train became disabled when one of the train cars derailed just south of 125th Street in Manhattan. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130619033545/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=resources/traffic&amp;id=9120422">According to WABC 7</a>, two wheels are off the track.</p>

<p>Part of the train is still in the tunnel. There do not appear to be any injuries and passengers are being evacuated, but it&apos;s taking some time&#x2014;one passenger Tweeted that the rescue train broke down, so they are waiting for another one:</p>
