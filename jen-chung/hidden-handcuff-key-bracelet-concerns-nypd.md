<p>Besides <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160824145923/http://gothamist.com/2012/04/01/teachers_arrested_for_dwi_after_cop.php">vomiting teachers</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160824145923/http://gothamist.com/2012/04/04/video_cyclist_vietnam_vet_arrested.php">Vietnam vets who bicycle</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160824145923/http://gothamist.com/2012/03/09/nypd_kept_files_on_innocent_new_yor.php">Muslims</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160824145923/http://gothamist.com/tags/occupywallstreet">Occupy Wall Street</a>, here&apos;s another thing that the NYPD is worrying about: Jewelry.  More specifically, jewelry that may have handcuff keys hidden in them!</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160824145923/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/bracelet-handcuff-key-cops-watch-list-article-1.1055873">According to the Daily News</a>, &quot;Although the key hasn&#x2019;t yet been used to escape police custody in the city, a police source said, the key has been popular in other states, especially Virginia. An internal NYPD memo issued March 29 warned officers to remove all jewelry from prisoners.&quot; Police spokesman Paul Browne said, &quot;When we learn of something like this, we make it public among the ranks.&quot;</p>

<p>Besides the bracelet version, there are little ones that are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160824145923/http://www.trueswords.com/handcuff-lock-pick-disguised-zipper-pull-p-6524.html">hidden as zipper pulls</a> or <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160824145923/http://www.magnalight.com/showproduct.aspx?productid=514&amp;categoryid=95&amp;sename=concealable-backup-universal-handcuff-key-plastic-handcuff-key--4-pack">ones that can be attached to buttons</a>.  </p>
