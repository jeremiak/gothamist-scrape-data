</div></div>					<p>Day one of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150615020548/http://cfa-iams-cat-championship.org/">CFA-Iams Cat Championship</a> feature breed competitions, cats running through the feline agility course, and overall animal adorableness.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150615020548/http://gothamist.com/2008/10/15/kitty_calling_cat_championship_show.php">As promised</a>, this year&apos;s cat show had a bit of election year spirit thrown in, as spectators could vote for either a Republicat or a Democat.</p>

<p><img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150615020548im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2008_10_cfa18.jpg"></p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150615020548/http://www.cfa-iams-cat-championship.org/spectatorinfo.html">cat show runs through 5 p.m. today</a> at Madison Square Garden. There are also many wonderful cats to be adopted at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150615020548/http://cfa-iams-cat-championship.org/adoptacat.html">adoption event</a> there at MSG&apos;s terrace.  If you can&apos;t make it, there are many <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150615020548/http://www.AnimalAllianceNYC.org/events/adoption.htm">adoption events</a> in and around the city--and here are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150615020548/http://www.AnimalAllianceNYC.org/events/apohours.htm">some organizations</a>, via the Mayor&apos;s Alliance for NYC&apos;s Animals, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150615020548/http://www.AnimalAllianceNYC.org/events/apohours.htm">that have adoption hours at their facilities</a>.</p>
