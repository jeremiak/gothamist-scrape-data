<p>Police are looking for a man who robbed a woman last Friday night in Cobble Hill.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 11:30 p.m. on April 1st, inside a residential building at Hicks Street and Baltic Street. Police say the mugger &quot;followed a 29-year-old female walking into her building. As the female victim approached her apartment, the suspect came from behind, placed his hand over her mouth, and forcibly took the victim&apos;s black &apos;Head Porter&apos; hand bag containing a gold iPhone 6 and credit cards before fleeing the scene.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was not injured. Police shared video of the suspect, who is described as being bald and was last seen wearing black jeans, black shoes and a black long sleeve sweater.<br>
