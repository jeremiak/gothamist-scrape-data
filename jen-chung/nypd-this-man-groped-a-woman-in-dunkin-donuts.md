<p>The police are hoping the public can help them identify a suspect accused of molesting a woman at a Dunkin&apos; Donuts in Queens last week.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on Tuesday, May 17, 2016 at about 4:27 a.m.: The individual entered a store in the vicinity of Roosevelt Avenue and 74 Street (PIX11 reports it was a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161006133342/http://pix11.com/2016/05/23/man-sought-for-grabbing-womans-groin-inside-jackson-heights-store-nypd/">Dunkin&apos; Donuts</a>), and approached the 46 year-old female victim. The individual proceeded to grab the victim&apos;s groin area over her pants and fled. </p>

<p>Police say the suspect is wanted for forcible touching and describe him as  25-35 years old, 5&apos;6&quot;-5&apos;10&quot; and 160-180 pounds.  He was last seen wearing grey sweater, red tank top, brown jeans, black sneakers, a green camouflage baseball cap, and a red back pack.
