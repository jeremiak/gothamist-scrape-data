<p>The police released surveillance video of a man suspected of sexually abusing a female MTA bus operator last month.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on May 15 at 3:35 p.m. on a BX18 bus. The suspect allegedly started a conversation with the bus driver and then sexually assaulted her before fleeing the bus at Undercliff Avenue and 171st Street in the Bronx.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130618051928if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/VbOea9VpmZs" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe><br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as being his 30s, around 5&apos;7&quot; and 180 pounds with a scar under his left eye.</p>

<p>Anyone with information regarding this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130618051928/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
