										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_10_evattack1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012236im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_10_evattack1.jpg" width="640" height="392" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The police are looking for a suspect in a stabbing that took place early yesterday morning. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the attack occurred around 1:30 a.m. on Saturday, November 1st: &quot;[The] suspect became involved in a physical altercation with an unknown male in the rear of 141 East 13 Street. During the altercation, the victim, a 41-year-old male, started filming. The suspect then directed his attention towards the victim, pulled out a knife and stabbed the victim in the torso.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect fled and the victim was treated at Bellevue Hospital. The police released images of the suspect, showing him wearing a King Tut shirt. Anyone with information in regards to this assault is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012236/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
