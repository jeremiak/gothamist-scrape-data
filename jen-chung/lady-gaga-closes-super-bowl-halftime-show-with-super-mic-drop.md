					<p>Lady Gaga brought her signature drama to the Super Bowl LI halftime show from start to finish. She opened by singing from the roof of NRG Stadium with hundreds of drones behind and then jumped (thanks to wires) to the ground, and, after a medley of songs including a ballad and use of a keytar, she made an epic mic drop to exit the show.</p>
