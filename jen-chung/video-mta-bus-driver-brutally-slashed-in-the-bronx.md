<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_07_busattacker2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813082346im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_07_busattacker2.jpg" width="280" height="296" class="image-right"> </span>The police are looking for a woman who allegedly attacked an MTA bus driver in the Bronx yesterday afternoon. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, just before 4 p.m., cops responded to a 911 calls about an assault on a BX5 driver. The victim, a 52-year-old man, was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813082346/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/manhattan/mta_bus_driver_slashed_by_attacker_aFWtc8kSOAaDHGeuY1Iq2N">slashed in the forearm</a> after he discharged his passengers at Westchester Avenue and Southern Boulevard. The driver is in stable condition.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as a woman last seen wearing a grey hooded sweatshirt and grey sweatpants. This attack comes two days after a bus driver was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813082346/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/02/mta_bus_driver_punched_robbed_in_lo.php">punched and robbed</a> at the end of her route on the Lower East Side.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130813082346/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
