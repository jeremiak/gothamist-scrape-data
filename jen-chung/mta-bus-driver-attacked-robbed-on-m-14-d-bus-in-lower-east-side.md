title: MTA Bus Driver Attacked, Robbed On M14D Bus In Lower East Side
---


                            
                            
                            
                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_06_mtabusg.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807024112im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_06_mtabusg.jpg" width="640" height="469" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>An MTA bus driver was assaulted and robbed by a man while she was operating the bus on Delancey Street near the FDR Drive last night. </p>

<p>Police say they received a call about an on-duty bus driver who was robbed at around 8:35 p.m. The driver had let off passengers from the M14D bus when a man jumped onto the bus, punched her a number of times, and then took her purse.</p>

<p>The driver was taken to NY Downtown Hospital. The Transit Workers&apos; Union&apos;s safety director <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807024112/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/m14-bus-driver-punched-robbed-les-article-1.1387813?localLinksEnabled=false">described her as &quot;traumatized&quot;</a> and added that her face has many lacerations. The police are still investigating the robbery.</p>
