This is a lot more than, they&apos;re <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150404050319/http://www.observer.com/2010/politics/bloomberg-praises-uninos-jeb-bush">on the same side</a> when it comes to education.&quot; Bush is one of dozens of names being mentioned as a 2012 or 2016 contender, prompting even <em>more</em> speculation (one Republican consultant: &quot;I&apos;m not saying it&apos;s going to happen, but it would be a hell of a ticket&quot;).  <p></p>

<p>At last week&apos;s GOP convention, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150404050319/http://www.nydailynews.com/blogs/dailypolitics/2010/06/bloomberg-cheers-on-donovan-at.html">Bush boasted</a>, &quot;Mayor Bloomberg is one of the most interesting guys I&apos;ve ever met in my life,&quot; while Bloomberg said, &quot;If there&apos;s anyone I would want on my side waging all those important battles and helping provide the government Americans deserve, it is&quot; Bush.</p>
