                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left"> <img alt="2013_08_kalee.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928034615im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_08_kalee.jpg" width="200" height="297"> <br> <i style=" width:200px; ;display:block"> Kalee Warnick</i></div> A young female teacher was arrested earlier this week for allegedly having a sexual relationship with a 15-year-old student. <p></p>

<p>Kalee Warnick, an English teacher in the Wall Township, NJ school system, was charged with &quot;third-degree aggravated criminal sexual contact and second-degree having a sexual relationship with a minor she had care over,&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928034615/http://www.app.com/article/20130802/NJNEWS14/308020056/Wall-teacher-accused-sex-student-15">according to the Asbury Park Press</a>. </p>

<p>The alleged relationship was with a male student and was reported on July 14. Warnick is a high school teacher, but<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928034615/http://wall.patch.com/groups/schools/p/wall-woman-charged-with-having-sex-with-student"> Patch reports</a> she was &quot;one of four teachers approved for summer in-home instruction for a student at a rate of $52 an hour for 10 hours.&quot; She did work as a home instructor in June.</p>

<p>Warnick was released after posting $50,000 bail. There is a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928034615/https://twitter.com/search?q=%23freewarnick&amp;src=hash">#freewarnick hashtag</a>.</p>
