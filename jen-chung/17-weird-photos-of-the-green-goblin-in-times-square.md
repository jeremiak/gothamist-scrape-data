	<p>The Green Goblin, the villain from the Broadway musical <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130512024743/http://spidermanonbroadway.marvel.com/">Spider-Man: Turn Off The Dark</a> (also known as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130512024743/http://gothamist.com/2010/12/21/spider-man_adds_a_fourth_accident_t.php">Spider-Man: Final Destination</a>), was seen walking around Times Square yesterday. It was apparently a tie-in with St. Patrick&apos;s Day (<em>Green</em> Goblin&#x2014;get it? GET IT?) but he wasn&apos;t his usual evil self, since he was posing with everyone (and was accompanied by a bodyguard). Maybe this was all just part of an effort to let people know that Spider-Man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130512024743/http://gothamist.com/2013/03/16/williamsburg_hasidic_community_angr.php">isn&apos;t actually anti-Semitic</a>.</p>
