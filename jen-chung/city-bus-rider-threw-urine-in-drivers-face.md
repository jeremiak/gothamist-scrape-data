										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_10_bx12bus.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151228221745im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_10_bx12bus.jpg" width="640" height="409" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>A bus driver was doused with urine after getting into a fight with a passenger who had a child with him, police say.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on October 1, at 5:15 p.m.: A passenger boarded a BX12 bus at White Plains Road and Pelham Parkway, and then got into a verbal dispute with the bus operator. &quot;[U]pon arrival at Pelham Bay Station, he threw an object containing urine at the bus operator.&quot; </p>

<p>The suspect fled and the driver, 58, was treated for irritation to his face and eyes at Jacobi Hospital.</p>

<p>Police released video of the suspect, described as being about 20 years old, 5&apos;8&quot; and 180 pounds. He was last  seen wearing a grey sweatshirt, blue jeans and dark-color footwear&#x2014;and cops say he got on the bus with a five year-old boy. </p>
