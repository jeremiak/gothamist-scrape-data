<p>A fire broke out in a 35-story building on John Street earlier this morning.  The Fire Department tells us that they received a call just after 10 a.m. and it turned out that a tank exploded on the 17th floor.</p>

<p>The tank may <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140926020902/http://www.buffnetwork.com/content.php?263-FDNY-MANHATTAN-*A-H-HIGH-RISE-FIRE*-BOX-190-116-JOHN-ST-6-1-12">have been an acetylene tank</a> and there were three injuries. The fire was under control by 11 a.m.  </p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140926020902/http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20120601/financial-district/firefighters-battle-blaze-downtown-high-rise-building">fire occurred in an office</a>, but the building is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140926020902/http://ny.curbed.com/archives/2012/05/31/the_rentals_of_fidis_116_john_street_conversion_revealed.php">currently being converted into rentals</a>.</p>
