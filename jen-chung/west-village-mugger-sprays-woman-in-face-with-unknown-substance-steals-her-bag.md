										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_11_wvillrobbery.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012635im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_11_wvillrobbery.jpg" width="640" height="470" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are looking for a man who robbed a woman after spraying something into her face in Manhattan earlier this week.</p>

<p>The incident occurred around 2:15 a.m. on Tuesday, November 15th, near Horatio Street and Greenwich Street in the West Village. The NYPD says that the man approached a 33-year-old woman and &quot;sprayed an unknown substance in her face.&quot; Then he snatched her purse and fled towards Jane Street. </p>

<p>The victim was taken to Lenox Hill Hospital for treatment.</p>

<p>Authorities released surveillance video of the suspect, who is described as about 230 pounds and 5&apos;9&quot; tall, and was last seen wearing a black jacket and blue jeans, running down a street:</p>
