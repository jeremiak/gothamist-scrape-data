<p>The police are hoping the public can help them identify and locate three persons of interests for questions about a series of burglaries in Bronx apartments. </p>

<p>According to the police, the same three men were seen on video surveillance entering the apartment buildings on the dates of the burglaries: &quot;In each incidents unknown suspect(s) enter apartments by breaking the cylinder lock on the front doors before removing cash, jewelry and small electronic devices.  Each of the incidents were committed during the day time while the apartments were not occupied.&quot; Here&apos;s video from one of the incidents:</p>

<p>Here&apos;s where and when the burglaries occurred:</p>

2.            On Monday, August 18, 2014 inside of 92 Van Cortlandt Park West
3.            On Monday, August 18, 2014 inside of 3815 Putnam Avenue West
4.            On Monday, August 18, 2014 inside of 130 Van Cortlandt Avenue West
5.            On Wednesday, August 27, 2014 inside of 3980 Hillman Avenue
6.            On Wednesday, August 27, 2014 inside of 3980 Hillman Avenue
7.            On Wednesday, August 27, 2014 inside of 3985 Gouverneur Avenue</blockquote>

<p>Police say the three persons of interest are about 30-40 years old. Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013537/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">Crime Stoppers website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
