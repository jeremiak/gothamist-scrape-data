title: Residents Sue NYPD's Over Bunker Plans
---

	
										<p><img alt="2008_09_1pp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312043128im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2008_09_1pp.jpg" width="130" height="109" class="right">The AP reports that three residential co-ops are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312043128/http://wcbstv.com/local/manhattan.bunker.nypd.2.808960.html">suing the NYPD</a> over its plan to build a &quot;high-tech command bunker&quot; at 1 Police Plaza.  The residents believe that the bunker &quot;must undergo environmental and land use reviews,&quot; but the NYPD says no review is required, plus there&apos;s &quot;no change to the exterior or its existing footprint.&quot;  Another issue residents are concerned about it traffic that construction will bring--<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312043128/http://www.google.com/maps?f=q&amp;hl=en&amp;q=One+police+Plaza,+NYC&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;om=1">1 Police Plaza</a> is just north of the Brooklyn Bridge exits.  Chinatown has been hit hard by the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312043128/http://www.downtownexpress.com/de_232/chinatown.html">closure of Park Row after September 11</a>, and one resident adds that the NYPD &quot;should be moving critical functions away [from] densely populated&quot; areas.</p>
