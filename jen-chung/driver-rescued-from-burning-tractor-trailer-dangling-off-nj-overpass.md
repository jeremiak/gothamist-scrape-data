<p>NJ State Troopers rescued a truck driver whose tractor-trailer had dangled off a NJ Turnpike overpass and then fell into the Overpeck Creek this morning.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014315/http://7online.com/traffic/tractor-trailer-crashes-catches-fire-on-nj-turnpike-in-ridgefield-park/270386/">According to WABC 7</a>, &quot;The accident happened at about 5:35 a.m., just north of the Vince Lombardi service area, where the vehicle went out of control and crashed through a guardrail.&quot; The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014315/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/New-Jersey-I-95-Wreck-Ridgefield-Park-Creek-NYC-Traffic-271811791.html">tractor-trailer caught fire</a> as it dangled off the road. </p>

<p>Three troopers were able to rescue the driver. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014315/http://www.nj.com/bergen/index.ssf/2014/08/driver_rescued_from_truck_accident_in_ridgefield_park.html#incart_m-rpt-1">NJ.com reports</a>, &quot;The trailer, blackened by fire, leaned against the rails, and pieces of the cab could be seen in the water.&quot; A helicopter landed on the Turnpike and airlifted the driver, whose condition is known.</p>
