<p>Police are searching for a man who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110105137/http://gothamist.com/2016/08/29/groper_nypd_regards.php">molested a man on an A train last month</a> while also allegedly telling him, &quot;Give my regards to the NYPD, they know me well.&quot;</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 2:40 p.m. on Sunday, August 28th. The 36-year-old man, who was traveling with his family, was on an A train when he was &quot;approached by a male who grabbed the victim&apos;s buttocks&quot; as the train headed from 72nd Street to Columbus Circle. </p>

<p>The victim also said the groper told him, &quot;Yeah you know you like that. You know what I mean&#x2014;you have a nice soft ass. You know you like that.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim took a photograph of his assailant before he left the train. </p>
