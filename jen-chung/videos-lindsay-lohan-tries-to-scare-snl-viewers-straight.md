</div></div><p>Lindsay Lohan made her <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120304201559/http://gothamist.com/2012/02/29/lets_play_whats_up_with_linsday_loh.php">ballyhooed return</a> to Saturday Night Live, and while it wasn&apos;t a great episode, but it wasn&apos;t <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120304201559/http://www.mediaite.com/tv/january-jones-snl-which-camera/">January Jones-bad</a>.  Lohan did make light of her legal problems during the monologue (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120304201559/http://gothamist.com/2012/03/03/lindsay_lohan_will_reportedly_sing.php">but didn&apos;t sing</a>)&#x2014;Kenan Thompson shined a flashlight in her eyes while Kristen Wiig frisked her: Lohan asked if she was being searched and Wiig replied, &#x201C;No, I&#x2019;m a lesbian now,&quot; to which Lohan joked, &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120304201559/http://gothamist.com/2008/09/12/lindsay_lohan_samantha_ronson_4ever.php">Been there, done that</a>.&quot; Then, Jimmy Fallon came by and revealed that Jon Hamm was ready&#x2014;and in the audience&#x2014;to take over as host if things went downhill. </p>

<p>Lohan&apos;s legal woes were also on display during a &quot;Scared Straight&quot; sketch where she played herself.  The other highlight was hilarious spoof on The Real Housewives franchise with The Real Housewives of Disney (Jasmine and Aladdin are broke! Cinderella is a bitch! Belle is a terrible singer!).  But let&apos;s face it: We were all transfixed by how weird Lohan looks now.  A plastic surgeon, who has not treated Lohan, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120304201559/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/national/charting_the_lo_TvkEPx7OVJamNYf81AxPvM">told the Post</a>, &quot;The lips are overdone... [Her face] looks like a dead fish. It doesn&#x2019;t look appetizing at all... She has vertical lines of the upper lip, and that&#x2019;s typical of a heavy smoker.&quot;</p>

<p>Other sketches include: A cold opening feature Mitt and Ann Romney and their sons; a sketch about an old school teen girl gang movie with actresses with names like Angie Biggums; the 2012 Psychic Awards; &quot;Snooki&quot; on Weekend Update, with a cameo from Jon Hamm; and a Digital Short about afros.  And Jack White was the musical guest.</p>
