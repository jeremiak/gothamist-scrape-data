					<p>Yes, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015026/http://gothamist.com/2017/01/05/snow_warning_nyc.php">it did snow</a>. No, it doesn&apos;t seem like it&apos;s enough to build a snowman because it&apos;s essentially melted on streets and walkways. However, it is cold enough to make those roads and sidewalks super icy&#x2014;it&apos;s like a skating rink out there in places, so <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015026/http://gothamist.com/2015/02/03/toe_pick_bro.php">remember to walk like a penguin</a>. </p>
