<p>The police are hoping that the public can help them find suspects who apparently broke into the Bowlerland bowling alley in the Bronx&#x2026; through the ceiling.</p>

<p>According to police, on Friday, April 4, at 4:50 a.m., a paid of men cut a hole in the roof of 2417 Hollers Avenue&#x2014;the home of Bowlerland&#x2014;and entered the establishment. They took an undetermined amount of money from six cash registers and then fled.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_03_bowlerland2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140407222909im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_03_bowlerland2.jpg" width="640" height="309"> <br> <i> DCPI</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Police also released images of the suspects: Both are in 30-40 years old; one is wearing a shirt with the name &quot;Taylor&quot; on it while the other is wearing a shirt with the words &quot;Wild Thing&quot; on it.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this burglary is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
