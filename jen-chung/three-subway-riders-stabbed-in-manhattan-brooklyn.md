<p>Police are investigating two separate early-morning subway stabbings.</p>

<p>The first occured in Brooklyn, by the Prospect Park subway station. At around 2:30 a.m., a 50-year-old man was on a southbound Q train when two people approached him and demanded his money, He refused and was stabbed during an altercation. The suspects fled and victim was taken to a hospital. The investigation is ongoing.</p>

<p>A little later, at the 116th Street and Lexington Avenue subway station, two men were stabbed. The police are still investigating. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150424233222/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/05/27/police-say-3-hurt-in-separate-subway-stabbing-incidents/">WCBS 2 reports</a> that the victims were taken to a hospital and are expected to survive.</p>
