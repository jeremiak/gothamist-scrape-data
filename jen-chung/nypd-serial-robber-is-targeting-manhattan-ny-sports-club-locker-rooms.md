<p>The police are looking for a robbery suspect who has been stealing credit cards, headphones, clothing, even a laptop and more from locked and unlocked lockers at New York Sports Clubs in Midtown Manhattan, Soho, Gramercy Park, Union Square, and Chelsea.</p>

<p>The NYPD listed the 15 reported incidents, including what was taken:</p>

<blockquote>Incident #1(MTN): On Friday, September 23, 2016, at approximately 1730 hours, the individual removed a 27-year-old male victim&apos;s backpack from a locker(unlocked) containing shoes, headphones, credit cards and clothing at The New York Sports Club located at 1221 Avenue of the Americas. (Photo #3)<br><br>
 
Incident #2(13 Pct): On Thursday, October 13, 2016, at approximately 1600 hours, the individual removed a 22-year-old male victim&apos;s credit cards and keys from a locker(unlocked) at The New York Sports Club located at 113 East 23rd Street.<br><br>
 
Incident #3(6 Pct): On Tuesday, November 8, 2016, at approximately 1245 hours, the individual removed a 23-year-old male victim&apos;s wallet, jacket and credit cards from a locker at The New York Sports Club located at 225 Varick Street.<br><br>
 
Incident #4(MTS): On Wednesday, November 9, 2016, at approximately 1829 hours, the individual removed a 27-year-old male victim&apos;s coach belt, credit cards and keys from a locker at The New York Sports Club located at 1372 Broadway. (Photo #1)<br><br>
 
Incident #5(MTS): On Thursday, November 10, 2016, at approximately 1757 hours, the individual removed a 25-year-old male victim&apos;s jacket, keys and credit card from a locker at The New York Sports Club located at 200 Park Avenue.<br><br>
 
Incident #6(10 Pct): On Friday, November 18, 2016, at approximately 1815 hours, the individual removed a 29-year-old male victim&apos;s Apple Macbook, clothes and credit cards from a locker at The New York Sports Club located at 128 8th Avenue.<br><br>
 
Incident #7(10 Pct): On Tuesday, November 22, 2016, at approximately 1600 hours, the individual removed a 25-year-old male victim&apos;s clothing, keys and Goose jacket from a locker at The New York Sports Club located at 128 8th Avenue.<br><br>
