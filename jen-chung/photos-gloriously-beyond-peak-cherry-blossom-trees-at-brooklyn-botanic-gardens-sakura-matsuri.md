					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170430154712/http://gothamist.com/2017/04/15/photos_cherry_blossoms_are_looking.php#photo-1">After all the worrying</a>, the cherry trees at the Brooklyn Botanic Garden were showing off their colors for the annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170430154712/https://www.bbg.org/visit/event/sakura_matsuri_2017/">Sakura Matsuri festival</a>. Don&apos;t worry if you weren&apos;t there on Saturday&#x2014;there&apos;s another day of activities today!</p>

<p>The up-and-down weather had concerned some, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170430154712/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/01/warm_winter_flowers.php">but not the experts</a>. The blossoms on trees around the Japanese Hill-and-Pond Garden have already come and gone, but the BBG&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170430154712/http://gothamist.com/2017/04/25/cherry_blossoms_from_above.php">Cherry Esplanade is spectacular</a>.  </p>

<p>In fact, our photographer <a href="saimokhtari.com">Sai Mokhtari</a> says, &quot;The bloom is just beyond peak and petals would rain down every time there was a gust of wind,&quot; creating a flurry of pink petals.</p>

<p>Here&apos;s the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170430154712/https://www.bbg.org/visit/event/sakura_matsuri_2017_sunday_events">list of April 30&apos;s Sakura Matsuri&apos;s events</a>&#x2014;like a samurai sword demonstration as well as the Eighth Annual Sakura Matsuri Cosplay Fashion Show&#x2014;you&apos;ll <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170430154712/http://bbg2017.ticketmob.com/event.cfm?cart&amp;showTimingID=157772">need advance tickets</a>.</p>
