title: Woman Robbed Of $2000, Sprayed With "Unknown Substance" In East Village
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_11_eastvillageroobbers.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011455im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_11_eastvillageroobbers.jpg" width="200" height="365" class="image-right"> </span>The police are looking for two men who allegedly robbed a woman of her cash-filled backpack on East 10th Street last week. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on Monday, November 17th, at 10:15 am., a 21-year-old woman was in front of 208 East 10th Street when two men approached her. They grabbed her backpack, police say, &quot;which held $2000 in cash. Upon the victim resisting the suspects, the suspects sprayed the victim in the face with an unknown substance and fled with the knapsack and cash.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was not hospitalized. Police released an image of the suspects, describing one as being in 20-30 years old, with a close cut beard and about 200 pounds, last seen wearing a  black puffy jacket, and blue jeans, and the other as also being 20-30 years old, 6&apos; tall and a close cut beard, but with a thin build and last seen wearing a black jacket, hooded sweatshirt, and blue jeans.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011455/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
