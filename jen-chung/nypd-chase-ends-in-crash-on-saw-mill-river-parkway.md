<p>A police pursuit that began in the Bronx has ended with a dramatic crash, after the pursued car ended up <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161226053811/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2015/12/08/saw-mill-river-yonkers-police-chase-crash/">&quot;pinned between two police vehicles.&quot;</a> </p>

<p>An NYPD patrol car <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161226053811/http://abc7ny.com/news/saw-mill-river-parkway-shut-down-following-crash-involving-police-pursuit/1114221/">tried to pull over a car before noon</a> in the Bronx, but the driver of the vehicle&#x2014;occupied by three allegedly armed suspects&#x2014;got away. The driver headed towards Yonkers. </p>

<p>After the crash, two suspects were arrested and the third one apparently ran into Tibbetts Brook Park. Shots were also reportedly fired. </p>
