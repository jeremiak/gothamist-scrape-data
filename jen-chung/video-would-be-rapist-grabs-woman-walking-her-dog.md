<p>The police have released a video of a man they say was trying to rape a woman in upper Manhattan yesterday morning.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, a 30-year-old woman was walking her dog near West 212st Street at 6:55 a.m. when a man grabbed her from behind and apparently tried to remove her clothing. The victim was able to fight off him off.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as a man 25-30 years old, 5&apos;11&quot; and 180 pounds who was last seen wearing a white t-shirt, blue denim shorts and a brown or red baseball cap.
