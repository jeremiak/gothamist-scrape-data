										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2009_05_unbldg.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503232017im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2009_05_unbldg.jpg" width="120" height="153" class="image-right"> </span>Today, North Korea&apos;s news agency announced the country had &quot;successfully conducted one more underground nuclear test on May 25 as part of the measures to bolster up its nuclear deterrent for self-defense in every way as requested by its scientists and technicians.&quot; President Obama <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503232017/http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2009/05/25/AR2009052500297.html?hpid=topnews">denounced the test</a>, &quot;North Korea&apos;s attempts to develop nuclear weapons, as well as its ballistic missile program, constitute a threat to international peace and security,&quot; as did <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503232017/http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/asia-pacific/8066861.stm">China and Russia</a>. The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503232017/http://www.nytimes.com/2009/05/26/world/asia/26nuke.html?ref=asia">NY Times reports</a> that a security expert &quot;estimated the test had a power of one kiloton of explosives... If correct, that would be a fraction of the size of the blasts from American bombs that destroyed the Japanese cities of Hiroshima and Nagasaki in August, 1945 &#x2014; themselves considered small by current standards.&quot; The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503232017/http://www.un.org/sc/members.asp">United Nations Security Council</a> will <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503232017/http://www.bloomberg.com/apps/news?pid=20601101&amp;sid=avKz43Ks6a2k&amp;refer=japan">meet later today to discuss</a> the test; according to Bloomberg News, &quot;Japan and South Korea...called for the UN body to take action against North Korea.&quot; Back in April, North Korea<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503232017/http://gothamist.com/2009/04/05/n_korea_launches_rocket_claims_its.php"> launched a rocket</a>, though claimed it was a satellite.</p>
