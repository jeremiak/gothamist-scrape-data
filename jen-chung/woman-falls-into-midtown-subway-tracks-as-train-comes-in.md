<p>A woman was removed from the tracks of a Manhattan subway station earlier this morning.</p>

<p>According to the FDNY, just after 6 a.m., they responded to a call at the 49th Street and 7th Avenue N/Q/R station. A woman fell into the tracks. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140630192423/http://7online.com/news/woman-falls-onto-subway-tracks-in-midtown-/146978/">WABC 7 reports</a>, &quot;Authorities say the woman fell from the platform as the train was coming, but was able to tuck herself between the train and the platform.&quot;</p>

<p>The FDNY says she was taken to Bellevue Hospital in serious condition. </p>
