<p>The NYPD has released images and video of a man suspected of trying to rape a woman in the Bronx early Friday morning. <br>
 <br>
According to the police, a 48-year-old entered her building in the Tremont neighborhood and was followed in by a man around 6:45 a.m. on April 11th. When she got to the second floor, the man allegedly grabbed her and attempted to rape her. Police say the victim screamed and the suspect fled. The victim was taken to an area hospital for evaluation and released.<br>
 <br>
<iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20140415063549if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/Bfrev7SkHS8" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspect is described as being 30-35 years old and 5&apos;8&quot; to 5&apos;10&quot; tall; he was wearing a white tee shirt, black jacket, blue jeans, light boots and a Philadelphia Phillies baseball hat.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this attempted rape is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140415063549/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
