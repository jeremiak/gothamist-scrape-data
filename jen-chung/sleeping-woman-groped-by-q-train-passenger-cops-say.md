										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_08_sleepingsusp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161027170028im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_08_sleepingsusp.jpg" width="275" height="505" class="image-right"> </span>A woman who fell asleep on a Q train was allegedly molested by a fellow passenger, and now the police have released a photograph of the suspect in hopes the public can help identify him.</p>

<p>The incident occurred on Friday, August 12, 2016. The 29-year-old woman got onto a southbound Q train from the 34th Street station around 9:45 p.m., heading towards Ocean Parkway. According to the NYPD, she fell asleep and &quot;when the victim awoke to exit the train at the Ocean Parkway subway station, she was informed by a witness that an unidentified individual did touch her chest while she was asleep.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect remained on the train. </p>

<p>Police describe the suspect as approximately 35 years old, 5&apos;9&quot;, tall, 175 pounds with a  light complexion and  brown hair. He was last seen wearing brown sandals, blue shorts, and a pink shirt.<br>
