										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_04_77bust1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160821062527im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_04_77bust1.jpg" width="640" height="388"> <br> <i> NYPD</i></div> <p></p>

<p>An NYPD Anti-Crime Unit team executing a search warrant on an alleged drug dealer found a stash of weapons and drugs in his home.</p>

<p>The NYPD says the officers entered the residence at 1798 Pacific Street in Crown Heights shortly after 6 a.m. on Saturday. There they found suspect Joseph Candelaria and a Mossenberg rifle, a Winchester rifle, a Stevens rifle, an imitation rifle, a Cobra .380 firearm, 10 grams of MDMA (ecstasy/molly) and a small stash of marijuana.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_04_77bust2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160821062527im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_04_77bust2.jpg" width="640" height="368"> <br> <i> NYPD</i></div> <br>
 <br>
Candelaria, 43, was arrested without incident. He&apos;s charged with criminal possession of a controlled substance, five counts of criminal possession of a weapon and unlawful possession of marijuana. <p></p>

<p>There&apos;s apparently a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160821062527/https://www.showmetherent.com/1798-Pacific-St-Brooklyn-NY-11233-3">two-bedroom apartment available for rent</a> ($2100/month) in the building. </p>
