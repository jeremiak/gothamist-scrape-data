<p>A motorist fatally collided with a tree in East Flatbush earlier this morning. The unidentified driver, a 22-year-old man, was pronounced dead at Brookdale Hospital, and there are reports he may <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145822/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Police-Officer-NYPD-Off-Duty-Killed-Brooklyn-Car-Accident-201414201.html">have been an off-duty police officer</a>.</p>

<p>According to police, they responded to a call about an accident at Kings Highway and Beverly Road at 4:17 a.m. They found the driver with serious trauma and determined he had been driving his Nissan westbound on Kings Highway and hit a tree between Beverly Road and Tilden Avenue. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145822/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9052489">WABC 7 reports</a> that the driver was in an &quot;apparent high-speed crash.&quot; The Post says the off-duty cop <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130510145822/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/brooklyn/off_duty_cop_killed_while_driving_yiH9JXWT5Xn5IZ5dO3loeJ?utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_content=%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20%20Brooklyn">was driving home from work</a>.</p>
