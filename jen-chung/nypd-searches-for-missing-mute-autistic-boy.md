title: NYPD Searches For Missing Mute Autistic Boy
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_09_avonteo.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150129041010im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_09_avonteo.jpg" width="200" height="225" class="image-right"> </span>A 14-year-old boy from Queens has been missing since Friday, and the police are asking for the public&apos;s help in finding him. It&apos;s particularly critical to find the child because he&apos;s mute and autistic.</p>

<p>Avonte Oquendo, who lives on 67th Avenue, was last seen at 12:38 p.m. leaving the Center Boulevard School at 1-50 51 Avenue. He was wearing a grey striped shirt, black jeans and black sneakers. He&apos;s 5&apos;3&quot; and about 125 pounds&#x2014;and he cannot verbally communicate. </p>

<p>His mother <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150129041010/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Search-Continues-Missing-Queens-Teen-Long-Island-City-Autistic-Mute-226676711.html">begs the public</a>, &quot;Anyone out there, any borough, if you see this child please have compassion and please just call the police and say I&apos;ve seen him.&quot;</p>
