<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811072753/http://www.nytimes.com/2009/10/05/nyregion/05samurai.html?_r=1&amp;partner=rss&amp;emc=rss">According to the NY Times</a>, cops received a call about an incident on East 91st Street in East Flatbush: At 8 a.m., &quot;They found Mr. Dixon &apos;with lacerations to the head, arm and hand,&apos;&quot; and arrested Joseph for charged with assault with intent to cause a serious injury, menacing and criminal possession of a weapon.  Cops also took the sword. </p>

<p>Mackeral&apos;s girlfriend, who saw the altercation unfold in their building&apos;s hall, said the men were arguing over the weapon at one point&#x2014;and that she tried to take seize the weapon as well. She also <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811072753/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2009/10/04/2009-10-04_1980s_reggae_star_major_mackarel_attacked_by_jealous_neighbor_with_samurai_sword.html">told the Daily News</a>, &quot;He was saying [Mackerel] was harassing his wife. But] he doesn&apos;t mess with his wife. I don&apos;t know why he said that.&quot;</p>

<p>Mackerel did admit to the News that his friends may have cat-called Joseph&apos;s wife.  But, in spite of the attack and ensuing hospital visit, he was positive after the event, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811072753/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2009/10/04/2009-10-04_1980s_reggae_star_major_mackarel_attacked_by_jealous_neighbor_with_samurai_sword.html">waxing philosophical to the News</a>, &quot;I don&apos;t feel bad. These things happen...It&apos;s a Jamaican thing... <strong>How can I feel bad for someone who tried to kill me?</strong>&quot;</p>
