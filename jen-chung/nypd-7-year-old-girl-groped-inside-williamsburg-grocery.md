<p>A child was groped by a man in Williamsburg, Brooklyn on Monday, and investigators are hoping the public can help them track down the guy who did it.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 5:30 p.m. on Monday, May 2nd. The victim, a 7-year-old girl, was inside the Southside Food Plaza at 428 Bedford Avenue &quot;when the individual grabbed her buttocks and then fled the location in an unknown direction.&quot;<br>
 <br>
Cops released surveillance video of the suspect, describing him as about 45 years old and 5&apos;8&quot;, with short white hair and a tattoo of dots of his hand, and possibly under his right eye.  He was last seen wearing a black hooded sweatshirt, blue jeans, and a black brimmed baseball cap.</p>
