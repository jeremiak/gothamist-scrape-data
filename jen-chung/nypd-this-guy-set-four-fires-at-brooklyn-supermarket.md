<p>Authorities are looking for a person who started a number of fires at a grocery store in Brownsville earlier this week.</p>

<p>The incident occurred around 6:15 p.m. on Monday, January 30th, at the Food Bazaar, located at 417 Junius Street. The NYPD says the suspect &quot;set four separate fires thought the store, endangering the safety of the store occupants and causing property damage.&quot; The unidentified man then left the store and boarded a westbound B14 bus. Cops say store customers and security staff used bottled water and a hose to to fight the fires, and the FDNY also responded to put the fires out.</p>

<p>No one was injured. The suspect is described as 5&apos;3&quot; and 115 pounds, and was last seen wearing a black ski cap and a black parka with a fur-trimmed hood. Police also released video of the arson suspect:<br>
