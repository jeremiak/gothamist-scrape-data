										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_08_bxdaylight1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015037im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_08_bxdaylight1.jpg" width="640" height="400" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Yesterday afternoon, three men were shot while they stood on a Bronx sidewalk. A surveillance video shows a large group congregation and then they start ducking and running as a gunman appears, firing at them:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510015037if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/m8hLn05LiKY?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the shooting occurred at 2:54 p.m. near the intersection of Summit Avenue and West 165 Street. Police say the shooter &quot;displayed a firearm and discharged several rounds at the group, striking the three victims, before fleeing the location. A 29-year-old male was shot in the torso, a 21-year-old male was grazed in the chest, and a 28-year-old male was shot in the right knee.&quot;</p>

<p>The three men are in stable condition at Lincoln Hospital. Police are asking for the public&apos;s help in finding the gunman, who is described as a man in his 20&apos;s, approximately 5&apos;10&quot; tall, weighing 175 lbs. and wearing a grey shirt and grey sweat pants, armed with a handgun.<br>
