---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_07_bushwickr1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016022031im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_07_bushwickr1.jpg" width="640" height="471" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>A woman was sleeping in her Brooklyn apartment when she was awoken by an armed man who tied her up and then raped her early yesterday, police say. </p>

<p>The suspect told her, &quot;Do you want me to rape you or kill you?&quot;, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016022031/http://abc7ny.com/news/suspect-asks-victim-do-you-want-me-to-rape-you-or-kill-you/1442823/">according to WABC 7</a>.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_07_bushwickr2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016022031im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_07_bushwickr2.jpg" width="300" height="366" class="image-right"> </span>The assault occurred at 1:30 a.m. on July 25, inside an apartment near DeKalb Avenue and Wilson Avenue: The NYPD says &quot;a 29-year-old female victim was sleeping when she heard her door being opened at which time an unknown male approached her displaying a silver firearm then proceeded to forcibly rape the victim and remove some personal items of the victim before fleeing in an unknown direction.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was treated at Woodhull Hospital. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as being around 34 years old, 6&apos;1&quot; and 195 pounds. The police released a surveillance image as well as a police sketch of him. <br>
