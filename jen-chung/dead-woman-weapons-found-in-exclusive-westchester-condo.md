                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left"> <img alt="2013_08_tarrytownsho.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905212458im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_08_tarrytownsho.jpg" width="365" height="233"> <br> <i style=" width:365px; ;display:block"> Via News 12</i></div> Police in Tarrytown, NY are calling the death of a 23-year-old &quot;suspicious&quot; after finding her <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905212458/http://www.lohud.com/article/20130902/NEWS02/309020040/Woman-23-dead-suspicious-Tarrytown-shooting?gcheck=1">body in a condo in an &quot;exclusive&quot; part of town</a>. The woman apparently did not reside in the home.<p></p>

<p>Tarrytown police Lt. William Herguth said they received a call yesterday afternoon about someone being shot at 157 West Main Street. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905212458/http://tarrytown.patch.com/groups/police-and-fire/p/23yearold-woman-found-dead-in-tarrytowns-hudson-harbor-townhouse">Patch reports</a>, &quot;He could not reveal if the caller was someone at the scene itself or who may have heard shots nearby.&quot; They found the woman in a fourth floor bedroom, per Herguth, &quot;mortally wounded and pronounced dead at the scene.&quot; Other weapons were found in the room, and police haven&apos;t ruled out homicide or suicide yet as cause of death.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130905212458/http://pix11.com/2013/09/03/woman-shot-and-killed-in-suspicious-tarrytown-shooting">According to PIX</a>, &quot;Neighbors say a couple lived in the home with their two children. One of them went to college.&quot; In fact, entertainment lawyer Robert Gaulin is recorded as the condo&apos;s owner. </p>

<p>A neighbor told the Journal News, &quot;It&#x2019;s exclusive; it&#x2019;s an exclusive area.&quot;</p>
