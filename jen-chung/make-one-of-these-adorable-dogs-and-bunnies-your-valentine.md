					<p>Are you tired of buying chocolates and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160628061249/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/12/bodega_flowers_forever.php">bodega flowers</a> to celebrate today&apos;s over-commercialized holiday? And are you ready to make the most heartwarming commitment of all? Then go to the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160628061249/https://www.aspca.org/nyc/aspca-adoption-center">ASPCA&apos;s Adoption Center</a> on the Upper East Side to meet the future love of your life!</p>

<p>These five dogs and five bunnies are ready to find their furever homes&#x2014;and the ASPCA also wants prospective adopters to know that their pickup game is STRONG. To mark the occasion, adoption fees for dogs over 30 pounds are $14, slashed from $75. Adoption fees for the bunnies, who are the ones rescued in Brooklyn last year, are also only $14 (originally $75), and come with a free cage and water bottle. </p>

<p>The dogs are at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160628061249/https://www.aspca.org/nyc/aspca-adoption-center">ASPCA Adoption Center</a> at 424 East 92nd Street, while the bunnies are at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160628061249/https://www.aspca.org/nyc/aspca-adoption-center">ASPCA Adoption Center</a> on 415 East 91st Street. </p>
