										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020949/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_08_upper1.jpg"> <img alt="2016_08_upper1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020949im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2016/08/2016_08_upper1-thumb-320x494-960808.jpg" width="300" height="463" class="image-left" style="float:left"> </a> </span>The NYPD says this man wearing a patriotic Phillies cap is wanted for unlawful surveillance, for allegedly taking upskirt photos of a subway rider in Manhattan over the weekend.</p>

<p>The incident occurred on a 7 train approaching Grand Central Terminal at 11:40 p.m. on Saturday, August 7th, when, according to the NYPD, the man was caught holding his cell phone under the shorts of a 27-year-old woman and taking photos.   </p>

<p>The suspect is described as being approximately 5&apos;9 and 175 pounds, with brown eyes and brown hair. </p>

<p>He was last seen wearing a white baseball hat (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020949/http://www.ebay.com/itm/NEW-PHILADELPHIA-PHILLIES-NEW-ERA-STARS-STRIPES-RED-WHITE-BLUE-USA-FLAG-HAT-8/371683654711?_trksid=p2045573.c100642.m3226&amp;_trkparms=aid%3D555014%26algo%3DPL.DEFAULT%26ao%3D1%26asc%3D20160706104836%26meid%3D5ebe438d63264802b85f2f568906de3b%26pid%3D100642%26rk%3D1%26rkt%3D1%26">which looks like this</a>), black sneakers, green t-shirt, and blue jeans. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782).  </p>
