title: 1 Dead, 1 Missing After 5-Alarm Williamsburg Fire And "Explosion"

										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2015_12_77firewb.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427030715im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_12_77firewb.jpg" width="640" height="361"> <br> <i> Via WABC 7</i></div> <p></p>

<p>A devastating five-alarm fire ripped through a Brooklyn building early this morning, leaving one person dead and another missing. Some Williamsburg residents say they heard an explosion before the fire.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427030715/https://twitter.com/FDNY/status/681370134590668800">According to the FDNY</a>, the fire occurred at 40 Richardson Street, a three-story building just a block from McCarren Park, on the second and third floors as well as the rear tenement. A male body was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160427030715/http://abc7ny.com/news/1-dead-1-missing-as-fire-burns-through-building-in-williamsburg/1137720/">found on the second floor</a>.</p>
