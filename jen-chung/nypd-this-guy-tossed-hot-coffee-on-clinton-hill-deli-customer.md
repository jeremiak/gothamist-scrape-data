<p>Police are searching for a man who burned a Brooklyn deli customer with scalding hot coffee during an argument earlier this month.</p>

<p>According to police, the incident occurred at about 11 a.m. on April 19th, at the Jabran Deli and Grocery at 994 Bedford Avenue in Clinton Hill. Investigators say the man &quot;threw hot coffee on the 32-year-old victim&apos;s chest after a dispute. The victim suffered burns to his chest.&quot;</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813174608/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_05_bkdelisuspect.jpg"> <img alt="2016_05_bkdelisuspect.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813174608im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2016/05/2016_05_bkdelisuspect-thumb-800x306-945609.jpg" width="640" height="244" class="image-none"> </a> </span></p>

<p>Police say the coffee tosser has blond hair and was wearing sunglasses, a black t-shirt, black shorts, black sneakers and a black backpack. Investigators released images and video of the suspect; the video shows him filling his cup, tossing it and later cleaning his arm:</p>
