title: NYPD: Man Threatened To Slap Woman, Then Flashed A Gun On E Train
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_10_menacing1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020051im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_10_menacing1.jpg" width="320" height="484" class="image-left"> </span>The police are looking for a man who is accused of threatening a woman on an E train on Friday&#x2014;and he was allegedly armed. </p>

<p>The incident occurred during the morning commute, at 8:40 a.m., on a Manhattan-bound E train on October 14 in the vicinity of Broadway and Roosevelt Avenue. The NYPD says &quot;an unidentified male sat next to&quot; a 33-year-old woman &quot;and threatened to slap her.&quot;</p>

<p>The woman moved to another part of the train, and police say, &quot;The unidentified male approached, lifted his shirt to expose the handle of a firearm.  The victim exited the train, while the male remained on the train.&quot; The victim was not injured.</p>

<p>The authorities released an image of the suspect, who is described as being in his 30s and about 6&apos; tall and was last seen wearing a black baseball hat, black sneakers, green jacket, and black jeans. </p>

<p>He is wanted for criminal menacing.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782).
