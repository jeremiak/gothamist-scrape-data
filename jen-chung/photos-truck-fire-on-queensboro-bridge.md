                            <p>A vehicle caught fire on the Queensboro Bridge (or Ed Koch Queensboro Bridge), which is causing closures on the lower level. According to the FDNY, it&apos;s an all-hands fire with 12 units on the scene.</p>

<p>The Fire Department received the call about the fire at 11:08 p.m. Photographs show thick black smoke coming from the bridge. </p>
