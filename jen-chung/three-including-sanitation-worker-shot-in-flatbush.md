title: Three, Including Sanitation Worker, Shot in Flatbush

										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2009_05_bklynshtn.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150805150716im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2009_05_bklynshtn.jpg" width="145" height="109" class="image-right"> </span>Yesterday around 7 p.m., a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150805150716/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=6835573">drive-by gunman fired at three people</a> on Lenox Road near Flatbush Avenue in Brooklyn (here&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150805150716/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=96QB1x_5_Y0">video of the area after the shooting</a>).  One of the victims was a sanitation worker, in a street sweeper, who had taken a break to get a drink from a bodega.  The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150805150716/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2009/05/28/2009-05-28_brooklyn_driveby_shooting_injures_3.html">Daily News reports</a> that san worker Anthony Pollard &quot;told cops he had parked his sweeper to say hello to a cousin and buy a soda at the ND Food Market, his usual spot for a nightly break.&quot; He had been standing outside the store with two other men when a car drove by and someone fired more than a dozen times at the store from inside the vehicle. Pollard <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150805150716/http://www.nypost.com/seven/05282009/news/regionalnews/sanit_worker_shot_in_bklyn_171293.htm">was shot in the stomach</a> while the other men were hit in the leg and elbow; their injuries are not life-threatening. Police are asking people with information to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS or send a text message to &quot;CRIMES,&quot; (274637), then enter &quot;TIP577&quot;.</p>
