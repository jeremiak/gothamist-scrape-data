										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_02_punchout.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170312035133im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_02_punchout.jpg" width="640" height="437" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Authorities are looking for a group accused of stealing a man&apos;s cellphone and credit cards after attacking him on a Brooklyn street.</p>

<p>The incident occurred on January 20th, around 11 p.m. Police say the victim, 23, was walking near Evergreen Avenue and Jefferson Street &quot;when one of the individuals struck him in the back of the head. Once the victim was on the ground, individuals #2 and #3 proceeded to punch and kick him in the face and body.&quot;</p>

<p>While the victim was on the ground, they allegedly took his iPhone 6, debit card, and credit cards from his pocket and fled. The victim suffered a black eye as well as cuts and bruises. </p>
