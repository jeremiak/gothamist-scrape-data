<p>The NYPD announced that it conducted a four-day prostitution sting in 27 police precincts across the city, resulting in many arrests.  One hundred eighty-six johns were arrested&#x2014;the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120120034536/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/cops_bust_almost_in_prostitution_vDPw5BLq6fYOl5sZoRcAzM?CMP=OTC-rss&amp;FEEDNAME=">Post says</a>, &quot;Undercover cops pose as hookers and collar those johns looking for a prostitute,&quot; and the sting was called &#x201C;Operation Losing Proposition.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120120034536/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/scores-johns-busted-nypd-prostitution-crackdown-article-1.1007342?localLinksEnabled=false">Ten prostitutes were arrested</a> and 56 cars were seized.  The neighborhood with the most arrests was Brownsville in Brooklyn (3 prostitutes, 16 johns) and runner-up was Jackson Heights in Queens (14 johns).  </p>
