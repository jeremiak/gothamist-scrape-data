                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_10_sohorapeatt.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128062927im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_10_sohorapeatt.jpg" width="640" height="488" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police have released a surveillance video of a man who is accused of attempting to rape and rob a woman in a Soho early on Tuesday.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident happened at 1:15 a.m. on October 8th at Thompson Street between Prince and Spring Streets. The suspect approached a 20-year-old woman, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128062927/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/nyu-student-robbed-raped-cops-article-1.1482569">described by the Daily News as a NYU student</a>, from behind, placing her in a chokehold while trying to rape her. He left the scene with her purse. The woman was treated for facial injuries.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128062927/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
