<p>The Paley Center announced an <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030205438/http://gothamist.com/2014/09/16/ny_paleyfest_features_walking_dead.php">exciting slate of PaleyFest panels</a> last month, including ones for <em>The Walking Dead</em> and <em>Gotham</em>, but now there&apos;s a great reunion as well: <em>The Wire</em>&apos;s creator David Simon will be on hand with actors Wendell Pierce, Sonja Sohn, Seth Gilliam, Jim True-Frost, John Doman, Lawrence Gilliard Jr, Jamie Hector and, yes, Omar himself, Michael K. Williams on October 7th.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20171030205438if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/TOw8ZSQXFvE?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030205438/http://insidetv.ew.com/2014/10/03/the-wire-cast-members-reunite-paleyfest/">Entertainment Weekly announced the news yesterday</a>, noting that executive producer Nina Noble will also be there and &quot;Dominic West will do a special video intro for the reunion.&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030205438/http://sepinwall.blogspot.com/2012/06/where-you-can-find-my-wire-season-5.html">Alan Sepinwall</a> of Hitfix will be moderating; he focused on the show in his book, <em><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030205438/http://www.amazon.com/Revolution-Was-Televised-Alan-Sepinwall-ebook/dp/B00B26PWFE/ref=la_B001K8C6KS_1_1?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1412390039&amp;sr=1-1">The Revolution Was Televised</a></em>. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030205438/http://media.paleycenter.org/paleyfest-ny-2014/">Tickets for the panel</a>, which is on October 16, will go on-sale Tuesday, Oct. 7 at 10 a.m.</p>
