<p>The police are looking for four suspects in a cellphone robbery that took place in Morningside Heights in September.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on September 25, at 5:15 p.m., a 29-year-old man was on a southbound 1 train. As the train approached 110th Street, police say that four suspects &quot;surrounded him&quot; and &quot;Upon pulling into the station and the train car doors opening, Suspect #1 removed the victim&apos;s iPhone 4S and fled on the subway platform with the other suspects, as well as with the victim in pursuit.&quot;</p>

<p>One suspect (&quot;Suspect #1) allegedly punched the victim in the face, which caused a lip laceration. Police released photos of the suspects; Suspect #1 was wearing a red and white jacket (photo above, far left). <br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012045/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
