										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left"> <img alt="2016_04_joshluteran.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160825154301im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_04_joshluteran.jpg" width="280" height="342"> <br> <i style=" width:280px; ;display:block"> Josh Luteran (NYPD)</i></div> Cops are searching for a Brooklyn man accused of critically injuring his girlfriend during a dispute.<p></p>

<p>The NYPD says the incident happened shortly after 1 a.m. yesterday (Tuesday, April 12th) when Josh Luteran, 30, and his girlfriend argued in their Bedford-Stuyvesant apartment near Bedford and Jefferson Avenues. During the dispute Luteran allegedly struck the girlfriend, 28, in the head.</p>

<p>Luteran allegedly fled the scene. A police source <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160825154301/http://www.amny.com/news/josh-luteran-accused-of-brutally-beating-woman-in-bedford-stuyvesant-home-nypd-1.11684505">tells AMNY</a> that the victim at first refused medical attention, but she was eventually persuaded to let EMTs take her to the hospital. She fell into a coma <em>en route</em> to Methodist Hospital, and is currently in critical but stable condition.<br>
 <br>
Luteran, described as about 5&apos;10&quot; and last seen wearing a black skull cap, jacket and black sweatpants, is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160825154301/https://www.backstage.com/JoshLuteran/">an actor</a> and amateur boxer:</p>
