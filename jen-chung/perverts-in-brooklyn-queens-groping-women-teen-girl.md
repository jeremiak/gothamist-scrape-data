<p>The police are looking for two different suspects in forcible touching incidents in Brooklyn and Queens. And the Queens suspect is accused of groping three different victims.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the Brooklyn incident occurred on Thursday, August 21st at around 11:35 a.m. when a 68-year-old woman walking near 5th Avenue and 39th Street was approached from behind. The suspect grabbed her buttocks, then fled. </p>

<p>Police released video of the suspect, who is described as an Asian male, approximately 25 years old, 5&apos;9&quot; and 150 pounds with straight black hair. </p>

<p>The Queens incidents occurred last month. The police say that in each instance, the suspect grabbed the victims&apos; breasts before fleeing. Here&apos;s list of the incidents: </p>

<p>1) Monday August 11th, 2014 at 7:30 a.m. hours at Haspel Street and 55th Avenue (the victim was a 21-year-old woman); <br>
2) Wednesday August 13th, 2014 8:30 a.m. hours in front of 44-20 Ketcham Street (the victim was a 28-year-old woman); and <br>
