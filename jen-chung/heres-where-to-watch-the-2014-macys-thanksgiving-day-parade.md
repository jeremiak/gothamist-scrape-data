<p>If you can even fathom venturing outside after <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011447/http://gothamist.com/2014/11/26/thanksgiving_weather_travel.php">today&apos;s nasty mess</a>, we&apos;ve got your details about where you can watch the Macy&apos;s Thanksgiving Day Parade tomorrow!</p>

<p>The parade starts&#x2014;at 9 a.m.&#x2014;at Central Park West and 77th Street and goes down CPW. It travels across Central Park South, then parade heads down 6th Avenue, and then across 34th Street to Macy&apos;s. </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_11_paraderoute.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011447im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_11_paraderoute.jpg" width="640" height="828" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>This year&apos;s performers include the Big Apple Circus, Cirque du Soleil, Hilary Duff, Renee Fleming, Nick Jonas, KISS, Idina Menzel, Quvenzhan&#xE9; Wallis and the cast of Sesame Street, while marching bands from 12 high schools and universities and numerous performance groups will bring energy to the route. Plus, there are floats and the amazing balloons, which are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011447/http://gothamist.com/2014/11/25/map_where_to_see_macys_2014_thanksg.php">being blown up as we speak</a> outside the American Museum of Natural History.</p>
