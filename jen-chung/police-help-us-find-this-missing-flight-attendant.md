										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left"> <img alt="2016_01_sierras.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160503161023im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_01_sierras.jpg" width="300" height="314"> <br> <i style=" width:300px; ;display:block"> Sierra Shielfs</i></div> The NYPD hopes the public can help them find a missing Queens resident who was last seen on Thursday. <p></p>

<p>Sierra Shields, 30, is a Shuttle America flight attendant, and she was last seen leaving LaGuardia Airport on Thursday, January 14th, at around noon. </p>

<p>Shields is described as being 5&apos;7&quot; tall and 160 pounds, with a medium build, medium complexion, black hair and brown eyes. She was last seen wearing a navy blue dress.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160503161023/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2016/01/17/missing-queens-woman/">According to WCBS 2</a>, &quot;Her family is not sure if she was scheduled to fly out Thursday night... [and] says this is out of character for Shields.&quot;</p>

<p>The Find Sierra Shields Facebook page <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160503161023/https://www.facebook.com/Findsierrashields/posts/1658639874391494">notes</a>, &quot;To all those posting, sharing, walking around New York, giving everything to find Sierra, thank you! I just want to make sure people are aware that the uniform Sierra was wearing resembles a blue dress, something you would find in a department store window.&quot; </p>

<p>The Astoria resident is also a member of the Connection Church.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this missing is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160503161023/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
