title: NYPD: Man Used Car Club Lock To Attack Two People In Brooklyn
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_05_carclublockattack.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140531035801im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_05_carclublockattack.jpg" width="300" height="333" class="image-right"> </span>The police are hoping the public can help identify a man accused of a brutal beating carried out with a steering wheel lock in Brooklyn.</p>

<p>The police say the incident occurred just after midnight on May 10th at 140 Hamilton Avenue, which is the address of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140531035801/http://www.brooklynmotorinn.com/">Brooklyn Motor Inn</a>. According to the NYPD, &quot;The suspect entered the location and struck two victims with a car club.&quot; The victims were a 13-year-old boy, who suffered a broken arm and a head laceration, and a 29-year-old man who suffered a head laceration. The victims were treated at a hospital.<br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as being around 45-50 years old. Anyone with information is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140531035801/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
