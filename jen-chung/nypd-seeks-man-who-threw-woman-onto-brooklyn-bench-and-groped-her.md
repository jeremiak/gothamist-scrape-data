title: NYPD Seeks Man Who Threw Woman Onto Brooklyn Bench And Groped Her
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014930/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_08_ftoususp.jpg"> <img alt="2014_08_ftoususp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014930im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2014/08/2014_08_ftoususp-thumb-445x280-854422.jpg" width="400" height="251" class="image-right"> </a> </span>Police are hoping the public can help them find a man accused of forcible touching in Bay Ridge.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 10:58 p.m. on Wednesday, July 16, near 95th Street and 3rd Avenue. Police say the suspect grabbed a 35-year-old woman from behind, threw her onto a bench and then &quot;placed his hand up her skirt and touched her over her underwear.&quot; The suspect then fled on foot. </p>

<p>Police released photos of the suspect. Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto Crime Stoppers&apos; website at<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014930/http:// www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/"> www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.</p>
