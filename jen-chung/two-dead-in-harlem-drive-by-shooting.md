<p>Police are investigating the double murder of two men outside a Harlem bodega earlier this morning.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at around 2:53 a.m. Sunday, officers responded to a 911 call about two men who were shot outside the Fedel Gourmet Deli on Broadway at 151st Street. When police got there, they found a 21-year-old man, unconscious, with a gunshot wound to his neck and a 29-year-old man, also unconscious, with a gunshot wound to his back.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_08_hshooting1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014342im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_08_hshooting1.jpg" width="640" height="426"> <br> <i> Photograph by David Torres</i></div> <p></p>

<p>The 21-year-old, Kenny Jimenez, who lived on the same block, was pronounced dead at the scene while the 29-year-old, Francisco Mercedez of the Bronx, died at Harlem Hospital. <br>
<span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_08_hshooting3.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014342im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_08_hshooting3.jpg" width="640" height="426"> <br> <i> Photograph by David Torres</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Other reports say that the men were killed by a motorist who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014342/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/2-Dead-in-Harlem-Street-Shooting-Broadway-West-151st-Street-Kenny-Jiminez-Francisco-Mercedez-271579451.html">&quot;got out of his car and opened fire.&quot;</a></p>
