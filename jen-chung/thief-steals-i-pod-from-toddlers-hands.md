title: Thief Steals iPod From Toddler's Hands
---


                            
                            
                            
                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_09_ipodthief.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130915221133im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_09_ipodthief.jpg" width="175" height="233" class="image-right"> </span>The police hope to find the dastardly person who stole an electronic gadget from a little kid in the Bronx.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on Saturday, September 7th, at around 5:20 p.m., a woman was strolling her 2-year-old son when the suspect walked up to the toddler and took the iPod right out of his hands. Aside from his irrevocably shattered innocence, the child was uninjured.</p>

<p>The police released an image of the suspect and asks that anyone with information in regards to this Grand Larceny is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130915221133/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>

<p>In recent months, a three-year-old has been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130915221133/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/08/war_on_tots_creep_allegedly_stole_n.php">robbed of a gold necklace</a>, another three-year-old was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130915221133/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/08/thief_steals_300_from_3-year-old_af.php">robbed of $500</a>, and third three-year-old <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130915221133/http://gothamist.com/2013/04/19/man_who_stole_iphone_from_toddler_i.php">was robbed of an iPhone</a>.</p>
