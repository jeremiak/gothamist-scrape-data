<p>An electrical fire on West 53rd Street&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161228193437/http://www.wnyc.org/story/subway-service-disrupted-after-fire-knocks-out-third-rail/">at an MTA substation</a>&#x2014; knocked out power to the third rail along the 7th Avenue line. Passengers were stuck on a train and had to be evacuated (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161228193437/http://gothamist.com/2016/12/26/post_184.php">more details here</a>). Now, with subways already on a holiday schedule for today&apos;s observed Christmas holiday, here&apos;s how things are running, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161228193437/http://www.mta.info/status/subway/ACE/24712651">according to the MTA</a>:</p>

<blockquote>There is no D or F train service between 47-50 Sts-Rockefeller Ctr and W 4 St-Washington Sq in both directions. (Reminder: Today, trains run on a holiday schedule, no B trains running, M Subway trains operate between Delancey St-Essex St and Middle Village-Metropolitan Av.)

<p>There is no service on the E line between World Trade Center and Queens Plaza in both directions.</p>

<p>D trains are running on the A line between 59 St-Columbus Circle and West 4 St-Washington Square, then over the F line between Broadway-Lafayette St and Coney Island-Stillwell Av in both directions. </p>

<p>E trains are running on the R line between Queens Plaza and Whitehall St in both directions.</p>

<p>F trains are running on the R line between Jackson Heights-Roosevelt Av and 34 St Herald Square, then over the Q line between 34 St-Herald Square and DeKalb Av, then over the D line between Atlantic Av-Barclays Center and Coney Island-Stillwell Av in both directions.</p>

<p>Shuttle bus service is available between Queens Plaza and Roosevelt Island stations.</p>

<p>For Court Square-23 St, take 7 train service instead.</p>

<p>For Lexington Av-53 St and 5 Av-53 St, take E or F trains to Lexington Av-59 St and transfer to the 6 train to nearby 51 St station.</p>

<p>For Lexington Av-63 St, take E and F trains stopping at nearby Lexington Av-59 St.</p>

<p>For 57 St, 7 Av, 47-50 Sts-Rockefeller Center and 42 St-Bryant Park, take E trains stopping at nearby N line stations.</p>

<p>For 23 St and 14 St, take E trains stopping at nearby N line stations.</p>

<p>For Grand St station, take Dtrains stopping at nearby East Broadway F station.</p>

<p>As an alternative, for service between Queens and Manhattan, use 7 and N train service.</p>

<p>Expect delays on A, D, E, F, N Subway, Q and R trains.  </p></blockquote><p></p>

<p><b>Update</b>: Service has been restored</p>
