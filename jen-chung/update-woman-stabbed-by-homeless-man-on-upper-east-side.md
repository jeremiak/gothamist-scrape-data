title: UPDATE: Woman Stabbed By Homeless Man On Upper East Side
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2012_07_stabbingues.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150809234952im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_07_stabbingues.jpg" width="365" height="244"> <br> <i style=" width:365px; ;display:block"> Via WABC 7</i></div> Around 10:30 a.m., a 24-year-old woman was stabbed by a homeless man at East 86th Street and 2nd Avenue in Manhattan.   <b>Update</b>: Police released photos of the suspect (see below).<p></p>

<p>Police say that the victim&apos;s injuries were not life-threatening and she was taken to New York Hospital. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150809234952/http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20120717/upper-east-side/homeless-man-stabs-woman-on-upper-east-side-police-say">According to DNAinfo</a>, she was stabbed in the stomach.</p>

<p>The assailant, who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150809234952/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=8739189">some described as emotionally disturbed</a>, fled the scene.  </p>

<p>Police are currently canvasing the area for him.  </p>

<p><strong>Update:</strong>  The police have released photographs of the suspect now.  </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2012_07_suspectuesstab.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150809234952im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_07_suspectuesstab.jpg" width="640" height="480" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The NYPD says the suspect first doused the victim with an &quot;unknown liquid&quot; and then stabbed her in the abdomen. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as a black male around 5&apos;9&quot;-5&apos;11&quot;, 180 lbs with a beard and afro style hair, last seen wearing a maroon shirt and carrying a backpack.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150809234952/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=8739189">WABC 7 reports</a>, &quot;He is believed to be a street person, perhaps known to neighbors in the area. &apos;We think he&apos;s a little off, so we really want to get him quickly,&apos;&quot; said a law enforcement source.  </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2012_07_suspeues2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150809234952im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_07_suspeues2.jpg" width="640" height="408" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150809234952/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Homeless-Man-Stabbing-Upper-East-Side-Police-162734686.html">According to NBC New York</a>, he was last seen running west on 85th Street.</p>
