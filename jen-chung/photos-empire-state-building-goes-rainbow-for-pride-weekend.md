</div></div>					<p>Take a moment to enjoy how beautiful the Empire State Building looks this weekend, because it&apos;s lit up in rainbow colors for NYC Pride Week! And notice&#x2014;there&apos;s also a sparking halo!</p>

<p>The Empire State Building posted many photographs from last night on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503145441/https://www.facebook.com/media/set/?set=a.685461421471032.1073741868.153817204635459&amp;type=1">its Facebook page</a> (see some of them above) and the lights will be on tonight and tomorrow night. Tonight, there&apos;s the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503145441/http://www.nycpride.org/events">Party on the Pier</a> with Cher&#x2014;who was at Marquee <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503145441/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/28/photos_videos_cher_starts_celebrati.php#photo-1">on Thursday night</a>&#x2014;and Sunday is the big Pride March, which starts at noon at 36th Street and Fifth Avenue and ends at Christopher and Greenwich Streets. </p>

<p>This afternoon, there&apos;s the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503145441/https://www.facebook.com/dykemarchnyc">Dyke March</a> at 5 p.m., starting at Fifth Avenue at 42nd Street. </p>
