										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_04_sasusp1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140410043857im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_04_sasusp1.jpg" width="250" height="273" class="image-right"> </span>The police are seeking the public&apos;s help in finding a man accused of an attempted sexual assault in the Financial District last Monday.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD,  at 11 p.m. on March 31, a 21-year-old woman was walking inside the Fulton Street subway station at Fulton and Nassau Streets: &quot;As she approached a turnstile, an unknown individual came from behind the victim, grabbed her by the neck, covered her mouth and dragged her towards the station elevator.  The suspect stated &apos;I just want to love you baby.&apos;&quot; </p>

<p>Police say that then the victim started to scream and kicked the suspect. She also kicked the emergency button by the elevator. The suspect released her and fled&#x2014;the NYPD released video of the suspect running outside:</p>

The suspect is described as being in his 20&apos;s, 5&apos;8&quot; tall, weighing 180 lbs. He was last seen wearing a gray hooded snorkel-style ski jacket. Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140410043857/http://www.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.com/">www.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.com</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
