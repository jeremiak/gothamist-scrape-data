</div></div><p>Daniel Radcliffe, the <em>Harry Potter</em> star who has grown up in front of our eyes, was a game host on last night&apos;s <em>Saturday Night Live</em>.  He played Harry Potter, as well as a Target store employee, himself, Casey Anthony&apos;s dog, and someone who can Irish step dance and do Chinese calligraphy.  He did reference his stint in &quot;How To Succeed in Business&quot; but he did not go naked the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120119081459/http://gothamist.com/2008/09/11/equus.php">way he did in &quot;Equus.&quot;</a> Watch the videos to see the skits.</p>
