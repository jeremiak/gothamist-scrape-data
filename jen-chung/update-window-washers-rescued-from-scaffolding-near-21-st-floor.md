---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_08_windowwasher.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014836im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_08_windowwasher.jpg" width="640" height="372"> <br> <i> Via MyFoxNY</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Two window washers are currently trapped in their gondola outside the 21st floor of 290 Broadway in lower Manhattan. The building is a total of 32 floors. <b>Update</b>: Firefighters pulled the window washers back into the building through a window.</p>

<p>The FDNY is on the scene and Notify NYC says that people should expect traffic delays near Broadway and Reade Street.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014836/http://7online.com/news/workers-stuck-on-stalled-scaffolding-in-lower-manhattan/252667/">According to WABC 7</a>, &quot;The scaffold is stable, it&apos;s just not operating. Operators are trying to figure out if it lost power or the motor malfunctioned.&quot;</p>
