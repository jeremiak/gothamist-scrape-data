<p>The police are hoping the public can help them locate a man who tried to rape a woman last week in a Tribeca building.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 9:15 p.m. on Friday, September 20, a 39-year-old woman walked into her apartment building when a man pushed her to the floor and attempted to rape her. Another person walked in and scared the attacked away. The victim was treated for bruising. </p>

The police describe him as a man in his late 40&apos;s to mid-50&apos;s, 5&apos;8&quot;-5&apos;10&quot; tall, weighing 170-200 pounds. He was wearing a navy blue blazer, white shirt, dark pants and black dress shoes.<br>

Anyone with information in regards to this attempted rape is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS.
