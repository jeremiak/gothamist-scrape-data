                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_07_attrapesuspt.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130731234937im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_07_attrapesuspt.jpg" width="250" height="282" class="image-right"> </span>The police are hoping the public can help identify and locate a suspect wanted for an attempted rape and attempted robbery in Brooklyn this week.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on Tuesday, July 23, at 2:40 a.m.: A 24-year-old woman was walking near 275 Webster Avenue &quot;when she grabbed from behind and thrown to the ground. While on the ground, the suspect demanded her money and attempted to rape her but fled when the victim resisted.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as a man about 27-32 years old and 5&apos;10&quot;-6&apos;1&quot; with dark hair and last seen wearing a black T-shirt and dark blue jeans.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130731234937/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
 </p>
