<p>A motorcyclist traveling in the Upper East Side died after hitting an ambulance earlier this morning. The ambulance was apparently responding to an emergency.</p>

<p>According to police, the motorcyclist was traveling southbound on Lexington Avenue at 9:40 a.m. when he collided with an ambulance for New York Presbyterian Hospital, which was traveling eastbound on East 96th Street. The 44-year-old man was taken to Metropolitan Hospital, where he was pronounced dead at 10:20 a.m.</p>
