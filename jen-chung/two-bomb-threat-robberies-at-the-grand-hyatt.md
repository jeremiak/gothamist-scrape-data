title: Two Bomb Threat Robberies At The Grand Hyatt
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2009_05_hyattg.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140809im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2009_05_hyattg.jpg" width="134" height="140" class="image-right"> </span>Apparently, a successful robbery M.O. means attempting it again less than 24 hours later and the Grand Hyatt, which is just east of Grand Central Terminal, was the victim of <em>two</em> robberies. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140809/http://ny1.com/content/top_stories/100204/police-investigate-double-robbery-at-midtown-hotel/Default.aspx">NY1 reports</a> that on Tuesday night, &quot;Investigators say a man carrying a blue duffle bag went up to a hotel clerk, passed a note saying there was a bomb in the bag, and demanded money.&quot;  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140809/http://www.nypost.com/seven/06042009/news/regionalnews/manhattan/bomber_check_inn_172534.htm">The Post says</a> the note read &quot;I have a bomb. Remain calm. Empty your register and no police&quot;&#x2014;the clerk gave him $1,000.  Then yesterday afternoon, the same robber came back, giving a similar note to another clerk, who also complied and gave him $1,000.  Cops are investigating and ask that anyone with information contact <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140809/http://NYPDCrimeStoppers.com/">CrimeStoppers</a> by calling 1-800-577-TIPS or texting TIP577 to CRIMES.</p>
