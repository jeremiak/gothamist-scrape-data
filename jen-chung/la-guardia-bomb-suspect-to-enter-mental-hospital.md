										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140726/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2009_08_mcgann.jpg"> <img alt="2009_08_mcgann.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140726im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2009/08/2009_08_mcgann-thumb-210x254-428565.jpg" width="130" height="157" class="image-right"> </a> </span>Scott McGann, the 32-year-old who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140726/http://gothamist.com/2009/08/01/laguardia_evacuated_after_man_makes.php">caused a lot of chaos</a> at Laguardia Airport a few weeks ago, was found mentally unfit to be arraigned and was instead &quot;r<strong>emanded into the custody of the New York State of Mental Health&apos;s office, where he will be held for a period of one year or until he regains competency</strong>,&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140726/http://www.newsday.com/news/new-york/laguardia-suspect-ruled-unfit-to-face-criminal-charges-1.1376956">according to Newsday</a>.  On August 1, a United ticket agent alert Port Authority officers to McGann, who seemed out of it and inebriated; the officers were <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140726/http://gothamist.com/2009/08/02/laguardia_bomb_threat_suspect_to_un.php">suspicious of the wires coming from his bag</a> and detonator-like device, so they tackled him and the airport&apos;s terminal was shut down. It was later revealed his bag had papers that suggested McGann was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140726/http://gothamist.com/2009/08/06/laguardia_bomb_threat_suspect_indic.php">building a time machine</a> (there were &quot;drawings of a bald man&apos;s head, with the words &apos;Age at 17&apos; above him and the phrases &apos;John Doe&apos; and &apos;Lifespan 35 years&apos; below him,&quot; as well as &quot;a downward arrow pointing at the word &apos;Time,&apos; several other arrows pointing at the words &apos;Dimension 1,&apos; &apos;Dimension 2,&apos; and &apos;Dimension 3&apos;&quot;). McGann allegedly suffers from catatonic schizophrenia; Queens DA Richard Brown said, &quot;<strong>The defendant is clearly a very troubled young man. Under the circumstances, the court&apos;s decision is the right one for all concerned.</strong>&quot;</p>
