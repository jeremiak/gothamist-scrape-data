<p>The police released surveillance video of a man who allegedly attacked a woman in a Queens shop and sexually assaulted her.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred last Saturday, June 22, at 4:50 p.m. on Union Street <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130630065655/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/queens/cops-hunt-man-committed-daytime-rape-queens-article-1.1383701?localLinksEnabled=false">near Northern Boulevard in Flushing</a>. A man, with a firearm in his waistband, &quot;forced his way&quot; into the shop and sexually assaulted a 46-year-old woman. </p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20130630065655if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/ZzMBngzQwTA" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The victim was taken to Queens General Hospital in stable condition. Police are looking for the suspect, who is described as being a man in his 40s, 6&apos;-6&apos;4&quot;, and 220 pounds who was last seen wearing a light blue t-shirt, shorts, sneakers and a baseball cap. <br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130630065655/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577</p>
