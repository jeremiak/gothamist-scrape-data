<p>A man was shot during an apparent robbery at a NJ Transit station this morning. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015218/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Shooting-Train-Station-Elizabeth-New-Jersey-NY-269517211.html">According to NBC New York</a>, &quot;The victim was shot and robbed on the New York-bound platform at the New Jersey Transit station on West Grand Street in Elizabeth at about 4:50 a.m.&quot; </p>

<p>The victim was taken to the hospital. Train service is not affected, but some areas of the platform are cordoned off.</p>

<p>The police have three people&#x2014;two males and one female&#x2014;in custody and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015218/http://7online.com/news/3-in-custody-after-man-shot-at-nj-transit-train-station/232167/">WABC 7 reports</a> that there may be additional suspects. </p>
