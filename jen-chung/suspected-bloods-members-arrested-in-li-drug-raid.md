<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left" style=" width:365px; "> <img alt="2011_06_ligare.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20110601173152im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2011_06_ligare.jpg" width="365" height="274"> <br> <i>FBI agents with a suspect (WCBS 2)</i></div> Federal and local authorities have raided a Long Island apartment complex in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110601173152/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2011/06/01/early-morning-drug-bust-nets-15-on-long-island/">what&apos;s being called a &quot;major drug bust.&quot;</a>  At least 15 have been arrested&#x2014;many of them suspected members of the Bloods&#x2014;in Rockville Centre.<p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110601173152/http://www.newsday.com/long-island/nassau/fbi-gang-member-raid-in-rockville-centre-1.2918705">According to the FBI</a>, the gang &quot;has been controlling the crack cocaine and heroin trade in the area.&quot;  WCBS 2 reports, &quot;The courtyards behind the apartment complex, according to police, were known as &apos;Iraq&apos;and the drug-dealing suspects were known as &apos;The Taliban.&apos;&quot;</p>

<p>Rockville Centre Police Commissioner Chuck Gennario said, &quot;We&#x2019;ve had armed robberies. We&#x2019;ve had shootings. We&#x2019;ve had two homicides and an attempted homicide in the last three years. We have other individuals we&#x2019;ve arrested for homicides. We&#x2019;ve taken four homicide suspects out of here in the last year. This morning was the result of a narcotics investigation. These people have been dealing drugs in this community and in the surrounding communities for quite a long time.&quot;</p>
