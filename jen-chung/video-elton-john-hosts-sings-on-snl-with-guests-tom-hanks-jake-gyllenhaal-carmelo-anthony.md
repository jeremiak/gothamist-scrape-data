</div></div>					<p>Last night, Sir Elton John hosted Saturday Night Live, while also taking a turn as the musical guest with Leon Russell (who was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150409201805/http://gothamist.com/2011/03/15/diamond_waits_inducted_into_rock_an.php">just inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame</a>).
