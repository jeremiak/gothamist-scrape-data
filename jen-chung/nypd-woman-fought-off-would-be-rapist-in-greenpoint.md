<p>The NYPD is asking for the public&apos;s help in identifying a suspect who attempted to rape a woman in Brooklyn yesterday morning. </p>

<p>According to police, at 2:56 a.m. on September 28, a 25-year-old woman was walking near the corner Morgan Avenue and Driggs Streets when she was grabbed from behind. The suspect pushed her against a wall and tried to rape her, police say, but she was able to fend him off <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013235/http://nypost.com/2014/09/29/woman-fights-off-attempted-rapist-by-biting-him/">by biting his arm</a>. The suspect fled on foot.</p>

<p>The NYPD released video of the suspect, describing him as wearing a t-shirt, blue jeans and running sneakers.</p>
