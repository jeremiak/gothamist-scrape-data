title: Peralta Defeats Monserrate For State Senate Seat
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171024140805/http://gothamist.com/attachments/NYC_Billy/2009_09_monserratehl.jpg"> <img alt="2009_09_monserratehl.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171024140805im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2009/09/2009_09_monserratehl-thumb-80x88-443866.jpg" width="90" height="99" class="image-right"> </a> </span>After a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171024140805/http://gothamist.com/2010/03/12/monserrate_heckled_during_debate_tr.php">contentious</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171024140805/http://gothamist.com/2010/03/16/todays_the_day_voters_in.php">nasty</a> battle, Democratic Assemblyman Jose Peralta won the special election for the 13th District in Queens, beating Hiram Monserrate.  Monserrate was voted to the seat in 2008, but his<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171024140805/http://gothamist.com/2010/02/10/state_senate_votes_to_expel_monserr.php"> colleagues voted to expel him</a> last month after his October 2009 <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171024140805/http://gothamist.com/2009/10/15/monserrate_acquitted_on_felony_coun.php">conviction of misdemeanor assault</a> for slashing his girlfriend.  With <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171024140805/http://www.ny1.com/1-all-boroughs-news-content/top_stories/115318/peralta-wins-state-senate-seat-in-queens-special-election">66% of the precincts reporting</a>, Peralta has 65.6% of the vote, Monserrate has 27.14% and Republican candidate Robert Beltrani got 7.21%.  </p>
