										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_04_margoman1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506144421im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_04_margoman1.jpg" width="640" height="401" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>A jewelry store whose clientele includes Selena Gomez, Kate Winslet and Beyonce was robbed yesterday, police say. The NYPD released a video of the suspect who allegedly robbed the Upper East Side store, Margo Manhattan. The owner also added that her employee was choked by the man.</p>

<p>According to the police, it happened at 1:50 p.m. on Friday. A man walked into the store at 1202 Madison Avenue (near 88th Street) and asked to view some jewelry. A 53-year-old female employee began to help him, but then police say, &quot;he grabbed her by the neck and forced her into the back room where he tied her up. The suspect then removed a key from the victim to open a display case and removed jewelry.  He returned to the back room where he removed additional items fled the location with approximately $54,000 worth of jewelry.&quot;</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150506144421if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/-GVZoWDYCs8" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The owner, Margo Manhattan, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506144421/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/upper-east-side-jewelry-store-clients-include-beyonce-robbed-worker-tied-article-1.1753804">told the Daily News</a> that her employee was the only one there and had buzzed the man in: &quot;She thought he was maybe a rap star.&quot; But the employee was viciously attacked&#x2014;Manhattan says that the man had a stun gun: &quot;She came to a point where she saw her life in front of her. He was really forceful. He choked her. He said, &#x2018;I&#x2019;m going to kill you.&#x2019;&quot; </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_04_beyonceblow.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506144421im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_04_beyonceblow.jpg" width="640" height="494" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Manhattan&apos;s jewelry has been worn by numerous celebrities and one stunning necklace was worn by Beyonce in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506144421/http://www.metatube.com/en/videos/217800/Beyonce-Blow-Official-Music-Video-HD/">her video for &quot;Blow.&quot;</a> Of the stolen items, the News says there was &quot;an 18-karat white gold bracelet with pav&#xE9; diamonds worth $10,000. Also nabbed were a $45,000 necklace, a $7,500 bracelet, a $4,500 bracelet and a $2,100 bracelet in a style worn by Beyonc&#xE9;.&quot; Manhattan <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506144421/http://nypost.com/2014/04/11/thief-nets-beyonce-visual-video-jewels-in-brazen-store-heist/">said she doesn&apos;t care about the jewelry</a>, &quot;I&#x2019;d rather them get the jewelry than have them hurt anyone. Their safety is more important. I can replace the jewelry but I can&#x2019;t replace their life.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as wearing a black Brooklyn Nets caps, blue jeans, black T-shirt, black belt with a silver colored buckle and a beaded silver chain.  Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
