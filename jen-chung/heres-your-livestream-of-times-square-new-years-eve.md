<p>For those of you who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170517101403/http://gothamist.com/2016/12/31/nye_in_the_times_square_viewing_pen.php#photo-1">haven&apos;t been shivering in the cold without bathroom access since, oh, 5:40 a.m.</a>, but you like seeing what Times Square looks like on New Year&apos;s Eve, we&apos;ve got your cord-cutting option here: The organizers of the festivities, Times Square Alliance and Countdown Entertainment, have a livestream concert that&apos;s on right now!</p>

<p>This webcast started at 6 p.m., and among the performers tonight are Gavin DeGraw (&quot;I Don&#x2019;t Want To Be&quot;), Rachel Platten (&quot;Fight Song&quot;) and Silent&#xF3; (&quot;Watch Me (Whip/Nae Nae)&quot;). </p>
