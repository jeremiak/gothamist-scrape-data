</div></div>					<p>While this year&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131657/http://gothamist.com/2010/07/04/nathans_hot_dog_eating_contest_is_o.php">Nathan&apos;s Famous Hot Dog Eating Contest</a> threatened to be overshadowed by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131657/http://gothamist.com/2010/07/04/tsunami_strikes_kobayashi_arrested.php">Takeru Kobayashi&apos;s arrest</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131657/http://gothamist.com/2010/07/05/chestnut_wins_hot_dog_eating_contes.php">after the event</a>, two people who enjoyed the display for its pure, human endurance-testing, Coney Island-exciting, 100% beef self were Michael Bisberg and Laura Silver.  The pair won <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131657/http://gothamist.com/2010/07/01/want_vip_passes_to_the_july_4th_hot.php">VIP passes to the contest from us</a> and shared their photographs and observations from the heart of the action.</p>

<p>Laura wrote, &quot;As contestants dunked, shoveled, smooshed, and generally got hot dogs all over their faces, gagging noises abounded,&quot; while Michael noticed how Major League Eating Chairman George Shea &quot;strode the stage and screamed evangelically,&quot; yeling &quot;TODAY WE WILL TRANSCEND!&quot; Read about their experiences&#x2014;complete with vuvuzelas, the &quot; after the jump&#x2014;and stayed tuned to Gothamist through <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131657/http://twitter.com/gothamist">Twitter</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529131657/http://gothamist.com/2010/07/02/pencil_this_in_1006.php">our newsletter</a> for other fun opportunities like this.</p>
