<p>The police are hoping the public can help them find the driver who struck a traffic agent in Brooklyn last week.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident happened on Friday, October 23rd at around 10:42 p.m.: In the vicinity of Washington Avenue and Myrtle Avenue in Clinton Hill, &quot;an on-duty 30-year-old male traffic agent was walking to his marked police vehicle when he was struck by a vehicle that was travelling northbound on Washington Avenue. The vehicle struck the traffic agent causing him to fall to the ground and the vehicle fled the scene.&quot;</p>

<p>The traffic agent was taken to Methodist Hospital in stable condition. </p>

<p>Police released surveillance video of the incident and described the car as a &quot;red or burgundy Volkswagen Passat with passenger side mirror damage.&quot;</p>
