<p>You may have <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/25/where_to_watch_the_debates_without.php">considered going out</a> and watching tonight&apos;s first 2016 presidential election debate in a large group. But then you remembered you will need to be somewhere quiet where you may roll up into a little ball and rock yourself into a calmer state after Donald Trump <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/http://www.insideedition.com/headlines/18876-paula-jones-says-she-would-attend-debate-if-invited-to-make-hillary-nervous">unveils Paula Jones</a> as one of his guests. Or maybe you don&apos;t want to be thrown out of a bar when you try to tear the TV from the wall! Or maybe you just do your best political tweeting in private. So here are some options on where you can stream the debate, starting at 9 p.m. EST, from the intimacy of your own abode.</p>

<p>The NBC live stream on YouTube; Lester Holt is moderating the debate, and the pressure is on him to not <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/08/matt_lauer_president_trump.php">pull a Matt Lauer</a>:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/855Am6ovK7s" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Many other outlets, like PBS and the Washington Post, are a<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/https://youtube.googleblog.com/2016/09/voting-matters-now-register-youtube.html">lso streaming the debate</a> on their YouTube channel. </p>

<p>Bloomberg Politics is streaming the debate on Twitter (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/https://twitter.com/i/live/778347749217406976">here&apos;s the link</a>)&#x2014;and Bloomberg TV says they are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/http://www.politico.com/blogs/on-media/2016/09/bloomberg-to-fact-check-debate-on-screen-228670">offering on-screen fact checking</a> (it&apos;s unclear whether Bloomberg TV and Bloomberg Politics/Twitter will intersect).</p>

<p>ABC News and PBS Newshour will <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/http://www.theverge.com/2016/9/20/12989946/facebook-live-presidential-debate-abc-news-stream">broadcast the debate</a> on Facebook Live&#x2014;here&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/https://www.facebook.com/ABCNews/">ABC News&apos; Facebook</a>, here&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/https://www.facebook.com/newshour">PBS Newshour</a>.</p>

<p>You can always <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/http://lifehacker.com/how-to-stream-tonights-first-presidential-debate-online-1787080213">download apps for CNN and Fox News</a> for your various smartphones and tablets&#x2014;if you have (your parents&apos;) cable subscription</p>

<p>If you want to go the VR route, NBC News <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/http://www.nbcnews.com/politics/elections/virtual-democracy-plaza-join-al-roker-nbcu-talent-free-virtual-n651211">will bring &quot;Democracy Plaza&quot; to everyone</a>.</p>

<p>Have a Roku? <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/https://blog.roku.com/blog/2016/09/24/how-to-stream-the-presidential-debates/">They&apos;ve got you</a>.</p>

<p>Finally, say you just want to tune everything out:  You can stream <em>Armageddon</em> for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020212/https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&amp;rls=en&amp;q=armageddon+movie+streaming&amp;ie=UTF-8&amp;oe=UTF-8">$2.99 on a variety of platforms</a>.</p>
