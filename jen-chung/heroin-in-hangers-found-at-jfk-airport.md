<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505142155/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2010/08/05/feds-seize-56000-in-heroin-at-jfk-airport/">1010WINS reports</a> that $56,000 (street value) worth of heroin was seized from a shipment at JFK Airport.  Described as a &quot;garment sample,&quot; &quot;The shipment arrived Tuesday from Ghaziabad, India and was destined for Detroit, Michigan. Wooden hangers found in the shipment were examined and a white powdery substance found in them tested positive for heroin, officials said.&quot;  This is not as impressive as the $22 million worth of heroin that was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505142155/http://gothamist.com/2003/04/15/heroin_smuggled_in_the_furnitureliterally.php">molded into furniture</a>, but it&apos;s on par with hiding it in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505142155/http://gothamist.com/2006/06/22/flipflips_furni.php">soles of flip-flops</a>.</p>
