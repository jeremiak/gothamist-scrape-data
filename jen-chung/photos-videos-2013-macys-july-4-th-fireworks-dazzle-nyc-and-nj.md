</div></div>					<p>Last night, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503145438/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/03/map_where_to_watch_the_2013_macys_f.php#photo-1">37th Annual Macy&apos;s 4th of July Fireworks</a> painted the sky and delighted millions who caught the display over the Hudson River.</p>

<p>The Macy&apos;s show is the biggest in the country&#x2014;there are 40,000 effects over 25 minutes, with about 1,600 shells launched a minute.
