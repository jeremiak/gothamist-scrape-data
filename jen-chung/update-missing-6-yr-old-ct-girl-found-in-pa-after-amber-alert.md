										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2017_02_aylin.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170227222256im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_02_aylin.jpg" width="211" height="234"> <br> <i style=" width:211px; ;display:block"> Aylin Sofia Hernandez</i></div> Authorities in Connecticut and New York are looking for a girl who went missing after a deadly double stabbing in Bridgeport, CT. It&apos;s believed her father, who is a suspect in the stabbing, took her and that she may be in danger. <b>Update</b>: The girl was found safe and her father was arrested in Pennsylvania. [More details below.]<p></p>

<p>Aylin Sofia Hernandez, 6, is described as having brown hair and brown eyes. She is four feet tall and 55 pounds. </p>

<p>Earlier this morning, Bridgeport police <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170227222256/http://abc7ny.com/news/amber-alert-issued-for-6-year-old-girl;-father-wanted-in-stabbing/1770524/">responded to an incident at a home</a> on Greenwood Street, and found two people stabbed. One of the victims died, and Oscar Hernandez, 39, was named as a suspect. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170227222256/http://fox61.com/2017/02/24/state-police-issue-amber-alert-for-6-year-old-bridgeport-girl/">According to Fox 61</a>, &quot;Police believe [Aylin&apos;s]  26-year-old mother was stabbed to death in what was believed to be a domestic dispute with her father. The mother&#x2019;s female friend is in critical condition and in surgery after the double-stabbing.&quot;</p>

<p>Oscar Hernandez was apparently spotted this morning on Jennings Road in the Bronx around 6:45 a.m.</p>
