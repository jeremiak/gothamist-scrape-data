<p>Police are hoping the public can help them find a man who allegedly told a stranger in Central Park that his 11-year-old son was available for sex. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred Thursday, April 24th, at around 10:30 p.m., inside the park near the Bow Bridge and West 74th Street: &quot;The victim, an 11-year-old male was with his father in the vicinity of Bow Bridge and West 74th Street, when the child&apos;s father approached a 50-year-old male and offered his son for a sex act in exchange for an undetermined amount of money. The male declined and called police to report the incident.&quot;</p>

<p>The authorities describe the victim&apos;s father as a while male about 36 years old, 5&apos;7&quot; tall and 160 pounds, with brown hair and a medium build. He was last seen wearing a dark colored winter coat with a fur hood and dark pants. The victim was described as being a boy with a slim build and wearing a red or orange winder breaker type of coat. </p>

<p>The suspect is wanted for attempted child luring. Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or for Spanish 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161006141706/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
