title: NYPD: This Guy Shoved A 69-Year-Old Man Down The Subway Stairs
---

	
										<p>Investigators are looking for a person accused of violently shoving an elderly man down a set of a stairs at a Queens subway station last week. </p>

<p>The incident occurred on Wednesday, July 6th, at 1:30 p.m., at the 36th Street M/R station in Long Island City. Police say the man they&apos;re looking for pushed the 69-year-old male victim down a set of stairs. Apparently the victim and his assailant did not exchange words before assault. </p>

<p>The victim was treated for a fractured knee at Mt. Sinai Hospital.  Police released video footage of the suspect, described as being 25 to 30 years old and 6&apos;0&quot; with a heavy set build and a bald head. He was last seen wearing a gray t-shirt and black pants.</p>
