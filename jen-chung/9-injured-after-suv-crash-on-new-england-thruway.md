title: 9 Injured After SUV Crash On New England Thruway
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_04_nethru.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509135153im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_04_nethru.jpg" width="640" height="355"> <br> <i> Via PIX 11</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Nine people were seriously or critically injured after an SUV <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509135153/http://pix11.com/2014/04/25/crash-injures-at-least-9-on-bronx-highway/">crashed into a wall </a>on the New England Thruway in the Bronx earlier this morning. </p>

<p>The FDNY says they responded to a call that came in at 5:13 a.m., about an accident near the Connor Street exit. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509135153/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9516133">WABC 7 reports</a> the vehicle, a Cadillac Escalade, &quot;veered out of control and slammed head-on into a wall near the exit ramp.&quot; </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_04_nethru2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509135153im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_04_nethru2.jpg" width="640" height="360"> <br> <i> Via <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509135153/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/04/25/9-injured-in-crash-on-new-england-thruway-in-the-bronx/">WCBS 2</a></i></div> <p></p>

<p>According to the FDNY, the nine victims were all adults&#x2014;all in the same SUV&#x2014;and all were in serious or critical condition. They were taken to Jacobi Hospital. <br>
