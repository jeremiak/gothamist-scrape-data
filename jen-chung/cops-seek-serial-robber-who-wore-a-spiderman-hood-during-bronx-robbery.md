title: Cops Seek Serial Robber Who Wore A Spiderman Hood During Bronx Robbery
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_04_spiderman1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170429195933im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_04_spiderman1.jpg" width="640" height="460" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police believe the same person is behind an attempted robbery in East Harlem and then a robbery at a Bronx bank the next day. During the bank robbery, the suspect made sure to cover up his face with a Spiderman hood or mask.<br>
 <br>
<span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_04_suspim.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170429195933im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_04_suspim.jpg" width="300" height="295" class="image-right"> </span>The first incident occurred on Monday, April 24, around 10:15 a.m. at the East Harlem Pharmacy at 2371 2nd Avenue. </p>

<p>The NYPD says that &quot;an unidentified male jumped over the counter top, where he displayed a box cutter and demanded drugs. The employee refused and the unidentified individual fled to parts unknown.&quot;</p>

<p>The following day, around 12:50 p.m., authorities say that the suspect entered the HSBC Bank at 1 East Fordham Road in the Bronx &quot;with his face exposed, approached a teller and handed a note demanding money.&quot;</p>

<p>The teller agreed and then the suspect then &quot;covered his face with a Spiderman hood/mask and fled on foot with the money,&quot; police said.  </p>

<p>The suspect is described as being about 5&apos;5&quot; and 145-150 pounds. He was was last seen wearing a black jacket, a black pants and, in the bank robbery, a Spiderman mask.<br>
