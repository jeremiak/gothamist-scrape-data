<p>The police are hoping the public can help them identify a man who is accused of groping two women and two girls in Queens on Tuesday. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the suspect first approached the victims, tried to speak with them and then &quot;touched their buttocks over their clothing.&quot; Here&apos;s a list of where the police say the suspect struck:<br>
 <br>
- At around 6:40 a.m., he approached a 23-year-old woman at Northern Boulevard and 57th Street. <br>
 <br>
- At 7:43 a.m., he approached at 25-year-old woman at 32-87 Steinway Street, at a Rite-Aid (the surveillance photographs above are from this incident).</p>

<p>- At 8 a.m., he approached a 16-year-old girl at 38-01 35 Avenue. <br>
 <br>
- At 8:26 a.m., he approached a 14-year-old girl at 37-15 31 Avenue.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as being 25-30 years old, 6&apos;0&quot; and  220-250 pounds. Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto Crime Stoppers&apos; website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427151047/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.<br>
