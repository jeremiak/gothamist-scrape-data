										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2012_01_obad.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312172225im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_01_obad.jpg" width="640" height="395" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Days after the Koch brothers&apos;-funded PAC <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312172225/http://thehill.com/video/campaign/204357-koch-backed-group-spends-6-million-on-anti-obama-solyndra-ad">released an ad critical</a> of President Obama and Solyndra, Obama&apos;s re-election campaign has released its first TV ad to fight back.</p>

<p>As <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312172225/http://thecaucus.blogs.nytimes.com/2012/01/18/obama-fights-back-against-koch-brothers-in-new-ad/?hp">The Caucus reports</a>, &quot;The Obama campaign responded hastily. It placed orders for television time on Wednesday, telling broadcast stations that it wanted the advertising on the air immediately. Typically, stations require more than a day&#x2019;s notice.&quot;</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312172225if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/sq3GGwgV7R0?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>A voiceover says, &quot;Secretive oil billionaires attacking President Obama with ads fact checkers say are not tethered to the facts&quot;&#x2014;a reference to the billionaire, Tea Party-loving <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312172225/http://gothamist.com/2011/02/22/insanely_rich_koch_bros_demand_fisc.php">Koch brothers</a> who have funded the PAC Americans for Prosperity. The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312172225/http://www.google.com/hostednews/ap/article/ALeqM5gmYn4tsm8756VskgcS1eImS0yPbg?docId=dd19ea2b9b0941c5a07f460825f41d3f">ad mentions</a> how Obama has created 2.7 million clean energy jobs, reduced dependence on foreign oil and says his record on ethics is &quot;unprecedented,&quot; ending with, &quot;President Obama. Kept his promise to toughen ethics rules and strengthen America&apos;s energy economy.&quot;</p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312172225/http://www.bloomberg.com/news/2012-01-19/obama-campaign-set-to-run-first-campaign-advertising-in-six-swing-states.html">ads begin running today</a> in Iowa, Michigan, North Carolina, Ohio, Virginia and Wisconsin.&quot;<br>
</p>
