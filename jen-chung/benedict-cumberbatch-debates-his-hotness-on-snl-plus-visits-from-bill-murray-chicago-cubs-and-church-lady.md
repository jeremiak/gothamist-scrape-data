</div></div>					<p>Benedict Cumberbatch has already won a legion of Cumberbitches as Sherlock Holmes and elevated a sort of second-tier Marvel Comics superhero <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016014106/http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/weekend-box-office-doctor-strange-heads-huge-80m-pus-bow-326m-friday-944438">to box office A-list</a>. So maybe it was too much to hope for him to have a killer <em>Saturday Night Live</em>.</p>

<p>The best sketches were about himself, his characters, and dedicated fans&#x2014;in a game show called &quot;Why Is Benedict Cumberbatch Hot?&quot; the contestants, himself included, had to answer that question. Cumberbatch declared that he looks like a lizard man and Sid the Sloth from <em>Ice Age</em>. &quot;Wow, so striking and charming&quot; and humble.</p>

<p>The other standout sketch was a digital short called &quot;Office Hours,&quot; which had Cumberbatch as a professor and Pete Davidson playing an easy-going stoner student he&apos;s become attached to.</p>

<p>The rest of the time, SNL had Cumberbatch take on the roles of stripper (and get his freak on with a the corpse of a grandmother); non-conformist (when it comes to toilets); and a criminal mastermind. Click through to see the sketches, including The Church Lady&apos;s return to Weekend Update.</p>

<p><img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016014106im_/http://gothamist.com/upload/2016/11/2016_11_snlbc_wucubs.jpg" width="640" height="427" alt="2016_11_snlbc_wucubs.jpg"></p>

<p>There were a lot of appearances by the 2016 World Series champion Chicago Cubs&#x2014;David Ross, Anthony Rizzo, Dexter Fowler. and honorary Cub Bill Murray. You&apos;d think they <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016014106/http://chicagoist.com/2016/11/03/the_night_belongs_to_these_out-of-t.php#photo-1">hadn&apos;t won a World Series in 108 years</a> or something. </p>
