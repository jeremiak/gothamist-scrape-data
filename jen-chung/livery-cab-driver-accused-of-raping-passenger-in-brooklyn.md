                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_09_livcabrape.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130926075823im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_09_livcabrape.jpg" width="365" height="209" class="image-right"> </span>The police have released photos of a man suspected of raping a woman in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130926075823/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/precincts/precinct_067.shtml">67th Precinct</a> last week. And the suspect is a livery cab driver.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on Tuesday, September 17 at about 5 a.m., a 35-year-old woman hailed a SUV livery cab. When she was inside, she was raped by the livery cab operator. She was able to got out of the car around East 95th Street and Avenue B. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as a man around 20-25 years old, about 6&apos;2&quot; and 175-210 pounds. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130926075823/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
