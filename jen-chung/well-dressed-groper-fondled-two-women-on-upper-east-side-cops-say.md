---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2012_02_uesgroper.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160706003403im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_02_uesgroper.jpg" width="300" height="238" class="image-left"> </span>Police are looking for a man suspected of groping two women on the Upper East Side <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160706003403/http://www.dnainfo.com/20120229/upper-east-side/well-dressed-man-groped-two-women-on-ues-police-say">in separate incidents</a>&#x2014;and they released surveillance video that show the suspect as being &quot;neatly dressed.&quot;</p>

<p>The first incident occurred on February 25:  A 33-year-old woman was walking at Park Avenue and East 62nd Street at 12:30 p.m. when the suspect grabbed her inner thigh.  Then, two days later, at 1:55 p.m., he grabbed a 19-year-old woman&apos;s buttocks at Second Avenue and East 67th Street.  </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160706003403/http://www.myfoxny.com/dpp/news/upper-east-side-groper-20120229-akd">According to police&apos;s description</a>, the suspect is white, clean shaven, neatly dressed, with brown hair, about 30 years old, 5 feet 11 inches tall, and 180 pounds.</p>
