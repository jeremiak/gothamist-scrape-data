<p>Somewhere out there in the city, there&apos;s a new <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131203062317/http://gothamist.com/tags/banksy">Banksy</a> piece. However, it&apos;s not the stencil of a doctor with an &quot;I [Fart] NY&quot; sign. Because that&apos;s fake!</p>

<p>The image popped on Twitter yesterday, but it&apos;s clearly a Photoshop of a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131203062317/http://gothamist.com/2010/05/17/banksy_arrives_in_new_york.php#photo-5">2010 Banksy stencil in the Financial District</a>.<br>

<p>It&apos;s a pretty half-hearted Photoshop&#x2014;street art aficionado Nic Garcia points out &quot;The Musical&quot; doesn&apos;t have the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131203062317/http://gothamist.com/2013/10/04/new_banksy_goes_up_in_bushwick.php">3D effect the others have</a> (plus &quot;The Musical&quot; is applied to existing graffiti in the neighborhood). </p>

<p>Also, the humor is a little too juvenile, even for Banksy. Of course, next he&apos;ll do a &quot;Who cut the cheese?&quot; coming out of one of his rats&apos; mouths or something. </p>

<p>Happy hunting for the new Banksy&#x2014;it&apos;s apparently on a moving truck!</p>
