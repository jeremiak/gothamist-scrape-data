										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150424141708/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_06_5trainperv.jpg"> <img alt="2014_06_5trainperv.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150424141708im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2014/06/2014_06_5trainperv-thumb-300x348-847427.jpg" width="250" height="290" class="image-right"> </a> </span>The police hope the public can help them find a man suspected of public lewdness in the Bronx last month.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on May 11, at 10:58, a 42-year-old woman and a 10-year-old girl got on a southbound 5 train at Dyre Avenue. A man also got on the train, and once on board, he exposed himself to the woman and girl. Then, two stops later at the Gun Hill Road station, the he ran away.</p>

<p>The police describe the suspect as a man in his 50s, about 6&apos;6&quot; tall and weighing 245 pounds. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this public lewdness is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150424141708/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
