										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_04_suspcet19th.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150915040710im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_04_suspcet19th.jpg" width="250" height="183" class="image-right"> </span>The NYPD released surveillance video of a man who allegedly tried to steal a woman&apos;s cellphone and money&#x2014;and then sexually assaulted her. </p>

<p>The incident occurred on April 6th, at 2 a.m., on East 27th Street near Third Avenue. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150915040710/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/manhattan/sicko_sex_assault_on_woman_walking_lkVY4AXacx8orr7PswAJwM">According to the Post</a>, the 28-year-old victim had &quot;returned home with her boyfriend after a night out on the town and took her dog for a stroll, sources said.&quot; According to police, when she was outside, a man approached her, showed a black handgun and demanded her money and cellphone. When she told him that she didn&apos;t have anything on her, the man then allegedly sexually abused her. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as a black male, 6&apos;, heavy-set with a bald head. He was wearing a dark-colored hooded sweatshirt and bubble vest. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150915040710/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
