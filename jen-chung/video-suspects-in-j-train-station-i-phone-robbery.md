<p>Cops are asking for the public&apos;s help in identifying two men who allegedly robbed a subway passenger at the Canal Street J train station.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on March 24, at about 10:30 p.m., the two men approached a 21-year-old man, displayed a gun and took the victim&apos;s iPhone. </p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130515065358if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/b44IeanfUTE" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspects are described as a Hispanic male in his 20s, wearing a green coat and black hat, and a black male in his 20s, wearing a black coat and a red hooded sweatshirt.</p>

<p>Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto Crime Stoppers&apos; website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130515065358/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.<br>
