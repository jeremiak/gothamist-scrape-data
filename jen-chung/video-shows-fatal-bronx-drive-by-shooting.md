<p>A 17-year-old boy was killed during a drive-by shooting in the Bronx on Wednesday and now the police have released surveillance footage of violence, in hopes the public can help identify a suspect.</p>

<p>The shooting took place on August 17 at around 9:19 p.m. The NYPD says officers responded to a 911 call around 9:19 p.m. about two people shot in front of 1375 College Avenue: &quot;Upon arrival officers discovered a 17-year-old male with a gunshot wound to the chest and a 21-year-old male with a gunshot wound to the stomach.&quot; The teen, Dontay Gordon, died at Lincoln Hospital while the 21-year-old was critically injured.  </p>
