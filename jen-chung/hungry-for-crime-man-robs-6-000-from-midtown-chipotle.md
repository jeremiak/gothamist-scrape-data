title: Hungry For Crime: Man Robs $6,000 From Midtown Chipotle
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_04_chipotlechump.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428131426im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_04_chipotlechump.jpg" width="300" height="214" class="image-right"> </span>The police are hoping the public can help them identify a man behind a gunpoint robbery of a Park Avenue Chipotle.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, last Thursday, April 25, at around 11:55 p.m., a man entered the Chipotle at 320 Park Avenue, demanding money. He had a silver handgun and &quot;forced one of the employees to restrain his co-worker with zip ties during the Robbery,&quot; police say.  The suspect left with $6,000 in cash.<br>
 <br>
He&apos;s described as a man about 6&apos;-6&apos;1&quot;, 200-225 pounds and wearing a black Northface jacket, black hooded sweatshirt and a black winter hat with a white emblem on the front.  He also wore white gloves with red palms. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regard to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (5477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428131426/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
