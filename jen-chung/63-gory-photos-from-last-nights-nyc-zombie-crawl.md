                            <p>Were you wandering through Times Square yesterday and suddenly accosted by a group of marauding zombies? Don&apos;t panic&#x2014;those zombies were affiliated with the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140227100906/http://www.nyczombiecrawl.com/">7th Annual NYC Zombie Crawl</a>.</p>

<p>The crawl, a way to bid good-bye to NY Comic Con, began at Twins Pub and McGarry&apos;s Pub (on 9th Avenue between 33rd Street and 34th Street). That&apos;s where &quot;zombie technicians&quot; helped the undead look a little undeader. Then the group left the bars, in search of fresh brains from tourists and the unlucky in Times Square. Finally, there was dancing and air hockey.</p>

<p>Check out these photographs from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140227100906/http://nadiachaudhury.com/">Nadia Chaudhury</a>, who somehow made it out alive. </p>
