title: Video: Brooklyn Food Deliveryman Violently Beaten, Robbed Of iPhone, Cash And Food
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_03_fooddelrob.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212127im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_03_fooddelrob.jpg" width="640" height="420" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The police released surveillance video of suspects who allegedly beat and robbed a food deliveryman on Saturday in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509212127/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/precincts/precinct_071.shtml">Brooklyn&apos;s 71st Precinct</a>, which covers Crown Heights and Prospect Lefferts Gardens.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on March 22 at 9 p.m., a 44-year-old male deliveryman headed to 305 Clarkson Avenue to make a delivery. However, upon arrival, five people exited the building and started punching and kicking the him in the head. The police say the suspects &quot;removed the victim&apos;s Apple I-Phone, $500, personal items, and food.&quot;</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150509212127if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/tVtpzE8hMHM" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The victim was treated at Kings County Hospital for his injuries, including a broken nose, facial lacerations and head trauma. </p>

<p>The suspects are described as five males, with short black hair. Anyone with information in regard to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
