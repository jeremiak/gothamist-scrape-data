										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_02_attack.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170213182425im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_02_attack.jpg" width="640" height="501" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>A 22-year-old man walking in East New York was attacked by a group of suspects, and now police are hoping the public can help identify them.</p>

<p>The incident occurred around 6:30 p.m. on Wednesday, February 8, when the victim was in front of 331 Crescent Street. Police say he &quot;was followed by four individuals, who inquired if the victim was a gang member. The victim declined the question and the individuals punched him in the face and used a box cutter on the left side of his face.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspects fled and the victim was taken to Brookdale Hospital for treatment.</p>

<p>The suspects are described as four males, around 20 years old. <br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or for Spanish 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170213182425/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
