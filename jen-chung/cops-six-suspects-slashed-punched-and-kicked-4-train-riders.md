title: Cops: Six Suspects Slashed, Punched And Kicked 4 Train Riders
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_10_19assault.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013036im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_10_19assault.jpg" width="640" height="444" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The police say that a group of six people attacked two passengers on a 4 train early yesterday morning.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 3:30 a.m. on Sunday, October 5. Two men, one 20-year-old and one 23-year-old, were on a northbound 4 train approaching East 96th Street when they got into an argument with a group. The police say that &quot;led to [the suspects] attacking both victims.&quot; The first victim, the 20-year-old, was slashed in the face while the other victim was punched and kicked numerous times. </p>

<p>Both victims were taken to St. Luke&apos;s Hospital, where the first victim received stitches and the second victim refused medical attention. </p>

<p>Police released video of the suspects, describing two suspects as males about 18 to 23 years old and the other four suspects as females about 16 to 22 years old. </p>
