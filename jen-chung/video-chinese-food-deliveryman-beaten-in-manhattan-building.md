title: Video: Chinese Food Deliveryman Beaten In Manhattan Building
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2011_04_chinesedelivery.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150522110901im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2011_04_chinesedelivery.jpg" width="365" height="275" class="image-left"> </span>Police released video of a Chinese food deliveryman <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150522110901/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=8096227">being beaten by three men</a> in a Morningside Heights apartment building&apos;s hallway; a fourth man acts as a lookout. The assault occurred on Friday afternoon; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150522110901/http://manhattan.ny1.com/content/top_stories/138100/police-seek-four-suspects-in-beating-of-manhattan-delivery-man">according to NY1</a>, &quot;Police say they ordered Chinese food, then waited outside the sixth-floor elevator for the deliveryman to arrive.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspects tried to rob the deliveryman, while kicking and punching him, but <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150522110901/http://www.dnainfo.com/20110427/harlem/teens-ambush-chinese-food-deliveryman-morningside-heights">ran away before taking his wallet</a>. Police say the suspects, who are between 15 and 19 years old, ran to Morningside Park. </p>

<p>There are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150522110901/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2011/04/27/brutal-food-delivery-man-attack-caught-on-tape-in-morningside-heights/">clear pictures of the suspects from the video</a>; a resident of the building, 40 Morningside Avenue, told WCBS 2, &quot;I&#x2019;m ashamed as a citizen. People thought they could get away with it.&quot; The deliveryman suffered facial injuries from the attack.</p>

<p>Food deliverymen have been targets before: In 2008, a Chinese food deliveryman was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150522110901/http://gothamist.com/2008/09/29/chinese_food_delivery_man_ambushed.php">fatally shot in the Bronx</a> and in 2004, two teens <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150522110901/http://gothamist.com/2005/03/07/big_trials_around_the_city.php">killed a Queens Chinese food deliveryman</a>.</p>
