<p>The police are hoping the public can help them identify a person suspected of stealing an old lady&apos;s purse&#x2014;while she was being pushed in a wheelchair!</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the grand larceny occurred on Sunday, June 29th, at 1 p.m. A 98-year-old woman in a wheelchair was with her home attendant, walking along Ocean Parkway. When they got to Church Avenue, police say that a cyclist rode past them, grabbed the senior citizen&apos;s purse out of her lap and kept biking away. Here&apos;s video of the suspect:</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150406153533/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
