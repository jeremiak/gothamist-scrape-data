										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_01_deicig.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015528im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_01_deicig.jpg" width="640" height="403" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are looking for an armed suspect who is accused of robbing a deli of one cigarette. </p>

<p>The incident occurred last week, on January 3rd, at around 8:15 p.m. Authorities say the male suspect &quot;entered a grocery store located at 267 East Tremont Avenue, engaged in a verbal dispute and physical altercation with two patrons inside the store which spilled out into the street. The individual then re-entered the store brandishing a black and silver firearm, demanding the store employee give him a cigarette.&quot;</p>

<p>The employee gave him the cigarette and the suspect fled. No one was injured. </p>

<p>Video shows the suspect, who is described as being about 20 years old, 6&apos;5&quot; and 150 pounds with brown eyes and black hair; and last seen wearing a gray hooded sweatshirt, black sneakers and black sweat pants: </p>
