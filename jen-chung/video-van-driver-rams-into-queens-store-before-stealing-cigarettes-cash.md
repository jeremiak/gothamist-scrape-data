										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_01_vanstore.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015322im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_01_vanstore.jpg" width="640" height="408" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>A thief used his van as a battering ram to break into a Queens store and steal cigarettes and cash early Monday morning. Now investigators are releasing video of the burglary and suspect in hopes the public can help ID him.</p>

<p>The incident occurred around 4 a.m. on Monday, January 23rd. According to the NYPD, &quot;The individual crashed a white van into the front gate of 82-31 153 Avenue&quot;&#x2014;Village Card and Gift&#x2014;&quot;and drove away. The individual then entered the location after he approached again on foot, and removed cigarettes and $750 cash.&quot;</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015322if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/QM56_KP27Qk" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspect is described as being about 25 years old and 190 pounds with black hair in cornrows. He was last seen wearing blue jeans. <br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015322/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
