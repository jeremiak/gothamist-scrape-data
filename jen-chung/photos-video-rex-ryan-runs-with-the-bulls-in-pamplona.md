                            <p>Have you ever wondered what it would look like if the coach of the New York Jets and a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820085357/http://gothamist.com/2013/01/05/jets_players_had_no_idea_about_rex.php">Pretty Feet fan</a> risked his life in Spain by joining the running of the bulls? Well now there are photos and video to prove that while Rex Ryan may be a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820085357/http://gothamist.com/2011/02/27/jets_wont_be_back_on_hard_knocks_th.php">pushover for snacks</a>, he doesn&apos;t want to be gored by a bull.</p>

<p>An <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820085357/http://deadspin.com/wheres-rex-photos-of-a-terrified-rex-ryan-running-wit-709539437">eagle-eyed Deadspin reader noticed Ryan</a> in a gallery of pictures from the annual event. And then someone found video! </p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20130820085357if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/0rq3SuHIAeU" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The Jets confirmed that Ryan ran with the bulls TWICE. He was also seen wearing a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130820085357/http://www.nydailynews.com/sports/football/jets/rex-works-running-game-plays-dodge-bull-vacation-article-1.1393481?localLinksEnabled=false">shirt that said &quot;Bad Toro&quot; on it</a> during the weekend of revelry.</p>
