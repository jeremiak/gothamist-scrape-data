<p>The police are searching for a man accused of menacing a Brooklyn subway rider with a knife on Sunday.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_11_menae.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160308034323im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_11_menae.jpg" width="280" height="230" class="image-right"> </span>According to the NYPD, on Sunday, November 1st, at about 5 a.m., a 19-year-old woman was on the northbound platform of the Smith-9th Street F train subway station when the suspect &quot;displayed a knife to her.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect then fled, but police released surveillance images of him and described him as about 25 to 30 years old, 5&apos;4&quot; and 180 pounds, with braids and was last seen wearing a black vest, gray sweater with white stripes, black jeans, and red sneakers.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160308034323/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
