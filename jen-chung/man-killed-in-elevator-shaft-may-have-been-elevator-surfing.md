title: Man Killed In Elevator Shaft May Have Been "Elevator Surfing"
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2014_07_tmorales.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015321im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_07_tmorales.jpg" width="240" height="308"> <br> <i style=" width:240px; ;display:block"> Tino Morales</i></div> The young man who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015321/http://gothamist.com/2014/07/28/man_dies_after_bring_trapped_betwee.php">died after getting trapped in a Bronx building&apos;s elevator shaft</a> yesterday morning was identified as 20-year-old Tino Morales. And police are now investigating whether he had been &quot;surfing&quot; on the elevator.<p></p>

<p>Morales was trapped between the elevator car and the wall of the shaft, between the 18th and 19th floors at 680 Tinton Avenue, part of the NYC Housing Authority&apos;s Adams Houses. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015321/http://nypost.com/2014/07/28/man-found-dead-in-public-housing-elevator/">The Post reports</a>, &quot;Sources said Morales was riding on top of the elevator as it went down the shaft &#x2014; although his girlfriend claimed that he climbed on top only after it got stuck.&quot; Jessica Sanchez said, &quot;My sister saw everything. She said it was horrible. She saw his body dangling. When the elevator went up, he flew up and then he fell.&quot;</p>

<p>Numerous residents said the elevators were unreliable and get stuck; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510015321/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/bronx/man-crushed-death-elevator-shaft-bronx-public-housing-building-article-1.1882404">one said to the Daily News</a> that they were &quot;always shaking.&quot;</p>

<p>Morales, who was pronounced dead at the scene, was an aspiring tattoo artist. Sanchez said, &quot;Before he left, he was like, &#x2018;I love you baby.&apos; He was just telling me he really really loves me.&quot;</p>
