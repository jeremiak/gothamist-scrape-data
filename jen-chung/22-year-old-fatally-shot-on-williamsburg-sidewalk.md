<p>A 22-year-old man has died after being shot in Williamsburg this morning. He apparently collapsed while calling 911.</p>

<p>The NYPD says officers responded at 1:28 a.m. to a 911 call about a man assaulted at North 6th Street and Kent Avenue. After they arrived, police say they found a man &quot;with gunshot wounds to his chest and right leg.&quot; The victim, who has not been identified yet, died at Woodhull Hospital.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_09_wmbrgshooting.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113153408im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_09_wmbrgshooting.jpg" width="640" height="360"> <br> <i> Via <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113153408/http://abc7ny.com/news/22-year-old-shot-killed-in-williamsburg/1499914/">WABC 7</a></i></div> <p></p>

<p>The victim was shot in front of what <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113153408/http://pix11.com/2016/09/06/22-year-old-man-shot-in-williamsburg/">PIX 11 says</a> is a &quot;waterfront residential high-rise building&quot; near Kent and North 6th: &quot;After he was struck twice &#x2014; in the leg and chest &#x2014; he ran toward North Seventh Street where he pulled out his cellphone and called 911 before collapsing. When cops arrived, the man was struggling to stay conscious while the call to the dispatchers was still connected, police said.&quot; </p>
