                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_04_suspcihsa.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20131005224013im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_04_suspcihsa.jpg" width="640" height="440" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The police released a photograph of a man who is wanted for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131005224013/http://gothamist.com/2013/04/14/woman_sexually_assaulted_by_man_nea.php">trying to rape a woman yesterday morning</a> just steps from City Hall and blocks from 1 Police Plaza.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the 28-year-old victim was followed into <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131005224013/http://www.scoutingny.com/?p=1645">Theatre Alley</a>, where the suspect &quot;threw her to the ground and attempted to rape her.&quot; The suspect apparently pulled down her underwear, and the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131005224013/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/cops-hunt-city-hall-perv-article-1.1316295?localLinksEnabled=false">Daily News reported</a> that he &quot;penetrated with his finger and told her not to scream.&quot; </p>

<p>She did scream, drawing the attention of construction workers. The suspect fled and the victim was was taken to Beth Israel hospital where she is in stable condition.</p>

<p>The police released photo of the suspect, who is wearing a red American Eagle hooded sweatshirt, a Yankees cap and huge earphones. Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131005224013/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
