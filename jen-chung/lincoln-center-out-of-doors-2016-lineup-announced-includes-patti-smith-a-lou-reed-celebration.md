<p>It doesn&apos;t feel like summer is around the corner, but it is, and Lincoln Center is announcing its <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108032849/http://www.lcoutofdoors.org/">Lincoln Center Out of Doors lineup</a> of free performances. Patti Smith will be kicking things off on July 20th, offering &quot;A Night of Words and Music.&quot;</p>

<p>Other performer include: Ladysmith Black Mambazo on July 21st; Darlene Love on July 23rd; Salsa duo Richie Ray and Bobby Cruz on July 24th; AFROPUNK&apos;s Girrrl Riot on July 27th; Nigerian pop star Davido and Antibalas on July 29th; flamenco company Soledad Barrio &amp; Noche Flamenca on August 3rd (it&apos;s a specially commissioned work); and, for the final event on August 13th, the premiere of David Lang&apos;s &quot;the public domain,&quot; a co-presentation from the Mostly Mozart Festival, which is  &quot;a site-specific work performed by a chorus of up to 1,000 volunteer amateur and professional singers on Josie Robertson Plaza.&quot;</p>

<p>July 23rd is Family Day, with performances from Illstyle &amp; Peace and Dance Theatre of Harlem, and between July 7th and August 7th there will be 15 &quot;micro-concerts&quot; (they are 15 minutes long) from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108032849/http://mostlymozart.org/events?series=international-contemporary-ensemble">Mostly Mozart Artists-in Residence International Contemporary Ensemble</a>.</p>

<p>Also, on July 30th, there&apos;s &quot;The Bells: A Daylong Celebration of Lou Reed;&quot; the press release notes the program is &quot;featuring a mass tai-chi session, a sound installation, afternoon concerts, readings, and an evening concert in Damrosch Park followed by the late-night screening of Julian Schnabel&apos;s film Berlin. The special tribute to the late musician and consummate New Yorker is curated by Reed&apos;s wife, Laurie Anderson, and Hal Willner, his longtime producer.&quot;</p>

<p>All performances at at Lincoln Center; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108032849/http://aboutlincolncenter.org/press-room/release/938?category_id=recent">see the schedule here</a>.</p>
