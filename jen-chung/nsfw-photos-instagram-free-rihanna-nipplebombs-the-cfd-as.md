					<p>Nearly a month after Instagram <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161012141256/http://www.eonline.com/news/538815/what-happened-to-rihanna-s-instagram-account">pulled her account by mistake</a> after too many provocative (naked) pictures, Rihanna has struck back&#x2014;by arriving to the glittery CFDA Awards basically nude. She wore a custom-made &quot;dress&quot; covered in 230,000 Swarovski crystals <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161012141256/http://www.nytimes.com/2013/03/03/fashion/adam-selman-dresses-rihanna-for-the-stage.html?_r=0">by Adam Selman</a>. And it looks like there was some underwear. Thong underwear.</p>

<p>Rihanna, who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161012141256/http://www.bet.com/news/music/2014/05/21/rihanna-confirms-she-quit-instagram.html">abandoned Instagram after the dustup</a>, was receiving the Icon Award from the CFDA (Council of Fashion Designers of America) for being &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161012141256/http://cfda.com/the-latest/introducing-the-2014-cfda-fashion-icon-of-the-year">fashion&#x2019;s most exciting ambassador in recent memory.</a>&quot; (Fashion designers clamored to give her <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161012141256/http://www.nytimes.com/2014/05/29/fashion/rihanna-style-stylist-mel-ottenberg.html">front row seats to their shows</a> at Paris Fashion Week.) And she did not fail in titillating the crowd AND pleasing the awards&apos; co-sponsor... Swarkovski crystals.</p>

<p>Vogue editor-in-chief Anna Wintour presented her with the award, and Rihanna&#x2014;who showed off her butt on the way to the stage&#x2014;thanked the audience and her inspirations. She called fashion a &quot;defense mechanism&quot; and said it was full of &quot;thrills... There are rules&#x2014;but rules are meant to be broken!&quot; In a backstage interview, she added, &quot;It&apos;s every little girl&apos;s dream to dress up.&quot;</p>
