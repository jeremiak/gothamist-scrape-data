</div></div>					<p>Tina Fey returned to her old stomping grounds at Saturday Night Live to host the show for a second time.  As expected, Fey portrayed former VP candidate Sarah Palin as a bugging media mogul (The Sarah Palin Network with shows like Tea Party Wheel of Fortune and Are You Smarter Than a Half-Term Governor).  She also did a segment on Weekend Update about women&apos;s issues, mentioning John Boehner (the &quot;biggest bitch&quot; in D.C.!) and the so-called Oscar curse where Best Actress winners&apos; spouses cheat of them...man, did she <em>rip</em> into Michelle McGee, aka the other extremely tattooed woman in Sandra Bullock and Jesse James&apos; marriage.</p>
