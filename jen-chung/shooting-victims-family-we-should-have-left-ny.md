title: Shooting Victim's Family: "We Should Have Left NY"
---

<p><img alt="2008_05_queensshooting.JPG" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811084513im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2008_05_queensshooting.JPG" width="245" height="200" class="right">The family of the 15-year-old girl who was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811084513/http://gothamist.com/2008/05/18/gunplay_in_quee.php">fatally shot in front of a Queens housing project</a> says they had moved out of the project to escape the violence.  Brandon Bethea was killed late Saturday night when she was visiting friends at the Redfern Houses; she had been dancing with friends in a courtyard.  </p>

<p>Police say Bethea <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811084513/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2008/05/19/2008-05-19_teen_bystander_killed_in_same_queens_pro.html">was an innocent bystander</a> when the gunfire rang out, and her brother thinks it was a gang turf war, telling the Daily News, &quot;They&apos;ve been going at it for years.&quot;   Her stepfather Robert Drakeford said they moved to Jamaica because Brandon&apos;s friend was killed by a stray bullet, &quot;<strong>I guess we should have moved further. We should have left New York altogether</strong>.&#x201D;  </p>

<p>Her <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811084513/http://www.nypost.com/seven/05192008/news/regionalnews/grief_over_prom_teen_111503.htm">mother grieved</a>, &quot;We were supposed to be shopping for a prom dress, but now we&apos;re going to be shopping for a coffin.&quot; The police <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110811084513/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=6149933">are still looking</a> for the shooter.  </p>
