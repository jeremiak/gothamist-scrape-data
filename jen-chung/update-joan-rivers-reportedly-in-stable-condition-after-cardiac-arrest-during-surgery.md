<p>The comedienne Joan Rivers is at Mount Sinai Hospital now, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014119/http://www.tmz.com/2014/08/28/joan-rivers-hospitalized-not-breathing-medical-emergency/">according to TMZ</a>, which adds that she&apos;s currently in critical condition.</p>

<p>TMZ hears that she &quot;was rushed to a hospital... after she stopped breathing during a surgery on her throat ... TMZ has learned. We&apos;re told Rivers was undergoing the throat procedure in a clinic (specifically on her vocal chords) ... when she stopped breathing during the surgery.&quot; Apparently the 911 call made at 9:39 a.m. recorded someone saying, &quot;We have somebody in either cardiac or respiratory arrest.&quot;</p>

<p>Rivers, 81, had recently taped an <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014119/http://joanrivers.com/2014/08/27/fashion-police-special-2014-vmas-and-emmys-episode/">episode of Fashion Police for E!</a>. She&apos;s also been very active on Twitter:</p>
