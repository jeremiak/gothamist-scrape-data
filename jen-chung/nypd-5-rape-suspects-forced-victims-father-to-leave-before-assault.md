<p>The authorities released surveillance video of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160529172458/http://gothamist.com/2016/01/09/police_teen_raped_by_five_men_in_br.php">five suspects accused of raping an 18-year-old woman</a> in a Brooklyn park last week. And it turns out that the teen had been with her father when the group approached them.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 9:10 p.m. on Thursday, January 7th. The teen and her father were near the Osborne Playground in Brownsville &quot;when they were approached by five black males, one of whom was armed with a gun. The male with the gun pointed it at them and told the father to leave the area. The father complied and each one of the five suspects raped the victim. The father returned a short time later with two uniformed police officers and the suspects fled the location.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was treated at Kings County Hospital. </p>
