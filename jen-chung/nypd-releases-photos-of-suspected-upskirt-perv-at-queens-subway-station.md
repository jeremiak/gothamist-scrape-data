<p>According to the NYPD, an incident occurred at 8:17 a.m. last Wednesday, October 2nd. At the Junction Blvd and Roosevelt Avenue 7 line station, a man was seen recording a video under an 18-year-old woman&apos;s skirt. The police say, &quot;The suspect continued to record under the victim&apos;s skirt as she walked up the staircase leading to the southbound #7 platform. The suspect was confronted and fled on foot.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect, who is wanted for unlawful surveillance, is described as a man in his early to mid 30s, last seen wearing a black tank top shirt, blue jeans and black sneakers. Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto Crime Stoppers&apos; website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509195459/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.<br>
