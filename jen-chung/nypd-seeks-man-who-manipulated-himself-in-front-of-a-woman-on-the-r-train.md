<p>The police are searching for a subway rider accused of public lewdness on a train in Brooklyn last week.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident took place around 4:20 p.m. on Wednesday, April 13th: On a southbound R train at the 59th Street station in Sunset Park, &quot;a male manipulated himself in front of a 21-year-old female victim.&quot; He later fled the train on foot.<br>
 <br>
The authorities describe the suspect as being in his 20s, 6&apos; and 180 pounds, and have distributed these images of him. </p>
