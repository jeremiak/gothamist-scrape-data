<p>Police are searching for yet <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161025143721/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/22/nypd_subway_pervert_crackdown.php">another alleged subway pervert</a>, this time after a sexual assault on a Brooklyn-bound L train.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_02_subpervl.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161025143721im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_02_subpervl.jpg" width="200" height="356" class="image-right"> </span>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on Wednesday, February 24th, during the evening rush: At 6:30 p.m., a 28-year-old woman on an L train at Union Square noticed a man lift a woman&apos;s jacket &quot;while grabbing and rubbing his groin against her buttocks.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The suspect, who fled at the Jefferson Avenue subway station, is described as being 25-years-old, about 5&apos;11&quot; and 180 pounds. He last seen wearing a black baseball hat, tan work boots, blue jeans and a black coat. Police released an image of him.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or for Spanish 1-888-57-PISTA (74782)<br>
The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161025143721/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
