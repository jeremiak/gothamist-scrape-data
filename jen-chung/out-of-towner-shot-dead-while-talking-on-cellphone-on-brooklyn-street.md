										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2011_04_nostae.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20141227232638im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2011_04_nostae.jpg" width="230" height="200" class="image-left"> </span>Last night after 7:30 p.m., a 24-year-old man was shot on Nostrand Avenue <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141227232638/http://maps.google.com/maps?q=nostrand+and+glenwood+brooklyn+ny&amp;um=1&amp;ie=UTF-8&amp;hq=&amp;hnear=Nostrand+Ave+%26+Glenwood+Rd,+Brooklyn,+NY+11210&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=vS60TdTADZC3tgfYr63nDg&amp;sa=X&amp;oi=geocode_result&amp;ct=title&amp;resnum=1&amp;ved=0CBUQ8gEwAA">near Glenwood</a> in Brooklyn.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141227232638/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2011/04/24/2011-04-24_sc_visitor_24_shot_and_killed_in_brooklyn_while_talking_on_cell_phone.html">According to the Daily News</a>, the victim, who was from South Carolina and visiting relatives, was on the cellphone when a&quot; gunman walked up... and shot him in the torso.&quot; His cousin said, &quot;I left for a second. <p>The victim died at Kings County Hospital. The cousin says it was probably a mistaken identity shooting, &quot;He didn&apos;t know anyone up here. My family wants answers from me, and I simply don&apos;t have any.&quot;</p>
