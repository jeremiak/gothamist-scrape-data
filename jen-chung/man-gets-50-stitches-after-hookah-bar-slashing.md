title: Man Gets 50 Stitches After Hookah Bar Slashing
---


	
	
	
	<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130715201217/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_07_hookahbar.jpg"> <img alt="2013_07_hookahbar.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130715201217im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2013/07/2013_07_hookahbar-thumb-253x346-798893.jpg" width="200" height="273" class="image-left"> </a> </span>The NYPD is looking for the public&apos;s help in identifying a man suspected of attacking someone at a hookah bar on Staten Island.</p>

<p>According to the police,  the incident occurred on June 29th at 3:50 a.m. at 101 Cafe, located on Akron Street. It was there that a 29-year-old man was slashed by another customer, who then fled the scene. </p>

<p>The victim was given 50 stitches at Richmond University Medical Center. The police released this photograph of the suspect (left).<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips to the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130715201217/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers website</a> or by texting tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
