title: NYPD: Uh, A Loaded Gun Was Found In A Chair
---


	
	
	
	<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_03_qweird.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130521035439im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_03_qweird.jpg" width="170" height="208" class="image-right"> </span>The police just released details on a weird incident: A loaded handgun was found in chair cushion in a lower Manhattan hotel.</p>

<p>The NYPD says that they&apos;d like to talk to this man in regards to the incident which happened on Sunday, April 14, at around 2 p.m. Apparently he walked into the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130521035439/http://www.clubquarters.com/">Club Quarters Hotel</a> at 144 Washington Street and placed a &quot;loaded handgun in a chair cushion and left the location.&quot;</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).&#xA0; The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130521035439/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
