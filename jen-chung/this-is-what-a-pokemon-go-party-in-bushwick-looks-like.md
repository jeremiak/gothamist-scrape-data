					<p>On Friday night, the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016021309/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/11/pokemon_go_black_mirror.php">Pok&#xE9;mon Go Zombie Apocalypse</a> got an official Bushwick bar party. Because there&apos;s nothing more cool than drinking PBR and evolving your Eevee at the same time.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016021309/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/12/pokemon_go_trainer_for_hire.php">Professional Pokemon Go trainer Ivy St. Ive</a> hosted the party at Father Knows Best, with promises of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016021309/https://www.facebook.com/fathersbk/photos/a.1706964616252030.1073741827.1706886389593186/1794459224169235/?type=3&amp;theater">free PBR and well shots for anyone in Pok&#xE9; Cosplay</a>. Ahead of the party, bar owners Christopher Taha and Colleen Makary <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016021309/http://bushwickdaily.com/bushwick/categories/bushwick/4049-bushwick-pokemon-ivy-st-ive-father-knows-best">told Buskwick Daily</a> they were &quot;super stoked that kids and adults are getting off their butts, having fun, and being active.&quot; (They also boasted their backyard is near a Pok&#xE9;stop and there are gyms nearby&#x2014;and promised to drop lures.)</p>

<p>The crowd at showed up seemed mixed in Pok&#xE9;nthusiam: Our photographer Sai Mohktari observed that about half the crowd was &quot;regulars who weren&apos;t necessarily planning on coming for the Pok&#xE9;mon&#x2014;but most of those people just whipped out their phones and started catching Pok&#xE9;mon in the background while they all hung out because everyone clearly has this game on their phones anyway.&quot; </p>

<p>Decked out in Pok&#xE9;mon gear (in fact, one of the few to actually dress up), Ive told us that she can take on anybody, but lately it&apos;s been getting harder to keep up. Apparently there are 10-year-old on Ive&apos;s block who are surpassing her (in numbers though). Ive hasn&apos;t been able to play as much as before because she&apos;s been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016021309/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/15/pokemon_based_economy.php">working on that book deal</a>. Well, obviously she needs an intern&#x2014;is Darren finished with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016021309/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ycjjkAPhhsQ">his gig at Kramerica Industries</a>?</p>
