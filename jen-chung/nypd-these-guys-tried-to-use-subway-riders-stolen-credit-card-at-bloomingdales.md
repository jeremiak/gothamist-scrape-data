										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_03_lvtheft.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813162553im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_03_lvtheft.jpg" width="640" height="475" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are on the lookout for two men who swiped a straphanger&apos;s wallet from her purse and tried to use one of her credit cards to buy designer items at Bloomingdale&apos;s.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on Monday, February 29th, the victim realized that her wallet was missing from her purse around 6 p.m. while in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813162553/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/transit_bureau/district_20.shtml">Transit District 20</a>/the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160813162553/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/precincts/precinct_108.shtml">108th Precinct</a> in Queens. Then, around 8 p.m., police say the men in the video below tried to charge $1,900 in merchandise on the woman&apos;s Citibank credit card at the Louis Vuitton store in Bloomingdale&apos;s in Manhattan.</p>
