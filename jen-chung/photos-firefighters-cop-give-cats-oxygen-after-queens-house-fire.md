<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160428154731/http://nypost.com/2015/11/23/firefighters-rescue-two-cats-in-house-fire/">According to the Post</a>, &quot;The lucky felines, Kit Kat and Jinx, were plucked from the smoky Glendale home during a 10:18 a.m. kitchen fire... One of the cats was saved after being spotted under a pile of moving laundry, while the other was found huddled next to a TV set. A third family cat, Bo, was found dead in a bathroom.&quot;</p>
