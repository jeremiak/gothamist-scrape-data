<p>The police is hoping for the public&apos;s help in identifying two people suspected of stealing a smartphone in the Bronx.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident happened on Sunday, September 29th, at 5:10 p.m. A 17-year-old male was walking in front of 270 East Burnside Avenue when two males approached him and said, &quot;We have a gun, give me your phone&quot;. The teen agreed, giving them his Samsung Galaxy IV, and the suspects fled. The victim wasn&apos;t injured. Here&apos;s video of the suspects:</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this robbery is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131008051454/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
