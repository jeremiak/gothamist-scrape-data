<p>Police are searching for a man accused of exposing himself to a young child at a Queens discount store on Monday. According to the NYPD, the incident occurred inside the Dollar Tree, at 39-09 Queens Boulevard, around 12:25 p.m. on January 16, when the suspect &quot;exposed his genitals to the 7 year-old female victim.&quot;</p>

<p>Investigators have released surveillance video of the suspect, who was wearing a black hat, a white shirt, and gray pants. He is wanted for endangering the welfare of a child.</p>
