<p>Finally, you can sleep restfully now: Producers of the planned &quot;Jersey Shore&quot; spinoff featuring Snooki and J-Woww have permits to film from Jersey City. Just in time for the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120212215714/http://www.nj.com/jjournal-news/index.ssf/2012/02/jersey_city_activists_acccuse.html">city&apos;s crime wave</a>!</p>

<p>Last month, the city of Hoboken <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120212215714/http://gothamist.com/2012/01/31/hoboken_denies_jwoww_snooki_permit.php">denied the reality star duo&apos;s production team permits to film</a>, citing &quot;public safety and quality of life concerns for Hoboken residents.&quot;  But <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120212215714/http://www.cityofjerseycity.com/">Jersey City</a> Mayor Jeremiah Healy says, &quot;The more people that see anything about Jersey City and see what we have in Jersey, the better it is for Jersey City.&quot;  We can&apos;t wait to see Snooki and J-Woww take the PATH!</p>
