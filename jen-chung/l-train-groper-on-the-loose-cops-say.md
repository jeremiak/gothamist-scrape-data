title: L Train Groper On The Loose, Cops Say
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_04_ltraingroper.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150110131244im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_04_ltraingroper.jpg" width="200" height="229" class="image-left"> </span>The NYPD is looking for a man accused of groping a woman on an L train last week.</p>

<p>The police say that on April 8, around 7:05 p.m., a 28-year-old woman was riding a Brooklyn-bound L train when, at Bedford Avenue, a man repeatedly touched her buttocks. The victim was not injured and she was able to take a photograph of the suspect.</p>

<p>The alleged groper is described as 40-50 years old, 5&apos;5&quot;-5&apos;6&quot;, and 200lbs, with a mustache. He was last seen wearing a black and gray baseball cap, and a grey hooded sweater. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150110131244/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577. All calls are strictly confidential.</p>
