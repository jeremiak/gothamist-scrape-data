<p>People, you <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130729105025/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/29/its_official_the_mermaid_parade_is.php">helped save the Mermaid Parade</a>&#x2014;so head out to Coney Island to enjoy it! The parade, which attracted 750,000 people last year, starts at 1 p.m. at  West 21st Street and Surf Avenue (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130729105025/http://www.coneyisland.com/programs/mermaid-parade">here is the parade route</a>). </p>

<p>A <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130729105025/http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/377116752/save-the-coney-island-mermaid-from-extinction">Kickstarter campaign</a> helped raise over $117,000 for the parade. And <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130729105025/http://www.ny1.com/content/top_stories/184299/coney-island-mermaid-parade-to-go-on-saturday-after-fundraising-effort">King Neptune</a> is Judah Friedlander and the Queen Mermaid is Carole Radziwill who is getting ready right now!</p>
