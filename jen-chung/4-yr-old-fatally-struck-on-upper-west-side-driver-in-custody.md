<p>Earlier this morning, police say that a driver jumped a curb on the Upper West Side, striking a four-year-old and her grandmother and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305230304/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/06/04/police-pedestrians-struck-by-car-on-upper-west-side/">pinning them to a building</a>. The girl, who went into cardiac arrest and was taken to St. Luke&apos;s Hospital, has died. The grandmother is there in serious condition.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305230304/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9126723">According to WABC 7</a>, &quot;The teen driver of the vehicle apparently refused to pull over for cops after a collision in the intersection.&quot; </p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305230304/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/manhattan/tot_struck_by_teen_driver_allegedly_YyiSdl5yqdPLWWw4qAioTO">Post reports</a>, &quot;A teen driving a black Nissan Frontier plowed into a 3-year-old girl and her mom about 8:20 a.m. on 97th Street and Amsterdam Avenue while trying to escape the police, witnesses said... &apos;He sped up to get away from the police &#x2014; that&#x2019;s when he hit the lady and the child,&apos; said one witness.&quot;</p>

<p>The driver is in custody.</p>
