<p>At least six people who waited outside a Broadway theater to catch a glimpse of the actors from the musical <em>Hamilton</em> have been robbed by the same pickpocket, police say.</p>

<p>The NYPD says the victims were robbed between May 25th and November 6th of this year, as they waited outside the Richard Rodgers Theater at 226 West 46th Street. According to investigators, the suspect then used their credit cards &quot;to purchase approximately $2,200 worth of merchandise at various locations including Macy&apos;s department store, Junior&apos;s Restaurant, Rite Aid, Target, DSW, Burlington Coat Factory and Duane Reade.&quot;</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_11_susphm.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016020424im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_11_susphm.jpg" width="640" height="287"> <br> <i> Grand larceny suspect (NYPD)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>The suspect, described as a woman with black hair and glasses and last seen wearing a black jacket, a grey sweater, black pants and a grey scarf, is wanted for grand larceny. Police released surveillance images and video of her.</p>
