<p>The police are looking for a man who suddenly attacked a woman in an apartment building elevator yesterday morning.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, a 57-year-old woman was in the elevator of a building near East 156th Street and Third Avenue in the Bronx along with the alleged attacker. At one point he &quot;suddenly slashed her several times about the face with an unknown object,&quot; police say, before he fled. </p>

<p>The victim was treated at a hospital for her injuries. Police say the suspect is a male who is approximately 40 years-old, 5&apos;8&quot; and as last seen wearing a black jacket, but there&apos;s video of him in the elevator, including when he attacked:</p>
