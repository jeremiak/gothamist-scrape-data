</div></div>					<p>Yesterday afternoon there was a massive <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150629172947/https://www.facebook.com/bushwickblockparty">Bushwick Block Party</a> organized by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150629172947/http://www.robertaspizza.com/">Roberta&apos;s</a>. The event featured music, double dutch, skateboarding and much more&#x2014;including food from Roberta&apos;s as well as vendors like Crif Dogs, Arancini Bros.,Yuji Ramen, Momo&apos;s and Red Hook Lobster. But as with so many food-based events, disorganization and large crowds led to angry patrons.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150629172947/https://www.facebook.com/events/118945891609053/">On a Facebook Event page</a> there were complaints about the large crowd: &quot;The fucking worst. Stand in line like an asshole for 2 hours and then spend spend spend. Lame, guys... very lame,&quot; and &quot;yo, real shit, there should&apos;ve been better planning. The cops were saying that you guys weren&apos;t expecting that many people, are you kidding me? who the fuck was in charge of this? IT&apos;S A BLOCK PARTY, why aren&apos;t people allowed to go in and out as they please? no, instead there&apos;s a damn line to go into a block party. just ridiculous. &amp; to think I was ready to donate some money to this shit. fuck y&apos;all and your bullshit ass block party.&quot; (A part of the vendors&apos; proceeds go to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150629172947/http://www.breadandlife.org/">St. John&#x2019;s Bread and Life</a>.) </p>

Others &quot;stood in the 400+ person line for 5 minutes then decided I couldn&apos;t give a fuck and went elsewhere.&quot;</p>
