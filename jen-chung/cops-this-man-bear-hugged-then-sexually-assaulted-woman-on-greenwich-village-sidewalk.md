<p>Police are still searching for the man accused of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015120/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/31/woman_nearly_raped_while_walking_in.php">trying to rape a young woman</a> on a Manhattan sidewalk last month, and now investigators have released video they hope will help them find the perp.</p>

<p>The incident occurred in Greenwich Village at 4 a.m. on July 30th. Police say the man approached the 20-year-old victim in the vicinity of 10th Street and 5th Avenue and &quot;put her into a bear hug.&quot; He then sexually assaulted her.   </p>

<p>The victim initially refused medical attention but was later taken to a hospital. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as 25 to 35 years-old, 5&apos;5&quot; to 5&apos;8&quot; and 140-170 pounds with medium length dark hair; he was  last seen wearing jeans, a short sleeve shirt, and a dark baseball cap.  He was also wearing a backpack. The surveillance video shows him walking down 5th Avenue outside of the New School: </p>
