										<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429162954/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2010/10/31/2010-10-31_queens_hitandrun_leaves_one_dead_in_jackson_heights.html?r=ny_local&amp;utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+nydnrss%2Fny_local+%28NY+Local%29&amp;utm_content=Google+Reader">Daily News reported</a> that, according to police, a &quot;male pedestrian in his 40s was killed in a hit-and-run crash in Queens Sunday night,&quot; after &quot;walking at the intersection of 37th Ave. and 91st St. in Jackson Heights when he was struck about 8:50 p.m.&quot;  He was later pronounced dead at Elmhurst Hospital, and now the police think he was beaten by a gang&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429162954/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local&amp;id=7757429">WABC 7 says</a>, &quot;The man&apos;s injuries were so severe that it was initially believed he was hit by a car.&quot;</p>
