<p>Police have released video of a robbery suspect accused of attacking a woman at a Manhattan acupuncture center yesterday. </p>

<p>The incident occurred at the Green Tea Acupuncture Wellness spa at 240 East 28th Street in Kips Bay, around 12:50 p.m. The NYPD says the suspect entered the establishment and &quot;demanded money from the victim, a 40-year-old female.&quot; He then punched the woman in the face and about the body, and demanded that she take off her clothes. Police say he took $500 from the establishment and then fled.   </p>

<p>The victim was treated for her injuries at a hospital. Police say the suspect is about 6&apos;2 tall and 220 pounds, with blotchy skin, brown eyes and short curly black hair. He was last seen wearing a dark blue jacket, blue jeans, black sneakers and a multi-colored back pack.<br>
