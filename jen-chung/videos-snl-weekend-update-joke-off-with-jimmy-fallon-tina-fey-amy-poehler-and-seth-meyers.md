</div></div>					<p>Jimmy Fallon&apos;s first stint as an <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509173859/http://www.nbc.com/saturday-night-live/index.shtml">Saturday Night Live</a> host meant many veteran SNL performers showed up for the last show of 2011.  Rachel Dratch was Denise to Fallon&apos;s Sully in the cold open; Amy Poehler showed up, too; Chris Kattan, Tracy Morgan and a svelte Hortaio Sanz performed a song with Fallon; and, in the highlight, Fallon and his Weekend Update co-host Tina Fey had a joke-off with current Update host Seth Meyers and Poehler.  </p>

<p>The show played to Fallon&apos;s strengths with some fun celebrity impressions&#x2014;a Michael Buble Duets album gave Fallon a chance to portray Sting, Russell Brand and Justin Bieber&#x2014; and his monologue acknowledged how he often cracked up and broke character. There were also shout-outs to Tim Tebow&apos;s ridiculous luck and Regis Philbin&apos;s retirement.</p>
