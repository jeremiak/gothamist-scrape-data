title: NYPD: Pregnant Subway Rider Attacked By Cell Phone Robber
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_01_bxrob.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170114154820im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_01_bxrob.jpg" width="300" height="210" class="image-right"> </span>A pregnant woman was shoved to the ground during a cell phone robbery that began on a D train in the Bronx last month.</p>

<p>The incident occurred on December 20th, at around 6 p.m. The victim, a 20-year-old woman, was on a northbound D train at the Grand Concourse and East Tremont Avenue subway station when, according to the NYPD, a male individual &quot;grabbed... [her] cell phone and tried to run off the train. When the victim struggled to maintain possession of her cell phone the individual shoved her into a pillar on the platform. The victim fell to the ground and let go of her cell phone; the individual fled to the street area.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was taken to St. Barnabas Hospital as a precaution. The suspect was described as being about 25 years old and was last seen wearing a black and gray hooded jacket and light blue jeans.<br>

All calls are strictly confidential.</p>
