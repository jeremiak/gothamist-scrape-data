<p>This NYC-based season of<em> Curb Your Enthusiasm</em> has been taking advantage of the Big Apple&apos;s standbys (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150929080101/http://gothamist.com/2011/07/08/video_larry_david_finally_getting_n.php">subways</a>! <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150929080101/http://www.hitfix.com/blogs/whats-alan-watching/posts/curb-your-enthusiasm-the-bi-sexual-juicing-the-game">the High Line</a>!) and last night, the HBO comedy featured Mister Softee.  Of course, Larry David has experienced a childhood trauma that haunts him whenever he hears <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150929080101/http://gothamist.com/2009/03/27/mister_softee_truck_jingle_driving.php">Mister Softee&apos;s infectious theme</a>.
