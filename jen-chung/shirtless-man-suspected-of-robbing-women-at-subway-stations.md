title: Shirtless Man Suspected Of Robbing Women At Subway Stations
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150624120037im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2011_08_shitsusp.jpg" width="320" height="235" class="image-left"> </span>Police are looking for a man who has allegedly robbed two women at Queens subway stations.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150624120037/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=8296096">According to WABC 7</a>, &quot;The suspect approached the victim&apos;s, verbally abused them, grabbed their cell phones, and fled on foot.&quot; Surveillance footage shows the suspect fleeing down the street, without his shirt.</p>

<p>A 35-year-old woman was robbed at the Liberty Ave. and 88 St. last Tuesday and the second victim was a 36-year-old woman, also August 2, at the Liberty Ave. and Rockaway Blvd. station.  Police describe the suspect as a black man, age 30-40-years-old, 6&apos;0&quot;, is of slim build, and was wearing a white T-shirt, khaki shorts, and reading glasses. Anyone with information is asked to contact <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150624120037/http://nypdcrimestoppers.com/">NYPD Crime Stoppers</a>.</p>
