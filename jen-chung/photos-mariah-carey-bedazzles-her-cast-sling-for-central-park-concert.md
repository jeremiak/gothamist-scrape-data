                            <p>Last week, Mariah Carey <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821025956/http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/gossip/confidential/mariah-carey-dislocates-shoulder-video-shoot-source-article-1.1393270">dislocated her shoulder</a> while filming a music video for &quot;#Beautiful.&quot; She went to the hospital and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821025956/http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/gossip/nick-cannon-mariah-carey-shoulder-injury-pretty-serious-article-1.1394046">then returned to finish the video</a>, but she was in pain. Last night, she still sang with the New York Philharmonic in Central Park for the Major League Baseball All-Star Charity Concert&#x2014;and her injury did not cramp her style.</p>

<p>Mimi had three different slings for the event. Here&apos;s how the<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821025956/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/manhattan/pop_slinger_mariah_2ZxylU9ugu7iBSWFNE5W4O?utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_content=Manhattan"> Post described them</a>: &quot;One white and rhinestone-encrusted; one a white fur; and a black-feathered job that looked like a wing.&quot; And yes, there was a hashtag&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130821025956/https://twitter.com/search?q=%23blingsling&amp;src=typd">#blingsling</a>.</p>
