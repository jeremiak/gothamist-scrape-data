										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left" style=" width:270px; "> <img alt="2011_06_bklyncrash.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160704053705im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2011_06_bklyncrash.jpg" width="270" height="212"> <br> <i>Via WABC 7</i></div> A <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160704053705/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=8214754">32-year-old woman is dead</a> after a car driven by her boyfriend drove into a street median and hit a light pole and another car on Fourth Avenue in Sunset Park last night.  And not only was the boyfriend charged with DWI, apparently the car belonged to the boyfriend&apos;s wife.  The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160704053705/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2011/06/26/2011-06-26_fatal_accident.html">Daily News reports</a>, &quot;[Nyron] Isaac, an unemployed maintenance worker, was supposed to be home watching his 10-year-old son, according to his wife, Tennile Isaac, 29, who has been seeking a divorce. She had warned him not to drive the Cadillac, which she was transferring ownership of to him, because it was uninsured, she said.&quot;  <p></p>

<p>Tennile Isaac said that her soon-to-be-ex decided to leave his son home alone to give girlfriend Keisha Nelson a ride.  Nelson, who had a 9-year-old son, had been in the backseat with Nyron Isaac&apos;s 21-year-old female cousin. Nelson was pronounced dead at the scene while Nyron Isaac and his cousin were taken to a hospital in serious by stable condition.  </p>

<p>Isaac was charged with charged with manslaughter, driving while intoxicated, and driving without a license.  Witnesses told the Daily News that &quot;when he got out of the car, Isaac was spinning in circles and said he didn&apos;t know who was driving.&quot;</p>
