<p>At yesterday&apos;s Yankees-Astros games, TV cameras zeroed in on actors Chris Rock and Bobby Cannavale sitting in the crowd behind the visitors dugout. And after the next pitch, the Houston Astros&apos; Jon Singleton hit a foul ball right at them.</p>

<p>Rock made a half-hearted effort to catch the ball, but was handicapped by the cellphone he held in one hand:</p>
