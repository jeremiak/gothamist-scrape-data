										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2010_10_newsbag.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140707im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2010_10_newsbag.jpg" width="130" height="84" class="image-right"> </span>At 7 a.m. this morning, police and fire officials evacuated residents from a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140707/http://gothamist.com/2007/12/09/montclair_new_j.php">Montclair, NJ</a> street after a &quot;pipe bomb wrapped in a plastic newspaper bag was found this morning on the front lawn of&quot; one home.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150427140707/http://www.northjersey.com/news/crime_courts/104702484_Pipe_bomb_found_outside_house.html">According to the Montclair Times</a>, &quot;After two attempts, a bomb squad detonated the device, made of a pipe with a 2-inch diameter that had wires protruding from it,&quot; with an audience of neighbors watching from past the police&apos;s security tape. A neighbor said the home where the device was found &quot;belongs to a couple with a baby girl that has resided at that address for about two years. She did not know the homeowners&apos; names.&quot; </p>
