<p>Police hope the public can help them identify the person shown in this video who allegedly tried to rape a woman yesterday.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 1 a.m. on August 9th, the male suspect approached a 30-year-old woman in the third floor stairwell of her Crown Heights building. Police say he tried to engage her in conversation before pushing her to the ground and trying to rape her; however the victim was able to resist and the suspect fled.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510014900if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/qfkoSAxKkXI?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe><br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as being in his 20&apos;s, approximately 5&apos;9&quot;, and 130 pounds. The suspect was clean shaven, with close-cut hair and wearing a white T-shirt and blue jeans.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014900/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers website</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
