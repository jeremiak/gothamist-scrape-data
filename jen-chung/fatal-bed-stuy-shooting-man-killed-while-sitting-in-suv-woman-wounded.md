title: Fatal Bed-Stuy Shooting: Man Killed While Sitting In SUV, Woman Wounded

										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_07_mbenz.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091818im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_07_mbenz.jpg" width="640" height="387"> <br> <i> The SUV on Halsey Street (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091818/http://abc7ny.com/news/man-dead-woman-wounded-in-brooklyn-double-shooting/1453352/">WABC 7</a>)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>A man was fatally shot while sitting in a double-parked SUV on a Brooklyn street early this morning. A woman who was also in the SUV and is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091818/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/brooklyn/man-killed-woman-wounded-bed-stuy-shooting-article-1.2735186">reportedly</a> the man&apos;s girlfriend was wounded.</p>

<p>Police say that shooting occurred just before 1 a.m. in front of 675 Halsey Street in Bedford-Stuyvesant. Responding officers found the male driver and female passenger shot inside a double-parked 2007 Mercedes Benz SUV. The woman, 22, was taken to Kings County Hospital where she was treated for a gunshot wound to the torso.</p>

<p>The driver, Ernest Brown, 33, was pronounced dead at Interfaith Hospital; he had been shot in the head and torso.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091818/http://pix11.com/2016/08/02/man-sitting-in-parked-car-killed-in-bed-stuy-double-shooting-police-say/">PIX 11 reports</a> that the shooter was wearing &quot;a dark blue-colored hoodie&quot; and &quot;walked up to their vehicle and fired at least 12 times at point-blank range. </p>

<p>Brown, a handyman and the father of eight children, had been shot last year. The mother of one of Brown&apos;s children <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091818/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-Brooklyn-Shooting-Man-Opens-Fire-People-in-SUV-Bed-Stuy-388911942.html">told NBC New York</a>, &quot;He tried to do right. He lived by his kids. All he wanted was the best for his kids.&quot; </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110091818/http://pix11.com/2016/08/02/man-sitting-in-parked-car-killed-in-bed-stuy-double-shooting-police-say/">A relative told PIX 11</a> that Brown might have been targeted because of an old &quot;beef.&quot; &quot;This is Brooklyn, you don&apos;t what&apos;s going on in the streets. He don&apos;t have nothing to do with it but by being with someone they don&apos;t like and it just goes back and forth.&quot;</p>
