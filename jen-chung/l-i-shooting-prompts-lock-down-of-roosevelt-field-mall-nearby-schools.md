                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2013_09_rifmall.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128045140im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_09_rifmall.jpg" width="640" height="360"> <br> <i> Via WCBS 2</i></div> <p></p>

<p>Authorities are searching for a man who opened fire on two people in Garden City, Long Island. The Roosevelt Field Mall is locked down and Garden City schools are not allowing anyone inside.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128045140/http://www.newsday.com/long-island/nassau/cops-hunt-shooting-suspect-near-roosevelt-field-1.6137281">According to Newsday</a>, &quot;Det. Vincent Garcia of the Nassau police said a man walked into a single-story commercial building on South Street at 10:10 a.m. and shot two people... The shooter left in a white sport utility vehicle, possibly a Honda Pilot, Garcia said.&quot;</p>

<p>The condition of the victims is not known yet. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128045140/https://twitter.com/eyewitnessnyc/status/382897862196985856">Eyewitness News reports</a>, &quot;GardenCity Police are searching for a suspect that they described as a 6&apos;2&quot; Asian male, wearing a red shirt, grey or brown suit jacket.&quot;</p>

<p>A shopper at the mall told Newsday, &quot;This is terrifying. After what happened in Kenya, everyone is already on edge.&quot;</p>

<p><b>Update</b>: NBC New York <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128045140/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Shooting-Roosevelt-Field-Mall-Long-Island-Police-School-Lockdown-225200502.html?_osource=SocialFlowTwt_NYBrand">reports</a> the shooter was an ex-employee and that one of the two victims was killed.</p>
