<p>Hey, it may <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509172751/http://gothamist.com/2013/04/04/why_is_it_still_so_freaking_cold.php">still be freaking cold</a>, but it&apos;s technically spring! The Central Park Conservancy is getting ready by turning on the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509172751/http://www.centralparknyc.org/visit/things-to-see/south-end/bethesda-fountain.html">Bethesda Fountain</a> for the first time this year. And the fountain needs to be up and running because next week is the CPC&apos;s big <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509172751/http://www.centralparknyc.org/central-to-spring/central-to-spring.html">Central to Spring event</a>, which will include fireworks!</p>

<p>Next Wednesday, starting at 7 p.m., the Central Park Conservancy is hosting its first <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509172751/http://www.centralparknyc.org/central-to-spring/central-to-spring.html">Central to Spring</a> bash, to &quot;Say goodbye to winter and hello to spring.&quot; There will be music, refreshments from The City Bakery, and a 15-minute fireworks display at 7:45 p.m., all at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509172751/http://www.centralparknyc.org/visit/things-to-see/south-end/bethesda-terrace.html">Bethesda Terrace</a>, which is mid-park at 72nd Street. And it&apos;s all free!</p>
