										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140626031947/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_06_tourist1.jpg"> <img alt="2014_06_tourist1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140626031947im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2014/06/2014_06_tourist1-thumb-316x592-848124.jpg" width="150" height="281" class="image-right"> </a> </span>The police is asking for the public&apos;s help in locating a missing woman.</p>

<p>Vivian Minnielli, 61, was last seen yesterday, June 22, at 3 p.m. in the vicinity of the Hilton Hotel at 1335 Sixth Avenue, which is the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140626031947/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Missing-Ohio-Tourist-Midtown-Manhattan-Hotel-264200521.html">hotel that she has been staying in</a>. She is visiting from Hamilton, Ohio.</p>

<p>According to police, she is 5&apos;2 and 180 pounds with blonde hair and blue eyes. She was last seen wearing a long sleeved blue sweater, white striped pants and white sneakers. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this missing person is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  </p>

<p>The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140626031947/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
