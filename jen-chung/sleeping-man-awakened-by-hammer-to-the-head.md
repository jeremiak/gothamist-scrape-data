title: Sleeping Man Awakened By Hammer To The Head
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_10_madstreet.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012854im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_10_madstreet.jpg" width="200" height="267" class="image-right"> </span>The police are hoping the public can help them find a person who is accused of an attack in lower Manhattan last month.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at around 8 a.m. on Tuesday, September 7 in an apartment at 160 Madison Street: A 22-year-old man was sleeping &quot;when he awoke to the suspect striking him in the head with a hammer.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim tried to flee the apartment, but then the suspect &quot;struck the victim again both on the head and his arm.&quot; The suspect fled the apartment, and the victim was taken to Bellevue Hospital in stable condition. </p>

<p>Police released a photograph of the suspect. Anyone with information in regards to this assault is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012854/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
