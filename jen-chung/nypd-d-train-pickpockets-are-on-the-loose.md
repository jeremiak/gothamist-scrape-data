										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_10_pickpock.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151222150256im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_10_pickpock.jpg" width="300" height="328" class="image-right"> </span>Police want help identifying two suspects wanted for grand larceny after picking a woman&apos;s pocket on a subway during the summer.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on July 30, 2015 at about 7:35 p.m., on a D train near Grand Concourse and East Tremont Avenue: When they boarded the train , the &quot;suspects approached a 49-year old female victim. Suspect #1 removed the victim&apos;s cellphone and credit cards from her rear pocket and fled with suspect #2 in an unknown direction.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was not injured.</p>

<p>Police released images of the suspects, describing both as about 17-18 years old, 5&apos;6&quot;-5&apos;7&quot; and 150 pounds. Suspect #1 was last seen wearing a black sleeveless tank top, and shorts with white sneakers and black shoelaces, while Suspect #2 was  last seen wearing a black sleeveless tank top, black shorts, black sneakers, white socks and a chain with a cross around his neck.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS.The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151222150256/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
