<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130513141642/http://gothamist.com/tags/grandcentralterminal">Grand Central Terminal</a> is 100 this year, and the celebrations are happening all year long: Tomorrow, Wednesday, April 10, different Grand Central Terminal retailers are offering <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130513141642/http://www.grandcentralterminal.com/100FREE">special giveaways to the first 100 customers</a>. There&apos;s a &quot;bambini&quot; scoop of gelato or sorbet from Ciao Bella and free cookies from Magnolia as well as a &quot;free vanilla cupcake with Grand Central clock topper&quot; from Zaro&apos;s. </p>

<p>Some of the deals do require you to purchase something: For instance, if you buy a coffee at Oren&apos;s Daily Roast, you&apos;ll get a free Bodum travel tumbler.
