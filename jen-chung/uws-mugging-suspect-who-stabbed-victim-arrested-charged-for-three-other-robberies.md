										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_02_uwsmg.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630062913im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_02_uwsmg.jpg" width="640" height="488" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police say the person who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630062913/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/19/two_men_slashed_during_separate_mug.php">stabbed a man on the Upper West Side</a> during a robbery on Thursday is the same suspect involved in four other violent encounters this week. </p>

<p>Rondell Smith, 21, has been arrested and charged with robbery for four incidents, plus menacing in a fifth, between February 17th and February 19th. The NYPD detailed them:</p>

<ul>
	<li>On February 17th, 2016, at 6:40 a.m., police say a 70-year-old man walking his dogs on West 90th Street was approached by Smith who &quot;asked him what time it was. After being told the time, Smith opened his jacket, revealing the handle of a black firearm. The victim fled and there were no reported injuries.</li>
	<li>February 17th, 2016, at 5 p.m.: A 13-year-old boy walking on West 89th Street was allegedly approached by Smith, who &quot;threatened him with a knife and then removed an undetermined amount of money from the victim.&quot;</li>
	<li>February 18th, 2016, 2:30 p.m.: A 34-year-old man walking on Broadway was allegedly asked by Smith &quot;where Columbus was. The victim told him and Smith replied asking for $50 dollars. The victim said no and Smith threatened to shoot him. Smith then forcibly removed the victim&apos;s wallet from his pants and fled.&quot;</li>
	<li>February 18th, 2016, 4:55 p.m.: A 33-year-old man walking in front of 105 West 88th Street was, the NYPD says, allegedly &quot;approached from behind by&quot; Smith, who then &quot;placed a knife to his neck and demanded his property. The victim complied giving the unknown male his Apple Kindle and Apple headphones, a struggle ensued and the unknown individual stabbed the victim in the upper back.&quot; </li>
	<li>Yesterday, at 2:05 a.m., a 25-year-old man was robbed on the northbound platform of the #2 train at the 96 Street station. Police say Smith was &quot;armed with a box cutter and simulated having a gun, then fled on the #2 train, north bound.&quot; The victim notified police, who apprehended Smith at at 149 Street and Grand Concourse in the Bronx. </li>
</ul>
