<p>Earlier this morning, a police officer was shot in the foot outside Harlem Hospital. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506151009/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9133187">According to WABC 7</a>, &quot;NYPD Officer Fausto Gomez was apparently shot in the leg at the ambulance entrance and taken to the hospital&apos;s emergency room for treatment. Police say he and his partner were bringing an emotionally-disturbed person inside when a struggled ensued.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506151009/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Police-Officer-Shot-Foot-Harlem-Hospital-210799281.html">NBC New York reports</a>, &quot;Witnesses told law enforcement officials the suspect&apos;s hands were handcuffed in back. The two struggled over the gun and at least one shot was fired; the officer was struck in the foot.&quot; The NYPD <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506151009/http://www.myfoxny.com/story/22545870/report-shots-fired-outside-harlem-hospital">closed the area by 135th Street and Lenox Avenue</a> off for their investigation.</p>

<p>Police characterized the shooter as an emotionally disturbed person and updated the public on Gomez&apos;s condition:</p>
