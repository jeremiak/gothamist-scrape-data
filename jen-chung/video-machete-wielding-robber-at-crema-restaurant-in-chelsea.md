<p>The cops are looking for three suspects who robbed a restaurant in Chelsea on Friday night. As you&apos;ll see in the video below, one of them came armed with a machete.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, police received a 911 call at around 10:30 p.m. about a robbery at Crema restaurant at 111 West 17th Street. A restaurant employee told police officers that the three suspects &quot;walked into the location and removed a cash register behind the bar. Victims further stated that one of the suspects pulled out a machete and demanded money in Spanish.&quot; Here&apos;s video:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510013707if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/URMPpmKcCu4?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspects, who fled west on West 17th Street towards 7th Avenue, are described as being in their 20s; they were wearing masks.<br>
