<p>If you&apos;re driving in in Midtown Manhattan today, you&apos;ll need more than the luck of the Irish, because numerous streets are closed for today&apos;s big St. Patrick&apos;s Day Parade. </p>

<p>This is a historic year for the parade: It&apos;s the first time in 20 years that a Democratic mayor has marched, as the parade has <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160628081747/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/03/de_blasio_st_patricks_parade.php">finally opened its arms to a wider group of LGBT groups</a>. If you&apos;re out there and see a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160628081747/http://gothamist.com/2014/03/17/st_patricks_button_man.php#photo-1">guy with his face painted with Irish colors</a> and wearing a well-worn green sweater decked with buttons, say, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160628081747/http://gothamist.com/2014/03/17/st_patricks_button_man.php#photo-1">&quot;Hi, Dennis!&quot;</a></p>
