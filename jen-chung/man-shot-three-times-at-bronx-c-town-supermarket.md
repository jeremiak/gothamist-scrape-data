<p>Police are looking for a suspect in a shooting that occurred yesterday at a Bronx supermarket. Investigators believe the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160402182030/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/C-Town-Supermarket-Shooting-Bronx-New-York-Police-363163811.html">victim was the intended target</a>.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, a man wielding a gun entered the C-Town Supermarket at 564 Southern Boulevard just before noon and &quot;discharged a firearm, striking the 21 year-old male victim three times in the torso. The suspect fled on foot southbound on Southern Boulevard.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was taken to a hospital and is in stable condition. The suspect, described as being around 20 years old, is seen aiming his gun and firing this surveillance video. He also appears to be talking to some employees before the shooting:</p>
