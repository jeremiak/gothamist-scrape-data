<p>A young woman was killed when a car she was riding in barreled into a divider on the Williamsburg Bridge this morning. Police say the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170810085452/https://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20170316/lower-east-side/williamsburg-bridge-crash-birthday-nypd">driver was drunk</a>, and the driver, the victim and another passenger had been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170810085452/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Woman-Killed-in-Early-Morning-Crash-on-Williamsburg-Bridge-Lower-East-Side-416303853.html">celebrating the dead woman&apos;s 21st birthday</a>.</p>

<p>The crash occurred on the Williamsburg Bridge before 3:30 a.m. The driver, reportedly a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170810085452/http://abc7ny.com/news/woman-killed-in-1-car-wreck-on-williamsburg-bridge/1803289/">26-year-old off-duty traffic enforcement officer</a>, was driving an Infiniti from Manhattan into Brooklyn when he lost control and hit the divider and then a support column, which split the car in two. </p>
