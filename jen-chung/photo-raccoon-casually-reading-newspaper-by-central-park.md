<p>While we should be recycling your newspapers, sometimes it&apos;s okay to leave behind a copy on a park bench so a member of the wild kingdom can help keep print journalism alive. This raccoon was spotted reading the paper&#x2014;and it&apos;s not just any raccoon, it&apos;s a raccoon who can read UPSIDE DOWN.</p>

<p>Colin Stark noticed this little varmint on Central Park West near 100th Street last night. The raccoon also appears to be doing origami while looking for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601000821/http://gothamist.com/2014/08/21/dead_brooklyn_raccoon_tests_positiv.php">good rabies doctors</a> in the classifieds. </p>
