										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left" style=" width:300px; "> <img alt="2011_03_456usq.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504131025im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2011_03_456usq.jpg" width="300" height="231"> <br> <i>Via <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504131025/http://www.nycsubway.org/">NYC Subway</a></i></div> Earlier this morning, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504131025/http://twitter.com/nyctsubwayscoop">NYC Transit warned</a> its Twitter followers, &quot;Downtown 4, 5 6 bypassing 14th St due to NYPD investigation of customer injury. Expect delays.&quot;  One person <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504131025/http://twitter.com/WillWei/statuses/53797197166297088">noticed</a>, &quot;Saw a trail of blood coming out of the union square subway.. Stabbing or some April fool&apos;s prank?&quot; It was neither: A man was actually <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504131025/http://twitter.com/NYScanner/status/53797681075724288">hit in the head</a> by an oncoming downtown express train at the platform around 8 a.m.<br>
 <br>
DNAinfo has a photograph of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504131025/http://www.dnainfo.com/20110401/murray-hill-gramercy/man-hit-head-by-subway-train-at-union-square">blood on the platform and reports</a>, &quot;The man, who was not identified, was knocked unconscious onto the platform, according to an MTA worker at the scene. The worker said the injured man looked like he was in his 20s.&quot;<p></p>

<p>The 4, 5, and 6 train tracks at the Union Square subway station curve, and last December, a man was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504131025/http://gothamist.com/2010/12/11/mind_the_gap_man_crushed_between_tr.php">trapped between the platform edge and a 4 train</a> (he <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504131025/http://gothamist.com/tags/michaeldion">is now suing</a>, but the MTA claims he was drunk).</p>
