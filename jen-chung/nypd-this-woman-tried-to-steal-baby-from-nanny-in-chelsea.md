title: NYPD: This Woman Tried To Steal Baby From Nanny In Chelsea
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_03_chesleakidnapping.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509171024im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_03_chesleakidnapping.jpg" width="270" height="318" class="image-right"> </span>Yesterday, a woman apparently tried to kidnap an eight-month-old baby from the care of his nanny while he was being strolled in Chelsea.</p>

<p>Police have distributed a sketch of the woman accused of trying to kidnap the baby. According to the NYPD, around 3:45 p.m., the 43-year-old nanny was pushing the baby in his stroller outside of 140 8th Avenue, near 17th Street, when they were approached by a woman who came up to talk to them.</p>

<p>The woman was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509171024/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/04/17/police-woman-confronted-nanny-grabbed-stroller-in-chelsea/">apparently mumbling</a> or saying <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509171024/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9507825">&quot;something to the nanny in a threatening manner&quot;</a> before she tried to walk away with the stroller. The nanny struggled with the woman and eventually got the stroller back with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509171024/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9507825">help from a UPS worker</a>. </p>

<p>The suspect, who fled southbound on 8th Avenue, is described as a female in her 50&apos;s and wearing a beige hat and beige overcoat. Anyone with information is urged to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150509171024/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
