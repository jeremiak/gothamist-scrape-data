<p>A verbal dispute escalated into a stabbing at an East Williamsburg deli Saturday morning, and police are still searching for the suspect. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 2 a.m. on Saturday, February 13th after the victim, a 33-year-old man, &quot;became involved in a verbal dispute with the suspect inside of the deli located at 168 Graham Avenue. The dispute then escalated into the suspect stabbing the victim in the torso and hand.&quot;  </p>

<p>The victim was treated at Wyckoff Hospital. Police released surveillance video of the suspect, who fled:</p>
