<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at about 2:30 p.m. on Monday, December 21st when a man exposed and &quot;manipulated his private parts in front of a 43-year-old female victim while on board the northbound &apos;F&apos; train at the 7 Avenue and 9 Street station.&quot;<br>
 <br>
A photograph of the suspect was released. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630064340/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>

<p>You can also report any inappropriate subway behavior to the MTA: Here is their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630064340/http://mta-nyc.custhelp.com/app/ask_sh">website where you can detail the incident</a>, as well upload a photo.</p>
