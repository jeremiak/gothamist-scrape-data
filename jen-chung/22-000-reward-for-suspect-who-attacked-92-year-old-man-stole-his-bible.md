										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_06_10pctattacker.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150419090546im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_06_10pctattacker.jpg" width="640" height="433" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The NYPD has announced a $22,000 reward for information leading to the arrest and conviction of a man who attacked a 92-year-old man in Manhattan on Monday night.</p>

<p>According to the police, at 11:26 p.m. on June 10, the suspect followed the elderly man into a residential location on West 18th Street, shoved him to the ground and took his bag. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150419090546/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/man-robs-92-year-old-glasses-bible-manhattan-article-1.1371204?localLinksEnabled=false">According to the Daily News</a>, the bag contained the victim&apos;s glasses and Bible. The victim also suffered a hip injury. </p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150419090546if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/NXRRxj0W-zE" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspect is described a man in 30s, and video shows him wearing a black shirt and red hat. Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150419090546/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">the Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577. </p>

<p>And here&apos;s the breakdown of reward money: &quot;$10,000 from the Mayor&apos;s Office City of New York - Arrest and Conviction; $10,000 from the New York City Police Dept. - Arrest and Conviction; $2,000 from Crime Stoppers - Arrest and Indictment.&quot;</p>
