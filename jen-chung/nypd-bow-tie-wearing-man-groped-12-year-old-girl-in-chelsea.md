<p>The police say a young girl was sexually assaulted while walking in Chelsea yesterday afternoon.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at about 4:30 p.m. on January 12th, officers responded &quot;to a call of a past assault in front of 528 West 28th Street.&quot; There they found a 12-year-old girl and her mother, who said her daughter was walking west on 28th when she was approached &quot;by a white male, who grabbed [the girl&apos;s] buttocks as he passed her walking eastbound.&quot; The girl screamed and ran home, where she told her mother what happened. </p>

<p>The suspect, who is wanted for sex abuse, is described as being in his early twenties, approximately 5&apos;6&quot; tall, with short brown hair, wearing dark green trousers and a dark bow tie.</p>
