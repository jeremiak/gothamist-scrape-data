title: Pedestrian Killed By Hit-And-Run Driver In Brooklyn
---

	
										<p>A man crossing an East New York street was fatally struck by a hit-and-run driver earlier this morning. When police arrived at the scene, the driver was already gone.</p>

<p>The incident occurred around 4 a.m. today. According to the NYPD, officers responded to a call about a pedestrian struck by a car and found the 39-year-old victim &quot;unconscious and unresponsive with severe trauma about the body.&quot; He was taken to Brookdale Hospital and pronounced dead.</p>

<p>A police investigation so far found that the victim was struck by the driver of an unidentified black sedan. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016021805/http://abc7ny.com/traffic/police-searching-for-driver-in-fatal-hit-and-run-in-brooklyn/1663082/">WABC 7 reports</a>, &quot;Police are looking for a woman, possibly driving a Nissan Maxima with front-end damage. It was last seen speeding westbound on Jamaica Avenue.&quot; And a video of the aftermath <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016021805/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-39-Year-Old-Man-Killed-in-Hit-and-Run-in-Brooklyn-407415545.html">reportedly shows</a> a mailbox being knocked down.</p>
