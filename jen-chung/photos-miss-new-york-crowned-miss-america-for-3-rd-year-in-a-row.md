</div></div>					<p>New York is so hot right now: For the third consecutive year, a contestant from the Empire State, Kira Kazantsev, was named Miss America. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013717/http://www.nj.com/entertainment/index.ssf/2014/09/miss_america_2015_miss_new_york_kira_kazantsev_wins.html">She said last night</a>, &quot;The thing about New York, especially being Miss New York, is that you have to make it. You have to bust your little tushy to get to where you want to go, and that creates somebody that&apos;s very independent.&quot;</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510013717if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/iio6ZORTtUY" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Kazantsev&apos;s talent performance was signing Pharrell Williams&apos; &quot;Happy&quot; with a red cup:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510013717if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/uqL4pcINCv0?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>It was apparently inspired by actress <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013717/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cmSbXsFE3l8">Anna Kendrick&apos;s &quot;Cups&quot;</a>. And Kendrick noticed:</p>

<p>Kazantsev, whose platform is domestic violence (she was stalked by a boyfriend while attending Hofstra ),  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013717/http://www.npr.org/blogs/health/2014/09/11/347728725/pageant-contestant-felt-helpless-against-domestic-violence">discussed the situation of</a> football player Ray Rice&apos;s wife&apos;s Janay Rice&apos;s situation with NPR, &quot;I want people to stop asking, &apos;Why doesn&apos;t she just leave?&apos; Every woman is an expert in her own case, and there are so many extenuating circumstances that lead to a woman staying with her abuser,&quot; and told NJ Advance Media, &quot;In the United States, the justice system is driving the getaway car for abusers.&quot;</p>

<p>Kazantsev, who grew up in California, will use the $50,000 scholarship for law school; the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013717/http://www.nj.com/entertainment/index.ssf/2014/09/miss_america_2015_miss_new_york_kira_kazantsev_wins.html">Star-Ledger reports </a>that she deferred from University of Notre Dame while the Post says <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013717/http://nypost.com/2014/09/15/miss-new-york-crowned-miss-america/">she was accepted at Fordham</a>.</p>

<p>Last year, Miss New York <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013717/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/16/another_miss_new_york_is_crowned_mi.php#photo-1">Nina Davuluri won the crown</a>; her predecessor was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013717/http://gothamist.com/2013/01/13/new_miss_america_mallory_hagan_is_f.php#photo-1">Mallory Hagan from Park Slope</a>.</p>
