					<p>Yes, it&apos;s only ONE DAY until the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016011917/https://twitter.com/AP_Planner/status/795691500319440901">possible end of civilization</a> as we know it, but on the bright side, Thanksgiving is just around the corner. Macy&apos;s is getting ready for their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016011917/http://social.macys.com/parade/2016/">90th Thanksgiving Day Parade</a> by testing five new balloons at Citi Field this past weekend. </p>

<p>The new big character balloons are an updated Charlie Brown, an updated Diary of A Wimpy Kid and a brand new <em>Trolls</em> balloon. There&apos;s also a bouncing dog named Trixie and, as a throwback to the very first parade in 1927, a Felix the Cat balloon. This Felix is an <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016011917/http://nypost.com/2016/11/06/thanksgiving-day-parade-balloons-go-for-test-run-at-citi-field/">&quot;exact replica&quot;</a> of the one in 1927. </p>

<p>There will be a total of 42 balloons, plus 26 floats; 1,100 cheerleaders and dancers; more than 1,000 clowns; and 16 marching bands and performance groups.</p>
