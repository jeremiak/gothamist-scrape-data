</div></div>					<p>Last night was the Muppets&apos; long-awaited <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150406171421/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Muppet_Show">return</a> to sketch comedy&#x2014;well, at least they got two sketches.  They appeared on Saturday Night Live to give a hand to host Jason Segel, who is the human lead in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150406171421/http://gothamist.com/2011/06/18/video_the_official_muppets_trailer.php">new, upcoming Muppets movie</a>. Kermit, Miss Piggy, Fozzie Bear, Gonzo, Scooter, and Rowlf sang with Segel in the monologue and Kermit, a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150406171421/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2Wl553wsVw">seasoned reporter</a>, also showed up on Weekend Update to discuss how <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150406171421/http://gothamist.com/2011/11/16/pizza_now_counts_as_a_vegetable_acc.php">pizza is now a vegetable</a>, suggesting maybe now the national anthem should be the Hot Pockets theme.  </p>

<p>Segel&apos;s goofy demeanor served him well in many skits, and the Andre the Giant skit was particularly funny. Other highlights: Kelly Ripa auditioning replacements for Regis Philbin, a bed commercial and Jon Huntsman&apos;s appearance on Weekend Update The show is off next week but <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150406171421/http://www.nbc.com/saturday-night-live/backstage/backstage/steve-buscemi-the-black-keys-on-snl-december-3rd/">will return on December 3 </a>with Steve Buscemi and the Black Keys.</p>
