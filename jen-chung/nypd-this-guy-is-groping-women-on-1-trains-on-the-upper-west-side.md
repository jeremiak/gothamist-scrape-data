<p>Police are looking for a person accusing of forcibly touching two different women during the morning rush hour commute along the 1 subway line earlier this month.</p>

<p>Both incidents occurred on December 7th. The first was around 9:20 a.m., on a southbound 1 train near Broadway and 96th Street; police say the suspect &quot;grabbed a 31-year-old female victim&apos;s buttocks&quot; before leaving the train at the 96th Street station.</p>

<p>Minutes later, at 9:38 a.m., police say the same man grabbed &quot;a 19-year-old female victim&apos;s buttocks&quot; on a 1 southbound train near Broadway and 72nd Street. He fled the train at the 72nd Street station, where the left the subway system.</p>

<p>The authorities released a sketch of the suspect, describing him as being around 20-30 years old and 6&apos;, last seen wearing a black hoodie and blue jeans.</p>
