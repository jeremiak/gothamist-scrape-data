										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012738/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_10_bushmail.jpg"> <img alt="2014_10_bushmail.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012738im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2014/10/2014_10_bushmail-thumb-900x932-863552.jpg" width="250" height="258" class="image-right"> </a> </span>The police released a photograph of a man they say nearly got away with some packages from a Brooklyn apartment building. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, last Friday, October 10th, a man entered a building in Bushwick and &quot;removed multiple packages from the lobby area that belonged to residents. The suspect was then stopped outside by Good Samaritans at which point he dropped the packages and fled on a bicycle.&quot;</p>

<p>The suspect is described as being around 5&apos;8&quot; and 150-180 pounds. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regard to these incidents is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
