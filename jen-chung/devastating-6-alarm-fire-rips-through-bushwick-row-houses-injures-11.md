title: Devastating 6-Alarm Fire Rips Through Bushwick Row Houses, Injures 11
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_03_bushwickfire1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002170711im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_03_bushwickfire1.jpg" width="640" height="561"> <br> <i> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002170711/https://twitter.com/tomrmartin/status/715008555749924864">@tomrmartin</a></i></div> <p></p>

<p>More than 200 firefighters battled a six-alarm fire that engulfed buildings on DeKalb Avenue in Brooklyn last night. Fire Commissioner Daniel Nigro said, &quot;Many people homeless, unfortunately, right now.&quot;</p>

<p>The fire was reported at 9:51 p.m. at 1427 DeKalb Avenue near Wilson Avenue in Bushwick, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002170711/http://abc7ny.com/news/6-alarm-fire-burns-through-3-buildings-in-bushwick/1268429/">it quickly spread</a> to neighboring buildings. Here&apos;s a drone video of the fire:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161002170711if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/-bl0ApCaza0?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Eleven people were injured; one seriously and the others described as minor. It took firefighters over three hours to control the fire. </p>
