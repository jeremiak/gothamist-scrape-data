<p>The police are looking for a suspect in an attempted rape that occurred in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012607/https://www.google.com/maps/place/Stuyvesant+Town,+New+York,+NY/@40.7316903,-73.9778494,7z/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x89c259737e225aa1:0x652ff78189c0e104?sa=X&amp;ei=I8RBVNLYH7SQsQTIwYLwCQ&amp;ved=0CJwBEPIBMA8">Stuyvesant Town</a> early yesterday morning. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, a 20-year-old woman was followed into an elevator by a man at the apartment complex at around 4 a.m. on Friday, October 17th. Police say the man then tried to rape her, but she &quot;yelled and struggled with the suspect causing him to eventually flee the elevator.&quot;</p>

<p>Video shows the suspect fleeing and making his way down a tree.<br>

<p>The suspect is described as having dark hair and wearing a black hooded sweat shirt, blue jeans and white sneakers. <br>

Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012607/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">Crime Stoppers website</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
