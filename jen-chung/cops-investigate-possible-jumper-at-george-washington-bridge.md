<p>Port Authority police are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305224728/http://www.nj.com/bergen/index.ssf/2011/12/police_investigating_possible_suicide_on_george_washington_bridge.html">investigating a possible suicide</a> at the George Washington Bridge.  NJ.com reports, &quot;A bicyclist discovered an iPad, a set of keys and a pair of shoes on the upper-level, eastbound walkway about halfway across the bridge,&quot; and a Port Authority spokesman said, &quot;It appears we have a jumper, about 8 o&apos;clock this morning.&quot;</p>

<p>The NYPD&apos;s harbor patrol was called to assist, but hasn&apos;t found anything yet.  </p>
