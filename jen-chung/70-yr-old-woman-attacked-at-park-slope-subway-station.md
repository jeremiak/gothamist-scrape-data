										<p><img src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140406182714im_/http://gothamist.com/upload/2014/04/2014_03_pslopeassault1.jpg" width="640" height="436" alt="2014_03_pslopeassault1.jpg"></p>

<p>Police have released images of a man suspected of attacking an elderly woman at the 4th Avenue-9th Street subway station in Brooklyn.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred on Sunday, March 30. A 70-year-old woman was on the southbound R platform at 10:45 a.m. when a man approached her. He allegedly struck her head with his fist before fleeing.</p>
