<p>Two years ago, Staten Island Chuck died after <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160507202640/http://gothamist.com/2014/09/25/deblasio_groundhog_killer.php">Mayor Bill &quot;Butterfingers&quot; de Blasio dropped her</a>. Last year, Chuck&apos;s replacement, Chuck, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160507202640/http://gothamist.com/2015/02/02/staten_island_chuck_doesnt_die_says.php#photo-1">was put in a plexiglass cage</a>, far from the mayor&apos;s homicidal hands. </p>

<p>This year, Lieutenant Governor Kathy Hochul (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160507202640/https://www.governor.ny.gov/meet-lieutenant-governor-kathy-hochul">who?</a>) had the honors of not killing the most important weather forecaster to come from Staten Island. She was conveniently wearing red to mask any potential groundhog blood, but, as you can see, a trained professional held Chuck:</p>
