title: NYPD Seek Sexual Assault Suspects In Forest Hills, Astoria
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140608215542/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_06_asuspect.jpg"> <img alt="2014_06_asuspect.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140608215542im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2014/06/2014_06_asuspect-thumb-250x340-845680.jpg" width="170" height="231" class="image-right"> </a> </span>Police are looking for suspects in two different sexual assaults in Queens.</p>

<p>The first occurred on Monday, June 2, at 11:30 a.m. in Astoria. Police say that a 53-year-old woman entered her apartment&apos;s elevator when a man punched and choked her. Then he attempted to sexually assault her before fleeing.</p>

<p>The victim was treated at a hospital and later released. </p>

<p>Police say the suspect in the incident is named Elvis McKenzie (pictured, right), who is 27, 6&apos;4&quot; and 200 pounds. </p>

<p>The second incident was in Forest Hills on Tuesday at 6:55 p.m. </p>

<p>A man followed a 47-year-old woman into her apartment building located at Queens Boulevard and 76th Road. He grabbed her from behind and sexually abused her.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140608215542/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_06_fhsuspect.jpg"> <img alt="2014_06_fhsuspect.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140608215542im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2014/06/2014_06_fhsuspect-thumb-242x333-845679.jpg" width="180" height="247" class="image-left"> </a> </span>The suspect threw her to the ground and fled. The victim was treated at a hospital for minor injuries to her right arm and knee. </p>

<p>Police released a sketch (left) of the suspect.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to these incidents is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. </p>

<p>The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140608215542/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
