<p>The police say they arrested three people after an investigation led them to a Bedford-Stuyvesant apartment where a drug ring was operating.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, &quot;members of the 81 Precinct Field Intelligence team&quot;&#x2014;Sergeant Lauryl Philip, Detective Cortney Wallace and Police Officer William Sitzman&#x2014;got a search warrant for apartment #2 at 245 Throop Avenue and they executed the warrant last Friday, May 13, and found: a .32 caliber Smith &amp; Wesson gun; $12,157.00 in cash; 35.15 grams of cocaine; 3 ounces of marijuana; and 1,910 glassine envelopes of heroin which were stamped &quot;Tap Out.&quot; </p>
