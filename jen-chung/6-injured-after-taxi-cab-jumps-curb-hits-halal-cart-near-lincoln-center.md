title: 6 Injured After Taxi Cab Jumps Curb, Hits Halal Cart Near Lincoln Center
---

	
										<p>Six people were injured when a taxi cab jumped a curb in Manhattan this afternoon. And the cab may have been rear-ended.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160816115500/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/New-York-NYC-Broadway-Upper-West-Side-62-Columbus-Circle-Lincoln-Center-378704571.html?_osource=SocialFlowTwt_NYBrand">NBC New York reports</a>, &quot;It&apos;s not clear what caused the crash, which involved a yellow cab, a Con Edison van and a food cart, at West 62nd Street and Broadway at about 5 p.m. A photo from the scene shows the taxi on the sidewalk, with the food cart dipped over into the roadway.&quot;</p>
