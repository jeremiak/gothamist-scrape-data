</div></div>					<p>Scarlett Johansson is now a member of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314190401/http://snl.wikia.com/wiki/Five-Timers_Club">Five Timers Club</a>, the elite group of performers who have hosted <em>Saturday Night Live</em> five or more times. She pointed out her last hosting gig <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314190401/http://gothamist.com/2015/05/03/videos_scarlett_johansson_gets_blac.php#photo-1">was in 2015</a> &quot;when the most upsetting thing in the news was that <em>Mad Men</em> was ending.&quot;</p>

<p>The cold open was a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314190401/http://gothamist.com/2017/03/12/snl_cold_open_alec_baldwin_returns.php">President Trump vs. aliens skit</a>, but there was a much sharper attack on a different Trump later in the show. Johansson played Ivanka Trump in a perfume commercial for a fragrance called, &quot;Complicit,&quot; with a voiceover asking, &quot;A feminist, an advocate, a champion for women, but, like, how? She&apos;s loyal, devoted but probably should have bounced after the whole <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314190401/http://gothamist.com/2016/10/07/trump_what_this_time_oh_god.php">Access Hollywood bus thing</a>... I bet when she watches Titanic she thinks she&#x2019;s Rose &#x2014; Sorry girl, you&#x2019;re Billy Zane.&quot; The ad signed off, &quot;The fragrance for the woman who could stop all this, but won&apos;t.&quot;</p>

<p>Another clever pre-taped bit was a lesbian counterpart to Logo TV&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314190401/http://www.logotv.com/shows/fire-island"><em>Fire Island</em></a>&#x2014;instead of ripped gay men partying, <em>Cherry Grove</em> features lesbians restricting their children&apos;s screen time and completing puzzles.</p>

<p>Leslie Jones brought back her ninja character, Shanice Goodwin, now competing against another ninja (Johansson), while Kate McKinnon reprised <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170314190401/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/13/snl_videos_ariana_grande_does_doubl.php#photo-7">her frisky part-mermaid, part blobfish</a>, Shud. McKinnon also popped up as Attorney General Jeff Sessions on &quot;Weekend Update,&quot; along with Alex Moffat as Senator Al Franken; McKinnon said that &quot;Franken&quot; showed him &quot;Jew stuff,&quot; so Moffat explained, &quot;We had lunch at a deli, Jeff.&quot;</p>

<p>&quot;Weekend Update&quot; also saw Pete Davidson give his youth report, which included the assessment that White House adviser Stephen Miller looks like &quot;Fredo Corleone, had he been sicker as a child.&quot; </p>

<p>You can see all the show&apos;s sketches&#x2014;including a zoo &quot;pornographer&quot;; a dog translator; the sketch that Kyle Mooney and Beck Bennett wrote on the &quot;Day Without Women&quot;; behind-the-scenes of an Olive Garden commercial shoot; a funeral service revelation&#x2014;and Lorde&apos;s performances if you click through the gallery above. </p>
