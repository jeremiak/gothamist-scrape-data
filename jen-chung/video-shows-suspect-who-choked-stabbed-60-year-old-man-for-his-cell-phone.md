<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013-08_brsone2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130923181021im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013-08_brsone2.jpg" width="180" height="191" class="image-right"> </span>The NYPD hopes the public can help them identify two people wanted for a brutal robbery that took place earlier this week in the Bronx. </p>

<p>According to police, at around 10:40 p.m. on Tuesday, September 17, a 60-year-old man was entering a building on Intervale Avenue. He was followed into the lobby by a male who choked him from behind and then stabbed him before stealing his cellphone. A woman was waiting outside as his lookout. </p>

<p>The victim survived the attack.</p>
