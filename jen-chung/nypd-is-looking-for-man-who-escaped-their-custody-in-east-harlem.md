title: NYPD Is Looking For Man Who Escaped Their Custody In East Harlem
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_02_eharlep.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170215142703im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_02_eharlep.jpg" width="640" height="466" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are looking for a robbery suspect who escaped from custody in East Harlem Saturday as officers attempted to put him into their patrol car.</p>

<p>Just before 12:30 p.m. Saturday, officers had taken the male suspect, who was<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170215142703/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Escaped-Prisoner-Shoplifter-Duane-Reade-NYPD-Handcuffed--413497963.html"> accused of shoplifting at a Duane Reade</a>, and were, police say, &quot;placing him into a vehicle in front of 50 East 102 Street when he fled from the officers heading eastbound on East 102 Street.&quot;</p>

<p>Authorities say the suspect was &quot;rear-cuffed at the time of arrest&quot; and released surveillance videos of him. They described him as being  5&apos;11&quot; tall and 150 pounds with a thin build; he was last seen wearing an olive green waist length jacket, green hoodie, gray sweat pants, and a black V-neck shirt. </p>
