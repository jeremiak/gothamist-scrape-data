										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_03_smilingipadsuspect.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425174959im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_03_smilingipadsuspect.jpg" width="309" height="268" class="image-right"> </span>The NYPD has released this photograph of a smiling individual suspected of stealing an iPad from a subway passenger.</p>

<p>The 34-year-old male victim was enjoying his iPad on a Queens-bound A train on Sunday, March 30th. When the train arrived at the Nostrand Avenue station around 3:20 p.m., police say the suspect snatched the iPad out of the victim&apos;s hands and ran off. Just like the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425174959/http://gothamist.com/2012/09/25/nypd_to_apple_customers_watch_your.php">NYPD predicted in this video</a>!</p>

<p>The police describe the suspect as approximately 20 years old, 5&apos;10&quot; and 150 lbs. Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto Crime Stoppers&apos; website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425174959/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.</p>
