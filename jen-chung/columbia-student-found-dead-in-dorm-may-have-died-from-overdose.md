title: Columbia Student Found Dead In Dorm May Have Died From Overdose
---


	
	
	
	<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-left"> <img alt="2013_03_jessicafingers1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605052302im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_03_jessicafingers1.jpg" width="371" height="270"> <br> <i style=" width:371px; ;display:block"> Via Columbia Athletics</i></div> The 21-year-old woman who was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605052302/http://gothamist.com/2013/03/31/woman_found_dead_in_columbia_dorm.php">found dead in a Columbia University dorm</a> has been identified as Jessica Fingers. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605052302/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/co-ed-dead-columbia-article-1.1304028?localLinksEnabled=false">According to the Daily News</a>, she and her boyfriend Peter Russell had been visiting a friend and current student in a dorm room at the East Campus residence hall when Russell found her unconscious, facing up, at 11:45 a.m. yesterday morning.  <p></p>

<p>Fingers was pronounced dead at the scene. Sources believe she <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605052302/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/manhattan/columbia_student_dead_in_dorm_room_kwdpY56DocIjwi1GvkjFLK">died from an overdose</a>.</p>

<p>Fingers, a resident of Monticello, NY,  had been <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605052302/http://www.columbiaspectator.com/2013/03/31/jessica-fingers-columbia-college-13-student-leave-found-dead-east-campus">accepted to the undergraduate college</a> on a full scholarship and was majoring in sociology and a member of the track team. While she would have graduated with this year&apos;s class, she had been on leave since last year&#x2014;the News notes that Fingers&apos;s mother had posted on her Facebook page that her daughter was recovering from a stroke and blood clots.  </p>

<p>Russell, a freelance tattoo artist, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605052302/https://www.facebook.com/pjr345/posts/10151483055963762">posted on Facebook</a>, &quot;My life is over... Shes gone so i have nothing to live for.. Bye,&quot; &quot;I woke up and the love of my life was dead next to me,&quot; and &quot;I wanna die.&quot; Earlier, someone apparently related to Fingers <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605052302/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/co-ed-dead-columbia-article-1.1304028">posted on Russell&apos;s page</a>, &quot;F--k u peter im coming for ur life.&quot;</p>

<p>Fingers&apos; stepfather told the News that she had been cleared to go back to school, &quot;She loved Columbia. It was her dream school.&quot;</p>
