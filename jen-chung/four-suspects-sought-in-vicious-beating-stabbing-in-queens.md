										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_10_qnsattack1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161013184825im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_10_qnsattack1.jpg" width="640" height="414" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are searching for four suspects who brutally beat a man on a Queens sidewalk in August. A fifth suspect has already been arrested and charged with attempted murder.</p>

<p>The incident occurred around 11:25 p.m. on Sunday, August 28th, outside 39-09 104 Street. Police say five male &quot;individuals punched and kicked the 26 year-old male victim. Additionally they hit him with a bike chain and stabbed him with a knife.&quot;</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161013184825if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/6jHgYEmOeSg" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The victim had puncture wounds to his stomach and a laceration on his head; he was taken to Elmhurst General Hospital in critical condition. </p>

<p>The NYPD described the suspects: one had a backpack and was last seen wearing a blue T-shirt, dark colored pants and dark colored sneakers; another was last seen wearing a white T-shirt and dark colored pants; a third suspect was last seen wearing a blue shirt with the number 72 on it; and the last suspect was last seen wearing a dark colored T-shirt and dark colored pants. </p>
