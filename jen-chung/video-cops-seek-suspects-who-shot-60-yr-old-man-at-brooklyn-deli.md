<p>Early Friday morning, a 60-year-old customer at a Crown Heights deli was shot in the head. Now police are asking for the public&apos;s help in finding four suspects.</p>

<p>At 2:40 a.m., Samuel Waterman, 60, was at the service window of deli at 43 Lincoln Road before the suspects entered. The NYPD says, &quot;The victim proceeded to make a purchase and began to leave with the suspects following him.&quot; Then one suspect fired at Waterman&apos;s head before fleeing.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130320034628if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/qsL2ptPE1mM" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Waterman is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130320034628/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/brooklyn/brooklyn_man_shot_in_head_SsEdNGiB4Y4HBkip8O3EdL?utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_content=Brooklyn">reportedly clinging</a> to life at Kings County Hospital. </p>

<p>The shooter is described as a black male around 25-30 years old, with a beard and mustache, last seen wearing a black and grey striped hoodie, black jeans with black and white sneakers. The other suspects, all black males, were described as wearing an Adidas shirt white black and white sneakers; wearing a black and red lumberjack jacket with white sneakers; and wearing a grey hooded sweatshirt and white and black sneakers. They are wanted for felony assault.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130320034628/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
