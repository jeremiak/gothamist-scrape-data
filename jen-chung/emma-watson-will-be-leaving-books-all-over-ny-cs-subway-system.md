<p>If you see someone who looks suspiciously like Hermione Granger would as an adult, nonchalantly placing books in the subway system, then it is probably <em>Harry Potter</em> star Emma Watson.</p>

<p>Watson is a book fairy with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170630083338/http://booksontheunderground.co.uk/">Books on the Underground</a>, a London-based group that puts about 150 books in the Underground a week for commuters to enjoy. Here she is, paying it forward last week in London:</p>
