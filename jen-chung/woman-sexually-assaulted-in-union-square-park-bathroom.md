<p>Police are investigating a sexual assault that took place in Manhattan yesterday afternoon when a woman went into the public park bathroom in Union Square.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident took place around 1 p.m. near East 17th Street. A 63-year-old woman was approached by a man who demanded she perform a criminal sex act on him. She apparently bit down on his hand, causing him to flee.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2015_10_parkbathroom.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305153041im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_10_parkbathroom.jpg" width="640" height="360"> <br> <i> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305153041/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-Union-Square-Park-Assault-Restroom-63-Year-Old-Woman-334062691.html">NBC New York</a></i></div> <p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305153041/http://abc7ny.com/news/police-homeless-man-sexually-assaults-woman-in-union-square-park-restroom/1039394/">WABC 7 reports</a> that the suspect is homeless and that he groped her. A parkgoer said, &quot;I just showed up when the officer had went in there, and the door was open, and he was talked to a lady. I think she was freaking out. I think she had just got done crying.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was taken to a local hospital for treatment of pain and is in stable condition. The park bathroom was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305153041/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-Union-Square-Park-Assault-Restroom-63-Year-Old-Woman-334062691.html">closed</a> after the incident. </p>

<p>The suspect, described as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305153041/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/park-bathroom-rape-attempt-woman-63-article-1.2402274">being in his 30s</a> and wearing a camouflage jacket, is being sought.</p>
