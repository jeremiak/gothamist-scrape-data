	<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2013_05_wbcrash.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608075512im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_05_wbcrash.jpg" width="365" height="275"> <br> <i style=" width:365px; ;display:block"> Via WCBS 2</i></div> Earlier this morning at 3:55 a.m., a car crashed at South 4th Street and Roebling in  Williamsburg. A woman was killed, after apparently being ejected from a car, and four others were injured. It&apos;s believed that the car <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608075512/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/05/13/police-investigating-fatal-crash-in-brooklyn-2/">crashed into a guardrail</a> on a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608075512/http://www.myfoxny.com/story/22231728/deadly-crash-on-ramp-to-williamsburg-bridge">ramp to the</a> Williamsburg Bridge.<p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608075512/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Car-Crash-Fatal-Brooklyn-Williamsburg-Brooklyn-Queens-Expressway-BQE-Highway-207164021.html">According to NBC New York</a>, &quot;Only one car was involved in the accident and the woman who died was not the driver, police said... A preliminary investigation reveals the driver of the car may have lost control while moving at a high rate of speed, causing the vehicle to flip, police said.&quot;</p>

<p>The ramp was closed during the investigation, which is continuing. Traffic was diverted to the Manhattan Bridge.</p>
