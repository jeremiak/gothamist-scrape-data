title: Video: Subway Stabbing Victim Was Trying To 'Defuse The Situation' Before Attack
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_02_subconfrontation.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307013222im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_02_subconfrontation.jpg" width="640" height="443" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Police are still searching for the person <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307013222/http://gothamist.com/2017/02/25/man_allegedly_trying_to_calm_subway.php">accused of punching and stabbing a 1 train rider</a> on Friday, and now new video has surfaced showing the attack in the crowded subway car. The victim has also <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307013222/http://abc7ny.com/news/exclusive-subway-stabbing-caught-on-camera;-victim-recounts-attack-/1776167/">spoken out</a>, explaining that he was merely trying to stop his assailant from harassing another passenger. </p>

<p>Around 4:20 p.m. on February 24th, on a northbound 1 train near West 145th Street and Broadway in Manhattan, the NYPD says the victim, Richard Reyes, 31, and the suspect got into a &quot;verbal dispute&quot; resulting in the suspect punching Reyes in the face and then stabbing him. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307013222/http://abc7ny.com/news/exclusive-subway-stabbing-caught-on-camera;-victim-recounts-attack-/1776167/">WABC 7 obtained video</a> from another rider showing the punch and stabbing:<br>

<p>The train pulled into the station, and the suspect fled. Reyes was stabbed through his leather jacket and was treated at Harlem Hospital. He told WABC ,  &quot;He pulled out a blade. I didn&apos;t see the blade, but I felt it. As I was tackling him to the ground, he stabbed me three times in the back.&quot;</p>

<p>He added, &quot;I&apos;ve always been somebody to stop things. It&apos;s just never escalated to this. I&apos;ve always been able to defuse the situation.&quot;</p>

<p>Police also released images of the suspect, describing him as around 30 years old, 180 pounds and 6&apos;1&quot; with brown eyes and black hair. He was last seen wearing  a blue jacket, blue shoes, and a green bookbag.</p>
