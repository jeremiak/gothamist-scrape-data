										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_07_ltj.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405014308im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_07_ltj.jpg" width="300" height="274" class="image-right"> </span>Lawrence Taylor Jr., the son of NY Giants football great Lawrence Taylor, was arrested yesterday in Georgia on charges of statutory rape, aggravated child molestation and sodomy. As it happens, his father <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405014308/http://gothamist.com/2011/03/22/lawrence_taylor_sentenced_to_6_year.php">was sentenced in 2011 to six years probation</a> for having sex with a 16-year-old prostitute and was registered as a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405014308/http://gothamist.com/2011/04/12/lawrence_taylor_classified_as_level.php">Level 1 sex offender</a>.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405014308/http://www.ajc.com/news/news/local/powder-springs-police-charge-son-of-ex-nfl-star-la/nYgQR/">According to the Atlanta Journal Constitution</a>Taylor Jr., 31, who lives in Powder Springs, is accused of forcibly performing a sex act on one of the girls &quot;at his mother&#x2019;s house. She first notified investigators by phone while at lunch with Taylor Jr. and the other alleged victim, said Powder Springs Police Maj. Matt Boyd.&quot; </p>

<p>TMZ <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405014308/http://www.tmz.com/2013/07/08/lawrence-taylors-son-arrested-statutory-rape-sodomy/?adid=hero1">notes that the second alleged victim</a> told police she had sex with Taylor Jr. &quot;approximately 5 or 6 times&quot; before she was 16. Also: &quot;According to the Cobb County District Attorney&apos;s Office, Lawrence Taylor Jr. could face 25 YEARS TO LIFE in prison if convicted on either the sodomy charge or the child molestation charge.  The statutory rape charge carries a sentence of 1 to 20 years behind bars.&quot; (TMZ has graphic details of the alleged assault&#x2014;basically, in this case, he allegedly performed oral sex on the victim).</p>

<p>Taylor Jr. is being held without bond. </p>
