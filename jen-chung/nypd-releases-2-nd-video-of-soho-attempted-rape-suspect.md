<p>Last week, a young woman was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128051019/http://gothamist.com/2013/10/11/nypd_heres_video_man_who_tried_to_r.php">allegedly attacked and nearly raped</a> on Thompson Street in Soho. Now the NYPD has released another video of the suspect.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident happened at 1:15 a.m. on October 8th at Thompson Street between Prince and Spring Streets. The suspect approached a 20-year-old woman, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128051019/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/nyu-student-robbed-raped-cops-article-1.1482569">described by the Daily News</a> as a NYU student, from behind, placing her in a chokehold while trying to rape her. He left the scene with her purse. The woman was treated for facial injuries.</p>
