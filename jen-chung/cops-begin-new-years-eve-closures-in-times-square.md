</div></div><p>Besides not firing your <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120104082714/http://gothamist.com/2011/12/30/ray_kelly_dont_shoot_your_gun_in_ai.php">gun into the air to somehow welcome the New Year</a>, the NYPD has some advice for you, if you&apos;re going to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120104082714/http://gothamist.com/2011/12/30/what_to_expecting_when_youre_expect.php">Times Square tonight</a>: Don&apos;t bring backpacks, packages, large bags or any alcohol.  Also, pocketbooks will be inspected.</p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120104082714/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/pr/pr_2011_traffic_2011_12_30.shtml">NYPD started to close street at 3 p.m.</a>, and won&apos;t be reopening them until 3 a.m. tomorrow.  Here are the closed streets: 41st Street between 8th &amp; 6th Avenues; 58th Street between 8th &amp; 6th Avenues; 8th Avenue between 41st &amp; 58th Streets; 6th Avenue between 41st &amp; 58th Streets (All inclusive); and Seventh Avenue and Broadway between 41st Street and 59th Streets, inclusive of all cross streets.  Also, &quot;All closures are at discretion of NYPD.&quot; </p>

<p>Everyone, please have a happy and safe New Year&apos;s!</p>
