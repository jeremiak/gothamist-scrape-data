<p>A man was found with two puncture wounds to the head in a parking garage at Lincoln Center this morning. He was assaulted during an apparent domestic dispute near 90 Amsterdam Avenue around 5:43 a.m., and police say his partner has been arrested.</p>

<p>Investigators say the suspect stabbed the 28-year-old victim with a screwdriver. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160710161748/http://abc7ny.com/news/man-stabbed-twice-in-head-near-lincoln-center/1229044/">WABC 7 reports</a> that the victim fled across the street to the parking garage, where first responders later found him.</p>

<p>The victim was taken to Roosevelt Hospital. Charges against the partner are pending. </p>
