<p>A man is dead after being struck by his own vehicle in Central Park this morning. </p>

<p>The FDNY responded to the incident at 6:40 a.m., near East Drive and East 74th Street. The investigation is still in its early stages and the NYPD could not immediately confirm details of the incident. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630050741/http://abc7ny.com/traffic/man-killed-after-being-pinned-under-van-in-central-park/1212039/">WABC 7 reports</a> that the victim was killed when his van hit him, &quot;The victim may have gotten out to look under his van when it rolled over him and into the wooded area off East Drive, just before 7 a.m.&quot; </p>
