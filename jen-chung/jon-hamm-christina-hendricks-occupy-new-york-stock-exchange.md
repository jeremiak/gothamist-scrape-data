</div></div>					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030172734/http://gothamist.com/tags/madmen"><em>Mad Men</em></a> is coming back after a long hiatus this Sunday, much to the relief of non-fans who are enduring the Don Draper onslaught.  But for fans, it just means they can look at more and more photos of Jon Hamm, Christina Hendricks, January Jones, John Slattery and a really grown-up-looking Kiernan Shipka (aka Sally Draper!).  The show&apos;s stars, as well as creator Matthew Weiner and Lionsgate executives, were at the New York Stock Exchange to ring the opening bell.  </p>

<p>While the stars looked good and traders were pumped, stocks ended mixed, with the Dow <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030172734/http://www.cnbc.com/id/46806817">closing 45 points down</a>.</p>
