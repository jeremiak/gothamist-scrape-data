<p>Celebrities, they&apos;re just like us: shocked and weirdly riveted by the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015100/http://laist.com/2016/09/20/brangelina_bye.php">divorce of Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt</a>. George Clooney hadn&apos;t heard about the news yet yesterday when CNN&apos;s Richard Roth asked him about it at the United Nations&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015100/http://www.cnn.com/2016/09/20/entertainment/george-clooney-brad-angelina-divorce/">here&apos;s video of that</a>, where Clooney seems surprised, declaring, &quot;I didn&apos;t know that. Wow.&quot; Others reacted on Twitter. And Adele dedicated her entire concert to the former couple last night.</p>

<p>During her concert in Madison Square Garden, Adele took a moment to address the &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015100/http://www.vulture.com/2016/09/adele-dedicated-her-nyc-concert-to-brangelina.html">elephant in the room</a>...  Brangelina are broken up,&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015100/http://www.tmz.com/2016/09/20/adele-dedication-brad-pitt-angelina-jolie/">she said</a>, &quot;I don&apos;t like gossiping and private lives should be private but I feel like it&apos;s an end of an era and I&apos;m dedicating tonight&apos;s show to them.&quot; She added that she felt &quot;genuinely sad and shocked.&quot; </p>

<p>The performer (who has <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016015100/http://www.forbes.com/sites/carminegallo/2015/12/05/how-adele-is-managing-stage-fright/#6a2de15514a1">overcome serious stage fright</a>) also asked the crowd, &quot;Are you up for a good time?&quot; before explaining, &quot;Well, you&apos;ve come the wrong place... It&apos;s two hours of misery. Two hours of egotistical songs about moi and my ex-boyfriends.&quot;</p>
