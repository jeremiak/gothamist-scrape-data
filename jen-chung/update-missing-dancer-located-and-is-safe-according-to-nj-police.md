<p>A NJ dance teacher and occasional backup performer for Beyonce, Rihanna and other musicians <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012124/http://gothamist.com/2016/10/24/beyonce_backup_dancer_and_dance_ins.php">is still missing</a>. Shirlene Quigley, 32, was last seen early Sunday morning at the Port Authority Bus Terminal, and her friends and family are very worried. Rihanna posted a video of Quigley, saying, &quot;This beautiful soul, and former dancer of mine is MISSING!!! My heart aches thinking of how heavy this is on all who love her! If anyone has seen or has any information on @shirlenequigley&apos;s whereabouts .....&quot; <b>Update, 5:30 p.m. October 25:</b> North Bergen Police said that Quigley has been found and is safe.</p>

<p>Quigley, who was described as very religious, was seen leaving her North Bergen home by her landlord on Saturday afternoon; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012124/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Missing-Dancer-Celebrity-Shirlene-Quigley-Manhattan-New-Jersey-Beyonce-Phone-Chelsea-398210081.html">NBC New York reports</a> that landlord &quot;later called a friend to say Quigley did not seem well, the friend said.&quot;</p>

<p>Friends say Quigley was last seen wearing a pink top, jeans, and 3-4&#x201D; high heeled shoes. She is 5&apos;10&quot; and 150 pounds, and has a few tattoos: &quot;Emotion&quot; on her inside right forearm, &quot;Daddy&apos;s Girl&quot; on top of her left foot,&quot; and the zodiac sign for Cancer on the small of her back. The North Bergen Police Department is accepting tips at (201) 392-2092.</p>

<p>Quigley&apos;s father <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012124/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Missing-Dancer-Celebrity-Shirlene-Quigley-Manhattan-New-Jersey-Beyonce-Phone-Chelsea-398210081.html">told NBC New York</a> that he just wants her to return home, &quot;She&apos;s everything to me, she&apos;s why I live.&quot;<br>
