<p>Around 3:45 a.m., the tractor-trailer overturned near Hamilton Avenue in the eastbound lanes. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110084927/http://pix11.com/2016/08/08/overturned-tractor-trailer-covers-bqe-with-beer-cars-prompts-closure-of-all-eastbound-lanes/">PIX11 reports</a>, &quot;It was unclear when or why the tractor trailer initially flipped, but crews continued to attempt to get it upright as of 6 a.m.&quot;</p>

<p>The tractor-trailer&apos;s driver <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161110084927/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/brooklyn/tractor-trailer-crash-spills-budweiser-beer-brooklyn-highway-article-1.2742481">was hospitalized</a>.</p>
