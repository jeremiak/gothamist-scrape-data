title: NYPD: Subway Rider Buying MetroCard Attacked By Hammer-Wielding Man
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_05_bksuro.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140510220432im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_05_bksuro.jpg" width="220" height="269" class="image-right"> </span>The NYPD is looking for a man accused of robbing a man at a Brooklyn subway station&#x2014;and using a hammer to assault him.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140510220432/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/05/07/cops-suspect-struck-man-in-the-head-with-hammer-at-brooklyn-subway-stop/">According to WCBS 2</a>, it happening yesterday morning at 9:20 a.m.: Police say a &quot;32-year-old man was buying a Metro Card at the Fulton Street &amp; Rockaway Avenue subway stop when the suspect approached him from behind, hit him in the head with a hammer, and demanded money.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim gave him money and the suspect fled. The victim was treated for a head injury at Brookdale Hospital. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as 5&apos;10&quot; and weighing between 130 and 150 pounds. Anyone with information regarding this robbery has been asked to contact the NYPD&#x2019;s Crime Stoppers hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140510220432/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting tips to 274637 [CRIMES] and entering TIP577.</p>
