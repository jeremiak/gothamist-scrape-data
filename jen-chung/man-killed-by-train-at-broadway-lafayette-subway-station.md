<p>A man has been fatally struck by a train at a downtown Manhattan subway station. </p>

<p>Police confirmed that the incident occurred at 1:08 p.m. and that the victim was DOA.</p>

<p>The MTA&apos;s website says:</p><blockquote>Due to police investigation of a customer injury at Broadway- Lafayette:<br>
In both directions B and D train service bypass Broadway- Lafayette.<br>
Some northbound C train service running exp from Canal St to 59 St- Columbus Circle.<br>
Northbound F via A  from Jay St- MetroTech to W 4 St- Washington Sq.<br>
Southbound F train service bypassing Broadway- Lafayette.<br>
There is no M service between Essex St and Forest Hills- 71 Av in both directions.<br>
Allow additional travel time.</blockquote><p></p>

<p>We&apos;ll update as more information becomes available.</p>
