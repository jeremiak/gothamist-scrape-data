<p>Police are still searching for the suspect in a sexual assault that took place in Brooklyn nearly two weeks ago.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident happened at 11:15 p.m. on October 2nd: A 36 year-old woman entered her Bushwick apartment building followed by a man &quot;who engaged her in a conversation. Upon entering her apartment, the suspect followed her in, and attempted to sexually assault her.&quot;</p>

<p>The man fled, but police have now released surveillance footage of the suspect, who is described as being in mid 20&apos;s, approximately 5&apos;7&quot; and about 170 pounds with brown hair.<br>
