										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113144500/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jsaxena/062110handcuffs.jpg"> <img alt="062110handcuffs.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113144500im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2010/06/062110handcuffs-thumb-250x167-520196.jpg" width="120" height="80" class="image-right"> </a> </span>Newark resident Oquan Blake was arrested for allegedly raping and killing the 7-month-old baby girl of a woman he once dated.  Blake, who was recently released from jail and has a &quot;dozen arrests for drug and weapons charges,&quot; was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113144500/http://www.1010wins.com/Newark-Man-Arrested-in-Rape--Death-of-7-Month-Old-/7603133">reportedly upset</a> that the baby&apos;s mother spent time with her friend. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113144500/http://www.nj.com/news/index.ssf/2010/07/newark_police_arrest_man_in_co.html">According to the Star-Ledger</a>, &quot;The baby&#x2019;s 21-year-old mother, who was planning to attend an event in Manhattan for Gay Pride Week, instead spent the day at a female friend&#x2019;s home in Newark, authorities said.&quot; When the mother returned, she called police because the baby was unresponsive: &quot;The infant was taken to University Hospital in Newark with a fractured skull, ruptured spleen, broken ribs, bruising to the spine and a lacerated liver.&quot;</p>
