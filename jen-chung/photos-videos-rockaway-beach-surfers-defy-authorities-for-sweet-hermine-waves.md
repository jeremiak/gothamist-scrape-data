					<p>Even though Hermine <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170319044933/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/04/hermine.php">ended up being a big bust</a>, all city beaches <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170319044933/http://gothamist.com/2016/09/02/hermine_beaches_closing.php">were closed yesterday</a> as officials had earlier warned, &quot;We could see some of the most intense riptides in literally ten or fifteen years.&quot; But that only made things more tempting to surfers at Rockaway Beach.</p>

<p>Many surfers ventured out yesterday to enjoy the waves, and the NYC Parks Enforcement Patrol had no luck in clearing the waters. The NYPD was called upon for assistance.</p>
