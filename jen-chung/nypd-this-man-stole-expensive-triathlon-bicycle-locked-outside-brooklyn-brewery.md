<p>Police are searching for a man caught on camera stealing a specialized triathlon bicycle that was locked on a Williamsburg street. </p>

<p>According to police, the bike was chained outside Brooklyn Brewery located at 79 North 11th Street on Sunday, November 8th. It was stolen around 4:10 p.m.</p>

<p>Investigators released surveillance video of the suspect, described as 30-40 years old and wearing a green hooded jacket, gray jeans, black sneakers and carrying a black book bag. The video shows him contemplating the bike, walking away and then turning around&#x2014;he puts his hood on before stealing the bike:<br>
