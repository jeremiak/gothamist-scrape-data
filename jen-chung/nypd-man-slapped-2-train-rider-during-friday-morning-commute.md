<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_04_84pct.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170402200459im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_04_84pct.jpg" width="250" height="401" class="image-right"> </span>A woman was attacked on a 2 train in Brooklyn this week, according to police, who are hoping the public can help identify the suspect.</p>

<p>The incident took place on a northbound 2 train on Friday, March 31, at 8:30 a.m. The 30-year-old woman was on the train when, the NYPD says, an unidentified man slapped her on the left side of her face. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170402200459/http://nypost.com/2017/04/01/man-strikes-woman-in-seemingly-random-subway-attack/">According to the Post</a>, the woman been talking with friends when the man suddenly hit her.</p>

<p>When the train arrived at Borough Hall, the suspect allegedly fled. The victim was treated by EMS at the station. <br>
 <br>
The authorities released an image of the suspect&#x2014;apparently <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170402200459/http://nypost.com/2017/04/01/man-strikes-woman-in-seemingly-random-subway-attack/">taken by a Good Samaritan</a>&#x2014;and describe as him as about 35 years old, 5&apos;11&quot; and 160 pounds. He was last seen wearing a blue skull cap, red and white coat,  and black jeans.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or for Spanish 1-888-57-PISTA (74782)<br>
The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170402200459/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
