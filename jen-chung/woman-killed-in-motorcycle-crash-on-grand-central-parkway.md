<p>A woman riding as a passenger on a motorcycle died after a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014822/http://7online.com/traffic/gcp-motorcycle-crash-leaves-1-dead-traffic-backed-up/253889/">crash on the Grand Central Parkway this morning</a>. </p>

<p>Police say they responded to a 911 call about a vehicle accident on the parkway at 4:27 a.m. They found a 20-year-old woman and a 21-year-old man with body trauma; the woman was pronounced dead at Elmhurst General Hospital while the male victim had injuries to his shoulder and legs. </p>

<p>It&apos;s believed the man was operating the motorcycle when it lost control before the 94th Street exit, near LaGuardia Airport. Police are still investigating the crash, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014822/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/New-York-Grand-Central-Parkway-Crash-Fatality-270877001.html/">traffic is backed up for miles</a>. </p>
