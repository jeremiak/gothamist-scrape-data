					<p>Yesterday, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160916204749/http://gothamist.com/2014/11/03/one_world_trade_center_is_open_for.php">1 World Trade Center opened its doors to the first wave</a> of Conde Nast employees who will be occupying floors 20 through 44. Here&apos;s a sampling of how they feel:</p>

<p>-<em> &quot;I&#x2019;m extremely nervous.&quot;</em> (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160916204749/http://nypost.com/2014/11/04/memories-triggered-as-1-world-trade-center-opens/">NY Post</a>)<br>
- <em>&quot;I told [my mother], &apos;This is probably the safest building in New York.&apos;&quot;</em> (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160916204749/http://www.nytimes.com/2014/11/04/nyregion/first-tenant-of-1-world-trade-center-arrives-highlighting-lower-manhattans-renewal.html?ref=nyregion&amp;_r=0">NY Times</a>)<br>
- <em>&quot;I think it&apos;s spectacular. And I think it&apos;s a great tribute to America to see this. I&apos;m proud to be able to work here.&quot;</em> (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160916204749/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/conde-nast-moves-now-open-1-world-trade-center-article-1.1997198">Daily News</a>)<br>
- <em>&quot;It&#x2019;s sad, but it was a long time ago. We&#x2019;re moving on... Twenty-one of my neighbors in Garden City died. I went to about five or six funerals. &#x2026; As long as the memorial does justice to the people, then we have to move on.&quot; </em>(NY Post)<br>
- <em>&quot;I can understand the fear behind it... [But] If you live in fear. you can&#x2019;t get anything done in your life.&quot;</em> (NY Times)<br>
-<em> &quot;You know, a lot of sad things happened here and this is a good start to get things going in the right direction. It will turn out fine. Cond&#xE9; Nast has a great security team.&quot;</em> (Daily News)</p>

<p>Conde Nast CEO Chuck Townsend was permitted to have his Mercedes Benz limousine pull up in the front of the building. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160916204749/http://www.capitalnewyork.com/article/city-hall/2014/11/8555979/1-world-trade-center-opens-awkwardly?news-image">From Capital New York</a>:</p><blockquote>A man in a dark suit, blue shirt and no tie stepped out and walked to a stand that held microphones from the likes of Times Warner Cable and &#x201C;Inside Edition.&#x201D;<p></p>

<p>&#x201C;This is a terrific day for Lower Manhattan, a wonderful day for New York City and an absolutely great day for Cond&#xE9; Nast,&#x201D; the man said.</p>

<p>&#x201C;Who are you?&#x201D; a reporter shouted out. &#x201C;What&#x2019;s your name?&#x201D;</p>

<p>&#x201C;I&#x2019;m Chuck Townsend,&#x201D; he said, leaving out his title, chief executive of Cond&#xE9; Nast.</p>

<p>&#x201C;And what are you trying to show us by rolling up here in a Mercedes Benz, Chuck?&#x201D; the same reporter asked. &#x201C;What are you trying to say?&#x201D;</p>

<p>&#x201C;I&#x2019;m trying to say that that is my driver, and he drives me every day. Thank you very much,&#x201D; Townsend said, turning around and walking into the building.</p></blockquote>Townsend later told the Post that he did see <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160916204749/http://gothamist.com/2014/11/02/videos_chris_rock_prince_ebola_joke.php#photo-3">Chris Rock&apos;s Saturday Night Live monologue</a>, where the comedian emphatically said, &quot;They should change the name from the Freedom Tower to the &apos;Never Going in There Tower,&apos; &apos;cause I&apos;m never going in there... Does this building duck? Who&apos;s the corporate sponsor, Target? ... They put another skyscraper in the same spot? What kind arrogant Floyd Mayweather crap is this?&quot; <p></p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160916204749/http://nypost.com/2014/11/04/memories-triggered-as-1-world-trade-center-opens/">Townsend said</a>, &quot;I saw it Saturday night, and I was thinking about it over the weekend, and you know, I don&#x2019;t feel that way. I don&#x2019;t mean that in a cavalier way at all. Of course, you always think about security.&quot;</p>
