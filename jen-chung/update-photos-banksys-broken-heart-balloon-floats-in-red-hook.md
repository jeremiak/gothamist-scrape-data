                            <p>It&apos;s unclear if Banksy actually <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140208071538/http://gothamist.com/2013/10/06/banksys_latest_update_video_of_rebe.php">bombed the neighborhood of DUMBO</a>, but here&apos;s something he did do in Brooklyn: This street art piece depicting a heart-shaped balloon, covered in bandages.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2013_09_brokenheart2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140208071538im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_09_brokenheart2.jpg" width="640" height="429"> <br> <i> Via Banksy.co.uk</i></div> <p></p>

<p>The audio accompanying the installation says, &quot;The helium balloon. An object of such poetry. Its lightness, its fragility, its way of wandering on a breeze. This piece is obviously an iconic representation of the battle to surviving a broken heart. It&apos;s an uplifting visual poem to that most fragile of human emotions.&quot; Aww&#x2014;Banksy&apos;s gone all sentimental on us.</p>

<p>This looks like North Brooklyn to us&#x2014;where do you think it is?</p>

<p><b>Update</b>: It&apos;s in Red Hook&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140208071538/http://animalnewyork.com/2013/banksys-new-red-balloon-is-in-red-hook/">Animal New York says</a> it&apos;s on King Street, right off Van Brunt. And the<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140208071538/https://maps.google.com/maps?client=safari&amp;hl=en&amp;q=king+street+van+brunt+brooklyn+ny+map&amp;ie=UTF-8&amp;hq=&amp;hnear=0x89c25a61df03e909:0xef8e191a230508b3,Van+Brunt+St,+Brooklyn,+NY+11231&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=sddSUoH-DNes4AOe1IH4CQ&amp;ved=0CCsQ8gEwAA"> Google Map supports this</a>!</p>
