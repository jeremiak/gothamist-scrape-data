<p>Last night, President Obama held a big reelection fundraiser at the Apollo Theater.  While there <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160523030915/http://gothamist.com/2012/01/19/obamas_nyc_agenda_tonight_spike_lee.php">were protesters</a> (more about that later), Obama charmed the crowd and hit a chord when he sang a little of the Rev. Al Green&apos;s &quot;Let&apos;s Stay Together&quot;:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160523030915if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/T-hDt2E8MoE?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The President warbled, &quot;I&apos;m so in love with you,&quot; and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160523030915/http://abcnews.go.com/blogs/politics/2012/01/obama-sings-soul-tune-in-harlem">explained that some</a> &quot;didn&apos;t think I would do it. I told you I was gonna do it... The sandman did not come out.&quot; And referring to Green himself, who was at the Apollo, Obama said, &quot;Don&apos;t worry Rev, I cannot sing like you, but I just wanted to show my appreciation.&quot;  Aww.</p>
