										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2017_02_hitrun.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170307204956im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_02_hitrun.jpg" width="640" height="402" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>A 76-year-old bicyclist is in critical condition after being hit by a driver in Queens on Friday afternoon, and the driver is wanted by the police for leaving the scene.</p>

<p>The collision occurred around 5:30 p.m., on March 3. According to the NYPD, the bicyclist was heading north on Colden Street &quot;when he was struck by a vehicle at the intersection of Elder Avenue and Colden Street.&quot; </p>

<p>The driver, in a teal-colored Honda CRV, fled south on Kissena Boulevard from Franklin Avenue. </p>

<p>The victim is at Booth Memorial Hospital after being found with head trauma. Police released video of the car believed to be involved in the incident:<br>
