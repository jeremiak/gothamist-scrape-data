<p>An expensive Gibson guitar was stolen from a Guitar Center in Queens, and police have video of the thief in action.</p>

<p>The guitar grand larceny occurred on October 18 th at 3:50 p.m. at the Long Island City location at 34-17 48 Street. Police say &quot;an unknown male suspect removed a custom Gibson guitar valued at $4,650 from a display stand, secreted the guitar under his shirt and exited the store without paying.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The NYPD released video of the theft and describe the suspect as about 30, 5&apos;10&quot; and 165 pounds; he was wearing a two-tone gray long sleeve shirt over a black t-shirt with a white chest design, blue jeans, black sneakers and eyeglasses.  <br>
