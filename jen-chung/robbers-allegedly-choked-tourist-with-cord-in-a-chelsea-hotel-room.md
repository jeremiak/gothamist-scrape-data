										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2017_03_hotel309a.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170309235539im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2017_03_hotel309a.jpg" width="640" height="420"> <br> <i> A room at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170309235539/http://hotel309.com/">Hotel 309</a></i></div> <p></p>

<p>A Canadian man was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170309235539/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-Canadian-Tourist-Beaten-Robbed-in-Chelsea-Hotel-Room-Police--415541313.html">attacked and robbed</a> early yesterday morning in his Manhattan hotel room. The trio of allegedly armed male robbers used the cord of a hair dryer to choke him during the struggle, police say. </p>

<p>The victim, 31, had reportedly befriended one of the suspects and, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170309235539/http://www.amny.com/news/canadian-tourist-robbed-in-chelsea-hotel-room-nypd-says-1.13220346">per AMNY</a>, &quot;invited one of the men back to his room&quot; at Hotel 309 on West 14th Street. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170309235539/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/manhattan/canadian-tourist-choked-robbed-assailants-nyc-hotel-article-1.2990288">According to the Daily News</a>, &quot;He got a call early Monday from one of the suspects, asking to come to his room. When [he] opened the door, three men forced their way in, sources said. They pistol-whipped and beat him, and one used the cord to choke [him], forcing him to reveal the code to his room safe.&quot;</p>

<p>The robbers <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170309235539/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/manhattan/canadian-tourist-choked-robbed-assailants-nyc-hotel-article-1.2990288">reportedly took $12,000</a>, &quot;David Yurman designer rings and necklaces, a cell phone, a laptop and credit cards.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was taken to a hospital in stable condition.</p>
