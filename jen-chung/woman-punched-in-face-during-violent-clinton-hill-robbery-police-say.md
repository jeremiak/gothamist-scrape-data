title: Woman Punched In Face During Violent Clinton Hill Robbery, Police Say
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_02_fgrobbery.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160605064555im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_02_fgrobbery.jpg" width="200" height="267" class="image-right"> </span>Police are hoping the public can help them identify a person accused of robbing a woman last week.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, they received a report around 7 p.m. on Sunday, February 21st, that a 35-year-old woman was approached by a male who &quot;punched her in the face and demanded her property&quot; around Classon and Lafayette Avenues. The &quot;victim complied and the unknown male fled on foot with $500.&quot;</p>

<p>The authorities say that victim, who was in pain and bruised in the face, refused medical attention. </p>

<p>Police said the suspect is in his 30s and released the photo above of him. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160605064555/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">the Crime Stoppers website</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then entering TIP577.<br>
