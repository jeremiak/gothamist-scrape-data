<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred last Tuesday, March 15th, at 2 a.m.: &quot;[W]hile aboard a South bound N train that was entering the 36 Street station, the suspect placed his hand on the 24 year-old female victim&apos;s leg and then he began to rub his crotch with his other hand. <p>The police released images of the suspect, who is described as 20-25 years old, 5&apos;2&quot; and 120 pounds. He was last seen wearing a grey hooded sweater, light colored pants and dark colored shoes.<br>
