You could tell they were in a relationship.&quot; Later on, the pair were spotted <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110704124143/http://channel6newsonline.com/2011/07/justin-bieber-and-selena-gomez-spotted-in-the-hamptons/">going onto a yacht</a>. Page Six says Bieber, 17, and Gomez, 18, who had been in Manhattan on Thursday, are staying at a private residence.
