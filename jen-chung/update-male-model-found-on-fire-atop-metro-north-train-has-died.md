title: [UPDATE] Male Model Found On Fire Atop Metro-North Train Has Died
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2014_11_bmclell.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012020im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_11_bmclell.jpg" width="365" height="399"> <br> <i style=" width:365px; ;display:block"> Brian McClellan (Facebook)</i></div> <strong>[UPDATE BELOW] T</strong>he man who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012020/http://gothamist.com/2014/11/09/man_on_fire_found_on_top_of_metro-n.php">was found on fire on top of a Metro-North train car</a> yesterday in Connecticut is a 21-year-old model from Ohio. Brian McClellen, who is also an aspiring actor and has been living in New York, had &quot;electrical burns, some third degree, on more than 90 percent of his body,&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012020/http://nypost.com/2014/11/09/man-catches-fire-atop-nyc-commuter-train/">according to the Post</a>.<p></p>

<p>The Metro-North train was headed to New York when it stopped at the Riverside Station in Greenwich just after 5 a.m. The train lost power and train workers were to investigate what happened&#x2014;and found McClellen on fire. The workers put out the fire with a fire extinguisher and McClellen was taken to Westchester Medical Center&apos;s burn unit. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012020/http://connecticut.news12.com/news/police-man-jumps-onto-moving-metro-north-train-1.9599908">News 12 Connecticut reported</a> that police believe the man jumped onto the moving train near the Greenwich/Stamford line and rode it for a while. From the Post: </p><blockquote>Video taken at the Stamford rail station shows an apparently intoxicated McClellen climbing on top of the train, a source said.<p></p>

<p>Investigators believe the victim, who lives near Union Square, was burned after a coming in contact with the train&#x2019;s pantograph, which collects power from the 12,500 volts in the wires.</p></blockquote>The Post adds that the young man is &quot;in critical condition Sunday night after several surgeries... He was wrapped head to toe in bandages, and hospital sources said he was horribly disfigured.&quot;<p></p>

<p>McClellen was booked to work on the upcoming TV show <em>Younger</em> this week.</p>

<p><strong>Update 11:58 a.m.:</strong> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012020/http://www.19actionnews.com/story/27343100/tragic-north-royalton-native-killed-train-surfing">According to one local news outlet</a> and multiple messages from grieving friends and relatives on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012020/https://www.facebook.com/brian.mcclellen?ref=ts&amp;fref=ts">McClellen&apos;s Facebook page</a>, he has died from his injuries. </p>

<p><strong>Update 12:32 p.m.:</strong> A Metro-North spokesman confirmed that McClellen has died.</p>
