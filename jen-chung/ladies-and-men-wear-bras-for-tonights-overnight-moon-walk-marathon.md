<p>Numerous streets around Manhattan will be closed for the first <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130822102331/http://www.walkthewalkamerica.org/site/TR/PowerWalk/WTWFY13?pg=informational&amp;fr_id=1040&amp;type=fr_informational&amp;sid=1000">MoonWalk Marathon tonight</a> into early tomorrow. The 26.2 mile event, run by Walk the Walk, features women and men in decorated bras who are raising awareness and money for Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center&#x2019;s Breast Examination Center in Harlem.</p>

<p>The event started 17 years ago when Londoner Nina Barough decided it would be fun to powerwalk the NYC Marathon in her bra. She and 13 friends raised $40,000 for Breakthrough Breast Cancer Research UK. Since then, the event has been held in London and other UK locations but this year is the first time it&apos;s come back to NYC. </p>

<p>Walkers&#x2014;who are encouraged to decorate their bras creatively&#x2014; will start at 10:00 p.m., walk from Randall&apos;s Island to the East Side of Manhattan, head downtown, then up the West Side, and back to Randall&apos;s Island.
