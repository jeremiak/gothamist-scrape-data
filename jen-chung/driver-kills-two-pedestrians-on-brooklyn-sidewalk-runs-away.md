title: Driver Kills Two Pedestrians On Brooklyn Sidewalk, Runs Away

										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2016_07_bklyncrash75.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012056im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_07_bklyncrash75.jpg" width="640" height="390"> <br> <i> Cops at the scene (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012056/http://pix11.com/2016/08/01/hit-and-run-driver-sought-after-deadly-crash-in-brooklyn-1-in-critical-condition/">PIX 11</a>)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>A speeding driver fatally ran over two friends walking on a Brooklyn sidewalk shortly after midnight this morning and then fled the scene on foot.</p>

<p>Police responded to the crash, near the intersection of Fulton and Chestnut Streets in Cypress Hills, at 12:12  a.m. today and found Delman Maldonado, 41, and Israel Turcios, 56, unconscious and unresponsive. Investigators say the driver of an Acura TL sedan was headed east on Fulton Street at a &quot;high rate of speed&quot; when the driver hit Maldonado and Turcios. The motorist then hit two parked cars, propelling one of the parked cars in front of Faro Del Pacifico Pupuseria restaurant, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012056/http://pix11.com/2016/08/01/hit-and-run-driver-sought-after-deadly-crash-in-brooklyn-1-in-critical-condition/">PIX 11 reports</a>.</p>

<p>Maldonado was pronounced dead at the scene, while Turcio was taken to Brookdale Hospital, where he died. &quot;Friends say they were at a nearby bar owned by the brother of one of the victims and were walking home after putting a friend in a cab,&quot; <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012056/http://abc7ny.com/news/2-men-killed-in-hit-and-run-crash-in-brooklyn/1451681/">according to WABC 7</a>.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012056/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-2-Men-Killed-in-Brooklyn-Hit-and-Run-NYPD-388806742.html">NBC New York reports</a> that friends and family gathered at the crash scene to grieve. &quot;Somebody called me before. &apos;My friend is dead in the street,&apos; Wilmer Tuscio told NBC.  </p>

<p>A friend of Turcio&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016012056/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2016/08/01/brooklyn-hit-and-run-pedestrians-killed/">told WCBS 2</a>, &quot;His kids were grown with kids of their own. He would pay any price to see everyone happy.&quot;</p>

<p>No arrests have been made and the investigation is ongoing.</p>
