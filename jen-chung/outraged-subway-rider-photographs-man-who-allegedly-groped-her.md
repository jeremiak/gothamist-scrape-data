When the victim confronted the individual, he fled off the train at the 149 Street/Grand Concourse station. The victim followed him and took his picture with her cell phone.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The suspect, who is wanted for forcible touching, is described as being 35 to 45 years old, 5&apos;9&quot; and 170 pounds; he was last seen wearing a blue baseball cap, a gray t-shirt and blue jeans.<br>
