<p>Now that the club where <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160301210257/http://gothamist.com/2012/06/15/chris_brown_and_drakes_club_clash_c.php">Drake and Chris Brown</a>&#x2014;or at least their entourages&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160301210257/http://gothamist.com/2012/06/14/chris_brown_tweets_photo_of_injured.php">fought</a>, injuring various other patrons (including <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160301210257/http://gothamist.com/2012/06/22/tony_parker_sues_soho_club_over_dra.php">NBA star Tony Parker</a>), is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160301210257/http://gothamist.com/2012/06/23/wip_charged_by_sla_might_close_down.php">on the verge of being shut down</a>, the club is fighting back by suing the city of New York!  </p>

<p>The company that runs Greenhouse and its basement lounge WiP, where the scuffle occurred, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160301210257/http://online.wsj.com/article/AP02576796b56e44e6a78fe366195766a7.html">claims it&apos;s losing $264,000 a week</a> because of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160301210257/http://gothamist.com/2012/06/17/drake_expected_to_get_slap_on_the_w.php">city-imposed shutdown</a> over code violations.  According to the AP, &quot;City lawyers say the club also filed other papers last week seeking to reopen and the matter remains unresolved.&quot;</p>

<p>The NY State Liquor Authority <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160301210257/http://gothamist.com/2012/06/23/wip_charged_by_sla_might_close_down.php">issued 14 violations</a> to the club, which is also facing numerous lawsuits from injured customers.  Parker, the star from the San Antonio Spurs, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160301210257/http://www.nypost.com/p/sports/olympics/tony_parker_could_miss_olympics_domNmAe6g2WHMKxjGOgAvK">says glass from a thrown bottle</a> penetrated 99% of his left eye and that his hopes of competing in the Olympics are now in jeopardy.  </p>
