</div></div>					<p>Ahead of tonight&apos;s big Met Gala (the annual Costume Institute Gala Benefit), the Metropolitan Museum of Art officially renamed its Costume Institute, changing it to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505201830/http://gothamist.com/2014/01/14/metropolitan_museum_of_art_renames.php">The Anna Wintour Costume Center</a>. First Lady Michelle Obama was on hand for the rechristening.</p>

<p>The First Lady said, &quot;For our young people...this center will be a source of learning &amp; inspiration,&quot; and helped the Vogue editor cut the ribbon on the space (even though it was held at the Temple of Dendur). </p>

<p>This year&apos;s gala will celebrate the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505201830/http://www.metmuseum.org/exhibitions/listings/2014/charles-james-beyond-fashion">Charles James exhibit</a>.</p>
