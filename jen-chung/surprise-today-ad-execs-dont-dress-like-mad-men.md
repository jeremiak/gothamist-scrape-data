Boutique ad agency Droga5 CEO Andrew Essex sums it up: &#x201C;Today, it&#x2019;s the man in the artisanal T-shirt, selvaged denim with holes in them and really expensive vintage sneakers from a small boutique in Japan.  The personalities haven&#x2019;t changed that much, just the accouterments, like carefully harvested facial hair. <strong>Our agency people dress like well-paid teenagers, but that teenager may be 38.</strong>&#x201D;   Yes, and Peggy Olsen never left Don Draper a note letting him know <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428150313/http://www.nytimes.com/slideshow/2008/08/10/nyregion/20080811MAD_12.html">she was getting a tattoo tomorrow</a>.  </p>
