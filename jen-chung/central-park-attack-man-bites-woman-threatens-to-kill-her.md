<p>A woman was bitten by a man while she was jogging in Central Park on Monday night. According to police, it occurred near 102nd Street on the West Drive around 9:20 p.m.</p>

<p>The 30-year-old was apparently yanked from behind. She then struggled with her attacker, and her clothes were ripped in the process. The man <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130907020123/http://awalkintheparknyc.blogspot.com/2013/09/woman-bitten-in-central-park-attack.html">reportedly bit her and said</a>, &quot;I will kill you,&quot; before running off. The victim was treated at Harlem Hospital and released.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130907020123/http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20130903/upper-east-side/woman-bitten-scratched-by-man-while-walking-central-park-sources">According to DNAinfo</a>, the suspect is described as a &quot;man wearing a red flannel shirt, black shorts and white sneakers.&quot;</p>
