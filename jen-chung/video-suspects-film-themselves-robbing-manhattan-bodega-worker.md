<p>The police are asking for the public&apos;s help in identifying three suspects who robbed a bodega worker on Bleecker Street in Manhattan last Saturday.  According to police, the robbery occurred around 1:30 a.m. on April 7, with the suspects approaching the Bleecker Farm and Deli employee with a knife.  A surveillance camera from the bodega shows the suspects, including one who is <em>filming</em> the crime:</p>

The suspects took the employee&apos;s cellphone; the victim was not hurt.  The police describe the suspects as: &quot;1. M/H wearing a dark hooded coat, and a skeleton mask; 2. M/H wearing a dark coat, tan pants, NY Yankee baseball hat and holding a camcorder; 3. M/H wearing a blue hooded sweatshirt, blue jeans and a NY Yankee baseball hat.&quot;   Police ask anyone with information in regards to this robbery to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or to submit tips to the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120602110702/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or to text their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577. </p>
