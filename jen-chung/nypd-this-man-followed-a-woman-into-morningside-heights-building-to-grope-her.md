A woman was molested in front of her apartment door in Morningside Heights on Saturday night, and now police are looking for the culprit.</p>

<p>The incident occurred on August 13th at 11 p.m. in a residential building in the vicinity of West 112th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. According to the NYPD, &quot;the suspect followed the 24 year-old female victim to the door of her apartment and touched her buttocks and genitals over her clothing. The suspect then fled the building eastbound on 112 Street.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The police released images of the man, describing him as 20 to 25 years old, 5&apos;6&quot; and 150 pounds. He was last seen wearing a black baseball cap, a grey shirt, gray shorts, white sneakers, and glasses. Police say he had on a backpack.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). All calls are strictly confidential.<br>
