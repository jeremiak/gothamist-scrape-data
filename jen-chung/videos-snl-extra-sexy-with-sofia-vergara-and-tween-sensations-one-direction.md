Well, if it means getting rid of Gilly, we could probably live with it&#x2014;but Sudeikis&apos;s Mitt Romney impression is getting better and Wiig makes a great Ann!</p>

<p>Of course, SNL took the opportunity to send up Vergara&apos;s va-va-voom looks and her Colombian accent.  She also parlayed her loud, accented persona to play Fran Drescher to Abby Elliott&apos;s Zooey Deschanel. The musical guest was tween sensation <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120408192618/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One_Direction">One Direction</a>, the Simon Cowell-concocted super group from Britain&apos;s X-Factor. </p>
