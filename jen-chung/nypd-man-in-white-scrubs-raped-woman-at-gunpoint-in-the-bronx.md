<p>The police have released a video of a man who allegedly raped a woman at gunpoint on a Bronx street this past weekend and hope the public can help identify him.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at around 5:30 a.m. on Sunday, July 20th. A 24-year-old woman was walking near East Tremont Avenue and Meyers Street when a man, wearing white scrubs and red sneakers, approached her. Police say he had a gun and raped her. </p>
