title: Mugger Threatens Mother & 4-Year-Old: "Give Me Your Bag Or I'm Taking Your Daughter"
---


                            
                            
                            
                            <p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_07_simugger.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130817005602im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_07_simugger.jpg" width="150" height="268" class="image-right"> </span>Police are looking for a suspect wanted in a robbery that occurred on Staten Island on Sunday.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 10:45 p.m., a 41-year-old woman and her four-year-old daughter were at a bus stop in front of 1377 Forest Avenue. A man approached them and stuck a sharp object in the mother&apos;s back, saying, &quot;Give me your bag or I&apos;m taking your daughter.&quot; Police say that he grabbed the child&apos;s hand during the threat and then fled with the mother&apos;s bag. The mother and child did not suffer any injuries. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as being approximately 30 years old, 200 pounds with a stocky build, and around 5&apos;5&quot;. He was wearing a baseball cap, black t-shirt, black and white sneakers and gray shorts.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130817005602/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">the Crime Stoppers website</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577. All tips are confidential. </p>
