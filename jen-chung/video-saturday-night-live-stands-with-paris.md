We stand with you.&quot; She then repeated it in French:</p>

<p>On Friday night, as information was emerging about the devastating attacks in Paris that ultimately claimed 129 lives and injured 352, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160406052105/http://gothamist.com/2015/11/14/video_stephen_colbert_delivers_brie.php">Stephen Colbert said</a>, &quot;We add our thoughts and prayers to everyone in Paris.&quot; He added the show would return on Monday, then sighed.</p>
