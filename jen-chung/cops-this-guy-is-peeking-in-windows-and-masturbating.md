<p>A Bronx peeper is on the loose, police say.<br>
 <br>
On Thursday, July 25, around 12:40 a.m., police received a report about a man who, according to the NYPD, was seen &quot;peering through windows while residents were home and then appeared to masturbate while looking through windows.&quot;</p>

<p>The incident occurred near 2017 Blackrock Avenue. The authorities released video of the suspect, described as being 20-25 years old, 5&apos;7&quot; and 180 pounds. <br>
 <br>

<p>He is wanted for criminal trespass.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782).
