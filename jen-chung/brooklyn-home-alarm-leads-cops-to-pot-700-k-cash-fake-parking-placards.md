<p>Police say that officers checking out a home alarm in East New York ended with the home&apos;s resident being arrested after they found a lot of cash, a gun and 10 pounds of marijuana.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, cops from the 75th Precinct headed to 874 Blake Avenue, after receiving a 911 call about a triggered home security alarm.</p>

<blockquote> Upon arrival, the officers observed the front door and kitchen window opened.  The officers announced their presence and entered the home.  Once inside the house, the officers observe a large quantity of marijuana, large sum of cash, loaded firearm, replica parking placards and drug paraphernalia. </blockquote>

<p>The police got a warrant and say they recovered, &quot;approximately 10.4 pounds of Marijuana; approximately $700,000 US currency; .38 Caliber firearm - loaded and defaced; 2 Replica Parking Placards (New York City Sheriff&apos;s Office and Uniformed Firefighter&apos;s Association); Drug Paraphernalia- scale.&quot;</p>

<p>Chanisha William, 31, who lives at 874 Blake, was arrested and charged with criminal possession of marijuana, criminal possession of a weapon, possession of a forged instrument and drug paraphernalia, plus violation of local law.</p>
