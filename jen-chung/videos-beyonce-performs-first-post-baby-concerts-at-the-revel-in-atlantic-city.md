<p>Last night Beyonce sang hits (&quot;Crazy in Love,&quot; &quot;Single Ladies&quot;) and also merged songs, like her own &quot;If I Were A Boy&quot; with The Goo Goo Dolls&apos; &quot;Iris.&quot; And <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120606204555/http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/music-arts/beyonce-back-diva-performed-live-months-giving-birth-article-1.1084987">while the shows were sellouts</a>, Beyonce fan <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120606204555/https://twitter.com/MissJia/status/206276693143457793">Miss Jia Tweeted</a>, &quot;About a little over midway thru, Revel security got slack and people got in free,&quot; and noted that a few seats around her were free.  Fie on you, ticket resellers! </p>
