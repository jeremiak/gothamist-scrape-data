<p>A party of four walked out of a Staten Island restaurant without paying their hefty New Year&apos;s Eve dinner bill and they&apos;re now being sought for larceny.</p>

<p>On January 1, police say that <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170723090212/http://chinarontheisland.com/">Chinar on the Island</a>, located at 283 Sand Lane, called the police at 3:34 a.m. after one man and three women &quot;departed without paying the bill of $645.00. They were observed leaving the area in a silver SUV towards Father Capodanno Boulevard.&quot;</p>
