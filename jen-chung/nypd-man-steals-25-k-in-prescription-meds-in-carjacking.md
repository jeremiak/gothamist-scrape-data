title: NYPD: Man Steals $25K In Prescription Meds In Carjacking
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_04_robbyqns.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140428030856im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_04_robbyqns.jpg" width="240" height="309" class="image-right"> </span>The police hope the public can help them identify a man who allegedly forced a deliveryman into turning over his vehicle, which was full of medicine, by threatening him at gunpoint.</p>

<p>According to the police, the incident happened on April 18th at 8 a.m. A 49-year-old man making pharmaceutical deliveries was stopped at 150th Street and 14th Avenue by a suspect who had a gun. Police say the suspect &quot;forced the victim into the passenger seat of his blue 2000 Dodge Caravan&quot; and then &quot;drove the victim&apos;s vehicle into Yonkers, NY where the victim was told to exit.&quot; The suspect then continued to drive on with the car, which had $25,000 in prescription medicine. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as being 25-30 years old with a pockmarked face and a bald head. He also speaks Spanish. <br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140428030856/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
