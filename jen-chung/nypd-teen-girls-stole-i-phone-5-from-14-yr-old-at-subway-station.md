---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_11_teensusp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011901im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_11_teensusp.jpg" width="640" height="480" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The NYPD released a photograph of three suspects accused of stealing a 14-year-old girl&apos;s iPhone last month.</p>

<p>According to the police, the victim was walking up the stairs at the Cleveland Street J station when &quot;three female suspects walked up behind her, grabbed her iPhone 5 from her rear pants pocket and fled the location.&quot; The victim wasn&apos;t injured.</p>

<p>Police believe the suspects are all females, ages 14 to 18, and one has dyed red hair. Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto Crime Stoppers&apos; website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011901/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.</p>
