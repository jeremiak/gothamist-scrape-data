										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_06_bxrobbergp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20140629222752im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_06_bxrobbergp.jpg" width="640" height="396" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The police are looking for three suspects in a Bronx incident that resulted in the theft of $8. At gunpoint.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on Wednesday, June 18, at about 4:10 p.m., three individuals followed a 30-year-old man into a Morris Heights building. The police say, &quot;One of the suspects displayed a black firearm and forcibly removed $8 dollars from the victim while the other suspects acted as lookouts. Suspects fled on foot in unknown direction.&quot; The victim was not injured.</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20140629222752if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/Oc29YTDyd2E" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The first suspect is described as a being about 30 years old and 5&apos;4&quot;, wearing black jeans, blue sneakers and black t-shirt. The second suspect was wearing a black t-shirt, dark colored shorts and white sneaker while the third suspect was wearing a white t-shirt, black jeans, black and white sneakers carrying a backpack.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140629222752/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
