<p>The NYPD is searching for a robbery suspect who got extremely violent with a victim in the Bronx.</p>

<p>According to the police, on August 8 at 5:20 p.m., a 21-year-old woman was walking down the stairs at the Fordham Road subway station, when a man approached her. The man tried to take her cellphone and when she resisted, he punched her, breaking her jaw.<br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as M/B/20-25, 5&apos;9&quot;, 180 pounds, last seen wearing a dark colored shirt, blue jeans and silver sneakers. Here&apos;s video:</p>
