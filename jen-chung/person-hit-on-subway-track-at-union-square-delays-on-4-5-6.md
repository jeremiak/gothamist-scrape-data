title: Person Hit on Subway Track at Union Square; Delays on 4, 5, 6
---

	
										<p><img alt="2008_06_ussub.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171031071608im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2008_06_ussub.jpg" width="250" height="182" class="left">This morning, a person was hit by a train by a 4 train at the Union Square subway station around 8:30 a.m.  The police investigation is ongoing (power is being shut off/needs to be shut off), and NYC Transit is running Brooklyn-bound 4 and 5 trains on the local tracks between Grand Central and Brooklyn Bridge-City Hall.  Which means delays on the 4, 5, and 6 lines.</p>

<p>Some 5 trains are also running on the 2 line between 149th Street-Grand Concourse and Nevins.  MTA NYC Transit is also reporting that D and N train service &quot;has resumed with residual delays&quot; after an earlier incident.  </p>
