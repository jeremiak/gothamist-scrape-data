<p>The co-owner of a Bronx bar is at Jacobi Hospital after being shot twice by an apparently angry customer. The victim&apos;s brother and co-owner of the bar says that he was shot because <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108040027/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/men-shot-gut-separate-bronx-harlem-incidents-article-1.2721243">he refused to continuing serving alcohol to the shooter:</a> &quot;He was drunk and because of that, we didn&apos;t serve him.&quot;</p>

<p>Mario Ramirez, 52, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108040027/http://nypost.com/2016/07/22/bar-manager-shot-after-refusing-to-serve-drunk-patron/">got into an argument</a> with the patron at El Rey De Copas, after &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108040027/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2016/07/22/bronx-bar-shooting/">he had been cut off by a waitress</a>.&quot;  The customer, who was described to be in his 40s, then shot Ramirez in the neck and chest.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108040027/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/Bronx-New-York-City-Bar-Owner-Shooting-Injury-387930302.html">NBC New York reports</a>, &quot;Ramirez&apos;s brother said that the man&#x2014;a known patron who had been banned from the bar for causing trouble two years ago&#x2014;ran off down White Plains Road.&quot;</p>

<p>It&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108040027/http://abc7ny.com/news/police-drunk-patron-is-accused-of-shooting-bar-co-owner/1438021/">believed that shooting</a> was caught on surveillance cameras. Police are continuing to investigate.</p>
