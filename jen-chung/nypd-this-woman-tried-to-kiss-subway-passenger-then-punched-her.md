title: NYPD: This Woman Tried To Kiss Subway Passenger, Then Punched Her
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150424233310/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_04_subattack.jpg"> <img alt="2013_04_subattack.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150424233310im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2013/04/2013_04_subattack-thumb-176x329-784392.jpg" width="140" height="261" class="image-right"> </a> </span>The police hope the public can help them find a woman who is accused of assaulting a senior citizen on a 6 train on Wednesday&#x2014;apparently the suspect first tried to kiss the 68-year-old woman and then attacked her when the other woman refused the kiss.  </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at around 8 p.m. on Wednesday, April 10, the 68-year-old woman got onto the 6 train at the East 59th Street station. The suspect sat down next to her, and, the police say, &quot;put a hand on the victim and kissed her on the forehead. When the victim rebuffed the suspect&apos;s advances, the suspect became aggressive and grabbed the victim&apos;s hair, and punched her on the head and face.&quot; </p>

<p>The suspect then fled at the East 68th Street stop. The victim suffered a bruise on her head. </p>

<p>Police describe the suspect as a black female in her mid-40s, about 5&apos;11&quot;, 150 lbs., with black hair and brown eyes. The police also released a photo of her (right).</p>

<p>Anyone with information regarding this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150424233310/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.  All calls are strictly confidential.</p>
