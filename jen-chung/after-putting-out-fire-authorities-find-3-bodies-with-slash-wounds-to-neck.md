title: After Putting Out Fire, Authorities Find 3 Bodies With Slash Wounds To Neck
---

<p>A <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013835/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/09/09/3-dead-after-early-morning-apartment-fire-in-queens/">fire tore through a Queens apartment</a> this morning, and after firefighters put out the blaze, three bodies were found. Police say they had slash wounds to the neck.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013835/http://7online.com/news/3-found-dead-with-stab-wounds-after-queens-fire-extinguished/300618/">A neighbor called when smoke</a> was spotted coming an apartment door at 143-40 Roosevelt Avenue around 4:50 a.m. The fire was extinguished by 5:30 a.m., and then firefighters found the three bodies in the living room. While the victims appeared to be adults, two males and a female, WABC 7 reports that &quot;the bodies were burned beyond recognition.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The ME&apos;s office will determine the cause of death. The investigation is ongoing.</p>
