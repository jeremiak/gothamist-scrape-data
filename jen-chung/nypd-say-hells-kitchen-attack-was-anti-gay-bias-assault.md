<p>Police say a man beaten up on a Manhattan sidewalk is the victim of an apparent hate crime.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 4:45 a.m. on March 8th. A 36-year-old man standing in front of 765 9 Avenue, the address of the pub The Cannery, &quot;when the two suspects assaulted him while invoking his sexual orientation. The suspects then fled the scene.&quot;</p>

<p>The victim was treated at St. Luke&apos;s Roosevelt Hospital for a fractured nose and bruising to the face.</p>
