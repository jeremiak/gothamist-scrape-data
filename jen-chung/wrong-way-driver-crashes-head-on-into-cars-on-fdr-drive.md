<p>The FDR Drive has reopened after crashes caused by a wrong way driver this morning. </p>

<p>Apparently the driver, a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011935/http://7online.com/news/fdr-drive-reopened-following-wrong-way-crash/393225/">woman in her 30s</a>, entered the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011935/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/11/13/several-injured-in-wrong-way-crash-on-fdr-drive/">FDR at 34th Street</a>&#x2014;she went south in the northbound lanes at 4 a.m. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011935/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-FDR-Drive-East-Side-Highway-Wrong-Way-Crash-Traffic-Jam-282538721.html">According to NBC New York</a>, her car &quot;struck two vehicles near 23rd Street...Several people were hurt, with at least three victims taken to Bellevue Hospital in serious condition.&quot;</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2014_11_wrongway2.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510011935im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_11_wrongway2.jpg" width="640" height="360"> <br> <i> Via WCBS 2</i></div> <p></p>

<p>The driver is in critical condition, as is another person; three others were injured. The FDR was closed for about three hours. </p>
