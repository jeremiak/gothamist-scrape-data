<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_04_robbery73.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130706041345im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_04_robbery73.jpg" width="150" height="236" class="image-right"> </span>The police are asking for the public&apos;s help in identifying and finding a man who robbed a Brooklyn supermarket&#x2014;and shot the manager.</p>

<p>On Monday, April 29, at about 3:44 p.m., the NYPD says man entered the Fine Fare supermarket at 33 Truxman Street, pointing a gun at the cashier and taking the cash register door. The police say when the manager tried to intervene, &quot;the suspect fired one shot, striking him in the shoulder.&quot; A worker <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130706041345/http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20130430/bedford-stuyvesant/video-robber-shoots-bed-stuy-supermarket-manager-flees-with-cash">told DNAinfo</a>, &quot;I heard a shot, and I saw him go down on floor.&quot; No one else was injured.</p>

<p>The suspect, who fled, is described as a man in his 40s, about 5&apos;10&quot; with a medium build, wearing a black Kansas City Royals baseball cap, blue jeans and a black jacket.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regard to this incident or the identity and whereabouts of the suspect is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130706041345/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
