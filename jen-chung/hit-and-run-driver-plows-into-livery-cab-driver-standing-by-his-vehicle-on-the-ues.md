title: Hit-And-Run Driver Plows Into Livery Cab Driver Standing By His Vehicle On The UES
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2015_10_livcabues.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151222151126im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_10_livcabues.jpg" width="640" height="426"> <br> <i> The scene on Madison Avenue (David Torres)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>A livery cab driver who was standing by his vehicle was struck by a hit-and-run driver on the Upper East Side around 1:45 a.m. today. Police say they apprehended the driver on the west side of Manhattan later this morning. </p>

<p>The livery cab driver, 62, appears to have been standing behind his car&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151222151126/http://abc7ny.com/news/livery-driver-struck-in-hit-and-run-on-upper-east-side/1022777/">WABC 7 says</a> he was getting out of his car&#x2014;on Madison Avenue near 80th Street when he was sideswiped by the driver of a Grand Marquis. The driver fled the scene, and the victim was taken to Bellevue Hospital with a serious leg injury.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-none"> <img alt="2015_10_livcabuws1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151222151126im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_10_livcabuws1.jpg" width="640" height="426"> <br> <i>The alleged hit-and-run driver and his passenger on West End Avenue (David Torres)</i></div> <p></p>

<p>An NYPD spokesman says the driver and his passenger were taken into custody at 71st Street and West End Avenue. Charges are pending. </p>
