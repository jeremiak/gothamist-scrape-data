title: Cops Seek Suspect Who Stabbed 6 Train Rider In The Bronx

										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_10_6trainsuspe.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012452im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_10_6trainsuspe.jpg" width="640" height="424" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The police are looking for a suspect involved in a violent dispute at a 6 train station yesterday afternoon. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 2:40 p.m., the 18-year-old male victim standing at the southbound platform of the Elder Avenue 6 train station got into a dispute with another male. The fight escalated and the suspect stabbed the victim several times in the torso before fleeing.</p>

<p>The victim is in stable condition at St. Barnabas Hospital. Police released video of the suspect:</p>
