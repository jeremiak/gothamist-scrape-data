<p>Police officers are investigating the death of a woman who was found on a Brooklyn street early this morning.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at around 4:31 a.m. today, officers responded to a 911 call about a &quot;past assault in front of 110 West 9 Street... Upon arrival, officers observed a 51-year-old female with a slash wound to the throat, sitting on a bench in front of the location. EMS also responded and pronounced the female deceased at the scene.&quot;</p>

<p>There are no arrests at this time. </p>
