<p>Police are investigating a homicide on a midtown Manhattan street that occurred earlier this morning.</p>

<p>Authorities say that around 5:10 a.m., officers responded to a 911 call about &quot;an assault in progress in front of 11 West 32 Street.&quot; When the cops arrived, they found a 33-year-old man who was stabbed in the chest.</p>

<p>The victim, identified as Upper East Side resident Howard Chi, was taken to Bellevue Hospital, where he was pronounced deceased. </p>

<p>There are no arrests at this time.</p>
