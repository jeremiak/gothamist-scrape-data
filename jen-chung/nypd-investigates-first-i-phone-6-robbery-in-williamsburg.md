<p>The NYPD is asking for your help in solving what appears to be their first <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013111/http://gothamist.com/tags/iphone6">iPhone 6</a> robbery investigation.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident took place on Tuesday, September 30th, at 8:55 p.m.: A 28-year-old woman was walking near 85 North 5th Street when the male suspect &quot;removed her white and silver iPhone 6 and fled on foot to parts unknown.&quot; Here&apos;s video of the suspect:</p>

<p><iframe width="480" height="360" src="//web.archive.org/web/20150510013111if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/Atb16U1kZXM?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Police describe him as a &quot;Male, White Hispanic, wearing sneakers and jeans.&quot; Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510013111/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
