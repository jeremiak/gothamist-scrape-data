<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430041138/http://gothamist.com/2012/03/22/omg_baby_giraffe_photos_baby_giraff.php#photo-1">adorable baby Baringo giraffe</a> at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150430041138/http://www.bronxzoo.com/">Bronx Zoo</a> was enjoying the fine weather this past weekend.  Watch her walking around the African Plains exhibit&#x2014;and, at 42 seconds in, discover a butterfly!</p>

<p>The calf, which is still unnamed (the Bronx Zoo names all of its giraffes in memory of Mr. and Mrs. James Carter, benefactors for the Carter Giraffe Building),  was approximately 6 feet tall and over 100 pounds at birth; the Wildlife Conservation Society says she could eventually grow to 16 feet and weigh 2,600 pounds.</p>
