title: Video: Cops Seek Tattooed Man Who Robbed Livery Cab In Murray Hill
---

	
										<p> <span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2012_02_livcabsus.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508164150im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_02_livcabsus.jpg" width="350" height="316" class="image-left"> </span>Police have released very clear surveillance video of a man who allegedly robbed a livery cab driver after a trip from Queens to Manhattan.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, last Tuesday, March 20, a 10 p.m., the suspect picked up a livery cab in Queens and asked to go to 200 East 36th Street.  Once there, the suspect threatened the driver with a knife and forced him out of the cab.  He took an unspecified amount of money from the victim and then fled the scene.  The driver was unhurt.</p>

<p>The suspect is described as a male with star tattoos on both elbows; the video below is pretty clear.  Police ask anyone with information in regards to this robbery is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS or to submit tips to the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508164150/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or to text their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
