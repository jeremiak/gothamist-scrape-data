title: Anti-Gay Ketchup Bottle Attack In West Village Diner
---


	
	
	
	<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2013_04_biascrimesu.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20130511001643im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_04_biascrimesu.jpg" width="180" height="223" class="image-right"> </span>The police released photographs and a description of a man accused of a bias crime in the West Village, inside the Waverly Diner.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, on Monday, April 1st, at 4:40 a.m., a 33-year-old woman was inside the diner when she was approached by a man who &quot;made anti-gay statements and struck her in the face with a ketchup bottle causing a laceration to her forehead.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The suspect is described as a black male, approximately 6&apos;1&quot; and 200 pounds, who was wearing a dark-colored baseball cap, a grey baseball-style shirt with dark sleeves, blue jeans and dark-colored sneakers. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477).  The public can also submit tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130511001643/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.</p>
