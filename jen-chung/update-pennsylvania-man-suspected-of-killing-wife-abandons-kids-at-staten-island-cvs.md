title: UPDATE: Pennsylvania Man Suspected Of Killing Wife Abandons Kids At Staten Island CVS
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2014_08_pasusp.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014933im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_08_pasusp.jpg" width="278" height="323"> <br> <i style=" width:278px; ;display:block"> Keith Belajonas</i></div> After allegedly killing his wife and kidnapping their children, a Pennsylvania man headed to Staten Island. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014933/http://www.nbcphiladelphia.com/news/local/Amber-Alert-Belajonas-270278501.html">According to NBC 10</a>, Keith Belajonas apparently &quot;drove to a Staten Island CVS where a relative works and allegedly robbed the pharmacy at gunpoint then left the children at the store.&quot; <p></p>

<p>Upper Darby police say that they responded to calls about gunshots at Belajonas&apos; home this morning. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014933/http://www.philly.com/philly/news/Amber_Alert_issued_after_woman_found_dead_in_Drexel_Hill_2_children_missing.html?c=r">Philly.com reports</a>, &quot;Belajonas apparently posted a message on Facebook about an argument with his wife, and said she was cheating on him... Upper Darby police said neighbors heard a struggle at the residence and a child said his mother was dead as Belajonas and the boys left the home.&quot; Neighbors also said they heard one child say, &quot;is Mommy dead?&quot;</p>

<p>An Amber Alert was issued for the children, ages 4 and 6, saying that Belajonas was in a gray Nissan Pathfinder with the Pennsylvania license plate JDH7477. Police are still looking for him. </p>

<p><b>Update</b>: <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510014933/http://www.nbcphiladelphia.com/news/local/Amber-Alert-Belajonas-270278501.html">Belajonas was found dead</a>, in his car, on a Staten Island Street, according to police.</p>
