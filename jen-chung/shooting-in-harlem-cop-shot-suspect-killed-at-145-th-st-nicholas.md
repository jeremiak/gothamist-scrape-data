<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right" style=" width:365px; "> <img alt="2012_02_stnich.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20120216080118im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2012_02_stnich.jpg" width="365" height="302"> <br> <i>The St. Nicholas Avenue subway station at 145th Street</i></div> A police officer chasing a suspect in Harlem was shot around 4:20 p.m. this afternoon&#x2014;and  the suspect is dead.  <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120216080118/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/manhattan/nypd_officer_shot_in_uptown_manhattan_1yibbDyCXLrGwvq9Uupe9O?utm_source=SFnewyorkpost&amp;utm_medium=SFnewyorkpost">According to the Post</a>, a Queens detective had been chasing a man into the subway station at West 145th Street and St. Nicholas Avenue when he was hit: &quot;The officer was shot once in the left arm and rushed to Columbia Presbyterian Hospital, officials said. He is expected to survive, they said.&quot;<p></p>

<p>The suspect was pronounced dead at the scene.  Apparently the man was &quot;being sought in the brutal shooting of a 21-year-old woman in the Rockaways yesterday, was shot once in the chest, authorities said.&quot;  (The cop is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120216080118/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/harlem-shooting-leaves-injured-suspect-dead-article-1.1022561">part of the violent felony squad</a>.)</p>

<p>The MTA announced, &quot;Due to a police investigation at the 145th Street Station #A #B #C #D trains are bypassing the station in both directions.&quot;</p>
