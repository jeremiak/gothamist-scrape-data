<p>The police are looking for a sicko who forcibly touched a 12-year-old girl on a Queens sidewalk over the weekend.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, at 8 a.m. on Saturday, December 6th, a 12-year-old girl was walking near 195 Street and Hillside Avenue &quot;when the suspect walked up behind her, pulled her towards him and made a comment about her body.&quot;</p>

<p>Luckily the girl was able to pull away and the suspect fled. Police released video of the suspect, describing him as a 20-year-old male:</p>
