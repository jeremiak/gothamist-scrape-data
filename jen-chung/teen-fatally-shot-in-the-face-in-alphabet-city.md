										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="2013_07_118aven.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504120740im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_07_118aven.jpg" width="300" height="278"> <br> <i style=" width:300px; ;display:block"> 118 Avenue 3 (Google Maps)</i></div> An 18-year-old died after being shot in the face last night <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504120740/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/2-men-shoot-teen-face-ave-nypd-article-1.1404283?localLinksEnabled=false">in front of the Jacob Riis Houses on Avenue D</a>. <p></p>

<p>According to police, they responded to a 911 call at 10:47 p.m. at 118 Avenue D and found Deontay Moore with a gunshot wound to the head. Moore was brought to Bellevue Hospital, where he died. The suspected gunman fled the scene, and it&apos;s believed he was on a bicycle.</p>

<p>A woman who lives at 108 Avenue D <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504120740/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/manhattan/man_shot_in_the_face_in_alphabet_D6vOX1BKtV2uZE80WNAw1I?utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_content=">told the Post</a>, &quot;This is why I keep my kids inside. He pulls his gun out and just shot him down. I just yelled &#x2018;Oh my god!&#x2019;I just started screaming.&quot; The Post adds, &quot;According to cops, unruly residents began throwing trash and other objects onto the crime scene below, forcing cops to call in back-up and emergency services.&quot;</p>

<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504120740/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/crime/1-dead-9-hurt-separate-shootings-nyc-article-1.1404355?localLinksEnabled=false">Daily News reports</a> that the shooting might have stemmed from the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504120740/http://gothamist.com/2013/01/05/teen_fatally_shot_on_les_may_have_b.php">January 4 fatal shooting of Raphael Ward</a>: &quot;Sources said Ward was targeted because of a prior shooting in Harlem. But Moore&#x2019;s pals said he was just out with the wrong people when the bullets flew.&quot; A friend insisted, &quot;He was just a normal teenager, hanging outside.&quot;</p>
