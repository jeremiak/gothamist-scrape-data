<p>On the last <em>Saturday Night Live</em> of the year, the show decided to pay homage to President Barack Obama, &quot;the first, maybe last, black President,&quot; while also paying tribute to Run-D.M.C.&apos;s seasonal classic, &quot;Christmas in Hollis.&quot; Chance the Rapper and Kenan Thompson rap, &quot;Hey kids enjoy the presents while you can, because next year you might get a bomb from Iran,&quot; and &quot;I&apos;ve been drinking eggnog like I don&apos;t care / Imma hurt myself before I lose Obamacare.&quot; </p>

<p>At one point, Leslie Jones appears: &quot;Girls what&apos;s my weakness? Joe Biden / He makes me feel funny in my Christmas wreath.&quot;</p>
