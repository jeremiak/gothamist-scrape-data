<p>The Paley Center just announced its <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030205349/http://media.paleycenter.org/paleyfest-ny-2014/">2014 PaleyFest lineup</a>, and get ready, &apos;cause Daryl Dixon and Detective Olivia Benson will both be on hand to discuss their respective shows.</p>

<p>The fest (which <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030205349/http://gothamist.com/2013/09/03/paleyfest_orange_is_the_new_black.php">fully arrived here</a> last year) takes place October 11th through 21st, and here are the seven big panels they&apos;ll be hosting:</p><blockquote><strong>10/11: The Walking Dead</strong>, 7 p.m. <br>
Scheduled to appear: Andrew Lincoln, Norman Reedus, Danai Gurira, Lauren Cohan, Steven Yeun, Melissa McBride, Robert Kirkman, Scott Gimple, Gale Anne Hurd, Greg Nicotero<p></p>

<p><strong>10/12: Archer</strong>, 2 p.m.<br>
Scheduled to appear: H. Jon Benjamin, Aisha Tyler, Jessica Walter, Judy Greer, Chris Parnell, Adam Reed, Matt Thompson, Casey Willis</p>

<p><strong>10/13: Law &amp; Order: SVU</strong>, 7 p.m.<br>
Scheduled to appear: Dick Wolf, Warren Leight, Mariska Hargitay<br>
Other panelists to be announced</p>

<p><strong>10/15: Bob&apos;s Burgers</strong>, 7 p.m.<br>
Scheduled to appear: Loren Bouchard, H. Jon Benjamin<br>
Other panelists to be announced</p>

<p><strong>10/18: Blue Bloods</strong>, 3:30 p.m.<br>
Scheduled to appear: Tom Selleck<br>
Other panelists to be announced</p>

<p><strong>10/18: Gotham</strong>, 7 p.m.<br>
Scheduled to appear: Benjamin McKenzie, Robin Lord Taylor, Sean Pertwee, Danny Cannon</p>

<p><strong>10/19: Marvel&apos;s Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.</strong>, 4 pm<br>
Scheduled to appear:  Clark Gregg, Jeph Loeb<br>
Other panelists to be announced</p></blockquote>Tickets go on sale tomorrow (9/19) to the general public, but <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030205349/http://www.ticketweb.com/snl/EventListings.action?pl=nypf&amp;orgId=153842">Paley Center members</a> can buy tickets today. <p></p>
