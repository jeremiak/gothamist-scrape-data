title: NYPD: Brooklyn Bridge Park Cellphone Thief Took This Helpful Selfie
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2016_10_selfie1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171016014110im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2016_10_selfie1.jpg" width="200" height="267" class="image-right"> </span>Police are looking for the person who stole a cellphone from a bag at Brooklyn Bridge Park&#x2014;and they say the suspect took this selfie after stealing the gadget.</p>

<p>The incident occurred last month, on September 23rd, at around 6:40 p.m.: The victim, a 21-year-old man, was playing soccer at Pier 5 and left his bag on the ground.</p>

<p>&quot;Upon returning, he noticed his bag was missing. The bag contained his Sony Experia cell phone, a Chase credit card and other personal items.... A photo was obtained from the victim&apos;s cellphone as the individual took a photo of himself and was captured via victim&apos;s storage subscription service,&quot; according to the NYPD.</p>

<p>The suspect is wanted for grand larceny.<br>
 <br>
Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). All calls are strictly confidential.</p>
