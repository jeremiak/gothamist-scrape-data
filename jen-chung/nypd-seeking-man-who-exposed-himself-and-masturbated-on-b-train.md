<p>Police are hoping the public can help them identify a man who was accused of public lewdness on a subway train last week. </p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the man &quot;exposed himself and masturbated while aboard a Brooklyn bound B train&quot; on February 20th, around 9:20 p.m. He left the train at DeKalb Avenue.</p>

<p>He is described as being around 59 years old and 245 pounds, and authorities released images of him. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170226084735/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577. All calls are strictly confidential.</p>
