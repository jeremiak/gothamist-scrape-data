<p>The police are asking for the public&apos;s help in identifying a man who almost attacked a police officer in the Bronx yesterday morning.</p>

<p>According to the police, at about 8:50 a.m., a cop noticed a man jumping a turnstile at the White Plains Road and Burke Avenue subway station. When the police officer tried to stop the suspect, the suspect &quot;began to swing a wooden baseball bat at the officer and then fled on foot out of the station.&quot; The cop wasn&apos;t injured and the bat was recovered. Here&apos;s video of the suspect running: </p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20131013020444if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/83nVTItDMP8" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>The suspect is described as about 5&apos;4&quot; and 150lbs with a medium build, last seen wearing a black sweatshirt, black t-shirt, blue jeans and red sneakers with white shoe laces. Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto Crime Stoppers&apos; website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131013020444/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.<br>
