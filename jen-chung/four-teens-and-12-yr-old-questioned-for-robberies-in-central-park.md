<p>Police are reportedly questioning a number of young individuals for robberies in and near Central Park this morning. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140424034657/http://awalkintheparknyc.blogspot.com/2014/04/3-teens-nabbed-in-central-park-attacks.html">According to A Walk In The Park</a>, &quot;Police say the children - ages 12, 13 and 14,  first approached a 32-year-old man at 5th Avenue and East 95th Street outside the park at 5:15 a.m. this morning. One of the teens was wielding a stick. The group demanded the man&apos;s property,  he hesitated and one of the teens punched him in the face. The attackers then fled into the park at 96th Street.&quot; </p>

<p>Then, about 15 minutes later, &quot;[T]he teens approached two women, 48 and 46, near the reservoir.  The assailants approached the woman and demanded their property.  The 48 year-old handed over and iphone and the 46-year-old handed over an ipod and the perps fled.&quot;</p>

<p>Police told us that five people were being questioned&#x2014;the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140424034657/http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/teen-muggers-busted-central-park-spree-cops-article-1.1763649">Daily News says</a> that three were taken into custody, &quot;Then the oldest suspect, a 16-year-old, and a 14-year-old went to the 23rd Precinct to ask about their friends. Police realized they were part of the robbery group and arrested them too, officials said.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140424034657/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2014/04/21/cbs-2-5-teens-questioned-after-alleged-central-park-robbery-spree/">WCB2 2 reports</a> that Angel Munoz, 16, was charged with two counts of robbery.</p>
