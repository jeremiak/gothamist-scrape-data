<p>A 76-year-old woman died yesterday after being struck by a driver. Police say that the driver was apparently backing into a parking spot.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred at 5:50 p.m. near Madison Avenue and 95th Street. Police officers found Mary Jo Myszelow conscious with head injuries; she later died at Mount Sinai Hospital.</p>

<p>Police say that an investigation revealed that &quot;a black 2013 Lincoln sedan was heading north bound on Madison Avenue when the operator noticed a parking spot and attempted to back his vehicle into it.  As the operator backed up, his car struck the 76 year-old female as she was attempting to cross Madison Avenue midblock from the east side to the west.&quot;</p>

<p>The driver remained on the scene. The investigation is ongoing.<br>
