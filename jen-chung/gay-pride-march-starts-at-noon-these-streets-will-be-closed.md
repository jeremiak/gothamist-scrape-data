<p>Today is the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807005026/http://www.nycpride.org/events">Heritage of Pride parade</a>! The grand marshals are DOMA plaintiff <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130807005026/http://gothamist.com/tags/ediewindsor">Edie Windsor</a>, Harry Belafonte and Earl Fowlkes.
