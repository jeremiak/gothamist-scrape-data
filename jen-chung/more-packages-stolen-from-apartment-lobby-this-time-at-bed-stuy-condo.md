title: More Packages Stolen From Apartment Lobby, This Time At Bed-Stuy Condo
---

<p>Package thieves are <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160711234216/http://gothamist.com/2015/11/11/bedford_stop_unsafe.php">running rampant</a>, stealing items left in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160711234216/http://gothamist.com/2015/11/03/people_suck.php">unattended apartment building lobbies</a>. The NYPD just announced another burglary, and hope that the public can help them identify two males wanted for questioning.</p>

<p>Police say that on November 19th at 1:50 p.m., a suspect or suspects &quot;entered 582 Marcy Avenue and removed packages from the lobby of the building that were delivered by Fed-Ex earlier in the day.&quot; </p>

<p>The authorities released photos of two persons of interest who are wanted for questioning about the incident. </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2015_12_packthievespoi.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160711234216im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2015_12_packthievespoi.jpg" width="640" height="457" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>The building <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160711234216/http://www.elliman.com/new-york-city/582-marcy-avenue-3a-brooklyn-llfuprf">has been called &quot;The Pioneer Condo&quot;</a> because it&apos;s one of the first built in Bed-Stuy.</p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160711234216/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers website</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then entering TIP577.<br>
