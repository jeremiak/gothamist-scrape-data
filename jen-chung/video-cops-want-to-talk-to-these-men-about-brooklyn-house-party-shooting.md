<p>The NYPD is asking for the public&apos;s help in identifying two men wanted for questioning in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130704065648/http://gothamist.com/2013/06/30/eight_people_shot_at_brooklyn_house.php">Saturday&apos;s house party shooting</a> that left nine people wounded. The incident, who occurred just before 1 a.m. East Flatbush, was allegedly sparked when someone was denied entry into the party on East 52nd Street. </p>

<p>There were dozens of people at the party. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130704065648/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/nypd_blotter/nypd_daily_blotter_iqPJQNIn7hZHc4ULguUeOP?utm_medium=rss&amp;utm_content=NYPD%20Blotter">According to the Post</a>, the rejected person came back at 12:30 a.m. and started firing. A neighbor said, &#x201C;It was so fast and loud that I thought it was fireworks. Then people were running and screaming, &#x2018;Gun!&#x2019; It was raining bullets.&quot; Here&apos;s video of the two people:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="//web.archive.org/web/20130704065648if_/http://www.youtube.com/embed/1iIZwPzrE4o" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>According to the police, they were called at 12:50 a.m. and when they got to the home, they found seven people shot. EMD took five victims to Brookdale Hospital and two to Kings County Hospital. Then it turns out a victim walked into Methodist Hospital and yet another went to Beth Israel Hospital.  All victims were in stable condition&#x2014;the injuries were gunshot wounds to the feet, legs, side and thigh.</p>

<p>The police describe the persons of interest as a man in his 20s, 6&apos;, about 200 pounds and wearing a white tank top and another man with a slim build wearing a white tank top and baseball cap. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers Website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130704065648/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577</p>
