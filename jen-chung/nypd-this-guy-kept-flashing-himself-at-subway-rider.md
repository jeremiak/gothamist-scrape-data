										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="2014_10_plbx.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012801im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2014_10_plbx.jpg" width="240" height="307" class="image-right"> </span>The police hope that the public can help them identify a suspect in a public lewdness incident that took place over the summer.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, it happened on Wednesday, July 16, at 7:10 p.m., along the  2/5 line near West 160th Street: A 50-year-old woman was sitting across from a man who &quot;exposed himself numerous times to her.&quot; Police say, &quot;The victim was able to take a photo of the suspect while he pretended to sleep.&quot;<br>
 <br>
The suspect appears to be in his early 40s.  Anyone with information in regards to this public lewdness is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS.</p>

<p>The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510012801/http://WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM/">Crime Stoppers Website</a> or texting their tips to 274637(CRIMES) then enter TIP577. All tips are confidential. </p>
