										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502175708/http://gothamist.com/attachments/jen/2013_09_mohawk1.jpg"> <img alt="2013_09_mohawk1.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502175708im_/http://gothamist.com/assets_c/2013/09/2013_09_mohawk1-thumb-300x394-810131.jpg" width="190" height="249" class="image-right"> </a> </span>The police hope the public can help them find a man wanted for a forcible touching incident in Queens</p>

<p>On Saturday, September 21st, at around 9:10 p.m., a 24-year-old woman was walking near <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502175708/https://maps.google.com/maps?client=safari&amp;oe=UTF-8&amp;q=24+Avenue+and+Crescent+Street&amp;ie=UTF-8&amp;hq=&amp;hnear=0x89c25f44bac87103:0xc294485db6fa5194,Crescent+St+%26+24th+Ave,+Queens,+NY&amp;gl=us&amp;ei=liJEUozzOsz54APgo4DIDQ&amp;ved=0CCsQ8gEwAA">24 Avenue and Crescent Street</a> when a man on a bicycle approached from behind and grabbed her buttocks. He fled on the bicycle. </p>

<p>The suspect is described as a man about 20-30 years old with a medium build, about 5&apos;6&quot;-5&apos;10&quot; and a &quot;long curly Mohawk-style hair shaved on the sides. He was wearing a dark jacket with a red stripe on the sleeves, and blue jeans and his bike was silver with a metal basket in the back.&quot;</p>

<p>Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (800) 577-TIPS (8477). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto Crime Stoppers&apos; website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150502175708/http://www.nypdcrimestoppers.com/">www.nypdcrimestoppers.com</a> or by texting their tips to CRIMES (274637), then enter TIP577.<br>
