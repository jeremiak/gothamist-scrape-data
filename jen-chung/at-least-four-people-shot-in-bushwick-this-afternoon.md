<p>Multiple people were hospitalized after being shot in a Brooklyn park earlier this afternoon.</p>

<p>According to police, the incident occurred at 2:20 p.m. in a park at 195 Menahan Street in Bushwick. Four people, three males and one females, were shot.</p>

<p>The FDNY tells us that five people were shot, three &quot;green&quot; tags and two &quot;red&quot; tags, and were taken to Elmhurst, Kings County and Brookdale Hospitals (here&apos;s some <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928020510/http://nycfire.net/forums/index.php?topic=1273.0">info on color-tagging</a>).</p>

<p>We&apos;ll update when more information becomes available. </p>
