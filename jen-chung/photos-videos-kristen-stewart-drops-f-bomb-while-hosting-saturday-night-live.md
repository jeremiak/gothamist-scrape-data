</div></div>					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170329095128/http://gothamist.com/2017/02/05/melissa_mccarthy_plays_sean_spicer.php">Melissa McCarthy&apos;s Sean Spicer impression</a> was certainly the highlight of last night&apos;s <em>Saturday Night Live</em>, and possibly the highlight of the Trump Administration thus far, but rest of the show was an extremely loose affair&#x2014;probably partly due to host Kristen Stewart. While she&apos;s not really a sketch performer, the actress won her monologue where she finally addressed the president&apos;s bizarre obsession with her and Robert Pattinson, &quot;Donald, if you didn&apos;t like me then, you&apos;re really probably not going to like me now. Because I&apos;m hosting SNL and I&apos;m, like, <em>so gay</em>, dude.&quot;</p>

<p>Trump spent <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170329095128/http://gawker.com/remember-donald-trumps-freakish-obsession-with-robert-p-1783687005">many days in 2012 Tweeting about how Stewart cheated</a> on her <em>Twilight</em> co-star Pattinson, and she may have figured out why last night, &quot;Okay. To be fair, I don&apos;t think Donald Trump hated me. I think he&apos;s in love with my boyfriend.&quot; The rest of the monologue had Kate McKinnon and Aidy Bryant showing they could be cool like Stewart. But then Stewart showed how fucking cool she was by saying, &quot;We&apos;ve got a good show and I totally care that I&apos;m here because it&apos;s the coolest f**king thing ever&#x2014;&quot; only for her to cover her mouth and Bryant and McKinnon to gasp. </p>

<p>Stewart recovered by saying, &quot;I&apos;m sorry and Alessia Cara is also here and I&apos;ll never come back. Stick around!&quot;</p>

<p>Stewart, definitely one of the best actresses working today (if you haven&apos;t seen <em>The Clouds of Sils Maria</em> then spare me your opinions), had a couple sketches&#x2014;mainly the pre-taped ones. Vanessa Bayer got to reprise her <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170329095128/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/07/larry_david_snl_pretty_pretty_good.php#photo-7">excellent Totino&apos;s wife</a> commercial, with Stewart playing the sultry sister of one of the Super Bowl party guests. Then, in a digital short, Stewart starred with Pete Davidson in a &quot;Meet Cute&quot; gone terribly wrong to hilarious effect (props to Davidson&apos;s character&apos;s artistic skills).</p>

<p>Other sketches included the Alec Baldwin-as-Trump cold open; &quot;Dry Fridays&quot; at U.Conn; a Super Bowl-themed Family Feud sketch, with McKinnon&apos;s Justin Bieber, Stewart as Gisele Bundchen and Leslie Jones playing Samuel L. Jackson while trying not to crack; a Department of Homeland Security welcome video; and a Weekend Update with Michael Che discussing his Sunday plans to watch &quot;the blackest city in America beat the most racist city I&apos;ve ever been to.&quot; </p>
