Derrick Praileau took a deal for 23 years to life for strangling Sara Bejjani after trying to rape her in condo. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110517121733/http://www.nydailynews.com/news/ny_crime/2011/05/16/2011-05-16_housekeeper_derrick_praileau_pleads_guilty_to_2009_murder_attempted_rape_of_andr.html">The victim&apos;s brother, Joe Bejjani, said</a>, &quot;Nothing is going to help... We lost... I don&apos;t really care about the sentence&#x2014;my sister&apos;s life is gone,&quot; but added, &quot;I&apos;m glad he confessed&#x2014;finally.&quot;<p></p>

<p>Bejjani&apos;s body was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110517121733/http://gothamist.com/2009/09/20/woman_found_brutally_murdered_insid.php">found with a rope around her neck</a>; she was also stabbed.  Praileau <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110517121733/http://gothamist.com/2009/09/21/essex_housekeeper_drunkenly_stabbed.php">had told police</a> that he showed up drunk before his 6 a.m. shift (apparently <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110517121733/http://www.nypost.com/p/news/local/essex_house_sicko_pleads_guilty_vwIx2eba9Ixaq5yzZU6iHJ">looking for a woman to rape</a>), used his key to get into Bejjani&apos;s 10th floor apartment and &quot;just lost it.&quot;  He also told cops he overpowered Bejjani, an investment banker, and told her to &quot;chill.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110517121733/http://manhattanda.org/press-release/district-attorney-vance-announces-guilty-plea-2009-jumeirah-essex-house-murder">Manhattan DA Cyrus Vance said</a>, &quot;This was a brutal and senseless crime. There is an unstated trust we place in people who have access to our homes by virtue of their profession. Today&apos;s guilty plea ensures that this man will serve a lengthy sentence behind bars.&quot;</p>
