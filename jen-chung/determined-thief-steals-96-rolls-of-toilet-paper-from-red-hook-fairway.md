<p>Cops say that a thief managed to steal 96 (!) rolls of toilet paper from the Fairway Market in Red Hook. Naturally, the sight of someone walking out with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130714004215/http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20130710/red-hook/thief-nabs-eight-pack-of-toilet-paper-at-fairway-market-cops-say">eight 12-packs of TP </a>aroused suspicions and a Fairway employee tried to stop him, but then the thief brandished a knife.</p>

<p>The incident occurred last month on June 30th at around 9:45 a.m. The suspect got away, and police say he&apos;s about 5&apos;7&quot; and 165 pounds, wearing a green t-shirt and white sneakers. (He&apos;ll probably also tell you he <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130714004215/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Gysu0kgFwT0">doesn&apos;t have a spare to square</a>.)</p>
