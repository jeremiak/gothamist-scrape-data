<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130619014245/http://austinist.com/tags/noblepig">Beloved</a> Northwest Austin sandwich shop <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130619014245/http://noblesandwiches.com/">The Noble Pig</a> will be serving a special menu at Dale Talde&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130619014245/http://gothamist.com/2012/08/16/pork_slope_brings_honky_tonk_road_h.php#photo-1">Pork Slope</a> tonight. And bring your appetite; some of the specialties include &quot;Crispy Trotters and Pickles&quot; and &quot;Duck Cotto Salami.&quot; </p>

<p>This is the second night of The Noble Pig&apos;s two-night stint: Chef John Bates is also serving &quot;30 Day Aged and Pressed Ham Sandwich with Red Eye Gravy and Greens&quot;&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130619014245/https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=539379712767082&amp;set=a.375889189116136.82181.359458994092489&amp;type=1&amp;relevant_count=1">which looks pretty insane</a>&#x2014; and a &quot;Smoked Beef Tongue Sandwich with Red Pepper Relish and Aioli.&quot; The Crispy Trotters are $9, the Duck Cotto Salami is $10, Pressed Ham is $12 and Tongue Sandwich is $13. </p>

<p>Pork Slope <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130619014245/https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=539378649433855&amp;set=pb.359458994092489.-2207520000.1368643446.&amp;type=3&amp;src=https%3A%2F%2Ffbcdn-sphotos-h-a.akamaihd.net%2Fhphotos-ak-ash3%2F431778_539378649433855_1567350486_n.jpg&amp;size=960%2C776">wrote on Facebook</a>, &quot;Come get some over the next two days before we run out.&quot; The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130619014245/http://www.porkslopebrooklyn.com/">full Pork Slope menu</a> is available as well. </p>
