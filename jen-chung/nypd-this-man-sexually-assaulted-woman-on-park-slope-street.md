<p>Police are hoping the public can help them find a man accused of sexually assaulting a woman on a Brooklyn street last month.</p>

<p>According to the NYPD, the incident occurred in the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160503165930/http://www.nyc.gov/html/nypd/html/precincts/precinct_078.shtml">78th Precinct</a> at 5:25 a.m. on Sunday, December 6th: A 24-year-old woman &quot;was walking on Bergen Street when the suspect approached her from behind, grabbed her buttocks and attempted to pull down her pants. The victim screamed and the suspect fled.&quot;</p>

<p>Police released surveillance video of the suspect, describing him as being in his 30s. </p>
