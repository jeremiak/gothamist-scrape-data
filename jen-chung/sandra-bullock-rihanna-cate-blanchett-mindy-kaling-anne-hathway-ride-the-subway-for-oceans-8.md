<p>The all-female sequel to the <em>Ocean&apos;s Eleven</em> trilogy, titled <em>Ocean&apos;s Eight</em>, isn&apos;t coming to theaters until the summer of 2018, but the lead up photos of the crew filming around NYC are pretty entertaining for now. While a few images have circulated on social media over the past couple of months, the latest comes from the studio as a &quot;first look&quot; teaser, and shows the band of thieves on a subway. (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170202111202/http://gothamist.com/2012/12/04/does_the_mindy_project_have_the_mos.php">A real one</a>!) Do you they get monthly MetroCards, or did, like, Sandra Bullock and Cate Blanchett just get a few $40 pay-per-ride cards and swipe everyone in? </p>

<p>The cast also includes Helen Bonham Carter, Rihanna, Mindy Kaling, Sarah Paulson, Anne Hathaway and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170202111202/http://gothamist.com/tags/awkwafina">Awkafina</a>, who will attempt to pull a heist at the Met Gala. The studio just released this description:</p>

<blockquote>&quot;In Summer 2018, the tide will turn as Debbie Ocean (Sandra Bullock) attempts to pull off the heist of the century at New York City&#x2019;s star-studded annual Met Gala. Her first stop is to assemble the perfect crew: Lou (Blanchett); Nine Ball (Rihanna); Amita (Kaling); Constance (Awkwafina); Rose (Bonham Carter); Daphne Kluger (Hathaway); and Tammy (Paulson).&quot;</blockquote>

<p>They really did film at the Metropolitan Museum of Art, too, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170202111202/http://pagesix.com/2017/01/17/kim-kardashians-oceans-eight-cameo-involves-a-jewelry-heist/">Anna Wintour and Kim Kardashian</a> are among those appearing in the scene.</p>
