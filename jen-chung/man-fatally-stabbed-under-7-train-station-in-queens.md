<p>Earlier today, a man was killed near an Elmhurst subway station after an apparent dispute with a younger man.</p>

<p>The incident occurred around 1:30 p.m., when a 69-year-old man and a 22-year-old man had an argument by the 90th Street station and the older man was fatally stabbed. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130919073230/http://abclocal.go.com/wabc/story?section=news/local/new_york&amp;id=9250354">According to WABC 7</a>, &quot;He was stabbed on the street level and there was no interruption to subway service.&quot;</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130919073230/http://newyork.cbslocal.com/2013/09/16/man-dead-suspect-in-custody-following-stabbing-in-elmhurst/">One witness told WCBS 880</a> that the victim was &quot;yelling in Spanish &#x2018;help me, help me,&apos;&quot; adding that the attacker went up the subway station&apos;s stairs but then turned back to stab the victim again. The witness said he and other witnesses chased the suspect before the police apprehended him.</p>

<p>The victim was pronounced dead at  Elmhurst Hospital. The suspect is identified as Bronx resident Steven Torres, 22. </p>
