</div></div>					<p>Haven&apos;t had your fill of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150108174404/http://gothamist.com/2011/01/09/first_peek_at_2011_no_pants_subway.php">bare-skinned straphangers</a> yet? Here are some of the official <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150108174404/http://improveverywhere.com/2011/01/09/no-pants-2011-new-york-reports/">Improv Everywhere</a> photos documenting this year&apos;s No Pants Subway Ride, which included around 3500 riders. The pranking organization promises official video later this week (fingers crossed for IMAX 3-D!), but for now, enjoy this colorful still documentation from photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150108174404/http://colormekatie.blogspot.com/">Katie Sokoler</a>.</p>
