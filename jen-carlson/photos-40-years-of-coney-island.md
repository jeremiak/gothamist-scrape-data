</div></div><p>Tonight the New York Public Library takes a trip to Coney Island&#x2014;you&apos;re invited and you don&apos;t even have to take that long subway trip. Just head to 42nd Street, where the NYPL will be hosting photographer Harvey Stein, who will be on hand to celebrate his newest book, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110629191614/http://coneyisland40years.com/"><em>Coney Island 40 Years</em></a>.
