</div></div>					<p>Leo is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150511041132/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8LuUM4z9l0M">the new garden gnome</a>! Someone has been bringing cutouts of 1990s Leonardo DiCaprio all over New York City (and sometimes outside of the five boroughs... like Poughkeepsie), and documenting it all on an Instagram account called <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150511041132/http://instagram.com/mydaywithleo">My Day With Leo</a>. Check out Romeo Leo in Grand Central, <em>Titanic</em> Leo in Bryant Park, and is that <em>Growing Pains</em> Leo outside of the Brooklyn Bridge Park ice cream shop? We reached out to the mysterious person behind the project to find out more, and will update when we hear back... but do we really need to know the reasons behind such a beautifully &apos;90s nostalgia project?</p>
