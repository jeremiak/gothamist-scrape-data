<p>Labor Day Weekend is upon us, and you didn&apos;t book an Airbnb in the Hamptons yet? Well, you&apos;ll never get anything now. But perhaps it&apos;s time to open your mind&#x2014;who needs <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151209172826/http://gothamist.com/2014/08/27/rose_shortage.php">a nice crisp ros&#xE9; on the beach</a> when you can eat fried Diet Coke in a parking lot? The NY State Fair is still open for business <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151209172826/http://nysfair.org/">through the 7th</a>. </p>

<p>Instead of dipping your lobster in melted butter, gaze at the rotating butter sculpture featuring Lady Liberty holding high a gallon of milk...</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151209172826if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/YoK2NhRrju8" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Instead of watching your amateur children try and fail to build a sandcastle, behold this sand <em>sculpture</em>...</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151209172826if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/oi9NQKZGwiw" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>Instead of riding your bicycle down the beach roads of Montauk, wind blowing through your hair, ride this exotic animal that&apos;s been taken out of its natural habitat for your entertainment...</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151209172826if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/9HhZf_33f5w" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>You can also check out sets from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151209172826/http://nysfair.org/entertainment/music/">the Steve Miller Band <em>and</em> Nick Jonas</a>, which you can&apos;t do at the Montauk Surf Club. </p>

<p>Seriously though, State Fairs are fun and cheap ($1 to $10) and even <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151209172826/http://gothamist.com/2014/10/27/taylor_swift_nyc_nye.php">NYC Cultural Ambassador Taylor Swift</a> enjoyed this one in Syracuse.</p>
