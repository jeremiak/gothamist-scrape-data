</div></div>					<p>We&apos;ve been keeping our eye on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160318020800/http://brooklyncrab.com/">Brooklyn Crab</a>, which has been building out their space in Red Hook for a little while now. The restaurant is run by the people behind the great rooftop Mexican restaurant <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160318020800/http://www.yelp.com/biz/alma-brooklyn">Alma</a>, and they haven&apos;t announced their opening date yet (but hint that it&apos;s coming &quot;soon&quot;). Here&apos;s what we do know so far:</p>

<ul><li>There will be an 18 hole mini golf course
</li><li>There will be ping pong
</li><li>There will be a bocce court
</li><li>There will be shuffleboard (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160318020800/http://gothamist.com/tags/shuffleboard">YES!</a>)
</li><li>There will be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160318020800/https://www.facebook.com/permalink.php?story_fbid=405361889503819&amp;id=321855861187756">a free shuttle</a> to bring you to and from the subway
</li><li>There will be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160318020800/https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=227504690695439&amp;set=o.321855861187756&amp;type=1&amp;theater">a giant shark</a>
</li><li>There will be two stories, and some <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160318020800/https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=366400073400001&amp;set=a.362614177111924.89263.321855861187756&amp;type=1&amp;theater">outdoor seating</a>
</li><li>There will be blue crabs, jonah crabs, shrimp, salads, oysters, lobsters, creamed spinach, corn on the cob, clams, and more on the menu</li></ul>

<p>Their hours will be Sunday through Thursday, 4 to 10 p.m., and Friday and Saturday, 4 to 11 p.m. Keep updated via their Twitter <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160318020800/https://twitter.com/#!/BrooklynCrab">@BrooklynCrab</a>.</p>
