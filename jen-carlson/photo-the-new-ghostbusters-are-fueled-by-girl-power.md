										<p>The <em>Ghostbusters</em> reboot has been filming up in Boston (which will fill in for NYC, <a href="www.theonion.com/article/pretty-cute-watching-boston-residents-play-daily-g-31554">which is pretty darn cute</a>) and images from the set have slowly been released over the past month.</p>

<p>While an entire coffee table book filled with correspondences and rumors regarding a <em>Ghostbusters</em> reboot could (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151030042751/http://gothamist.com/2011/12/22/bill_murray_allegedly_shredded_the.php">and should</a>) be published, the final decision was to step away from the core four: Dr. Peter Venkman, Dr. Raymond Stantz, Dr. Egon Spengler, and Winston Zeddmore. Instead, this one stars Melissa McCarthy, Kristen Wiig, Leslie Jones and Kate McKinnon as the new Ghostbusters. As you may have heard, these are all women.</p>
