</div></div><p>We&apos;ve been staring into the cloudy abyss of fog all week, and are beginning to feel like we&apos;re living in an <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120525154643/http://gothamist.com/2012/05/14/mad_men_11.php">episode of <em>Mad Men</em></a>, or maybe <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120525154643/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5s7XG0eEHSk">that Stephen King movie</a>. Either way, it&apos;s <em>FEAR WITHOUT END</em>... but it&apos;s also kind of pretty, albeit dreary. So send photos of this beautiful nightmare to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120525154643/mailto:photos@gothamist.com">photos@gothamist.com</a> and we&apos;ll compose a gallery. You can also hashtag them #Gothamist and #Fog on Instagram, or tag them &quot;Gothamist&quot; and &quot;Fog&quot; on Flickr. Feel free to include a line of prose, it&apos;s what Sylvia Plath would have done.</p>
