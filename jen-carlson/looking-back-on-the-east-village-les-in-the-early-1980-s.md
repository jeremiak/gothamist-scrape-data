					<p>Artist and photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170107165526/http://sbutcher.com/">Steve Butcher</a> documented Manhattan in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170107165526/http://gothamist.com/tags/1980s">the 1980s</a> and 1990s, with a strong focus on the East Village and Lower East Side. Click through for a look at his photos from those neighborhoods, all shot between 1981 and 1984&#x2014;a time he calls &quot;the good old, bad old days.&quot;</p>
