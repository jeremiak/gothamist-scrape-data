</div></div>					<p>Have you seen more blimps than usual in the sky today? HP Hood, the dairy brand and frequent blimp user, has brought in four blimps for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141005005851/http://nycaviation.com/forum/threads/44152-Blimp-Parade-Over-New-York-Thursday-9-6">a blimp parade</a> above the Hudson River&#x2014;the others include a MetLife Snoopy 1 blimp, a DIRECTV Starship blimp, and a Horizon Blue Cross Blue Shield of New Jersey blimp. This is actually a pretty rare occurrence, as Hood&apos;s press release states, &#x201C;This is a &#x2018;blue moon&#x2019; event, as multiple blimps rarely fly together in the same city at the same time.&#x201D;</p>

<p>If you see them in the sky, use the hashtag #LookUp with your Tweets. And we recommend cranking up Phish&apos;s &quot;Harry Hood&quot; while doing so:</p>
