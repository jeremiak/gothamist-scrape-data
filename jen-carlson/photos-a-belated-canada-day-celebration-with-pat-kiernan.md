</div></div>					<p>Last night we <em>finally</em> got to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312011241/http://gothamist.com/2011/06/30/drink_beer_with_pat_kiernan_who_wil.php">celebrate Canada Day</a>&#x2014;a little belatedly, but very properly. We headed to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312011241/http://windfallnyc.com/">Windfall Bar</a> with everyone&apos;s favorite Canadian Pat Kiernan (of NY1 and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312011241/http://www.patspapers.com/">Pat&apos;s Papers</a>), and our contest winners, for our <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312011241/http://gothamist.com/2010/07/16/poutine.php#photo-1">annual outing</a>. Poutine was had, beer was drunk, and we got the real dirt on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312011241/http://gothamist.com/2011/07/14/talking_with_jamie_shupak_traffic_r.php">Jamie Shupak</a> (just kidding Jamie Shupak). Thanks Pat, thanks readers and Pat fans, and thanks Windfall! </p>

<p>Next up: we&apos;re going to try to wrangle <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160312011241/https://twitter.com/#!/Rogerclark41">Roger Clark</a> in for a beer and jukebox night. Stay tuned!</p>
