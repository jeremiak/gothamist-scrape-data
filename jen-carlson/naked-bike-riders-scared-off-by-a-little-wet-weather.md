</div></div>					<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160619043102/http://gothamist.com/tags/worldnakedbikeride">World Naked Bike Ride</a> that was all set to take place <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160619043102/http://gothamist.com/2011/06/09/heads_up_naked_bike_ride_coming_thi.php">yesterday</a>, was postponed due to the rain. The ride is being rescheduled for sometime in July, but until that special day, here are some shots of the riders who showed up <em>briefly</em> yesterday. While you look at them you can ponder why the World Wet T-Shirt Bike Ride organizers didn&apos;t seize the opportunity.</p>
