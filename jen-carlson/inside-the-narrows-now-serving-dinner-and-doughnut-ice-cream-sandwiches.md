</div></div>					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151230224549/http://narrowsbar.com/">The Narrows</a> has been around for a couple of years, but if you haven&apos;t checked out the Bushwick spot, <em>now is the time</em>. (Seriously, do it before you won&apos;t be able to get a table.)</p>

<p>While the bar has always served up small bites, they recently launched a full dinner service <em>and</em> added a new dining room. And we hear this spot is a favorite amongst the crews behind <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151230224549/http://gothamist.com/tags/doordine">Do or Dine</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151230224549/http://gothamist.com/tags/robertas">Roberta&apos;s</a>, so you know the food is pre-approved.</p>

<p>Click through for a look at the cozy space&#x2014;which includes an outdoor area for the warmer seasons&#x2014;as well as some of the dishes and cocktails they&apos;re now serving up. Their current menu, created by Nick Subic and Keith Cochran, includes lamb pot pie ($16), steak delmonico ($24), welsh rarebit ($7), and pork loin &amp; rosemary tea sandwiches ($8).</p>

<p>Dinner is served &quot;late&quot; (around11 p.m.) every night, with a bar menu available until 1 a.m. on weeknights and 3 a.m. on weekend nights). If you&apos;re more of an earlybird, get there any day before 7 p.m. for their $1 oyster special.</p>
