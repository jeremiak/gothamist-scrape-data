</div></div>					<p>Zosia Mamet&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150525025045/http://gothamist.com/2013/03/15/zosia_mamet.php"><em>Girls</em> star</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150525025045/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/28/the_best_one_tip_we_have_for_zosia.php">failed Kickstarter musician</a>, and daughter of playwright David&#x2014;is moving to Bushwick. <em>Obviously</em>.</p>

<p>The 25-year-old and her boyfriend (actor Evan Jonigkeit) purchased a building (which <em>was</em> a multi-family dwelling containing four apartments) on Flushing Avenue for a little over $1 million late last year. They will now renovate the entire 2,500-square-foot place for their own. According to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150525025045/http://www.corcoran.com/nyc/Listings/Display/2731546">the Corcoran listing</a>, the property boasts a backyard and &quot;Manhattan views&quot;... which <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150525025045/https://maps.google.com/maps?q=896+flushing+ave&amp;hnear=896+Flushing+Ave,+Brooklyn,+New+York+11206&amp;gl=us&amp;t=h&amp;z=16">maybe if you stand on your tippy toes on the roof</a>? </p>
