title: Watch The Met Gala LIVE In Your Pajamas
---

	
										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="met2012gala.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505201836im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/arts_jen/met2012gala.jpg" width="640" height="426" class="image-none"> </span><br>
<span class="photo_caption">Trying too hard. (Getty Images)</span></p>

<p>While Lena Dunham, Psy, Gwyneth Paltrow, Kim and Kanye, and other people who are rich and famous will be at the annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505201836/http://gothamist.com/tags/metgala">Met Gala</a> tonight, you will just be at home eating cold leftovers and staring at your laptop in hopes of making some connection with the outside world from your lonely bed. But maybe tonight can be different? As the famous people enter the gala <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505201836/http://www.metmuseum.org/metgala">there will be a livestream</a> so you can watch them, just laughing it up in their jewels and couture with not a care in the world! This year&apos;s theme is <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505201836/http://www.metmuseum.org/metgala">PUNK: Chaos and Couture</a>, so to help you get into the mindset and to really feel like you&apos;re a part of it, take some heroin (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505201836/http://gothamist.com/2007/06/01/sid.php">not too much</a>!) and pair <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150505201836/http://gothamist.com/2013/05/06/photos_punk_fashion_at_the_metropol.php#photo-43">your &quot;vintage&quot; Urban Outfitters tee</a> with an upscale, overpriced version of something <em>punk</em>.</p>
