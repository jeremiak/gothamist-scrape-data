</div></div><p>Recently we found ourselves wondering: who buys CMJ badges? The music marathon, taking place this week, has never really compared to its Austin counterpart SXSW&#x2014;after all, it&apos;s pretty much just like any other week in the city, but with a few more bands. So yesterday we staked out the badge pick-up spot, Judson Memorial Church, to talk to some people getting their laminates. Many were picking up comped badges, but there <em>were</em> a few shelling out up to $200 for a badge. Click through to meet them all...</p>
