										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="phpq4DzSZAM.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115074108im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/arts_jen/phpq4DzSZAM.jpg" width="640" height="637" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Since <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115074108/http://gothamist.com/2015/08/10/ryan_adams_t_swift.php">early August</a> Ryan Adams has been teasing us with a Taylor Swift <em>1989</em> cover album. He was dropping clips all over social media as he worked on recording and mastering the masterpiece out in Los Angeles. Even T. Swift was excited...</p>

<center><blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en"><p lang="en" dir="ltr"><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115074108/https://twitter.com/hasief">@hasief</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115074108/https://twitter.com/TheRyanAdams">@TheRyanAdams</a> is this true??????? I WILL PASS OUT.</p>&#x2014; Taylor Swift (@taylorswift13) <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115074108/https://twitter.com/taylorswift13/status/629212668218314752">August 6, 2015</a></blockquote>
<script async src="//web.archive.org/web/20160115074108js_/http://platform.twitter.com/widgets.js" charset="utf-8"></script></center> 

<p>Then: silence. Then he was <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115074108/https://twitter.com/TheRyanAdams/status/642801304029528065">back in NYC</a>. Then we worried he would never release it and we&apos;d just have to stitch <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160115074108/http://gothamist.com/2015/08/14/here_is_every_clip_we_have_of_ryan.php">all the clips together</a> so we could listen on endless repeat to some frankenversion of the album.</p>

<p>This morning, however, Adams announced the album will be released digitally on Monday, September 21st. On Monday the world will become a little more beautiful.</p>
