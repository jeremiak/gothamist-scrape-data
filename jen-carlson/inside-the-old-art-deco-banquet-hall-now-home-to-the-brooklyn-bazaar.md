					<p>The old Greenpoint banquet hall, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015222937/http://gothamist.com/2016/03/04/brooklyn_night_bazaar_might_take_ov.php">Polonaise Terrace</a>, has recently undergone a huge renovation as the Brooklyn Bazaar has <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015222937/http://gothamist.com/2016/04/28/brooklyn_night_bazaar_huge.php#photo-1">taken over the old spot</a>. </p>

<p>The sprawling, 15,000-sq-ft Greenpoint space&#x2014;located on Greenpoint Avenue near the corner of Manhattan Avenue&#x2014;has been vacant since 2013, and when the Bazaar was ousted from their former Williamsburg spot <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015222937/http://gothamist.com/2015/05/11/brooklyn_night_bazaar_bmw.php">by BMW</a>, they moved on in. With plenty of mirrors, art deco fixtures and fonts, and wall-to-wall patterned carpeting, the space remains of another era.</p>

<p>Co-founder Belvy Klein told us this week that he and partner Aaron Broudo &quot;had been worried that these old school local guys were pissed off about us, but they were really, really into it.&quot; In fact, some former employees of the hall dropped by this week&#x2014;&quot;Two old Polish guys were talking out on the street and were very animated... they kind of made gestures&quot; and were talking about food. Turns out the two worked the kitchen thirty years ago&#x2014;&quot;They had cooked here in the &apos;70s when it was a banquet hall, and I took them back to see what we&apos;d done with the restaurant. They told me stories... it was the opposite of what I&apos;d feared. They were totally stoked that someone came in and restored so much of it. That&apos;s always been our goal... to restore as much of the art deco aesthetic as possible and not make it just another cookie-cutter West side club.&quot;</p>

<p>Klein and Broudo worked 18 hour days, for six weeks, to make the space sparkle, and it&apos;s now officially open. This weekend you can check out their new, multi-level home during their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015222937/http://bkbazaar.com/event/gothamist-presents-clap-your-hands-say-yeah/">kick-off</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015222937/http://bkbazaar.com/event/gothamist-presents-pissed-jeans/">concert weekend</a> (hosted by Gothamist!). Inside you&apos;ll also find an after-dark flea market, a restaurant run by Brooklyn Star&apos;s Joaquin Baca, an arcade, mini golf, and table tennis.</p>

<p><em><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015222937/http://bkbazaar.com/">Brooklyn Bazaar</a> is located at 150 Greenpoint Ave, Brooklyn, NY, 11222</em></p>
