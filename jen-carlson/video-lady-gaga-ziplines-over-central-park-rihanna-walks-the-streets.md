</div></div>					<p>This morning <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506144958/http://gothamist.com/tags/ladygaga">Lady Gaga</a> kicked off <em>Good Morning America</em>&apos;s Summer Concert Series in Central Park... where she arrived on a zip line (as <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506144958/http://twitter.com/#!/joneski2002/statuses/74101712352325632">one fan Tweeted</a>: &quot;Gaga on a zip line!!!! Goosebumps!!!!&quot;) According <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150506144958/http://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/lady-gaga-makes-zipline-arrival-good-morning-america/story?id=13694695">to ABC</a> it was the biggest turnout they&apos;ve ever had for an event there, with fans lining up as early as 3 a.m. on Tuesday this week. They were treated to a performance that included &quot;Born This Way,&quot; &quot;Judas,&quot; &quot;Bad Romance,&quot; and &quot;Edge of Glory.&quot;</p>
