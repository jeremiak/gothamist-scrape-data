</div></div>					<p>Today around noon, the 20-member Alarm Will Sound band (who most recently performed <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305194657/http://gothamist.com/2013/11/15/an_interview_with_steve_reich_who_r.php">Steve Reich&apos;s <em>Radio Rewrite</em></a>) surprised Metropolitan Museum patrons with a musical flash mob in the Charles Engelhard Court. The troupe performed &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305194657/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i524g6JMkwI">Cliffs</a>&quot; by Aphex Twin&#x2014;a minimal, theatrical piece with a gorgeous slow build-up that will put you in a trance. </p>

<p>Here&apos;s some initial video of the performance from our man on the ground, Jon Reznick... sadly, The Met&apos;s video won&apos;t be release for <em>months</em>, so if you shot some video of this today, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160305194657/mailto:tips@gothamist.com">send it along</a>!</p>
