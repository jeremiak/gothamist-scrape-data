We&apos;re not complaining, and they <em>have been</em> looking for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508152017/http://gothamist.com/2010/12/14/fdny_in_hot_water_over_proposed_fee.php">revenue-generating ideas</a>, after all. But while some may be heated up over the FDNY&apos;s proposed &quot;accident tax&quot; plan&#x2014;we&apos;re more concerned about <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508152017/http://twitter.com/#!/FDNYnews">their Twitter</a> operator getting a better cameraphone. Enjoy this (blurry) behind-the-scenes sneak peek of the calendar&#x2014;these photos were posted earlier today on the aforementioned Twitter, which declared they are already &quot;finishing up our 2012 calendar shoot! It will be at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508152017/http://www.fdnyfirezone.org/">www.fdnyfirezone.org</a> soon!&quot;</p>
