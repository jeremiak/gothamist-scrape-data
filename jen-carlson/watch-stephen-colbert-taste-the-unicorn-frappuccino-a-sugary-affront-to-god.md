title: Watch Stephen Colbert Taste The Unicorn Frappuccino, 'A Sugary Affront To God'
---

	
										<p>Last night, despite all of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015111042/http://gothamist.com/2017/04/19/unicorn_frappuccino_review.php">our warnings</a>, Stephen Colbert tasted Starbucks new <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015111042/http://gothamist.com/tags/unicornFrappuccino">Unicorn Frappuccino</a> live on air. (#Brave!) &quot;Oh, I wish I was dead,&quot; Colbert immediately declared after the frothy melted crayon drink hit his lips. &quot;Tastes like I french kissed Tinkerbell.&quot;</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="360" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20171015111042if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/PBBDlgwm8dg" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>&quot;I&apos;m not sure how many unicorns they&apos;re grinding up for this, but I&apos;m guessing PETA is furious,&quot; Colbert declared, before schooling his viewers on the real ingredients: &quot;Mango, pink, blue, and obviously topping. The FDA recommends at least three servings of topping a day.&quot; Of course, real unicorn purists know that the mythical creature would prefer a scotch, neat, in solitude&#x2014;even mortals know this is the real magical elixir.</p>
