</div></div>					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151004154234/http://gothamist.com/2008/12/11/todays_headlines_from_sesame_street.php">As mentioned</a>, yesterday we found ourselves on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151004154234/http://www.sesamestreet.org/home">Sesame Street</a>, where even the sunny days couldn&apos;t sweep the rain clouds away&#x2014;but Elmo &amp; Co. sure made it all seem a little bit brighter. Speaking of the lil red guy, he had a message for you all: </p>

<p><object width="640" height="483"><param name="allowfullscreen" value="true"><param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always"><param name="movie" value="http://vimeo.com/moogaloop.swf?clip_id=2506225&amp;server=vimeo.com&amp;show_title=1&amp;show_byline=1&amp;show_portrait=0&amp;color=bac0c2&amp;fullscreen=1"><embed src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151004154234oe_/http://vimeo.com/moogaloop.swf?clip_id=2506225&amp;server=vimeo.com&amp;show_title=1&amp;show_byline=1&amp;show_portrait=0&amp;color=bac0c2&amp;fullscreen=1" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" width="640" height="483"><p></p>

<p>The series is now filming their 40th season, which will begin airing next November. From Big Bird&apos;s doors, to the stoop, to Oscar&apos;s garbage can...the set has pretty much remained the same for all those decades, while PBS and the show remain <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151004154234/http://archive.salon.com/tech/letters/2001/05/10/sesame_street/">ad-free</a> (check out some of the brands on the shelf at Hooper&apos;s). And don&apos;t forget: you can <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151004154234/http://gothamist.com/2008/10/23/make_a_muppet.php">make your <em>own</em> muppet</a> now!</p>

<p>We&apos;ll be heading back to Sesame soon to ask the muppets some hard-hitting questions, if you have any questions you need answers to&#x2014;leave them in the comments!</p>
