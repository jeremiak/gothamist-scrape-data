</div></div>					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601233314/http://dianeworland.com/">Diane Worland</a> has a treasure trove of old photos of New York City, mostly taken in the 1970s. Click through for a look at her street shots from <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601233314/http://gothamist.com/tags/1977">1977</a>, when everyone was drinking TAB or wearing a Nixon mask. </p>

<p>This was the year the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601233314/http://gothamist.com/2011/05/26/original_sketch_for_i_3_ny.php">I &#x2665;  NY</a> campaign was used to promote tourism to the city, the year of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601233314/http://gothamist.com/2009/07/13/flashback_blackout.php#photo-1">the blackout</a>, the year <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601233314/http://gothamist.com/2009/07/09/ny_airways.php">of that New York Airways</a> disaster, the Bronx burned, George Willig <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150601233314/http://gothamist.com/2012/05/26/35_years_ago_today_george_willig_th.php">climbed up</a> one of the WTC towers, and the Son of Sam was terrorizing the city. </p>
