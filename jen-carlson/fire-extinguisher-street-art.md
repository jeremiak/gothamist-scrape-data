title: Fire Extinguisher Street Art
---

	
										<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160306071344/http://razorapple.com/2008/04/30/moral-paints-nyc-with-fire-extinguisher">Razor Apple</a> has some photos of street artist MORAL&apos;s work. He&apos;s been bombing the city with fire extinguisher tags, one of a few taggers who uses that medium -- where the fire extinguisher is loaded up with paint. Just one of the many contributing to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160306071344/http://gothamist.com/2008/04/22/graffiti_on_the.php">the rise of graffiti</a> in town!</p>

<p>Here&apos;s an instruction manual on <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160306071344/http://www.instructables.com/id/Fire-Extinguisher-super-soaker!-can-be-modded-to-/?comments=all">how to make your own</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160306071344/http://www.instructables.com/id/How-to-Paint-with-a-Fire-Extinguisher/">use one</a>; and check it out in action <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160306071344/http://graffitiresearchlab.com/?page_id=80">here</a>.</p>
