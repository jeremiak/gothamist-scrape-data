</div></div>					<p>This weekend <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150709005716/http://gothamist.com/2011/06/29/enter_jim_hensons_fantastic_world.php">Jim Henson&apos;s Fantastic World</a> finally opens at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150709005716/http://www.movingimage.us/">Museum of Moving Image</a>. His sketches, his photographs, and his muppets will all be on display through January 16th, 2012. We dropped by yesterday for a preview, click through for a sneak peek at the truly fantastic world Henson created. And if you drop by this weekend, you can even catch a screening of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150709005716/http://www.movingimage.us/visit/calendar/2011/07/16/detail/the-muppet-movie"><em>The Muppet Movie</em></a>!</p>
