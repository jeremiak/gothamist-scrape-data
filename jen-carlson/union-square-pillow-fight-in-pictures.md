title: Union Square Pillow Fight...in Pictures!
---

	
										<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405203013/http://gothamist.com/2008/03/20/its_pillow_figh.php">pillow fight</a> just started 30 minutes ago, and the pictures are already coming in. Part of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405203013/http://www.pillowfightday.com/">World Pillow Fight Day</a>, this is the third consecutive year the feathers have flown around Union Square. </p>

<p>Were you there? Don&apos;t forget to tag your pillow fight pics with &quot;Gothamist&quot; at Flickr. </p>

<p><strong>UPDATE:</strong> Some locals aren&apos;t too happy with the feathers blanketing Union Square -- <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150405203013/http://www.thegreenguide.com/reports/product.mhtml?id=80&amp;sec=2">National Geographic</a> has a Green Guide to pillows, which may explain why. For those craving more pillow fight action, you can check out some great videos after the jump, and re-live the day...in slow motion! </p>
