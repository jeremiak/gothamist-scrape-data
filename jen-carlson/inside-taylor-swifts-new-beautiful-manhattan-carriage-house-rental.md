					<p>Earlier this week we were all enlightened on Taylor Swift&apos;s NYC living situation: while her $19 million Tribeca penthouse gets renovated, she&apos;s rented out this $40,000/month historic carriage house in the West Village*. This news came just days after I watched a giant cockroach squeeze its body through the drain of my bathroom sink and scutter off to take up valuable space somewhere in my 212-square-foot studio. Maybe as I slept he crawled into my ear and lives in there now? Just a little something I think about at night. </p>

<p>While Taylor Swift is not immune to cockroaches, should she spot one she could just burn this place down and build a new one. It&apos;s a tough one to replace, though...</p>

<p>Listen, we don&apos;t really care how or where Taylor Swift lives, but who can resist a look inside a circa-1912 Manhattan carriage house? While <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113150115/http://gothamist.com/2016/06/13/taylor_swift_real_estate_beat.php#photo-1">the original news</a> came with scant photos, more have <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161113150115/http://www.teenvogue.com/gallery/taylor-swift-rents-new-york-city-west-village-home#17">just surfaced</a>. That pool shot really burns.</p>
