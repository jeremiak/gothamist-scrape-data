</div></div>					<p>Earlier this year <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160116023957/http://www.brooklyn-usa.org/press/2011/may12_FR.htm">Macy&apos;s announced</a> they were giving their ugly garage in Downtown Brooklyn a makeover. They brought on graffiti artist ESPO (aka Steve Powers of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160116023957/http://gothamist.com/2008/08/06/waterboard_thrill_ride_splashes_dow.php">the Waterboard Thrill Ride</a>) to create a love letter to the borough, which he&apos;s just about done with. Yesterday Gothamist&apos;s Jake Dobkin photographed the piece, still in progress, and sprawling around Hoyt, Livingston, and Fulton Streets. This isn&apos;t the first <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160116023957/http://www.aloveletterforyou.com/">love letter</a> he&apos;s written though, so don&apos;t feel <em>too</em> special.</p>
