<p>Earlier today Surprise Industries&#x2014;which aims to bring little surprise experiences to New Yorkers&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608075230/https://www.facebook.com/surpriseindustries/posts/10151368965186750">asked Facebook</a>: &quot;Can anyone tell us where there&apos;s a vending machine in NYC?&quot; They are looking to bring this renegade vending machine project, spearheaded by Amy Krouse Rosenthal, to life. It&apos;s called <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608075230/http://www.mendingmachine.com/">The Mending Machine</a>, and we will now keep quarters in our pockets at all times until we find a machine with heart-mending duct tape in it. See other options, like the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130608075230/http://www.mendingmachine.com/needleandthread.html">hole in your life needle and thread</a>, in the below video:</p>
