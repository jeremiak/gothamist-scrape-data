<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120602130041/http://www.myspace.com/harperblynn">Harper Blynn</a> (formerly Pete and J) have been picking up new fans everywhere they go&#x2014;including <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120602130041/http://www.facebook.com/pages/Harper-Blynn/196074178551#!/photo.php?pid=4907374&amp;id=196074178551&amp;comments&amp;ref=mf">Sissy Spacek</a> <em>and</em> Victoria from <em>Twilight</em>, aka Rachelle Lefevre, who <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120602130041/http://twitter.com/RachelleLefevre/status/11018570335">recently Twittered</a> from one of their shows: &quot;Saw an amazing band tonight called Harper Blynn. They have an album dropping in May &amp; are based in NYC. U need to check them out!&quot; Well now&apos;s your chance, they not only stopped by our office this week to play a couple of tunes, but they&apos;ve got an April residency going on at the Living Room. See them tonight (or next Wednesday), where on top of their own set they&apos;ll also play the role of backing band for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120602130041/http://www.myspace.com/schuylerfisk">Schuyler Fisk</a>.</p>

<p>Below are two songs from their forthcoming David Kahne (The Strokes, Regina Spektor, Paul McCartney) produced LP, <em>Loneliest Generation</em>. </p>
