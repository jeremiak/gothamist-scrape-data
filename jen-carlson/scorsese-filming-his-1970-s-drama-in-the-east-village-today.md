<p>Martin Scorsese has been spotted around New York filming his <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160605051003/http://www.imdb.com/title/tt3186130/?ref_=nm_flmg_prd_9">1970s Rock n&apos; Roll Project</a>, which <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160605051003/http://gothamist.com/2014/12/02/hbo_orders_martin_scorsese_and_mick.php">will air on HBO</a> and counts Mick Jagger as a producer. Today, they&apos;re filming around 6th Avenue and 10th Street, with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160605051003/https://twitter.com/T1ger/status/623970315031420928">signs spotted</a> all the way to 15th. </p>

<p>The series stars Bobby Cannavale, Ray Romano, Andrew Dice Clay, and <em>a lot</em> more, including James Jagger (the son of Jerry Hall and Mick), and Olivia Wilde: </p>

<p>According to Bedford + Bowery, filming will likely continue through the 7 o&apos;clock hour tonight, at least in the area of &quot;East 2nd and 5th Streets, between Avenues A and B.&quot; They also note today&apos;s shoots are taking place close to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160605051003/http://hbowatch.com/scorseses-rock-n-roll-project-gives-us-red-hot-olivia-wilde/">the Led Zeppelin <em>Physical Graffiti</em> building</a>, where the Rolling Stones filmed their &quot;Waiting On A Friend&quot; video.</p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160605051003/http://gothamist.com/2014/12/02/hbo_orders_martin_scorsese_and_mick.php">The hourlong drama</a> will focus on Cannavale&apos;s Richie Finestra character, the founder and president of a top-tier record label, American Century Record: &quot;Richie undergoes a crisis of character when confronted with a life-altering decision. Olivia Wilde will play his wife, Devon, a former actress/model who lived a Bohemian life in 1960s New York. Ray Romano will appear as Zak Yankovich, one of Richie&#x2019;s partners. Juno Temple will also star as Jamie Vine, is an ambitious assistant in the A&amp;R; Department of American Century.&quot;</p>
