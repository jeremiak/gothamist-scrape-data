                            <p>Last night, Cynthia Rowley hosted her Fashion Week show inside of the beautiful 5 Beekman building, which was abandoned for decades before developers started eyeing it. While it&apos;s still in a state of gorgeous decay, its become the backdrop for photoshoots, parties, and even our own <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131225012638/http://gothamist.com/2010/11/10/live_from_gothamist_house_small_bla.php#photo-1">Gothamist House</a> video. </p>

<p>Click through for a look at the show (which took place throughout the building) through the lens of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131225012638/http://samhorine.com/">Sam Horine</a>, who tells us, &quot;there was a lineup presentation on the first floor that was open to the people invited,&quot; while on the 9th floor only a select group of invited photographers were allowed to take part. Check it out&#x2014;there&apos;s fashion, there&apos;s confetti, there&apos;s an atrium!</p>
