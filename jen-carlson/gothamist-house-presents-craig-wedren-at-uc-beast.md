</div></div><p>A couple of weeks ago we headed over to the fairly new UCB Theater in the East Village (known for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111020040404/http://gothamist.com/2011/04/22/amy_poehler_promises_247_stripping.php">its &quot;Hot Chicks Room&quot; scandal</a>&#x2014;click through for some photos!) to film a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111020040404/http://gothamist.com/tags/LiveatGothamistHouse">Gothamist House</a> video with Craig Wedren, formerly of Shudder To Think. (After filming we headed down to Zuccotti Park to visit with the Occupy Wall Street protesters&#x2014;which you can see more of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111020040404/http://gothamist.com/2011/10/12/visiting_occupy_wall_street_with_cr.php">right here</a>.)</p>

<p>These songs are from Wedren&apos;s new album, <em>Wand</em>, which is getting <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111020040404/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NNwIOL0g1AM&amp;feature=related">a lot of love</a> from the likes <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111020040404/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O4wX3D5KSBk">of Paul Rudd</a>, and other comedians he&apos;s worked with (Wedren has scored many movies, including <em>Wet Hot American Summer</em>). Enjoy!</p>
