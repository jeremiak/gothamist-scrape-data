                            <p>While the Barclays Center has been hosting New York&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128053946/http://gothamist.com/2012/10/07/videos_jay-z_brings_out_beyonce_for.php">Shiniest</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128053946/http://gothamist.com/2012/12/09/photos_videos_rolling_stones_rock_b.php#photo-1">Shitshows</a> for nearly a year, last night&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128053946/http://gothamist.com/tags/vmas">MTV VMAs</a> produced the biggest spectacle we&apos;ve witnessed at the arena to date. And possibly the first big awards show (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128053946/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/21/smallest_penis_contest_brooklyn.php">besides this one</a>) the borough has ever been host to?</p>

<p>Photographer Katie Sokoler threw herself into the midst of the chaotic soup, which <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128053946/http://brooklyn.news12.com/news/brooklyn-returns-to-normal-after-mtv-video-music-awards-at-barclays-center-1.5955890">shut down streets</a> in the surrounding area, allowing for starstruck gawpers to get up close to the red carpet area and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20131128053946/http://gothamist.com/2013/08/26/miley_cyrus_vmas.php">Scream Without Feeling</a>.</p>
