</div></div><p>Last night the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111018081151/http://www.nycbmc.com/">2009 NYC Beard &amp; Moustache Championships</a> went down at Public Assembly in Williamsburg. The hairy set, and those who love them, were out in full force sporting everything from magnificent muttonchops to classic &apos;70s porn &apos;staches. While competitions like this have been going on for years, certain facial hair looks are even <em>more</em> in fashion now with the popularity of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111018081151/http://gothamist.com/2009/03/10/dressing_for_the_recession.php">recession chic</a> on the rise. The winner of one prize even began growing his beard after being laid off five months ago as a financial broker, according to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20111018081151/http://www.nydailynews.com/ny_local/brooklyn/2009/03/14/2009-03-14_harried_contestants_brush_with_fame_in_n.html">the Daily News</a>. Wonder how many of the 80 contestants shaved this morning?</p>
