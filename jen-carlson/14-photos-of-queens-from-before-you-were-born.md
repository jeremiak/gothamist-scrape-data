</div></div>					<p>We&apos;ve looked at some very old photos of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429093053/http://gothamist.com/2012/03/05/14_photos_of_manhattan_before_you_w.php">Manhattan</a>, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429093053/http://gothamist.com/2012/03/02/14_photos_of_brooklyn_before_you_we.php#photo-1">Brooklyn</a>, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429093053/http://gothamist.com/2012/03/07/14_photos_of_the_bronx_before_you_w.php#photo-1">the Bronx</a>, and now it&apos;s time to revisit a Queens you most likely never knew. From the War Gardens in 1918 to a newspaper in 1903 declaring Jamaica Bay our own Venice to the Planters Peanut Warehouse (which fine, maybe you&apos;ve saw in your lifetime).</p>
