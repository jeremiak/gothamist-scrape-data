										<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160521072412/http://vimeo.com/2860274">This is adorable</a>. Urban Anthropologists Andy and Carolyn London have given voices to some of New York City&apos;s more overlooked citizens: sewer grates, newspaper stands and pay phones, to name a few. In the below video they all tell their stories (in the credits some of the voices are attributed to Italian tourists, two drunks in Penn Station and an awesome Harlem lady).</p>
