</div></div>					<p>Today brings the third installation of our <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429095959/http://gothamist.com/tags/bodegacats">Bodega Cat series</a>... or shall we say <em>Round Three</em> of our Bodega Cat Series. That&apos;s right, we&apos;re not just here to look at cute cats, <em>that is not what the internet is for</em>&#x2014;we&apos;re here to <em>judge them</em>. Just as we <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429095959/http://gothamist.com/2012/02/06/bad_kitty_voting_fraud_rocks_gotham.php">crowned Moo</a> the winner of our <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429095959/http://gothamist.com/2012/01/31/gothamist_cat_battle_which_office_c.php">staff cat contest</a> earlier this year, by the end of the 2012 installment of this series, we&apos;ll also crown the city&apos;s top bodega cat.</p>

<p>In the coming weeks we&apos;ll take a look at all of our catestants, and judging by cuteness level, personality, and ability to sleep on vegetables, we&apos;ll narrow it down to a reasonable number of finalists. A voting system will then be installed where you can help choose New York City&apos;s Next Top Bodega Cat! He or she will be rewarded with pats on the head, belly rubs, a 10th life, and a free pass from the Department of Health.<br>
</p>
