<p>Hey Lennon fans, mark your GCals or your Filofaxes or whatever: <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110629163418/http://lennontribute.org/">the 30th Annual John Lennon Tribute Concert</a> is coming up on November 12th, and tickets go on sale next Monday. The date falls between his October birthday and his <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110629163418/http://gothamist.com/2008/12/06/lennon_1.php">December death anniversary</a>, and the concert is &quot;to honor the memory of John Lennon and celebrate his music, his passion for peace and his love for New York City.&quot; The show will take place at Beacon Theater, and will feature performances from the likes of Jackson Browne, Patti Smith, Cyndi Lauper, Aimee Mann, Keb&apos; Mo&apos;, Joan Osborne, Martin Sexton, Shelby Lynne, Taj Mahal, The Kennedys, and even the Fab Faux. What, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20110629163418/http://gothamist.com/2010/02/17/live_from_bam_yoko_ono_friends.php">no Yoko</a>?</p>
