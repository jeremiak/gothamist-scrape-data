</div></div>					<p>Tomorrow at 10 a.m. Grand Central Terminal will become more crowded than usual, with the grand opening of the new Apple Store. We got <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150503144348/http://gothamist.com/2011/12/07/photos_were_inside_the_new_grand_ce.php#photo-1">a sneak peek</a> yesterday, and you can click through for more of our photos, showing off the 23,000-square-foot space. </p>

<p>Our photographer Katie Sokoler tells us, &quot;One of the employees showed me a new way to purchase stuff from the store. If you have the Apple Store App on your phone there&apos;s an easy pay option, all you have to do is scan the bar code on the product and it charges it to your iTunes account. So now you don&apos;t have to talk to anyone and just check yourself out!&quot; </p>

<p>An added treat: the packaging for the grand opening t-shirts gives a nod to the Grand Central Concourse ceiling&#x2014;Sokoler says, &quot;When you undo the box it opens up into the constellation design on the ceiling.&quot; Nice touch, Apple... though we wish this packaging came with every purchase there!</p>
