	<p>New York <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605044225/http://gothamist.com/tags/fashionweek">Fashion Week</a> has ended, so what can we&#x2014;the simple people living off-the-fashion-grid&#x2014;learn from what just went down on the runways? Since Vogue&apos;s Anna Wintour is like some totalitarian dictator reigning over high end fashion designers worldwide, let&apos;s look to her for couture cues. Click through for a look at what she wore to the shows over this past week&#x2014;while it may not surprise you that blacks and taupes are still in, you may be taken aback by Wintour&apos;s bold choice to accessorize with a human donning <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130605044225/http://gothamist.com/2012/07/31/listen_dark_knights_bane_re-recorde.php">a Bane mask</a>. Brava, Wintour, Brava.</p>
