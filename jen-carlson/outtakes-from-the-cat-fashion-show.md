</div></div>					<p>Wednesday night the Algonquin Hotel cat, Matilda, celebrated her 15th Birthday in the hotel&apos;s lobby, where a cat fashion show hosted by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150715225243/http://www.facebook.com/mustlovecatsfanpage"><em>Must Love Cats</em></a> host John Fulton was held in her honor. Our photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150715225243/http://colormekatie.blogspot.com/">Katie Sokoler</a> told us she took an obscene amount of photos at the event, so here are some more (on top of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150715225243/http://gothamist.com/2010/08/12/cat_fashion_show.php">yesterday&apos;s selection</a>). By now Matilda&apos;s likely back to her regular schedule of roaming around the lobby:</p>
