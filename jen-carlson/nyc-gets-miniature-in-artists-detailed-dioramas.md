</div></div><p>New York native Alan Wolfson has spent years creating these insanely detailed dioramas of the city. From Peep World to a 1982 Times Square hotel room to the Canal Street subway station, he&apos;s built a miniature version of NYC&apos;s urban landscape. He notes that some scenes (specifically the Canal Street one) are not <em>exact</em> representations, &quot;but rather a combination of existing and fabricated environments.&quot; (The entire thing took him 18 months to make!) You can check out more of his work <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130305014506/http://www.alanwolfson.net/index.htm">right here</a>.</p>
