<p>The subway system is vital to New York City. It may break sometimes, or bring you face to face with some ugly parts of humanity; it may make you grumble, sigh or shed tears, but its dilapidated platforms feel like home. And its daily tests are only making you <em>stronger</em>. Below filmmaker Aidan Tanner has perfectly captured their appearance, and the feeling you get when your tired bones are barely holding you up at the end of the day, and you gaze into the windows of a passing train...</p>
