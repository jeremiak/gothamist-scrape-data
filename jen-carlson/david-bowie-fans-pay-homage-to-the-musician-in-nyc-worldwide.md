					<p>David Bowie&apos;s death <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630062838/http://laist.com/2016/01/10/rip_starman.php">was announced</a> in the early morning hours of January 11th, and since then a memorial has been steadily growing outside of his home on Lafayette Street, and worldwide, including in his hometown of Brixton, England, and over <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630062838/http://laist.com/2016/01/11/david_bowie_tribute_los_angeles.php">in Los Angeles</a>.</p>

<p>In New York City, fans have been leaving flowers and candles outside of the residence, which he shared with wife Iman. The couple was originally planning to settle in Los Angeles, but moved here <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630062838/https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Bowie#1992.E2.80.9398:_Electronic_period">in 1992</a> instead, and NYC has remained their primary home ever since.</p>

<p>Nikki Sinclair, a 47-year-old Bowie fan from England, came to her respects at the SoHo memorial. &quot;I woke up this morning and I saw my Facebook feed and I just couldn&apos;t believe it,&quot; Sinclair said. &quot;His music played a significant part in my adolescence.&quot;</p>

<p>Sinclair explained her particular fondness for Bowie&apos;s hit single <em>Changes</em>. &quot;I think that album [<em>Hunky Dory</em>] and the whole Ziggy thing as well gave&#x2014;I mean I grew up gay and transgender at a time of less tolerant attitudes. And so what Bowie did, he just said, &apos;Here I am. This is different. I&apos;m different. So what?&apos; And that was groundbreaking. Without him there&apos;s no George Michael, no Boy George. There&apos;s no Steve Strange. And without that we wouldn&apos;t have moved forward to make it okay for someone like me, today. So I just think we owe a huge gratitude to Bowie, who for all his life pushed the boundaries. I mean, he released an album just a few days ago; he even made his death an art.&quot;</p>

<p>Fans have also been gathering at the New York Theatre Workshop, where Bowie&apos;s musical <em>Lazarus</em> is playing&#x2014;according <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630062838/http://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/NYC-Fans-Pay-Tribute-To-Music-Legend-Star-Glam-Rocker-David-Bowie--364827711.html">to NBC</a>, &quot;Michael C. Hall, the show&apos;s star, said that he sought the role out because of Bowie&apos;s influence.&quot;</p>

<p>Bowie&apos;s death (he was being treated for cancer for the past 18 months) was confirmed by his publicist at Nasty Little Man, Steve Martin. Though few other details were provided, this statement was released via <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630062838/https://www.facebook.com/davidbowie/posts/10153176666977665">Facebook</a>: &quot;David Bowie died peacefully today surrounded by his family after a courageous 18 month battle with cancer. While many of you will share in this loss, we ask that you respect the family&#x2019;s privacy during their time of grief.&quot;</p>

<p>A celebration of Bowie, which was already scheduled before the 69-year-old legend passed, will be held at Carnegie Hall <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160630062838/http://artsbeat.blogs.nytimes.com/2016/01/10/david-bowie-to-be-honored-at-carnegie-hall/">on March 31st</a>. </p>
