					<p>Street artist and pun king <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161016145517/http://gothamist.com/tags/hanksy">Hanksy</a> has stepped a little bit outside of himself for a Memorial Day Weekend project that he pasted up around Manhattan last night. </p>

<p>The four pieces are a little darker than his typical work&#x2014;he explained to us this morning: &quot;At 19 my grandfather was liberating France during World War II. While at the same age my largest concern was figuring out how to sneak beer into my college dorm. I&apos;m not a fan of war, but I respect those who have served. Willingly or not. These posters are just a small reminder that in-between your long weekend beach selfies and third bottle of ros&#xE9;, you should acknowledge the real reason everyone has the day off.&quot;</p>

<p>The pieces are still up and you&apos;ll find them on the LES and in Chinatown on your way to brunch.</p>
