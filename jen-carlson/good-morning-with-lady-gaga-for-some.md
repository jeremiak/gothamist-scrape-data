</div></div>					<p>Bright n&apos; early this morning, almost <em>too</em> early for such a spectacle, Lady Gaga&apos;s monster masses converged on Rockefeller Plaza for a free show as part of the <em>Today</em> show&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021063438/http://today.msnbc.msn.com/id/38164085/ns/today-toyota_concert_series_on_today/">concert series</a>. If you missed it, or even if you were there and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021063438/http://gothamist.com/2010/07/09/lady_gaga_3.php">somehow survived</a> the madness (at least two fans were taken away by EMS), here&apos;s a little audio/visual recap! </p>

<p>By the way, the recording breaking crowd was estimated to be around 20,000 fans (though some were likely confused, terrified passerby). And elsewhere in the city, her fan&apos;s quirkiness brightened up some people&apos;s morning commutes&#x2014;one woman <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021063438/http://twitter.com/jessgutzzz/statuses/18012697664">Twittered</a>: &quot;The amount of gay guys dressed up in insane attire coming from the lady gaga concert def made my wait in penn station worth it...&quot;</p>
