</div></div>					<p>On Tuesday night <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141226082448/http://gothamist.com/2012/08/08/gothamist_presents_an_evening_with.php">we hosted</a> an event <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141226082448/http://nitehawkhatched.com/2012/08/31/video-gothamist-qa-with-whit-stillman-chris-eigeman-at-nitehawk/">at Nitehawk</a> to celebrate the classic Whit Stillman film <em>Metropolitan</em>, which was released about 22 years ago. Following the screening, Gothamist&apos;s Jen Chung moderated a Q&amp;A; with director Whit Stillman and actor Chris Eigeman. There were no tuxedos, but otherwise the event kept true to Stillman&apos;s style&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141226082448/http://gothamist.com/2012/04/06/wtf_happened_at_that_lena_dunham_wh.php">no action packed bloodbath</a>, just a lot of dialogue (and even more prosecco). If you weren&apos;t able to attend, here&apos;s video of the Q&amp;A.;</p>
