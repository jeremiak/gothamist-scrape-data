<p>The subway system of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428200631/http://gothamist.com/tags/1970s">1970s</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428200631/http://gothamist.com/tags/1980s">1980s</a> was a very different beast than what we&apos;ve got now, and while there are plenty of photographs documenting <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428200631/http://gothamist.com/2012/01/18/amazing_photos_of_1980s_new_york_ci.php#photo-11">the graffiti-adorned cars</a> of way back then, in order to <em>really</em> get its dirt and grime under your nails you&apos;ve got to immerse yourself in some moving footage. Below enjoy a highly entertaining 17-minutes of movie clips that show the Bad Old Days of the city&apos;s subway system. </p>

<p>You&apos;ve got your <em>Bananas</em> (see Woody Allen throw uncredited thug Sylvester Stallone off his subway car!), you&apos;ve got your <em>Death Wish</em>, you&apos;ve got your <em>Warriors</em>, and you&apos;ve got that one where there&apos;s a makeshift wooden coffin on the Shuttle...</p>
