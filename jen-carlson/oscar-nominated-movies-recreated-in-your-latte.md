</div></div>					<p>Latte art just seems <em>old</em> at this point, doesn&apos;t it? Like something that was big in the 1990s&#x2014;a fad, like the Rachel haircut. But it&apos;s also nice, like when the person making your latte hands it to you and there&apos;s a heart staring up at you. Awww. And <em>also</em>, it is impressive... we could certainly not do this.</p>

<p>Brooklyn-based Michael Breach is like the Renoir of the caffeine and milk-based art world, and recently he created masterpieces featuring this year&apos;s Best Picture nominees. We&apos;re told it takes only 10 to 15 minutes to create each (you can see Breach working on other pieces in the video below), though each one will disappear in just 3.</p>
