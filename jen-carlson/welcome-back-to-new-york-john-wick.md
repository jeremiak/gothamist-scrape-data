					<p><em>John Wick</em> is a perfect neo-noir action film, starring Keanu Reeves in a role we never knew we wanted him to play&#x2014;angry, grunting, vengeful, a real loose cannon on a mission to basically kill everyone (he has his reasons). If you know someone who proclaims to dislike action films, show them the first <em>John Wick</em> (which came out in 2014 to rave reviews) and you&apos;ll see a whole new side of them. That first film was such a success, that we&apos;re getting more...</p>

<p>This February, John Wick returns. And he&apos;s coming back to New York. A new trailer was just released, which shows Wick on a path of destruction through the city, declaring, &quot;Whoever comes, I&apos;ll kill them. I&apos;ll kill them all.&quot; He hits Lincoln Center, the Oculus and PATH Station, Bethesda Fountain, Times Square, and that rooftop in DUMBO, from where we <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161222162458/http://gothamist.com/2015/12/15/keanu_reaves_fishburne_wick.php#photo-1">exclusively reported</a> that this time around Wick will be meeting up with Laurence Fishburne, who appears to be playing a pigeon trainer. </p>
