					<p>Put on your best puffy shirt/urban sombrero/velvet track suit/8-ball jacket and sidle right up to Jerry&apos;s apartment. In celebration of the show <em>finally</em> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170402223545/http://gothamist.com/2015/04/29/seinfeld_will_stream_on_hulu_in_180.php">being in streaming syndication</a>, Hulu <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170402223545/http://gothamist.com/2015/06/12/seinfeld_the_apartment.php">has recreated</a> Seinfeld&apos;s apartment (modeled after season 8), down to every little detail. Well, it&apos;s actually more of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170402223545/https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Bizarro_Jerry">a bizarro</a> Jerry apartment, but still, it&apos;s got an open door policy. </p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170402223545if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/XXSGV5wEv1o" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>They&apos;re calling the apartment a &quot;full gallery fan experience,&quot; as it&apos;s really doubling as a Seinfeld Museum. Inside you&apos;ll find Puddy&apos;s Devil&apos;s jersey, the Festivus pole, the Frogger machine, the Maestro&apos;s wand, a box of Junior Mints, the Pez dispenser, and more precious artifacts from the series. But there are also interactive areas&#x2014;on top of barging into Jerry&apos;s apartment Kramer-style, you can pose for your own photo shoot like George did in season 8&apos;s &quot;The Package,&quot; do some stand-up, or flip through Seinfeld scripts.</p>

<p>While Jerry&apos;s apartment was on the Upper West Side, this one is located at 451 West 14th Street. You can drop by between now and Sunday, June 28th, between 10 a.m. to 7 p.m. BYO sponge.</p>
