</div></div>					<p>Photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160728130944/http://www.birchlane.net/NotThereAnymore.htm">Bruce Barone</a> has a treasure trove of old photographs he snapped in the late 1970s and early 1980s, while working at Hearst Magazines. He tells us he is now self-publishing a book featuring some hand-selected images, which should be ready by the end of February. For now, here&apos;s a look back through his lens at an older New York.</p>
