										<p>Madonna is currently in Washington Square Park performing a free concert in support of Hillary Clinton. Madonna asked upon arrival: &quot;Are we gonna settle for second best?&quot; (That seems generous.) Then declaring, &quot;No way motherf*ckers!&quot; The show started around 7:30 p.m., but she only <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161111072638/https://twitter.com/Madonna/status/795791218751995904">tweeted an announcement</a> around 8 p.m. (Update: Aaaand it ended around 8:01 p.m.)</p>

<p>A few attendees are streaming it on Facebook live (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161111072638/https://www.facebook.com/katielevittan/videos/10208218090674292/">here is a good one</a>), and here are some videos from the scene:</p>
