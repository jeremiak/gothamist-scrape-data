<p>The Kardashian sisters have stripped down to their undies for Sears. And that isn&apos;t even the most depressing sentence you&apos;ll read today! This is: Annie Leibovitz was the photographer for the campaign. Oh wait, this is: <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151205012731/http://gothamist.com/tags/annieleibovitz">Annie Leibovitz</a> is now shooting for the Sears catalog. Anyway, the sisters Kardashian have teamed up with the department store for their new fashion line: the Kardashian Kollection, and according to a press release, they&apos;ll be celebrating the launch on September 6th at Leibovitz&apos;s studio (you aren&apos;t invited).</p>

<p>You can check out what &quot;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151205012731/http://www.styleite.com/media/kardashian-klan-press-release/">the Kardashian klan</a>&quot; (that is what they are calling themselves!) has to offer right now at your local Sears. And guess what, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151205012731/http://www.sears.com/shc/s/dap_10153_12605_DAP_Kardashian+Collection+Category?category=womens&amp;adCell=W4">the Leopard Print Sequin Harem Pants</a> are on sale for just $68.99!</p>
