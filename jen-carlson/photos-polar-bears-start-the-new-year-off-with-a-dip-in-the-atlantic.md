</div></div>					<p>Yesterday the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160521201617/http://www.polarbearclub.org/">Coney Island Polar Bear Club</a>, the oldest winter bathing organization in the United States, got a few extra members for their annual <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160521201617/http://gothamist.com/2012/01/01/join_the_polar_bears_as_they_plunge.php">New Year&apos;s Day swim</a>. While the members swim in the Atlantic Ocean at Coney Island <em>every</em> Sunday from November through April, the New Year&apos;s Day plunge always gets the biggest turnout. It&apos;s symbolic, or something! Click through to live vicariously through those who decided to cure their hangovers and/or start the year off re-energized with the freezing cold renewal ritual.</p>
