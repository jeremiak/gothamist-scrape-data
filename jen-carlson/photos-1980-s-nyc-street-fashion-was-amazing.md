</div></div>					<p>Tomorrow night photographer Jamel Shabazz will lead a discussion at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151102195930/http://www.tenement.org/vizcenter_events.php">the Tenement Museum</a>, complete with a slideshow of his best shots from 1980s New York City. Street fashion, hip-hop, and graffiti, oh my! Click through for a little preview, and there are many more photos <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151102195930/http://www.jamelshabazz.com/js_bitd.html">right here</a>. If you&apos;re left wanting more than a slideshow, pick up a boombox, some legwarmers, and Shabazz&apos;s book, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151102195930/http://www.amazon.com/Back-Days-Remix-10th-Anniversary/dp/1576875679/ref=sr_1_2?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1307045796&amp;sr=1-2"><em>Back In The Days</em></a> (the 10th anniversary edition will be released on June 21st).</p>
