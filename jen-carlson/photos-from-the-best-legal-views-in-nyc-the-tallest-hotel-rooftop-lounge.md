					<p>The views around New York City&#x2014;whether you&apos;re on the ground or 100-floors over it, whether you&apos;re a tourist or native&#x2014;are spectacular to behold, and you don&apos;t need to be an urban explorer leaning precariously over a ledge to enjoy some of the best ones. Over the next week, we&apos;ll be visiting some of the highest legally-accessed views around Manhattan&#x2014;<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021122807/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/11/legal_highs_1wtc.php#photo-1">we previously went over to 1WTC</a> and the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021122807/http://gothamist.com/2016/07/12/top_of_the_rock_views.php">Top of the Rock</a>, and today we&apos;re looking at Hyatt Times Square. Fun fact: it&apos;s the tallest hotel rooftop lounge in the city.</p>

<p>While Times Square and corporate hotels may not scream &quot;romance,&quot; the combo can provide a fine perch to take in the views. The Hyatt Times Square has a rooftop bar (Bar 54) in the middle of the Midtown tourist hub, which will put you 54-stories above the bustling area. Click through (and check out the timelapse below) for a look at the views you can catch while sipping on a... <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021122807/http://timessquare.hyatt.com/content/dam/PropertyWebsites/hyatt/nycts/Documents/All/Summer2016Bar54MenuHTS.pdf">$25 cocktail</a>.</p>
