</div></div>					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170326011650/http://gothamist.com/tags/improveverywhere">Improv Everywhere</a> brought their antics to Coney Island yesterday, turning a beach day into a black tie affair! Hundreds of folks dressed in tuxedos and gowns confused the regular beachgoers, as they played in the sand and swam in the ocean in their formal wear. </p>

<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170326011650/http://improveverywhere.com/2010/08/21/thank-you-black-tie-beach/">According to IE</a>, they &quot;covered a mile-long stretch of beach with a diverse group of people of all ages (from babies to sixty-somethings) laying out, playing games, and swimming in the ocean, all in formal wear. The mostly Russian crowd of beachgoers appeared to have a blast watching the spectacle unfold, laughing, taking photos, and asking questions.&quot; Stay tuned for video!</p>
