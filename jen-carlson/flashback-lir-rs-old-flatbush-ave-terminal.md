</div></div>					<p>The new Atlantic Terminal is here (officially opening yesterday), and here&apos;s a look back on the old Flatbush Avenue Terminal, which used to be the LIRR&apos;s station in the area &#x2014; currently where the Atlantic Terminal Mall stands. More photos of the old station, which opened in 1907, can be seen <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141229234058/http://arrts-arrchives.com/FAT1Rext.html">here</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141229234058/http://arrts-arrchives.com/FAT2int.html">here</a>. By the late &apos;70s &quot;the stores and offices were unoccupied, vandals entered and their looting caused water damage so severe that the upper waiting room was closed to the public.&quot;</p>
