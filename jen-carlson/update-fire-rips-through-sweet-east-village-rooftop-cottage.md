					<p><strong>[UPDATE BELOW] </strong>There&apos;s a fire at the rooftop cottage at the corner of East 13th Street and 3rd Avenue. Recently deemed <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161028111448/http://gothamist.com/2015/06/16/rooftop_cottage_envy.php#photo-1">the ultimate NYC home</a>, the cottage was just taken off the market and is in contract for around <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161028111448/http://www.corcoran.com/nyc/Listings/Display/3467444">$4.5 million</a>.</p>

<p>A resident in the building told us she was in her kitchen and heard someone running down the street saying: &quot;Dude, it&apos;s on fire! I&apos;m not shitting you.&quot;</p>

<p>Nearby resident Dylan Chenfeld sent in this video:</p>

<p><iframe width="640" height="480" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20161028111448if_/https://www.youtube.com/embed/xzdWOweVJEI" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>

<p>An FDNY spokesperson tells us there are no serious injuries reported at this time, but could not immediately provide any further details. We will update here as we find out more.</p>

<span class="photo_caption">(Gothamist)</span></p>

<p>It&apos;s unclear at this time how much of the property has been destroyed. We&apos;re hearing at the scene that it was &quot;just the elevator unit.&quot;</p>
