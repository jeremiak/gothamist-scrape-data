					<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160803040809/http://morbidanatomymuseum.org/">Morbid Anatomy Museum</a>, which <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160803040809/http://gothamist.com/2014/06/27/inside_brooklyns_morbid_museum.php">opened almost exactly one year ago</a>, hosted a six hour summer event yesterday which fittingly featured everything from cat skulls to human teeth. And pretty much everyone who&apos;s into that sort of thing crammed into the museum&apos;s basement after waiting an hour on line to see the goods, and take some home.</p>
