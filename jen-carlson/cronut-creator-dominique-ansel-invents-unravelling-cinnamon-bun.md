Also: butter. 

</p><p>This thing is only available through December 31st, and you can <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021063306/http://gothamist.com/2015/04/29/dominique_ansel_kitchen.php#photo-1">pre-order it online</a> since it will probably sell out fast every day, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171021063306/http://gothamist.com/tags/cronuts">Cronut-style</a>. Currently the earliest pick-up time is this Friday.</p>
