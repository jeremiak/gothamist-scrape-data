                            <p>Burlesque is best when served with some inked up skin, Bj&#xF6;rk&apos;s &quot;Army Of Me,&quot; and some Manic Panic-era rebellion. Therefore Suicide Girls burlesque is probably the best burlesque. The troupe recently hit Gramercy Theatre with their <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140206185115/http://blackheartburlesque.com/">Blackheart tour</a>, and if you missed it you can catch them in Brooklyn this weekend&#x2014;Sunday, November 24th... right after church, and just in time for the holidays.</p>
