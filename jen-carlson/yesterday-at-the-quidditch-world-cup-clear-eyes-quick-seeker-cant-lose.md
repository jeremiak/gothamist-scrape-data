</div></div>					<p>Yesterday this year&apos;s <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150522013153/http://gothamist.com/2011/11/12/the_quidditch_world_cup_has_commenc.php#photo-1">Quidditch World Cup</a> kicked off on Randall&apos;s Island, and the teams have been back at it all day today, brooms in hand. Despite not having the ability to fly, a key element in the game, players have come from all over the world. Yesterday a commentator was heard declaring Texas A&amp;M;&apos;s team to be the Dillon Panthers of the bunch, or was it the East Dillon Lions? Either way, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150522013153/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=35IETpZHQHk">Hogwarts Forever</a>!</p>
