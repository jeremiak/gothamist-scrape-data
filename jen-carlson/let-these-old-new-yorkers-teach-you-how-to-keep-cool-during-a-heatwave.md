title: Let These Old New Yorkers Teach You How To Keep Cool During A Heatwave
---


                            
                            
                            
                            <p>If our modern day tips on beating the heat weren&apos;t your thing, well, that&apos;s just crazy, because <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928023456/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/16/heat_wave_cool_tips.php">sharing a tiny bathtub with a friend</a> is quite simply the perfect way to cool down. But here are some more traditional tips, from the New Yorkers who came before us and didn&apos;t even have <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928023456/http://gothamist.com/2012/07/12/did_you_know_the_air_conditioner_is.php">air conditioning</a> <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130928023456/http://gothamist.com/2012/07/19/we_got_our_first_air_conditioned_su.php">in their subway cars yet</a>.</p>
