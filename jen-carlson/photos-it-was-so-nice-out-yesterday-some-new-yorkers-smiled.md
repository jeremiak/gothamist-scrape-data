					<p>We were spoiled by Spring this year&#x2014;its beautiful weather may have been broken up by some intermittent storms, but the second most perfect season (it&apos;s no Autumn!) still lasted longer than usual. Right? It felt that way, at least. But now <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161021121211/http://gothamist.com/2016/05/25/hot_humid_hell_alert.php">the heat is on</a>, and while we&apos;ll grow to despise it as we sweat on crowded subway platforms for the next few months, at least, for now, some New Yorkers seem pretty goddamn happy it&apos;s here.  </p>

<p>Yesterday photographer Scott Lynch scoped out the scene&#x2014;visiting Tompkins Square Park, Washington Square Park, Hudson River Park, Christopher Street Pier, the Pier 62 Skatepark, Herald Square, Bryant Park, Times Square, Columbus Circle, and Sheep Meadow&#x2014;capturing gleeful humans soaking up the sun. Have you ever seen such happiness? People are doing cartwheels! Cartwheel-level happiness is rare, let&apos;s appreciate it... here are 42 photos of people (and dogs) emanating pure joy.</p>
