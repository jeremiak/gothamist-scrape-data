</div></div>					<p>This weekend some very flexible folks took over the Hudson Theatre on West 44th Street for the NY Regional Yoga Championship. The competition&#x2014;hosted by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504180443/http://www.usayoga.org/">USA Yoga</a>&#x2014;promoted all styles of yoga, and featured over 140 competitors who were judged on technical execution, level of difficulty, poise and composure, and grace of movement. Considering yoga means &quot;union&quot; it seems a little weird to have a divisive yoga <em>competition</em>, but whatever, check out these rock hard abs! Do you do these poses in your adorable little Brooklyn yoga class before skipping off to brunch?</p>
