</div></div>					<p> According to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160521044900/http://www.newyorkology.com/archives/2010/03/lego_repairs_co_1.php">NewYorkology</a>, artist Jan Vormann has been busy repairing some holes in New York City buildings with Legos! Vormann&#x2019;s website now has a <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160521044900/http://www.dispatchwork.info/new-york/">DispatchWork&#x2014;New York</a> section, which documents the latest Lego frenzy. He writes, &#x201C;In cooperation with the Gallery Jarmuschek+Partner and the kind support of Henk Holzheimer (LEGO Graffity Styles Convention), I went to New York City, as part of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160521044900/http://www.voltashow.com/Jan-Vormann.5905.0.html">VOLTA</a> artshow, to support Mayor Bloomberg in his everyday-struggle to make this city even more amazing.&quot; We think he succeeded.</p>
