</div></div>					<p>The recently opened art installation <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504180434/http://headscapes-show.tumblr.com/">Headscapes </a>in Long Island City is housed inside of a former taxi service center, and boasts large-scale and interactive works. The pieces were built by artists to represent &quot;an inhabitable environment where their creativity springs from. The structures&apos; interior spaces materialize the internal palette of symbols, feelings, theories and processes each artist uses to make their work.&quot; In other words, you&apos;ll get to get inside their head space and see where the art comes from <em>while being inside of it</em>&#x2014;click through for what&apos;s in store.</p>

<p>The installation will be in place through March 24th, and the space is open Wednesdays through Sundays, 12 to 6 p.m. A list of the artists involved can be <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150504180434/http://headscapes-show.tumblr.com/announcement">found here</a>.</p>
