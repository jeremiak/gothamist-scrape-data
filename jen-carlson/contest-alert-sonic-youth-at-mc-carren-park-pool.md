Don&apos;t want to spend $34 + fees for <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150408153933/http://www.ticketmaster.com/event/00003EA19FD8C068?artistid=736153&amp;majorcatid=10001&amp;minorcatid=60">a ticket</a>?
