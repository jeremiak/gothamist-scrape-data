<p>Amy Schumer continues her Conquering The World Tour with the announcement that, in addition to her <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160309000802/http://gothamist.com/2015/04/22/best_amy_schumer_video.php">popular show</a> and upcoming<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160309000802/http://gothamist.com/2015/02/11/video_amy_schumer_takes_spotlight_i.php"> Judd Apatow movie</a>, she&apos;ll be opening for Madonna at Madison Square Garden and Barclays Center this September. She made the announcement via social media, posting the below video along with this message: &quot;Guess who auditioned for Madonna and got the part?&quot;</p>
