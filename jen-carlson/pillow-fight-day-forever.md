The police admired our cleanup effort, and I am about to deliver 1000 pillows to two midtown no-kill animal shelters to be transformed into bedding for dogs and cats. While we can&apos;t say &apos;only in New York&apos; anymore with the 150+ cities that participated this year, we still have the biggest!&quot;</p>
