										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="madmentv0711.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428150117im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/arts_jen/madmentv0711.jpg" width="640" height="433" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Don Draper &amp; Co. may be on hiatus, with season 5 of the show not airing until <em>next year</em>, but AMC will re-air every single <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428150117/http://gothamist.com/tags/madmen"><em>Mad Men</em></a> episode ever made, from beginning to end, starting July 31st! According to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428150117/http://content.usatoday.com/communities/popcandy/post/2011/07/amc-lets-mad-men-viewers-catch-up/1">Pop Candy</a>, they will air in three-hour blocks starting at 6 a.m.&#x2014;right around the time you&apos;re pouring whiskey into your first cup of Folgers. Set your DVRs, and if <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428150117/http://www.theawl.com/2011/07/what-kind-of-%E2%80%98i-don%E2%80%99t-own-a-tv%E2%80%99-people-do-you-have-in-your-life">you don&apos;t have a television</a>, you can watch every episode on your laptop <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150428150117/http://gothamist.com/2011/04/06/netflix_will_be_your_mad_men_fix_th.php">via Netflix Instant</a>, starting July 27th.</p>
