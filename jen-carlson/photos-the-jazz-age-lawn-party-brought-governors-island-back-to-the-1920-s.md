					<p>The <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161212025552/http://gothamist.com/tags/jazzagelawnparty">Jazz Age Lawn Party</a> returned to Governors Island this past weekend, bringing New Yorkers decked out in 1920s gear over to the out-of-this-era oasis. Photographer Sai Mokhtari was there to capture the boas, the fringed frocks, and fedoras&#x2014;click through for a look.</p>

<p>It&apos;s truly one of the greatest annual events in NYC, and luckily there&apos;s another chance to experience it in August. Tickets are still available for their final two parties, with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161212025552/http://jazzagelawnparty.ticketfly.com/">prices ranging from $35 to $5,000</a>&#x2014;that kind of Gatsby-level extravagance never ends well, though, so buyer beware. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161212025552/http://gothamist.com/2014/06/16/photos_jazz_age_lawn_party.php#photo-1">We maintain</a> that the cheap seats are all you really need to enjoy this one.</p>
