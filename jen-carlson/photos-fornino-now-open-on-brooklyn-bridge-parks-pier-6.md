</div></div>					<p>Beloved pizza joint <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141016201749/http://gothamist.com/tags/fornino">Fornino</a> opened on Brooklyn Bridge Park&apos;s Pier 6 ahead of schedule over the long weekend, so you can now head there seven days a week for your pizza and skyline fix.</p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="skylinefornino.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20141016201749im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/arts_jen/skylinefornino.jpg" width="640" height="463" class="image-none"> </span><br>
<span class="photo_caption">The view</span></p>

<p>But that&apos;s not all Chef Ayoub is offering <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141016201749/http://forninopizza.com/">at the location</a>&#x2014;he&apos;ll be serving up pies (a limited menu of six, listed below), salads, sandwiches, soft serve, <em>and</em> there&apos;s a full bar on the roof open until midnight. We&apos;re told &quot;food works on a ticketing system similar to the Standard beer garden and tickets are $3/each. A 4 ticket pie is $12, beer/wine are 2 tickets/$6, Prosecco is 3 tickets/$9 and cocktails are 4 tickets/$12.&quot; Which seems unnecessarily confusing! So, when looking at the menu, keep in mind those are ticket numbers, not prices. <strong>UPDATE:</strong> The day after this was published Fornino abandoned their ticket system&#x2014;hurrah!</p>

<p><strong>PIZZA</strong><br>
Genovese - mozzarella, ricotta, pesto, cherry tomatoes, parmigiano, 4 <br>
Funghi Misti - mozzarella, mixed wild mushrooms, cacio avalio, white truffle oil, 5,  <br>
Al Roker - tomato, mozzarella, fontina, roasted peppers, caramelized onions, sopressata picante, rosemary, 5 <br>
Marinara - tomato, garlic, oregano, olive oil, 2<br>
Margherita Classica - tomato, mozzarella, basil, olive oil, parmigiano, 3<br>
Calabrese - tomato, mozzarella, sopressata picante, parmigiano, 4</p>

<p>Note: We&apos;re told pies are on the smaller side, &quot;about the size of a standard dinner plate.&quot;</p>

<p><strong>SALAD</strong><br>
Fornino&apos;s Ceasar - parmigiano, creamy parmigiano dressing, 2<br>
Wild Arugula - Gorganzola Dolce, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Lemon, 2<br>
Baby Lettuce - Aged Balsamic Vinaigrette, Shaved Parmigiano, 2</p>

<p>The full bar includes Peroni and a rotating selection of Brooklyn Brewery beers on tap, as well as the cocktails and wine.</p>
