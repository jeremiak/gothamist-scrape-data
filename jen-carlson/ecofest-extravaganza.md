										<p><img alt="200709eco.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508175650im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/arts_jen/200709eco.jpg" width="240" height="199" class="right">End September with (the Kelly Ripa-approved!) <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508175650/http://www.ecofest.com/">Ecofest</a> this Sunday, the East Coast&apos;s largest environmental event. In it&apos;s 19th year it continues to showcase all aspects of the green movement from technology to lifestyle, and even fashion. The fest will feature:</p><blockquote>&#x2022;    Launch of the newest fleet of Chevrolet&apos;s and Volkswagen&apos;s fuel cell SUVs and hybrids in a first-ever preview<br>
&#x2022;    Education sessions with green thought leaders<br>
&#x2022;    For kids, eco-education through arts, crafts and music<br>
&#x2022;    Green lifestyle education including fitness and health topics</blockquote>It looks like green is the new black, with eco-fashions becoming more in style. The fest will also show some innovative designs which will be for sale (here are some shots <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508175650/http://www.ecofest.com/webpages/2006fashion_photos.htm">from last year&apos;s fashion show</a>). Kids, parents, fashionistas, gearheads, health nuts, artists...will all be coming together in the name of green, join them!<p></p>

<p>When: Sunday September 30th, from 11am to 6pm<br>
Where: Lincoln Center Plaza, Damrosch Park, (62nd Street at Amsterdam Avenue)</p>
