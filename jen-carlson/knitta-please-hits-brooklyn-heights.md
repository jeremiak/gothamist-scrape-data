</div></div>					<p>Last night <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425001015/http://gothamist.com/2009/04/20/magda_sayeg_knittaplease.php">Magda Sayeg</a> gathered up the knitting troops to bring these colorful knit &quot;tags&quot; to Brooklyn. 69 Meters is a public art installation commissioned by the Montague Street Business Improvement District and created by <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150425001015/http://knittaplease.com/KNITTA_PLEASE.html">Knitta Please</a> (the group which Sayeg is the founder). They&apos;ve tagged everything from city buses to the Great Wall of China, so why not Brooklyn Heights? Let&apos;s hope the rain this morning didn&apos;t make the crafty pieces as soggy as that puppy did.</p>
