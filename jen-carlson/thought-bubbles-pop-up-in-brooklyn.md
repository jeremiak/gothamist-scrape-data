It&apos;s nice to imagine a New York City where people run around pondering cupcakes, love and ladies in bikinis&#x2014;even though you know they were <em>probably</em> obsessing about unpaid bills, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120118235318/http://gothamist.com/2008/11/25/knicks_deal_hits_a_snag.php">the Knicks</a>, and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120118235318/http://gothamist.com/2008/12/22/hello_3_subway_ride.php">the $3 MetroCard</a>.</p>
