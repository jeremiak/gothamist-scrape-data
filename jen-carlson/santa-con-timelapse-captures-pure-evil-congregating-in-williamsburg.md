<p>From atop the luxury living units of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161212025542/http://gothamist.com/2015/05/28/101_bedford_speedo_ready.php">101 Bedford</a> on Saturday morning, the view was grim. Hundreds of humans congregated in McCarren Park like an army of blood-engorged bedbugs poised to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161212025542/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OLaaqX6A3AU">traumatically inseminate</a> New York City. They were all decked in red plush holiday cheer, which would later to be encrusted in vomit and the unmistakeable aroma of bad decisions.</p>

<p>Santa is supposed to be on the rooftop, looking down unto the people, not the other way around&#x2014;this is the kind of bizarro <em>Black Mirror</em> reality we should not be creating for ourselves.</p>
