</div></div>					<p>Recently we dusted off some of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429010150/http://www.flickr.com/photos/stevensiegel/">Steven Siegel</a>&apos;s old photos from 1980s New York City, showing the five boroughs during what many dub The Bad Old Days. Sure, things looked grim and downright apocalyptic <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150429010150/http://gothamist.com/2012/01/18/amazing_photos_of_1980s_new_york_ci.php">in that series of images</a>, but there was some life here, too. Here&apos;s another collection of photographs from Seigel&apos;s archives, showing New Yorkers in the 1980s, mugging for his camera. Click through for a glimpse at the faces of those living here back in those Bad Old Days, and we&apos;ll have more from Siegel in the near future.</p>

<p>While specific locations and years aren&apos;t known for some of the photos, they were all taken in NYC in the 1980s.</p>
