                            <p>Yesterday we looked at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130926053349/http://gothamist.com/2013/07/15/photos_punk_rock_tattoos_free_hugs.php#photo-17">the style of Vans Warped Tour attendees</a>, but now it&apos;s time to check out their crowdsurfing skills. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130926053349/http://www.flickr.com/photos/jimkiernan/sets/72157634640621512">Jim Kiernan</a> photographed the festival&#x2014;already in its 19th year!&#x2014;for us, noting that the &quot;kids seemed very safe and mellow. I didn&apos;t see anybody who seemed drunk, high or super-aggro the entire day.&quot; But while the fest is pretty Punk Lite, there were some that braved the human waves, presumably for crowdsurfing selfies. Holding your camera vertically while crowdsurfing is super punk...</p>
