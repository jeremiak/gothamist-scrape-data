					<p>Last night the Algonquin Hotel hosted <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161026033042/http://gothamist.com/2016/08/09/the_algonquins_cat_fashion_show_ret.php#photo-1">their annual Cat Fashion Show</a> for a very good cause: to help raise money for the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161026033042/http://www.animalalliancenyc.org/">Mayor&apos;s Alliance for NYC&apos;s Animals</a>. But the cats participating seemed even more subdued than the audience... and a little uncomfortable in their costumes. </p>

<p>This is the 10th year the Cat Fashion Show has taken place at the Algonquin&#x2014;where <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161026033042/http://gothamist.com/2011/12/29/matilda_the_cat_leaves_the_algonqui.php">Matilda III the Cat</a> resides&#x2014;and the theme was: Through the Decades. On hand were Cat Al Hirschfeld, Cat Michael Feinstein, Cat Marilyn Monroe, Cat John Barrymore and... Millennial Cat, who was just wearing a garment with skulls on it, which is really more early-aughts Hot Topic Cat. </p>

<p>Our favorite, however, was the adorable Harpo, who has two different color eyes, and now... our hearts.</p>
