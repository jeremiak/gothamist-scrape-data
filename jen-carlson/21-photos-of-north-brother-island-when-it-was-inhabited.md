</div></div>					<p>We have a little obsession with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510032241/http://gothamist.com/tags/northbrotherisland">North Brother Island</a> over here, which is located just off shore from the Bronx in the Hell Gate section of the East River. We&apos;ve looked at the former home of Typhoid Mary in its <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510032241/http://gothamist.com/2012/02/21/john_krawchuk_interview.php#photo-1">current state</a> of <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150510032241/http://gothamist.com/2011/01/14/north_brother_island.php#photo-1">decaying beauty</a>, but what did it look like when it was inhabited? Click through for a look back&#x2014;these are mostly from the 1950s, when the isalnd was home to an experimental drug treatment program, which was mired in staff corruption as well as beliefs that patients were being held against their will.</p>
