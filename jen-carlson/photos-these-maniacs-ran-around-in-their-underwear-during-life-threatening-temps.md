					<p>On Saturday some unhinged lunatics actually <em>paid money</em> to be out in <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161108050020/http://gothamist.com/2016/02/13/de_blasio_wants_new_yorkers_to_stay.php">the below-freezing &quot;life-threatening&quot; weather</a> in their underwear. The annual Cupid Undie Run&#x2014;which raises money for the Children&apos;s Tumor Foundation&#x2014;went down around 2 p.m. near Pier 84 in Manhattan. </p>

<p>Intrepid photographer Gretchen Robinette tells us the run was planned to be one mile, but it was shortened due to the extreme cold&#x2014;&quot;I think about 200 or so people ran, most of them in their underwear, some wore a little bit more.&quot; (Organizers tell us there were 600 participants.) After the quick jaunt the attendees headed to a toasty after-party in their knickers. Click through for a look, and remember that pants prevent hypothermia.</p>
