					<p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170131203024/http://gothamist.com/tags/idiotarod">The Idiotarod</a> is back, and kicked off this afternoon in Brooklyn Bridge Park, just as snow flurries started in the area. For the first time ever the event is being held in Trump&apos;s U.S. of Idiocracy, and while that&apos;s not being recognized by many teams, there were a few small gestures of protest spotted. Members of Team Edward Scissorhands, for example, are carrying signs that read:  &quot;Scissorhand not tiny hands,&quot; and &quot;Make America Goth Again.&quot; </p>

<p>This is the 13th annual Idiotarod, and photographer Scott Lynch tells us there are &quot;at least fifteen teams&#x2014;largest turnout in a several years.&quot;</p>

<p>The route is kept a secret, but the teams have departed Brooklyn Bridge Park and are making their way through the borough. More photos coming on Sunday.</p>
