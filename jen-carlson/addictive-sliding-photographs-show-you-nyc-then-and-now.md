</div></div>					<p>If you haven&apos;t been checking <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150511040635/http://www.nyc-grid.com/">NYC Grid</a>&apos;s before and after posts, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150511040635/http://www.nyc-grid.com/home/tag/before-after"><em>the time is now</em></a>. The site takes a present day photo of a landmark or intersection or building in New York, and overlays it with a photo from the past (way back, to as recent as 2009), allowing the viewer to slide back and forth between the two. It&apos;s incredibly addictive!</p>
