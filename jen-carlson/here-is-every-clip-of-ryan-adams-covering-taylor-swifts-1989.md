										<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> <img alt="TSADAMS15.jpg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20151223225906im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/arts_jen/TSADAMS15.jpg" width="640" height="589" class="image-none"> </span></p>

<p>Ryan Adams has been slowly delivering glimpses into his <em>1989</em> cover sessions via a series of dimly lit videos of his mixing board. The musician is currently recording every song from Taylor Swift&apos;s most recent album (<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151223225906/https://twitter.com/TheRyanAdams/status/631763928515670016">including bonus tracks</a> &#x201C;Wonderland&#x201D; and &#x201C;New Romantics&quot;), adding his own touch with synths, slides, and strings. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151223225906/http://gothamist.com/2015/08/10/ryan_adams_t_swift.php">It is goddamn beautiful</a>. </p>

<p>It&apos;s unclear how the project came about, as it appears Swift herself learned about it <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151223225906/https://twitter.com/taylorswift13/status/629212668218314752">through Twitter</a>. Details regarding when the cover album will be available also haven&apos;t been released, but <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151223225906/https://twitter.com/TheRyanAdams/status/631512601688584192">as of August 12th</a> he had four songs left to go. </p>

<p>Below, find every clip Adams has shared via social media&#x2014;the latest is &quot;Wildest Dreams,&quot; which features a swampy tremolo soaked in reverb (our in-house musician <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151223225906/https://twitter.com/yenbakas">Ben Yakas</a> tells us). You&apos;ll also find &quot;Welcome To New York,&quot; &quot;Shake It Off,&quot; &quot;Stay,&quot; &quot;Bad Blood,&quot; and &quot;I Know Places,&quot; but Adams seems to be keeping &quot;Blank Space,&quot; which he&apos;s already recorded, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20151223225906/https://instagram.com/p/6CJk04zDOA/">under wraps</a>. (Update: Scroll down for &quot;Blank Space&quot; &amp; &quot;Style&quot; &amp; more... we&apos;re constantly updating this as new clips are released.)</p>
