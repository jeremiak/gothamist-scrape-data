<p>The Crossroads of the World tops the list of the 20 most Instagrammed spots <em>in the world</em>, according to a new list released by Instagram. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160426130819/http://www.travelandleisure.com/articles/most-instagrammed-places-world">Travel + Leisure</a> notes that the list reflects this year&apos;s top spots... so next year, let&apos;s try to be a little more original. </p>

<p>Out of the 20 spots, five were in the U.S.&#x2014;outside of Times Square, there&apos;s Dodger Stadium in L.A., Wrigley Field in Chicago, the Bellagio in Las Vegas, and the Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco. Maybe only tourists use the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160426130819/https://www.instagram.com/explore/locations/482771150/">geotagging</a> feature?</p>

<p>Instagram has also sent us this list of the top 20 Instagrammed places in just NYC: </p>

<p>1. Times Square<br>
2. Central Park<br>
3. Madison Square Garden<br>
4. Yankee Stadium<br>
5. The Metropolitan Museum of Art<br>
6. Brooklyn Bridge<br>
7. Empire State Building<br>
8. The High Line<br>
9. MoMA The Museum of Modern Art<br>
10. Rockefeller Center<br>
11. Citi Field<br>
12. Statue of Liberty National Monument<br>
13. Top of the Rock NYC<br>
14. Bryant Park<br>
15. Barclays Center<br>
16. Grand Central Terminal<br>
17. American Museum of Natural History<br>
18. Whitney Museum of American Art<br>
19. Washington Square Park<br>
20. Coney Island Boardwalk</p>

<p>Below are the current top posts from Times Square, via the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160426130819/https://www.instagram.com/explore/tags/timessquare/">#timessquare hashtag</a>, which has a total of 1,380,576 posts under it. For the record, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160426130819/https://www.instagram.com/explore/tags/centralpark/">Central Park</a> has 2,730,912. </p>
