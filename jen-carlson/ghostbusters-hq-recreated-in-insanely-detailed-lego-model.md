He write, &quot;Two months of endless hours watching <em>Ghostbusters</em> and <em>Ghostbusters 2</em>,  and the animated show, I can truly say I know a lot about <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104448/http://gothamist.com/2011/05/19/the_ghostbusters_firehouse_could_be.php">the building</a> at North Moore Street.&quot; He then goes into excruciating details about how the building&apos;s details differ in each version that exists, including the video game... but what&apos;s really important is the outcome, which is awesome. Click through for a look, and more images can be seen <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104448/http://orionpax.de/23/index.php/showroom/gallery/31/GHOSTBUSTERS-HQ">on his website</a>. [via <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130604104448/http://laughingsquid.com/a-lego-ghostbusters-fire-station-headquarters/">Laughing Squid</a>]</p>
