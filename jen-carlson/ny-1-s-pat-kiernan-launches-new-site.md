										<p>Everyone&apos;s favorite NY1 anchor (and Paul Rudd <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160229223754/http://gothamist.com/2008/11/12/paul_rudd_brings_fantasy_football_a.php">fantasy football team</a>), Pat Kiernan, has branched out his <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160229223754/http://www.ny1.com/content/features/in_the_papers/default.aspx">In the Papers</a> feature with a website called <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160229223754/http://www.patspapers.com/">Pat&apos;s Papers</a>. Described as &quot;a carefully edited collection of US news headlines,&quot; the top stories are delivered once Kiernan edits down the best from not only the nation&apos;s papers, but &quot;international news to domestic politics to science to gossip.&quot; In about six minutes, he sums it all up&#x2014;take a look at today&apos;s webcast:</p>
