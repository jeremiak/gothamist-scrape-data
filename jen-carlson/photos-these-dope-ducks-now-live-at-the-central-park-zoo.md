					<p>Flocks of new waterfowl have hatched at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160426150141/http://www.centralparkzoo.com/">Central Park Zoo</a>, and the Wildlife Conservation Society has shared photos of the new additions&#x2014;which are all different rare or endangered species. Click through for some dope duck shots, along with a little bit about each of the creatures. </p>

<p>The Central Park Zoo has been operating a waterfowl breeding program for four years, and currently have 23 different duck species on exhibit&#x2014;&quot;the largest public collection of sea ducks both by number of individuals and species represented in the world.&quot; Most of the species are uncommon to zoos, or endangered.</p>
