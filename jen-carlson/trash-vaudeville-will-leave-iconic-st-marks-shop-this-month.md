					<p>Last summer a rumor circulated <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160810181359/http://gothamist.com/2015/07/28/trash_and_vaudeville_st_marks.php">that Trash &amp; Vaudeville would be moving</a> out of its storefront in St. Mark&apos;s Place, which they&apos;ve inhabited for over 40 years. When we dropped by, we were told this wasn&apos;t true, but owner Ray Goodman has now all but confirmed it with <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160810181359/http://evgrieve.com/2016/02/feb-29-is-last-day-on-st-marks-place.html#.VsdSYds8DqQ.twitter">EV Grieve</a>, saying: &quot;The rent is creeping up. Rent was a factor, but it wasn&apos;t the sole reason.&quot; And today, the shop posted <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160810181359/https://www.instagram.com/p/BB-Yl-TrS9-/?taken-by=trashandvaudeville">on their Instagram</a> that their time in the space is coming to a close at the end of the month.</p>

<blockquote>Hey Ho Lets Go... to 96 East 7th street! After over 40 years at 4 saint marks place, Trash &amp; Vaudeville will be relocating to 96 East 7th street between 1st Avenue and Avenue A (just a few blocks away!) We&apos;ll be on Saint Marks till Feb 29th. We would love to see you here before we move and we look forward to seeing you on 7th street in early March! We&apos;ll keep you updated with confirmed dates.</blockquote>
