</div></div><p><a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120428230448/http://gothamist.com/tags/fashionweek">Fashion Week</a> ended with a whimper last night (or, if there was a bang, we weren&apos;t invited to witness it). So let&apos;s take a look back at what people were wearing <em>off</em> the runway at the shows. Photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20120428230448/http://samhorine.com/">Sam Horine</a> dropped by the DKNY show on 26th Street and 3.1 Phillip Lim in the Meatpacking District, and here&apos;s what he saw. To make this interesting, grab a bottle of champagne and drink every time you see: a ballerina bun, an animal print, fur (real or fake), the color &quot;electric blue,&quot; sunglasses, or naked eyes with a twinkle of self-satisfaction in them.</p>
