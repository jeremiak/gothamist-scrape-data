</div></div>					<p>On this day 49 years ago, two planes collided in midair above Staten Island &#x2014; causing the United Airlines flight to crash into a Park Slope intersection (7th Avenue and Sterling Street) and a TWA aircraft&apos;s wreckage to land in Miller Field on Staten Island. The crash killed 134 passengers as well as 6 people on the ground &#x2014; one young boy survived the crash, but died at the NY Methodist hospital the next day. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141102030700/http://theboweryboys.blogspot.com/2009/12/today-in-history-dual-tragedies-in.html">The Bowery Boys</a> note there are still scars to be seen on Sterling Place, and more images of the aftermath can be seen at the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20141102030700/http://iii.brooklynpubliclibrary.org/search~S63/?searchtype=X&amp;searcharg=plane+crash+park+slope&amp;searchscope=63&amp;SORT=DZ&amp;extended=0&amp;SUBMIT=Search&amp;searchlimits=&amp;searchorigarg=Xplane+crash%26SORT%3DDZ">Brooklyn Public LIbrary</a>.</p>
