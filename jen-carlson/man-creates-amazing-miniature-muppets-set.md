</div></div><p>If you were interested in procuring the original Palisades Muppet toys, it would cost you some big bucks on eBay (the backstage playset is around <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130404055006/http://cgi.ebay.com/Muppets-Palisades-Playset-Backstage-Rowlf-Henson-Show-/380324335788?pt=LH_DefaultDomain_0&amp;hash=item588d18d0ac">$600</a>!). So, what&apos;s an avid collector to do when the company producing these items goes belly up without having shipped Muppet Theatre Playset? If you&apos;re Lance Cardinal, you build your own. He&apos;s recreated not only <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130404055006/http://lancecardinal.blogspot.com/2011/03/scratch-built-palisades-muppet-theatre.html">the theater</a>, but <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130404055006/http://lancecardinal.blogspot.com/2011/03/custom-palisades-muppet-show-backstage.html">the backstage set</a> as well, noting, &quot;I have tried to capture the essence of the Palisades brand, including aesthetics, attention to detail and interactive fun!&quot; Yeah, we&apos;d say he captured it all right. [via <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20130404055006/http://www.boingboing.net/2011/04/06/scratch-built-muppet.html">Boing Boing</a>]</p>
