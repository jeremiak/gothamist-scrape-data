</div></div>					<p>In 1978 and 1979 photographer <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150508080652/http://digitalgallery.nypl.org/nypldigital/explore/dgexplore.cfm?col_id=622">Dinanda Nooney</a> took a series of photographs of the interiors of homes in Brooklyn. She photographed around 200 homes, coming out with nearly 2000 photos of Brooklynites in their natural habitats. From Bushwick to Bed-Stuy to Park Slope to Boerum Hill, here are 21 of the photographs she took, complete with addresses (maybe you&apos;ll see your apartment in there!).</p>
