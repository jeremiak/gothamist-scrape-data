title: [UPDATE] Two Window Washers Stuck Outside 65th Floor Of Midtown Marriott
---

	
										<p>Two window washers are stuck on scaffolding outside the 65th floor of the <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160702233937/http://www.marriott.com/hotels/travel/nycpk-courtyard-new-york-manhattan-central-park/">Courtyard Marriott hotel</a> between West 54th and 55th streets in Midtown, according to the FDNY [see update below]. Rescue teams are on site attempting to rescue the washers. A spokeswoman for the department described 1717 Broadway as an &quot;active scene.&quot; </p>

<p>According to the FDNY, a call came in at 1:29 p.m. on Tuesday. <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20160702233937/http://abc7ny.com/news/2-window-washers-stuck-outside-65th-floor-of-midtown-high-rise/1214983/">ABC reports</a> that mechanics have been unable to get the stalled scaffolding to budge, and that firefighters are working to remove a pane of glass in order to pull the window washers inside. </p>

<p>No injuries have been reported at this time. We&apos;ll update with new information as it becomes available. </p>

<p><strong>UPDATE:</strong> An FDNY spokeswoman confirmed that both window washers were rescued from the scaffolding around 3:00 p.m. Both were evaluated on the scene by EMS. There were no documented injuries, and no hospital transports. </p>
