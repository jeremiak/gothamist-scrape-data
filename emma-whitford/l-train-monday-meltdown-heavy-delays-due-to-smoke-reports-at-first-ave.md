title: L Train Monday Meltdown: Heavy Delays Due To Smoke Reports At First Ave
---

	
										<p>In a humorless Monday morning preview of the impending <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030143053/http://gothamist.com/2016/01/13/l_train_tunnel_closure_years.php">L-pocalypse</a>, L train service was down between Bedford Avenue and 8th Avenue in both directions this morning. </p>

<p>Service resumed shortly before 10:00 a.m., but <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20171030143053/http://www.mta.info/status/subway/L/24480847">residual delays are expected</a>. </p>

<p>The cause of this morning&apos;s sweaty nightmare was a report of smoke at the 1st Avenue L station in Manhattan, according to the FDNY. Firefighters responded at 9:04 a.m., and service was restored shortly before 10:00 a.m. There are no reported injuries.</p>
