<p>Police are searching for three muggers who forcibly robbed a man at the Delancey-Essex Street subway station on a recent Sunday evening, slicing the victim&apos;s back jeans pocket and grabbing a wallet and phone out of the front pocket of his sweatshirt. </p>

<p><span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;"> </span></p><div class="image-right"> <img alt="030716_DCPI.jpeg" src="https://web.archive.org/web/20170313155752im_/http://gothamist.com/attachments/nyc_ewhitford/030716_DCPI.jpeg" width="300" height="300"> <br> <i style=" width:300px; ;display:block"> The NYPD is looking for these three men in connection with the robbery (DCPI). </i></div> According to the NYPD, the victim, a 27-year-old man, was entering the Delancey-Essex F/J/M/Z station around 5:15 p.m. on Sunday February 28th, when one of the suspects opened an emergency exit and asked if he wanted to pass through. The victim declined. <p></p>

<p>As soon as he passed through the turnstile, however, the same suspect allegedly grabbed him from behind and shoved him against a wall. Another man stepped up and cut the back right pocket of his jeans, and a third grabbed a wallet and iPhone 6 out his sweatshirt. </p>

<p>No arrests have been made, and the investigation is ongoing. </p>

<p>Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD&apos;s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 1-800-577-TIPS (8477) or for Spanish, 1-888-57-PISTA (74782). The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website at <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170313155752/http://a056-crimestoppers.nyc.gov/crimestoppers/public/index.html">WWW.NYPDCRIMESTOPPERS.COM</a> or by texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.<br>
