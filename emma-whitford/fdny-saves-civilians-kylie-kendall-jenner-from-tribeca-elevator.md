title: FDNY Saves 'Civilians' Kylie & Kendall Jenner From Tribeca Elevator
---

	
										<p>An FDNY spokesman chided us today when we called to inquire about some celebrities who were trapped inside an elevator on Leonard Street in Tribeca last night.</p>

<p>&quot;<em>Civilians</em>,&quot; the spokesman said, correcting us. &quot;Because everybody&apos;s a civilian to the FDNY.&quot; </p>

<p>At any rate, Kendall and Kylie Jenner&#x2014;daughters of Kris and Caitlin, half-sisters of Kim Kardashian, brand ambassadors, models, etc.&#x2014;got trapped inside an elevator at a residential building at 15 Leonard Street last night, with famous friends <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017163552/https://www.instagram.com/haileybaldwin/?hl=en">Hailey Baldwin</a> and <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20161017163552/https://www.instagram.com/jordynwoods/?hl=en">Jordyn Woods</a>. </p>
