<p>Some N trains are terminating at 42nd Street or 57th Street-7th Avenue, and W trains are running along the Q line between 57th Street-7th Avenue and 96th Street in both directions. Southbound R trains are running on the F line from Jackson Heats-Roosevelt Avenue to Lexington Ave-63rd Street, and then via the Q line to 57th Street-7th Avenue. Check back <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20170519145011/http://www.mta.info/">here</a> for updates. </p>
